I know the CD Guide that Karen talks about, but from my quick read in the 
magazine store (not worth $6.95 for the whole thing), I don't think Kate was
saying  that Q didn't interview her. What I think she said was that they made up
the  "booze, blokes, fags and me" quote that was on the cover - that
she didn't actually  say that and they made it up to parody the type of headline
that was in tabloid  newspapers. But as far as I remember they actually did
conduct the face to face  interview that made up the bulk of the article.
