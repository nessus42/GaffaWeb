The Star

Wow! It's Blooming Great To Be Kate


Songbird: Kate, safe from vampires.


WOW! This is one Bush that even puts the flowers  in the shade. But it
wasn't the daffs that made budding  superstar Kate feel so blooming marvelous
yesterday...


The singer had just opened at the London Palladiun  after her first
smash-hit British tour and felt like sharing  her joy.


So she took a stroll through a city-centre park and told  her success story
to the trees. yes, Kate everything in  the garden's lovely.
