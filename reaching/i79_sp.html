"Fruit And Nut Kate"
 October 20, 1979


EVER wondered how the delicious Kate Bush  manages to keep her curves in the
right places  in all those tight leotards the lady leaps around  stage in?


Well, it's all down to what the lady eats, which  apparently isn't vary much
anyway, but when she  does feel peckish, there's nothing Miss Bush likes to 
bite into better than a nut cutlet, some raw vegetables  or a chunk of cheese,
'cos shapely Kate is a strict  vegetarian.


However, that's not to say she isn't partial to the odd  bar of choccy, and
on her recent tour her mother  would turn up at rehearsals with a huge bag
containing  fruit, nuts and countless Kit Kats -- Kate's favourite brand  of
choccy bar.


Despite the pressures of a pop packed life this little  lady somehow manages
to find the time to do all her  housework herself.


Explains Kate "Unless the flat is tidy I just can't get  on with my
songwriting.


"There's just no way I can sit down at the piano  when in the kitchen
there's a sinkful of washing up."
