Subject: New Musical Express by Len Brown 12th November 1988

The following article appears in this week's NME. I think all those who have heard any of the Voix Bulgares discs or the Trio Bulgarka will be very excited about the news that Kate is including Bulgarian voices on the new album. There's even mention of a tour (well kinda).

Down at the old Bul and Bush

Len Brown - New Musical Express 12th November 1988 Copyright IPC Magazines Ltd. 1988

Kate Bush and Yanka Rupkina sit on a bean-bag couch and sing to me, Bulgarian style; Rupkina bleating high and joyous above Bush's harmony.
