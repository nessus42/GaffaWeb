Daily Mail
 April 18, 1979


And now -- shudder heights?


By PAUL DONOVAN


THE wailing voice and haunting looks of Kate Bush  have already sold a
million records. So what's the next  step for the 20-year-old doctor's daughter
who this  week is playing to sell-out audiences at the London  Palladlum? If the
movie makers get their way, they'll  turn her into a sexy vampire and bring
shuddering frights  from the girl who rose to fame with her number one  smash
hit "Wuthering Heights." 


Elfin-faced Kate revealed yesterday : 'I've had two  scripts offered to me
recently. Both were horror films.


"But I wouldn't have thought I was a vampire.  And I don't consider
myself an actress, anyway."


Kate, who dances sinuously on stage to a background of  conjurers, smoke and
back-projected films, was asked  about the other role in which she has been
cast...a vamp. 


"I was very rebellious when the Press first gave me  the label of a sex
symbol," she said. 


"But it s an incredible compliment for a female to  get, so long as it
doesn't stand in the way of the music."
