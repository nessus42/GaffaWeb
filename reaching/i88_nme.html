Len Brown's New Musical Express interview, November 12, 1988



     [Edited by Andrew Marvick.]



 



                   Down at the old Bul' and Bush 



     Kate Bush and Yanka Rupkina sit on a bean-bag couch and sing to me,
Bulgarian style; Rupkina bleating high and joyous above Bush's harmony.



     Short but sweet, a brief musical meeting of East and West, it's the
sort of sensual aural treat every philanthropist would wish to share with the
world. Except that the World would have been pushed to fit in this studio broom
cupboard off Upper Street, Islington.
