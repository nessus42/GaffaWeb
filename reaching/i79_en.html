"In Loving Memory From Kate And Friends"


A tragic accident almost wrecked Kate Bush's debut  tour this month, she
revealed today. 


Kate and her band were rehearsing at Poole Arts Centre  on April 1 when
their lighting director slipped and fell  25 feel to his death from a gantry.


"Kate was so upset that her first reaction was to want  to call
everything off." a friend tells me.


But she was persuaded to carry on. And now Kate is giving  a special show at
Hammersmith Odeon on May 12 in tribute  to Bill Duffield, the man who died.


With her will be two other friends of Bill -- Steve Harley, making  his
first British state appearance for two-and-a-half years, and  Peter Gabriel.


All profits will be given to a trust fund set up in Bill's memory.
