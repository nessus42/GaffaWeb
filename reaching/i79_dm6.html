Daily Mirror
 April 18, 1979


What Kate won't do next


By STAN SAYER


SINGER Kate Bush has turned down the chance  to be a film star.


For the two leading roles she was offered  were in HORROR movies -- about
vampires.


And the prospect didn't wow 20-year-old  Kate at all.


She chuckled yesterday: " It's interesting  to see how people cast you.
But I don't think I'm anything like a vampire."


  Best


Kate, who was voted best female singer in the  Daily Mirror Rock and Pop
Awards last week is  currently starring in her first show at the London 
Palladium.


And she insisted: "Music and the theatre  are the only important things
in my life."


Did that exclude marriage ?


Kate said: "I have some boyfrlends, but  no one steady."


"The only reason I would marry is to have children  and they are a
responsibility that can't be taken lightly. 


"I couldn't have a career and children. It's either  one or the other."



What about her "sex symbol" tag?


Kate said: " It worried me at first that people  would look upon me as
a body and a face and  ignore my music.


"Nowadays I accept the sex symbol remarks  as a compliment."
