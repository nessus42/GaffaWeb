So, what's a Chop Shop? Well, here, in the basement rec-room hobbies part of the Gaffaweb, are lots of little bits and pieces of image excerpts taken from Kate's singles/albums/whatnot for you to look at, and take if you can find a use for them.

It all started when I was looking for suitable backgrounds for the FAQ. I tried solid colors, public-domain backgrounds, and backgrounds that I made myself in Paint Shop Pro. None of them seemed right, and finally I realized that the problem was that the background needed to be specific to Kate.

That realization led me to begin taking scans of her album covers and singles, cropping out parts of them, and manipulating the hell out of them in PSP. I don't have an artistic bone in my body, which made my quest even harder. I know what I think looks good, which may not be what anyone else thinks looks good, but I did what pleased me, because you can't please everybody.

I finally got pretty much what I wanted, but I had all these bits and pieces of experimentation fodder left over. I felt like a seamstress, who is loathe to throw away any piece of fabric in case it might come in handy later. I didn't want to delete them, but I didn't know what to do with them. Then I had the bright(?) idea that perhaps others might get a kick out of them. Maybe even use them for something, either personal or Kate-related.

So, here you go. Rummage through this pile of Kate fabric, lace, ribbons and gee-gaws, and if you find something that you might want as a background, icon, or picture, take it and have fun!

Each thing is available in both jpg and gif form, since some look better in one or the other format. There are jpgs, especially, that look like crapola, but look fine as gifs. Start looking at them in one format, and you'll see that you can switch back and forth between formats on each page.

There are a lot of pages, but there are only a few images on each page, to make loading faster.

Some of these things are very cool, and some are very silly. I take no responsibility for my frame of mind when I cropped them in the first place, because I most likely had none.

Have fun!
