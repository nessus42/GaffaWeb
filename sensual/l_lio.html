[*--"We let the weirdness in": This last line, heard nine
times, was  recorded in a very strange, "double-backwards" manner. As
a result, it  was extremely hard to decipher and, because it had been
deliberately  omitted from the lyric sheet, its solution became the object of a 
2-yearlong competition in the Club. There are some fans who even now  argue that
in addition to this line, another can be heard when the  track is played
backwards: "They said they would let us in," or "They  said they
were buried here," have become popular interpretations. But  others believe
these lines are only imagined."]
