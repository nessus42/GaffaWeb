This volume of The Garden contains every word known to have been written by
Kate Bush.  



It does not include transcriptions of interviews, with the exception of the
two or three interviews for which Kate herself devised and wrote the questions. 




Included are all of Kate's contributions to the Kate Bush Club Newsletter;
the complete lyrics of all of her songs; and various other brief texts which
have appeared from time to time over the last thirteen years.
