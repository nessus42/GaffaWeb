Disbelieving Angel

Allegedly, this song first appeared on the illustrious "Early Years" LP, to later resurface on the demo-tapes.


OoOoO


This song is classic early Kate. A haunting voice, the simple but effective sound of the piano, and typically early, poetic, lyrics. And whether the song really deals with an angel who's not really up to saving someone's soul yet, or instead is a straight-out-attack at organized religion, I guess is for the listener to decide.. .
