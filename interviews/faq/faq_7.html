The history of this FAQ starts with Jorn Barger writing a first
        FAQ for rec.music.gaffa/Love-Hounds. Later Ron Hill continued writing
        it. After Ron stopped to post I (i.e. Uli Grepel) continued this with a
        halfway rewrite. Even if I've written a lot of the stuff above it's not
        all my knowledge, it's yours, since I've pulled most of it from
        rec.music.gaffa itself. To list the contributors in finest detail would
        be impossible, since there are so many, especially of those who 'just
        pointed out some errors' to me. Steve Berlin,
        who spell checked it, and wrote small (very small - [SB]) parts of this
        FAQ.
