1.5. Other Artists using Kate's Work




    
    




    
    

      
      
 This section compiles a list of all (known) cover versions of Kate's
        songs, as well as a list of all (known) cases where Kate's music was
        sampled for use in another song. There are some question marks in there,
        which is when I didn't know more details. If you know the answer, don't
        hesitate! 

      
      
 Don't Give Up is also listed, despite the fact that it is not really
        a Kate Bush song. In some cases, there's no known recording of a live
        performance. Nevertheless these cases are listed as far as they are
        known.

          Bonheur / Kate Bush', album 'Il Est Libre Max') The song 'Kate Bush'
          is about a girl who has listened to Kate's records through a whole
          winter and then leaves her boyfriend. 
    •

          maxi single 'Babbittry Revision / Pretty Boys In Pink') Babbittry
          Prevision has 'very Kate-related lyrics'.

          the punchbowl) The song contains lines 'I've seen the likes of Kate
          Bush and Van Morrison / teaching the parrots to sing' [AR2]
