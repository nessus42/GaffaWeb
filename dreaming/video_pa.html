Date: Sun, 21 Sep 86 17:20:37 EDT
From: nessus (Doug Alan)
Subject: This year's KBC Convention

There is going to be another Kate Bush Convention in England this year. It will be on November 9th, and this one is going to be much more informal than the previous ones. A video dance club is being rented, and for six hours all the screens are going to be showing all sorts of Kate Bush related video. Kate's band and family will be there and people will be encouraged to just wander around and talk to everyone else. Kate will almost definitely be there, and this time, the folks at *Homeground* hope to convince Kate to come at the beginning and just mingle with everyone for the six hours. Tickets will be sold by mail in the next issue of *Homeground* and through the Kate Bush Club.

I'm not sure if I can picture Kate just mingling with 400 rabid fans... But people tend to be much politer in England...

-Doug
