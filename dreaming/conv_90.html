Page 2, Message from Lisa, Dave, Peter and Krys

17th November 1990

TODAY is the largest ever gathering of Kate fans.

TODAY we welcome people from all over the world, Europe, America, Canada, Japan and Australia.

TODAY we will belatedly celebrate the release of 'The Sensual World', Kate's most intimate and personal album to date.

TODAY is about 'this woman's work.' The beautiful box that, for the first time, brings all Kate's EMI releases together in one place.

TODAY we will entertain you with vidos, quizzes and live music from other fans.

TODAY is for you!
