Received the notice for John's new book on Kate, entitled "Cathy".
      Sounds very much as expected: great care taken over the printing, binding,
      paper, color processing ("duo-tone" prints of black and white
      photos); highly personal, hermetic shots of the little girl -- all very
      Victorian/Aestheticist/ Arts and Crafts
      movement/quasi-Pre-Raphaelite/Lewis Carrollish. In short, it sounds very
      promising. About 30 Pounds including air mail.
