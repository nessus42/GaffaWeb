Here's another "Kate first experience": My daughter, also
      named Kate (hmmmm...), is almost 5 and has always enjoyed Kate Bush. I've
      been playing it for her since she was born. She really enjoys listening to
      Kate Bush and expressing her interpretation of the music through dance.
      It's really quite amazing to watch what she does to a particular song. She
      makes quite the little Babooshka! 

    
    
Back a couple of years ago, she used to SAY that SHE was Kate Bush and
      would dance around trying to sing along and say, "Daddy, listen! It's
      Me Bush!" So, if nobody has seen or heard anything from Kate Bush
      lately...
