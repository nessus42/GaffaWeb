 Subject: Patriot Games 

    
    
The music for Patriot Games was indeed performed by Clannad. One of the
      songs that was feature was "Theme from Harry's Game" which can
      be found on Clannad's album "Magical Ring." This song was
      originally written for a UK TV show and made it to No. 5 on the UK charts
      back in the early 80's. Just to jog memory "Harry's Game" was
      the song playing when the IRA tried to shoot (what's his face) in the
      house while the TV was playing. If you looked closely at the screen you
      might have noticed that not only does the voice sound like Enya but the
      woman on the TV somewhat LOOKED like her too! This is easily explained
      though, Clannad's lead singer is Maire Ni Bhraonain --- Enya's older
      sister. In fact Enya is the baby sister of three of Clannad's members and
      the niece of the other two members. The only mystery that still remains
      for me is who is Maggie Boyle listed in the music credits....I haven't
      heard of any connection with Clannad before. Well, like I said to my
      boyfriend 5 seconds after the movie started "I'm getting the
      soundtrack!!" 

    
    
 --- Lionheart

She's a successful surgeon, has time to play with her daughter and knows
      how to use a shot gun WITHOUT ammunition!!!
