4.11. What about the
    
inscriptions on the vinyl discs? 




    
    




    
    

      
      
 On a lot of English first pressings of vinyl records you can find an
        inscription in the runout area of the disc. Sometimes these can still be
        found on later pressings, sometimes not. Except for the messages on the
        'Breathing' and 'There Goes A Tenner' discs these messages do not appear
        on the singles from the 'The Single File' boxed set. Kate does not only
        know about the inscriptions, she even writes them, so most of them are
        of some significance (comments in []s). Most vinyl records do have some
        numbers and/or the name of the mastering facility/person scratched in
        the runout area (e.g. Townhouse, Orlake). These normally appear on more
        than one record and are not written by Kate.
