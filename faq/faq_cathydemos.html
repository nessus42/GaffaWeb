        5.1. The Cathy Demos / The
		Studio Demos
 
	 
 
		
 Kate has never commented on any bootleg material, and so she hasn't
		  said anything about these as bootlegs. But she has said plenty of things about
		  these recordings - generally to the effect that there are about 200 or more of
		  them (20-30 have been 'released' on bootlegs) and that they should remain
		  buried and will never re-record any of them. The sound quality of all of them
		  reminds of a boombox set to 'record' in a living room. Nevertheless most fans
		  do LOVE these songs and would almost kill to hear the rest. The Experience
		  Music Project reportedly has a white tape box as part of an exhibit labeled
		  "Cathy Bush". Paul Allen is reportedly a Kate fan.
