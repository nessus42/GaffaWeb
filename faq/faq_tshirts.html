 There were two editions of The Official Lovehounds-T-Shirt. It
		  featured the album cover of The
		  Dreaming, but "Suspended in rec.music.gaffa" above it (the Suspended
		  In Gaffa single used the same picture). The back shows
		  "love-hounds@uunet.uu.com" (the old
		  address of LH) over a handwritten letter Kate gave to Doug Alan in New York in
		  December '93. 
 
		
 To Everyone at 
 "Love Hounds" 
 Thanks for all
		  your support 
 With Love 
 Kate Bush 
 xx
 
		
 The shirt was originally designed by Steve Berlin [SB]. It has been out of print for several years.
