Kate Bush Discussion Forums
 
	 
 
	 
 
	 
 
	 
There are several discussion groups you can join nowadays, each with its
		own format and flavor. Here are some of the major forums: 
 
	 
     
		
  • Lovehounds/rec.music.gaffa - The oldest
		  Kate forum on the internet (first started in 1985), but also one of the most
		  flame- and troll- infested forums. Highly recommended for those with very thick
		  skin. Unlike the other forums, this is a usenet group/mailing list. 
    
		   
    •  
		
  •  The Homeground/Kate Bush News and
		  Information Forum - The H/KBN&I Forum is run by Peter
		  Fitzgerald-Morris, editor of the Homeground fanzine, and Sean Twomey, who runs
		  the Kate Bush News and Information
		  site. With people like that behind it, it's no wonder that this is the best
		  source for Kate news and talk that is out there. 
      
    •  
		
  • The Kate Bush
		  Forum - A pretty straightforward Kate board with admins who work hard
		  to keep the place looking nice. 
      
    •  
		
  • The Sensual
		  World - Another nice-looking board which has separate sub-forums for
		  each album for in-depth discussion. 
      
    •  
		
  • Kate@forumz -
		  While not as feature-filled as most other Kate boards out there, what it
		  lacks in bells and whistles it more than makes up for in a high number of good
		  posts. 
    •  
	 
 
	 
For dozens of more groups, look at the

		Bush category of Yahoo Groups (doing
