Other Artists using Kate's Work 
 
	 
 
	 
 
	 
 
	 
 
		
 This section compiles a list of all (known) cover versions of Kate's
		  songs, as well as a list of all (known) cases where Kate's music was sampled
		  for use in another song. There are some question marks in there, which is when
		  I didn't know more details. If you know the answer, don't hesitate! 
 
		
 Don't Give Up is also listed, despite the fact that it is not really
		  a Kate Bush song. In some cases, there's no known recording of a live
		  performance. Nevertheless these cases are listed as far as they are known.
