The history of this FAQ starts with Jorn Barger writing a first FAQ for rec.music.gaffa/Love-Hounds. Later Ron Hill continued writing it. After Ron stopped posting, Uli Grepel continued this with a halfway rewrite. Even though Uli has written a lot of the stuff above it's not all his knowledge, it's yours, since he's pulled most of it from rec.music.gaffa itself. To list the contributors in finest detail would be impossible, since there are so many, especially of those who 'just pointed out some errors' to me. Steve Berlin, who spell checked it, and wrote small (very small - [SB]) parts of this FAQ. Recently the torch has been given to me (Birgitte Breemerkamp) to keep this page up to date.
