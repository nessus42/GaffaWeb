1.6. Chart success 




    
    




    
    

      
      
 This section is a basic listing of chart position of Kate's singles,
        albums and videos. Listing all chart positions from all charts from all
        countries with all dates would need much too much
        space for the FAQ, but such a list is in fact available. Send an e-mail
      to uli@zoodle.robin.de  and
        you'll get the latest copy. 

      
      
 Each item is only listed with the highest chart entry for each
        country . Some countries have official or
        semi-official charts. For these countries there might be two entries, if
        a specialised, non-local & non-radio- station chart shows a higher
        position. Sometimes the chart publisher isn't known. Some entries might
        even be wrong. 

      
      
 The (semi-)official charts are: 

      
      
 UK: BMRB/Gallup
        
 USA: Billboard Hot 100 (singles) / Top 200 (albums)
        
 Germany: Musikmarkt / Mediacontrol
        
 Australia: Aria
