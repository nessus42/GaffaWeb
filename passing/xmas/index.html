The Christmas Special

December 28, 1979



Kate, a forty-five-minute television special, BBC2
TV,
 featured songs old and new. Some of these were filmed during live

television-studio performances, others were videos prepared in advance
 and
featured studio recordings in more or less the same form as their
 album
counterparts. In addition, one or two small pieces of incidental
 music were
recorded specifically for the programme, which included a
 guest appearance
by Peter Gabriel, and a duet by Gabriel and Kate of
 Roy Harper's song,
Another Day.
