IED:

Kate sang Wuthering Heights live to a backing vocal; on the German TV
programme Scene 78.
 This clip was for some time the source of considerable
confusion, because it is simply a  German re-broadcast, from April 1978, of Kate’s
fourth performance of the song on March 23. 
[???No it isn't. VM] The KT Bush Band does not appear. Kate makes less of an
attempt to convey her dance movements in this  performance than she does in the
Bio's Bahnhof performance. Her vocal delivery is noticeably more assured,  also,
and a slight reverb effect adds greatly to the success. She is dressed in a
black dress [Nope. VM], and her make-up  is more
unassuming than on earlier the earlier German television appearances.  



      



 

VM:

I don't believe Andy had seen this at the time he wrote the above. The
appearance is live on a sound stage, with an audience, but is a lip-sync of the
album track. She sings into the camera the whole time, rather than to the
audience, and she and the camera twirl around each other. She wears pretty much
the same outfit she wore on TopPop (next entry), red feather thingy around her
neck, a black top and the black and white print
skirt often seen elsewhere.
