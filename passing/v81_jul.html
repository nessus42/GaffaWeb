IED:
  



    Kate discusses with the host and a group of children the     
making of the video Sat In Your Lap. This clip shows Kate sitting      among a
group of about fifteen children. She is wearing a skirt      over a leotard. She
discusses the making of her videos and her      way of devising ideas for the
visual presentation of her songs;      and she shares some of the costume-props
which were made and used      for the Sat In Your Lap video. Also, two of her
official videos are shown.
