Welcome to Passing Through Air! PTA is an effort to gather together all
pertinent information about  Kate's many public TV performances, lip-snycs,
interviews, appearances and mentions since the beginnning of her career. PTA was
compiled by combining two important video lists: one by Peter David
Fitzgerald-Morris and one by Andrew "IED" Marvick. Whenever possible,
an identifying image is (or soon will be) included. In some cases, those
thumbnail clips will lead to other images from that appearance.  



Occasionally there will be discrepancies in information, and little or no
information at all about an appearance. If you can add any information,
especially wrt the early and mid-career appearances, please write to
vickie@wwa. com so we can continue to build
as complete a record as possible. 



Passing Through Air is by no means finished, far from it. Many more images,
and (hopefully) much more information, will be added. Also on the horizon are
entries for the official videos, and listings by show titles and songs sung. I
hope you'll enjoy what's here now.
