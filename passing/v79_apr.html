    Documentary report on the preparations for     the Tour of
Life, aired on U.K. TV programme Nationwide.
     This film presents a
valuable look at Kate's preparations for the Tour     of Life concerts. There
are brief interview sections featuring Kate,     her brothers John Carder Bush
and Paddy Bush, and her dance instructor     and co-choreographer Anthony Van
Laast. At the end of the film Kate is     interviewed in a recording studio
where she is working on her third     album, Never For Ever.
