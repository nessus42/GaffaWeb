	item re Keef and videos - esp "Wow"       



 

IED:
  



    Kate's appearance on the news magazine report     about Keith "Keef"
MacMillan's work, including the making      of the Wow video. This brief film
focuses equally on MacMillan,      Kate's experience during the making of the
video, and the     topical subject of the rise of a then-new phenomenon: the
promotional     music-video. Kate predicts a great future for the
laser-video-disk,     before the home video-cassette market had yet taken off.
Her early     prediction may well prove ultimately accurate, however. This clip 
   includes footage of MacMillan directing the "flying" special-effect
    which involved connecting Kate to wires and hoisting her in the air; as    
well as one or two brief shots of Kate rehearsing the lip-synch.

    Lip-synch for Top of the Pops.     Also shown on the British

where, singing the      word "Vaseline", Kate pats her hip (the camera
studiously      avoids the relevant choreography).
