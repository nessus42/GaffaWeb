IED:
  



    The first of two videos (duet with Peter Gabriel), November
1986.     An extremely simple visual setting for the song by Gabriel,    
directed by Godley & Creme. Kate and Gabriel stand against a large    
blue-screen, on a nominal set of rocks and weeds, in a long and more or     less
static embrace throughout the entire video. Behind them on the     screen a huge
image of a solar eclipse slowly moves by. This video, by     Gabriel's own
admission, upset his wife despite his warnings to her     prior to the taping;
although there is nothing particularly romantic     about the embrace.
