IED:
  



    Sung live during the The Prince's Trust Gala, staged July 21,
1982; from the     home video. This is an interesting performance, in that Kate
is backed by an     all-star band: Phil Collins on drums, Gary Brooker on
keyboards, Midge     Ure and Peter Townsend on guitars and backing vocals, and
Mick Karn on     fretless bass. Kate's movements are severely limited by the
necessity     of holding the microphone in one hand; and during her performance
the     strap of her top breaks--a minor emergency which she covers with    
admirable professionalism, never missing a beat.
