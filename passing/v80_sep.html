IED:
  



    Lip-synch from the French TV programme, Champs-Elysees.    
Kate performs on a typically unmysterious and gaudily lit stage-set, dressed    
in an odd costume featuring "plus-fours" of some kind. At the end of  
  the performance a quaintly uniformed man with a monocle--perhaps Del    
Palmer?--rushes onstage, literally sweeps Kate off her feet, and carries her
off.

	lip-synch. "Babooshka" & "Army Dreamers"
(Mrs.Mopp)       



 

IED:
  



    Lip-synchs, from the German TV programme RockPop. The first of
these,      the "Mrs. Mopp" version, is unquestionably one of Kate's
greatest      performances to date. It features the simplest of props: Kate,
dressed as      an Irish housewife, is seen on the television soundstage
sweeping up with      a broom. She dances is accompanied by two men in uniform. 
In the Babooshka      segment Kate wears a red pants-suit and uses a double bass
as a prop.
