James and The Cold Gun

In ``james and the cold gun'' did you refer to anyone in particular?

I've had lots of letters about this, many from people called James, with plenty of suggestions for identities of the ``James", but the answer is: nobody. When I wrote the song, James was the right name for it. (1979, KBC 3)
