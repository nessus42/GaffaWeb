Night Scented Stock

That was `` the infant kiss", with ``night scented stock'' to follow...

Yes.

Which you particularly wanted us to play. Ah, it's almost classical, isn't it?

Yes, I think it is. That's probably why I'm fond of it because it's a new area. And I love music without words when it works because it suddenly becomes so much freer, it's like landscapes moving around you and I've always wanted to work in a musical area sometimes rather than always putting lyrics to my songs. (1980, Never For Ever Debut)
