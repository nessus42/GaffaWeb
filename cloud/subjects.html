Cloudbusting -- Kate Bush In Her Own Words

Part III: Other Subjects

Edition 2 - March 1, 1992

The sections contains all of the quotes from Kate that are not related to a specific song or time. It is ordered alphabetically and contains quotes relating to Kate's feelings on particular subjects (people, places, things, emotions, influences, etc.). However this is not a concordance or an index to the other sections. Obviously you should refer to other sections for more information on a subject, IE The Sensual World for more info on Sexuality/Sensuality. Obviously there is some overlap with quotes from sections I + II but I have tried to keep that down to a minimum and when it occurs I try to use different quotes which say the same thing.
