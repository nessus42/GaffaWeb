Cloudbusting -- Kate Bush In Her Own Words

Emotion

That's one of the main things I like about the music - the emotions running around.

I think everyone is emotional, and I think a lot of people are afraid of being so. They feel that it's vulnerable. Myself, I feel that it's the key to everything, and that the more you can find out about your emotions the better. Some of the things that come into your head can be a surprise when you're thinking. (1980, Zigzag)
