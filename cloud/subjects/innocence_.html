Innocence

You can retain your innocence throughout life, SHE BELIEVES, BLESS HER. It never really goes away. No, innocence has nothing to do with being childlike. No, I'm not a child-woman. No, I'm not reluctant to grow up. What is this world for if you can't always appreciate the innocence in life? (1989, You)
