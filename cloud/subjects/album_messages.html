Cloudbusting -- Kate Bush In Her Own Words

Album Messages

Do you know anything about the messages scratched on the smooth circle just before the centre of the records? Why are they there?

Yes, I do know something about these messages because I wrote them, and they are messages to go with the record. It's something that has been practised by several people. In fact, have a look through your albums, you'll probably find quite a few that you didn't even know were there. (1980, KBC 5)
