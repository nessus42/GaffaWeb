Cloudbusting -- Kate Bush In Her Own Words

Weirdness

I often find myself inspired by unusual, distorted, weird subjects, as opposed to things that are straightforward. It's a reflection of me, my liking for weirdness. (1980, Oct 10, Melody Maker)

I don't think I am eccentric as a person. When I get older I might be. Maybe my music is a little eccentric sometimes. (1982, NME)
