I'm not sure if I've every really had a fantasy life, not how it seems to me other people have fantasy lives. After I left school, I was working for what I considered to be a goal, which was to make an album. And I suppose that was my fantasy, and it happened. So I think my fantasies are very down to earth. (1984, L.A. Times)
