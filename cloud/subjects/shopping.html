Cloudbusting -- Kate Bush In Her Own Words

Shopping

Do you have a local store that you go to, or not?

Yeah, I do, for vegetables and things like that.

Do you always buy the same things, I find when I go to the store I always think ``hmmm, what should I get?'' well, I always end up getting exactly the same things every week.

Well, I try to get different things, but normally it's the same things, yes.

Do you get out to shop much on saturdays or not?

Not much, no.

You maybe stay in bed after working all week.

Yeah, I try to sleep as much as I can, normally. (1982, Saturday Superstore)
