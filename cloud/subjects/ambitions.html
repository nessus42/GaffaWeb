Cloudbusting -- Kate Bush In Her Own Words

Ambitions

Have you got any ambitions?

Ambitions. Oh, I've got so many. I want to do everything. [Laughs] Yeah.

What are the main ones, though.

Well, really all I want to keep doing is what I'm doing now and just try to get better because I've got a long way to go. I want to keep writing songs and singing and I'd like to keep training as a dancer cause that really makes me feel good. (1979, Personal Call)
