 Back to Phoenix 




    
    
First transcribed by Andrew B. Marvick (IED), later on improved and
    corrected by various love-hounds (e.g. Bryan Dongray, Emmy May Lombaerts,
    Violet... ). 

    
    
We should all try to use the Phoenix broadcast titles, because they
    seem to be correct. At least no inconsistencies have been found. IED's
    titles are given in brackets. 

    
    
Everybody is invited and encouraged to listen carefully again to the
    difficult passages. Some are almost inaudible. Present your ideas to
    Love-Hounds! I have numbered the lines to make the discussion easier. I have
    added some annotations e.g. differences between the demos and the album
    versions and different suggestions for the uncertain lines. What follows are
    the lyrics in succession as they appear on the Phoenix broadcast tape. 




    
    
ENJOY THE MUSIC!
