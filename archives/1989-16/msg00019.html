>Now who's Alan Stivell?

	Alan Stivell is a British (?) Celtic Harp player. He has four
albumns that I know of:

	1. The Renaissance of the Celtic Harp.
	2. Celtic Symphony.
	3. & 4. I can't quite remember because I don't own either of them.

	His first two albumns were fairly traditional, but I believe
his other two have a more "New Age" quality to them. In Britain,
his albumns are distributed by Rounder Records. In Australia, they
MIGHT be distributed by Larrikin Records.

	Personally, I do like his albumns. He has a very "soft" style
of harp-playing.

	I hope that helped.
