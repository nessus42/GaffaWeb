Boy, it's good to be back on the Net (I think).  Anyway, the question is:
How far would YOU go to see Kate in Koncert?  Well, I think the question
should be:  How much would you spend to see Kate?  After all, most everyone
here would say, "Yeah, I'ld go to Mars to see her!" but not many of us can
plunk down the cash for said journey.

My answer to my question:  If she was ONLY touring the UK, and I had AT 
LEAST a three month warning, I'ld cough up a couple thousand to see her
(This is including round trip airfare & a week or two lodging), because
after all, hey, spending 2 grand to see her includes a free round trip
to London!

If she was ONLY touring on the other coast, I'll spend up to, say $750.00
(Most of this being round trip expenses) - That would be at a moment's
notice.  With a month's notice, cut the cost in half (I'm a good shopper).

If she were playing the next town over, I don't think I'ld spend more than,
say, $75.00 to see her.

- Stev0 the economical
