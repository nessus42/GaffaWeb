"50 Years Of Sunshine" is a double-CD compilation released by San
Francisco's Silent Records to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the
discovery of LSD-25 by Swiss chemist Albert Hoffman.  It features lots
of great music, from ambient noise to 4ADish pop with female vocals.
The reason I'm promoting it is because it contains a track from my
band, The Ultraviolet Catastrophe.  So buy the damn thing already!
