saurav@tgevax.life.uiuc.edu (Saurav Misra) writes:
>Hey, throughout the periods of relentless self-prmotion, I never actually
>noticed the name od Drukman's band...The Ultraviolet Catastrophe! What a
>great name! Jon-boy, were you a physics major at one time?

No.  But one of my bandmates ex-roommates is a theoretical physicist
at SLAC, and we got the name indirectly from him.

So, here's a riddle for y'all: what do I have in common with Kate
Bush?  Answer: we're both gorgeous people who have had records in the
Melody Maker and NME charts!  Yes, unbelievable as it may seem, The
UVC's latest piece of vinyl has hit #5 in MM's "Stone Free" this week
- they call us the Grateful Dead of techno, which may or may not be a
good thing - you decide.  Check it out, anyway - April 17th issue.

I haven't seen the NME in question, so if any regular NME readers
spots us in the chart, I would LOVE it if you could send me a xerox of
the relevant page.  In fact, I'll send you a free copy of the UVC 12"
in exchange.
