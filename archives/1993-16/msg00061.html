and off for about a year and feel it is time to toss my 
hat into the ring.  I was quite surprised and excited to see

set your newsreader believability controls to "grain of salt"
and we'll get started!

*** Kate Koincidence or Strange Phenomena ***

Did you ever notice that the title track from Ozzy Ozbourne's
album _No_More_Tears_ has an interlude that sounds a lot like
the beginning of Kate's _Lord_of_the_Reedy_River_.  If that 
was the ONLY connection, I wouldn't bother to post...........
BUT THERE'S MORE!!  Twice during the song (in the middle and 
at the end?) there is a male voice-over that says something 
like "..[stuff deleted]...hand in the bush."  The words 
"in the bush" echo repeatedly and fade out.

What do YOU think?  Is this an honest-to-goodness Kate reference
or am I suspended in gaffa?  I welcome your thoughts and theories.
If this is old news, forgive me for rehashing, and please keep 
flames on low. :^)
