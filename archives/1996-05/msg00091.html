>>> the fourth (The Dreaming). In Tori's work, the piano still remains
>>> the leading instrument.
>>> That's why I think BFP is NOT Tori's "The Dreaming", as there's
>>> more continuity between UtP and BfP than between Kate's N4E and
>>> THD.
>>> Last thing: they should be both appreciated for what they are,
>>> without this kind of Tori vs Kate thing, or (this is for Kate's 
>>> fans), without rating Tori as an ersatz to help waiting for Kate's
>>> next record. Tori's not a Kate substitute !
