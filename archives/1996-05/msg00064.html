What does this mean?
This question arises every year or so and IED's canonical answer is always
that JB is John C. Bush, who is into martial arts. This is a good guess,
but yesterday I found this interview quote:

(from: The Dreaming Debut  Radio 2  Sept. 13, 1982)

I: ... And Colin [who called in] also says, "in Sat In Your Lap cover
   it's written 'well done J.B....'" and he wants to know who J.B. is. 
K: J.B. is a guy called John Barrett who certainly deserves to be
   congratulated 'cause he did something very clever. [Laughs] 
I: Very clever, you're not going to tell us anything further. No? 
K: Nope! 

Now, have a look at the SIYL sleeve: A John Barrett is listed under the
assistant engineers!

Best wishes
    Wieland, KaTe's archivist
