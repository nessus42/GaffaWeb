Last night (27-Jan-96 about 22:45 CST), MTV (ie eMpTy-V) showed the
entire video to "Love And Anger". HOORAY! Unfortunately I missed the
very beginning, didn't have my VCR ready. You know the one, ballet
dances, guys dressed in Turkish/Eygption outfits. Pity I still don't
have a copy of this (or the other Sensual World videos), human memory
is never the best media to store videos on! OK, a little background. I
was trying to get a new printer to work, stupid thing, I think the shop
sold me the wrong cable, anyway, my daughter (Emily) calls me upstairs
for the above reason.

Turns out it was on "Beavis & Butthead", so actually most of video had
those two giggling, saying inane (sp?) statements, and parts of the
video were missed when showing those two on their sofa.

OK, on one hand I admit B&B are total idiots, and I understand they
are watched (worshipped) by millions, but surely this is a great
advert for Kate, the full description was there too.

I understand this was a repeat, so perhaps everyone knows of this
apperance already, if so, that's ok, you now know it was on last night too.
