to the situation we are in: Kate Bush created a new style, some people
are simple plagiators (german Mandy Winter), others just use the technics  
KT developed, not only in singing but also in writing texts, composing and

of artists. And KT got her entry in encyclopedias (german Brockhaus), she  
has become a part of our common culture and  our history, as the Beatles,  
Longfellow, Nixon ( ;) ), Helmut Kohl ( ;-))) ), Pink Floyd, Michael  
Jackson.
And KT was one of the founders of the gothic-style/culture (esp. TNW).

Why should not someone other use this for his ideas? Some people do, and  
some of them do a good job.                 0
