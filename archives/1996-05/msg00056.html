Tori's style is often strongly reminiscient of Laura Nyro circa "New
York Tendaberry" and "Christmas and the Beads of Sweat"...especially
the solo piano/voice tracks. To my ears, Tori owes as much or more of a
stylistic debt to Laura Nyro and Joni Mitchell than to Kate Bush. 

It bugs me (in a small way) that performers like Tori are always
automatically compared to Kate Bush as if she invented the concept of
the female singer/songwriter/pianist. Kate was not the first woman in
pop/rock music to write, sing, and play the piano. Her approach to
doing so was/is incredibly innovative and original, but I can certainly
understand how Tori's music could have evolved without her ever hearing
"The Kick Inside".
