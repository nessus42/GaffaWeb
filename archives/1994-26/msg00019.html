George eh?  Well kids, anyone remember the mystery line in Moments of 
Pleasure?  George the Wipe?  Maybe we're onto something . . . some sort 
of in-family menstrual joke?

I hate to be a T-total you-know-what, but I have Alice in Wonderland 
memorized and there is no box in it, except a glass one containing a bite 
of cake, with the words "Eat Me" elegantly written on it.

There is a house, in which Alice eats the incorrect bit of mushroom and 
starts to grow all out of proportion, and gives Bill the Lizard a good 
sharp kick.  It would have been very easy to stuff Tori into a dollhouse 
for a photo.  I don't buy it.

Karen kln@crl.com  "Alice was getting very tired of sitting by the bank, 
and of having nothing to do."  When she found out that Kate Bush might 
have a new single coming out and she said "Yay!  I hope she has the time 
to add a new B-Side too!"
