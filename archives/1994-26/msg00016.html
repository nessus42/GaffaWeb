On Fri, 19 Aug 1994, Gerald E Hough wrote:

> I've been reading the numerous posts about the new set of commercials, and I
> (unlike a lot of people) really like them..  I acknowledge that the 
> kaleidoscope effect is a bit ostentatious, but I'm thrilled that KaTe did the
> backup track for it...  Also, it's nice to see a commercial that doesn't have
> people jumping off of monuments, power biking in the Amazon or rollerblading
> at high speeds through the city...  I dunno anyone that is so professionally
> recreative, but I do know people doing nifty things with multimedia-type
> advertising...  

I, too, am quite impressed that someone in corporate America has realized 
the talents of Kate and the power of her music.  I did like the music 
before I found out that Kate had written it, but I like it even more now 
and am impressed with the new ranges that she is experimenting with.  I 
hope that this means that more of her music will be heard in the fututre, 
both in TV ads and on radio.
