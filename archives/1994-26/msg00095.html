I heard lots of talk good and bad about the Fruitopia ads with Kate Bush's
new musical shorts recently, here's a list of the names of all the ads -

	1. "Nice"
	2. "Fighting Fruit"
	3. "Summer Solstice"
	4. "Soul"
	5. "What If"

"What If" played in movie theatres and is in 'widescreen'. They are all
produced by a company called "Click 3X" I believe.

All the music is different, with the "What If" ad being the longest one.
