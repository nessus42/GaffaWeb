Personally, I think the Fruitopia ads should be on the '95 tape tree.  
It's some of Kate's work that will probably be unavailable after this 
campaign ends and it wouldn't take up much space.  They could even be 
used to fill out the end of a tape when another segment would be too long 
for the bit of remaining tape.

Of course, if someone could convince me that she was responsible for the 
ads for Zima (rrr), Miller Light Iced (Aaaa!), or Mentos (Stop! Make it 
stop!) I wouldn't be so interested.  But we all know Kate would never do 
anything the heinous.

Or that Denny's-is-our-place late-night-talk-radio-host thing!  It makes 
me want to hurt people.
