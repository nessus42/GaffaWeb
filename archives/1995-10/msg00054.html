I just got back from a whirlwind tour of Portland, Oregon, 
Boston, and a surprise visit to Washington D.C.  First of all,
how do folks like rock stars DO it?  It took me all day just
to catch up...

Also, while visiting my home town of Northampton, Mass, I found
a Major Shrine of Katedom:

Main Street Records, STILL the coolest record store in the world.
They have several Kate Posters up, including a HUGE poster of
The Sensual World, SIGNED by our very Kateness herself!  No, it
was NOT for sale.

- Stev0 the blue devil
