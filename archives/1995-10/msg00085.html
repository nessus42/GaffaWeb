>Also, while visiting my home town of Northampton, Mass, I found

>Main Street Records, STILL the coolest record store in the world.

Been there once... great place.  Since this is in the NE, I thought
I'd add a great place for those in the same area.  In Waterbury
Connecticut, there's this little ship off of 84 on the main road
called Brass City Records.  I haven't been there in a few years,
but I assume it's still there.  The guy Walter is a MAJOR fan and
he owns the shop, so there's always a TON of KaTe stuff in stock.
If you can't find something KaTe-ish, he can find it!  I remember
that right after TSW was released, she (supposedly) gave him a
Happy Birthday call when in NY for the promotion...

Also, he doesn't dig you for cash, the stuff was very reasonably
priced!
