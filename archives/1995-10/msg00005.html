Here's a brief anecdote about Neil Gaiman-Kate Bush:  I was at
the World Fantasy Convention in New Orleans last October, which
was also attended by Neil Gaiman.  At one point I walked by
him wearing my Lovehounds shirt, and as I passed I saw him do a
doubletake and call out, "Look, Kate Bush!"  I can only assume
he must've been surprised to see Kate's face sported by a Yank.
