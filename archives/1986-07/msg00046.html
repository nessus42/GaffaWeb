	This Sunday's (4/20) New York Times Entertainment section contains
an article about Laurie Anderson's film "Home of the Brave" and it
is written by Laurie Anderson. It is very amusing. The woman must have
a great sense of humor. She describes the trials and tribulations of
getting funding for a artsy film (her brand of art is called performance
art, and this is what is in the film), and actually making the film. At
one point she's eating lunch with a friend and thinking to herself 'the
light is all wrong for this shot. Who wrote this???' thereby turning her
whole world into a film. Like I said, it is very entertaining.

Sharon Badian
