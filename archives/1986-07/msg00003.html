The Lion Sleeps Tonight

From: KNIGHT%MAINE.BITNET@WISCVM.WISC.EDU (Michael Knight)
Date: Wed, 16 Apr 1986 10:00 EST
Subject: The Lion Sleeps Tonight

One of my friends who purchased the boxed collection of Eno albums
has told me that it includes an otherwise un-released disk entitled
'Rarities' which includes 'The Lion Sleeps Tonight'. This boxed set
had, I believe, 8 or 9 albums by Eno and was priced accordingly. I
have no idea if this is still in print, but you could ask at your
local record store if you feel so inclined.  Might be a great way to
start an Eno collection, but would cause much duplication in a lot of
Eno fans collections. Of course those disks might make great Christmas
gifts for your friends....

It made my day a few weeks ago when the Impulse Dance Company from
Boston was up here at UMO, and heard them use 'Delius' from 'Never
for Ever' for a number.  She's EVERYWHERE!

-Mike
