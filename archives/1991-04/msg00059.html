Subject: Fan Club/Mailing List Addresses

   The Cleveland Freenet Music SIG of Ohio has been asked if there
are other mailing lists out there.  After careful examination of the
nature of the questions, we, too, are curious.

   We would like to compile a complete listing/collection of any and
ALL Fan Club/Mailing Lists, and make it available for public access
as well as providing the completed (and compiled) listing to all
USENET users interested (via a public posting in each section).

   We are asking everyone and anyone who knows of any e-mailing 
addresses to either reply publically here with your additions
of data, or feel free to send as much information as you can/have
to:

     xx158@cleveland.Freenet.Edu   (The Music Sig)
or   aa590@cleveland.Freenet.Edu   (Jason Kraley)

""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
Example of what we have so far:
     aon@polyslo.calpoly.edu - Art of Noise
     aon-request@polyslo.calpoly.edu - AoN's Request address
     yello@polyslo.calpoly.edu - Yello
     yello-request@polyslo.calpoly.edu - Yello's Request address
""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""""
   As you can see, we don't have many at all, and we ask you give us
all correct information, a short description of what the address is
for (obviously), and if there is a request address of some sort, please
include it with its sister-address.

   We, the Music SIG staff, thank all of you in advance for your
cooperation with this endeavor!
