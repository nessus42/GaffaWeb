out news connection has been real flakey lately so maybe this is old news,
but i just got a katestuff pricelist from c-side records (pob 8456,
clearwater, fl. 34618, 813-461-4327).  i bought my "it's xmas" from him
(tom richards) and he was very responsive to my crackling problems with it.
 in the list he says "a special thank you to chris and vickie in chicago
for their efforts in trying to get me in 'the love hounds' network..."

his inventory makes me green with desire, but some highlights:

he's got the japanese boxset for $172, and says it has an extra book but no
stickers.  what's the book, anybody?

he mentions a tsw promo _bathrobe and shower curtain_!!!  anybody seen
these?  ooooooooooohhhhh!

he claims he'll have the passing thru air cd soon for $26.

singles file for $200


also, i ran into bill from the record exchange on the train on my way home
last night, and he'd just acquired (from reckless) for $18 (!!) a limited
edition picture-disc interview cd in a larger box with several strange
photos of kate in front of some waxworks astronauts!?!?
