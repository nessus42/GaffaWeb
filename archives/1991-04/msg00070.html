        If you are into serious Alternative Music, Redd Kross is an 
interesting band.  Weird, but they have made some good stuff.
Depending on how much you like the Beatles, Jellyfish is a good band
they take their look from seventies pop music and have alot of harmonies 
and intricate songs.  Also, speaking of Female singers (nowhere near Kate)
there is Transvision Vamp("I Want Your Love") this girl ENJOYS whining.
        On the AOR side, Chris Isaacs, "Wicked Game" is becoming popular.
The Black Crowes are definitely headed for the top of the charts (for what 
it is worth.)  And there is rumblings of a new "Ultra-group" coming from one 
of the big three about a new AOR/Alternative crossover band that will be 
big someday soon.
