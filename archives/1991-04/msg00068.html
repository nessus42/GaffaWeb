Hello KB fans,

A few days ago I received a letter from a Dutch KB-fanclub. 
They are offering a videotape with some interesting pictures. 
The video doesn't contain the commercially available stuff, 
but only unique material.
Total running time of the tape is about three hours.
The price is Dfl80 (about US$50 without postage).
The tape is only available on VHS-PAL (so not on NTSC!).
The ordering time of the tape is a few weeks.
If there are Americans interested, then they might find someone
to transfer the PAL video to the NTSC system and distribute
it over there. However, I'm not sure whether this is legal
(low risks though I guess).

Also available is a tape recording of the CD's 'Passing Through
Air' and 'If You Could See Me Fly', containing all home demo 
recordings of the 1973-1976 period (a total of 23 songs).
The price of this tape is Dfl50 (about US$31 without postage). 

If you are interested in this material, than I will be able to
order the stuff and send it to you, but not after I received your
money. I have not seen/heard the tapes, so don't ask me about
the quality of them.
E-Mail me if you are interested in the tape(s) (shared account!).

You can also contact the selling fanclub yourself:
Theo Haast, Torenvalk 99, 4872 PS Etten-Leur, The Netherlands.

Greetings, Raymond Hoving, The Netherlands.
