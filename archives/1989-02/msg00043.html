I to am a Shriekback fan and would like some more info on them. As for the
questions asked earlier regarding Dave Allen... is he really gone? I know
he got some credit for helping the band do 'GO BANG!' but I really couldn't
tell if he was going to leave the band and that was his way of sneaking out.
I didn't think much about 'GO BANG!' but songs like "Shark Walk" and
"GO BANG!" were fun. I also enjoyed "Dust and a Shadow" which sounded like
the Shriekback I know and love. I was also wondering if there are two
versions of <<JAM SCIENCE>>. I have two friends who each have an album
(one from the states and one from Europe) of <<JAM SCIENCE>> and they say
the covers are different and some of the songs are different. Is 'All
Lined Up' on <<JAM SCIENCE>>?

Please send me info if anybody's got some.
