     Steve Raspail asked IED about the availability of the _Moving_
bootleg single. The only place IED has yet seen this single was at
a record swap meet that's held each month in Carson, California.
This is usually the best of the four swap meets in the L.A. area, as far
as IED knows. The record still hasn't shown up at any of the local
import shops, and IED only found the one copy at the swap meet, so
he doesn't really know how "available" it is. If he finds any more
copies, though, he'll let you know. He's always checking around
for this kind of thing, and can't believe it won't turn up
again relatively soon.
