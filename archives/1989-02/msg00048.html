I got the New Bohemians album around the end of January, and love it! It's
one of the best albums I've heard in a long time. I especially like the song
_She_. Voice of the Beehive I like, but have not bought, so I am limited to
what I hear on the radio (I Say Nothing, and The Beat Of Love). I think the
best new album in a year has been the new Siouxsie and the Banshees album,
Peepshow. Every time I listen to it it gets better. A very unusual thing.
