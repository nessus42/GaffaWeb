     Daniel Ouellette got almost all of it right. But the CD-single's
extended re-mix of Cloudbusting, called the Orgonon Mix, is
_not_ the same as the video's mix. The video is identical to the
album mix except for the 1-minute-twenty-second-long insert of
new music in the first interior scenes of the video (where Kate
sings "It's you and me, Daddy"). That part of the video mix is
not included anywhere in the Orgonon Mix. On the other hand,
the whole of the Orgonon Mix is quite different from the album version,
and is extremely neato. It appeared originally on the 12" single of
_Cloudbusting_ (both in the UK and in the US), but the CD-single sounds
much better.

-- Andy
