     Although I read the net whenever I get a chance, I have
never seen discussion of the group Shriekback.  I have always
found it difficult to find records (cd's) and information about
them.  So I hope someone on the net can give me some help.  I
don't know exactly where to post this, so it may be on two or
three newsgroups.
     Does anyone know the answer to any of the following
questions?

    1:  Where did Dave Allen go?  Was there a fight?  And what is
he doing now, making re-mixes for Depeche Mode? (one version of
Strangelove - according to the name on the record) 

    2:  Where can I find a copy of Tench - their first album?  I
have been looking for three years and nobody has ever even owned a
copy (but most have seen it)?

    3:  Are Jam Science or Care ever going to be released on CD? 
(I have the two compilation CD's on the Kaz label - but they don't
have all the tracks.)  Information on video compilations/concerts
would also be appreciated.

     4:  And MOST IMPORTANT! where can I get the lyrics for the
album Care.  Especially the songs Evaporation, Accretions, and
Lined up.  Does anybody have them.  I would give my arm (or any
other protrusion) for a copy.

     I hope someone can help. 

                                           Thank you,

                                                 Brian Matchick
