From: sco!stevies@ucscc.UCSC.EDU
> Since the last Dukes of Stratosphere album (Psionic Psunspot)
> came out only about two months after Skylarking, does this
> mean that we can (hopefully) expect a new Dukes album in the
> near future?

First of all, it took more than two months after Skylarking was released
for Psunspot to show up.

Secondly, in the articles in Little Express written soon after Psonic
Psunspot came out, Andy seemed fairly certain that the joke had totally
worn out its welcome, and there would be no more Dukes records.  (He
lied, didn't he?  _Oranges and Lemons_ is VERY Dukesish!)

Thirdly, there was a possibility that there would be a Dukes track recorded
for the Ray Davies tribute album, which has been shelved for the time being,
so that track will probably never get made now.
