Everyone's talking about their favorite Kate Live
performances, I might as well tell mine.  I never saw
her live in person, but I vividly remember her performance
doing the halftime show of the 1993 Superbowl.  I think
the highlight was when she did a duet with Adam Ant
of his song "Strip".  Although I have to admit I did
cringe when she did that duet with Michael Jackson
of "Don't Give Up", with her singing the part we
associate with Peter Gabrial and the Gloved One
singing the part we associate with her.

One question:  Who was that weird red-haired
woman who was playing the piano, humping the
piano stool?
