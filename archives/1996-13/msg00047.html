You're in for a treat on two counts -- you can find this domestically on
CD on:  "There is sweet music  English choral songs, 1890-1950"
performed by the Cambridge Singers under the direction of John Rutter
on Collegium CD COLCD 104

both CDNow! ($14.77) and CDConnection ($13.10) have this disc currently
listed; a trick - search by LABEL (Collegium) to find Cambridge Singers
discs, as they're scattered all over the on-line catalogs when you try
searching by performer/conductor/etc...

there's something like 20 discs listed by CDNow! and 27 by CDConnection
and I recommend them ALL ;)

good luck
