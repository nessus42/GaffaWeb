     I don't personally have a problem with the term 'Katemas'. Sure, if 
     you prounounce it 'Kayt-mass' it sounds a bit clunky and indeed 
     suggests something resembling the Michelin man. However, I prefer the 
     pronounciation 'KTmiss' ie kay-tee-miss (similar to the way people 
     pronounce Christmas 'chrismiss'). This has a nice ring to it don't you 
     think. Using a term that reflects her Irish roots, while interesting, 
     might not be appropriate. After all, she is more English than Irish 
     (by virtue of the Bush lineage and place of birth/upbringing). 
     Furthermore, her maternal lineage is only one of several major factors 
     that influence her music.
     
     So, who votes to keep kay-tee-miss?
