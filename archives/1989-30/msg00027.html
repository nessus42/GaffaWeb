      *Love-Hounds who are truly in the know will see that the set
which began with 28th Day and ended with "The Fog" is inter-connected:
Barbara Manning (of 28th Day) also recorded the solo song _about_
Brix of Adult Net; Brix is currently the girlfriend of the classical
violin virtuoso Nigel Kennedy; and Nigel Kennedy plays solos on Kate
Bush's "Experiment IV (extended version)" and "The Fog".*
