AIR MAIL to England is $0.42 per half oz. I'd 
send a postcard asking for a catalog. You can save big bucks having the CDs 
from England sent by surface mail, but it takes 4-8 weeks to get them. Each 
company has its own rules on postage, but with care on your part, the VAT 
discount should cover all postage. You may find that the min. postage 
covers more than one CD, and therefore you might as well transport all that 
you are paying to transport.

Recommended places with the most competative prices that I could find, and 
accepting Visa credit cards allowing you to not pay if you do not get the 
goods and allowing for virtually cost-free currancy exchange at current 
market rates at date of transaction posting: (Note that I have bought CDs 
from both and get catalog(ue)s from both.
