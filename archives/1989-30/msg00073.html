Some more 2to1s for you:

The Tear Garden - "Tired Eyes, Slowly Burning" (Nettwerk)
Contains the first self-titled EP and all of "Tired Eyes" as well.

Skinny Puppy - "Bites and Remission" (Nettwerk)
Contains the EP Remission and the LP Bites
There is another version of this disc called "Remission and Bites"
on Play It Again Sam (france) which has slightly different tracks,
since the running order of "Bites" was highly fluid...

XTC - English Settlement
Double album on one CD - the british (virgin) pressing loses
two songs "Leisure" and "Down In The Cockpit."
The AMerican (geffen) pressing restores them.
