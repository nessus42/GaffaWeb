I was struck by what Len Bullard said about not listening to Kate for 4 
years.  That seemed unusual for someone so active on the list over the last 
year.  That started me thinking (slowly I haven't had my coffee yet) about 
my own situation.  My favourite album for the last 20 years has been The 
Who's "Quadrophenia" (apologies to those Kate fans who are singular in their 
devotions) but I rarely listen to it.  I find it hard to generate the 
emotional energy required to listen to it  and it seems offensive to try and 
listen casually.  Has anyone had this type of reaction to something of 
Kate's?
