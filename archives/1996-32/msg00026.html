Here's another game we can play while waiting for the next tour...

I ran across this quote from Samuel Coleridge the other day:

  "He looked at his own Soul with a Telescope. What seemed all irregular, he 
   saw and shewed to be beautiful Constellations; and he added to the 
   Consciousness hidden worlds within worlds."

And immediately thought of Constellation of the Heart. And subsequently 
started trying to identify KaTe's possible literary influences. Here are the 
easy ones:
   Wuthering Heights (duh);
   The Sensual World, Love and Anger(chorus, at least) (Ulysses, James Joyce);
   Cloudbusting (A Book of Dreams, Peter Reich);
   Song of Solomon (Judaeo-Christian duh).

Any additions?
