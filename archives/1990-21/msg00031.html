wonder! Dave Gilmour (we all know about Gilmour finding KT etc.) can

 Did Kate preform at this concert? And if not why? I have been at Kate

to preform at something like this whit someone like Gilmour who is
most likely a friend (Love and Anger). This concert was 2 years in the
planing, seems like plenty of time to work it out...
