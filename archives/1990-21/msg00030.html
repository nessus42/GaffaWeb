Vickie here.

I talked to Peter Fitzgerald-Morris Saturday and there is NO new info
about either the convention or the Box Set.

Peter said that it would be at least two weeks before a venue and date
is set and when he knows, he'll call me. When I know, you'll know.
Love-Hounds won't have to worry about getting tickets. Even though they'll
be first come-first serve, all Hounds who want to go are guaranteed a
ticket. Once the date is set and plans are made by those of you who can
go, give your name to me and I'll give a master list to Peter. Your names
will be at the door. You'll still have to pay of course (I'll let you know
how much) and you may need to send your money in advance (I'll let you know
that too), but no matter what, you WILL get in, without having to wait for
a ticket to be sent to you in the mail.

That's all I know for now. I'll keep you posted whenever I hear more.
