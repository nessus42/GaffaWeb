    The current issue (#4) of _Fringe Ware Review_ features an interview
of Alaura P'Orridge of Throbbing Gristle and Psychic TV by Tiffany Lee Brown.

Tiffany writes, in the intro "Last year, we sat down one evening,
put on a Kate Bush CD, and talked about women in the age of men and
machines."

Other Kate mention: Alaura writes "I've met so many people, ranging from
Kate Bush to Timothy Leary to William Burroughs, and lots of people
in the music industry." 

    This is the sort of small-press magazine that you can probably find
at most big bookstores. I found it at Borders in Kansas City, Mo.
