Hi all,

I picked up Alan Stivell's album, AGAIN, which features Kate singing background 
vocals and playing keybaords on track 10, KIMIAD.  I think its a small part, but
the vocals are unquestionably Kate. Del Palmer is credited as the recording 
engineer on the track and Kate is credited as the producer.  John Giblin, 
Charlie Morgan, and Alan Murphy also play on the track. 

Overall, I think this is an enjoyable album, particularly for fans of Celtic or 
New Age music.  In fact I would describe Stivell's songs as spacy Celtic.  
Stivell plays harps and pipes and assorted other instruments while others 
proivde the other instrumentation, as on Kate's track. Shane McGowan, late of 
the Pogues sings on a few of them.  Most of the lyrics are in Gaelic.   

The album is a French import on the Disques Dreyfus label and is currently 
available at Tower Records in the San Francisco Bay area, at least.
