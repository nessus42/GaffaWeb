Peter Chow from Brighton (UK) writes:

{News just out from the KBC here is that 'The Man I Love'
is indeed to be the first single that will be released
from the Gershwin album.
The single will be released in the UK and Europe on 
18th July, with the album to follow on the 25th.

It is hoped that the US releases will follow later in the
Autumn.

Kate and Larry have made a video to go with the single.

End of newsflash...
