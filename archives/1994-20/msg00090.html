	It's amazing how easy it is to make stupid little errors in a project 
as complicated as "The Lovehounds Collection" tapetree.  Two errors that I made 
in the tracklist materials that accompany the tapes are particularly galling.

	First, I dated the broadcast of the b&w video for "Rocket Man" in 1992, 
whereas in fact it was the BPI Awards *for* 1992, broadcast in February, 1993.  
Chris's title on the tape itself is correct.

	Second, I misspelled Annie Lennox as Lenox.

	I make these confessions as part of a general invitation:  as people 
discover errors and omissions in the printed materials for the tapetree, feel 
free to email them to me directly for an ERRATA list that I can post in a few 
weeks.  I'm hoping that it's all little things like these two, but you never 
know....
