Celebrate Kate!

Where:  625 Ashbury St, between Haight and Waller
Entry Code:  "#16" rings apt. 4
When: August 6, 1994, Saturday  Noon onwards

We will have food and drink, chocolate!, door prizes, displays of rare

everyone's stuff a display), videos, and {the best music in the world}

welcome to bring stuff to sell/trade.

this time of year, so bring a jacket or sweater unless we're having a 
heat wave.

If you'd like invitations to give to your Kate Kontacts who aren't 
online, please e-mail me.
