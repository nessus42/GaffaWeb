> I'd love to be the first person in San Francisco to have a shirt from

Karen, my feeling is this: the purpose of this effort is not to create
a new ad t-shirt for KaTe, but to create a t-shirt that uniquely 
represents this discussion group.  That's why I'm going to some 
personal trouble to build a consensus on the design of the shirt.

When artwork from TRS becomes available, it can surely be nominated,
but I'm hoping to have reached a verdict on the design before October. :)
