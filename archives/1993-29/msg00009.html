> What's HR-5?

It's a promo release by Happy Rhodes, sent to radio stations all over
the USA, containing "Feed the Fire" (album version), "I Say" (album
version), "Ashes To Ashes" (Bowie cover, acoustic), "Save Our Souls"
(acoustic version) and "Feed the Fire" (acoustic version, which includes
Happy singing Kate's line "if I only could, I'd make a deal with god and
I'd get him to swap our places"). These 5 songs will soon be officially
released on an upcoming compilation called _Rhodes Songs_ (also containing
11 other tracks from previous Happy albums), which is due in the stores
within a couple of weeks.

Mike was just kidding, I'm sure. WXRT's copies have probably already hit
the used record stores in Chicago :-(.
