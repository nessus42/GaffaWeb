Tori Amos has one album (Little Earthquakes), one EP I don't know much about
(Y Cant Tori Reed, or some such), and four singles (Crucify, Winter, Silent All
These Years, and China).  Also there are a plethera of single types and releases.
I saw a list once, and it showed about four or five different releases of each
single, all with different tracks (e.g., Crucify US, Crucify UK, Crucify 
Collector's EP, etc.).  I've even seen the Tori Amos Little Earthquakes Sheet
Music book (I think it was at Tower, or someplace like it).
