the computer into the realm of the singer/songwriter in much the
same way that Ingrid Karlins or Kate Bush have.  Innumerable albums
have brought her cult fame on the Internet but little popular
recognition.  A good intro to her work is <Rhodes Songs> {Aural 
Gratification, P.O. Box 380, Bearsville, NY 12409/914-679-4728},
a mix of production tracks from her recent albums "Equipoise"
and "Warpaint" and some acoustic remakes of her songs, giving an

composer." (CMJ 8/16/93 New World Music Chart pg 20)...
