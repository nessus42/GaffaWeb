The booklet (I should say book) that comes with LaH is different, it is album-
sized and has 48 pages of full-page pics and text. A lot of the pictures are
"previously unpublished", or so it says on the box cover, and are earlier
publicity shots and the like (some really good stuff). The same goes for the
Never For Ever box set. I bought them both because I am a truly devout
follower of Ms. Bush, and they are worth the ($80) price just for the photo
books. However, it should be noted that the CDs that come in the sets
($80 for 1 CD!) are not limted edition or anything, as far as I can tell. But
you do get a free T-shirt and postcard...
