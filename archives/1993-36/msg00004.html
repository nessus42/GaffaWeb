Hey, Kate-o-philes:

I'm new and just have to say I have never seen a more fanatic group
anywhere. It's kinda cool and kinda scary.

For me, I'll just say that Kate is among a handful of performers whose work
becomes more intense with repeated listenings. (Somehow, I suspect this
sentiment has been expressed in this group *several* hundred times.) The
music has depth, complexity, freshness, and mystery - and then there's the
Kate voice. 

Mainly I like the challenge. She's not afraid to layer and suggest. And I
love dense production. And then, there's the Kate voice.
