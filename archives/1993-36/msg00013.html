Our cable system let us have The Disney Channel for free over the weekend,
whereon I saw a rather good documentary on Fleetwood Mac (voices from my
childhood, okay?  Sheesh...).  At one point they showed part of a video for
the song "Gypsy" which featured Stevie Nicks walk/dancing through a forest
in the moonlight- disconcertingly like Kate's moving through the moonlit
trees in the "TSW" video.  In fact, it was very much like it.  Like, lots.

Coincidence?  Bizarre similarity?  The world may never know.

(Kate looked better in her dress, anyway. ;>)
