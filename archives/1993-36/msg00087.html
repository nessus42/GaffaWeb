Will TRS be release this year?

From: jim@medinah.atc.ucarb.com
Date: Mon, 27 Sep 1993 14:02:56 -0500
Subject: Will TRS be release this year?
To: Love-Hounds@uunet.UU.NET

The delays of releasing the Red Shoes is getting to me. My local record
store owner told me that Columbia records told him that his order for TRS
would be cancelled for this year and that the album would not be released
until next year. 

I would rather believe the November release dates, but in any case this
release seems to be very disorganized and I'm afraid that might hurt its
promotion here in the US. Certainly, a release after christmas is going to
miss out on the gift buying shoppers.

Personally, I would like to hear more of the new album. It would be nice if
someone who has a pre-release of it would create some sound file of some of
the other songs or maybe even a sampler tape. I also have not seen any of
the videos being shown and wonder if it might be possible to do another
tape tree.
