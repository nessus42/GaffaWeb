I noticed the other day that if you hold down the CD-Fast Forward button,
which on my player will play short little samples from every 1/2
second or so, while listening to "Candle in the Wind", you'll end up
with something that sounds remarkably like one of the weird vocal
effects off HoL.  This is probably worth mixing with *something*, I
just haven't figured out what, yet.

Reminds me of one of the KaTemas celebrations I went to where Greg
(footah!) had set up a synthesizer with various KaTe samples assigned
to various keys.  One could certainly play some very interesting
songs... :-)
