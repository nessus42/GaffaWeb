are the same as those at the beginning of the sensual world?  This has 
probably already been brought up, but I can always hope to be the first to
point this out.  

By the way, I'm getting hooked (expensively).  I originally started about a
year or two ago with the sensual world.  Then came the whole story.  Next, the
dreaming.  Today I finally got the hounds of love.  I guess you could say I'm
working my way backwards.  And I'm falling in love with hounds.  The ninth

recommendations for B-sides and if I should continue this backward trend.
