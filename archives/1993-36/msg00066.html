Other news:

*  Live 105 (KITS, 105.3, San Francisco) plays the top-three most
   requested songs on Fridays at 8.  Last Friday, Sept. 17, the
   number one requested song for the previous week was none other
   than . . . Eat The Music

*  Also, Rasputin Records on Telegraph in Berkeley had a pile of
   promos for The Red Shoes free for the taking, so I snagged one.
   It's on nice glossy paper, with a full-color picture of ruby
   ballerina shoes on one side, and the following text on the other
   side:

KATE BUSH
"THE RED SHOES"

Over three years in the making
an album of immeasurable depth...
of love and loving...
of incredible beauty...

(small photo of Kate with a male dancer)

Featuring:
_Eat The Music_
_Rubberband Girl_
_And So Is Love_

In Stores-October 5, 1993

Written and Produced by Kate

COLUMBIA


Rasputin staff had not been informed of any delay as of last week.
Something tells me I'm not going to be able to buy it on October
5th, however...
