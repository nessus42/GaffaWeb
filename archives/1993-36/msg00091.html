Hi all!

    I just got a very nice call from EMI Sydney to let me know about what's
happening with The Red Shoes release in Australia. Therasa had this to say...

1 o The Red Shoes will be released in Australia on the 1st of November
2 o The second CD single release here will be Moments of Pleasure
3 o There will be no Australian release of Eat the Music
4 o The limited edition releases (both UK and US) will not be released here!
:-(
5 o They have nothing on the video and say that it'll be sometime next year
    before they know anything.
6 o Rubberband Girl has already been released to local radio, so if you want to
    hear it played then ring them up and hassle for it!
