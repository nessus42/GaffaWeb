	My two cents.

	Notice in the Eat The Music CD, that the lyrics to Big Stipey Lie
were left out. Notice the Grungey Guitar. (Grunge, guitar, Grunge!).

	I smell a Prince. I smell copyright agreements. I think someone
is testing the water with the single before he puts his name on it. 
Prince didn't put his name on Manic Monday until after it became a 
hit. The strings, the pipe organ in the background, the grungey guitar.
(no real better word for it outside the recording suite..)
its got to be his Royal Badness. Rude Boy, etc. 

	I don't know if I like the borderline pop that I am hearing. Its 
different, so I won't pass judgement on it yet. Makes me think. I think
I am going to go downstairs and jump on the Akias for a while.. ciao!
