Hi all.  Someone mentioned a while back that there was an ad in Mondo for 
TRS.  There is also one in the Dec. Wired on page 16.  It is a vertical 
half page next to the masthead, with the album cover in full color, and a 
background of red-screened fruit.  There is a mention of the first two US 
singles, and the ad quotes the line from the song TRS "This curve is your 
smile, etc."  and goes on to say "See The Red Shoes come to life . . . 
Look for Kate's forthcoming film." No dates are given - though they have 
done a set-up for the movie title by quoting from the song.

And of course, Kate didn't FLY to the U.S., she was carried by The Ninth 
Wave -- "and all the wave was in a flame . . ."
