I want to personally thank Vicky for her messages concerning HR.
I first saw mention of her on this list some 2 years ago.  It wasn't 
until early this year that Tower Records in Campbell, CA. began to 
carry Happy's CD's.  I bought two and was pleased by them.  The only
complaint I had was the use of so much synthesizer in her music.
But I could appreciate her great voice and interesting writing.
Now with the release of Rhodesongs wich has much more acoustic 
instrumentation, and better recording/mastering, I am truly
in love.  What range she has! 
When I first got the CD, I think I listened to it every
day for 2 weeks straight!  I've turned my uncle (an avid Kate fan)
on to Happy as well.  Spread the word.  Maybe if she sells well
enough she will start turing.  Or, maybe she has already?  I'll
have to see if I can get on the ecto list.  



Thanks much Vicky!
