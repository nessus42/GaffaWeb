right now, they had a Top 10 list of artists who sounded like "naughty
parts". Anyway, at Number 8 was our very own, Kate Bush. How lovely, huh?

Bigg, Ce Ce Peniston, and (#1) Toad the Wet Sprocket. There's some (not-so)
great info. for ya! While mentioning Kate Bush, they played an excerpt from
the beginning of the EtM video. See ya!
