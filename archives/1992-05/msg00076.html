>Ummm, you know, that posting about the bootlegs bought in Boston

>(as I'm sorta inclined to believe), were the publishers ever caught or

 This will most likely be well covered by the time this post makes it
up but here goes anyway...
 
 The title of this double album bootleg is "Dreamtime" on the
"PHARTING PHAROAH" (I kid you not :) label.  "USA 13156" also appears
on the spine of the album jacket.  On the back of the album appears
the following:
 
    "KATE BUSH - DREAMTIME RECORDED LIVE AT THE PALLADIUM 1979."
 
Then song titles and photos.
 
  As far as I can tell this is a bootlegged "Live At The Hammersmith"
performance that is unedited or at least edited differently from the
official "LATH" video realise (what I'm trying to say is that it's not
just a soundtrack from the video:).  My understanding is that the name
of the lovely young lady on the back cover with her hooters showing is
model from England who called herself "Kate Simms" (sp) Indeed _not_
MS Bush.  The sound quality sucks 'kinda bad however this boot has one
thing going for it.  The photo of KaTe on the front cover is stunning!
Anyone with more info or who can correct any mistakes in the above
please jump in...
