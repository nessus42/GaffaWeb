Just got a new interview picture-CD: Words about Music WAM05. Limited
ed (500) comes in a cardboard box with colour photo on front, two
halftone photos inside. Two interviews, both late 1985, approx 15mins
each. I think they're both fairly well-known - most of what she says
sounds familiar. She doesn't sound particularly cheeful on the first
one (neither does the interviewer) but the second is a bit better.

On the subject of the possibilty of future tours:

    "but yes, I'd like to get the chance to do one before I'm old and grey"

At this rate, she'd better start stocking up on hair dye... 8^)

Oh, and the interview tapes have been processed by a "Phase Three
Technicmaster" - no idea what this is but it doesn't seem to make them
sound much better....
