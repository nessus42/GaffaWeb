        WOW!  I just got a package from Tom Richards of C-Side which 
includes the new book "The Illustrated Collector's Guide to Kate Bush" and 
two new 1978 interviews.  
        The book itself is, to me, absolutely indespensible, perhaps the 
most "fun" of all the books.  I don't collect rare records or anything, but 
found it very interesting to actually see all of them.  This book has 
entries and photographs for the Albums, CDs, CD singles and EPs, Vinyl 
Singles, Cassette Singles, Mini-Lps and 12" singles, Promotional Items, 
Interview Discs, Bootlegs, Videos, a list of Television appearances (not 
nearly as good as THE GARDEN'S and unlike the other entries, no pictures), 
Guest appearances, Books, Fanzines, and the tour programmes.  Remember all 
of these entries have B and W pictures, which is nice if, like me, you have 
much of this stuff, but in copies without the pics.  Highly recommended.

        More important, the two interviews I got are really incredible, both 
from 1978, the hardest time to get Kate interviews from.  These are both, 
needless to say, extremely rare and highly recommended reading.  The Trouser 
Press interview contains the most detailed talk Kate has ever done about the 
early demos, the Lionheart Promo interview contains much "new" insight on 
that album, maybe the best interview of all on it.  Thanks again to Tom for 
providing me with these.
