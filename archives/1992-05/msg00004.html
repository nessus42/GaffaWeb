  Well, just to lay any fears to rest there is an ad in the Jan issue of 
Record Collector (p 53) for limited edition lithographs of Kate by "...the 
American artist --- Hannah." Two are represented and seem to be the only two 
available. They are: 1. Kate from the Sensual World single 2. Kate from what 
looks like Wuthering Heights. Payment for these items seems to be to someone 
named K. Bollon but orders go to FBC Records in Surrey. Well, I guess it's  
in the publics hands now, make what you want of all that. I too am assuming 
that this is the same artist on the limited edition RM single, but then 
again I haven't seen that yet.
                                               --- Lionheart
