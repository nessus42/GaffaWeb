Subject: Putting in my 2 cents worth...


Is there anybody out there that likes music that is a bit more mainstream?
I've noticed a general tendency of people to talk about groups that I
would guess are "punk" or "new wave" (labels used very hesitantly).

I'm not saying that I don't like "that kind" of music, just that there's 
other stuff I'm at least more familiar with, and definately more
comfortable with.  Note though, that I can say I've gone from Billie Holiday
to Velvet Underground without any troubles...

And on radio stations...

One of the best I've ever heard was WXRT in Chicago.  It had(has) a college/
alternative feel to it, but is in it for the money.  That may have
changed...I haven't heard them for about 3 years...and I didn't realize
what I was missing when I could listen to them...

					Music, all I need is music...

						David
