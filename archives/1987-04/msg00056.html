was to be broadcast on the BBC or Channel 4. Would an English L-H 
please post a review if s/he has seen it? Thanks.

Thanks to all who responded to my noise about "Dear God". Does anybody
know if and when the XTC record with the addition of the song is to be
released? Is this record on CD? I cannot find it in that format around New York.

But beware, I have a feeling this is could go Top-40 in minutes.

To the 10,000 Maniacs fan: I asked them why John left and the
response was that he did not think they were playing as well live as he
would have liked and that they needed to be more "professional". Well,
whatever that means, they are still a good group.

Any Big Iron fans out there?
