Another breakthrough for Kate!  While watching WGN-TV (ch 9 here in Chicago)
I was roused from my torpor by the dulcet sounds of KT.  This week (2/24) they
are running ''Time Travel Week'' on the late movie, and are using ''Watching
You Without Me'' as the background music for the promo spot.
Yes, the same WGN that used Van Halen's ''Jump''
for the Cubs broadcast theme music, and until last year
used something from Roger Whittaker as their signature theme.

It's only a matter of time now. NBC Nightly News to the tune of ''Sat In 
Your Lap''... ''Hounds of Love'' in the elevator ... and ''Experiment IV''
on the organ at Wrigley Field.
