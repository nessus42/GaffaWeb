In fact there is at least one other CD that might be of interest to
Love-Hounds, Roy Harper's _Once_.

The order numbers are:

	Caterwaul    Portent Hue    13030
	Roy Harper   Once           1307

You can order these at 1-800-336-2IRS if you have VISA or Master Card.

You can also mail an order to:

IRS Merchandising 
3939 Lankershim Blvd.
Universal City, CA.  91604

The CDs are $5 each, add $3.75 P&H for the first item, $.75 for each
additional item.  California residents add 8.25% sales tax.  You may
want to reference their Spring '93 Merchandising catalog.

By the time you add P&H it's not exactly a huge bargain, but if you
can't find these anywhere else it's well worth it.
