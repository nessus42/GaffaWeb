hello, all.

i am a kate bush fanatic from the u.s.
i am basically OBSSESSED with everything about her.
i dream of spinning on scottish moors with her while singing "wuthering
heights" or just sitting down with her and chatting over a cup of 
coffee ("homeground", of course :) ).

anyhow, for as long as i have been freaked out on kate, a question has been
nagging at the back of my mind which NONE of the books, fanzines, interviews,
etc. have helped with.
it's time to introduce it to "rec.music.gaffa." (although i am new here and it
may have been discussed before... )

is kate bisexual? i have various conflicting thoughts on it in my head. it
is obvious that she is heterosexual in many ways. she is definitely NOT
a lesbian, at least i think. however, i have pondered the issue of whether
kate was bi for some time and i was wondering if anyone else had some
input. if not, keep on "breathing..." (out, in, out, in...)

suspended in confused kate gaffa, brian.
