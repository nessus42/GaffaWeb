	Okay, this is my first post here, and I don't really 
want to be responsible for yet another slew of New album release

fantasies, but...

	Yesterday's Pittsburgh Post Gazette provided a preview of
expected upcoming releases for the next few months.  Among the
list of names was Kate, listed for September release.  Being a
regular reader of the PPG, I'm used to minor, or otherwise, 
distortions or over-emphatic representations of the truth.
And, I figure that the writer of this article was just looking
at a list himself, in this case, probably little more than 
projected and hoped for releases.  I don't think this little
line in a nondescript article is worth getting too excited about,
but it does make me want to keep my eyes open.

	Has anyone heard anything to verify a September release
of a new Kate album?  Or is the PPG simply, in time-honored 
tradition, reporting upon old, and previously disproved,
news?

	Mr. Ed
