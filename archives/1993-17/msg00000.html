"Scott T. Lillis" <sl4q+@andrew.cmu.edu> writes:

>I saw a box set titled 'This Woman's Work' (so it must be somewhat
>recent) with that's a chinese import.  It had 8cd's(6 of which were
>titled with chinese writing and the other two were called Volume I &
>II).  It was $160 roughly.  Anyone know about
>this(material,time-span,sound quality, etc.)?

Chinese?  As far as I know, EMI manufactured this item in Japan, Germany,
Great Britain and Canada, with the Japanese set being the nicest of them all.
It had a book of pictures, whereas the others had postcard or stickers.  Its
her 6 studio releases, plus 2 disks of material from ep's, singles, etc.  
Check out the FAQ for more information.  The sounds quality on my set, the
japanese set is fantastic, and its a nice looking box set.  I picked mine up
for $150, which is about average in price.
