I've finally got round to making my KaTe album GIF image collection
available by anonymous FTP from ftp.epcc.ed.ac.uk [129.215.56.29] in
the directory pub/local/images/gif/kate. Most of these I scanned
myself from CD covers on an HP ScanJet Plus scanner a couple of years
ago.

In directory pub/local/images/gif/kate/greyscale are 16-level
greyscale GIFs of the covers of Lionheart, The Dreaming, HoL and TWS,
plus the Cloudbuster on the back of TWS and the Ninth Wave portrait on
the back of HoL. Most were scanned at 150 or 300 dpi and are anywhere
between 600 and 900 pixels wide/high.

In pub/local/images/gif/kate/backgrounds there are 1-bit dithered GIFs
enlarged and cropped to 1152x900 or thereabouts (just right for
desktop backgrounds on Sun workstations 8-) of the above Lionheart,
Ninth Wave and TWS GIFs, plus the pink leotard shot from the TWS CD
booklet, the "three Kates" superimposed shot from the TWW boxset
booklet (these two were derived from colour GIFs from the L-H archive,
can't remember who uploaded them) and the photo in the Feb 93 Rock
World magazine uploaded to the archive by Uli a while back.

There are also READMEs with more info.
