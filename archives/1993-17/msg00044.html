Sorry, but I don't think that qualifies the LP as a first pressing or 

anything else, since all of my 7 different versions (UK ltd. ed. pic disc, 

German (with & without 'Including...' pseudo sticker), USA, Canada, Japan, 

German 'fame' reprint) of the TKI LP have the lyrics on the outside cover.
They have different front pics, but the back is always the same with 

the lyrics.

Something else are CDs: None of my 5 different versions (UK, USA, UK-TWW,
J-TWW, UK-triple-CD-pack (with TKI, LH, and NfE)) has the lyrics on the
outside, the back is even different on them. The back of the booklet
of the US version is different from the back of the US CD, the others
are more or less identical on the back of the booklet and the back of
the CD.

(No, I'm not crazy...)
