The Butterfly Kisses bootleg CD has fairly good quality as far as boots
go - can't touch the real stuff, of course; you'll know it was recorded
on someone's Walkman.  It has mostly TV/tour stuff; a few songs are not
on official releases (Do Bears, The Long & Winding Road, She's Leaving
Home (ooh, I *love* SLH! It's very short; somewhere between Another Day
and Never Be Mine in sentiment. Gets to me.))

The cover has a somewhat unflattering picture of Kate in a bat (vampire?)
outfit, as on the back of Never for Ever.  (Naah, it can't be a vampire,
everybody knows *real* vampires don't have wings; that would be silly.)
