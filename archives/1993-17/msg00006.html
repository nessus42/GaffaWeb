1) If anyone is interested in getting The Garden and Cloudbusting files
  but have no access to ftp, email me for the seven 3.5" 2HD DOS discs.
  There is no cost but you are responsible for mailing it to the next person
  who wants them.

2) I have an interview of Jane Siberry done in January.  I'll send it to
  the JS mailing list, but woj thinks it may not be working.  If it isn't,
  I'll post it here.  The synopsis is: Brian Eno produce some tracks from
  her new album (someone on Ecto sez it'll be out in June); and
  after Jane was booed off the stage by Scottish fans when she opened 
  for Mike Oldfield's Tubular Bells, her outlook on making music 
  was completely changed...
