Dear Listusers,



First of all, please apologize my behavior with that silly "Birthday

surprise"-letter. It was really not one of my best ideas. Some users

wrote that there could be really much traffic on my friend's account,

even more than even I'd be expecting. The trouble is: I don't want to

be trown out of any list, and after I reflected on the letters I got,

I decided to write to this list in order to withdraw my "surprise".



Please, do not write any letter to my friend's account, not at last

because our server is not the strongest, he might crash if the capacity

is exceeded ... and my sysadmin will not be very pleased in that case !



Thank you very much for NOT (!!!!) writing, and I hope you accept

my apology (I will definitely NOT do so again !),



Yours,



Christian von Toerne
