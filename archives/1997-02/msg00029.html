Speaking of dreams...

About a month before _The_Sensual_World_ was released, I
dreamed Kate was touring America, and I was only, like, 3
rows back in the theater.  BUT, she's wasn't "performing",
she was standing at a podium at the base of a movie screen,
answering questions.  Then she presented her "film":

It was in a forest, late fall or early winter.  Kate runs
through the frame and we follow her.  She is wearing clothes
made from pieces of fur, kinda like from a depiction of
Cro-Magnons.  As we follow her flight we see another figure
pursuing her.  It is a man dressed similar to her.  She
dodges around trees and over the rough terrain, but the man
is gaining on her.  Suddenly, she has a mis-step and the man
overtakes her.  They struggle somewhat ferociously (I seem
to recall them swinging big clubs like something out of The
Flintstones) before he throws her to the ground and jumps on
top of her.

I wake up.

There!  You know everything.  Are you happy now?!
