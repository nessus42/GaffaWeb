      Staggered by emotion, toppled by illusion
        Freely I submit to her world of sound
        Swayed by devotion, lost in confusion
     Now my heart has gone to the lost and found

          Running up a hill, flying on a kite
      Sitting in a warm room, warm and soothing
       Blow away for Bill, Swallow in the night
        Passing through air that's moving, moving

           DreamBird sing your song of love

             Every time I hear your voice
          My existence just stands still
            Even then the simplest choice
         Becomes an act of strength and will

           DreamBird sing your song of life
