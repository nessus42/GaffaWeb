Natalie recounts:

>a couple of weeks ago i bought The Single File" video at Tower
>Records at 4th street & broadway in the village for $17.99. They had
>about 10 more copies of it.  By the way, the person who served me at
>the register made fun of KaTe, the swine.  Don't worry, I yelled at
>the moron ;-)

In all fairness to the Greenwich Village branch of Tower Records, I have to
point out that I've bought many Kate items there...and each purchase was
greeted with enthusiasm which bordered on outright gushing by the staff at
the register.  "Wow, you like Kate Bush?  Are you a fan?  Gosh!  I just
heard she has a new album coming out soon!" and suchlike.  And in fact, on
one occasion she actually *did* have a new album coming out...

Perhaps Natalie has been the victim of the proverbial "disgruntled
employee," frustrated by the constant Kate-boosting of his coworkers and
lashing out at the first available target?
