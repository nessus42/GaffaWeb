I know this is just barely related to anything Kate, but have you folks 
heard the new Donovan album "Sutra"?  It is pure Donovan - the 
distinctive talky-singing, the simple harmonies, the mystical lyrics, 
etc., and it is quite good!  If you are a fan of Donovan's old stuff, you 
will also like this one.  The album is very spiritual and meditative, and 
well produced and recorded.  And you just KNOW that Kate herself has a 
copy, right?
