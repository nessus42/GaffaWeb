In addition to the 3 Tori Amos CD singles released in the UK listed by Richard,
there is also a limited edition CD digipak version of "Winter". The tracks on
this are:
 
 1. Winter  2. Angie  3. Smells Like Teen Spirit  4. Thank You

Tracks 2-4 are piano/vocal covers of other people's songs - the Stones, Nirvana
and Led Zep respectively.

Thus, in all Tori has already released 11 non-album tracks.
