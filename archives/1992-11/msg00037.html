 Not to start things flying but.....
I like Kate's speakign voice, especially when she talks in those funny deep 
voices to illustrate a point, or that "snooty-rich" voice she also uses at 
times....well, no one said I had good taste, case in point I am a Kate Bush 
fan. Oh wait, I just remembered an incident at work you all might enjoy.
I put on The Sensual World, then suddenly a woman in the back of the store 
perked up, looked about and headed towards used records section "B." A few 
minutes later she came up to the register with a copy of Hounds of Love and 
smiled at me. That's all folks.....
