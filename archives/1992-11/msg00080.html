In response to Ron Hill's (at least I think it was Ron Hill's) question
about the picture of the KT Bush Band in "Q" Magazine: the picture is actually
in  a photo supplement called "Beginnings" or something like that, that
came free with the magazine. It's a collection of pictures of famous
musicians before they were really famous, e.g. Eric Clapton in his first
band The Roosters. The picture of the KT Bush Band was taken during a
pub date - shows Kate Bush in a long halter-necked dress, looking a lot
like she does in the publicity photos from The Kick Inside.
You can order back issues of Q from the magazine itself but I would be
careful to check that you get the photo supplement as well - the same would
probably hold if you were buying a copy of the magazine in a used book
store. The photo supplement isn't physically attached to the magazine (it
was sold along with the magazine in a plastic bag).
