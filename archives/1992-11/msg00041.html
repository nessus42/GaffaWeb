they showed some tour dates, including a stop in Boston.
Does anyone know where I should call for tickets?

IMO, the interviewing of Tori by John Norris just showed
how poor a veejay he is.  He was trying to pigeonhole her
and she just wouldn't answer him the way he wanted.  My
roommate, who doesn't know much at all about Tori, was
totally lost during the first interview segment because
she wasn't providing any context referring to her "monsters"
and talking about virgin/whore duality.  I, who
understood what she was talking about, was quite amused
my Norris' discomfort but annoyed there wasn't someone
better (e.g. Martha Quinn) to interview her.
