   I am not sure of the EXACT circumstances surrounding this situation,
but I have known SEVERAL people with AIDS, some where considered good
friends. While they indeed had AIDS, the virus often caused cancers that
were visible, often on the face. As a mother of a young child, if my son
asked why does a person look a certain way? I would reply that the person
had cancer, not AIDS. It is complicated to explain AIDS, when it IS
actually cancer a child is seeing. 
   Diane was not a paragon a virtue, and she would never claim to be. But
she did bring an awareness to the issue of AIDS, and helped raise money for
the cause. I think the author, (FASTLOW) is being rather petty, and snippy
over someone who is dead.
