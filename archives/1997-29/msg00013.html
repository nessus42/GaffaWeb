Hi gang,

Favorite album cover by far, (this could be because I am a male), is the cover
of the Dreaming!!!  It is one of the most beautiful pictures that I have ever
seen.  I have a story about this cover.

This will expose my geekdom that I live in, but about two years ago I was at a
gaming convention called GenCon.  It's a large role playing, miniature gaming
think, cool if your into that kind of thing, anyway.  I was walking around in
the art show and I saw a painting of the cover of the Dreaming.  With a bit of a
twist.

The picture was exactly the same except Kate was portrayed as a vampire.  The
painting was entitled "A Vampires Kiss..."!!!  Cool!  I tracked down the artist
and we talked about Kate for an hour or two and then I purchased the painting
from him.  Kind of neat hugh?

If anyone is interested in including this on one of their Kate web sights, I
could scan this and send it to them.  Whatever.

I do not have a least favorite album cover, but I do disagree with Ariusz
Nowak.  I think the Red Shoes cover is one of the better ones.  The ballet shoe
reminds me of Kate.  If she were to have a icon or symbol that would be forever
associated with her,  for me I would vote on the Red ballet shoe.  :)
