I don't remember who's idea this poll was, but it's a great one!

Anyway...

Probably my favorite album cover is "The Dreaming." Kate's mouth is open
and ready to pass the key with a kiss! I'm there, dude! Special mention
also must go to the stunning picture (with unfortunate cropping) on the
cover of the Japanese version of TKI.

I also like the simplicity of the cover of the English "Sat In Your Lap"
single. Plus, it's my favorite KB song and the cover reminds me of the
excellent video.

The weirdest one I've seen is the cover of the Japanese "Symphony In Blue"
single with KB in a red leotard riding on the back of some sea mammal.
What's that all about?

The worst has to be the cover and gatefold of "Never For Ever." It reminds
me of bad '60s "psychedelic" stationery that girls used to buy. Nick Price,
who's done other good work, should be ashamed. I also don't think much of
the tarted up photo of KB on the cover of the English "Army Dreamers"
single. Blech.

- Don
