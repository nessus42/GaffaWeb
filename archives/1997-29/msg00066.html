....on spending your gift certificates:
1. If you must get Happy, well, take Meredith's advice and get one of
her most recent albums.  I am not a fan of compilations like Rhode Songs
or The Whole Story.
2. BUT, better yet, get Jane Siberry's When I Was A Boy.  No other as a
substitute. WIWAB is by far her best. (Play it LOUD!)
3. AND, if you still have some paper left, have your record shop order
you the new Hounds Of Love reissue with the bonus tracks.  (Helpful hint:
my old HOL makes a nice gift for my brother.)

Still some left?  Well, you could mail them to me..... ;-)
