dropping out of high school does not make you a moron. i had to drop out
of high school, for various reasons which are no one elses business. many
people simply cannot handle high school, or like it, whatever. but it
doesn't make you an automatic idiot. second, not everyone in the entire
world is an artist. not everyone has the gift to write, or to paint, or
make music, or dance, whatever. it doesn't make you worth less if you can't
do any of those things. give me a break. of course kate is incredibly
talented, and that is fabulous and i am so glad that she's out there. but
does that mean that i think she's any better then a person who devotes
their life to say, studying the enviroment or rescueing abused animals or
something? of course not. we all have different paths in life.
