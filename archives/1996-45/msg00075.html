Hi all,

Does anyone remember the feature in early KB newsletters when fans 
wrote in about their "synchronicities" ?

Yesterday I was browsing through a record shop (Virgin) in Dublin and 
saw a display of singles with the words "The Single File" written on 
it , and behind that was another in-store display stand of the new 
Oasis CD box-sets with the caption "The Whole Story ?" printed in a 
box in the style of that bands logo ......then December Will Be Magic 
Again played over the stores sound-system (admittedly a very common 
occurrence in Irish shops at Christmas time).

To top off all of this I met a photographer friend and he told me 
that he had met Kate c.1984 in Windmill Lane studios working on 
Hounds Of Love .......

Just thought I'd tell you about it !

Sean :-)
