Anyhows, my advice is to go and rent Brazil.  I'm biased, 'cause it's my
favourite film of all time, but IMO everyone should see it (and preferably
at least two or three times -- it may take a couple just to figure out
what's going on :)

(Which reminds me... is the butchered US version actually available on
video? I always tell people to make sure it's the proper version, but
I don't even know if the US version was released on video... can
anyone enlighten me?)

Anyways, if you find that you love the film you will *not* want to be
without the soundtrack, Kate or no Kate, as it's a fantastic album!
(And if you're not familiar with the film, the dialogue in the soundtrack
won't make any sense :)
