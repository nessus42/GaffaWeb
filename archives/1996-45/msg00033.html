the December issue of german hifi magazine "stereoplay" has a three-page 
article with midprice recommendations, including this one:

"The Dreaming" certainly doesn't belong to the most successful works 
of Kate Bush, nevertheless the ballad ballerina demonstrates particularly 
here how modern music for head, belly and feet has to sound.

[german:
"The Dreaming" gehoert sicher nicht zu den erfolgreichsten Werken von

This sounds almost exactly like a similar tip in the January issue 
of "Audio". No wonder, both magazines are published by the same company
