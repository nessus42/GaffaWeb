"Goodnight, Baby" - yes, I know there's three but I like 'em so there!

"The Kick Inside" and "Never Forever".  I can't relegate the album that

Forever" was the album that showed her at the peak (at that stage) of her 
powers - ajoy to hear and feel her creativity flowering.  Gosh!
