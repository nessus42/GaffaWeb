Well I for one wondered if Kate and Prince's (as he was at the time)
work together went only one way (him on her album), well now I know -
on TAFKAP's new album - Emancipation, disc 3 track 9 "My Computer"
Kate provides additional vox.  The song's theme has some echos of
"Deeper Understanding".  Have a listen yourself, its certainly one of
the best tracks!!
