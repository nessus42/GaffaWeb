   Exactly. Put properly into the context of when they were created, Kate's
videos were not merely as good as everything else out there...they were
better. 

   I'd have to show you some of the videos from that time frame for 
comparison. At the time, the majority of videos were the classic
"Band In A Box" type. The US ABC program 20/20 had a feature
about the then brand new videodisc. The general opinion then was
that music videos might be the most compelling material.

   They showed a snippet of "Wuthering Heights" with these words:
"...from the straight-ahead rock of this performance by Tom Petty to the
sophisticated computer graphics of this one by Kate Bush".

    Kate's early videos were of their time, but are some of the best
of their time.
