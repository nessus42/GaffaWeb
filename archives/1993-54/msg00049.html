"Top of the City" is not a single.  WFNX just has a habit of sometimes
making their own picks as to what tracks on an album they think should
have been singles and then playing the tracks as if they *were*
singles.  Personally, I think "Top of the City" sounds mighty unlikely
as a single, and it seems quite strange to me when I hear it on WFNX.
I think "Constellation of the Heart" or "And So Is Love" would have
been much better choices.
