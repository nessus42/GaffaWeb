Tori will begin touring in March or April to support _Under
the Pink_ which is scheduled to be released February 1.

The tour is expected to start with ten or twelve dates in North
America followed by an extensive tour of Europe and elsewhere.  Later
in the year Tori will return to North America to do dates all over
the country much like she did in '92. 

You may want to consider subscribing to Really Deep Thoughts, the Tori
Amos mailing list.  Tour dates will be listed there as soon as they
become available.  You can subscribe by sending mail to
rdt-request@gradient.cis.upenn.edu.  Specify loose mail or digest
format.

If you don't want to keep up with another mailing list you may want to
subscribe to the Really Deep Thoughts, the official Tori Amos fanzine
You can automatically receive complete information on the RDT fanzine 
by sending mail (the contents don't matter) to info@bsbbs.columbus.oh.us
