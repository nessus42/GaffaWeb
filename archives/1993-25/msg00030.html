arrangement for "This Woman's Work"!

There is, of course, one other roundabout tie in:  Kamen worked closely
with Pink Floyd for some time, contributing arrangements to _The Wall_ and
_The Final Cut_, as well as Roger Waters' _The Pros and Cons of Hitchhiking_
and David Gilmour's _About Face_.
