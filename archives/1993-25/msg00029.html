Kenneth Bacso inquires:
>Which Kate Bush album would be the best for a new listener to buy and why?

The Dreaming.  Because it's the greatest album ever made.  It will
undoubtedly freak out and shock many people who are unprepared for its
genius, but that's just too darn bad.  Listen to it, love it, worship
it and breathe in its all-holy vapors.  If it sounds "too weird", then
listen to it again until it doesn't.  Repeat if necessary.

TAKE NO PRISONERS.
