My advice would be to go and get The Whole Story. It is a compilation of
her stuff from '78 - '85. It has lots of great pictures - of all of the 
singles, and of her.

Listen to that for a while and depending on which songs you really 
like, buy the corresponding album.

Maybe buy the whole story used, and use it as a guide...it will give you
some sort of feel for how she changed through the first five albums.
(Although it is hard to judge an album by two songs on a compilation)

Summary - buy _The Whole Story_ and follow your nose (or ears).
