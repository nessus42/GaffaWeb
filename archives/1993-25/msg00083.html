I (finally) downloaded the Moments of Pleasure recording and listened
through it. Then I listened it again. And yet another time. In
short, the _sound_ quality through the Sun IPC:s speaker or even with
headphones is atrocious (maybe somebody with the videotape and an
Amiga with a sampler could make a better recoding?), but the _song_
is WONDERFUL! The repeated listenings are really necessary to get
"through" the telephone-quality sound.
