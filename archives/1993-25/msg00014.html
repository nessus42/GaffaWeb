Someone mentioned it would be a good idea to print good KaTe lyrics on the
back of the shirt- yes, it would.

Obvious ones would be the song "Hounds of Love", or "Deeper Understanding",
but under the current circumstances, perhaps the most apt lyrics for the
shirt (assuming we go with the flaming terminal idea for the front of the
tee) would be:

     With her hand in his hand
     They were both happy again
     You started taking sides,
     They started arguing
     He said it was her fault
     She said it wasn't at all
     But the truth lies somewhere in the middle.

If we went with this idea, then instead of a KaTe-symbol in the background it
would be best to put an image of a withering sapling behind the words.
