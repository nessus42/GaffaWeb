Although the Hammersmith video was recently released in the US on VHS,
it was not issued on laser disc in the US.  The Hammersmith show has
been available as a Japanese import but I don't know if it's still
available.

The import disc is well worth the $56 I paid for it a year ago.  
You can either check with laser disc stores that can order imports 
or with the Kate specialty places like IGG.  There are also a few 
places that advertise rare music LDs in the Goldmine classifieds.
