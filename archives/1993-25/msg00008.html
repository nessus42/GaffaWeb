I remember reading in here that Kate Bush was once mentioned on MST3k
(Mystery Science Theater 3000).  Although not as amazing, last night a
rerun of a 1991 MST3k had Tom Servo playing Peter Gabriel as the bots

cream of wheat.  Keyword is "attempted". :)

Of no importance, but later in the same show one of them said:
"This guy's got a knife in his digeridu."

This might be old news...

released in the states the week of July 20 by Atlantic.  I don't know/remember
if they have been mentioned here before or not but they have been played on
MTV's 120 Minutes and are from the Netherlands.
