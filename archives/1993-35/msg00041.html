After living for a week with Eat the Music, here are some more thoughts:

o  Eat the Music:  Phillip Glass gone Calypso
o  I like the theme song to "Northern Exposure" better
o  Many Kate songs I did not like at first later became favorites of
   mine (such as "Night of the Swallow").  I liked EtM when I first heard
   it, but am growing weary of it.
o  The only other KateSong that I liked at first but now don't is Heads
   We're Dancing
o  The 12" Torturously Long Mix of EtM is still my least favorite KateSong
   (Edging out the 12" Completely Unneccessary Mix of Hounds of Love)
o  Remember:  I am judging things by Kate Standards.  "Sensual World" is a
   mediocre Kate album, yet it is still one of the best 10 albums ever made.
o  Big Stripey Lie kicks ass!  Big thumbs up from yours truly.
o  I *LIKE* Candle in te Wind, so Nyah!
