This is my opinion, of course, and I CAN see why others might not
like it: it is repetitive, and the composition, while interesting
is somewhat shallow.

I didn't like it that much the first time I heard it, but now
I LOVE it, and I play it again and again, including the extended
mix.

Let's say, for the sake of argument, that you WANT to like Eat the Music,
but you just can't get into it. Here's what you do.

1. Put it on.
2. Turn it up very loud.
3. Jump up and down to the beat. Dance! DANCE, DAMMIT!

I must admit that one of the reasons I love it so is the words:
"He's a woman at heart/And I love him for that."

It's a love song, to me. A complicated, difficult love, certainly,
but a love song.

Oh, yeah. Big Stripey Lie rocks, too.
