I've just been leafing through my copy of NME and came across a list of what is
in their opinion the best albums of the 70's. "The Kick Inside" came in at
number 40 (just in front of various albums like LA Woman - The Doors and Diana
Ross Presents - The Jackson Five.)
 
The top 5 included What's Going On - Marvin Gaye and Unknown Pleasures - Joy
Division.
 
On another note can any UK Gaffan remember NME reviewing KaTe's new single?
Melody Maker reviewed it and seemed to like (if you wade through all the usual
journalistic bilge.)
