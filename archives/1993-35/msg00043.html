>Anyway, we were talking about the brouhaha regarding the sound of The

>details are very easy to hear. The album is lush and "thick" (though

this is ridiculous.  the dreaming is NOT a heavy album!  do you even
have an *inkling* of what i'm talking about when i complain about the
engineering on TSW?  listen to the vocal and drum tracks - TD's have a

engineering style.  now listen to TSW - tearing, digital crud all over
the place... i could've done better with a 4-track cassette and a
radio shack microphone.  this is NOT an acceptable professional studio
production!  i would've SHOT the engineer and gone to real studio
after hearing that crap.

>it seems to be mainly Americans who are complaining about the sound,

>playing the Sony CD.

presumptiveness is really your forte, isn't it?  i have the british
EMI CD and a very nice system, thank you.  i'll gladly play you any
ten current dance records and stack them up against mine to prove that
i know what i'm talking about when it comes to engineering.
