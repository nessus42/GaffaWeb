Since other people are posting about local air play, I'll just throw
this out...WDST-FM (Woodstock NY, simulcast on WDSP-FM Poughkeepsie)
has now played ETM every morning five mornings running (I keep waking
up to this song).  Several of those mornings they have followed it
by mentioning the upcoming album (they're saying January as well).

As a humorous aside, one of the DJ's (night man Mark Zip) mentioned
Monday morning that "fans of Kate Bush are dedicated, they know every
track of every album, bootleg, and b-side."  This was his ON-AIR
opinion.  He once told me -- before he knew I was a Katefan -- that
he thought all Kate fans were *witches*.  :)  "They keep coming up
to me in record stores with these mad, staring eyes, asking when 
another album is coming out."
