OK, i got curious and looked it up.  The lovely sounding sultana is, according
to the American Heritage Dictionary, 1) The wife, mother, sister or daughter 
of a sultan, 2) the mistress of a sultan, king, or prince, 3) a small, sweet
seedless raisin of a kind originally produced in Asia Minor.

Perhaps a bit of word play at work?  "He's a woman at heart."  Here sweetie,
have a sultana.  Have a thousand of them.  They're small.
