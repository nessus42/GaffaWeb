My 2 cents:

In general I think Kate sounds in much better voice than she was during
the "Sensual World" sessions.  "Big Stripey Lie" is excellent, as good
as just about anything she's done.  I also enjoy "Rubberband Girl."
Lot's of energy in that track and Kate sounds like she's having a hell
of a good time.  It's fun and has an very catchy melody.  Great choice
for a single.  On the other hand "Eat the Music" ...  Well, I like it
better than several of the songs off "The Sensual World", but lord it
is a bit repititious.  Seems badly in need of some kind of a vocal 
bridge.  The voic sounds good, though.  It really seems to me that
RBG was a MUCH better choice for a single.  (Maybe it will be the
second US single?)

As for the release date...  The delay is sure annoying, but I guess better
Nov. than next year!
