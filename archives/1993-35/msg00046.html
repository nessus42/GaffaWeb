The good ole US versus UK sound quality thing rises again.  
I think that most people agree the UK CDs tend to sound
better than their US counterparts.  And now some of you
are in the postion to predict whether this will hold true 
for TRS.  

So, has anyone A-B'd BSL from the UK RBG and US ETM CD5s? 

If Sony release TRS in the US in Jan or even Oct then they will lose 
a great many sales as any store worth it's salt will stock the UK CD
until it comes out.  I'm sure that most Katefans cannot wait several
months for their new KaTefix.  

RBG grows on me, and as with BSL, Kate is using her voice much better
than on most of TSW.  Some of her vocals remind me of TD.  

I'm wating for the UK ETM so I can't A-B BSL and give you my impression.

As for her worst song.  For me it has to be Between a Man and a Woman.  
It does absolutely nothing for me except to want to press the "next track"
button.
