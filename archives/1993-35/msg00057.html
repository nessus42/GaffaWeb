I called Captiol-EMI Canada today and confirmed that the Kanadian relase daTe
is still Oct 5.  Alas, there is no info on a single, which means we'll have to 
get the UK and US singles as imports.

In this issue of the NOW magazine there's a full page ad of a local radio
station CFNY 102.1 FM "The Leading Edge".  The heading reads "NEW MUSIC NEWS".
The first paragraph of the text reads:

	*Kate Bush* albums are anxiously anticipated at CYNF... her new 
	release *The Red Shoes* is certainly no exception.  While it's being
	labeled her most accessible album to date, it shouldn't disappoint
	even Kate's most diligent fans.  Recorded at her home studio, support 
	personalities include Victor (aka PRINCE), David Gilmour, Jeff Beck
	and Eric Clapton.  Hello?

Lower down in the ad it sez "Listen for the New Music Show Sundays at 8 p.m."
Who knows, they may play something from the new album...  Torontonians
take note!
