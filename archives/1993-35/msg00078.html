I suspect not; either it's a big coincidence or there isn't a new
Shelleyan Orphan album out. However, the new Cocteau Twins album _Four
Calendar Cafi_ *is* out soon (October 11th, in U.K., I think it is --
it was going to be earlier but Robin Guthrie caught chicken-pox from
his & Liz's daughter).
Anyway, Liz Frazer sang on some (look, I haven't got any TMC albums,
so someone else will have to say which) TMC tracks/albums; well, she
sang on their version of Tim Buckley's _Song For The Siren_, which is
probably the only well-known TMC track in this country, at least. In
fact, it's one of very few cover versions that I'd rate more highly
than the original (along with Frazier Chorus' [no connection] rendition
of _Anarchy In The UK_, oh, and just about any cover by the Cowboy
Junkies -- I'm just a sucker for Margo's voice).
OBKaTe: I haven't heard _Candle In The Wind_, so I can't comment.
