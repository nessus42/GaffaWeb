Hmm. 
When I first, FIRST heard KaTe sing, it was "Wuthering Heights", and I
said to my self 'self, what IS this wailing Banshee?' I couldn't stand it.
Then, I heard more (and more...) of her songs, and heard the diversity of
her voice, and she is now my favorite female singer.

BUT...I still can't really stand "Wuthering Heights". My wife, whenever
she hears that song, leaves the room, though she >has< heard KaTe songs
she does like.
