I tell you Bruce, you're _sick_, _sick_, _sick_! :-) That list could
almost have been my favourites. I'd have to throw in "Jig of Life"
someplace among the top 3, though - that's the world's greatest track
for dancing. No contest. Her best _melody_ (if you know what I mean)
is "The man with the child in his eyes", so that would have to go in
there somewhere, too.

My least favourites?
  1.    Eat the music
	ARGH! Sony should know better than sending US releases onto
	the Norwegian market. Norwegian pop tastes has always been
	more British. KaTe shouldn't listen to the suits - the suits
	never liked her music, so they won't know what her fans will
	like. Bring back EMI!
  2.	The big sky
	Tedious, repetitious. The video is nice, though, one of her
	finest.
  3.	Uhm, can't think of more. :-)
