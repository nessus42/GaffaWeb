  I just picked up Sarah McLachlan's "Possession" and I have to agree with
previous posters who think it is excellent.  Everyone should run right 
out and get this!!!  And if it isn't available in the US or elsewhere yet
then I say "nyah nyah nyah nyah!" because so far I haven't been able to
locate a copy of "Eat The Music" anywhere.  If anyone knows of any record
stores in Buffalo that might have a copy of ETM, please email the details.
And FYI, Nettwerk (Sarah's label) is on the net at nettwerk@mindlink.bc.ca.
This is listed on the back of the single, along with their other address
info.
  As for "Rubberband Girl", it's great and "Big Stripey Lie" is even better
and I can't wait to hear the whole album.  Speaking of which, what's the
deal?  Is it still coming out Oct. 4 in the UK or has that also been pushed
back?  I have friends going over there and already told them not to bother
getting it for me because they aren't going to be back until the 11th, but 
that was before this latest delay.
  In other news, I saw the box set for $230 CAN when I was in North Bay on
the long weekend (a meer $100 more than it is in Hamilton).  If any north
Ontario love-hounds want one, I can fix you up for $230 with tax and 
shipping included.  :)  Next time someone complains because northerners 
only pay $10 to renew a car license, point out how much more the necessities
of life cost up there.  :)
