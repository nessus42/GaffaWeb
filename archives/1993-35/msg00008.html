First, there will be a special edition of TRS album, wrapped in fabric.

Second, Del has done some mixing on the album.  One must consider that it
is perhaps not "Del's incompetence" that is responsible for the sound of
Kate's albums, but that Kate WANTS them to sound that way.

Third, if Kate and Del have indeed broken up, our lady has lost two people
she loves in one year.  I'd personally like to see everyone be a little
more sensitive to the possibility that she's in a lot of pain.  Also, on
more than one occasion, the universe has been told to butt out of her life.
I believe that includes us.

Fourth, this album is going to blow you away.  It is very, very wonderful,
rich and strange and wild.
