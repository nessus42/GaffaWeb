we received the new Mick Karn CD @ the station
a couple of days ago.

it features: David Torn, Steve Jansen, Richard Barbieri,
		David Liebman, Joachim Kuhn, Glen Velez,
		Steve Gorn, & Ed Mann

8 cuts - "Bestial Cluster"
	 "Back In The Beginning"
	 "Beard In The Letterbox"
	 "The Drowning Dream"
	 "The Sad Velvet Breath Of Summer & Winter"
	 "Saday, Maday"
	 "Liver & Lungs"
	 "Bones Of Mud"

mostly Karn compositions w/ a couple being Karn/Torn
	(or atleast "in part" by Torn)

it's released by:
	CMP Records - 140 W 22nd St, #7b NY, NY  10011
	CMP Records - PO Box 1129 52368 Kreuzau, F.R. Germany

CMP CD 1002 - we also received a bio & a "track by track"
		w/ comments about the cuts.  if anyone wants
		a copy of this I'll be happy to post it to U.

CMP is a great label!  mostly progressive/avant-garde jazz & 
world beat.  I played the title track during my jazz shift & 
followed it w/ some Weather Report (from Mr. Gone) - sounded 
cool!!!
