This is info I got from a recent EMI Music Canada press release.  As we
know, release dates are subject to change, so get in touch with them for
more info.  The contact person for Kate Bush is given as Karen Klug.
There are several phone numbers listed but the one for Mississauga,
Ontario is (416) 677-5050; this is most likely the "headquarters".

Album:

10/5	CN	The Red Shoes		CD/MC		EMI Music Canada
				MC: E41V 27277, $11.29
				CD: E21S 27277, $19.29

Singles:

9/17	CN	Rubberband Girl		CD		EMI Music Canada
				CD: E25Q 80829, $7.98
				(tracks: RBG, remix, BSL)

	The copy says it's also going to be on cassette single format,
but doesn't give the catalog number, oddly enough.  The release date for
RBG was yesterday, but it still hasn't arrived here!  Aaaargh!  Having
not bothered to FTP the .wav files, I *still* haven't heard these songs
everyone else is talking about.  A guy I know scammed a promo copy of it,
though -- time to hunt him down!!
	Sign of KosmiK significance: I was freaked out when I saw the
cat. no. for TRS is 27277.  My birthday is July 27, and I've always
thought that `7' and `27' held some numerological meaning for me or my
life or whatever.  Part of me is skeptical about such things, but I
can't help but notice how the numbers have always "followed" me, popping
up all the time.  And now this!  Strange Phenomenon, indeed.
	Also, to the guy who's keeping the list of TRS info.: it's also
on MiniDisc in the States I think.  And is the US ltd. ed. in a Digipak
the same as the one someone mentioned that's wrapped in fabric?
