Hiya,

today I received a letter from the Univercity of Pennsylvania. It contained
the CD Straight Ahead from Counterparts, it contains a very nice a capella
version of The Man With The Child In His Eyes. I'd say the vocal style reminds
me a little bit of the Dark Orange version, but of course it's completely a 

capella. The comparison is only valid because these are the two most similar
versions of The Man With... I know. Oh, and it certainly has an American 

accent... There are 17 songs on the CD, giving 47 minutes of good a capella 

music.

Discography entry:

Counterparts - Straight Ahead
CP0003CD
Contains an a capella cover version of "The Man With The Child In His Eyes"

The only thing I wonder is why there ain't not a single credit to any of the
17 writers of the songs/lyrics neither on the CD nor in the booklet...

Bye,

Uli
