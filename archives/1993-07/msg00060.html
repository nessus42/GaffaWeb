If by "next year's winter games" you mean the Winter Olympics, then
she won't be able to use this song unless the rules are changed.  Songs
with vocals are not permitted at the Olympics, or indeed at any
amateur figure skating championships (e.g. the World Championships).
Rosalyn Sumners was in the Olympics quite a while ago (1984, I believe),
so she's almost certainly professional now.  The show you saw was most
probably a competition among professionals, where vocal music IS allowed.

I know that the rules governing who is eligible to compete in the
"amateur" competitions is changing now, so some people who are
professionals will probably be able to compete at the next Olympics.
But I have heard nothing about changes to the rules that determine
permissible music.
