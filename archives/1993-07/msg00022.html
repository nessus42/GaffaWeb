Hi all,

    I just got back from another of my trips to Sydney and Red Eye Records.
While there I got yet -another- KT interview. This one is called a "Stand Up &
Talk!" series interview. It's a 33rmp LP single picture disk. The picture is
taken from the "Them Heavy People" video and gives the LP a weird shape. You
also get a plinth (or stand) with the following instructions -

    - A plinth is included to enable Kate to stand up. To fit simply fit the
flat area of this disc into the slit on the plinth. Put on a shelf & drool." -

    I assume it's from a series of interview LP's since it's numbered "Standup
2". I'm also informed that it's a limited edition and I own No. 859 of 1500. I
haven't heard it yet as I haven't got home to play it yet so I don't even yet
know what interview it is. I'll post ASAP when I find out.
