Ok, so I shouldn't have cross-posted, sorry. I'm serious, I really am
sorry. Happy's 6th album is coming out in a month and there's going to
be plenty of talk about her then. The last thing I want is another
flame war centered around her. I try now, and will continue to try
to steer inquiries to Ecto. I respond via e-mail sometimes, and post
sometimes (so it doesn't seem as if inquiries are being ignored).
We *will* be letting people who might be interested know that the album
is out, though. Since the majority of Ectophiles found Happy via gaffa/
Love-Hounds, she will continue to be mentioned here. It's a fact of life.

This time around will be totally different. Now that we have Ecto, all
extended discussions can go on there, and we will do our best to see
that they do. No lyrics, interviews, official reviews, articles or
long posts will appear in gaffa, as they did in 1991 when _Warpaint_
came out, so I hope that those people who aren't interested in hearing
about her will be patient with the mentions that do appear.

Again, my apologies for posting those lyrics.
