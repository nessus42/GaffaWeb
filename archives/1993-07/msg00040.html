Greetings all...
 
  Wanted to pass along word about a BBS I now have up and running
called =The Electric Cafe= (805-399-3636).  It's a new BBS and some of
you may find the following of some interest...
 
Despite the fact that "The Electric Cafe" is not "another" KaTe Bush
BBS I _have_ been a fan since '78 thus I think it's only natural that
I have devoted a file and message area exclusively to her.
 
The "ELECTRIC CAFE" is a FREE BBS and all FIRST TIME callers HAVE FULL
ACCESS TO ALL KATE BUSH GIFS & JPGS.
 
In the "KaTe Bush" file area you will find a bunch of KaTe gifs and
jpgs, among these you will find _NEW_ b&w and color 800x600 JPG scans
from the "A Lioness At Heart" book (these scans turned out rather
nice and more will soon appear on the Cafe's menu).  You will also find a
small JPG to GIF conversion program in the event someone can only view
GIFs.  There is also a collection of high quality GIFs from the book
"CATHY" to be found here.
 
Files "0KTCAT01.JPG" and "0CATYCAT.GIF" are 2 files you may want to
grab first (along with the "0JPG2GIF.ZIP" conversion program if you
need it).  These 2 files are like proof sheets visually showing the
"KaTe Bush" JPGs & GIFs available for download along with there file
name.
 
If anyone wants to grab any or all of these files and upload them here
or to other boards please do so.  If you have anything you would like
to upload to the Cafe please do...
 
  I'm running a "WILDCAT" v3.55s BBS via a USRobotics 16.8 HST/DS
v32bis modem.  The cafe is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and is
_ABSOLUTELY FREE_ for the calling (sorry you have to pay your phone
bill yourself :) You will have FULL ACCESS to the "KaTe Bush" files on
your first call, just answer the questions ask of all first time
callers (name, phone number etc.) read the bulletins and then have at
it.  If you want "Page The SYSOP" and if I'm in we can chat.  If
nothing else drop me a note and say hi...
 
PS.  If there are any "Twin Peaks" fans out there you will also have
     full access to all the "Twin Peaks" jpgs on your first call...
 
Thanks for the read...
 
Ken Saint-John...
