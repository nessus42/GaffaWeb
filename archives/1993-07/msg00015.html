About the Box Set:
>That was a rhetorical question as the answer is ofcourse MONEY! ,but I am
>sure if Kate had an opinion on the matter she would allow the book and
>singles on CD to be released seperatly(I think?).A

   Sorry, but the Box Set *was* Kate's decision. A box set was proposed
by EMI and well known fans were asked for input. Andy Marvick and others
created a wonderful set of songs for an all inclusive collection,
including a well thought out arrangement. This was sent to EMI and lost.
The box set was apparently put on the back burner for a while, then one
day Kate called the folks from Homeground and asked them to get a list
together for her for the next morning. They were able to get a fairly
good list together on short notice, but missed a few. Kate then looked
the list over and vetoed several of the rarest songs as being too much
trouble to obtain (the studio version of _Another Day_ and the other
PG song, _Davey_, etc.)

   The principle reason for the inclusion of all the albums? Kate wanted
the Box Sets as Christmas prezzys. Really. 

> Finally before I sign off I wonder if there would be any chance of Kate

>Fanzine so why not this fanzine.She has clearly an interest in computers(see
>"A deeper understanding -The sensual World) so maybe it would be worth
>trying to let Kate be aware of us....Twas just a thought.

   No. There is no chance at all. Kate was interviewed by someone from
Love-Hounds and it did not go well.
