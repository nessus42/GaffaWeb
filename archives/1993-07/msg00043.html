Greetings all...
 
Would like to invite everyone to check out a BBS I now have up and
running called =The Electric Cafe= 805-399-3636 ...
 
Despite the fact that "The Electric Cafe" is not a "KaTe Bush BBS" I
have been a fan since '78 thus I think it's only natural that I have
devoted a file and message area exclusively to her.
 
The "ELECTRIC CAFE" is a FREE BBS and all FIRST TIME callers HAVE FULL
ACCESS TO ALL KATE BUSH GIFS & JPGS.
 
Give the Cafe a call and you will find KaTe gifs and jpgs, among these
you will find some _NEW_ never before posted b&w and color 800x600 jpg
scans from the stunning "A Lioness At Heart" book (these scans turned
out rather nice and more will soon appear on the Cafe's menu).  You
will also find a small jpg2gif conversion program in the event someone
can only view gifs.  There is also a collection of high quality gifs
from the book "CATHY" to be found here.
 
Files "0KTCAT01.JPG" and "0CATYCAT.GIF" are 2 files you may want to
grab first (along with the "0JPG2GIF.ZIP" conversion program if you
need it).  These 2 files are like proof sheets visually showing the
"KaTe Bush" jpgs & gifs available for download along with there file
name.
 
If anyone wants to grab any or all of these files and upload them here
or to other boards please do so.  I have no way of getting these files
up to the net so someone please have at it.  If you have anything you
would like to upload to the Cafe please do.
 
 The "Electric Cafe" is running "WILDCAT" v3.55s BBS software via a
USRobotics 16.8 HST/DS v32bis modem.  The cafe is open 24 hours a day,
7 days a week and is _ABSOLUTELY FREE_ for the calling (sorry you have
to pay your phone bill yourself :) You will have FULL ACCESS to the
"KaTe Bush" files on your first call, just answer the questions ask of
all first time callers (name, phone number etc.) read the bulletins
and then have at it.  If you want "Page The SYSOP" and if I'm in we
can chat.  If nothing else drop me a note and say hi...
 
PS.  If there are any "Twin Peaks" fans out there you will also have
     full access to all the "Twin Peaks" jpgs on your first call...
 
Thanks for the read, stop by the Cafe and check out the menu...
