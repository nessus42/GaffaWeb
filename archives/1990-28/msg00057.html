Subject: Attention conventioneers!!!

If anyone wants to be included in the arrangements I'm making for lodgings
for the week of Nov 16-22, I need to hear by Thursday the 1st.

Here is the deal: in the "Time Out" London Guide there is a bed&breakfast
listed called the Anne Elizabeth House.  I have tentatively booked beds for
ten of us there, at prices ranging from 8 pounds per night including
breakfast.  These cheap beds require that we sleep five to a room, so give
this some thought.  Other rates are:
2 beds per room: 12.50 pounds
3 beds per room: 11.25 pounds

These rooms are getting booked up fast, so tell me your needs and a phone
number for details.  My phone is 312-973-0874 home, 708-467-1013 work (ok
to call).
I need to know how willing you are to share a room with other love-hounds
in order to save money, and what night's you'll be staying.  This means a
firm commitment-- people backing out will cost us bucks.

The B&b is on the border between Earl's Court and Kensington, very near the
Gloucester Road tube station.  The neighborhood could be better, but I am
assured it is perfectly safe.  There is a laundromat on the premises, and
bathrooms with showers (not in the rooms), a tv room and cooking
facilities.

Time Out lists only a very few b&b's, so I assume this is an especially
recommended one.  They call it a good bargain.
