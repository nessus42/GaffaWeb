For those waiting with bated breath for a new Danielle Dax release,
THE TIME HAS COME!!!!  According to the "new release wall" at a local
record store (Music Warehouse), a full-length lp will be released
October 30, the title of which is unknown at this time. Now if only
her record label would pressure her to tour!!

Regarding other new releases, Area will be coming out with another lp
"Fragments of the Morning" November 3 (if not sooner) according to the
fine folks at C'est La Mort records.  There will be a limited edition
white vinyl lp available while the cd will contain 3 extra remixes.
If the track on C'est La Mort's new sampler cd is any indication, it
should be a real winner!!

A question for the net: Does anyone know when the new Severed Heads lp
"Cuisine"(?) will be released here in the states? Also, could someone
post a review of their casette "Flubberknife"?

That's all for now.....
