>-dick

Upon hearing this news, your Trusty Kanadian KonnecTion immediately
called up Capitol-EMI of Canada.  The person I spoke to tells me that
no, they are not being pressed in Canada, but the UK pressing will be
available as a "special import" (by EMI) thru most record chains.  I
went to the main "Sam the Record Man" store and they said to expect it
"early next week."
