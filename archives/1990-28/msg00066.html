I just picked up my copy of the boxed set yesterday.  Most of the
stores around LA (or at least the ones I talked to--probably not most,
just the ones I frequent :-) are taking orders for the set--pay in
full now, and you'll get it in a week or two.  And they want about
$180-200 for it.  I picked mine up at Go-Boys Records in Torrance for
$165.  I got the second to last copy.  They claim that different
suppliers are charging very different prices--everything from $125 to
$175, possibly explaining the wide variations in price the store are
quoting.

I'm happy :->
