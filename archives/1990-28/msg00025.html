For those of you who can't make it to the Convention,
how about Northern Colorado?
We'll be playing Kate videos and music (hopefully the box set),
and maybe an interview until wee hours of the morning!

There will be lots of fun food, Stuffed mushrooms,
artichokes, clams (ie every Hors d'eouvre we can figure
out how to make), and of course wine, beer, and punch.

The date is Saturday November 3.
Some time after 8:00 pm.

If you think you might be able to make it, give me
a call (303) 221 - 9489 or send mail.
