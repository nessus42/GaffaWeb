If some of you have been putting off going to the KonvenTion because because 
you either can't afford a ticket, or can't get enough time off of work, 
British Airways has a deal for you....

For $398 to $428, with no minimum stay (but must cross a Saturday) and no 
advance purchase, you can be in London for a long weekend.  You've got to 
reserve the ticket on a special 800 number, and if you're asked where you 
saw the price, tell them that you saw it on Prodigy, and that the Fare Code 
is (VIT).

The 800 is 1-800-UK-BOUND.
