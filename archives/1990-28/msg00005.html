I found this CD boot at a record fair in Oxford a couple of weeks
ago. The guy had several copies. I paid 20 pounds - I don't know if
this is normal for CD boots as I have never bought one before. However,
as I had never seen any of the Cathy demos on vinyl in this part of
the World I thought it worth parting with the money.

I have seen discussion in this forum and in Homeground about most
of the demo tracks but I don't remember anything about the two
"babooshka" demos being mentioned. Does anyone know anything about
them - are they thought to be from the same source?
