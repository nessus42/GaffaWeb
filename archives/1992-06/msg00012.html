Brian,

The "boxed set" is formerly known as "This Woman's Work:  Anthology
1978-1990" and is available through import (from U.K. or Japan) in CD,
cassette, or vinyl format.  The CD version (what I have) includes eight
CDs, Kate's first six albums and two more full-length CDs of various
B-side singles, 12" versions, and live cuts.
Personally, I recommend the U.K. version if you can find it, I've heard
all the print on the Japanese version is in Japanese (I can't verify
that myself).

The Rocket Man single you mentioned is a special single release of the
Elton John & Bernie Taupin song Kate made for the album "Two Rooms,"
honoring the work of these two gentlemen.  The single should also
include a version of the Elton John song "Candle in the Wind" as well as
an instrumental of the same.  "Two Rooms" is available almost anywhere.
That single has been spotted in various locations, I got it on CD in
Davenport, Iowa.

As I don't know your location, I can't recommend a good record store.
If, by some chance, you happen to be from the midwest, try a CO-OP Tapes
and Records.  If you're from somewhere else, post a message asking for
the name of a record store in your City/State (and reflect that in your
message title) which carries or will import Kate material.
