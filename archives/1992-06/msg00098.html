As I've been posted to Atlanta for a month, I've not been able to read
r.m.g., so I hope this isn't old news.  In the hotel's glossy TV guide,
in the section on films in progress, I found the following snippet:

"... brace yourself for the sixth remake of _Wuthering Heights_, this
one a British production starring unknowns *Ralph Fiennes* and *Juliette
Binoche*.  Irish singer *Sinead O'Connor* will narrate the film in the
role of Emily Bronte."

Given that Kate seemed keen on being involved at the convention, I can
only assume that the film makers didn't ask her.
