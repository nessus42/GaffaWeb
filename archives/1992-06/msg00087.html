Hi all!                                                                        
                                                                               
I found a book about Wilhelm Reich a few months back while visiting a          
friend in Ocala, Florida.  It is called _Orgone,_Reich,_and_Eros_.  I          
have only flipped through it so far.  Seems to tell all about the doctor       
and his theories as well as the trouble he was in with the government.         
                                                                               
I don't know how rare it is, but it seems to be out of print.  Has             
anyone heard of this one before?  It seems quite interesting, but I have       
been too busy to really dedicate any time to reading it.                       
                                                                               
Also has anyone seen a book by Orson Bean (of Match Game '74 obscurity)        
called _Me_and_the_Orgone_?  I saw an ad for it in the back of an older        
copy of _A_Book_of_Dreams_.  It would be interesting to find.  He is           
mentioned in the Reich book a couple of times.  I guess Bean was quite a       
proponent of Reichian theories.  Now I've gotta figure out why he was          
considered a celebrity in the early '70's.
