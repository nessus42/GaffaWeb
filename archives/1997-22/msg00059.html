But KfB is a more modern song, quite similar to the songs on the later
albums than any other song on LH.

LH is the album for Kate addicts, and it is maybe a bit to soft.
TKI was launched as a pure commercial project, I think, while LH also
presents less commercial songs and was produced in this way.

It was quite difficult for her and the team to produce LH, because EMI  
wanted to see a lot of sold records, so LH could not sound too differently  
from TKI. Another aspect is that KT did not have much time because EMI  
wanted to see the return of investment soon ;)

But then I think it got too soft, a bit too soft and the voice is  
sometimes overcompressed.
