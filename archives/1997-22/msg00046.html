The first Kate recording I ever listened to was The Sensual World. Wow was I 
ever hooked!  That was less than a year ago and I have since aquired the rest 
of her music (that has been available to me).

Favorites:

     Hounds of Love - Cloudbusting, The Ninth Wave
              Lionheart - Symphony in Blue
The Sensual World - Heads We're Dancing, Walk Straight Down the Middle
        Never Forever - Delius (Song of Summer), Blow Away (For Bill)
     The Red Shoes - Top of the City, You're the One

and others...
