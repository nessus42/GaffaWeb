While The Red Shoes is not my favourite Kate album (though I do not even
have a "least favourite) I feel it is much maligned.  Personally, I love
Constellation of the Heart!  While the theme of the song is again fear,
and fear of love, and the pain that can result from love, it is a fun
song.  It is so rare that Kate does anything just for fun (ie., The Big
Sky video -- one of the few where she is actually smiling) that I always
accept it with a glad heart.  That is just one of the many reasons that
I love Eat The Music so much.
