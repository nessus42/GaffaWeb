For those who do not normally buy Q magazine (it's a UK music magazine
for the benefit of those who have never heard of it), the re-release of HoL
has a full-page review in the latest issue (September 1997), along with a
full-page picture of Kate alongside.  As with the original release, it was
given a ***** rating (the highest).  Q magazine obviously like Kate,
because it's not often that a re-release gets so much space devoted to
it.
