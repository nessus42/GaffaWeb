As a working artist, I have to agree Don.  Inspiration is lovely and I am
thankful when it comes, but writing (music or poetry or plays or whatever)
is a SKILL.  YES, it takes some innate TALENT that we have not been able
to describe the source of, but it is basically a SKILL.  Now, I  do not
claim to now Ms. Bush's work habits or work ethics.  But, if she is not
writing SOMETHING (or revising) everyday, she needs to get on the ball! 
Even if it's something that will end up as a B-side or something she only
preforms at partys or something that is trashed at the end, she and I and
every other working writer needs to write EVERY DAY.  It doesn't matter
what the final outcome is, it is the ACT and the PRACTICE of writing that
is important.  Prehaps she does do this and is just HIGHLY critical of her
own work and then throws out everyting she preceives as crap (or stuff
that she no longer has the interest to hone) if so, great (but sad for me)
if not.......

That's my two cents on how she should run her life.
