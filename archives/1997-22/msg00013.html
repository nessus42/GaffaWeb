Recently, Melanie asked...
>I would just like a show of hands of those who, like me, find this track
to
>be one of their favorites on TRS.

I've put my hand up!
I must say that lyrics are not the reason I "like" a song, to me they're
secondary to the overall sound of the song (I very much enjoy
instrumentals).
That's probably not what songwriters intend!
So... it sounds great to me!

Better throw in my stats...
I'm 34, an Australian male. I first heard Kate's Wuthering Heights (the
original <g>) on the radio when it first came out, and the effect was
instant.
Bought the single. Got the Babooshka single. Never for Ever LP. Kick Inside
CD. Then all the other CDs

The othert artists that appear on my CD player are:
Tangerine Dream (about 30% of my listening time <g>)
Enya & Clannad (as they're related)
Maddy Prior
Tori (Boys for Pele doesn't phaze me. If you can find it, try the Hey
Jupiter special 5-song EP)
Alannah Myles (underrated)
Hmmm.... quite a few females there!
