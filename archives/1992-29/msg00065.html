Ron Buckmire writes, regarding _Little Earthquakes_:

>Was it released between Oct 1, 1991 and SDeptember 30, 1992? Then it's 
>eligible for GRAMMYs anyway.

Is tha based on domestic or international release?  _Little Earthquakes_
was released in...um...early March, I think, in the US, but this was
several months after the British release.  We kept hearing reports about
the album on r.m.g and I kept bothering folks at Tower so I could get a 
US release date.  I was *so* tempted when I saw a vinyl copy in New York
the first weekend of February, but decided to hold off for the CD.
Probablyf or the best...

Anybody remember exactly when _Little Earthquakes_ was released in the
UK?  I'm guessing November, but I'm really not sure.
