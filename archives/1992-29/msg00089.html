Hello Christine-this is Angel again. It's hard to stop listening to it isn't
it?  I listened to Star Me Kitten on the headphones. Yep-he sure says f**k
me kitten.  By the way, it doesn't bother me at all about using those
special words our parents wouldn't let us say but they could.  I just put
*** in place of the letters because there's probably someone out there that
does get offended.  Plus I'm really tense because I'm new at this.  I will
probably get more relaxed the more I get to know everyone on the net.

Everybody Hurts which kind of fits my mood lately.
