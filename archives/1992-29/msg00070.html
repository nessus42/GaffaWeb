Never Forever #20 is out. Interesting bits: In the "CK's Pop Forum"
newsletter accompanying it, there is a report of a rumour "from a
usually reliable source" claiming that KaTe and Del have split up.

Nigel Kennedy was at KaTe's mum's funeral. He turned up late, but
brought his Strad with him and played something appropriate.

The UK '70s music video compliation that features KaTe, has,
apparently, the original Rockflix Wuthering Heights video on it, not
the Keefco one.

My Never Forever box set arrived yesterday. IMHO, it's not as
impressive as the Lionheart one: the book design isn't quite so great,
and the photos aren't either. All the photos are from LFI (London
Features International) and are a wide and varied selection: the early
EMI ones of KaTe wearing those bizarre quilted Samurai-looking things
and kimonos, KaTe in red dress in field of daffodils, KaTe riding
porpoise, KaTe in mock chain-mail jumping out of giant magazine
cover, and even one from the Hounds of Love album launch.

The T-shirt is white with the original "Never Forever - Kate Bush"
logo, fairly small.

he's in the office next door! No, this isn't entirely coincidence 8^)]
