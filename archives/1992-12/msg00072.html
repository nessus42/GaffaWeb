        Fiona asks about a "Lionheart" tour poster with a "Babooshka" video 
picture on it.  Of course, "Babooshka" wasn't out at the time of the tour.  
The tour was _The_Tour_Of_Life_.  The tour program had "Lionheart" type 
pictures in it, and songs were performed from _Lionheart_ but it wasn't 
called a "Lionheart" tour, so the poster must be an un-official, later day 
poster. 
        As far as _The_Ninth_Hound_ poster, it's very common, though I don't 
know why it's called that.  Probably just to be different!  :-) 

        Michael Graham asks how hard it is to find _A_Book_of_Dreams_.  It 
was reprinted a few years back with Kate on the cover, and I was able to 
order it at my local book store.  I don't know what the "Canadian situation" 
is, but you can always buy it mail order, I think both C-Side and 
_Intergalactic_Garage_ have it, but am not certain. 

        Could some Sarah M. and Tori Amos fan write up a brief (one 
paragraph) description of them to add to the monthly post.  I don't really 
follow the discussion so I wouldn't know what to put. 

        Andrew - it is unwise to upset "Wizard's Assistant" [sic - not a 
person].  It is even more unwise to upset "Lionheart".  :-)
