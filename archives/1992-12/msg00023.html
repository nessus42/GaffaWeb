Tori Amos will be at the Iron Horse in Northampton Ma. on April 27th
(a monday night).  Tickets are $6 (!) and are general admission seating.
Just saw the add in the paper and figured someone on Gaffa might want
to catch this show...

programs for the Amiga computer.  As I flipped over a box to read the
back, I found a color computer drawing of Kate!  It was the cover of
TSW with the rose in red and Kates face flesh tone.  Obviously someone
thought it made a pretty example to include on the packaging...
