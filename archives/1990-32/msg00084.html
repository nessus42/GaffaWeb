I sent this as a comment to a letter you wrote so I hope it gets to you.  I am
not at all sure if any of my letters get though. I met up with Ray Russel (the
other guy from San Diego, Poway specifically) and he wanted me to tell you the
copy of your Infant Kiss video that he had came from Canada from the
Honsbergers and that it was labeled "The Innocents - The Infant Kiss  U.K". 
He also wanted me to tell you that it was good meeting you.  I spent a day
with him trading rather ratty copies of rare videos that we both had.   
  
BTW - I got a copy of the Infant Kiss video from him and despite the fact that
it isn't a good copy I REALLY liked it!  I think I told you when we saw it at
Peter + Krystyna's house but I like it even better now that I am home and more
"sane".  I like it much better than the films they made for the convention in
that it was made from a film that I hadn't seen and was edited very sharply. 
It like with the regula Kate Bush videos in that I don't want to listen to the
song alone now that I have seen the video.  Its really made me want to track
down the film and finally see it...  
Anyways, thanks again, it was great meeting you guys...
