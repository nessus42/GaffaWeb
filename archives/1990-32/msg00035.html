Richard, I'm still here though I haven't sat down and written anything
since my last long post. Now that winter's coming on I hope I can
spend more time with mail and posts.

I'd rather an English Love-hound were to send Peter the printouts
because he'd get them faster and wouldn't have to pay so much for
overseas airmail rates. I've asked Neil Calton and if he can't then
I'll ask one of the other UK Hounds that offered. Sorry I couldn't
write everyone personally. The weeks before the trip to England was
a whirlwind of activity and since we've gotten back we've hardly been
at home.  We've been to some amazing concerts since we've been back from
London. Dead Can Dance (an _awesome_ concert), Cocteau Twins (very good,
but I wish we'd seen them before DCD), Steve Tibbits, and Howard
Armstrong (aka "Louie Bluie"--he's a national treasure and since he's
81 years of age he may not be around that much longer), plus doing my
shows and working....well, I've hardly had time to catch my breath
since we've gotten back from England. Excuses....excuses....(sigh)

Thank you Valerie and Derek...
