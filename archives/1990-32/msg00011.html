 As the Kon has been fairly well covered by everyone else the only thing I can
add is this little Kate related tale.A friend went to the US group Circus Of

night talking to the bloke on the merchandise table,the company handling C.O.P

for the up coming Kate Bush Tour.Paul being Paul was blind drunk and didn't
remember any of this till I was talking to him the Wednesday after the Kon.

about 11 photos.Plenty of film,no batteries,and you couldn't get out to get
anymore,oh bugger.
