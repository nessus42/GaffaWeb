Surprise at Tower Mountain View

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Sat, 1 Dec 90 23:39:30 PST
Subject: Surprise at Tower Mountain View


The Mountain View branch of Tower Records has about ten copies
of the import CD of "This Woman's Work".  (The single, not the
box set.)  This is a surprise, since Tower has not traditionally
carried import Kate items.  They also had quite a few copies of
_Aspects of the Sensual World_ left.

The adjacent Tower Books had three copies of the _Visual Documentary_,
one of the few books on Kate that are worth buying.
