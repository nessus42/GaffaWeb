     Stephen seez (re Skippy the Bush Kanga):
     
     >I can't believe someone besides me actually remembers this show. It 
     >must have been late 60's when it was on. I remember watching it one 
     >morning at my grandparent's house and my grandfather walked in and 
     >asked, "Is that a goat?" My sister and I started laughing and 
     >singing, "Skippy, Skippy, Skippy the bush goat!!" Maybe you had to be 
     >there. 
     
     Yeh, we even got theat show over here (early 70's I think). But 
     speaking of wonderful old Aussie productions, does anyone remember 
     'The Magic Boomerang', or the even more obscure 'Woobinda - Animal 
     Doctor' - these were on TV here in the 60's. The boomerang was an old 
     coat-hangar. Time stood still for the person who threw it (too bad for 
     the flying doctor!). I'm sure there must be a song in here somewhere. 
     Woobinda (if I'm correctly remembering the spelling) was a Daktari 
     down under type of show -who remembers Daktari? -as an 8 yr old I 
     though Paula looked cute in Khaki.
