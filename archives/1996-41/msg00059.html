Speaking of gorgeous pictures of Kate, another one springs to mind which
might not be available (correct me if I'm wrong because I don't know for
sure). That is the stunning soft focus shot on the cover of the 12" UK
version of Experiment IV. It's full colour, takes up the entire cover,
and is a head and shoulders shot, with Kate looking directly at the
camera, expressionless. She looks serene, sensual, and exquisitely
beautiful. If the image cannot be found anywhere else, and anyone wants
it, I can _try_ and get it scanned in (it's bigger than A4 size) and
make it available. (Gaffaweb seems the obvious place again, if that is
OK with them?) Believe me, if you haven't seen it before, it is one of
the best pictures of Kate you will ever see!

BTW, the song title and her name are conveniently located in the top
corners of the picture so these could be omitted from the scan if
desired.
