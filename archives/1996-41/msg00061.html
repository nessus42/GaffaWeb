I think this comment could in some ways be applied to all of the albums. It
is easy to forget what the feeling was when you first got it. 

I still have fond memories of Lionheart and appreciate it today, if only 
because my sister had a huge Kate dressed up as a Lion photo on her wall that 
I spent many years trying to steal.

It was a long time ago in a place far less cynical and her music trashed
everything else on the day...

Ken.
