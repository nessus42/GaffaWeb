The CD is the UK release, catalogue number C2 93078.

The picture is black and white. As you look at it Kate would be looking
to your right. She has her mouth slightly open and her expression is
really difficult to describe. It isn't 'expressionless', but she isn't
'pulling a face' either. There seems to be a question in her eyes, but
that could just be my interpretation. Her hair slightly covers half of
her left eye. She has both hands just below chin height, one underneath
propping up the other. From the top one, which is palm upward, there
appears to be sand (or something similar) falling through her fingers.
Really, this is one of the most beautiful pictures of her that I have
seen.

If anyone has a copy of this picture or knows where to get it I would be
*really* interested. Perhaps Gaffaweb can track it down and scan it in
for everyone's benefit? :-)
