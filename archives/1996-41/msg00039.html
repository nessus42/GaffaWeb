> I'm a huge fan of both Tori and Kate, and I was amazed when the first

>   On to if Tori has acknowledged any influence.  I have read many, many
> interviews with Tori, and she has never mentioned Kate Bush.  I read a

> has never heard Tori's music, but heard that it was quite good.  I think
> that Kate was obviously an influence on Tori, but Tori is reluctant to
> admit it for some reason.


I agree with much of this assessment -- but have to add that 
Tori has mentioned Kate in a few interviews with varying degrees 
of praise. She thought that RuTH was a perfectly crafted record 
(and has recently taken to singing a line from it in concert). 
She's admitted that she and Kate "could probably sing each 
other's stuff" but asserted (hopefully jokingly) that Kate 
"smokes a lot more weed" than she does. And finally, she said 
that she wishes Kate would "stop screaming...there's no mouse."

Kate has heard a Tori song and said that it "was...umm...nice."
I don't know what that song was.
