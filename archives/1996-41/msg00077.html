Hi everyone out in KateLand. Just wanted to comment on the EMI 
Manhattan CDs. I bought two of them recently to update my old albums and 
to listen on my CD ROM at work. I bought The Dreaming and I was 
disappointed to find it didn't contain the photo or lyrics inside like 
the record did. I also just got TKI and surprise, it did have the lyrics 
and picture inside on the pullout pages although it didn't look like it 
from the back cover of the CD. 

	I'm not sure about the sound quality, I don't have another good 
recording to compare with although TKI sounds very good on my CD_ROM 
with just an inexpensive headset - (I'm listening to it at this moment) 
- and so does The Dreaming. But, I think before I buy anymore, except 
imports or special cds, I'll get TWW box set to have the rest. I 
understand TWW contains six disks up to TSW or just The Dreaming plus 2 
special disks?? Does someone know exactly?? 

	Also, in the Lionheart controversy, I want to say Lionheart 
ranks up at the top for me. I just think all the songs are so beautiful 
and sincere/innocent. It was my fourth KB album with the first being an 
EP with cuts from The Dreaming and NFE, (The Infant Kiss in French) and 
a KILLER live version of James & the Cold Gun), then NFE, TKI, & 
Lionheart. 

	Can anyone tell me about the tapetrees(???), I'm not sure what 
this is. Also, I'm still hoping someone out there can tell me about the 
Cathy Demos. I'd be eternally in your debt if someone could show me the 
way to these recordings.:)  Later.
