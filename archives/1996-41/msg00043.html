Well, I LOVE Donovan's music, but I must warn you that there are great 
extremes from Dylan-like folk to psychedelic flower-power music (I like 
this the best) to hard rock to jazz - you name it.  Donovan is laughed 
about alot because of his lyrics and such, but I find them very 
interesting - I think he had to have been stoned most of the time he 
wrote them.  Anyway, yes he is definitely worth checking out - perhaps 
his "greatest hits" package is a good start.  There is a 2-CD box set 
entitled "Troubadour" that covers most of his good stuff also.
