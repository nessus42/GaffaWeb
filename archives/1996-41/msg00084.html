Kate fans in or around the London area, who are looking to add to their
collection, might like to know that HMV in Oxford Street are currently
offering all Kate single CDs (excluding The Whole Story) for 7.99 UKP
each. This is the cheapest I have ever seen them, so grab 'em while you
can!

'The Dreaming' was not in stock when I looked, but TKI, NFE, HoL, TSW
(non-picture disc), and TRS were. I am not sure about LH (sorry - I was
in a bit of a hurry). I think there were only 3 or 4 copies each of NFE
and TSW.

As for TWS, it is cheaper than usual. (I think it was 11.99 UKP.)

[This information current as at 1 p.m. Tuesday 22nd October.]
