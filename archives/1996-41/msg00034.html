Hi folks.  I just joined the list recently, and decided to go ahead and 
update my worn Kate LPs with CD versions.  So far I have NFE, TD, and HOL 
from the EMI-Manhattan label, and as to be expected of EMI (the half-wits 
they are), they have excluded all lyrics and LP insleeve stuff like 
photos, credits, etc..  I am somewhat used to this but it never fails to 
really anger me that they would go ahead and reissue recordings but leave 
out such important info.  Just to save a few $ I guess - what dweebs!  Oh 
well, I am glad that Columbia (who put out TSW CD) didn't do the same.  I 
guess it is no big deal when you know all the lyrics and stuff by heart 
like most of us do, but for newcomers it is really a shame to miss out on 
the extras.  Sorry if this has been covered ad nauseum.....

Jeff
