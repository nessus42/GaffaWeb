On Tue, 6 Sep 1994, Michael R Osborne wrote:

>     Has anyone ever heard of a song titled "Desert Moon?"  I really
> would love to get it, but I don't know the group and can't find anything
> from the title.  It is old, maybe '88, I am unsure.  It was a love song,
> really good, and I just want to hear it again.  Thanks...

"Desert Moon" is on an album by the same name from Dennis DeYoung,
formerly of Styx.  1988 is about right.  I think your best bet for finding
it is a used record store, as I think this album has long since passed out
of the clearance bins.
