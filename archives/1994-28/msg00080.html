Just saw this on CyberSleaze:

* Rock stars are queuing up to contribute to EARTHRISE: THE RAINFOREST
ALBUM, an album to raise money for environmental causes. STING, U2,
PAUL MCCARTNEY, ELTON JOHN and PAUL SIMON have already recorded
tracks. Now two extra songs, SPIRIT OF THE RAINFOREST and YES YOU CAN,
has been taped. Spirit features an all-star cast, including BONNIE
RAITT, BRIAN WILSON, IGGY POP, DAVID GILMOUR, JON ANDERSON, B-52'S,
LENNY KRAVITZ, KATE BUSH, LL COOL J, RINGO STARR, DONNA SUMMER, JONI
MITCHELL and DEBORAH HARRY. And Yes You Can boast the combined talents
of JOE COCKER, MICHAEL MCDONALD, HERBIE HANCOCK, CHAKA KHAN, BRIAN MAY
and IAN ANDERSON from YES.

Kate and Iggy Pop on same song? Can't wait to hear this...
