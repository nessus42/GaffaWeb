Allan Balliett reports that there are still some tickets available for
the 5pm Sat Sept 10 (tomorrow!) showing of *The Line, The Cross, and
The Curve* in Shepherdstown, WV.  (Call 304-876-6818 for tickets.)

He also reports that he will have at least five and probably all nine
of the Fruitopia commercials to show in the video room on Saturday, in
addition to the 60 second Fruitopia movie theater commercial film.
Sony is providing a signed litho to be given away via drawing at the
5pm Sept 10 show and is providing at least one limited litho to be
given away at each of the other showings.  There will also be official
Red Shoes T-shirts and official Red Shoes buttons given away via
drawings at the 5pm shows -- and the other shows if supplies hold out.
There will be lots of free Fruitopia too.
