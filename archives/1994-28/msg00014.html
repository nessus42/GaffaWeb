Hey folks --

.. of course, it's in the _Alternative Music_ chart, but at least
"The Man I Love" is registering on a chart _somewhere_ in the USA.
Incidentally, Rolling Stone's Alternative Music chart (which used to
be compiled from playlists and recommendations from many "alternative"
radio stations around the country) is compiled based on the sales
figures of a single "alternative" music store -- the store is different
each issue.  This issue's store is located in Philadelphia, PA.  Now,
if I can just find a music store here in Boca Raton, FL that actually
carries import singles I'll be a happy guy....

-- Gregg!
