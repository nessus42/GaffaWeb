Tape tree- the organized distribution of copies of recorded material.
Usually video tapes (as here) or audio tapes...  Usually done cause the
people with the best equipment (and nicest ususally :)  make the best
copies...

Cloudbusting:
It's based on Wilhelm Reich's book The Book of Dreams..  it's been
out of press for some time (I think... it's been a while).  You may check
library booksales and other places that sell used paperbacks.  That's
where I got mine...  

And to answer your other question about the book Cathy: good luck!  I 
got mine directly from the publisher some years ago.  It was a pain getting
the international money order for 50 pounds....  Expensive too!
