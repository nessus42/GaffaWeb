Just finished listening to "Maria", the latest Jane Siberry
release.  *Oh my my*!!!!!!

She has split up this work in 2.  Kinda like HoL/TNW.  And
yes, "Oh my my" is quite interesting indeed.  This will
become one of my favorites for sure.  I listened to it once
and I haven't a clue of what's it about!   I love it!

Another trip into Jane's psyche... full of twists and turns
and unexpected stops and gos.

The first part, *Maria*, is a collection of jazz influenced
pieces.  Quite good. 
 
I STRONGLY recommend it.
