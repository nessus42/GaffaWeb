Hi, Vickie of Katefans and all who read this newsgroup:

Just a quick posting to tell one and all that the new kdlang album, called 
ALL YOU CAN EAT, will be out on 10 October.  I'm more than a little 
intrigued by the choice of title - any other time of year, especially just 
before April, I'd be tempted to think it's an April Fool's Day joke!  As 
it is, just have to wait for 10 October to get the album, listen to it 
before saying any more.  Altho' I don't know if it's the same release date 
worldwide or just 10 October for the States and Canada.

I used to read and occasionally post to this newsgroup when I was a grad 
student at UC San Diego.  Lost my on-line account when I dropped out of 
grad school, came home to Malaysia in 1991.  At that time, Internet 
facilities in Malaysia were restricted, but now easily available to anyone 
with the connections - both computer and money!

I look forward to more involvement with this newsgroup.
