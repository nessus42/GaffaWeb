I was wondering if Kate Bush heard of Tori Amos and what she thinks of 
her, since many think that Kate and Tori sound similar.  I have this 
dream that one day the two will collaborate a single, even though if I'm 
not mistaken, she hasn't collaborated with any women (whoops.  I forget 
that trio whose name slipped my mind on "Rocket's Tail" and other 
tracks.)  And not collaborate on just the vocals, but the songwriting, 
production, etc. as well.  By the way, Tori Amos says that she never 
listens to Kate Bush (much to my surprise.  I thought for the longest 
time she was the best demonstration of Kate's influence).  True, Tori is 
unique in her own right, (she and Kate are my two raves above every other 
rock artist I know of).

Anyway, I'm curious what Kate had to say, if anything.
