   Get a dish and aim it at Anik B1, transponder #6, MuchMusic. We used
to set a S-VHS tape and record six hours stretches of this channel when
I used to work in Kansas City. I'm sure that if this was my only music
channel I'd get bored by it, but it was much better than Empty-Vee.
Real VJs in a real set, actually talking in spaces between videos,
rather than recording all the talk in one 2-hour session for a 5-hour
show. Letters from real people and *requests*, rather than Empty-Vee's
"Dial MTV" stupidity. "The most popular videos of the day" insures
the lowest common denominator of taste. 

   Kinda makes you miss Night Flight. Oh sure, a pale imitation still
