This is something different.  The original compilation was called _It's
Christmas_, and was catalog number CD EMTV 49.  Both copyrights were 1989
and the CD was in a jewel case, not a digipack.  The original had 18
tracks, starting with John & Yoko, _Happy Xmas (War is Over)_ and ending
with Bing Crosby, _White Christmas_.  _December Will Be Magic Again_ is
track number 9 on the original.  The bongo version may well have been used
on the first compilation by mistake, so you should definitely check to see
which version this release contains.
