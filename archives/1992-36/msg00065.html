It has happened! I have a video clip of Kate that wasn't known to exist by
the Truefans around here. The sister of a friend of mine is a slight Katefan
since the early days (she was even in the Frankfurt Tour of Life concert,
just living around the corner of the place where it took place). As a fan,
she had recorded a show called WWF-Club where Kate performed a lip-synch
of RUTH. It seems for me to be a pretty normal lip-synch of that song, similar
to the two other ones I know. Unfortunately not the whole performance is on
the tape - you know the symptome: "where's that bl**dy empty tape?". Anyhow,
the WWF-Club was a regular show on the German TV station WDR some years ago.
This video recording is from a special that came from the September '85 IFA 
in Berlin. The IFA (Internationale Funkausstellung) is a very, very big fair 
that shows every second year the newest in hifi and video and always has a lot 
of people appearing on a lot of TV.

Someone please tell Andy that he should tell IED that he has to make an 
addition to his video list, since the WWF-Club doesn't appear on it. And
please do not shift all numbers up one place when you insert it. Use
sub-chapters, because otherwise any reference to any clip beyond the
insertion point had to include the list version.

Bye,

Uli
