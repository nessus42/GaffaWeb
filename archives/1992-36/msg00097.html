According to the latest ICE newsletter "December Will Be Magic Again"
is going to be on Volume 12 of On The Radio's promotional-only
_Rarities On Compact Disc_ series.  There's a total of 24 Holiday
tracks.  _RoCD_ is a series of rather rare tracks in promo form.  The
few that I've heard about all sound interesting but I've never seen
any of them anywhere... so good luck.
