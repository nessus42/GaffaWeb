     A local poster shop (Old Vic's) has a 5x5 foot poster of the "pixie
dust" Sensual World cover.  They were sold out of these $12 beauties, alas.
I went to this store for the first time, yesterday, so I didn't know what
to expect.  The first poster I saw was KaTe's leotard poster!  Someone who
works at the record store downstairs is a major Kate fan, so that might have
something to do with it.
     It obviously wouldn't fit on a door at 5x5.  It's _very_ big.  I just
hope they get more.  Naturally, they were sold out of the leotard poster too.
Alright, who's been stealing my posters!?! :-)
     If for some unbelievable reason you folks in the "big" cities can't
find such a poster, I could buy more than one for myself, and mail some
out.  If anyone's interested, drop me some email.
