The Tori Amos mailing list, Really-Deep-Thoughts, is now available
in digest format. This means that a concatenation of all the days
messages (minus some extraneous header information) is sent out
every 24 hours.

If you're interested in joining the list but don't want your mail
box cluttered with lots of little messages during the day, then
this may be a good option.

If you would like to subscribe to either the regular list or the
digest version please send mail to 

	really-deep-thoughts-request@gradient.cis.upenn.edu

or, for the terminally lazy,

	rdt-request@gradient.cis.upenn.edu

Be sure to specify if you want to be added to the digest version.

-Anthony (owner and of the Tori Amos mailing list)
