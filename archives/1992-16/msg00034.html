Minnie Riperton (as I've always seen it spelled) died in the late
seventies or early eighties, I believe of cancer or some such.  She
had a hit, "Loving You," in the seventies (I think that's her
"best-known" song, so to speak).  

Nowadays, I hear about her the most when people are comparing Mariah
Carey's vocal range to hers -- Minnie had an astronomical soprano
voice.
