The song on the tape is the Billy Bragg song A New England. He cribbed the
first two lines from Paul Simon. Billy wrote to Simon and asked him if
he minded - I cannot remember whether he got a reply. Kirsty did a good
cover but the original is better (IMHO).

I can highly recommend Billy Bragg to anyone who appreciates good songwriting.
N.B. his voice is not to everyone's liking and his guitar playing would not
get him a place in Dire Straits' line-up! Nevertheless he is still brilliant.
