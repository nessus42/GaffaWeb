This is to announce the formation of the new Tori Amos mailing list:
Really-Deep-Thoughts.

The address to subscribe to the mailing list is:

	really-deep-thoughts-request@gradient.cis.upenn.edu

(or, for the very lazy, rdt-request.gradient.cis.upenn.edu).

The address to post to the mailing list is:

	really-deep-thoughts@gradient.cis.upenn.edu

The purpose of the list is to discuss and disseminate information
about Tori Amos and her music, and anything else that seems relevant.

-Anthony
