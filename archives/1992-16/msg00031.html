Well, as will surprise no one, the Seattle concert was wonderful.  Despite 
the fact that Amtrak was 1.5 hours late (coming up from Portland), we got 
to the Backstage about 30 min. ahead of the start time (thanks, Kim!), and 
enjoyed ourselves completely.  She was in fine voice and had lots of fun with 
the crowd.

Afterwards, I was able to meet her and ask for her autograph, and receive 
-- and return! -- "a great big hug" :)  Made my year.  She's just a warm, 
friendly, lovely and immensely talented lady.  (But so tiny!)
