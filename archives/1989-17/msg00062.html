Just saw Jefferson Airplane (yes, you're reading it right, and NO,
I DO NOT MEAN STARSHIT or ANYTHING even remotely resembling
Jefferson Starship (not to be confused with Starshit - I always thought
"Kneedeep in the Hoopla" summed it up for them)) but I digress...

JEFFERSON AIRPLANE WAS ***AMAZING***!!!  Check them out when they
come to your town - they had a fabulously tight stage show,
played a great mix of old an (very good) new stuff (their new
album was released yesterday).  They opened with "She Has Funny Cars"
from the _Surrealistic Pillow_ album, did "Somebody to Love", 
lots of other favorites ("White Rabbit", of course, and closed
with "Volunteers" - though they did "Crown of Creation" in an 
AWESOME encore).  Oh, yeah, they did some "Hot-Fucking Tuna", too!!

WOW WOW WOW WOW WOW WOW FUCKING-AMAZING-AND-AWESOME-LIKE-TOTALLY-FER-SHURE!
