I have noticed that most of the people I know in the:

	SCA/Renn Faire/Computers/Technical/Folkie... etc.

genre seem to like both Kate Bush and Jethro Tull.  I would like
to know why people out there think this is.  Is it the complicatedness
and thickness of their arrangements?  Is it the fact that there's
very weird poetry in them?  That they are often highly electronic?
That there's often "statements" in them?  That they are "different"?

What do you people think?  Is there a connection here?
