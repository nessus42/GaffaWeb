This is a press release for several showings of Kate Bush's movie *The
Line, The Cross, and The Curve* that will happen in the DC area.  The
main showing, with door prizes and a live DJ, will be at 5pm on
Saturday, September 10.

			      KATE BUSH
		  THE LINE, THE CROSS, AND THE CURVE
	   Directed by Kate Bush / Screenplay by Kate Bush
       Staring Kate Bush, Miranda Richardson, and Lindsay Kemp
 
	    Exclusive Washington-Baltimore Area Screening
		      5pm Saturday, September 10

		     DOOR PRIZES WILL BE OFFERED
	   COURTESY OF INTERGALACTIC GARAGE AND SONY MUSIC!

There will also be a 3pm matinee and a 9:30pm late show on Saturday,
Sept 10, a 9:30pm late show on Friday, September 9, and a 3pm matinee
and 9:30pm late show on Sunday, September 11.
 
Presented by INTERGALACTIC GARAGE (Worldwide CDs and Music
Collectibles) in cooperation with SONY MUSIC and the Shepherdstown
Opera House and its state-of-the-art JBL soundsystem at: The
Shepherdstown Opera House 131 W. German Street Shepherdstown, West
Virginia (just 8 miles north of site for 1993-94 Lollapaloza, minutes
from I-70 and I-81).
 
All Seats $10  TICKETS ON SALE NOW!
 
ADVANCE TICKET SALES (VISA and MasterCard accepted)
Intergalactic Garage
110 W. German Street
Shepherdstown, WV 25443
(304) 876-6818 (12-10 pm 7 days)
FAX (304) 876-9414
 
MAIL ORDERS:
Intergalactic Garage

Tickets, maps and visitor info will be sent by return mail.  A $1
handling fee applies to each ticket order mailed.  (Ticket handling
fee is limited to $1 per order, regardless of number of tickets.)

SEE YOU THERE!

P.S. Shepherdstown is a fun place, with lots to do and see.  It is
close to the Antietam (Civil War) Battlefield, the C&O Canel, a
bikepath, and several worldclass restaurants! PLUS - the famous
INTERGALACTIC GARAGE STREETLEVEL!  Not to mention the Apalachian
Hammered Dulcimer Festival is the same weekend (here in Sheppytown).
