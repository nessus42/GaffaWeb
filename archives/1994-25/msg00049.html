 In GARDEN01, Peter FitzGerald-Morris' chronology of Kate's career states
 that Kate was born at Bexleyheath Maternity Hospital.

 In GARDEN05, the FlexiPop magazine article from September 1982 states that
 she was born in Bexley (on July 17th, no less).

 Also in GARDEN05, the interview at the end of the first Kate Bush Club
 Newsletter states that she was born in a nursing home in Erith.

 As the last reference supposedly came straight from Kate herself, I have
 to assume that it is the correct version.  As Kate has referred several
 times to having been born and raised in Kent, I suppose something must have
 changed over the intervening decades as her birthplace and school are now
 inside the Greater London boundary.  Anyone able to shed any more light on
 this?
