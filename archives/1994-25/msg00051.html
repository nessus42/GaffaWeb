Hi: I'm a irregular Gaffa-reader with no own news-access (That's why 
I'm using mr. Henricsons account). However, yesterday (Wed 17th) I 
had my radio tuned to one of the new commercial radio stations here 
in Stockholm, Sweden; the one that calls itself Bandit 105.5 - The 
Rock Home of Stockholm (catch the rhyme...). It plays rock and the 
presentation is in English. And to my utter surprise, yesterday they 
played _two_ Kate Bush songs! First Running Up That Hill in the late 
morning, and later in the early evening, they played Army Dreamers. 
Probably a station worth listening to ;-)
A tip maybe for visitors in and residents of Stockholm.
