AWP (not sounding dumb at all!) writes:

> P.S. I've been looking for these Happy Rhodes C.D's. Have they been 
> released in England? If not, how do I get them? Excuse me if I'm 
> sounding a little dumb here.

They haven't been released in England (or the rest of the UK, or
Europe) but they can be found in London at the HMV-Oxford St. store.
When we were there in May, I saw CDs in the "H" section and the "R"
section.  This was the regular rock/pop section, not the imports.

Some of Happy's albums are also available via cdeurope. Telnet to
cdeurope.com for more info.

You can always get them directly from Happy herself too.  Let me know
if you'd like more informationa about that.
