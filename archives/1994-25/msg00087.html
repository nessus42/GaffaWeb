Um, too late.  I gave IG the address of the guy who made the shirts.  Whether
or not he goes ahead and actually does anything, I don't know; except for
giving him the phone number & address, I have no part in this.

I did not do this hastily - I asked the opinions of a few people who've
flamed IG and/or C-side in the past; the common opinion seemed to be: yeah,
they're scum, but there IS a demand for more shirts (probably a LOT more
once school starts again and/or the Compuserve folks discover us), yet
NOT A SINGLE LOVEHOUND came foreward to do up more shirts!  (OK, *ONE* did,
which gave me the idea in the first place, but had to back down for some
very good reasons).  I thought C-side was on The Net, but was wrong.  IG
will (probably) do up more shirts, which is better than none at all.

(The following is to EVERYONE on Lovehounds/rec.music.gaffa):

Don't want IG to profit from us?  Fine; there are several courses of action
you can take:

o  You can STILL make up some more of "my" award-winning Official Lovehounds 
   shirts!  Email me how!
o  You can make your own design.  No one's stopping you.
o  Just don't buy 'em from IG if you don't like the price!
o  Or you can say "To hell with it" and buy one from 'em.
o  If you don't do any of the above, you have no right to bitch.
