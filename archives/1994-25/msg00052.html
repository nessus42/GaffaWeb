Well, the new Davy Spillane album (entitled "A Place Ammong The Stones") 
is out (in the UK at least), and, as has already been rumoured here, the 
cover of "Sexual Healing" with KaTe on lead vocals, which was played at 
the Konvention, has been left off.

To be honest I feel that song would have been rather out of place, given 
the nature of the album as released. It is mostly instrumental (7 tracks 
out of 10) and musically is a sort of Celtic folk/new age/jazz/AOR melange. 
All the tracks are written or cowritten by DS. The guest vocalists on 
the three songs are Maire Brennan (of Clannad), who cowrote the (title)
track, Steve Winwood and Sean Tyrrell (whoever he is).

I wonder what will happen to the recording of "Sexual Healing" now.

Geoff Parks
