I remember.. in the ancient mysts of the last decade (81-82 something like 
that)... way back when there were so few videos, MTV used to play the same 
ones every six or seven hours... I remember seeing Kate in a indian motif for 
"Running Up that Hill"... it was THE FIRST TIME I'D EVER SAW HER. I remember 
some back up persons, 5 or 6, with flag and one man with a snare drum... 
beating out the ryhmm... brat-bratta-brat bratta-bratta-brat-brat... and Kate 
being KATE. I was just coming down off that when, no lie, I turn on USA and 
their running the Kate Interview. IT was edited as I remember but I saw most 
of Cloudbusting, very very cool... I would SO MUCH like to have that Top of 
the Pops visit, I just didn't have my VCR together at the time.
