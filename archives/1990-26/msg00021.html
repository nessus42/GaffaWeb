Vickie here. Now for my Chicago shows. In a way these are easier because
I'm starting from scratch and can play anything I want, not having to
worry about how many times I've played it in the past or when was the
last time. Then again, it's harder because I feel that WZRD's listeners
have already heard EVERYTHING and I can't possibly suprise them. I also
feel that I should keep away from anything even slightly mainstream.
WZRD is 1000 times more alternative than KKFI and there are artists
that I wouldn't feel comfortable playing. All I can do is do my best and
that's all I can do. Sharp eyes will notice a lot of songs that appeared
on my recent Kansas City playlists. I confess to a certain amount of
laziness and also think that good songs in neat segues can be repeated.
Only you know the truth. :-) Onward....
