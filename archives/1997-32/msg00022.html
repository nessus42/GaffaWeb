Japanese, eh? My English version has the following:
Leaving out the six main albums, the separate booklet contains:
IFC:
Has it really been twelve years already?...
Is this how it was for Rip Van Winkle?

Looking through these photos I haven't seen for years, and listening to
B-sides of singles I'd forgotten about, has been an interesting exercise
for me.

I think my music and I have changed a lot, and when I was making the
first album, which at that time was my greatest goal, I never imagined
that a box-set of ny work would be out in 1990 and would still be making
albums. That's a nice feeling.

Whoever you are - thank you for listening and please
be kind to my mistakes.... because I'm not.

IBC:
Many, many thanks to EVERYONE involved in making these albums,
and to the photographers whose work appears here:-
Gered Mankowitz
Terry Walker
Brian Griffin
Guido Harari
and especially John Carder Bush

I would like to dedicate this special edition to the special memory of
Alan Murphy and Gary Hurst.
