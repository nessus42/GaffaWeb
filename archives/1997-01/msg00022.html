ariusz@byd.ternet.pl (Ariusz Nowak) wrote:

>Everybody, Kate Bush fans, please answer me !
>Do you like the album version of HoL or the Alternative version ?
>Thank you

>I like the album version, but for me it was always too short. So I was
>glad to hear the Alternate version. IMHO that version is very good. It's 
>not just the extended album version but it's quite different. When I
listen to
>it I like to think that it was made just for fun. Kate seems to have a
very good
>time singing it.

Obviously EMI America thought the album version was too short, too. When
they released the single in the U.S. (with a photo sleeve that was the
same photo as the HoL cover but with a frame), they included an extended
mix of the track on the b side.

It's a re-edited (not remixed) version of the lp version of HoL that goes
on for about four minutes (obviously, there's some repetition). I don't
think it's some great lost item but it is an interesting curiousity for
the hard-core fan. I occassionally see this 7" single at record
conventions in the U.S
