>     How many of you can honestly say that you don't hear something >"new" each time you listen to a piece of Kate's work?
>

One night I was really tired, so I just sprawled out on my bed and 
played the Dreaming album really loud . . . I was captivated once 
again.  I know the songs so well, but I heard nuances that had 
previously escaped me.  And just thinking about the incredible 
*process*--that Kate dreamed up these sounds and had the ability to 
create them on tape!  Incredible!!! (Or should I say WOW, 
unbelieveable!)

You can do this with any of her albums, really.  (And I have!)
