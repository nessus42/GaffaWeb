I didn't get response back from everyone I sent some holiday shruberries to
but assuming I got all that I'm gonna get, I found the travel times quite
interesting.  I mailed out cassettes in eight padded envelopes on the afternoon
of Mon 16 Dec from a rural post office near Birmingham, Alabama.
- The first one was received in Texas by Wed 18 Dec.  Understandable, since it
was pretty much a straight shot down Interstate Hwy #59 and #10, and Houston
makes a big sucking noise.
- The last one (not counting the CD to Elizabeth, which may have ended up on
a returning pineapple boat to Aloha Tower) finally got to Ontario yesterday,
02 Jan. I dunno, maybe they only do the border crossings at night when there is no moon. Or worse, it goes by Amtrak to the Canadian

belateds in the next week or two.
