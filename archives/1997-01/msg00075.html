Other love-hounds wrote:
> > How many of you can honestly say that you don't hear something "new" 
> >each time you listen to a piece of Kate's work?

>Indeed, each Kate Bush song is a gift, yet it is a *wrapped* gift that >is in need of constant unwrapping!  So hasten to your CD players, there >are surprises awaiting you all!

A couple suggestions for people who might feel like they've soaked up
EVERYTHING they can from Kate's previous output:

1) fuck with your graphic equalizer and listen to the albums again,
especially TD and HOL. You're bound to hear them through new ears.

2) drop your walkman repeatedly until it starts playing backwards and
then listen to your favorite kate mix tapes. this happened to me
accidentally and the songs have never seemed "old" since.
