This post was quite a coincedence for me. I just finished reading 'A
Book Of Dreams' on New Years. I thought that any book that would inspire
Kate to create a song and video of that calibre would be worth reading.

good book. I just had a little trouble keeping a straight face with the
places in the book where the flying saucers are mentioned ;-).

As she has said in her interviews, most of the book is written in a
childs perspective so only minimal details were given about Orgone
Energy and the cloudbusters. It sparked my interest enough to want to
know a little more about Wilhelm Reich's work and the OBRL site looks
like a good place to learn more.
