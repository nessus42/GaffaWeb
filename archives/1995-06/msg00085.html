    The errors on bootlegs are possibly some sort of lame attempt to
avoid prosecution for copyright infringement. Often the label has
different titles than the jacket. I think this is because it is easier
to find printers than CD plants, thus easier to find one willing to
print material they know to be bootleg. The CD plant, on the other
hand has more to lose. The changed/mangled song titles give
them an "out," no matter how lame. Someone who would press
a Kate bootleg *cannot* possibly be as stupid as some of these
people appear to be.
