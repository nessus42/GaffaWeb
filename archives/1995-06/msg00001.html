There are several typos on various releases of Kate's work, but I find it 
a little bit of a stretch to think she had a personal hand in typesetting 
any of her album covers.  While she may have worked with the art crew to 
design covers, pick typefaces, etc., she sure ain't sitting there typing 
the text in!

One of the more notable typos is the 12" Canadian single of The Big Sky 
which was released as "Astrological Mix" rather than "Meteorological Mix" 
-- quite an enormous typo if you ask me.  I believe a similar mistake may 
have been made with the Cloudbusting single, but now I can't remember.
  
Anyone out there have a running tally of typos?
