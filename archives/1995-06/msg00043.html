Under the Ivy is the B-side to the 12" of Running Up that HIll, and is 
one of my favourite Kate songs. If you can't find the single (it should 
be around used), it's included on the bonus CDs that come with TWW-the 
boxed set, which will put you back some $150. The lyrics are:

It wouldn't take me long
To tell you how to find it
To tell you where we'll meet
This little girl inside me
Is retreating to her favourite place
Go into the garden, go under the ivy
Under the leaves, away from the party
Go right to the rose
Go right to the white rose for me.

I sit here in the thunder
The green on the grey, I feel it alround me
And it's not easy for me to give away a secret
It's not safe
But go into the garden
Go under the ivy
Under the leaves, away from the party
Go right to the rose
Go right to the white rose for me
It wouldn't take me long to tell you how to find it.
