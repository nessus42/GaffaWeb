Peter Chow from Brighton (UK) adds:

It may be an official one but not the same as the UK issue. The UK one
has no T-shirt. I seem to remember that CBS/Sony had a different one made up. 
The UK one would not have been sent to the States as the video is PAL format 
and as such is unplayable on US NTSC equipment.

The video contains a copy of the US and UK versions of the RBG clip and a
clip from a UK chat show "Aspel & Company" featuring Kate "performing"
MoP (she played live to the studio audience but the broadcast output was dubbed
from the CD).
