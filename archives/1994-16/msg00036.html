Trevor Horn got started with the Buggles (Video Killed The Radio
Star), and was briefly in Yes on the Drama album. He's gone on to
fame and fortune as a member of The Art Of Noise and, especially, as
a record producer. He coproduced Yes' 90125, and produced numerous
pop albums such as Grace Jones' Slave To The Rhythm and the eponymous
debut by Seal.

However, I have no idea who Betsy Cook is. :(
