hi gang, i'm back!  betcha thought (hoped) i was gone for good.  well, both
|>oug and Stev0 have forwarded me articles about recording techniques used
on The Red Shoes wondering why I haven't said anything.  truth is, i have a
new job, and my email situation is pretty flaky.  also, there's not much to
say.  The Red Shoes sounds bad.  period.

i don't know what the deal is with pop music these days but most of it
sounds unbearably awful.

we just got a set of $5000 Meyer monitors for our studio and they do not
lie.  the red shoes sounds like shit.  the new enigma album sounds
incredibly good.  i don't like it much musically, but you can't deny that
the sound quality is top-notch.  anyone who doubts is welcome to come over
for a little round of comparison shopping.

i'm still not on love-hounds so if you want to flame me, make sure you cc a
copy here.

/jon
