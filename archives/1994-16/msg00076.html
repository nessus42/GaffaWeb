released two lemon kittens albums on disc in the uk: _the big dentist..._ 
and _we buy a hammer for daddy_. the lemon kittens are a way off-kilter 
pop sort of thing not for the faint of heart. patented weirdness. if you
like early danielle dax, it's likely that you'll appreciate the lemon
kittens. as for me, if i had heard the lemon kittens before i heard amy
denio, i would have thought that amy reminded me of them. however, it
was the other way around....
