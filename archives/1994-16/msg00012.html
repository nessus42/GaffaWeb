In a small bit about Danielle Dax's new album Pop Eyes, Dirk Preston in
Axcess magazine said:

 "...Pop Eyes features cover art that was originally considered so horrible
 it was banned in the UK.  Featuring a grinning face composed of torn out,
 cut-up images made from various parts of infected, bleeding, rotted
 flesh--a far cry from the usual Kate Bush-esque frills and glamour that
 many female songwriters can't seem to escape." 

Indeed, the small picture of the album cover is not pretty, but it sounds 
like an interesting album.
