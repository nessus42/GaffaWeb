   The song _This Woman's Work_ was written for John Hughes' film
_She's Having A Baby_. It was originally only available on the
soundtrack of that film. It later appeared on her album _The Sensual
World_. When the anthology was created, "This Woman's Work" was a
perfect description of the contents of a box set of (almost) all of
Kate's work. Which one did you mean?
