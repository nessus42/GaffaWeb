I agree completely. Del knows a certain amount about recording, but
he's not really a great engineer. The bit about learning to compress
Kate's vocals from "Ardman" (aka Hardiman) is an example. Yes, KB's
vocals have always been heavily compressed, but *nothing* like the
sound Palmer introduced on TSW, where you can hear the bloody
compressor pumping! This guy needs to turn over the console to
someone qualified, and get back to playing bass - something he was
really good at.
