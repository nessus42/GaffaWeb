A correction: the full title of the story is: "You know They Got a
Hell of a Band". Before any net.cop starts complaining about Missing
KaTe KontenT, here it is: the story is in an anthology called "Shock
Rock" edited by Jeff Gelb. In the starting pages there's a list of
artists, bands, etc that are credited as a source of inspiration, and
Kate Bush is included. However, I don't think King's story is really
the one inspired by a Kate song, but a story called "Odeed" by David
J. Schow. I don't tell which song I think it's based on, because that
would be a major spoiler if you want to read the story.
