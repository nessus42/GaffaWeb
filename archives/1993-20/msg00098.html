Recently the good folks at Home Ground posted a newsflash about Kate's 
new album called Red Shoes.  One of the "stars" to be guesting on it 
will be "Prince". I don't mean to be picky, but that's not his name 
anymore.  According to the local paper, "Prince" now wishes to be 
refered to by the symbol that was the title for his last album.  It 
combines the symbols for male and female.  As far as I know, there is 
no alphabetic equivalent for this symbol, so I suppose "Prince" is 
still the best way to refer to him, but I thought I'd make the point. 


        Scott "Useless Information" Frank
