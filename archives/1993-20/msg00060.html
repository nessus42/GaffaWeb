Hi Andrea!

There is quite a reasonable explanation for this. Kate's song 'The Dreaming'
is about Australian Aborigines, and the title of the song comes from their
'religious' (for want of a  better word) beliefs of how they came into
the world etc etc .... Thus 'The Dreaming' is an expression  that predates
Kate's song title somewhat, and hence it's appearance on a book about
Australia is perfectly reasonable. :)

What is in the book incidentally ... ? Is it mainly on the Aboriginal
culture, or is it a more generic book about all things Australian ... ?
