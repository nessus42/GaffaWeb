Could anyone say exactly what Her Kateness is saying at
the end of 'Waking the Witch' - "this blackbird - has a
stone around it?"  - Sounds like that but I don't think
so (but it certainly would fit the context of the song -
"help this blackbird - someone help this blackbird" .
I have gotten down the lines of the priest - "What is it
my child - you are a witch -- woman, I question your
innocence! -- I damn you woman! -- What say you good 
people (guilty - guilty - guilty!).....   

I really love this song - it seems to speak to the 
repression of female spirituality - the repression of
the witch in all of us.  Specifically - the Catholic
Church and it's fear and repression of it.... but that
subject is too deep to get into over the Internet...
