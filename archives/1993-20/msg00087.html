Hello Friends,
 
A slight correction to the newsflash re "Aspel and Co." The programme
tends to be variable in time of transmission. It may start earlier than
10.30pm. The "Radio Times" that will tell us the actual time will not
be published until Tuesday.
 
I'm told as this is the last of the series, tickets are not so easy
to come by. HOMEGROUND has managed to get three. Others who apply straight
away may be lucky.
 
Regarding the gaffa-archive CD-ROM, I see reference to "the HOMEGROUND
archives" which are invited for inclusion. I'm not sure what is meant here.
HOMEGROUND has only recently been prepared on word processor. There
is very little on disc. The only stuff I have begun to retype is the
"Five Years Ago" series, and progress is pretty slow since I have little 
time for that work. Clarification from the proponents of the idea please!
