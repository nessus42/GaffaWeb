Jennifer & Sharon (Jenshar@cris.com) wrote:
: I'm 26.  I first heard Running Up That Hill when I was about fifteen and
: *gasp* didn't like it.  I discovered the error of my opinion in 1991 when
: someone I met in France encouraged me to check out more of her songs.  I
: bought all of her albums within a month.

I didn't hear any Kate until I was 18 or so.  Someone was playing "The Big
Sky" in a local record shop and I was in love.  But, of course, I left and
forgot to ask who the artist was.

I found out later and put off buying an album until I'd gotten my first CD
player, when I was 20.  The first CD I bought for it was _The Whole Story_.
Instant Love.  Now I'm 25 and none of my friends can understand my Kate 
infatuation.  Oh well.

Viel Gluck!
