Hello, all.

    I am a 46 yr. old Kate Bush fan and proud of it.
IMHO, no-one else has ever produced such searingly 
beautiful music and it always amazes me how few 
people appreciate her talents.

    I am also a musician and have played guitar and
sung in bands for nearly thirty years.  Even so, I am
not a *good* musician but just enjoy expressing 
myself through actually being on stage and playing.

    The appreciation of music is a very subjective thing 
and each of us has a different perspective of what is 
heard.  Somehow, it saddens me that even though we 
might enjoy the same piece of music we are hearing 
and feeling differently.


Patiently waiting,
