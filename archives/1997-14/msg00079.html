Hey Love-hounds:

   Here's a lurker's little story -- I first heard Kate in Peter 
Gabriel's "Don't Give Up" when I bought his _Shaking the Tree_ CD.. and 
I really liked the voice of Kate.. a good compliment to Peter.  Then, in 
1993 when TRS came out, I was just trying out new artists.. and I 
decided to buy it on a whim.  On first listen, I was not drawn in, but 
upon many listens grew to love it.  And the rest is history!  I will be 
20 on July 1, and I guess I would have been 16 when I first caught on.  
My $0.02!
