>     By the way, are Tish Hinojosa and Sheila Chandra people that we in the

Sheila Chandra is British, by way of the Indian subcontinent.  In fact,
before she made her singing debut with a UK-based band called Monsoon, she
had an earlier career as an actress and appeared as a regular in a British
television series -- I think it may have been Grange Hill, or some other
program that had a lot of young actors in the cast.

I think all her most recent releases are on Peter Gabriel's Realworld
label, though she's recorded a lot of material for a lot of other labels.
It's mostly very "challenging" stuff -- with the double-edged meaning that
term implies -- very conceptual and not really pop oriented.

N.B.: the above is written entirely from memory; if I've offended any
hardcore Chandra fans with my fuzzy recollection of the facts, my
apologies.  I've got a memory like a, a, one of those things with lots of
holes in it, you use them for draining pasta, it's right on the tip of my
tongue...
