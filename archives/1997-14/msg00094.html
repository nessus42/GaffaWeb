I'm 24 and was first introduced to Kate's music in Nov. 1985, when I
heard "Running Up That Hill" on the radio. I went out the next month and
bought HOL and loved it. (I was only 12 and the 9th Wave freaked me out
then but I grew to love it). I REALLY got into Kate's music in 1990 when
I bought everything on cassette only to find the next week that a CD box
set (This Woman's Work) had just been released. Oh well...more money
lost but nice rarities gained :) I did put the tapes to good use though,
by placing them in a 10 tape holder and labelling it "Kate Kase" for all
to see!
