To: love-hounds@uunet.UU.NET (the rec.music.gaffa address)

	This info from a press release of new releases from EMI Music
Canada (their new "official" name):
	The TWW box set is being re-issued on CD only, but they still
have "limited cassettes in stock now!"

	Cat. No.	Sugg. List	Bar Code	Order

	KBX4 95237	$146.98		7777 95237 4	<blank>
	KBX2 95237	$146.98		7777 95237 2	<blank>

	(Don't ask me what that chart is supposed to mean, I just copied
it straight out of the booklet.)
	The release dates are given as follows:

	Order:	June 7, 1993
	Retail:	June 18, 1993

	The release also decribes the set in some detail, gives a complete
track listing, with info on where each song came from (B-sides of what
single), and has the picture from the TWW booklet of the "Sat in Your
Lap" dunce-cap Kate.  The headline, "FINALLY!" is emblazoned across the
top, so presumably there has been some fan demand for this re-issue.
