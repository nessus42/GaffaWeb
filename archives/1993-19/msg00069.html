haven't been keeping up to date!......so  I went digging through my old 
vinyls and I found it.  It has a two covers. The inner one has the Ninth 
Wave photo on it and the outter cover has the Hounds of Love photo on it. 
The outter cover is a wrap around sheet which is tied together by a pink 
ribbon that goes through the cover.  I hadn't listened to this one in a 
while but I really like it.  There is also an interview with Kate on the 
other side.
