"The Sweeney" was a popular British 70's police TV drama. "Sweeney
Todd" being the Cockney rhyming slang for "Flying Squad", a police
crime investigation unit. Note how KaTe mimes drawing a pistol during
the video and Hammersmith Odeon performance when she sings this line.
Coincidently an episode of The Sweeney was on BBC2 a couple of nights ago.
