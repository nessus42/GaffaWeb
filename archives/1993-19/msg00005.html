In the July issue of Tower _Pulse!_, they give the results of the
readers' poll from a few issues back; _Hounds of Love_ charted at
#17.  Tori's _Little Earthquakes_ made it to #37.  BTW, the rules
limited it to releases between 1983-93, the 10 years of _Pulse!_'s
existence.

There were also a couple of KaTe-quotes from previous interviews 
in the magazine, all part of the 10 year retrospective.
