                  NO! NO! NO! NO! NO! NO!

I've never heard ANY Joni Mitchell that sounds anything like Victoria!
I'll be the first to admit that I'm not exactly a Joni Mitchell fan,
though I admire and respect her DEEPLY. However, it's really getting
tiresome to hear people compare artists such as Jane Siberry and
now Victoria Williams to her.
Joni is the most talented of the "60s Women" and the most innovative
and the most experimental. I give her all kinds of credit for opening
doors and minds, but I just don't hold with these comparisons. If you're
going to compare Victoria to anybody, compare her to very early Dolly Parton.
I've heard Victoria described as "Dolly Parton on acid". Close...!

THANK YOU woj for printing the press release for Victoria's album.
THANK YOU! THANK YOU! THANK YOU!
