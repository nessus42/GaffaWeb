Nena recorded an album after _99_Red_Balloons_ called "All In The Game".
It's a good album, though not as moving as _99_ which is one of my favourite
tracks of all time.  Nena has (hopefully not 'had') an excellent and varied
voice, though the (light) rock backing musicians may not be the average
love-hound's cup of tea (not that any love-hound is average, of course :-).
