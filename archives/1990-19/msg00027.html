KATEMAS 1990.  The Northwest's version of how to celebrate Kate's
coming into existence to astound us with miraculous art.

All true Kate fans are welcome.  28 July 90, 4 pm "until MikeK says
so", come see rare videos, here rare music, and see and hear not so
rare videos and music at the one and only KateHouse.  This party
features a vegetarian bbq.  Bring partiables, and, of course, your
own bottle.

Last year our Katemas attracted 35 people, and we were mentioned,
thanks to Steve Schonberger, in _Homeground_ and, probably thanks
to Andrew Marvick (?), in the Los Angeles _Times_.

Devotion to Kate and appreciation of her art has kept our club
going for 30 months, and will make this Katemas season a joyous
one.

Again, 4 pm, 28 July, KateHouse.  Write or call if you want:
