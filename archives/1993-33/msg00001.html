Your Tower Records may have just gotten this album in the last two weeks,
but I bought in back the first week of June when it was released.
It is a very good album as long as you're listening to it as background 
music.  If you listen to it attentively, you realize as has been said,
how inane the lyrics are.  Also, one of the songs (I don't remember which
one--Once in a Lifetime?) is a dead ringer for Madonna's Like a Prayer.
Not to say its bad--I just find myself singing the wrong lyrics.
