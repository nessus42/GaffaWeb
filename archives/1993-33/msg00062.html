I just picked up the CD Single of _Rubberband Girl_ and ... well ... I 
think I unlike it.  I can't say that I like it, and I really can't say 
that I dislike it...I'm more or less feelingless towards it.

To me, it sounds like a song that anyone could be singing on the radio...
nothing special in the arrangement, nothing special in the lyrics.

Had it not come out from Kate, I doubt that it is something that I would
have ever bought...(cover art is ok though...)

I will agree with the net that _Big Stripey Lie_ has potential.

Just my $0.02...
