Hi all,

    Well I've had my copy of RBG for 4 days now and (fortunately) had the
chance to listen to it for all that time BEFORE I got back to read some gaffa
this morning. I like to try and formulate my own ideas and feelings for music
before I let others start to influence me.

    Cover: WOW, don't you just love it! When Stephen coined it the 'ninja KaTe'
picture I had to agree with him.

    Rubber Band Girl & RBG (Extended): To those who seem to be having second
thought about this one I can only say this. Listen to it A LOT!! Listen to it
LOUD!! I can just imagine KaTe in a wild dance to this one. It just contains so
much energy.

    Big Stripey Lie: The KaTe I remember from TD and THoL. What a great song.
It even has LOTS of KaTe screams (which I live for). I want MORE of this.

Now for some questions
=====================

    Can somebody put out a list of ALL the known releases for the new album?
I've got the RGB CD Single and vinyl single (had could resist getting it too).
How many ETM releases are there? What about this limited addition thing I hear
about?? ... For my music pusher to get them for me I need to tell him just what
to get!
