Well I purchased Eat the Music single yesterday and it was refreshing to 
hear new Kate music.  I am one of those people who love everything she
has put out over the years and am looking forward to the Red Shoes Video
(anyone have a release date on this?).

Anyway, I called the local (Detroit Area) so called ALTERNATIVE radio 
station and spoke to their British DJ (unusual his accent is not as
strong over the phone as it is on the radio) and  he first said they don't
have the single yet, then he said we have it but need to discuss it.  So much 
for cutting edge.  I am disappointed and have decided not to listen to 
this station no great loss, I just don't think they realize how many KaTe
followers there are in this area (Ann Arbor).
