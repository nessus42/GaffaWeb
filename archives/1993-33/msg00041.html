> Since then Sony Music took over Columbia (I'm speculating

This is near enough right.  More to the point, Sony bought the whole
of the Columbia organisation, movies, videos, music labels, the lot. q
The allocation of the Columbia label to Sony Music was a relatively
minor part of the assimilation.  The main target is understood to have
been the back catalogue of movies whose release on future video
formats Sony can now control.
