Most likely to be Gary Brooker. The singer, pianist, composer, and
general leading light of Procol (note spelling) Harum. He's a good
mate of Eric Clapton's which may be how come he gets to appear on the
new KaTe. He's made at least three solo albums since Procol folded and
last year (may have been '91) a number of ex-members got together to
make a rather underwhelming album called "Prodigal Stranger". No guest
vox from Ms. Bush though, as far as I know :-)
