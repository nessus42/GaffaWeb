During my first listen to Big Stripey Lie, I realized that the song
was pure genius.  I have endevoured to discern it's divine lyrics, and
following is my attempt at decipherment.  There are many words that I
am unsure of, so please comment/correct as I wish to achieve
enlightenment.
====

Oh big stripey lie
Movin like a wavey line
Comin up behind

All young gentle dreams
Drowning in life's grief
Can you hang on to me

	<Don't want to hurt you baby>
	<I only want to help you>
	<I could be good for you>

Your name is being called by sacred things
That are not addressed or listened to
Sometimes they blow trumpets

	<I only want to help you>
	<People want to hurt you>
	<You know I could be good for you>

(background something said during following lyrics and begining of
 next set as well)

Oh my God it's a jungle in here
You've got wild animals loose in here

	<I only want to help you>
	<It'll hurt you>
	<Good for you>

Hey all you little waves
Run away
Mmm run away
