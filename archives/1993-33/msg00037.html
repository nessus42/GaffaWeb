Here is the unpleasant text of the note sent to the DJ pool with the CD5 of
"Eat the Music":

"Columbia Alternative
9-2-93

Dear Tradesters:

Here it is,
Kate Bush
"Eat the Music" CD5

Our radio add date is
September 13th.

The Kate Bush album is now slated for early January.  This disc will be Kate's
sole output during this calendar year.  Please devote some of your valuable
print space to one of the most anticipated releases of this (and next) year . . .
Columbia Records 550 Madison Ave. New York, NY  10022-3211"

Doesn't sound very good . . . and why "The Kate Bush Album" rather than "The
Red Shoes"?
