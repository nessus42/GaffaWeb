Hello all,

Well, this damn song ETM is in my head and I wish it would get out.
It is stupendously dull -- no real chorus or bridge or anything outside
of two bars repeated over and over and over.  I despise it, yet maybe
it is telling that it's hook has lodged itself in my head.

BSL.  Hmmmmm.... yes!  This is great.  The drum machine and violin reminds me
ever so slightly of Experiment IV.  The guitar work is crunchy and brittle and
reminds me of Pull Out The Pin, not in sound but in attitude.  Set the CD 
player to "3" and auto-repeat.

CiTW.  I was hoping this would be allowed to die peacefully on the RM single,
but no... it's back like a bad rash.   :-(


IHMO, of course,
