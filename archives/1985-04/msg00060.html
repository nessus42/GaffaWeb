I agree!  Actually, it's just that on average the stuff on the left is
of higher quality and more original than the stuff on the right.  But
there are exceptions to any rule, so you can't close your mind.  Soft
Cell does some really good stuff ("Tainted Love" is *not* included!  Has
anyone heard Coil's version of "Tainted Love"?  Now *that* is good!)
There is some good heavy metal: Hawkwind.  Black Sabath had a couple of
good songs.  One of my favorite songs is done by Blue Oyster Cult
("Veteran of The Psychic Wars", with lyrics by Michael Moorcock), though
most of their stuff is mediocre, and the vast majority of heavy metal is
formulated dreck.

And most "mainstream" music is unimaginative almost by definition.

				-Doug
