Hey, now, wait, I have heard of them, but I am puzzled. 
Actually I just heard of them today.  One of the MTSs at the
company I work for had this tape which was broken which he
brought to me to fix; while fixing it, I noticed that it was a
very old (he said 10 yrs old) tape which said "Hawkwind/Space"
on it.  "Aha!" I thought, "This is the group Doug is always
talking about!"

So I played it... but it was very disappointing!  It had this
very ordinary rhythm, that went sort of bup-bup-de-bup over and
over, and a sort of soft, staccato accompaniment, which sounded
sort of like a vibraphone, and a woman singing what sounded like
a jazz-influenced song.  I listened to several spots on the tape
at random, and they all seemed very similar!  Nothing like the
variety and complexity of Kate Bush.  Do I have the wrong group,
or was this a bad example of their music, or what? 
(Incidentally, all the other tapes this person had with him were
"easy listening" tapes.  I told him I was going to loan him my
Kate Bush tape tomorrow so he could hear some interesting
music.)
