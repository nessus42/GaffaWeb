I'm sure Frank Zappa has written several.  Even sweet Kate Bush has

about a girl who is committing suicide by poisoning herself because she
is in love with her brother, who has gotten her pregnant.  She says that
she will meet with Zeus and that she will return as a ghost when there
is a solar eclipse over a certain hill.
