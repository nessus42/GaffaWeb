Today I was in Newboring (Newbury) Comix.  They are now stocking a lot
of Eno CDs, including Taking Tiger Mountain, No Pussyfooting, Before and
After Science, Another Green World, and others.  I guess EG has made enough
money from Killing Joke's psuedocommercial successes that they can afford
it... :)

I received "Before and After Science" as a holiday gift, and listened to it
this evening.  Superb sound, even though it's AAD.  "King's Lead Hat" has
been remixed slightly, but still blows my aural inputs.  Buy it.
