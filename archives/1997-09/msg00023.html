Interested in Shan's comments re "Heads we're Dancing" & comments about
"fantastical" vs realistic. For me the song goes back to story-telling,
which is one of the things that Kate does best - tho' always with an
emotional twist. And, yes, I love the track too.

Interested to see "Pull out the Pin" in someone's top 5.  I was really
shocked some years ago (around 1984/5?) when Homeground did a poll of
people's favourite tracks. Every single track which Kate had released
received at least one vote except..."Pull out the Pin".  But it's a
brilliant track!  Do people really dislike it, or was this a quirk in
this particular poll??  Any thoughts?
