First time writing to the list, but it's great to see someone referring to 
Canadian music.  There are a lot of great female singers in Canada besides 
Sarah McLachlan and Jane Siberry.  And no, I'm not Canadian.

If you are interested in Sarah at all I would suggest her label's sight...

www.nettwerk.com

Also of note, her Lileth Fair (Celebration of Women in Music) tour is hitting 
various cities in North America this summer and will include:

Fiona Apple, Autour de Lucie, The Cardigans, Mary Chapin Carpenter, Neneh 
Cherry, Paula Cole, Shawn Colvin, Indigo Girls, Jewel, Lisa Loeb, Aimee Mann, 
Joan Osborne, Suzanne Vega, Wild Colonials, Victoria Williams, and Kelly Willis 
so far in various lineups.

Information about the tour is also available on the Nettwerk site.

My top five (in no particular order)............

Love and Anger
Hello Earth
Wuthering Heights
The Man With The Child in His Eyes
Experiment IV

I would appreciate recommendations on other female singers and where to 
purchase.
