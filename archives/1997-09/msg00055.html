If you're at all interested in 640 x 480, 256 colour BMPs for the
desktop I've got four quite useable ones.

1. scan of Ninth Wave 'B/W' (floating in water), nice sepia tinge.
2. scan of a portrait from inside Lionheart album, backlit frizzy hair,

Transporting them could be tres awkward, though - they could be GIFs but
then need converting back to BMPs, or they could be ZIPped. Or I could
