Heather Dean-Caine inquired:

>Somebody once told me that there once was a feature on Kate and her
>music on the VH1 cable channel.  Does anybody know about it or have it
>on video?

Yes, it was called "The Sensual World of Kate Bush", and was first aired in
December of 1989, not surprisingly as a follow-up to the release of TSW.
The whole thing was 30 minutes long.  It consisted of an interview
(snippets of which were included on the 3-song TSW video compilation), as
well as bits of old videos and Tour of Life scenes, as appropriate to
whatever Kate was discussing at the time.  It was quite well done. 

I don't know how many times it was aired -- I stumbled across it completely
by accident, as I was channel-surfing and saw a promo for it about an hour
beforehand.  Naturally, we didn't have any blank tapes in the house at the
time. :P

The entire thing has never been officially released on video in the US,
though I think it was in the UK -- anyone with a better memory, please feel
free to jump in at this point. :)

Hope this helps,
