Grey Eye Glances, the ethereal progressive folk rock group whose music has
been compared to that of Clannad, Iona, Renaissance, Tori Amos, Kate Bush,
October Project, Mary Chapin Carpenter and the Indigo Girls will perform
at Orion Sound Studios at 2903 Whittington Avenue in Baltimore, Maryland
on Saturday May 31st at 7:30pm. Also on the bill are Still and Cloud Nine. 
Admission is $15 at the door. All ages are welcome.

Feel free to e-mail me if you need any more info.
