I have just returned from a Holiday in Cambodia which was absolutely
wonderful. I spent three days at the Angkor Wat, a set of temples built
some 800 to 1,000 years ago and they are simply magnificent. Anyway,
while walking through the main temple, I sang ''Running Up That Hill'',
thus perhaps being the first person in the temple's 1,000-year-history
to sing a Kate Bush song within its hallowed walls. Has anybody else
sung his/her favourite Bush song in such an auspicious place?
