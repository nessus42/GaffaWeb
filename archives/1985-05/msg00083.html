KB chart report

From: nessus (Doug Alan)
Date: Mon, 28 Oct 85 04:47:49 est
Subject: KB chart report

"Hounds of Love" has entered the U.S. Billboard top 200 albums chart at
number 74, and is the highest charting new entry!  This is *amazing*
considering that no previous Kate Bush album reached any higher than
about 150 on the U.S. charts.  RUTH is on the verge of entering the U.S.
top 40 at position 47.  (Someone tell me what Casey Casum says -- it
should be good for a laugh.)  RUTH is also number 21 on the disco/dance
12" single chart and number 18 on the club play disco/dance chart.

On the British charts, HoL had fallen into the number two position, with
Madrecka at number one, but HoL has regained the number one position and
Madrecka has fallen down to number three.

			"Running up that hill"
