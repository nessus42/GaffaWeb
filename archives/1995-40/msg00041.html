RE: CATHY Demos

While internal distribution of the demos in Love Hounds would drop the 
bootleggers out of the picture, I have to wonder if we wouldn't be doing Kate a 
grave disservice.  It is one thing for us to share videos available over the 
airwaves that she meant us to see, but quite another to redistribute these 
recordings that Kate has made clear she did not want circulated.

I am ambivalent on this, as I would dearly love to hear and have at my disposal 
a copy of her before-Prince "Why Should I Love You", or the legendary and 
mysterious "Early Years" album, the many songs that didn't make it onto The 
Dreaming (20 songs?), the 200 other songs she wrote in her youth.

Andy is quite right that EMI and Kate should put their heads together and issue 
a recording of these things.  It could be combined with that picture book we 
never got about "Catherine", since she was writing most of those early songs 
during the period of time that middle book was to be about.  

BUT, WE'RE GETTING A NEW SONG!  (imagine a series of Bulgarian style yips)
