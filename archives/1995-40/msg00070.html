IED has studied Uli's excellent evaluation and agrees
completely  (insofar as his own limited knowledge
allows) with Uli's judgement of the various editions' 
relative sound quality -- the difference between
the "original" 7-inch EPs and all other editions
is indeed very noticeable, which makes the 
abrupt cut-outs between songs on all but the
first EP even harder to bear.

"The Sensual Woman" -- IED believes this was
simply the alternate title used for some pressings
of the "Cathy's Album/Cathy's Album Too" LPs.
There is no discernible (to IED's ears) difference
in sound quality between these different pressings.

"Fiddle" -- Audio-cassette only as far as IED is
aware, and NOT very good sound quality.  IED once
received a relatively better audio-cassette dub
from an unnamed source from Australia which was
better than "Fiddle"'s sound, but still nowhere near
the quality of the EPs'.
