If I remember rightly from the KBC Sensual World magazine, the 'swop' 
noises found in the title track of the album were produced courtesy 
of Paddy (who else!!), by reeling out a fishing rod.It is the noise of 
the line being thrown out as it were (not too acquainted with fishing 
terminology I'm afraid). Paddy was trying to recreate the atmosphere 
of an Irish Lake etc... and was subsequently rather annoyed that he 
was credited on the album as playing 'whips' on the track.

Also a while back someone from Manchester wrote in asking about 
getting his hands on some Cathy demos. Well if he would like to email 
me (as I too am from the rainy city!) I would gladly send him a copy 
of the two tapes I have, which judging by reading the discussions 
over the last few days, have pretty much everything on. All I ask is 
for him to send me a tape (sorry I can't remember your name!).
