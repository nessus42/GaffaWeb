I have translated Scott D. Yelich's excellent rasters into GIF files
and sent them to woj to place in /ftp/pub/kb at clutx.clarkson.edu
(128.153.4.3).  This package includes seven GIF images:

TKI.GIF (New and Improved)
TWS.GIF (Now 50% Less KholesTerol)
RUTH.GIF (Replaces RUNNING.GIF)
KB.GIF (Didn't know what to call this -- KaTe sitting at her KeyBoard)
NFE.GIF (Open up and say, "CAAAAW!")
LIVE.GIF (The Infamous Babooshka BiKini KosTume)
DREAMING.GIF (Is it me, or does that guy look like Lt. Data from the back?)

The KlariTy of all these images is very high, due to Scott's digitizer being
better than the one I have access to.
