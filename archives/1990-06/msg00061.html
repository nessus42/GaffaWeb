Kate's legendary performance on Saturday Night Live (the only time she
*ever* performed in the U.S.) was only repeated once.  I do not know of
a commercial source for it.  When they show this episode of SNL (with 
guest host Eric Idle) on The Best Of SNL, Kate's performances are cut
out! :-(

However, if you ask around you might find someone who actually recorded
that show; you might be able to get a copy that way.

On the other hand, the _Live At Hammersmith_ show *is* a part of 
everyone's basic video library (or should be).  Information about
how to obtain this has been posted before, but if you'd like more
information feel free to e-mail me.
