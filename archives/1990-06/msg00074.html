Subject: March. 8 _Rolling Stone_


The new _Rolling Stone_ (Mar. 8) just came out.  There's still no
review, and the album fell to No.3 on their college chart, which is
really the same as the one on 120 Minutes.  However --

On the top fifty albums chart, _TSW_ went up from No. 29 to No. 18!

Also, this issue has the results of the critics and readers poll.
Kate doesn't appear anywhere, to nobody's surprise.  However --

There's a section where many artists list their top ten albums of
the year.  Robert Smith of The Cure and Ozzy Osbourne both list
_TSW_ among their choices!
