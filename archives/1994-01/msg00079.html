I've just been listening to Radio One over here and apparently KaTe is
not in the list of nominations for the BPI awards this year. The closing
date for nominations was October of last year, obviously The Red Shoes
was released too late, arrrgh!! What is particurly stupid is that she was
nominated last year and she didn't release a single (okay I guess Rocket Man
could count).

As for the next single release over here PDFM mentioned that it will be
either And So Is Love or Why Should I Love you, Radio One has been playing
Why Should I Love you fairly heavily for the last few days. Personaly I
would prefer it if The Red Shoes was released but who am I to criticise being
just a humble systems administrator.
