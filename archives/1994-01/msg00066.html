Hi everyone,
 
Something positive: Today I received a copy on tape from a radio
programme. It's a 1 hour special on Kate Bush, including 2 snippets
of interview which I don't know the origine of. It was a programme
on "Hofstad radio", a local not-so-legal station in the Hague,
which was very popular here between 1980 and 1986. They will reform
for a one-off legal programme soon, so someone was going through
the tapes and found this, and was kind enough to make me a copy.
It's very weird to hear this almost 13 years later!
The show contains, a.o. "Army dreamers", "James (live)", "Kashka
from Baghdad", "Babooshka", "Warm and soothing", "The empty
bullring", "No self control" (with P Gabriel) and "You" (with Roy
Harper).
Was very pleased about this. Hope you won't mind this blurb of
enthusiasm.
