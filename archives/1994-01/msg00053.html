Well, I just got done reading the FAQ and WOW do I feel enlightened!
Whoever thought up the idea of FAQs was a friggin' genious.

Anyway, now after reading the FAQ and discovering HOW MANY rare or
hard to find songs there are, I am almost sick with desire to obtain
these somehow.  I REALLY think there is a need to create some sort of
Kate Raretapes project.  Unfortunately, I don't have the proper equipment
to make quality copies on a mass scale, but I would be happy to help out
somehow.  Other music lists have done this and it has worked well.  People
with various copies of rare tracks send copies to one master dubber.  The
master dubber accumulates all the songs on one tape, and the tape is mailed
from regional distributor to regional distributor around the world (sometimes
more than one master tape exists) who makes copies en masse for people in
that geographical region.  The Genesis list presently has 12 volumes of
Raretapes and the Sting/Police list has 4 volumes.  The Yes list holds the
trophy with a whopping 15 volumes of rare Yes tracks.  We could have three
or four volumes EASY with the material mentioned in the FAQ.  The two CDs
from the TWW boxed set would do most of the work for us.

In the alternative, if we can't muster enough interest in this project,
would some kind soul in the UK please e-mail me so we can perhaps arrange
a copy of the two CDs from the boxed set in exchange for other progressive
bootlegs?  I have some stuff to trade.
