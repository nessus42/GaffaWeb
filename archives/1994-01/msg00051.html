Anyone looking for a VHS copy of "The Single File," please contact me.

I ordered a copy from a local vendor (Roscoes) many months ago knowing  
full well that TSF was temporarily "out-of-print" but the guy working  
there said he thought they could still get copies - OK sure.

A few weeks later, I found it at HMV Toronto.  So I bought it.

approx. 4 months later (!!!), Roscoes calls me to say, "Your copy of The  
Single File has just arrived."  So now I'm pretty much obligated to buy  
it.  If anyone wants it, please make an offer.  They're asking $24.99 but  
I'd be willing to absorb some of that cost seeing as this is my  
responsibility/problem.  It's of course, brand new, still shrinkwrapped  
(still sitting at the store in fact.)  I'll carefully mail it anywhere in  
the VHS-speaking world.  Mostly, I'd just like to see someone make use of  
it; I already have a copy.

For those not familiar with "The Single File" video collection, it  
contains most of the same singles on "The Whole Story" (- the hounds of  
love videos) but includes "There Goes a Tenner," "Hammer Horror," "Them  
Heavy People," and "Wow" (the original video - not the same as the cut-up  
sequence on The Whole Story.)
