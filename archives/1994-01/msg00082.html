Hi,

Last weekend I hade another one of those Kate Strange Phenomena things.
I was in a record store (Oh, big surprise there) and they had their
cassingles (God, I hate that word.  I also hate "Infotainment", "Infomercial",
and especially "Edutainment"!) by the checkout, and I saw the (U.S.) single
for Rubberband Girl.  Wondering what was on the B-side, I picked it up,
and to my surprise (Official Kate Pronouncement coming up:), The B-side
for the U.S. Cassingle of "Rubberband Girl" is... This Woman's Work!

NOT "Show A Little Devotion"!  NOT "Home for Christmas"!  NOT EVEN
"Big Stripey Lie" (The official B-side of Singles off of The Red Shoes")!
No, they chose a cut from one of her PREVIOUS albums! (OK, so it's a great
song, but has the Marketing Department for Columbia flipped?)
