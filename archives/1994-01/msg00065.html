Hello Friends,

We've now been to see Dave in Whittington Hospital, Highgate, North
London (Cloudsey Ward), and he is comfortable (well as comfortable as
you can be in his position). He is somewhat out of it, being dosed with
stringent pain-killers. He's OK, and expecting to be home next week.

Other News.

"The Red Shoes" will not be the next single in the UK, the choice is
between "Why Should I Love You" and "And so is Love". Well, apparently.

Convention.

We've now been given the definite go-ahead for the 1994 Kate Bush
Convention. The date is likely to be mid to late March, the venue most
likely the Hippodrome in London's West End.
