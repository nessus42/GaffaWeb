> First, where can I get more of her material?  I live very close to New

Tower Records will probably have all her albums.  Start with the very
best -- start with "The Dreaming".

> Second, towards the end of the video, say, in the last 10-15 seconds,
> the camera is panning to the left and as they pass the performers
> (Kate and the male dancer) the performer draws her/his arms apart in
> the same way one would draw a bow.  I suppose I notice this because I
> am an archer, but did anyone else pick up on this?

Yeah.  The bow drawing movement also occurs during the dance.  It's
really obvious if you've seen the cover of the single, where she holding
a drawn bow.  Also in the Terry Wogan clip, she aims a real bow and
arrow.

Not coincidentally, Kate's hobby these days, other than dancing, is
archery.

> I am not familiar with the lyric, is this in any way appropriate?

Yes, quite!  It's symbolic of a love/hate relationship.

Anyone else notice that at one point in the dance, Kate and the male dancer
become symbolically crucified on each other?

		"Is there so much hate for the ones we love?"
