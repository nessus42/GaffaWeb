Kate Bush on Empty-V!

From: Doug Alan <nessus>
Date: Sat, 14 Sep 85 04:38:36 edt
Subject: Kate Bush on Empty-V!

"Running Up That Hill" is now on Empty-V as a full video!  I haven't see
it yet (I don't have cable), but a friend of mine has.  Empty-V is *not*
showing the actual promotional video that Kate made up, instead they are
showing (I infer from the description the friend gave me) a film of
Kate's live performance of the song on the Terry Wogan show.  Paddy Bush
is in the video, and Kate comes out dressed in a cloak and at the end of
the song loads an arrow into her bow.
