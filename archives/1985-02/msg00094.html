my first Kate Bush experience

From: Greg Skinner <gds>
Date: Wed, 25 Sep 85 19:43:05 edt
Subject: my first Kate Bush experience

On a weeknightly Battle of the Bands on station KMEL in SF, I heard a
Kate Bush song for the first time, "Running up That Hill".  I was
surprised at how much I liked the song.  I hope it is representative
of the rest of Kate Bush's material.

Needless to say, she won, defeating another band (Ready for the
World).
