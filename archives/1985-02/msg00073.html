I think we all had similar experiences, Kevin - it sounded kind of
abrasive and noisy the first few times, but by the third or fourth time
around, the different lines emerged from her complex tracking and
it all became - AMAZING....

would be odd since she just invested in her own studio, so she's probably

but it sounds pleasantly like a blend between The Dreaming and her
earlier albums.  Not bad, but not the gorgeousness of TD - still, I've
no complaints!  It's Kate, it's gotta be good, no?
