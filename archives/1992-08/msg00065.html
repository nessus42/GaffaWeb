Back in late '90, I was staying with a friend in Florida and we were           
going out for sushi for the evening.  We went to pick up her then-             
boyfriend and he was watching "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles" (the movie)       
on videodisc.  We sat down for a minute and watched the opening                
sequence.                                                                      
                                                                               
During that opening sequence, I noticed a Kate Bush "Ninth Wave" poster        
in one scene, behind some graffiti.  I mentioned this, and he                  
backtracked to that frame.  It was confirmed.                                  
                                                                               
If in the off-chance you see the film, pay attention to the first few          
minutes and you will see it.                                                   
                                                                               
I wrote KBC and mentioned this.  Lisa replied that they had not seen the       
film, but would look for the poster when they do.  Imagine Kate watching       
that!                                                                          
                                                                               
I don't know if this has already been noticed, but I just thought I'd          
bring it up.
