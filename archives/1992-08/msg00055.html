So I'm in the CD store looking for Sarah's latest effort when I find a 
copy for the incredibly low price of $9.99.  Great!  I go up to the
counter and the goon charges me $12.99.  Apparently the $9.99 copy is
missing the 11th track, "Wear Your Love Like Heaven".  He had no
explanation other than maybe a batch of them were accidentally pressed
without the song.  Of course, I paid for the extra song knowing full well
I would stay up nights wondering what I'm missing.  The 10 track CD
was not in a longbox, the 11 track CD was. Anybody buy the copy w/o the 
song?  Are you upset?
