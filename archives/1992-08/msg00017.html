Let's see if this goes through, now that I think .gaffa may be fixed.

1.  The latest _Rolling Stone_ (March 5, with R.E.M. on the cover) has
their annual music awards.  There's a section where artists list their
picks.  Kendall Jones of Fishbone lists NfE.  He says:

"Kate Bush, _Never for Ever_:  It's hard for me to have a favorite Kate
Bush record -- and I have them all -- but this one seems to stick in the
CD player the longest."

2.  Sarah McLachlan's song "Into The Fire" is in its second week on the
Billboard Modern Rock Tracks Chart.  I've heard it exactly once on KITS,
but then I've been busy with work and the Olympics (:-)), so I haven't
had much time to listen to the radio.

3.  According to the latest Billboard, Tori Amos' first video has been
added at both MTV and VH-1.  I haven't seen it yet, though.

4.  The issue of Billboard before the current one had a special section
for some major anniversary of Hammersmith Odeon.  There's a tiny picture
of Kate as an example of the artists that have played there.  The picture
is from _Hounds of Love_ days, though.
