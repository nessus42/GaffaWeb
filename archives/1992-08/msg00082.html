Just picked up Tori Amos' _Little Earthquakes_.

Wow.

She has a nice, almost beautiful, and quite powerful voice, though I don't
find it as distinctive as KaTe's or Karen Peris' or Sarah McLachlan or Jane
Siberry, or any of a number of other folks.  On the other hand, her music
and lyrics make up for any lack of distinction in her voice.  Good stuff.

When I carried the CD to the counter in the record store today, the woman
at the register commented that it was a great album, to which I replied that
I'd heard that--I'd been waiting for it for a couple of months.  She then
wanted to know if I was a Kate Bush fan...weird how these things work
out, eh?
