Anyone wishing to send a donation to the Terrence Higgins Trust
can do so at the following address:

Terrence Higgins Trust
52-54 Grays Inn Road
London
WC1X 8JU
England

According to the Feb. issue of Q, Terrence Higgins are running with
a deficit of c. #400,000 on their current annual budget of #2.4 million.
Before Xmas they had to announce the first of 10 redundancies among
their professional staff of 65.

Although, Terrence Higgins Trust will benefit from all the royalties
from the re-release of Bohemian Rapsody - record royalties are paid
a minimum of 6 months in arrears. So I am sure they would be grateful
for any donation at the present time.
