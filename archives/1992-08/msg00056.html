>so I'm in the CD store looking for Sarah's latest effort when I find a 
>copy for the incredibly low price of $9.99.  Great!  I go up to the
>counter and the goon charges me $12.99.  Apparently the $9.99 copy is
>missing the 11th track, "Wear Your Love Like Heaven".  He had no

>without the song.

Actually, the explanation is quite simple.  The one without the 11th
track is probably the Canadian version, which has only ten tracks.
The same thing happened with her first album, _Touch_.  The Canadian
version had two fewer tracks than the American one, although in that
case one of the two tracks was a remixed version of a song that appeared
on both versions of the album.

This seems very strange to me.  I mean, Ms. McLachlan is Canadian, so
I'd have assumed they'd put the extra tracks on the Canadian versions,
but they didn't.
