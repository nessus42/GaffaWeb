I think it might have something to do with the fact that both _Touch_ and
_Solace were released much later in the US. I'm not sure about _Touch_ but
_Solace_ was released 8 months ago in Canada.

I hope they release this extra track on a future single. On the current
single Drawn to the Rythmn, there is the album version, a live version an
previously unreleased live track, Gloomy Sunday, I think.

The current version of _Touch_ sold in Canada now is the same as the US
version with the Vox remix. The cover on the current version is different,
with a color hand painted photo, instead of a different B+W photo.

Supposedly there is going to be a new Nettwerk compilation disc and video
with Sarah featured on both, but I haven't seen either of them yet.

I just heard a techno remix of Into the Fire on Brave New Waves. IMHO they
really pummeled the song to death. They used a thin vocal track from the
original and almost nothing else.

Sarah is going to tour around Canada again for the next month. I didn't hear
anything about US concerts yet.
