this is my seventh article today without even mentioning Tori at all (oops!
spoiled...)

Today I found the re-issue of the EMI compilation with all those Christmas
songs including Kate's bongo-version of December will be magic again.
The CD looks different than the photo in my memory that is in the
Illustrated Collector's Guide to Kate Bush, so this might be something
for the true collectors. I hope (no CD player found yet) that it really
is the bongo version, otherwise I might get frantic...

The CD itself is contained in a digipack that folds out into all four 
directions.

Anyway, for those who want to order it somewhere:

'Here is Christmas' by EMI, order number is 0777 7 98054 2 6

An interesting thing is the following:
the digipack has the following letters at the bottom:
(p) 1991 The copyright in this compilation is owned by EMI Records Ltd.
(c) 1992 EMI Records Ltd.

I thought that the CD was a little bit older, or is this a completely
different item?
