Derek wonders: 
 
}Apologies if this has been noticed before but anyone else notice that Tori
Amos
}poses in a wooden box on her album cover not unlike another album cover we all
}know.  Copycat?  Flattery?  Coincidence?  Anyone know or like to hazard a

Actually, this has been noticed many times before, but for the benefit of
yourself
and others who may also be wondering:
 
Tori, when asked, has said that the resemblance of her album cover to that
on the American pressing of TKI is pure coincidence.  She said that the liner
notes
have to do with perspective: thus the disproportionate blocks of lyrics on the 
pages, and herself crouched in a box over a tiny toy piano.
 
This is only an American coincidence, as the European cover of TKI is much
different, and has nothing to do with Kate in a box.
