I recently posted a message here to find out who is interested in 
attending a Katemas '92 in the San Francisco bay area, and I am pleased 
with the response so far. There have been around 15 people that seem 
determined to follow on with the tradition and have another Katemas over 
here, which is very good considerring that I only posted the message a 
few days back. The problem that I have had so far is that I haven't heard 
from hardly anyone that attended the Katemas last year, and the person 
that organized last year's Katemas seems to be going to Boston and I have 
no way of contacting him currently to re-invite last year's participants.
If anyone out there attended last year's Katemas, regardless of whether 
they plan on going to a local Katemas or not, please e-mail me, since you 
 may be able to help me quite significantly with information on local 
Katefans, collectors, etc.  ( Drukman, this means you... :)   )
Thanx -
