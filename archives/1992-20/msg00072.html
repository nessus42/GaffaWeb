Hi All!

As I got to the computer lab this morning I logged in and started to read my
mail. Suddenly, I came across a word I never encountered in my mail before:

                        techno

At the first moment I was shocked. Then, unbelieving, I went to check if the
mail really came from Love-Hounds, or if it was ment for me at all. But it was.

Techno??? IN LOVE-HOUNDS???????????????????????????????
What's going on here?

And so, for just a little dose of a (flaming) opinion:
I think (IMHO) that techno is one of the worst kinds of music (if you can call
that music) I have ever heard (and it took me only a few moment of unwanted
listening to decide that), it's more empty than outer space and more shallow
than a dried pool of water in the Sahara desert.

Uri Goldberg
