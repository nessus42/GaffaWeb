Hello,

It's been a long time since I have posted anything.  
Lurking around the last few months indicates to me we are in need of the new 
album to get things revved up a bit.  

After more than two years of owning both the TWW boxed set and all KaTe's 
album CDs on import I have decided to sell the 'spare' CDs in order
to continue nourishment of my CD addiction.

I thought I'd offer them here first before going to rec.music.marketplace.

I hope no-one is offended.

Details:

The Kick Inside: 
pressed by EMI in the UK, has original UK cover & full lyrics.

Lionheart : 
EMI UK pressing, full lyrics and photos from original gatefold album cover.

Never for Ever: 
pressed what was W. Germany, full lyrics plus most of gatefold album artwork.

The Dreaming: 
pressed by Nimbus UK (exceptionally clear), full lyrics plus close up of key 
in KaTe's mouth.

Hounds of Love:  
pressed by EMI-Toshiba in Japan, full lyrics.

The Sensual World: 
pressed by EMI UK, full lyrics.

I think it is agreed that the sound quality of the imports is better then the 
US domestics, especially on TSW, and especially compared to EMI America's
original CD releases.  I also find the packaging that much more classier 
and elegant.

The bottom line:

I am asking $13 for each disc.  Add $2 p&p for the first disc and 75c for 
each additional CD. If you want all 6 I'll let you have them for $70 +$5 p&p.


Contribution to discussion:

poses in a wooden box on her album cover not unlike another album cover we all
know.  Copycat?  Flattery?  Coincidence?  Anyone know or like to hazard a guess?
