Hello everybody,
     Ok, I saw mention of the film "Jesus Of Montreal."  I have seen this 
film at least three times and I have a copy of my own.  Two of the main 
characters in the film are women.  Mireille, played by Catherine Wilkining, 
is a young, gorgeous model and Constance, played by Johanne-Marie Tremblay, 
is an older woman who used be involved with theatre but now does volunteer 
work.  Not the most French-like names, but the movie is Canadian and the 
names are correct because, well, I checked before writing this. :)  As for 
a Kate Bush look-a-like, well, I'm not sure, I haven't watched the movie in 
awhile.  I hope this cleared up questions that any one may have had.  It's 
a great film and definitely worth watching.
