According to next week's Radio Times, as part of the National
Music Day celebrations, Kate Bush will join the Great Elastic Band
on Clapham Common (London). This is billed as the gathering of
Britain's largest band, incorporating famous names and orchestras
from the world of jazz, pop and classical music, both amateur and
professional in a cornucopia of common-interest enthusiasts.

BBC 1 has a programme on the event at 12 noon on Sunday 28th June
(National Music Day). No other details but they do print a small
colour photo of Kate peering round a tree.
