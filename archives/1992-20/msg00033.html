Lioness at Heart includes: A UK issue CD of Lionheart, a white t-shirt with 
kate's autograph, a book of B&W and color photos from about '78-'80, a 
poster of kate in the leotard pose and a postcard of nearly the same 
picture. There is an edition of the box set that comes with everything 
mentioned except the t-shirt. Shop around your "Kate dealers" for best price 
before jumping into a purchase. Prices seem to range from $70-$90.
