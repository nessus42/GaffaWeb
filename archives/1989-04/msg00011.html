 Subject: _Cathy_Demos_ distribution ideas

      First, thanks go to Philip Verdieck for reprinting verbatim
the relevant passages of the piece on Trio B. in _Option_, which
IED was only able to paraphrase from memory last months.
      Regarding Julian's suggestion: excellent idea! IED is all for
it. And he would be happy to make a 90-minute tape of little-known Kate
Bush music for Love-Hounds' edification and entertainment.
     So the question is, do we have any volunteers for the copying and/or
distribution of proposed tape? If there is anyone out there who would
be happy to make an unspecified number of copies of such a tape, IED
will gladly send a cassette to him/her.
     Naturally, anyone requesting a copy of the tape from said agent
would be expected to send him/her a blank cassette and a stamped,
self-addressed envelope.
     Let IED add, however, that if no-one volunteers, IED _will_ offer
to perform said services himself. He is not withdrawing his statement
of willingness to help out in at least some degree. It's just that
Julian's idea would probably bring swifter satisfaction to more people
than the alternative of waiting for IED to get around to making the
nth dub.

-- Andrew Marvick, feeling appropriately unworthy and guilty as he
   logs-off to go listen to _Feeling_Like_a_Waltz_ and _Keeping_Me_
   _Waiting_ for the million-and-oneth time.
