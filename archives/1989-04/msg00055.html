     Effusive thanks go to Scott Yelich, who has volunteered to
handle the tape requests--although when he sees the length of this list
(which may get longer) he may want to reconsider, which no one
would begrudge him the right to do. Matters could be hastened a bit
if Scott could send his mailing address to IED so that IED can
send Scott the "master" of the cassette.
     This cassette will contain only about half of the non-LP Kate
Bush music presently in circulation (not counting live performances
of LP music, which would take up another five or more 90-minute
cassettes). This cassette will not include the various guest-vocal
recordings Kate has done over the years, for example--some of them
are really good, but the b-sides should take priority for the benefit
of newer L-Hs who don't have them all yet.
     IED has interpreted Michael Portuesi's remarks to mean that he does
not wish to be put on the mailing list for this tape. If he should change
his mind (i.e., lower his standards), no grudge will be held against him
for looking the gift-horse in the mouth.
     If you expected to see your name on the list but didn't, IED must
have lost yours. Please re-send your name in that case. Apologies sent.

-- Andrew Marvick
