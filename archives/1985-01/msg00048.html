It's not that difficult to come up with images like these.  Here's a
certified, guaranteed method for doing it:  (1) Stay awake for 48 hours
(2) Place a blank piece of paper and pen next to your bed (3) Eat 1
quart of Vlassic Deli Dill Pickles (4) Eat one pint of Ben and Jerries
Dastardly Mash or Heathbar Crunch icecream (5) Put on the "The Dreaming"
at a loud volume level (6) Go to sleep (7) When you wake up write down
what you were dreaming about (8) Go to 5.

When you wake up in the morning, you may find what you have written down
on the paper to be quite strange!  And you don't even need to bathe in
goat's milk....
