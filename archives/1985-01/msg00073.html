Subject: Disco Kate

In the August 31st Billboard, Kate Bush is mentioned in Brian Chin's
column on dance and disco music.  He says

	Kate Bush's "Running Up That Hill" proves that if you wait long
	enough everyone will make a disco record.  This one is midtempo
	and dreamy, with a surprizingly propulsive electronic pattern.

It that same issue, RUTH is listed as the uncharted song with the second
most (second to Oinga Boinga) potential on the 12 inch dance/disco
charts.  In the September 7th Billboard, Kate Bush appears at number 49
on the 12 inch dance/disco charts.

				-Doug
