> i was looking through some titles from cdnow,and i came across
> this woman's work extended cd.  there are three editions, plus
> a live album. the description doesnt say much about what it is.

	Is this info on Gaffaweb? I think it is, but...

	These four bootleg albums consist of: TWWI (the same as 
TWWI in the box set, plus Rocket Man & Candle in the Wind), 
TWWII (the same as TWWII in the box set, plus Rubberband Girl), 
TWWIII (10 tracks, most of which are b-sides or re-mixes from 
TRS) and TWW Live (17 tracks of mostly poor quality taken from 
TV shows, Hammersmith, and the occasional benefit concert over 
the last 18 years).

	I may have it backward as to which TWW contains RBG, RM 
and CITW, but these three songs do appear in addition to the 
regular track list on I and II.
