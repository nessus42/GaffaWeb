No one has yet mentioned Kate's duets with her Japanese
hosts during her stint on "Sound in 'S'".  She shared vocals
on "Let It Be".  Also, she helped out on keyboards for Ray
Shell's cover single (EMI ca. 1980?) of "Man With the Child
In His Eyes".  One might also mention her participation in
both the Sun Zeebrugge Ferry Disaster charity single of
"Let It Be" and the "Spirit of the Forest" single, on both
of which she went in by herself to add a couple of sung
lines.  Not exactly duets, but you see the connection.
