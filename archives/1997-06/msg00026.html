Just saw this in Newsweek and thought "they must have made this for US!"

Check out March 10, 1997 Newsweek, Page 10:

"Mixing it Your Way -- Haven't you ever been at a dance club, listened to
the dee-jay's remix of your favourite song and wished that you could do
that too?  Now you can, thanks to Mixman ($39.95, 888-464-9626).  This
CD-Rom breaks a songdown into its component tracks -- drum, bass, snare,
special effects, lead and backup vocals -- then allows you to re-create the
song using the tracks you desire.  You can also control the tempo and
pitch, turning a regular song into a ponderous ambient groove or a
hopped-up jungle beat."

I see a new occupation for us at Katemas parties!!!
