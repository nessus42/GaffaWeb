Well.......lets just say that when I first heard the track....shortly 
after "Lionheart" was released.....I interpreted it as such.....

At the time, my sexuality was VERY much in question and the only 
images of gay men  I'd ever seen were either vicious psychopaths, 
hideously scarred (mentally and/or physically) or the limp wristed 
John Inman types.....

 I felt like none of them......nor indeed wanted to be associated 
with them......

Find a song Like "Kashka" by my favourite artist was a welcome 
sight indeed......some one I cared about writing a song about two guys 
dancing, laughing and loving together......can you imagine what a 
revelation that was :-)

All I ever wanted......

One of a very select few records that have personally "spoken" to 
me.......thanks Kate ;-)

Love ya!
