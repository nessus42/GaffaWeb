The Save The Earth Foundation will conduct a monthly auction Rock
& Roll memorabilia on the Web. Bands ranging from the Rolling
Stones, the Eagles, Billy Joel, John Lee Hooker, Julio Iglesias,
and Eddie Murphy are among the rock and roll artists and
performers donating their autographs to rare and modern concert
posters to raise money for the "Save The Earth Foundation.

Save The Earth Foundation kicked off their music memorabilia
auction on April, 22 and based on the auctions success and its
popularity, "Save The Earth" will continue it on a ongoing basis.

Internet users can view full color images of the items to be
auctioned online, check current bids and enter their own bids
online. Internet users can find auction information on the World
Wide Web at //www.commerce.com/save_earth 

Proceeds are used to address urgent regional, national and
global issues in air and water pollution, toxic and hazardous
waste and damage to sensitive ecological systems.  The Foundation
is non-political in nature, awarding  grants to institutions of
higher learning rather than trying to pass specific legislation
or elect candidates.     

On behalf of The Save The Earth Foundation, thank you. If you
do not have web access we can help place your bids.
