> Was "The Single File", from Kate Bush, already published in CD format?

> From where can I mail order this title, as I can't find it anywehre here in

As far as I know it hasn't been published in Video-CD (= MPEG-CDI) format yet.
It is (or rather was :-( ) available as a CD-Video (= LaserDisc), which is
the 12 inch instead of 12 cm format, uses different players, is analog,
and is quality-wise better. Much better.

Unfortunately the LaserDisc is impossible to find nowadays. There's someone
on the Musical Wantlist for an endless time now looking for it and offering
$150 - without success.
