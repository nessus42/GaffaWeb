Overview:


WHAT ARE SCREENERS?


    MusicScreeners represent an exciting breakthrough in computer
interactive entertainment! Each
    MusicScreener contains a 30-40 second music video clip which can be used
in 6 screensaver functions and 2
    challenging games. 

    What's amazing is that you'll get all this functionality on ONE 3.5"
DISKETTE (no CD-ROM Drive required!).
    MusicScreeners will run on most PC computer systems. 

    Over 20 MusicScreeners are available in music and computer stores
everywhere. Each one has a suggested
    list price of $12.98. For more fun, you can install multiple
MusicScreeners on your computer to create a
    virtual video jukebox! 

    Please visit back here regularly for more breaking news about
MusicScreeners. Right now, you have the
    chance to download a real-live music screener for free! 

    If you'd like regularly updated information about MusicScreeners and our
new releases, please join our
    MusicScreeners mailing list.

SCREENSAVER VARIATIONS


    Your Music Screener will be pre-loaded into the carousel, which serves
as the control center for Music
    Screeners. If you own more than one Music Screener, they can all be
loaded together. You can have up to five
    Music Screeners active at once. With more than one Music Screener, your
computer becomes a virtual video
    jukebox since you can program each Music Screener individually. The
carousel will automatically rotate
    through each Music Screener. 

    Each MusicScreener disc contains one video clip. This video, however,
may be configured with six different
    screensaver effects: 

    Screen Art: Video squares run across the screen and stop randomly -
within 30 seconds your screen is a
    collage of video images. Note: "Screen Art" is the default effect for
each carousel spot. 

    Video Wall: Mini-screens play simultaneously to turn your PC monitor
into a riveting wall of video. 

    Bounce: A video frame displaying the moving image bounces across your
screen. 

    Video Trail: A moving video image bounds across your screen leaving a
dreamy trail of images in its wake. 
[It sounds worth it just for this module alone - SB]

Jigsaw: Cubes with portions of moving video drop into your screen. Once the
image is complete, Jigsaw starts
    again. 

    Slide Show: Frames from the video randomly flash on your screen to
create a still-frame slide show. 


SCREENER GAMES


    In addition to the screensaver function, Screeners contains two games: 

    Game #1- Video Match: the objective of this game is to match the same
video segments in the top half of the
    screen with images in the bottom half. Once you identify the same image,
click on one after the other and they
    will disappear. Once all images disappear you will go to the next skill
level. At higher levels, the images are
    all mixed together. The levels of the game start at level 1 and go up to
level 20. 

    Game #2- Video Slide: The objective of this game is to construct a
complete video from individual "video
    pieces" which will travel down the screen. Use your left and right arrow
keys to move the falling "video
    piece" into the position where it belongs. If you believe you have it
lined up correctly, you can use the down
    arrow key to speed the "video piece" down the screen. If you are
correct, the piece will stay in the grid. If you
    are incorrect, the piece disappears. The pieces come down faster as you
go to each level of difficulty. 

    The levels of the game start at level 1 and go up to level 20. 

    To increase your playing options, each game has 5 different game types: 


      1.Video/Video- a small video segment plays in the game area while the
video clip plays in display area 
      2.Video/No hint- a small video segment plays in the game area with
nothing in display area 
      3.Frame/Video- a video frame is shown in the game area while the video
clip plays in game area 
      4.Frame/Frame- a video frame is shown in the game area while the same
video frame appears in display
        area 
      5.Frame/No Hint- a video frame is shown in the game area with nothing
in display area
