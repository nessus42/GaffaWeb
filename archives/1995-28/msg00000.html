For rabid collectors only, :-)

A new edition of TWS has been released in France with a slightly
different packaging (the CD is in a carton sleeve with the usual photo
but widened to fill the entire space and the writing in gold letters
says "The best of Kate Bush").

The track list is identical to the usual CD. The tape has the same
packaging and content. I haven't found a vynil version yet and it
doesn't seem to exist.

There are commercials on TV and radios, too. The TV spot is 10sec long
with excerpts from the RUTH, Cloudbusting and Babooshka videos.  It's
always great to see Kate on TV!

-- Yves.
