In February a few EMI albums were re-released on vinyl, as high-
quality pressings and limited editions. Among them was The Kick Inside.

Some time later this year they announced there would be a second
round of vinyl rereleases in autumn, then including Hounds Of Love.
I even remember having seen Oct. 6 as a release date, but the albums
never surfaced, at least not here. Neither there were ads by any of 
the mailorder companies. So I think the release has been put off
or cancelled.
