i also recommend rising mixes, an e.p. on which several hipster
production teams rework material from the original rising album.
tricky, thurston moore of sonic youth, cibo matto, ween and the aba
all-stars (a beastie boys side project) are the collaborators, and
there's also CD-rom content and a new 30-minute song whose title escapes
me. the ween remix of ask the dragon is phenomenal, and thruston moore's
take on rising is freaky, nightmare-inducing and wonderful.
