 Being a resident of the Emerald Isle, I think it is my duty to
inform the lovehounds of the great Irish local delicacy of Soda Farls.Pleas
pay attension now....


 The recipe is as follows


    You will need:-
        1/2 lb Plane Flour
        1 oz Margarine
       1/2 Tea spoon of salt
       1/2 Tea sppon baking soda
        and buttermilk to mix.


Measure and mix dry ingredients.Cut and rub in margarine.Mix with
buttermilk ,on a floured board, to a nice dough and roll 1/4in thick.
Cut into 4 pieces.Place on hot girdle(grill).Cooks 5-7
minutes on each side and cool...
  For oven soda add 2 teaspoon of sugar.Cut into two and roll out
1/2in thick and bake in hot oven.
