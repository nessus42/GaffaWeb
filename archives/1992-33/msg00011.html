My first experience with Katey Music was when I was
asked by my boyfriend to listen to some music that he 
had just bought.

He made me lie down in a darkened room with the music
loud through excellent headphones. In my humble
opinion this is one of the *best* ways to listen
to music.

He played me "Breathing" and then "Under Ice". From that
moment I was hooked....

After (in a short amount of time...) I had purchased some
more disks, I then asked my sister to listen to 3 songs.
In the same manner (dark room, loud fully enclosed headphones)
I played her "Breathing", then "And Dream of Sheep??" (I'm
guessing titles here) I followed this by "Waking the Witch",
you've never seen anyone jump like my sister did..
"Wake up!"

Debra.
