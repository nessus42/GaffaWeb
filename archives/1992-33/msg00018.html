stev0@sti.com (Steve Berlin) writes:

>Now, what *I* think is interesting is that I've yet to hear of anyone
>becoming a Katefan through what most of us consider to be here best
>album - The Dreaming.

Hmmm. It's undoubtedly the most popular albums that bring Kate to the
attention of many people. They then cause many people who never really
knew Kate to like Kate a lot, and go and buy all her other albums and
become a real Katefan.

That is, in fact, what happened to me. (sort of). A friend of mine had
just been given 'The Whole Story', and I listened to it and recalled a
song that I had heard of before, namely 'Babooshka'. I remember as a 
young kid, seeing the Babooshka video. It is a _very_ memorable video,
well it must be since I remembered it after about five or six years (or
however long it was). hearing this song again intrigued me, so I listened
to the rest of the album and came to love it. Eventually this friend had
bought all of her albums, and I taped them ('cos at the time I couldn't
afford to buy them), but now I have the beautiful box of this woman's
work, and I'm happy. Favourite album ...? I dunno. It depends very much
on how I feel at the time.
