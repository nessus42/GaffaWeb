	I just wanted to briefly mention two bizzare kate related happenings
in my life. The first is that I bought Never For Ever (sp?). This is not
in itself bizarre, of course, but what was particularly strange was the fact 
that it was marked with the proper stuff on side on and two (it was a cass.)
and the cass. case was normal, and shrinkwrapped closed.. the music was not
Kate.. it was maybe Sammy Hagar or something.. I wound up stopping in the 
middle of a street and getting out of my truck to view the tape via headlight.
to no avail.. a friend of mine says sometimes the wrong spools get put into
the wrong cases or something...

	The second thing is birefly this: two years ago I introduced my 
then-fiancee to Kate's music. She wouldnt hear it. plugged her ears and 
carried on about her 'sqwauking like glenda the good witch'. I was much
bereaved, and to put it mildly, as the above text reads, I should have 
known.
