>Now, what *I* think is interesting is that I've yet to hear of anyone

Well, it looks like I picked a winner.  Hi.  My first Kate experience
was last Monday, the 16th.  No kidding.  The album I chose was none
other than The Dreaming.

I stumbled on to this news group about a week or so ago, read the FAQ
and was so struck by your passionate loyalty that I decided to see (hear)
for myself.  I had absolutely no idea as to what album to buy for my 
initiation (the current thread of reminiscent discussion was only just
beginning) and was too shy to ask so I chose THE ONE which contained the 
"title song" of this newsgroup.

Wow.

After the smoke cleared, I zeroed in on 3 cuts that became instant favorites:
Pull Out the Pin, The Dreaming, and All the Love.  I don't know why; it's
just the way it worked.

I'd heard of Kate before some years ago but for some reason never heard any
of her stuff.  And whenever I was looking for something different in the
local record store I never seemed able to remember her as a possibility.

I'm on the way to becoming a fan, if not a follower.  My only regret is that
I've started so late and have so much ground to make up.
So I guess it is possible to become hooked through The Dreaming...
