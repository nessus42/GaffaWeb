Subject: Kate Bush/Stevie Nicks collector's magazine


I just got something very odd from Germany.  It's called _Dreaming
Fairies_, and they call themselves "The Collector's Magazine:  Stevie
Nicks, Kate Bush"

It is what it proclaims to be:  a magazine all about Kate and Stevie
collectable items.  Most of the first issue is made up of pages of people
listing their collections in vague ways  (e.g. just counting the number
of 7" singles without saying what they are), with some people asking for
trades for specific items.

There are also some pages with photographs of collectable items and
discussions about them, as well as some ads from people trying to sell
long lists of Kate things.
