i join jeff burka in commending mike for his work in expanding the
vital KTgif stores.  it is therefore with the utmost humility that
i suggest that the best way to distribute large files like gifs is to 
archive them on "eddie.mit.edu" for anonymous ftp access.  admittedly,
some people don't have ftp capability, but then again many people 
can't display gifs... and between the newsgroup and the mailing list
these files can eat up a lot of bandwidth...

i am hardly an expert on these matters -- please forgive if i am
incorrect in this assertion.
