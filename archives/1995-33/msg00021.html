I've just heard a few CDs by HEATHER NOVA, and she's definitely worth 
checking out if you love Kate Bush.  Her song structures and singing 
style are definitely influenced by Kate.  New CD is "Oyster" (Work/Sony 
Records)--listen to "Heal" and "Island" and then tell me she isn't a 
post-modern Kate Bush!
