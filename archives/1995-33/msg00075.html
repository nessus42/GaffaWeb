In article <45pg5p$k2u@news.unicomp.net>, mmoore@prysm.com wrote:

> Is anyone familiar with the band Sunday All Over the World?
> This band has been described as a cross between Kate Bush 
> and King Crimson.    I have the only CD I've ever found, and 
> the description is correct.  The band included Robert Fripp and
> his wife Toyah Wilcox.  Toyah does remind many people of Kate.

Indeed, it also includes Trey Gunn, who is in the latest Crimson
incarnation as well as the last Sylvian/Fripp albums. This is an excellent
album, and sadly overlooked by the entire world, it seems. I often see it
used for a measly four bucks.

> Has Toyah done any other music??

I believe she has two solo albums, one of which Fripp appears on. I sat
behind Toyah and Fripp's sister when Fripp played here a few years back.
She's stunning. I didn't have the nerve to speak to her though.
