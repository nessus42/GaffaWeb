Just a couple of quick comments:

Kate's music has changed a great deal since HoL.  Her songs have gotten 
more personal - more about her real life.  The experimental side of The 
Dreaming and HoL is gone, for the most part.  Sarah is a KT fan, just 
like us, and as such is entitled to her opinion.  FWIW, I can see her 
point.  I like the more personal approach to the lyrics, but miss the 
craziness.  (Can I have it all now?  Maybe?)

And, again, FWIW, I think Sarah's FTE is an excellent album.  She seems 
to have combined some of Kate's ideas with a more folk approach - reminds 
me of Kate on tranquilizers.

Stuart/Kate - you are insane.  I have a couple of questions for you.  
Hopefully now we can finally get some definitive answers.  So, with 
tongue firmly planted in cheek, away we go:

1)  What does "gaffa" mean?
2)  What's the backwards stuff at the end of "Leave it Open?"
3)  What's an IED?

and last but not least.....

4)  When do you plan to tour again?
