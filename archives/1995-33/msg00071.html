a biography of Woody Hayes I once read. It was very touching. Here are
the original lyrics:


I still dream of the Rose Bowl.
I wake up crying.
We're making plays and it's just in reach
Till the Big Ten title escapes me.

You're like my defensive line
That ground them underfoot.
What made them special
Made them dangerous
But they graduate
And forget

	But everytime we play
	I knock one in the head
	Like a long, high kick--
	Ooh, I just know that something good is going to happen.
	But just saying it won't make it happen you bums -- go out and 
		kick some butt!

On top of the polls
Before the Michigan game
You could see them coming.
They looked too small
In their blue and gold
To be a threat to the scarlet and gray.

Behind the line
I can't hide him
