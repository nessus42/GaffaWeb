There's a difference between creativity and integrity. Playing a character
in a song or even writing under a pseudonym fall in one class of behavior.
But, somehow, pretending to be someone else while she lurks around on her
fans' newsgroup, seems, I dunno ... different. Anybody know what I'm
talking about? Like how would everybody feel if you found out after all
these years that I am really Kate Bush. You'd all feel pretty weird about
it. I mean, it would be one thing if Kate popped up and said, "You know,
I've been reading this group for four years." A lot of us would be pretty
embarrassed. But if I suddenly revealed myself as Kate, well, that would
be spooky, knowing that I've not only been reading, but participating and
offering opinions about myself. I dunno, maybe it's no different. Just
random thoughts.
