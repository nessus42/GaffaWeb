I got both Prostitute and Ophelia's Shadow, two of Toyah's solo albums
on CD in New York City on the Caroline label.  Both albums are
excellent.  Toyah's not much like Kate, but both of the above albums
are quite artistic and innovative - esp. Prostitute.

Toyah also collaborated with Tony Banks (of Genesis fame) on a
beautiful, but obscure song called Lion of Symmetry.  It's on his solo
album called Soundtracks.

There you go!
