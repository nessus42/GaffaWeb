Subject: KT NEWS FLASH

    "Davy Spillane, whose credits include work with Moving Hearts,
Gerry Rafferty, Van Morrison and Mary Coughlan, has recorded uillean
pipes, whistles and low whistles for Kate Bush's next LP. Bush, a
Moving Hearts fan, also attended The Davy Spillane Band's debut London
concert (promoting the Atlantic Bridge album on Tara/Cooking Vinyl) at
Harlesden's Mean Fiddler Club."

-- Taken from Ken Hunt's London music news column in the
   June '88 issue of Tower Records' _Pulse!_ magazine, p. 22.

-- Andrew Marvick
