out there SOMEWHERE, 'cause y'all have been talking it up for awhile.

that has this (seemingly) rare jewel?  I'd be willing to pay cost +
postage/shipping/graft to get it sent here, or if some Georgia Tech
folks can find it in Atlanta, I can make the 4 hour drive to get it.

Probably best to send responses by e-mail.  I appreciate any help that
anyone can offer in this noble quest.
