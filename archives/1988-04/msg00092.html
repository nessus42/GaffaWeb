is an all-time record here....thanx |>oug.....  now for a few
comments....

to Jonathan Druckman's comments about no outstanding music this
year...

have you bought the new album by Tracy Chapman???  I wouldn't classify
her as "alternative" in any way, but her music is excellent and her
lyrics gripping.  Her primary emphasis musically is her voice and her
acoustic guitar, but it's too black a style to be saddled with the
"folkie" label.  Her songs are about life in the ghetto and on the
other side of the tracks.

My favorite two songs are "Across the Lines" about a race riot, and 
"Behind the Wall":

	Last night I heard the screaming
	Loud voices behind the wall
	Another sleepless night for me
	It won't do no good to call
	The police
	Always come late
	If they come at all
	...
	Last night I heard the screaming
	Then a silence that chilled my soul
	I prayed that I was dreaming
	when I saw the ambulance in the road
	...

not all of the album is nearly so depressing, but it is all
emphatically "GOOD STUFF!"

besides, if rumor is true, Kate is releasing her album in september,
and there's NO WAY that could be bad!
