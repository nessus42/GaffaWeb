Subject: concert announcement for Love Hounds

"An Evening with Laurie Anderson and Roger Miller" is happening
Thursday June 16 at the Berkelee Performance Center in Boston. Tickets
are $15. As of this June 4, there were still floor and balcony tickets
available. You can get tickets from the Berkelee box office (cash
only) or from Ticketron or Out of Town News in Harvard Square.
