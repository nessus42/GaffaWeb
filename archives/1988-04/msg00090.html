    bottom.

    self-destructed!!!   It sounded okay when I bought it, and it be-
    came unlistenable.  I just flipped the puppy over  and,  low  and
    BEHOLD,  GHOSTLY  see-through  writing...   Where the printing is

    residue.   One  area  that  has a 2mm wide black stripe has a 2mm
    wide AURA, which means something seriously traveled.   I  thought
    it  was  only $7 (3 years ago) cuz it was only twenty-two minutes
    long, but maybe someone *knew*...
