I've been losing myself almost daily for the past six months in a
tape that a friend made for me of the 'B-sides' stuff from the
corresponding two discs in TWW.  As most of you know, no true KaTe
fan should be without these tracks.  At the time of the release of
TWW, there was talk on GAFFA to the tune of 'it sure would be nice
if these would be released separately from the whole package.'
Has anyone heard, even remotely, any hints that this might occur?

Greg.
