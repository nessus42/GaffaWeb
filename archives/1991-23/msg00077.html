         ***********           A NIGHT WITH               *************


                                  ALDOUSH

                                    AND

                            THE HUMAN EXCHANGE   


         ****************************************************************

         Aldoush and The Human Exchange will perform contemporary Persian 
         folk music in Farsi, Shirazi, Lori and other Persian dialogs as 
         well as their own compositions in English, which will present 
         percussive instruments from Iran.

         Their music is a combination of Folk, Rock, Jazz and Newage with 
         an original sound and spiritual messages.


              Date:        Sunday July 14, 1991

              Time:        8:00 PM

              Place:       Ashkenaz in Berkeley, 1317 San Pablo Blvd.

              Directions:  Take HWY80, Gilman exit East. Left on SanPablo.

              Admission:   $ 6.00

              Information: Call 415 525-5054  USA


         Aldoush and The Human Exchange have a very successful album out 
         called " Yesterday - Today - Tomorrow " which is available in the 
         local Persian stores.


         The band's line up consists of the following:

              Aldoush:    Lead Vocals, Guitars and Morteza(!)

              Scott:      Soprano and Alto Saxophone, Clarinet and vocal

              Jeff:       Bass

              Tom:        Latin Percussion

              Shahram:    Zarb and Daff


         Have Fun
