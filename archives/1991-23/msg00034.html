Has anyone noticied the "Hush-Hush" whispered after Kate
breates 'Hello Earth' at the beginning of the song.  It's either
Del, Paddy, or Dr. B - I can't tell but I just noticied it
today.  Also in _Watching you Without Me_ Kate adds these
little Whoop-Whoops in the middle which I had never heard
before....bizarre to say the least.

If I were to meet Kate about the only thing I would have to
say is "Thanks for music."  After that I would probably
fall dead on the spot while muttering "Oh Kate, Oh Kate, Oh
Kate" under my breath.  But if I remained alive by some
miracle I would probably ask her if she could tell me a story
and then swap one back. Then I would give her a twix bar
and ask for a photo and say "Keep the ball rolling."  Then I
would go home and savor the day forever.
