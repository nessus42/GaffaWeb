LAA on MTV

From: vishal@m2.csc.ti.com (Vishal Markandey)
Date: Mon, 20 Nov 89 11:10:50 CST
Subject: LAA on MTV


"Love and Anger" seems to be getting a good rotation on MTV. I caught it twice
by accident this weekend. Late Saturday night and late Sunday afternoon. It was
also played on this Sunday's "120 Minutes". They have a countdown called the
"Top 10 College Chart Countdown". Last Sunday _TSW_ was not even part of this
countdown, this Sunday it entered the countdown at Number 1 !!! Apparently it 
is highly unusual for an album to enter the countdown at the number 1 spot. The
VJ said something like "this hasn't happened since the invention of the wheel".
Later in the show, Don and Karen Peris of "The Innocence Mission" were 
interviewed, and they mentioned that they were influenced by KT, Peter Gabriel 
and Jane Sibberry's work.
