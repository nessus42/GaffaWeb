_TSW_ continued to hold the top spot on this Sunday's "Top 10 College Chart 
Countdown" on MTV's "120 Minutes". Apparently they come up with the rankings
after compiling information from college and alternative radio stations around
the country. And of course, they played the _LAA_ video. 

Ofra Haza was interviewed on VH1 during the Thanksgiving weekend. She has a 
new album coming out January 15. I think it is called "The Desert Wind", but I
could be wrong.
