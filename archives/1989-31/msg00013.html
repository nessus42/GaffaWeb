Radio news

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Mon, 20 Nov 89 14:38:55 PST
Subject: Radio news


KITS has finally started to play "Love And Anger."  (Remember, they've
been playing "The Sensual World" for a long time.)

Last night, Audio-Vidya played it, and then she said:

"Live-105.  Had a request for some brand-new Kate Bush.  That's from the
album _The Sensual World_: 'Love And Anger.'  And she's also got a video
clip of that song out, and you can see her performing live in that clip.
I guess maybe they just kind of staged it so it looks like she's doing a
live show, but I hear that Kate *is* reconsidering touring again, which
will be the first time in, I think, about ten years that she'll be hitting
the road.  So, Kate Bush fans, cross your fingers and see what she's
going to do."

I advise all Love-Hounds to take this with a grain of salt, and don't hold
your breath.  I've heard rumors like this before.

Also, last night I heard KBLX play "The Sensual World" for the second
time.  I guess that means they are a full-fledged Kate-playing radio
station, although I don't really think she fits their format.  I won't
complain, though!
