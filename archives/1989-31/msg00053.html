_TSW_ on MTV countdown, also Ofra Haza news

From: vishal@m2.csc.ti.com (Vishal Markandey)
Date: Mon, 27 Nov 89 12:43:53 CST
Subject: _TSW_ on MTV countdown, also Ofra Haza news


_TSW_ continued to hold the top spot on this Sunday's "Top 10 College Chart 
Countdown" on MTV's "120 Minutes". Apparently they come up with the rankings
after compiling information from college and alternative radio stations around
the country. And of course, they played the _LAA_ video. For once they even
retained the "Yeah" at the end (they usually cut it off :-().

Ofra Haza was interviewed on VH1 a couple of days ago. She has a new album
coming out January 15. I think it is called "The Desert Wind", but I could be
wrong.
