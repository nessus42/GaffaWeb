I just found what appears to be a new folio of Kate's music. It is called
Kate Bush Collection - Off The Record. It contains orchestration of the
following songs.

Wuthering Heights
Babooshka
Army Dreamers
Between A Man and a Woman
Cloudbusting
Hounds of Love
The Kick Inside
Love and ANger
Running Up That Hill
The Man with the Child in his Eyes
The Sensual World
This Woman's Work

It is published by IMP ISBN 1-85909-169-5, price c. 24-95 pounds.

It seems like full transcriptions - there are c. 200 pages with 7 pages
of colour and B&W photos. The transcription of Wuthering Heights covers
33 pages alone. So if you want to play along with the Bouzouki part on The
Sensual World, or just dieing to know what notes the Valiha plays on
Love and Anger then this is the book for you!
