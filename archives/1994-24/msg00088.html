I've sent this to gaffa at least 3 times and I haven't seen it appear
yet.  I don't know what the problem is, but if anyone is seeing this
for the umteenth time, my hearty apologies.


Happy Rhodes' 7th album (8th if you include her compilation
_RhodeSongs_) is completely finished, recorded, mixed, mastered,
and is now at the CD plant.  Street date is August 31, 1994.

Here's the track listing for the album:

Building The Colossus

1)  Hold Me
2)  Just Like Tivoli
3)  Dying
4)  Collective Heart
5)  Building The Colossus
6)  Omar
7)  Pride
8)  You Never Told Me 
9)  If I Ever See The Girl Again
10) Down, Down
11) Big Dreams, Big Life
12) Glory

The CD runs at just over 58 minutes.

All songs written by Happy Rhodes
Produced by Happy Rhodes and Kevin Bartlett

Recorded at Cathedral Sound Studios in Rensselaer, New York
Mixed at Bearsville Studios in Bearsville, New York
Digital Mastering by Dr. Toby Mountain at Northeastern Digital Recording 

Jerry Marotta plays drums on tracks 2, 4 and 8.
David Torn plays guitar on tracks 1, 4, 7 and 10.
Monica Wilson plays cello on track 11.

If a full tour becomes a reality, I'll post about it.
