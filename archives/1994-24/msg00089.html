A thousand pardons to those who will see this multiple times, and to
those who can't get World Cafe.  I'm one of them...sigh...everyone 
listening will be hearing new HR songs before me...boo hoo. I've been
a Happy fan since 1988...spare a sympathetic thought for me while (if)
you listen to it :-).

There are other goodies here too.

Forwarded from the folk_music list:
=======================================================================

FROM:  Bruce Warren, Associate Producer, World Cafe/WXPN
RE:  IN-STUDIO VISITS, PLAYLIST, AND GENERAL OBESRVATIONS

Hi folks. We're back and trying to keep this thing updated.
Here is a list of our in-studio visits for the
month of August and September.

August  

17 - World Cafe "World Premiere" of the new Happy Rhodes album,
          Building the Colossus

18 - Me'Shell Ndegeocello and her band funk out on the Cafe with songs
          from her brilliant debut, Plantation Lullabies
19 - Guest dj Brad Roberts of the Crashtest Dummies picks his favorite
     records as Musician Magazine's Artist of the Month
20 - Encore performances from Michael Hedges and Luka Bloom
23 - Dave Alvin unplugs with songs from his new King of California
25 - Once a New Wave/Punk idol, now a "sensitive singer-songwriter",
     Tom Robinson stops by to perform songs from his new album
26 - From Australia, Frente' make their Cafe debut.
27 - Encore performances from Milla and Loreena McKennit
31 - -Freedy Johnston unleashes songs from his new Elektra release,
     This Perfect World.

September

1 - From Canada, Blue Rodeo stop by the Cafe
2 - After Hours on the Cafe - We've collected the best cover songs
     from our  in-studio peformers. You will not want to miss this
     special show!!!!
3 - Encore performances from Toad the Wet Sprocket and Jackopierce
7 - The World Cafe presents a tribute to singer-songwriter Mark Heard
9 - Can you believe it?  The Spin Doctors live on the Cafe
10 - Returning again to the Cafe are John Gorka and The Subdudes in
          the studio
13 - Acoustic blues from OKeh recording artist Keb' Mo'
15 - Guest dj MC 900 FT Jesus spins his own hip-hop, reggae and
     jazz records
16 - Jeffrey Gaines returns to the Cafe with his full band.

========================================================================
