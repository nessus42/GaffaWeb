future sound of london

check out the Future Sound Of London "Lifeforms Paths 1-7" single, which
features Liz Fraser of Cocteau Twins on vocals.  this, to me, is real music.
gorgeous swathes of sound flung into the sonic blender, only to emerge
dripping with chunks of vocals that sound like an angel's cries captured on
disc.

the album "Lifeforms" is also excellent but doesn't feature Liz anywhere, so
if that's what you're jonesin' for, make sure you get the single.  the
single sports a reddish cover with a photographically inverted picture of a
girl holding out her hand.  the girl is also on the album cover, but that
one doesn't have the predominant reddish tint.

Future Sound Of London - Lifeforms Paths 1-7
Astralwerks ASW 6114
