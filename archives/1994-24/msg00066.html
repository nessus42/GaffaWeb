The Tree writes:
>I watched Tape B--all of it--last night, and I just wanted to add my voice
>to the accolades.  This is a *treasure*.

   On behalf of the treers (right up your alley), thanks and you're
welcome.

>The Night Flight interview was indeed hellish.  I, too, got a kick out of
>that incredible Freudian slip ("prat").

   On our first trip to England we showed this video to Kris & Peter &
friends. They found this *extremely* funny. Sorry about the screw-up
with cutting off the ending. It was a result of trying to edit two
different tapes in two different editing rooms at the same time at
2AM.

>I realized, watching the "Experiment IV" clip, that I've never seen the
>majority of Kate's actual promo videos.  Anyone know where I can get hold of
>them or see them?

   We edited out the official promo videos to avoid infringing on Kate's
copyrights any more than absolutely necessary. I still see copies of
_The Sensual World_ and _The Whole Story_ in shops.

>"This Infant Kiss" was *brilliant*.  Great job, Chris (that *was* yours,
>wasn't it?)!

   Many thanks. Actually it was another collabrative effort. I did most
of the editing as a way of learning how to edit. The first version was
started on the night the company I worked for for received the editing
machines. I made a version and Vickie and our late friend Tim made
suggestions for edits and scenes. Several of the better choices are
Vickie's. So, while I'm credited, there are a number of others'
important contributions. Glad you liked it.
