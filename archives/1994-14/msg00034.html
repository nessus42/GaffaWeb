fmcquarr@upei.ca (Fiona McQuarrie) says:
 
>If she wants to expand her fan base beyond a devoted following, she NEEDS
>to do, as Chris Williams has pointed out, pretty much what Tori Amos
>does, and that is "work the system". 
 
But who sez that KaTe *wants* to expand her fandom at the expense of
"working the system"?  May She not be content with having only a small
following and keeping her own privacy?  You'll probably say "Who doesn't
want more money, more recognition?"  I'll offer myself as a
comparison.  I'm a self-employed software consultant/programmer,
and I only do work that I enjoy doing, sometimes goofing off for 
a day doing other things.  As a result, I make "just enough".  Of course,
if I find a high-paying, steady job, (as my parents etc. would like me
to do), I can afford things like $10 CD singles which have only ONE song
that I don't already have.  But I consider that a "sell-out" and 
would rather live a rather meager, but to me rewarding, life,
than do what my friends do - slaving away at their desks and counting
the days until their next vacation.  Some may say I'm an under-achiever.  
But that is their judgement.  I don't *have* to be successful for anyone 
but *myself*.
 
I realize that people only want KaTe to gain world-wide recognition and 
appreciation.  I do too, but let's not say what she should do or *needs*
to do, as if she doesn't know.

Oh, and thanks for listening to my personal grudegs :-)
