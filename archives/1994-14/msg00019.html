The radio station WHFS (Annapolis, MD) had an interview this morning
with Tori Amos's mother.  Apparently, Tori's on tour in Europe right now,
and will be coming over to this country in a month or two.

Specifically, she's going to give a concert at the Warner Theater in
Washington, DC at 8:00 pm on June 20th.  Tickets go on sale next
Friday (the 13th!) at noon, available through all TicketMaster
outlets.

ObTrivia:  Tori was accepted to study at the Peabody Institute in
Baltimore, MD at the age of 5 - the youngest person ever accepted.
