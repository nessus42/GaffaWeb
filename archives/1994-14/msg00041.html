Since nobody seemed to know when Paddy's birthday was, when I asked
here a while ago, I asked the man himself yesterday; it's the 9th of
December, if anyone's interested.

Incidentally, I can recommend giving his new CD "Skyscraping" a try.
I bought it on spec, and it's not at all what I expected; as Paddy
said to me "it's not _too_ folky" - if you can imagine an acoustic
version of Tangerine Dream, it's a bit like that.
