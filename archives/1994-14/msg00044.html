> What does the following line (Army Dreamers) mean:

> Tears o'er a tin box

I'm assuming you're not a native English speaker, so I don't mean to be
patronising.  It simply refers to a mother crying over her dead son -
"o'er" being a poetic contraction of "over", and "a tin box" being the
sort of coffin that a dead soldier's remains would be flown home in.
