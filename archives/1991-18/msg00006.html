     Hi all!
     I walked to the Rose Records here on the UofI campus yesterday to
ask about their Happy Rhodes shipment.  The clerk I talked to had never
heard of her, so she asked the guy in back.  The first thing he asked me
was "How did you find out about Happy?"  I told him that I knew someone
from the company, and he said "Is it Vickie?"
     So who's the love-hound in C-U?  Apparently someone had already gone in
and asked about it!  Their shipment has been delayed for some reason, as
all the other stores have received their shipments.  Pooey.
     Now to the "Stuff" part:
     Is there a Chicago Katemas party planned?  I'd love to have one here in
Champaign, but the only Kate fan that's in town that I know is our own
David Thomas (I'm catching your show tonight!) and I doubt anyone would
so terribly attracted to Champaign that they'd drive more than, say, 10 miles
to get here.
     And Chicago is so close!  It's the only real city in Illinois, frankly.
     Impatient for Happy Rhodes, and new KaTe stuff...
     A miserable weasel?  ;->
