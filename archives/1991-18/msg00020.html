Subject: 4Bay Area Hounds: Record swap June 7

Hi Love-Hounds, 

For those in the Bay Area (or those who might still benefit by mail order),
the twice-annual Record Swap put on by the college radio station KFJC (a 
source for Kate music at 89.7) is scheduled for JUNE 9 SUNDAY at Foothill
College (off of Hiway 280) from 0900 to 1600 (or thereabouts, the flyer
says).  This is the best swap in the Bay Area (IMHO) to find good rare 
stuff.  Always lots of Kate stuff around.  Some major advertisers in 
_Goldmine_ show up.  Joe Castle from LA might be there with his great 
Kate selection (special consideration paid to Love-Hounds).  Also a great
place to find other quality music and videotapes and who knows what else
if the heat's not <ahem> on... Good source for Jane stuff, too. :-) 

Don't miss it! 
Larry (l-h@cup.portal.COM)

p.s. - i'd be glad to look for something for you outoftowners.  Lemme know.
