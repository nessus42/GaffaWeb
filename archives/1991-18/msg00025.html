I got my Happy Rhodes Warpaint cd today! 

I love it! 

My favorite song so far is "feed the fire". 

I sent Happy a bunch of printouts of stuff from the group
about her albums, (i hope no one minds!!) and she didn't
write back but kevin bartlett did, and thanked me quite
a bit and said they thought it was quite fascinating and 
that they'd always love to see more. 

I can't wait to get all the tapes! (when i can afford it ;)
but dave steiner (my boss, friend, and fellow kate fan)
says he likes the cd too and we can each get two of the
tapes for now :) :) :) 

Just wanted to thank you all for recommending Happy.
