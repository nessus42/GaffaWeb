OK, I'm impressed.  Well, all right, make that very impressed.

I'm impressed that after being directed here by a fellow AOLer, and after
posting a general request in rec.music.gaffa for the 1994 Lovehounds
Collection, within a day or two I receive an offer from one of you
(you know who you are and thank you again) to make copies for me.  
No charge, no strings attached
-- I send out blanks, cover 2-way shipping, and get the LH Collection.  Then,
adding insult to injury, I receive my copies 6 (six) days (not
weeks...months...) after *sending out* my blanks to my benefactor.  Who lives
5 states away.  And then the tapes themselves... the selections, the quality,
the packaging, the inserts... don't you guys realize you're shaming
professionals who make a living distributing products not half as formidable?

And on those tapes -- yes, the performances were terrific (RotH live with
Gilmour is unbelievable... and it was the first time I'd seen Kate's SNL
appearance since 1978!), but more than that the extensive
interview pieces gave me an impressive overview of Kate the person.  Which
gave me some insight into the reasons behind the sometime-Beatlemania-like
fanaticism which frequently surrounds KT. (Katemania?)  She is a
beautiful lady -- both inside and out.

Which only served to whet my appetite for more -- as well as to bemoan the
disappearing act she has forever pulled on us Stateside Bushophiles.  Rather
than take my frustration out on some hapless pedestrian -- someone from EMI
Artist Promotions perhaps? -- I composed the following little ditty:
