on the opening act, i think 'fox' was saying that as a kid, the person in the
story was weirded out by eccentric female performers and now, ironically, she
is a fan of kate--one of those, if not the premiere, crazy women. i dont think
'fox' was saying that kate WAS the crazy woman . . .

as for the gaffa thread, as with most metaphors, isn't it great that it can
have such a diversity of interpretations? i don't think that if we say it was
'just' a reference to gaffer's tape that we are saying that kate was singing
about an unfortunate accident in the machine shop that got her tangled up for
the day. it's still a METAPHOR, a good one, b/c she coined a word that could
have its roots in many different places . . . .
