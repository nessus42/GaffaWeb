Does it not strike anyone else that it's significant that "Gaffa" is
capitalised in the lyrics?  I can see two possible reasons for this:

1) (The prosaic explanation) Kate has heard people talking about gaffer
   tape, and thought it was a brand name.

2) (My preferred theory) It's deliberately capitalised and misspelt
   to make it resemble a place name, and thereby add another level of
   meaning.
