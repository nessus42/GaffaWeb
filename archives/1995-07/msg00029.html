to ron girardin, who was kind enough to pick me up a copy in canada and mail it
to me . . . .

fantastic!

viewings, but thanks, ron, and thanks, kate!

watch TLTCTC and the Singles File videos i brought for him. he was blown away!

when he called it 'a visual feast.' i felt like i'd racked up community service
hours . . . . .
