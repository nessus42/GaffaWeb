This is a discography listing all available CD bootlegs
By Tori Amos. Most bootlegs are live recordings, usually
acquired by various illegitimate means or radio 
broadcasts. For sake of simplicity, I am assembling
ths list in alphabetical order instead of chronological.
If you have any comments about either format, please
e-mail me. I will try to update at least once a month,
if possible.

If You have any CDs that are not included here, please
e-mail me at fuzzbox@world.std.com with all relevant
information. Also e-mail me if you have any other 
questions about any of these CDs.
                                  -Jason

(Present list contains 58 CDs)

This CD is either an incredible ripoff or a saving grace,
depending on how you look at it. It assembles all of the
B-sides off of Little Earthquakes, except "Little 
Earthquakes (Live)" and "Crucify (Live)", both off of
the Crucify live limited box, and "Ring My Bell" off of
the Ruby Trax compilation. Quality is very good, but
it's a waste of money if you have all the singles
already. (like I did)

Be careful with this Disc! apparently, it has been 
reissued by the same company, with the exact same track
listing, but with completely different artwork. The 
catalog number has remained the same.
