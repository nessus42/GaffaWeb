If you ask any Swedish broadcasting or stage engineer what they think of 
when they hear the word gaffa, they'll say tape, the broad, silvery tape. 
Some will say that any broad tape could be called gaffa tape, some will 
claim that gaffa is a brand name for a very special type of tape, generally 
unavailable in Sweden.

A year ago I worked in a studio where the chief engineer had the reputation 
of fixing every broke piece of machinery by "tying it together with gaffa 
tape" to quote one of my colleauges (who never have heard of neither 
rec.music.gaffa, Suspended In Gaffa nor the Internet).
