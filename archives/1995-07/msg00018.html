> I think that Gaffa is the allegory for a self-sabotaging state of mind,

   Please do. This is exactly Vickie's defininition of the word gaffa,
and fits in well with my exploration/theory of the meaning of gaffa
as a plural of "gaffe." 

   Does anyone else see what I mean? Can you imagine Kate trying to 
explain to an interviewer...

Q: What does the word "gaffa" mean?
A: Well, that's rather complicated...er...I was reading one of my 
brother's books...and...uhm...

   At the time she was still having to fight the impression that the 
British press had of her as a "fruitcake." Bringing up Kabelistic texts
is *not* a good way of doing that. Gaffer's tape was a joke that has
obtained the status of gospel.
