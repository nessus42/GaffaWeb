Get the _LIVE_ Sarah McLachlan CD.  I am not a big fan of live albums,
but this one is simply incredible--Sarah and her band live show a
complete mastery of the songs that I find much more convincing than
the studio albums.  _LIVE_ is by far my favorite Sarah...the live
version of "Black" alone is worth the price of the CD (if you're
willing to overlook the usual live recording imperfections--which
aren't particularly bad on this disk).
