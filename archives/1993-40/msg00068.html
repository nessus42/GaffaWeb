There's a really scary movie on TBS this afternoon that features
Joan Collins. As I was zipping through the channels, I caught a glimpse
of Ms. COllins dressed as Cleopatra for some sort of costume party, and
I got this really unsettling vision of Kate in the year 2013 on some
kind of nostalgia/revival tour, looking just like Joan and warbling
a muzaky version of Egypt. It disturbs me, but while I find Kate quite
beautiful and Joan COllins pretty much repulsive, there is a startling
resemblance. Maybe Kate should make plans to pull a Greta Garbo in the 
next few years. Of course, dancers do age more gracefully than most 
people, but I'm sort of frightened...
