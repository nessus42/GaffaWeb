The box set contains all the albums plus two additional LPs or CDs of songs
that did not appear on any albums.  All the material has been previously
released, either on albums or as singles.  

For someone who already has all the albums, the box set is a very expensive
way to get the two discs of non-album material.  For me, it was worth it to
get some of the singles in CD form. 

I've seen postings about a re-release of the box set in Canada.  You might
try a Canadian dealer that ships overseas, such as THE CD BAR. You can
contact them via Internet - cdbar@sizone.jaywon.pci.on.ca - or
