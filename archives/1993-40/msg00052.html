I think the line that is hard to hear in TRS goes:    

"The minute I put them on, I knew I had done something wrong
All her gifts for the dance had gone, the red shoes can't stop dancing"

she finds they do not confer the same gifts on her.  A little like
putting on dragon-scale armor only to discover it is cursed (for any
