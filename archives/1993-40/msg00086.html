Fiona McQuarrie (fmcquarr@upei.ca) wrote:
: Has anyone heard a singer called Heidi Berry? I saw an ad for her record
: in the Utne Reader. It sounded kind of interesting, but then when has a
: record company put out an ad saying "This artist sings like a drunk in
: a karaoke bar, don't waste your money" :)

She's very, very, very good!  Her latest, self-titled album is her first
big-label, domestic release.  Her first album, _Below the Waves_ (?) was
put out on Creation, and her second, _Love_, was issued on 4AD.  Each
album is slightly different in terms of style.  My favorite is probably
_Love_, but I also like _Heidi Berry_ a lot.  The music on the latter is
sort of a cross between the ethereal 4AD sound (if there is such a thing)
and folk.  Heidi Berry's voice is very smooth and flowing--quite mellow.

Well, I know my descriptions aren't the best, but I hope they help!  I
think you'd like Heidi Berry if you like Kate Bush, but I might be wrong.
