I just saw an unusual item in a pawn shop.  It's the Cocteaa Twins single
box set.  It was for $35.97.  I have a limited exposure to them, that
being "Blue Bell Knoll".  I liked it quite a bit, but should I invest in
this box?  It's a damn good price for it but $40 is still a lot of money
to spend on one item of music.  Anything in it a MUST have?  I appreciate
your help and your patience with this bit of NonKaTe.
On another note someone mentioned Sarah McLauchlin (sp?) having recorded a
song called "Solsbury Hill".  I can't believe that whoever wrote that
didn't recognize that that was a cover of the classic Peter Gabriel track!
:)  I thought being a Peter Gabriel fan was every KaTeFan's moral
obligation.  Hehehehe...
I am in breathless anticipation of the new Kate album.  "Big Stripey Lie"
gives me chills.  The new album is gonna re-explore a lot of avenues left
since "The Dreaming".  I truly can't wait.
