i wouldn't say they're all that, but they are pretty good.  lotsa
chunky guitar and nice wigged out female vox (are you listening
Richard Caldwell?  might satisfy your Caterwaul fix for awhile!)

singles (BlackerThreeTrackerTwo) has remixes by Nine Inch Nails,
Future Sound Of London and The Drum Club.  Now *this* is The Shit!!
The FSoL mix in particular is unbelievable.  Easily my favorite Curve
item to date.  Jon-Bob says "check it out."
