NEW ADDRESS FOR ECTO!!!


Ecto has MOVED!  Greg Bossert, the huedo psumble list moderator, has
moved to the beautiful state of California, and he took Ecto with him.
Here's part of his letter to the mailing list:

Date: Sat, 17 Feb 1996 21:09:11 -0800
From: Greg Bossert (bossert@clairseach.ecto.org)
Subject: A Matter of Importance to the List

'tis myself, your too-often ecto list manager, with the second half,
as it were, of a message i sent out six months ago.  at that time, i
announced that i was moving to california, and would soon be bringing
the list out to join me.  it is with happiness and relief that i
announce that Ecto has arrived at its new home:

   ecto@ecto.org

and is running on a spiffy sgi indy computer, with a spacious 2
gigabytes of diskspace ('twas spacious on my credit card, as
well... ;)

all other addresses have changed accordingly:

  ecto-request@ecto.org - send un/subscribe messages for loose mail here

  ecto-digest-request@ecto.org - ditto for the digest version


  owner-ecto@ecto.org OR
  owner-ecto-digest@ecto.org OR
  bossert@ecto.org - me! (and the place to send problems that can't
                     be handled by the mailing list software


  http://www.ecto.org/ecto - Web home to the mailing list, archives
  ftp://ftp.ecto.org - ftp access to archives


yes, the ftp archive is back, or rather, it will be soon.  please hold
off on accessing either the web site or the ftp site for a few more
days -- there is still a long list of things i need to straighten up.

please help me by spreading these new addresses around, particularly
where people *not* on the list now can see them.  

the situation at rutgers is shaky, and access through there 
could disappear any day now.  the sooner everyone uses the
new addresses, the better!

that's it for now!  i'll post more when the web site and archives gets
into shape.

greg bossert
-- bossert@ecto.org  --  bossert@sgi.com --
