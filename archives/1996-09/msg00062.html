For the record collectors out there in Love-Hounds land, a British band have
just released a new single with a cover version of Wuthering Heights as the
b-side. Here are the details:-

Can't Stop These Things b/w Wuthering Heights by China Drum
Catalogue number : MNT 8CD
Record Company : Beggars Banquet Records

As I said a few weeks ago it is a very fast guitar version of WH, and its 
better than Pat Benetar's version!
