braitman@sirius.com (Stephen M. H. Braitman) asked:

>How can we prevent the intrusion of stupid spam advertising in our newsgroup?

     It would be quite impossible to keep all the spam away, unless we
switched to a closed group/list, but there is a way to act about individual
spammers. You could try sending a note to their postmaster  - ie
postmaster@hostname.domain. He should be able to deal with the matter in the
appropriate way. Of course, if the spammer is posting from a fake addy, you
have to figure out what the real posing-host was. You can do that by looking
at the headers.
