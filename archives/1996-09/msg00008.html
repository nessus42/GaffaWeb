The Fox station in our area is running the Mystery Science Theatre 3000 Hour -
asyndicated version of the show that's on Comedy Central. For non-CC
subscribers, it's this show where two robots and a guy watch old movies and
make wisecracks about the action.

Anyhow, the film this week was that great epic "Viking Women And the Sea
Serpent"...and at the start of the film the "bad" Viking woman (you can tell
she is bad because she has long dark hair and all the other Viking babes have
long blond hair :) ) comes out from behind a tree and throws a spear. And one
of the robots says "Look! It's Kate Bush!"

Well, I was thrilled to bits....

Cheers, Fiona
