Just listening to some new demos, (thankyou so much Emmy!)
Organic Acid is just so immense, the collective fruits of the Bush 
family combine in a beautifully haunting song.
I presume it is John Carder Bush reciting the poem, Kate's singing is 
so eerie.

What is the origin of the covers of 'Long and Winding Rd' and 'Shes 
leaving home'?

The live version of Don't Give Up is fantastic. I presume its from 
the Peter Gabriel Earls Court concerts of 1987. My elder brother went 
and said that everyone went crazy as she walked out on stage, its 
nice to be able to hear the crowds surprised delight!

Ok, enough fun I'll plough through some lyrics now...(in the bath!)
