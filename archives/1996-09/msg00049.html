>By the way, no one has yet answered my question about whether the promo
>video of "December Will BE Magic Again" has ever been commercially
>released?

   No, it has not.

   Officially, there never has been a promo video for _December Will Be Magic 
Again_. It was a performance specifically for the _Winter Snowtime Special_
not for distribution by the record company.

    But a surprisingly good copy is available on "The Lovehounds Collection"
a group project. It was one of three video projects so far.

   The collection is distributed in it's entireity (two 2-hour tapes at SP 
speed only) for free by various Love-Hounds. All you have to do is find
an upper level dubber and send them two good quality tapes in a pre-
paid padded envelope.

   The only charge for this service is a promise to make copies for
anyone who needs a copy, and to only do so at SP speed to maintain
the quality of the collection.

    There will be another collection, as I just purchased a PAL machine
and am buying a PAL to NTSC converter. The next collection will
be produced and distributed in Hi-Fi. A PAL master tape is also
a possibility.
