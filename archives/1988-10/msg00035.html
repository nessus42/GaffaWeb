Feb 1988 records mini-LP, a lot of older songs finally get a chance to be heard
by a wider public. This is the record others have discussed. Idea for labelling
record sides (Sunny side up v. Over easy) original, band unaware that McCartney
did it 9 years earlier.

Touring fall 88, supporting (late lamented) Lyres, Dash Rip Rock, They Might Be
Giants etc. Must've supported Campers and/or Feelies at some point !!

Hope to have full LP out in the spring. Looking forward to it !!

Frank Orrall writes great original songs in diverse styles, I hope he gets heard
- in PDP i also hear snippets of acts as diverse as VU, Pogues, Plastic Ono Band
Talking Heads, Richard Thompson, 10000 Maniacs, and (yes !) Paul McCartney. 

Maybe I shouldn't have said anything ......


(aside: Orrall is cousin of power popper turned country songwriter Robert Ellis
Orrall, who put out some excellent records in the Joe Jackson/Nick Lowe mould a 
few years ago, one features Calene Carter on vocals, he was UK-based then)
