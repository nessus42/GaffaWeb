Hi, everybody.  Happy Holidays in advance.  I'm
laying my head on the chopping block now, because
I'm going to ask for some sleuthing help on a KT tune
that I can't give you much info on.  So anyway, here
goes.
The song is sung a capella, and it sounds like a
reworking of a traditional/old Irish/Celtic song...
maybe she wrote her own lyrics to it.  Part of the
words are something like:
My love has come, my love has gone
And nothing's left to warm me
Save for a (___) on the traveling winds
And no one there to hold me." (apologies to Kate)
  This was put on a tape by a friend of mine three yrs
ago, and my car stereo got hungry one day and 86'd it.
She taped it from her brother's collection, so she
hasn't a clue where it came from.  I know this doesn't
give you all much to work with, but I'd appreciate any
suggestions you may have.  This song was my first intro
to Kate and a nice one, at that.  Thanks in advance for
the help.
