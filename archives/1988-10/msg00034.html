POI DOG PONDERING is very much the product of the vision of one person,
Frank "Gumby" Orrall, who writes almost all of their material, recorded
a lot of their early stuff alone, brought the band from Honolulu to the
mainland two years ago, and despite the hardships of life on the road
and losing most of the original band members, continues to front a unit
of anywhere from five to nine musicians, now featuring one Hawaiian
(Ted Cho, mandolin) apart from himself.

Frank's first released work was a single or two with the Squids, quite a way
back.


While he now plays a variety of stringed and percussion instruments, as well as
singing, he was the drummer (and singer) for the Honolulu band


HAT MAKES THE MAN 

( Peter Bond, Marti Kerton, Byron Lai, Matthew Miller, Frank Orrall)
