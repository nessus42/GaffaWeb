>I heard what was billed as a new Sinead O'Connor track on the radio
>last weekend while driving around SF (KITS, 105.3) - anyone know if
>there's a new single and/or album ? I don't remember the name of the
>track I heard, unfortunately. 

The last single I'm aware of was "Jump Into the Fire" from the Married
to the Mob soundtrack.  The 12" has a version with some obscene female
performance artist which is kind of interesting, but nothing spectacular.
I'd recommend ignoring the 12" (unless you're a fanatic) and getting
the soundtrack, because it's got lots of other good stuff--music
by the Tom Tom Club (partial Talking Heads doing fun stuff) among others,
and a new (!!) song by Brian Eno which is pretty good, if rather mellow.
