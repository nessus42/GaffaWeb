Subject: Our Lady's Image, this time in GIF

	Due to requests, I have converted (with pain and effort) my
Kate Bush images to GIF format.  I still do not have a general way
(program) to convert images to GIF. These GIF files have been resampled
and cropped to fit a VGA (320x200x256) screen. They are all monochrome
-- except ruth_c.gif . I am not too pleased with the color images and
will work on them some more before releasing color GIF's of Kate. These
images can be converted to EGA (16 color), but I doubt that they will
be very nice. Please let me know if these GIF's are appreciated.
Seeing as it is XMAS, I might be willing to make 5 1/4 IBM PC floppy
copies of these IF you send a floppy and SASE to me. Otherwise, grab them
via anonymous FTP from rna.rockefeller.edu (~ftp/katebush). Of course,
you will also want a GIF viewer. For the PC, I have found VGIF to be pretty
good.  You may also grab that in the ~ftp/pc directory.
