Subject: The Chocolate War



Unless somebody beats me to it, let me be the first to report Kate's
musical contribution to this film.  Over the end credits, "Running Up
That Hill" is played.

Nothing new here, it's not a new mix, although the drums sounded kind
of wimpy, but that might have been the sound system in the theater.
Also, the end credit states that Kate appears courtesy of
EMI-Manhattan/Capitol records.

The film itself wasn't too bad - a little long in places, but overall
rather good.
