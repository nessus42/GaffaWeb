Hey everyone...
  I've noticed everyone talking about Enya and Clannad but I haven't
seen anyone mention the connection (maybe the messages concerning
this got lost on this wonderfully accurate server!). Anyway, Enya's
FULL name is Enya ni Bhraonain; she's the sister of Maire, the singer
for Clannad. Enya previously was a part of Clannad, and then was in
Sinead O'Connor's band, and is now solo.
   My friend has about 9 Clannad CD's. Let me see if I can remember
them... Sirius, Macalla, Magical Ring, The Collection, Faum, In
Concert, Legend, maybe some others I can't remember. He also has a
few albums, Dulaman, Clannad 2... Oh well, you get the idea. Most
of their stuff is on CD, and they have a LOT of albums. Oh well.
Have a day!
     --Mike
