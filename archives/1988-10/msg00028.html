some early singles:


Yesterday's Love/Nice Girls (Stiff Buy 74)

Second Choice/Name Of The Game/Bible Belt (Buy 79) (released 27 June 1980)

Girls Are Always Right/No Idea (Buy 94)

Trouble With Love/She'll Belong To Me (Buy 119)


not all cuts are on the two stiff albums, the first comes in two versions,
a ten cut one and a twelve cut one (featuring (live ?) covers of springsteen
and abba !!), second one features a richard thompson cover.

two more "major label" lps, the last coves in both a single lp and double lp
form, the latter not released in the us. richard t one one or both of these,
his influence is apparant too ! (but these albums also feature a dance sound
not really found elsewhere in gregson's work)



also a live lp (german only ?)


after any trouble split, gregson joined r t's touring band for a while, then
put out some records with christine collister, very much in the r & l thomp.
vein, but extremely excellent and featuring brailliant songwriting, even if
the guitar work takes a leaf or two from r t's book !

new record "overdue", in the new year perhaps ?
