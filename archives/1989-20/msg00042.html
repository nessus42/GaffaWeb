To all Bay Area Kate fans!

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)

Subject: To all Bay Area Kate fans!


Someone has posted that "The Sensual World" has been played
on KITS (105.3 MHz) San Francisco.  Once a song has been played,
it's fair game for requests.

Every Friday night from 8 pm to midnight, and Saturday night from
7 pm to midnight, (including tonight!) are all-request nights on this
radio station.  ALL BAY AREA FANS SHOULD CALL THE REQUEST LINE TONIGHT
OR TOMORROW NIGHT TO REQUEST THIS SONG, NO MATTER WHETHER THEY LISTEN TO
THE RADIO OR NOT.  The more requests there are for a particular song, the
more likely that they'll play it.

The telephone number for the request line is (415)-478-LIVE.

They also have a number in the 408 area code, but I don't remember it
offhand.  The station is sure to announce it tonight, however.

This is important!  There is no better promotion possible for an artist
than to have her played on the radio.

Thanks,
