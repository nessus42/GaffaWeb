Summary: Where's the party?


In some obscurely numbered article or another, the infamous West-coast
party animal tracyr@uunet.uu.net (jane smallberries) writes in response
to IED's call for an LA gathering of the faithful:
>
>how delightful, i will most certainly attend.  i vote for any weekend
>...
>...
>i'd just like to remind love-hounds what a special event this is, and
>how imperative it is to celebrate!!  i mean katemas is fantastic, but
>it comes once a year.  but a new album??  it could be an infinity
>before we have this surplus of jubilation again!  while it may be
>difficult to coordinate nationwide parties to occur on the same
>day (for phonecall mania), we should at least give it a try.
>

To which I react:
So where should the Austin party be?  I'd offer my place, but...well...

There are certainly enough Love-Hounds in Austin (and the rest of Texas,
I suppose) to pull something together for this twice-a-decade event.
I may be able to come up with some rare videos and maybe a new promo EP
or two, along with the totally unrare 12" import EP and matching 3-song
CD (both available at Waterloo Records since last week).  Maybe even a
European (German) copy of the full TSW CD a few days early.  If someone
could provide an open house and a loud stereo, we could welcome the new
music to Austin.

By the way Austin Love-Hounds, John Aielli played side one (from the
promo cassette) Tuesday morning on Eklektikos, and he won't say when
he's going to play the rest.  Keep that dial set to 90.7...
