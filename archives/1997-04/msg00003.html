>Does anyone know, where to get (inWWW of course) the catalogue of official
>EMI release of KAte CDs.

   The most complete discography of Kate's work is available on:

http://www.gryphon.com/gaffa/discog/

   It's Uli Grepel's mammoth undertaking "This Woman's Work, a Kate Bush
Discography". It's part of Gaffaweb, the web site created by the combined
efforts of several readers of rec.music.gaffa.
 
>Or maybe, any of you know personally, what does EMI release beside Her
>major CD, Box Set and Hammersmith Odeon. I saw in an online store there's
>one called Collection, its catalogue indicate that its an EMI Canada
>release. 

   The short answer is: All of Kate's music has been released by EMI. EMI
is her British record company, and was her US record company until
_The Red Shoes_. In the US and UK everything was released on either
EMI or EMI America (or an affiliated label like Harvest or Manhattan).
