Just for the record to whoever posted that Tori is not in a league of her
own because she sounds like Kate Bush, why say such nonsense?

Tori and Kate Bush are nothing alike, Kate has a pop, 80-obsessed
style while Tori bridges into an alternative gap of poetry and 
brilliant

instruments
that Kate overdubs with does not come close to comparing Tori's 
beautiful
solo piano work. Tori has true musicianship and class.
