Check the "Gaffaweb Dictionary" section of Gaffaweb -- the official web
site of the Love-Hounds and rec.music.gaffa -- where you'll find listings
for "Saturday Night Live" and "Live At Five" which provide a glimpse of a
couple of visits Kate made to my home town.  These were by no means her
only visits to the United States, but they were the only ones I wrote
entries for.

As a general rule, if you have any questions about Kate or Kate-related
things, Gaffaweb should be the first place you look.  Make a point of
getting more familiar with the stunningly wonderful FAQ.  There will be a
quiz afterward.
