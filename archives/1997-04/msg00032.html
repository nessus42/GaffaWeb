Yes, it's actually called 'Collaborations' and contains the 
duets (or Kate on b'ground vocals) with Roy Harper, Zaine Griff, 
Leslie Duncan and Big Country as well as "This Woman's Work" 
which sounds like it was lifted directly from the film 
soundtrack of "She's Having A Baby" (and complete with the 
dialogue preceding the song).

The cover photo is the same as on the Vermorel book, with Kate 
being attacked by a quick-moving ivy plant.
