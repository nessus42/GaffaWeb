There is a track at the end of the film GLC that does not appear on any
official Kate
recording I have ever seen.  Since some of you may not have seen the film, I
have
digitised the track and uploaded it to my web space.  I don't suppose anyone
would
appreciate me posting it directly to love-hounds.
  The file is just over 1.0MB, lasts 95 seconds, and is in Windows .WAV
format,
recorded at 8 bits, 11 KHz, mono.  The sound quality is not stunning, but
anything
better would have produced a ridiculously large file.
  The file can be downloaded directly by FTP or via a web browser from
http://members.aol.com/spbarker/glctrack.wav
