Kate has been to New York city a few times. And I'm told she's been to the
Grand Canyon. That's it. She didn't tour (there was a rumor that she was
gonna tour after "Sensual World" but that's all it was), she's never
played a gig in Texas, she's only appeared live on one American TV show
for the purpose of performing (Saturday Night Live, 12/9/78), and THAT'S
IT.

all known about it by now.

sick.
