I've been meaning to mention this for quite a while, but most times I'd 
rather just read the posts...but the time has come to contribute.

There is always discussion of other kate-like artists on this list, and 
thought Id introduce another. While living inMexico for a couple of years 
(1983-1984) I was shocked to hear what I thought was Kate Bush singing in 
Spanish. Turns out it was an Argentine singer by the name of Amanda 
Miguel. Any Latino Kate fans familiar with her work? I only have three of 
her albums, but have listened to them almost as much as I have listened 
to Kate. The three albums are:

	El Sonido Vol. 1/El Me Mintio (He lied to me)
	El Sonido Vol 2
	Amanda Miguel 3 (Dont know the name - had some friends tape it 
		for me)

I recently purchased two more of her albums but havent received them yet.

She has similar vocal style, and the albums I have are reminiscent of 
KaTe's earler work. She even spends time on each album playing piano, 
accompnying herself on very sensual and romantic work.

I would be interested to know if anyone is familiar with Amanda Miguel 
and if they agree with my assessment and comparison to KaTe.
