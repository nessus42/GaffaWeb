The full orchestral versions of just a few, namely:
	Wuthering Heights
	Babooshka
	Army Dreamers
	Between A Man And A Women
	Cloudbusting
	Hounds Of Love
	The Kick Inside
	Love & Anger
	Running Up That Hill
	The Man With The Child In His Eyes
	The Sensual World
	This Women's Work
are in the most excellent book:
	Kate Bush - Off the Record	ISBN 1-85909-169-5
it also has 4 colour A4 sized prints, and 6 quarter A4 sized black&white
two per page prints. There is also the cover photo too.
