Ok, the 'train' at the end of Cloudbusting is a cover up. When Kate 
wrote the song everything just came to a sudden end. So the idea for 
the train was to provide an ending and to give a sense of the end of 
the journey.

The sound effect is not real, the steam is Del processed through the 
fairlight, which also provided the whistle.

My source for such detailed knowledge: 
The Radio 1 (UK) series 'Classic Albums' did HOL, Kate talked for an 
hour about all the tracks in some detail, really interesting.
