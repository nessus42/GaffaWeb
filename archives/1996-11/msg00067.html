I have the early Mick Karn solo, "Titles" and the collaboration with Peter
Murphy,
"Dali's Car."  "Dali's Car" is very interesting, filled with very peculiar
fretless
excursions.  Unfortunately, Mick is not an especially exciting vocalist, and
he sings on the lion's share of "Titles."  Karn appears to be at his best
when
he is buttressing other writers' material.  
Karn is the aural equivalent of an H.R. Giger painting.
