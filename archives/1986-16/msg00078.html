A few quickies while my simulations are running...

Anyone heard of 1919? I found this EP called Machine at the library
that has some good pounding drums and distorted guitars, kind of like
early Savage Republic or Killing Joke. Subsequent attempts to unearth
other albums have failed...

Also from the library: this deranged post-punk funk group called
Coitus Int., which I assume has nothing to do with Rossi's old
band. Any info will be much appreciated...

Pseudo reviews:

PART 1		PICTURES OF PAIN
I know Hof already panned this in Shredded Slime but it's so bad it
deserves another scathing review. I really wanted to like this one
because of the incredibly cool cover (kind of an industrial version
of Bosch) but the wimpy bass and half-assed distorted guitars really
drove me crazy. It sucked (oops, I mean I hated it.) There.

KILSLUG
Really primitive sludge rock. I was unimpressed but you might like
it if you're into this kind of stuff.

New SPK
Yet another plug: this is kind of a gentle industrial My Life In
The Bush of Ghosts. Even non-industrial/noize freaks may like it.
