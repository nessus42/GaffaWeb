Lizards in a Circle

Debut Performance at:

Place:  TT the Bears

Date:  Tuesday, December 2, 1986

Time:  10:00 pm (The Cry opens at 9:00)

Lizards in a Circle features Lizzit (Berklee '89) on vocals, Adrian Nye '84
on guitar, James Melton '85 on bass, and "Phil" (Berklee '86) on drums.  
The group plays mostly original rock music, with a commercial exploitative
piont of view.  Come see this band while you can still afford it, before they
start playing to sellout audiences at the Centrum for $20 a seat.  Band members
are very sexy, and are cured or currently under treatment for a variety of
the usual sex/drug/rock'n'roll related conditions.

How to get to the club:  In the heart of scenic Central Square!  Don't forget
to bring protection!  One block from Kabuki's.  10 Brookline Av, off Mass. Av.
