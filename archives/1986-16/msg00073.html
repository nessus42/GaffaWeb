Changes (from 9/26/86 posting):
- Philadelphia was dropped in favor of Richmond, as the venue in Philadelphia
was given to the Chameleons instead.
- Milwaukee was dropped as the venue (The Underground) has since converted
into a Gay Disco.
- Minneapolis, Austin, & Dallas were dropped, for various reasons.
- The Los Angeles date & venue are still tentative (as is the promoter! Shit,
WE might have to put this show on!  Yow!!)
- There is a (VERY slim) chance of a San Diego date (12/8 or 12/9)
- The Washington venue was moved as the 930 club's stage was too small.
- The Seattle venue was also moved.

Tour dates will be finalized by 11/8/86.

There will be no other bands on the bill.
