          KATE BUSH bounces back into action this week with a new
     single for EMI and the promise of a compilation LP and video
     next month.  The single is callled "Experiment IV" and is
     apparently about Kate's nightmare vision of a future where music
     usually a force for good, is harnessed by scientists as a weapon
     of destruction.
          The single comes in seven and 12 inch versions backed by a
     remixed version of "Wuthering Heights" to which Kate has added a
     newly recorded vocal.

     Wheee!  New Kate!  Now let's just hope it doesn't get blocked from
US release by some nasty record company...
