If the Neu album was indeed from the 1981 period (and not a re-release), it
isn't surprising that it might not be considered spacey.  By 81 most of
the Berlin School had disbanded, even eberhard Schroener (SP?) was doing
stuff with Poliece (minus Sting).  The reference to early Kraftwerk, is
misleading, however in that it suggests early 70's period, not 80's.  Is
the album you have done by Dinger and Rother, or are there other musicians
credited on the album.  Also, what studio was it done in?  Also, spacy doesnt
necessarily connote lots of weird synths, or a lot of special (heavy
resonance) effects, since the most spacy album of all time (Klaus Schulze's
Cyborg) was done for the most part with rather conventional instruments,
ans a complete string section.
