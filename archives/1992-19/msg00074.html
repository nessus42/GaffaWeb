> ... Clannad's lead singer is Maire Ni Bhraonain ---

Actually, Enya, although younger than Maire, is not the youngest sister.
There are three more - Bridin, Olive and Dee. They all sing backing-vocals
on Maire's solo album.
 
mtt@alaska.caedm.byu.edu (W. Stirling) then asked:
 
> Dunno if it's "Canon" Gaffa, but has anyone heard Maire Brennan's new
> solo album (Maire)?  What do you think of it?
 
As implied above, I have it. It is very much what you'd expect - somewhere
between Enya and Clannad. A couple of the tracks have a strong African
influence which works surprisingly well. One review I saw compared the
effect of listening to it to having a small Korean masseuse run up and
down your spine... I can't endorse this comparison from first hand
experience but I'd certainly agree with its sentiments!
