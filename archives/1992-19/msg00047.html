 Jane Siberry also performed on "Kick at the Darkness," the collection
of Bruce Cockburn covers. The song was a duet with - correct me if I'm
wrong on this - the guy from the Rheostatics. The album had
contributions from a number of people who've worked with Siberry in
the past, including Rebecca Jenkins and Anne Bourne. Not surprising,
since it was a very Toronto-centred selection of artists... Reasonably
good record, actually. The Barenaked Ladies' cover of "Lovers in a
Dangerous Time" got the most airplay, of course.
