I was semi-disappointed.  The first album really knocked me out.  Karen Peris
has a lovely voice and nearly all of the songs on the album were captivating
(IMHO).  The second album starts out with several "nice" cuts but covers
little new ground from there.  It's more difficult with this recording to sift
through the muck to find the engaging melodies that were more prevalent on the
first recording.  All in all, it's enjoyable if you really like the Innocence
Mission but nonetheless somewhat of a sophomore slump.
