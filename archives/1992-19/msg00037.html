Yes, Karen Peris and company will be on TOUR this summer!!! :)

The Innocence Mission is scheduled for two shows this month. I'm not sure
of any other dates, so if someone else has any others that I fail to mention,
please email me or post here.

The two dates that I have noticed are:

June 24 - Baltimore, MD - Max's On Broadway
June 25 - Washington, DC - 9:30 Club

I will be attending the show on the 25th, so if anyone decides to go, please
let me know. Maybe we can meet up there. If anyone plans on going to the June 24
show, please email me. I would like to get an update of how the show goes as
well as find out if it would be possible to record the show.

The Innocence Mission are planning on releasing a new LP next year so I hear.
I assume with this mini-tour that they are working out the kinks on some
new songs before going in to record.
