        My (purely emotional) reaction to Tori is that she's "The Evil" 
Kate Bush.  :-)  She has a lot of the same attraction that Kate has, but 
you get the feeling she's up to no good.  One of the strongest reactions 
I've ever had to a TV performance was when she was on 
_The_Dennis_Miller_Show_ when she looked straight at the camera at the 
end.  Freaked me out! 
        Note that none of this is meant to disparage Tori or her fans, 
just my own reaction to her and her music.
