    I posted something about this of to Really Deep Thoughts, I don't know how 
many hear read that this I'll repeat myself. I asked Tori about the cover to 
'Little Earthquakes' and she said that the whole album is about proportions. 
Proportions in love, life, anger, ... and that the entire CD has been designed 
in an attempt to convey this. So we see BIG Tori, little piano. BIG mushrooms. 
Writing in different case, style and colour. So, the reason she is in a box on 
the front cover is so she can be made to look larger NOT because she happened to 
want to copy Kate's TKI.
