First, I second all the nice things said about Anne Archer, one of my 
favorite actresses. <sigh>

After seeing the movie Sunday I went to the record store and found they 
already had copies of the soundtrack CD; the Clannad song used is "Theme 
from Harry's Game", and the other Irish track (used I believe at the start 
and end of the movie) was not Enya but Molly (Maggie?) Boyle.  I didn't 
pick it up because I've got the Clannad track twice over already and I had 
a problem paying full CD price for one (admittedly good) track.  I also 
suspect that may have been Maire on the TV while "Harry's Game" was playing.

I *did* pick up Loreena McKennitt's _The Visit_.  Wonderful stuff.

Congratulations, Cynthia!
