Jane Siberry's latest album, _Bound_by_the_Beauty_, was
released in 1990.  (That's her latest _new_ album; her 
very first album, recorded in 1981, was recently re-released
on CD).  She toured last year (did not make it to St. Louis, 
unfortunately :-( 

Jane also recorded a duet with k.d. lang (!) for the soundtrack
of the recent Wim Wenders film--title something like "At the
End of the World."  

So, she's quite active, as far as I can tell.  Our Canadian
colleagues can probably tell us more.
