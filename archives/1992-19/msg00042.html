                  KATEMAS in KOLUMBUS!
          
Missy and I are tentatively planning to hold our First Annual
Katemas in Kolumbus Kook OuT and ParTy on Saturday, August 1,
1992.  We would be very pleased and honored to have any and all
area Kate fans attend.
              
The plan is simple: we char something on the grill, gulp it down
and then enjoy mind numbing quantities of Kate music, video and
miscellania.  The tentative location is our house on the south
east side of Columbus.  Detailed directions to the home of The
Big Sky will be provided once plans are final.
              
If you're interested in attending, please let us know by email
(or feedback on The Big Sky).  We'll make every effort to adjust
our plans to make this as convientient for as many people as
possible.  It is possible that some fans may be traveling from
Cleveland so it may be possible to arrange rides.

                       -----------

I originally attempted to post the above while gaffa was down 
(again). You can put us down as favoring August 1 but we can 
probably move to whatever date is most popular.  Last I heard 
the Boston KT party (which was to be a collaboration between the 
people at  Little Light and Watching Storms) has been pushed back 
with no date set.
