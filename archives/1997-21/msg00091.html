...If you're serious about that headphone thing, you may be in for a
pleasant surprise. Certainly I haven't looked at a wide selection
myself, but in 1994 I ran across something in a magazine mentioning some
in-the-ear phones from Etymotic Research. The reviewer said, in essence,
that they had the best sound of any phones *or speakers* he'd ever
heard. There's a link to ER on the "music" page of my site (URL is
below--check out the other artists too while you're at it.) There's also
a link to Headroom, which makes portable headphone amps so you can take
that Er sound on the road (or in the air). I can vouch for the fact
that, although the in-the-ear bit takes some getting used to, these
little jobbies do *kick butt*. The bass is unsurpassed. And yes, I do
plan to get the remastered *Hounds of Love* quite soon.
