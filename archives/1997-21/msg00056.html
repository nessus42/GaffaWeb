Hi.  I'm Debbie.  Some of you may know me from the Split Enz/Frenz of the Enz
websites; apart from that, I'm a lurker here.  I had been planning on sending my
submission in for the Cover CD, and never quite did it.  Poor me - fell in love,
got engaged, moved, business going well.  :)  Anyway, I saw someone post about
having put theirs on the web as a RealAudio clip, and I figured what better way
to share my tune with KT fans since I won't be on the CD.

Yes, it's Running Up That Hill, but what will probably make it unique is that
it's an a cappella arragement I did a few years ago.  In this recording, it's my
keyboard playing drums and bass, and me singing 5 background parts, the solo
part, and a duet part.  It's not perfect, but it's something of which I'm quite
proud. :)

You can read my whole page on it at http://www.aswas.com/deb/music.html or just
open the RA clip from your player at: http://www.aswas.com/deb/running.ra
