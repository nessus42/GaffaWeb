Hi John,

    The Dead have ALWAYS been associated with the hippy-drug scene.  It's
natural to think that way because that's exactly where they came from.   But
drugs and hippies aside,  they made some damn good music...musically and
lyrically.  A few axamples:  Throwing Stones, I Will Take You Home, Stella
Blue,
Standing On The Moon, Black Peter, Sugar Magnolia....and the list goes on and
on.   In terms of a Live experience, there is no equal to a Grateful Dead show.
The atmosphere is pure bliss and love.  The love is sent out to the band and 
the band returns the love.  At a dead show, audience and band are one.  I feel
sad for those of you who haven't experienced a Dead show.    Their songs are 
about love, responsibility, respect and tolerence of, and for, all.  There is
no HATE at a Dead show. 


peace
