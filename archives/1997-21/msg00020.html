Saturday!  Of course, I live in a teeny weeny itsy bitsy little apartment and
cannot host it here, but I live not too far from the beach and there's lots
of space there.  Or, if there's someone else willing to host and can handle
the late notice....well, we'd all be grateful, I'm sure.  So, if you're
interested, please contact me A.S.A.P.  Time is running short.
