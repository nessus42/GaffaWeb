The 10 tracks featured on Gabriel's "US" album are:

"Come Talk To Me", "Love To Be Loved", "Blood Of Eden", "Steam", "Only
Us", "Washing Of The Water", "Digging In The Dirt" (the first single),
"14 Black Paintings", "Kiss That Frog" and "Secret World".

It is approximately 1 hour long and features John Paul Jones and Sinead
O'Conner as guest musicians.

Too bad he hasn't redone any Genesis tunes... perhaps FOUNTAIN OF
SALMACIS or RETURN OF THE GIANT HOGWEED! :)
