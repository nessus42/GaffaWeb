How can I resist? Lots of early songs, I see.

>
>STRANGE PHENOMENA
First song showing Kate's "mystical" bent. Not one of the more
memorable songs on the album, musically, though. I've always wondered
what does "Om mani padme" mean? 

>KITE 
Incredible song. One of my favorites. Surrealistic (there's
a hole in the sky with a big eyeball, watching me." Really fills you
with this feeling of being carried along in the sky lie a kite. One
interviewer brought up the mention of Beelzebub as an occult element,
but I think it was just there to convery the image of discontent.

>FEEL IT 
Is this the most erotic song ever written? If there's anything that
tops it, tell.

>0 L'AMOUR LOOKS SOMETHING LIKE YOU
 Second most erotic song ever written. :-) Perhaps the most beautiful
"one night stand" song I've ever heard. Her voice is inhuman, angelic
on this one. And the images are stirring "I'm dressed in lace sailing
down a black reverie, my heart is thrown to the pebbles and the
boatmen. All the time, I find I'm living in that evening with the
feeling of sticky love inside."

>0 THEM HEAVY PEOPLE
Really unusual song, topically. I've never heard another like it. Sort
of an ode to enlightenment.
