        Scott asks about the Nicholas Wade _Leaving_My_Tracks_ reference. 
 I believe it was in an early issue of the KBC.  It said something like 
"Nicholas has been close to Kate for many years, so he'll be in a great 
position to write this book".  As far as the other 1989 KBC mag, it said 
that it was eight pages, so I imagine it was one of those "pen pals"

something.  The only one I know of that came out was after the
