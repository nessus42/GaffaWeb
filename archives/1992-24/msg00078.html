|> Last night I saw Sarah McLachlan in concert at Symphony Space in NYC. She
|> was terrific! She played the songs from "Solace" (except "Wear Your
|> Love Like Heaven"), some from "Touch" and a new song called "Elsewhere" which
|> was very good. When she sang "Steaming" the band included little bits from
|> "Gonna Make You Sweat", "Vogue" and "Staying Alive" (didn't someone mention
|> something like this from a previous concert?) Here are some of the comments
|> she made on her songs:
|> 
|> "Out of the Shadows" -- the first song she ever wrote
|> "Lost" -- very sad
|> "Shelter" -- "dedicated to all creatures less fortunate than us"
|> "I Will Not Forget You" -- a young woman falls in love with a wonderful man,
|>      but unfortunately he's a figment of her vivid imagination
|> "Black" -- dedicated to corporate pigs
|> "Mercy" -- depressing; "probably the saddest song I've ever written"
