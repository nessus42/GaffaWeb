Homeground 45 is out but don't get too excited! I think a lot of people may
be disappointed with this one because it contains very little new material.
Instead  of  the  usual  format,  this  "10th  Anniversary  Edition"  is  a
compilation  of  the  best-of   Homeground  over   the  years.  The  cynics
(realists?) among us will not be surprised by what news there is: the album
is now  not expected until  Spring '93. Remember KaTe was predicting '91 at
the '90 convention. Apparently  there  will  be a  some kind of release
from KaTe before Christmas, but nothing new.
