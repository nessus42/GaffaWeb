        Following is a list of songs that haven't been discussed 
_at_all_ on Love-Hounds.  I should qualify that, these songs haven't 
been discussed as to their _meaning_ or their _production_.  There may 
have been passing references, or discussions of these on videos or 
bootlegs, or in transcriptions of articles on Kate.  And of course all 
but _L'Amour_ have been discussed in Kate's interviews.

thoughts on these, please post em'.
