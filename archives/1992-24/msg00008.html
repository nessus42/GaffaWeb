UG>What the HELL did Ms. O'Connor do to deserve such a bad rep?

     She refused to let the American national anthem, "The Star-Spangled
     Banner," be sung at one of her concerts, or something like that -
     and the media picked this up and blew it WAY out of proportion.  She
     said that she's not an American anyways, and she doesn't have much
     respect for the people running our country right now, so she did
     not feel obliged to salute America.  Or something like that.

     Personally, I agree with her.  The people currently running the
     government are a bunch of idiots, and I wouldn't stand up to say
     "hi" to them if they came begging to my front door.

     And anyways, I like Sinead O'Connor's albums.

     But I should bring this subject back to Kate, so...

     "I hid my yo-yo
     In the garden -
