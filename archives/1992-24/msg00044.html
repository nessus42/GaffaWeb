ObKaTe:  When i was in new jersey, i went to have dinner with a mormon
friend/neighbor of mine who was on her mission.  due to the regulations of
people on their missions, we had to have dinner with her two companions as well
(they can't spend any time away from one another -- stupid rule, imho, but i
guess that's how they keep them away from the men and unmarried.)  i wasn't
looking forward to this, needless to say.  so i planned on having kate playing
in my car to maybe introduce them to her and to brag about my excellent taste
in music.  :-)  sure enough, as soon as we got on the road to the tunes of the
ninth wave, one of the companions asks who is singing.  when i reply that it's
kate, all three of them mention how much they liked the sensual world.  take
heart, kate fans -- we really are making progress in the united states!
