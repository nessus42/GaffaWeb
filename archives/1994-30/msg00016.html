Ok all you San Francisco Torinos.  Natoma alley between Second and Third 
streets is plastered with Tori Amos posters.  They are the Spin cover 
with a blurb at the bottom about some album or another that is currently 
available.

A few of the posters have acquired amusing moustaches (not my doing, I 
assure you!) but a lot of them are pristine.  Anyone with a trick for 
getting them off the wall has a free poster, or a dozen of them!
