By coincidence, that same section of the New York Times contains a review 
by Janet Maslin of "Making Up!", the film that was shown in tandem with 
TLTCTC in Berkeley at the Women's Film Festival.

"Making Up!" has a scene that begins with a full screen shot of the cover 
of "The Dreaming", which caused all of us to explode with wile applause 
and shouts at this unexpected vision.

Anyone who attends this movie should be prepared to cheer wildly when 
"The Dreaming" appears on the screen.
