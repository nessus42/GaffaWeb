I just got the new _Rolling Stone_ "Women in Rock" special edition.  They
created a little time line to show the progress of women in rock.  1985
is shared by Alison Moyet and Kate, although only Kate got a picture.  

While everyone else got a little blurb when they were placed on the time
line, under Kate it simply says "Running Up That Hill."  I guess they 

think that's reason enough to put her on the time line.  I can think of
many more reasons, but at least she made it.
