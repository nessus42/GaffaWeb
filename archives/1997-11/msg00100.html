Greetings Love Hounds!

I am new to the world of news groups, but have been visiting the Gaffa web site 
semi regularly for about 4 months now.

Rolf kindly suggested that I query the group for a copy (non-profit, of 
course ;) ) of the love hound's video collection.  I suspect that this may be 
the very collection I had the priviledge to view back in 1988 /9 (?) at a "KaTe 
Bush Night in the Whole" at coffman memorial union on the university of 
minnesota campus.  Since that night, I have desperately trying to acquire the 
x-mas special, and the duet with Rowan Atkinson.  I have had no luck acquiring 
anything other than the standard US catalog, Red Shoe Box, and imported box 
set of TWW.  

The only thing my prior connections with the music industry has netted me, has 
been some demo singles, Passing through Air, and an autographed publicity photo 
(My prized possession!  Every once in a while, I hold the frame in my hand and 
sigh "SHE actually touched / held this")  Yes, my adoration for KaTe is 
probally not healthy, but at least here I may get some deeper understanding 
(instead of rolling eyes) from others.  

I do not collect for investment purposes, but for the pure love of the goddess 
who walks (and sings) among us.  If anyone can help me acquire some of the more 
elusive recordings (both audio and video) I would be EXTREMELY grateful!!
