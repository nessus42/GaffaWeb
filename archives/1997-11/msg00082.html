Being new to the group, I hope I don't alienate myself with this..

But,  I am inclined to put Peter Gabrel into the "KT Genre".  For
some reason, I also want Tom Waits and Leonard Cohen there.  I think
it has to do with the poetic / story telling lyrics, and interesting
(annoying by my previous roomate's opinion) vocal styles.  

I agree with Renee Rosen, that it all depends on how you classify the 
'genre'.  This could easily turn into a catolog of my music 
collection (to a certain extent) as I could compare a great deal of 
artists to KT in one vein or another.
