We're nearing that time of year when thoughts turn to chocolate, music, and
dance.  Yes, our annual Kate-fest is only two months away, so everyone
start thinking about when and where you're going to hold your party.

I volunteer to keep a running list going of party announcements, so when
you make your minds up, send me your name, your party date, your location,
a method of contacting you (e-mail and phone if possible), and the address

the most important info, let's have it.

E-mail me at: kln@staralliance.com

Thanks a bunch, looking forward to seeing all you left coast KateHeads again.
