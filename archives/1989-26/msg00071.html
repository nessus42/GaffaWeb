Hear!  Hear!  You hit the nail right on the head, as have several
others, when you bring up the point that judging TSW on the basis of
whether or not it's "better" than TD or HoL, or even Lionheart, fer
cryin' out loud, is pointless, meritless, and entirely subjective. 
Someone who cares little or not at all for TD will certainly feel that
TSW is inferior to it, while someone who thinks TD is the greatest rock
album ever made (bah-- we all know that HoL takes that spot, now don't
we? ;-)  ) will think that TSW, subsequently, isn't worth the paper its
cover was printed on.  Each album must be judged on the basis of whether
or not it achieved what it seemed to try to.  In the case of TSW, it
seemed to want to drown the listener in a Sensual (as it were), almost
aphrodisiacal (is that a word?) aural experience.  If such is the case,
then, IMHO, it is a terrific success.  (But I'll get into that later...)
