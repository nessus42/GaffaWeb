God, I hate top-40 radio, and this is the reason why.  It discourages,
without the slightest subtlety or tact, any form of music that steps out
of the norm, and when such unusual music becomes unexpectedly popular,

former self (eg. U2, REM, and Prince (almost)).

As for this particular DJ-- well, we'll not get into that, suffice it to
say that I hope he chokes to death on his Wheaties.  :-)
