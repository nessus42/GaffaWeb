Once again, _Hounds Of Love_ appears in someone's Desert Island Disks
in _Pulse!_ magazine (Nov. 89).  "This one really moves me," says
Neil Benson of New York City.

I just heard an ad on the radio for Star Records, a small Bay Area chain
specializing in 12" imports and dance music.  They advertised the 12"
double-grooved "The Sensual World"!

At Tower Concord, the album is only at #30. :-(  However, this store
is in a neighborhood not prone to alternative music fans.
