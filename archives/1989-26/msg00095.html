The Sensual World video is slowly starting to get some play in Australia. I
happened to see it on Rage, on the ABC on Saturday and Friday Nights. This
was Saturday morning at about 2:30 AM.

I had just finshed watching a movie on another channel and was flipping
channels in search of something else to relieve my boredom. Talk about well
relieved!! "Kate" I cried to Al, who was sitting there. Shame he looked at
me as if to say, "So What!" I'll convert him yet.

At least I got to make Graham (graham@wraith.uow.cs.oz.au) really really
jealous!

We tried again the other night with video remote contol in hand, but MTV
in Australia never played it. Damn!
