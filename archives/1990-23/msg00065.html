Anyway, here is a sample from one of my discographies. The format is
mine and many people might not consider it "the" way to do a
discography. Any list of albums is considered a discography and format
is up to the author... I have no "specifications" for the Archives.
most of the discographies don't have artist/instrumentation or track
times or something else. It is up to the author. Some are just year,
title, type of release.

(Jean-Michel Jarre)

1977      Oxygene

          Jean Michel-Jarre (ARP Synth, AKS Synth, VCS 83 Synth, RMI
                 Harmonic Synth, Farfisa Organ, Eminent, Mellotron,
                 Rhythmin' Computer)

          Michel Geiss (adapted the VC83 and devised new instruments)

          Recorded at Jean-Michel Jarre's private studio and mixed at
          Gang studio, Paris from August to November 1976.

           7:40  Oxygene I
           7:37  Oxygene II
           3:24  Oxygene II
           4:06  Oxygene IV
          10:26  Oxygene V
           6:24  Oxygene VI
