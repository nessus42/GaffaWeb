Siouxsie starter albums:

	Once Upon a Time/The Singles is the greatest hits package,
		covering all but the last couple of albums.  It 
		includes 'Israel' one of my all time faves.  Play
		it loud.

	Peepshow is the latest album, and I think it is the overall
		best.  It is also an 'easy' album.

While I think Siouxsie & TB are great, I am the first to admit that
many of their album cuts are 'droning'.  Peepshow doesn't have this
problem and is available in the US.  Go for it.  I've noticed a high
correlation between Kate fans and Siouxsie fans.

						-Sam
