Fellow Love-Hounds,

My apologies for some of the letter's lines being truncated.  I thought it was 
taken care of, however, it clearly wasn't.   Not much was lost though and I'm 
sure you all could fill in the missing parts (there won't be a quiz!).  I 
won't correct it and repost.   

Michael Portuesi asks :

>Would it help if all of us Love-Hounds printed a copy of this letter,
>attached our own note saying how very much we'd like this to happen,
>and sent it off to EMI as well?

I am not sure that Steve Davis would appreciate being flooded with multiple 
copies of this letter and many Love-Hounds may not have access to nice 
printers etc.  The original was laser printed (9 pages).  A brief letter 
voicing support for this plan might not be a bad idea.  Afterall, we assumed 
(hopefully not unadvisedly) that all Love-Hounds would support this format.  
Some real letters from other Love-Hounds would only confirm our view and back 
up our proposal.  

I regret that IED and I may only be dreaming but we felt we had to try.  Maybe 
it will inspire someone at EMI.  If I don't ever get to meet her or see her in 
concert the suggested CD collection would at least fill some of the holes in 
my KaTefandom.  Any comments are welcome.

Derek
