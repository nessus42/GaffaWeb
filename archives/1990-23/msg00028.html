Subject: lyrics request

I finally got around to buying all of the Kate Bush CDs, so I could get rid
of these old vinyls. However, much to my chagrin, the EMI-Manhattan
editions of The Dreaming, Hounds of Love, and Never For Ever, do NOT
contain the printed lyrics!!

So I was thinking of typing in the lyrics from the record sleeves so I
would at least have them after I sold the vinyls, but I realized, someone
else MUST have done this already.

If you have the lyrics to all songs on these three albums, could you send
a reply to this message saying you have them and can send them to me?

Then I will reply to your message either with "yes!please send them!" or
"thanks anyway, but I got them already" depending on how fast you reply to
this message.

thanks in advance!!

And a PS: DON'T BUY the EMI-Manhattan pressings of Kate Bush CDs!! The
booklets have a song listing on one page (without the time info) and a list
of what DDD ADD and AAD mean on the other, and NOTHING ELSE. I am really
pissed off about this - I think I'll write a nasty letter.
