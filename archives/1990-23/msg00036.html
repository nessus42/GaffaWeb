There's a decent dance club called La Locomotive, you should go on 
a Thursday 'cause it's cheaper than the weekends.  I don't remember 
what you call the district it's located in, but it's in the same
neighborhood as all the sleazoid stript joints, so it shouldn't
be too hard to find.

El Diablo de Lombard is a very cool place to just hang out in, or
eat before going out clubbing.  It's in the Les Halles district,
sez my girlfriend.

As for touristy things, you absolutely have to go see the catacombs,
especially if you're into death & crypts & vampires & all that 
crap.  They have very wierd hours, and no phone; go early in your visit,
so if they're closed you'll be able to try again another day.

It's also required that you spend at least a third of your time
cafe-sitting.

There wasn't much going on as far as live music when we were there
last April, and I was taking a welcome vacation from record shopping
at the time, so I can't help you in those departments.
