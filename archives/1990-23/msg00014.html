For Kate's sake guys this is supposed to be Rock 'N Roll not the Readers
Digest music circle. What happened to good old sex and drugs and
rock and roll as a young person's adage?

Do you anthem lovers tell your parents to turn down their record players
so that you can watch TV. Did all those rock rebels (Morrison, Moon, Joplin,
Vicious) die in vain just for you to get steamed up about playing the
US national anthem before a rock concert. Did punk pass you by. I can just
see the Pistols and the Clash standing to attention before they began their
concerts!! The Pistols sang "God save the Queen, your fascist regime"
and made it to the top of the UK charts without democracy crumbling.

It's just unbelievable. I bet Sinead jaywalked on her way to the concert
as well. Lock up your sons, American mothers, there's an Irish she-devil
on the loose.

Most American comedians who come over here have their stock of Royal
Family jokes. It may surprise you to learn that we tend to laugh (at
least at the good jokes) and do not cart the comedians off to the
Tower or write letters of protest to the Times. There must be an awful
lot of insecure people in the US if something as petty as Sinead's
refusal becomes hot news.
