Are any of you familiar with this amazing duo? I saw them tonight (yes in
Nova Scotia  :) and they were INCREDIBLE.

They have 3 albums/cds out and Tuck has a solo cd. 

For those who don't know, Tuck Andress is one of the best jazz/? electric
guitarists around - really great. His wife, Patti Cathcart sings incredible
vocals - soul(ish) sounding. 

They are on Windham Hill Jazz. Go out and buy Love Warriors NOW!  :)
