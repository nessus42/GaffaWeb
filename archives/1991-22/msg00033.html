I think the point missed here is that the anti-Happy factions are not really
anti-Happy -- in fact, they (I should say "we", because I'm on that side of
the fence) for the most part have no opinion of her music at all.  There was
just a general discontentment with seeing so many messages about her.

>From what I've seen, opinions of Happy are easily dividable into two
catagories:  the frothing-at-the-mouth kind (that should be reserved for
Herself alone :^) ), and the scrunched-up-face-what's-the-big-deal kind.
This makes me hesitant to try it, because EVERY artist has his/her/their
die-hard fans, and I tend to discard these people's opinion's immediately,
because they cannot be objective in re the artist.  But what does everyone
else think?  For instance, in KaTe's case, non-Love-Hounds are usually
saying "She is so weird", or else "Wow, that's really inventive".  I don't
know ANYONE who has a mild opinion concerning Her, and this tells me
something, or would if I didn't already recognize her genius.  But in
Happy's case, there have been mostly yawns outside of the die-hard camp,
and this isn't encouraging to me.
