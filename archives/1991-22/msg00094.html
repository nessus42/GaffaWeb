In order to fill a gross gap in KaTeMas festivities worldwide, we are pleased
to announce a KaTeMas bash for the Southeast!  On July 27 in Chapel Hill, those
who live below the Mason-Dixon (or who wish they did) can attend a party in
honor of the nativity of KB!  The party will take place at Shadowoods Apts.
If you want details, e-mail and a party-pack into message (tm) including a
snazzy map will be yours for the asking.

Linda Dunn and Charles Boyer
