Thanks to those of you who have made great suggestions for the Sojourn Homepage.
The new/improved page is now up and running at:

http://ssnet.com/~deltufjp/sojourn.html

I've added several new songs, pictures and bio information, as well as great new

links to other pages.  

For the uninitiated:  Sojourn is an amazing alterna-progressi-folk band who have
been selling out venues on the east coast.  They have 2 CD's out:  "Further On"
and "Songs of Leaving."  Sojourn have a very distinct sound, but comparisons
have been made to Sarah McLachlan, Tori Amos, Renaissance, Kate Bush, Marillion
and many others.  Their music is truly a treat, so surf over to the home page
and check them out.
