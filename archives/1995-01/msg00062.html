In a previous posting,  (JHILEM41@MAINE.MAINE.EDU) writes:
> I was wondering what people think of Milla's CD "The Divine Comedy"?
> I realy enjoy this CD and would like to read what others think.
> 

I quite like it as well. It has a spunky daring to it that is a close
enough reminder of The Kick Inside to bring a smile of to those of us who
remember that first thrill in '78.  Of course, with 5000+ mutant strains
of so-called "alternative" music now the norm, poor Milla has already
found out that to sound as "different" as Kate did when Wuthering Heights
pealed across radioland is a lot harder to do today.  Still, she comes
close behind Bjork as one of my favourite "new" female talents of the last
year or two (I know, Bjork's been around for years, but tell that to MTV).

It will be interesting to hear that oh-so-crucial second album...
