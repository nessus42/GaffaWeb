Recent FAQ diff says,

>      The Line, The Cross, and The Curve finally appeared on video and should
>      be available in good shops around the world.

> [anyone found it in Japan??]

Yes, it was released by Toshiba EMI in December.
	VHS		TOVW-3198 (4,800yen)
	Laserdisc	TOLW-3198 (4,800yen)

Both format accompanies Japanese captions for dialogue parts
and liner notes by Michinari Yamada.
VHS import tapes from US are easily found at stores, priced around 3,200yen.

I've noticed that Japanese version has copyright notice as `(c)1994'
on its package, while US version claims that in 1993.
Does this mean this film was complete in 1993?
