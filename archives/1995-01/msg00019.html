and then there are those of us whose only exposure to Steely Dan is the De Las
Soul sound Eye Know which samples the phrase "I know I love you better" and who
STILL love WSILY?.

In fact, during the various and assorted personal traumas that beset a close
friend of mine last summer, every time I'd go to visit her, she's beg me to
play "the happy katesong." she's not even a big kate OR prince fan, she just
appreciated the joyousness of it all. we both agree that it's a great song.
