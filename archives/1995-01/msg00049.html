[I'm cross-posting this to r.m.gaffa since there is more likely
a few KaTe fans there. :-]

Personally, I find RS to be a pretty unremarkable album. SW
is OK, but I find her earlier stuff (The Kick Inside, Hounds
of Love) to much more compelling.

I blame it all on Peter Gabriel.  Ever since he showed her how
easy it is to write a song with a drum-machine (later adding the
rest of the instrumentation and melody lines), her song-writing
has tended towards catchy, shallow, pop music[*]... not unlike
the recent work of Mr. Gabriel.  She used to write her songs
at the piano, and her earlier songs have a much richer melodic
and vocal quality.

* {This is, of course, KaTe-relative.  KaTe writing "shallow,
pop music" is still orders of magnitude more compelling and deep
than, say, Milli Vanilli or Peter Nesmith!}
