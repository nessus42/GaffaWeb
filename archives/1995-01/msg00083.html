Having been a cerebral Asimov rather than Heinlein action fan, I read
all the Foundation books when I was a kid, and I think I can safely
say that the chances are vanishingly small that Kate intended any
connection with Asimov's character.  Kate herself has said the
inspiration for mule imagery was the Disney animated film *Pinochio*.
The source for this tidbit is when I chatted with her.  *The Shining*
is indeed also an influence for the song.  In the KBC newsletter many
years ago Kate wrote that the book *The Shining* and the movie *The
Alien* inspired much of the imagery for the song.
