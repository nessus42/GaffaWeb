If you've got Mosaic, you may want to try pointing it toward 
http://www.netmarket.com/  and then click the button that says "Go To 
NetMarket".  After a couple more pages, you're at Noteworthy Music.  I've 
ordered from the printed catalog a few times and have always been 
pleased.

I tried looking for the soundtrack to Castaway to no avail.  (Note: 
soundtracks are searched for with  movie-title-as-artist-name).  Then I 
searched for Bush, Kate.  Apparently, the space after the comma was not 
acceptable--I'd guess--because there was nothing listed.  Then I searched 
for "Bush". They have 12 Kate Bush titles right now, about half of them 
have pictures available with the album details.  You can browse about, 
clicking the little boxes next to the titles to indicate what you want 
and then move on to ordering.

The prices look great, though less attractive after you add shipping 
charges.  A co-worker and I recently ordered a bunch of discs together 
and split the postage.

Um, yes.  Well.  There you have it.
