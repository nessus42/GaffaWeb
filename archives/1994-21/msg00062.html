Tapetree Closes

From: Peter Byrne Manchester <PMANCHESTER@ccmail.sunysb.edu>
Date: Mon, 25 Jul 1994 01:26:54 -0400 (EDT)
Subject: Tapetree Closes
To: love-hounds@uunet.uu.net
Cc: pmanchester@ccmail.sunysb.edu
Content-transfer-encoding: 7BIT

	The Summer 1994 Video Tapetree, distributing "The Lovehounds 
Collection" to every continent in virtually every format, *CLOSES* today, 
Monday, July 25, at the end of the business day locally (5pm EDT in the USA).

	What this means is that if you have not contacted the person(s) for 
your country by *today* and received back the surface mail address for that 
dubbing site (in the US there are 11, assigned through Cindy Loiselle), you 
are *CLOSED OUT* of the official Tapetree.

        USA:		Cindy Loiselle		cindy@aai.com
	Canada: 	David Zimmerly		am672@FreeNet.Carleton.CA
			  (Special arrangements; BetaCAM dubmaster)
			Tippi Chai		aj796@FreeNet.Carleton.CA
			  (VHS stereo dubmaster; VHS stereo, 8mm mono) 
	UK/Europe(PAL):	Adrian Neal		aneal@cix.compulink.co.uk
	Australia:	Graham.G.R.Dombkins@msm.bhp.com.au

Many dub sites are already closed, and as of this writing there is *no* 
guarantee new requests can be accepted.  More than that, since the whole 
premise of this tapetree was to distribute this collection by Katemas, if you 
*do* contact one of the Dispatch Centers today and receive a subdub 
assignment, *mail your blanks with SAS <NB special procedure in Canada> 
immediately*!  *NO TAPES WILL BE COPIED AFTER FRIDAY, JULY 29*!

	All of these notices concern only the *official Summer 1994 Lovehounds 
Collection Tapetree*, of course.  (And also, of course, individual subdubs may 
wish to extend their services and can post to that effect.)  Upwards of 200 
copies of the set will be in lovehounds' hands by the end of this week, and it 
should not be hard to find someone able and willing to make a copy for any 
newsgroup reader who missed the main event due to travel, closed account, etc.

	Thanks to those who have posted so many positive comments on the 
collection!  I'll get together a wrap-up report for later in the week.
