Fisher, Carlton D. <SLUMUS!X7E0@uunet.uu.net> wrote:
>I was wondering if anyone else on the list has heard of Ani Difranco.
>She is a folk-rock artist based in Buffalo, New York who sings alot of
>songs with a strong feminist message.  She's been around since 1990 and
>already has six albums.  I think she is really an excellent artist and
>I have every album that I have been able to find by her.  (I have been
>told that she has a strong Canadian following.)

	I never heard of her before I took a trip to Vancouver last X-mas,
but she seemed to be pretty big with the people I met there.  I heard a few
of her songs and I was really impressed.  Sorta how good Phranc would like
to be.  I guess a good comparison is Tracy Chapman, but with a more soulful
voice and more energy.  Or Faith Nolan.  I nearby heartily recommend her to
you all.
