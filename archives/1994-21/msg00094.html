Tori played here in Knoxville 23 July and I would say that it was a good
concert.  My only real problem with it was that she teased the audience a bit
to often with her tense and sexually loaded dramatic pauses.  After awhile one
just wants to hear a song from beginning to end without significant delays in
the music.  It's OK to vary the performance of a song; I just felt her pauses
were a bit overdone.  Oh, well, I'm first and foremost and forever a Kate fan
anyway.
