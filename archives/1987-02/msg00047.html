Gut Bank --look for it

From: hogge@p.cs.uiuc.edu (John Hogge)
Date: Mon, 2 Feb 87 14:58:56 CST
Subject: Gut Bank --look for it

There's a rockin' album coming out of Hoboken, New Jersey
by Gut Bank, produced by Roger Miller.  Three girls, one
guy.  I heard a demo copy at the record store today; the stuff
is abbrasive/noisy rock and roll, similar to Live Skull but with
prettier, listenable and strong vocals.  The songs weren't dirgy;
much more positive sounding noise.  I imagine they will stick to you.

Check it out.  "These Bitches Rock!!!"

--John
