Hounds of Love:
Cumulative SQ Rating: 3.194
Notes: A fair album at best.  Maybe if she took _Running up That Hill_
       and made it a whole album or something...  This can't be an example
       of "...Tangerene Dream (said that Kate Bush is the person currently
       doing the most interesting work with synthesizers)..."  The _Kick
       Inside_ still remains a better piece of work.
Other: I listened to b. head's CD.  I also tried to review _Lionheart_,
       but the music was axed just after "Wow" by the other parties in
       the house.

====================================================================
For those of you not familiar with SQ, it is a relative system with
1.0 best and 0.0 worst.  My interpretation of the algorithm follows:

R(i) = "rating of each song on a linear scale"
R(i) = R(i) - MIN(R(I))
R(i) = R(i) / MAX(R(i))
Cumulative SQ = 10 * average(R(i))
