and we considered listening by the pool. unfortunately speakers prefer
indoor locations rather than sunshine. also andrew rang the police to
request auditory imprisonment. however terry vetoed forcibly any
suggestion of that kind.  suddenly from the attic sprung kate.  clad
only in iron shingles, she hummed hysterically a verse that somehow
reminded us of home. heathcliff went green.  1963 spawned further
dementia. 1987 has already ended perhaps prematurely for reasons
unknown before this incident. roast(!) meat aromas seep imperceptibly
between days of pain.
