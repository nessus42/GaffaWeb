Doug didn't know of any record/video store in England with an
international mail-ordering service. Here is one :

     ADRIANS VIDEO,
     59 High Street,
     Wickford, Essex SS12 9AQ,
     ENGLAND. (Tel: (0268)733320 ).

Probably they don't sell a lot to people on the American continent
because they are ONLY supplying videos in the PAL format (VHS, BETA, 2000),
I recommend that you specifically states that you want this (PAL) format, or  
else they may be reluctant to accept your order.

If you are more interrested in RECORDS :

     ADRIANS RECORDS,
     36 High Street,
     Wickford, Essex SS12 9AZ,
     ENGLAND. (Tel: (0268)733318 ).

I have used their services many times myself and have never had any
reason to regret this. They accept American Express, Access, and 
Barclaycard, or an international money order.
