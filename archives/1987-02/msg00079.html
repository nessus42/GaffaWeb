good things... standing".  Who said these "nice people" respect anything
beyond their own puds?  Who said this is their "leisure time" - as Jim
says later on to me, "you wanna know what I'm all about?  I spend my whole
day on a garbage truck and all night at the Chef and I get a psychotic
reaction from it, so there..."  What if they don't have a concept of
"leisure time"?  What if it's all an urge, or "psychotic reaction"?  Your
letter here smacks of "I'm too wimpy to stand up to Hate but something
here bothers me (it might be toggling my own hypocracy) so I'll try and
say something to insulate me from it."  Have you ever heard their music?
Try the compilations, "Most Hated Bands in the World" or "Desperately
Seeking Suicide" (an import).
