I just spoke to Vickie and she says she and Chris have temporarily given
up their phone (and with it net access).  For anyone needing to contact them,
Vickie's work number is 312-281-8444.

She also said the Happy Rhodes CD will be out April 1, 1991.
The reason the first 4 albums will probably never be released officially
again, is that the quality is probably not commensurate with the cost
for a record company.  And, Happy is decidedly against rerecording her
older stuff (sound familiar?).  So, unless there is enough demand for 
them that record companies (and consumers) overlook the mediocre 
quality (although IMHO the quality is quite good) of the masters,
it seems unlikely volumes 1-4 will ever be "released".  Which
is really too bad.  Maybe the demand will be great enough eventually.
Right now, though, she is still looking for a label.

-mjm
