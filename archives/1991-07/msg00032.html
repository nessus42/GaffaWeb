while we're on the subject of Second Coming records:

i just got _Kathy's Album II_ (or whatever it's called) and _If You Could
See Me Fly_ at SC, for $10 and $24 respectively...  and then on the way
home, WFNX played _Wuthering Heights_ with the explanation "Kate Bush is
absolutely the most incredible vocalist in rock" (or words to that effect).

8-}

also, Second Coming usually has a good selection of posters (no, no _Dreaming_
posters...), singles, etc.  

also also, remember that they have at least one store in New York City --
a block or so west of Washington Square.  This store is, i think, the 
original, and they are very knowledgable about things KaTely.

finally, re the mike/Jeff/woj discussion of the T< symbol on the Iron
Maiden album -- it is worthwhile to recall Vickie's post KonvenTion posting
about this symbol's history with the Knights Templar.  much as i would hope
that Iron Maiden was expressing their awe and admiration for Ms. Bush, i 
suspect that the reference was to the original users of the symbol...

always happy to be corrected, i am (footah-wise)
