TD: look on Kate's sleeves
HOL: look on the back side, among the wrinkles in Kate's clothes
NFE: look on the gatefold, if it's a British LP; look on the inner
     sleeve, if it's an American LP.  I'm not sure that it can be found
     at all on the CD, but if it can, it would be somewhere inside the
     booklet.
