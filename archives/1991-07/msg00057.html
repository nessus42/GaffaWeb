For those who are clueless, the BOB(best of breed)S are a dynamite
accapella group with witty lyrics and a very full sound.
ISO is a modern dance group (with major MoMIX influences and some
former members thereof) who dance to BOBs songs (and to some on their
own, including Bulgarian Chorus songs, which provides the requisite link to
you know who...).  Go see them if you can!
