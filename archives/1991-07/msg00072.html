To all,

I was on this term and realized that I've got a few of really bizarre
little stories regarding KaTe that I thought I'd share them... maybe
we all have these little oddities that are strange.  Anyway, here I
go...

----------ROTC uses KaTe song on cadences tape!!!!!!--------------

A friend of mine is a ROTC, and he lived down the hall from me in
the dorm here.  I (as usual) was playing KaTe's music on a bright 
Saturday morning, with the door open to catch a breeze- it was just
turning fall, but still warm.  He was passing by on his way out for
his morning jog, and I was playing my Hounds CD.  He came in to chat
for a bit, and at the time- Cloudbusting was playing, and he commented
that it had a great beat to it-  almost like a military cadence...
He got so attached to the song that eventually he asked me if he could
record it at the end of his tape, so I put the Organon mix since it
was my favorite mix, and to this day is his favorite song on the tape!

-----------------------------------------------------------------

-----------Around the world in Katey days------------------------

I'm currently saving up money to travel around the world next summer,
or during christmas if I come into a lot of money somehow.  What I'd
like to do is visit as much people who listen to Kate...Because I 
won't have that much money extra, It'd be cheaper (and nicer) if I
could stay as a guest with someone rather than going the Bed@Breakfast
route.  I plan to definitely visit Japan, the British Isles, France,
Finland, Germany, Spain, Denmark, Italy and Greece, Plus Russia would
be cool.... Of course, if the tour does materialize, I may choose not
to leave home until I see the concert!!  ;^)  Or maybe I'll shorten
the trip to 12 days of KateMas!!  (Does anyone know of a cheap way
to get to England?  I'd like to fly, but I don't want to spend $500)

----------------------------------------------------------------

------------Kate Immortalized on cover of comic book------------

I just picked up two issues of this comic from a year or so ago called
Media Starr.  Ish's 2 & 3 used the covers of The Whole Story and The
Dreaming, and the only change in the pictures of Kate that I can tell
is that on the reproduction of The Dreaming, the artist left out the
key in Kate's mouth...  Don't know if these have any collector's value,
since I got them for $0.50 a piece....  
 [Oh, the comic wasn't that bad, either, but had nothing to do with K]

---------------------------------------------------------------

-------------Army Dreamers banned in UK, but not Cure.  Why?---

As we all know, AD was a nono on BBC, but for some reason, the Cure's
song KILLING AN ARAB didn't get on the blacklist.  Maybe that's on the
Play-as-much-as-possible list?  Strange...

---------------------------------------------------------------
