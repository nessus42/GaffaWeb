I finally succumbed to the temptation of the album `This Rhythm is
Mine' by Sally Barker yesterday. The cover has been tempting me for a
while now. 

I love it.

Suzanne Vega with C&W influences.

I never thought I'd hear a song with a steel guitar that I'd like.
