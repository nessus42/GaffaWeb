>Does anyone ever post photos of KB to this group?  Would love to view same...

Yes, people DO sometimes post photos, and get (deservedly) flamed for it.

which folks get by Email, and the bandwidth from binaries is NOT cool.

Check out these sources if you want pictures:
ftp://ftp.uu.net/pub/music/artists/b/bush.kate/
ftp://ftp.uwp.edu/pub/music/artists/b/bush.kate/

http://american.recordings.com/WWWoM/cgi-bin/ubl?card+1032
(This URL has no photos itself, but has pointers to places
with dozens of photos, as well as lyrics, the FAQ, etc.)
