Hello fellow Love Hounds,

Daniel Kian Mc Kiernan  responded to Mitch Roedoe's plea:

>> I'm not a Kate Bush fan myself. But there is this young woman, who is.
>> And she is the subject of all my dreams, the queen of all my fantasies
>> and the goal of all my wishes.
>>
>The appearance is that you're asking a bunch of Kate Bush fans to help
>you mislead another Kate Bush fan as to the depth of your appreciation
>of Kate Bush, or perhaps as to  your willingness to familiarize
>yourself with the work of Kate Bush.

>It would seem better all around for you to actually buy or borrow a
>bunch of Bush's stuff, and listen to it.  If you find that thought
>unacceptable, then I think that there's a real =problem= here.

An excellent suggestion, Daniel.

Mitch,  your object of desire obviously has good tastes.  Perhaps if
you were to make an effort to understand why the lady is a KaTe fan
by following Daniel's advice, your odds of winning her heart may
improve.  After all, your common interests would be expanded, and
you can't not gain insight into the lady's thought processes by
exploring her conviction to KaTe (sorry for the double negative!).

Even if you fail in your quest, you still come out a winner by opening
your own mind and discovering why it is that KaTe has such a
devoted following.

Hope this helps Mitch,

Van Smith
