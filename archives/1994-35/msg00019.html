HOMEGROUND 53 is now out with all the latest news of Kate's new single 
in the UK, and all the other news.
 
We are particularly pleased to have an article by Colin  Lloyd-Tucker,
who with Paddy Bush has just released their album "Skyscraping". Colin 
writes about his musical career, and his early work with Kate.
 
HOMEGROUND   is  the  oldest  established  Kate   Bush   fanzine   now
in production. It  was started in 1982  and now, as then,  is  run  by
three Kate Bush fans independently of any official fan organisation or 
record company.  It has readers  in  the  UK,  in  most  countries  of
Europe, in the United  States and Canada, South America, in Australia, 
New Zealand and Japan.  Our aim is to  provide a service of  news  and
information about Kate and her music, and to provide  a  platform  for
review and discussion of Kate's work on a world-wide basis.
 
HOMEGROUND  is  usually  published   four   times    a    year,    and
supplemented  (for subscribers) by HOMEGROUND UPDATE issued when there 
is urgent news.
