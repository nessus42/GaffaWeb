TLTCTC screenings in NY, Chicago, & Madison

Hi all!

A couple of the regional Sony alternative marketing reps asked me to pass on 
this information to everyone about free TLTCTC screenings that are happening in 
theaters in NY, Chicago, & Madison, WI.  in a couple of weeks. Here's the 
information about when's & where's in case anyone's interested. If you're there 
in NY look for Troy and if you're in Chicago or Madison look for Sally and say 
hi and tell them you read it here!

NY:  Screenings are Oct. 27th at 7:30 and 8:45 pm at Quad Cinemas, 34 W. 13th 
St. (betw. 5th & 6th Aves.).  Both screenings are free to the first 156 people 
who arrive at each one.

CHICAGO:  Screening is being held on Sun. Oct. 30th at 3 pm at Facets 
Multimedia, 1517 West Fullerton.  Tickets are free, but must be picked up in 
advance at one of two record stores: Inside Track, 825 W. Armitage in Chicago,  
(312) 348-1045  and Chicago Compact Disc, 1640 Orington Ave. in Evanston,  
(708) 328-2202.

MADISON:   Screening is Tues. Nov. 1st at 5:30 pm at the Orpheum Theater, 216 
State St. Tickets are free, but must be picked up in advance at B-Side, 436 
State St., (608) 255-1977.

By the way, the final, seemingly ever-changing word on the U.S. release dates 
for the film seems to be the Oct. 25th for the VHS version and Nov. 1st for the 
laser disc.  

Hope this is helpful,

Lisa
