I just picked up my copy of The Line, The Cross & The Curve on video over 
at The Virgin Megastore in Hollywood. After several calls to various 
outlets, they were the only store who had it and they stocked about 20 
copies, half of which are allready gone.

For those looking for it, it is released through Columbia Music Video 
(CMV) and has a stock number of 19V 50118.

Since I saw it in the theatre, I hope it doesn't loose too much in the 
transition to video.

Tim
