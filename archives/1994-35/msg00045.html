> And, as a sort of morbid curiosity, does anyne know how Alan Murphy died?

Murphy died of AIDS.  He was guitarist for Level 42 at the time, but
really only played on one of their albums (Staring At The Sun).  There's a
Level 42 documentary out in the US called Fait Accompli that follows the
band, including Murphy, on the tour for that album.
