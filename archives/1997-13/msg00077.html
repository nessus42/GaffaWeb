sonic experience. Seeing that we're on the subject of age and first 
times, I'm 26 and heard Kate for the first time when the video of

some more :-(. BTW are the rumours true that she is readying a new 
album for release somewhere in September? Are there any lovehounds in 
South Africa that are reading this?
