I'm 28 (but I *just* turned 28) and mostly a lurker on this list.  I
remember the song Running Up the Hill when it came out and loved it.  Bus
alas, I lived in rural West Virginia, and records/cd's that were not top 40
(as well as the $ to buy them with) were somewhat difficult to come by.
Anyway, a few years ago, right after I had just gotten my first "real" job
and was brain dead from lack of intellectual stimulation, a friend
introduced me to Kate.  She set my head on fire. . .it took me a year to
come down off of that high.  I remember when I realized that this Kate lady
he kept talking about was the one who sang Running Up the Hill, I started
constantly singing about "making a deal with God" and my mother asked me if
this Kate Bush sang religious music. . .


I told her it depended on how you look at it. . .and who you talked to. . .
