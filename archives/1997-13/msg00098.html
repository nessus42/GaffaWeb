I gave a tape of The Whole Story to a female friend and she loved it.  A
few weeks later she gave me a copy of Paula Cole's new album, saying Kate
(whom she'd never really heard) reminded her of Paula Cole.  I do love the
Paula Cole's album, much different from Kate Bush although there are some
simularities.  This friend of mine  said, "She is the new Kate Bush." 
Which of course I could never agree with, but it is an interesting
statement from a new Kate fan's point of view.

I do recommend the Paula Cole album, although everyone here probably
already knows of it since Peter Gabriel sings on one of the songs and
Paula Cole toured with him.  Does anyone know if she has any connection
with Kate BusH?
