Bought my copy of the anniversary HOL last night at Towers Records in
Piccadilly Circus, ?17.99.  It comes in a lovely hard slipcase with just
the picture of Kate and the hounds on the front and the extended track
listing on the back.

The HOL booklet looks lovely, lots of nice pics and all, with Peter's
commentary and the other album information inside.  The EMI booklet is
.. nothing special!

Unless it's a new album from Kate, I think I'll always be dissatisfied
at the content on specials like these - you know, wishing for rare
tracks, rare info, something a bit personal from Kate added in, etc -
but it does look lovely and I'm glad they've made it a bit special  -
for Kate's sake and that of her fans.
