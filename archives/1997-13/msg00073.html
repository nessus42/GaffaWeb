Steve Borden (sborden@netway1.mdc.net) wrote:
>And I thought I was a young one around here!  (I'm 27.)  Any other Kate
>fans out there in this age bracket?

I'm 24. Been a fan since '90, when a good friend and fellow high school 
student (we were seniors at the time) brought WH off TWS in to play for 
our AP English class when we were reading the novel. Reactions varied, 
from our much-beloved teacher finding it 'typical rock' (?!) to this one 
kid who was really into U2 (but later disavowed them in college when 
they entered their ironic phase) asking me a few weeks later at a party,
when I was enthusing about WH and my own new copies of TWS and TSW, 'You 
really liked it? I thought Jenny brought it in as a joke.'

Bought everything on cassette at used record shops in East Lansing
when I hit Michigan State in fall 1991.

Got my first taste of 'new' Kate in fall 93 with TRS.

Got the boxed set from my parents for Christmas '93--and nearly
lost a friend and a roommate because I played it so much.

Have spent the years since tracking down demos, bootlegs, videos,
guest appearances (Prince, Common Ground, Peter Gabriel), samples
(Aphex Twin) ... and waiting for the next album.
