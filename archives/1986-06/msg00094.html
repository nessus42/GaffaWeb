Subject: Music for Palaces

Beware of that Eno bootleg MUSIC FOR PALACES. It's a two disc album, one 33 and
 45. 'The Lion Sleeps Tonight' is on the 45, however, on the 33 I found not Eno
, but a live recording of Talking Heads. I imagine that more than one album got
 mislabelled. It's produced by Centrifugal Records in England.
