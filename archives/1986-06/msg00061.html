While I was in a record store today, I took a look at More Blank Than Frank
by Eno and I saw no credits for Lion Sleeps Tonight or anything else I've
ever heard that tune called.  It does seem to be a good compilation of
'Greatest Hits' (UHGH) from the period it covers, though.  No ambient
stuff, but some reasonably good Eno Fripp interactions (St. Elmo's Fire).
Has anybody actually heard this LP, I have everything Eno, I think, except
Lion Sleeps Tonight (I thought it would eventually show up on an LP), and
I might consider buying it since the single seems to be out of print.
