Recently bought the Shop Assistants' single called "Safety Net"...
tho' I think it is good, it does not deserve the hype that the
English music newspapers has given them...comparing them to Jesus
and the Mary Chain minus the feedback.  The group consists of all
females with the exception of the guitarist.  It sounds all too
same tho', plus the singer does has a weak voice.

I guess it is acceptable, but definately not a "must buy" single.
Oh well...  Nothing really new...

Maybe because the reviewers heard the words "trip you up" and
immediately thougt of JaMC...blah!
