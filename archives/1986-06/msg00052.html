As long as I've mentioned Cabaret Voltaire ... Hearing "Sensoria" first got
me interested, so I went out and got "Micro-Phonies", then "The Crackdown",
then the Crepuscle stuff (2 EPs), then "2x45".  Now I've bought "Voice of
America" and "Red Mecca" and, after first being turned off, am beginning to
see through the noise and actually like them.

SO: What should I buy next?  What is considered to be CV's best album?  Is
Hula really the new CV?
