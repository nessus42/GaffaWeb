Subject: Another rejoinder, a and humble request

Doug Alan (4/6/86):

> Now there are even people now on the mailing list (a mailing list for
> Kate Bush fans) who don't even like Kate Bush!  Oh well.

I don't know Kate Bush, so I can't tell whether I like her or not.
When I said earlier that I'm not overly impressed with her music, I meant
that.  Although some of her music is quite good, I simply had to respond
to the absolute, overwhelming praise that had been expressed so far.
No artist deserves the unqualified praise she's received in this little
digest.

Meanwhile, has anyone heard of a band called Japan?  I have two of their
albums, "Assemblage", and "Gentlemen Take Polaroids".  The former is a
collection of songs (hits?) from their other albums; the latter is
original material.  I'll review them, if anyone expressing expresses an
interest.

Geiger counters still clicking...
Mark

P.S. My favorite song is still "Starless" by King Crimson.
