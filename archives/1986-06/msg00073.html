Eno's Lion Sleeps Tonight

I HAD (sniff) a recording of this from an excellent Eno bootleg called Music
for Palaces.  This was a live recording, and with a startling amount of
energy compared with what you get on the typical Eno studio album.  If you
ever see it in a store, buy it, remember me, like a mensch, write me, and
I'll send you money for postage and a cassette.
