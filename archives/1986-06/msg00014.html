Re: Depressing music...

     While we are still on the subject of depressing songs (i.e. those that
make you feel like garbage), I thought I'd contribute a few of my 
favorites:

	-Song: When You Smile (off of _Days_of_Wine_and_Roses_)
	-Group: The Dream Syndicate
	-Remarks: boy, this one just makes me feel lousy.  The intro is a
		bit of distorted guitar into a dark sounding bass line. 
		Actually it's pretty hard to describe, so I'll just list 
		the lyrics to the chorus:

			When you smile,
			I don't know what to do,
			Cause I could lose everything,
			In a minute or two,
			And it seems like the end of the world,
			When you smile.

		Real cheery, huh?  Good one to play when your out with a
		new girl/boyfriend!

	-Song: The Pillow (off of _Geoffrey_Morgan_)
	-Group: UB40 
	-Remarks: this one has repetetive music that works well with the
		"basic" (read "simple") lyrics.  The song is about a
		prostitute lying on a white bed reflecting her life just
		before she slits her wrists as day breaks.  Makes you 
		feel a little better about your own life.

	-Song: intro to International (off of _Dazzle_Ships_)
	-Group: OMD (Orchestral Maneouvers in the Dark)
	-Remarks: not really a song, but rather a very dark introduction
		to an instrumental.  The intro is an actual broadcast 
		of a news announcer telling of a girl whose two hands had
		been cut off at the wrists by former Somoza guards.  The
		next line reads "The secretary general of the UN had this
		to say:" at which point the music starts.
