Vickie here. Well, to make a long story short, we were still in town (we
didn't leave Kansas City until Monday the 25th), Sue (who'll be taking 
over for me until we get my in-home "studio" up and running) got sick with
the flu and knew we hadn't left yet so she called me to fill in for her.
To wit-I got to do an unexpected live show. I typed this in a rush before
we left but never got a chance to post it. Belatedly, here it is:
