Before I so rudely interrupted myself by typing in "ok" and sending the 
last message off unfinished, I was mentioning that there is no estimate
as to how long it will be until C 'n V will be back online. 

But speaking of Happy Rhodes:  BUY ALL OF HER TAPES!!  Vickie was 
great enough to offer a big slice of Happy's recorded works near the end 
of the 6-hour _SIG_ marathon.  I have planned to purchase all of Happy's
tapes; please show your support and encouragement.  She is a great talent
to begin with, and as if that weren't enough, she sounds _just_ like a 
younger Kate at times.  As I was telling C 'n V, I almost swerved off of
Interstate 280 when _Would That I Could_ came on!  Amazing resemblance!! 
No slight to Jane, now, but Happy may just be Jesus after all! Of 
course we ALL know who God is.  

She really IS!
