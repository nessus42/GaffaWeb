   Now, on to the mailbag:

 >Now...if I could just see a film that has some KATE music in it, I'll be
 >all set.  Hmmmm.....guess I'll have to rent _She's Having A Baby_ soon.  :)
 >                                       All the best.
 >
 >-- Deb Wentorf

   Relax, Deb, it's not really a bad movie. But if you can't take John
Hughes, why not try Roeg's _Castaway_? It's got _Be_Kind_to_My_Mistakes_,
and it's on video in the U.S. now. And then there's _The_Chocolate_War_,
which makes pretty effective use of _Running_Up_That_Hill_. And if you
can ever find a decent copy of _The_Magician_of_Lublin_ (starring Alan
Arkin shouting unpleasantly over Kate's beautiful vocal of the Maurice
Jarre theme-song), let IED know, will you?

Harty died? Just curious.
