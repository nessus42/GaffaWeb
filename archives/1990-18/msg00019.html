   Jorn Barger, IED is sorry that you were disappointed in the
official _TSW_ t-shirt, though in IED's opinion it is _highly_
cool. IED would only remind you that thorough descriptions of
the black shirt were posted in Love-Hounds, and that there were
no doubt several current Love-Hounds who would have been happy
to re-post the pertinent information had you but asked for
it prior to sending off your hard-earned.
   As for the _Cathy_ book by JCB: There are a good many more
than fifteen photographs in the book, and although the price
_is_ ridiculously high, some artbooks of similar size and quality
have retailed for even higher prices, not counting custom
packaging and airmail delivery. It all depends on one's perspective.
   Back to t-shirts: Drukman describes a "sand-through-the-fingers"
shirt with Kate's name down the front in red letters. IED has
obtained another white U.S.-made t-shirt featuring the "sand-t-t-f"
shot, this time printed in light brown, with only a very small
"Kate Bush" in thin capital letters at the top left of the picture.
It's also a "bootleg", but it should be relatively easy to come by
in the U.S.
   Finally, about the "doily", Jorn: it's actually three flowers,
and the design (judging by its style) is, in IED's opinion, by
John Carder Bush.

-- Andrew Marvick
