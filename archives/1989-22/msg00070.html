Subject: The Es-Sensual Kate
Keywords: KT is full is suprises

Organization: Wollongong Justice Central



	I just got the 12" TSW single! I want you to imagen what it was
like when I drag my sister over to lissen to the new release!

	Me : "You've got to hear this you'll love it!"
	Sis : "ok, ok, get on with it"

	Play's side A and doesn't get TSW, but instead gets TSW instramental
	version.

	Me : "Something's wrong that should have had Kate singing!"
	Sis : "Can I go?"
	Me : "Hang on, this time for sure"

	Play's side A and gets the same thing again.

	Me : "Arrrgg"

	I play side A a few more times. Sister goes. Then after a break
	I give it one more try. What do I get... TSW!! Double tracking
	can be a real pain sometimes!

 I can hear KT laughing from here!

 - Graham
