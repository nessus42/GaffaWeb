KITS will feature _TSW_ on today's "New Music Wednesday"

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Wed, 11 Oct 89 11:05:11 PDT
Subject: KITS will feature _TSW_ on today's "New Music Wednesday"


I hope this gets to all Bay Area fans in time.  This is a once-in-a-
lifetime event, I think.

This morning, Mark Hamilton of KITS played "Love and Anger" (the first
time I've heard that song on the radio), and then he said,
   "From the new Kate Bush album _The Sensual World_, we heard 'Love
and Anger.'  That album will be in the stores October 17.  We'll be
playing cuts from it all day long, so keep it here:  'New Music 
Wednesday' at Live-105."

For those who can't listen to the radio until the evening, "New Music
Wednesday" lasts until midnight tonight.
