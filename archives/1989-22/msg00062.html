Erik Olson writes:

>What I want to know is what the heck is going on in her life that she
>suddenly puts out a "gosh, isn't life great" album after the very angry
>and disallusioned compositions in _The Walking_.  Any clues?


I recently saw a TV blurb about Jane's new album.  Evidently, during
the recording (and writing) of _The_Walking_ , Jane was in the midst
of breaking up with her then-boyfriend _____ Switzer (can't remember
his first name).  Note that he was also a producer on the record -
Hence the more "moody" sound.

Before recording her latest disc, she had just moved in with
her new boyfriend (some Canadian film-maker, whose name
escapes me at the moment).

Interesting bit of trivia:
    Did you know that before recording _No_Borders_Here_ Jane went to
    Spain on the sole purpose of meeting Salvador Dali?  She spent
    4 days at his castle (more specifically, on the doorstep) until
    S. D. would talk with her.
