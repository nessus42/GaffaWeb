To quote Ed "are you trying to make me look like a fool?" Of course I know
what Occam's Razor is.  You want me to apply it in this case and say
"well, the word he appears a lot in the song because she is singing about
a man", "mmmm" is there because it sounds good and is naturally paired with
"yes" in spoken English.  That's a sufficient explanation, and it's the
simplest, so it's a good one.

What can I say?  I don't agree.  Consider that Kate is very specific in
her allusions in the song, even using the proper name "Howth Head", but that
"he" still occurs six times without antecedents.  The easiest way to nail
down the source of the song would have been to mention "Bloom" by name.
I had to pick up the reference from "seedcake"!
