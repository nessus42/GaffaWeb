If by the "last verse" you mean the part where she sings:

"After she shot the guy,
She committed suicide,"

and so on, then you should know that the lyrics to this verse do not
appear in print on any version of this album, as far as I know.
Here are the lyrics, taken from _The Garden_. (Thanks, IED!)

(After she shot the guy,
She committed suicide.)
I'm coming, Rudi!
(And later, when they analyzed,
They found a little one inside.)
I'm coming, coming, coming, honey!
(It must have been Rudi's child.)
I shot him!
(Never mind, she got the guy.)
He hit the ground, Rudi!
(An eye for an eye.)
Ashes to ashes...
(Eye for an eye.)
Rudi!

I guess Kate just wanted us to figure out this verse by ourselves.
