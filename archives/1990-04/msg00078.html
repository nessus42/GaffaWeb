** Second week at #3 on MTV's 120 Minutes Top 10 College Countdown **

I can't take credit for this show. I was off working the bingo game (it's
the only thing that keeps KKFI on the air and all the programmers have to
work the game at least once every eight weeks) so my good friend Sue took
over for me. She's got great taste in music and a wonderful record collection. It was my 100th show and I didn't get to celebrate it :-(
but my two-year anniversary is coming up.
