Vickie here. Here's last night's show. Not all the artists are ones
I saw on the net. Most of them are though. The show developed into
a theme (that happens a lot, I start putting the show together and
it comes alive and takes over) of death, destruction, murder, suicide,
gloom, doom and fun stuff like that directly inspired by that cheery bunch 
over in alt.suicide.holiday! No, I didn't even *try* to explain the
newsgroup to my listeners. They're used to my getting into bizarre
"moods" and coming up with strange set or show topics. No use wasting
15 minutes of air time trying to explain the inexplicable.
