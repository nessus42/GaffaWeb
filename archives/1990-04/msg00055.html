Subject: _BIllboard_ and _Rolling Stone_


Here's the new chart numbers from _Billboard_:

Modern Rock Tracks:  L&A down from #17 to #30 (the bottom of the chart).
		     TSW up from #8 to #6, still with bullet.
Pop Albums Chart:  _TSW_ down from #46 to #49

Here's the most amazing thing of all:  The album has reentered the 
pop CD chart!  It is back on at number 23.

The new _Rolling Stone_ still has no review.  It still shows the album
at number one on the College chart, according to _The Gavin Report_.
Unfortunately, this is clearly out of date, as last week 120 Minutes
said that the album was down to #3, after being at #1 for eight weeks.

In _RS_'s own pop albums chart, _TSW_ went up slightly this time!
I can't remember the exact position.
