>Clannad - Is pastpresent a good buy?

It is sort of a greatest hits, and not a great one in any event. Not
all of Clannad's albums are available on CD, but among those that are,
I would rate them something like this:

Ring of Gold
Dulaman
Magical Ring
Macalla
Clannad in Concert
Sirius
Legend
Past Present
Atlantic Realm

Any of these is well worth having, but if you are not already a fan, I
don't think Past Present is a good place to start. If you are willing
to buy records or tapes, two of their best are not available on CD. I
will not attempt to reproduce their names from memory because my
Gaelic is more than a bit weak.
