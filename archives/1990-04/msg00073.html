Well, I haven't seen anyone else mention this yet, so....

"The Sensual World" on VH-1 or MTV. To that end, I am delighted to report

or so of "The Sensual World." Of course, those perusing the timestamp on
this message will realize that it is also 3:40 AM....so we're probably not
talking Heavy Rotation yet.

Still.....mmmmm, yesss.
