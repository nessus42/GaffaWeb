Hedges

From: Michael Mendelson <mendel@cs.uiuc.edu>
Date: Tue, 30 Jan 90 11:51:15 -0600
Subject: Hedges

If you ever get a chance to see Michael Hedges in concert, do *not*
pass it up.  He redefines the acoustic guitar.  It's that simple.  I
saw him here two nights ago and I am still in awe.  Among many stunning
and mesmorizing original compositions, he played brilliant covers of
Pinball Wizard, Eminence Front, Gimme Shelter, and All Along the
Watchtower.  He even did a FYC piece which was really funny (She Drives
Me Crazy).  His interpretations are bold, stirring, and very moving.
Needless to say just listening to his albums only scratches the
surface.  You don't appreciate the full extent of his talent until you
actually *see* him play one guitar three different ways at the same
time!  Check this guy out.
