KXLU played one side of this (apparantly first) album by a group "Partly
Cloudy", labelled "Left Hemisphere".  The other side is, you guessed it,
"Right Hemisphere".  My first impression was, "Hey, Laurie Anderson Jr.!"
Except there is a little less emphasis on (weird) lyrics and more on
music/sound-effects/noise.  Now I think their music is closer to Tones
on Tail (the 3EP compilation).  It's quite good.  I wonder what the
Right Hemisphere side is like (Cocteau Twins?)  Both fans of LA and
ToT may want to check them out.  Anybody heard of them?

By the way, anybody else think the song "Hawk" from the movie "Trouble
In Mind" is TOTALLY AWESOME?  Arrangement is extremely haunting, and
Marianne Faithful gives the performance of her life.  Any chance I
can get it without buying the whole soundtrack?
