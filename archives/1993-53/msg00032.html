Just because you have a business selling collectibles of an artist you
like a lot doesn't mean you obsess over whether or not "Gaffa" is

>   I haven't claimed that Allen thinks the opposite of me. I have

You have posted your reasons why you think Allen is not a Kate Bush
fan (i.e. opposite of Chris Williams).  Unfortunately, none of these
arguments hold any water.  These are your stated reasons so far:

	(1) Allen runs a business with the goal of making a profit.
	(2) Allen once indicated that he thought many Kate Bush fans
	    are weird.
        (3) Allen once indicated that he thought many Kate Bush fans
            are suckers.
        (4) Allen once publicly expressed anger at John Reimers.

Well, Chris, I am afraid to say that I am guilty of all of these too:
(1) I prostitute my love of computer programming to pay my rent.  (2)
I think that many Kate Bush fans are kind of weird and that (3) many
are suckers in some important ways.  (4) Though I think John Reimers
is a swell guy in most ways, I was mighty peved at him over the issue
of the Night Flight video tape and had no qualms saying so.
