All I can say (my $.02) is that this Jorn/Vickie/Chris etc. stuff is the 
most amazing shit I've ever heard about lately, outside of Oprah!  I'm 
finding myself signing on to read about *it*, vs. anything to do with Kate 
B.  As much as I'd like to keep up on the outcome (what is the deep dark 
secret that Vickie told...?), I'd probably prefer that it all be taken 
elsewhere.  Perhaps we can set up an r.m.jorn group or something?

P.S.  Vickie, I'm not trying to trivialize in any way whatever painful or 
difficult things have been a part of this for you.  It's just that for me (
and several fellow gaffa readers) the soap opera content is just getting too 
high!

So, if it keeps on, I'll no doubt be drawn into reading it, but I guess I'd 
rather see it pass on somewhere else...
