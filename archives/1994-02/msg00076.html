ALL POINTS BULLETIN

I was awakened this morning by the sound of Kate's voice! on National 
Public Radio.  I had to run but it was probably their show "All Things 
Considered" which is aired several times a day on most public radio 
stations.  Usually in the last five or eight minutes of the show they 
talk to a musician or artist.

Call your local public radio and find out when they're airing it and you 
can tape it right off the radio.  I have a nice 7 minute interview with 
Kate they did several years ago when TSW came out.

Good listening!
