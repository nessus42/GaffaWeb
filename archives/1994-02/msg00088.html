In case anyone is interested, the address to write for a tape of Friday's
Morning Edition is:

National Public Radio
2025 M St. NW
Washington, DC
20036
USA

Send $10 for a transcript, or $12 for a tape *of the entire show*.  They can't
do individual segments, as far as I know.  $12 may seem a bit steep, but if
you can't get hold of a good tape someone else made or were too stupid to make
a tape for yourself (like me :P), it's not bad- and you'll be supporting NPR
News, so they can continue to bring us such wonderful features in the future.

Be sure to mention the date it aired: Friday, January 21, 1994. :)
