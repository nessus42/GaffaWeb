I've been away for a few days but I did want to respond to Markku's
attempt to instigate a flame-war between Tori and Kate lovers.  
 
I find a flame-war to be irrelevant.  The two are completely
opposite.  Kate Bush is much more ethereal, spiritual and almost
mystical while Tori Amos is get down in the mud earthy.  Kate will
sing about angels, faeries and hobgoblins while Tori will blaspheme
the Bible, in a constructive manner, of course.
 
IT WOULD BE LIKE COMPARING APPLES TO ORANGES!  Instead, Eat the
music and savour both of them.
 
If anyone is interested I am willing to transcribe a taped
interview that Kate had here on CFNY in Toronto.  This is not the
interview that was broadcasted throughout North America but a much
more intimate and detailed interview at the local level.  I need to
know if there is interest before I set to the tedious task.
 
Thomas Brasch
