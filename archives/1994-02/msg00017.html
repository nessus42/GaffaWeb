Hi everyone,
 
This is a second calling.
 
_Calling all European KaTeFans_: I'd like to organise a European
version of Katemas, at the end of July this year. So, which
European KaTeFan has a house which we can use for the official
Central-European KaTeMas? How many people in and around this part
of central Europe can come to the 1994 KaTeMas? And who'd like to
organize this thing along with me? C'mon... this could be great!
 
For those wondering what this is about: KaTeMas is the yearly
celebration of Kate's birthday in different places on the globe at
someone's home (or at least, that's what I understand), by watching
the Goddess's visual and audial works. Central Europe was sadly
missing last year on the list of celebration sites, an error I'd
like to see corrected. After all, we here on the continent are her
biggest fans, aren't we? <smiling smugly and challengingly> <OK OK
so I like to brag - so what?>
Enthusiastic responses to my address please.
