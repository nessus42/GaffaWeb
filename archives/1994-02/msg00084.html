For those of you who are getting any of this, I called the Radio Station 
thiss morning and found out how to get copies oof the show with the KT 
interview this morning.  I'll post it on Monday when I'm back at the 
office - I f or got to bring it home with me.  The weird spelling is not 
mine -- it is being inserted by the system as I type -- somxething 
strange going on in the Networld . . .

The other news is that the Berkeley show is first come first served -- no 
advance ticket sales, no radio promo nonsense.  Kate will not, as of this 
date, be present. I asked the theatre if they were prepared for a huge 
crowd, and they pooh poohed me a bit -- then I said that their event had 
already been postd to the Inter net and that people would be coming from 
Sacramento and San Jose, not to menton the thousands of Bay Area 
Katefans.  WOW he said (he really did) thanks for the info.  That's 
terrific -- we can really hold a lot of people.
