Once upon a time, in the garden of Eve,
Adyn played with all the animals as if they were his pets
and sometimes the Unicorn herself would come
and lay her head in his lap
and sing sweet unicorn songs

But one day, Unicorn hit a wrong note
and Devil came out of the bushes
and scared the unicorn away
and Adyn stood up to protest but Devil
hit Adyn upside the head with "Fruit!"

And Adyn sat down and cried.
But it happened again, and again.
So finally Adyn picked up the fruit and took a bite
But the bite stuck in his throat

And the next day when Devil came
The fruit in his throat shouted
You have a severe personality disorder!
And Devil came running over,
shook Adyn's hand
hit him with another piece of fruit
and left.

And Adyn called his new voice
Devil's Evil Image

and when devils came around
DEI took care of them.

But the unicorn hardly ever came back, after that.
It was just too dangerous for her.
