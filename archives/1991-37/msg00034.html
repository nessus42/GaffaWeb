   We have fairly extensive archives of music videos from the late '70s
and early '80s. As one who believed the Buggles (and the guy who wrote it)
statement that video would kill the radio stars, and one who was sorely
disappointed when that didn't happen, I was first attracted to Kate's music
by her presentation. She was one of the new generation who would topple
the bloated "arena rock" crap my friends listened to. Little did I know
that the dinosuars would just hire film-makers and co-opt MTV. Anyone
who doesn't believe me when I say that Kate's early videos stood head
and shoulders above the rest of what was out there....well just drop on
by and we'll play samples of the average early eighties videos until you
try to knaw you're own leg off...
