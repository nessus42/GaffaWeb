> 4. Is Kate on the 4AD label?  Just what IS the 4AD label anyway, and who

Those are my favorite 4AD groups too!!  You may also like "This Mortal Coil".
TMC is not really a band, it is compilations of contributions by various
4AD artists.  Three albums: "It will end in tears", "Filigree and Shadow",
and "Blood".  Great stuff!  Also check out "Lonely is an eyesore", a sampler 
of various 4AD groups.

The address of the 4AD mailing list is: 4ad-l@jhuvm.bitnet
