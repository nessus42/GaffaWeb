of PostScript programming.

my picture-laden version of the books should be ready, oh let's see,
say in 2003, give or take a year... ;-\

a tip of the obscurity hat to the druk-man, for managing to work
the Legendary Pink Dots and Uncle Bonzai into the same paragraph.
while i don't quite grok the UB/Happy connection, i was glad for an
excuse to go and listen to my Bonzai albums again...

can we *please* stop debating the Happy question?  there *are* no
more lyrics to post.  anyway, a post about Happy *might* interest
someone;  a post about posts about Happy will almost certainly *not*.
(hmm, gonna wear out the * key...)

from Indipop/MNW (INDCD 1) -- this is a Steve Coe project featuring
a number of indian/indian influenced artists.  check out _Pump Up the
Bhangra_ from the New Pardesi Music Machine.  B-}  Ms. Chandra contributes
_One_: one of my favorite post-Monsoon songs.
