Kate appeared on Saturday Night Live one of the times Eric Idle was the
host.  HOWEVER -- whenever this show has been repeated in the U.S., Kate's
parts have been cut out!!  In fact, this episode has appeared on
videotape, with a picture of Kate's performance on the back of the box,
but again, her part has been cut out!!  This is why nobody seems to have a
decent copy of this performance -- the only copies around are 
multi-generational ones from the first time this show actually aired,
back in 1979.  I would extremely interested if they show Kate in the
version MTV Europe is airing.
