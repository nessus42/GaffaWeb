The next (?) message is the start of me posting excerpts from the original
articles from the Garden.  Each message will have a subject something like
this:

THE GARDEN - GARDEN1.TXT - SECTION 1 (OF 5)

This is to make it easier, hopefully to keep track of what is what.  I have
chosen file names that roughly match the content of the file, but most files
have more than just what their name implies.  The files are between 75k and
200k so some people may need to keep them in there seperate sections for their
particular system.  Each message will have one section which will be rouphly
20k-25k long and I will mail each section, one a day, at about 11pm california
time, so they should (I guess) arive at roughly the same time.  

        Also be aware that these files were originally meant to be printed and
 oops printed out, so they refer to "Volumes" rather than file names.  Also be
aware that they have not even been seen by IED since I converted them, so they
may have errors in them that I somehow introduced, for instance the
introduction has a cut off line, but I don't think that one was my fault. 
Anyway, enjoy.  I will probably continue to send them until I am finished with
Cloudbusting 1.0 and then start sending that.
