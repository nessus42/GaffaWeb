Richard Caldwell noted some things in _Twin Peaks_ reminiscent of
Kate Bush.

I saw in some magazine article over a year ago that Kyle
McLachlan was a big Kate Bush fan.  The guy who plays Sheriff
Truman (obviously I don't follow the show too well if I can't
remember the actor's name) says he was in a hotel room next to
Kyle's and would hear Kate Bush and Led Zeppelin through the
walls.  This allows for some possibility that Kate has been an
indirect influence on the show.
