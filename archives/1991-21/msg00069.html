Just thought Vickie and others might be curious....

Happy is getting a pretty good response here.  I've played her almost
every week since we got the CD (Wed nights 8-10pm), and other djs have
found her CD too and are playing it.  I got a not from one dj and he
says: "I played 'Phobos' on my Monday night show, and I'll be *featuring*
most  of the rest of the album on my thur. show...Tasty stuff!" (Emphasis
added by me).

And according to the local Rose Records, her CD is snatched up
immediately whenever they get a new one in.  

David Thomas
Champaign IL
WEFT radio
"Primary Objects" Wed 8-10pm

p.s. A nod to woj's review of Happy:  As much as I enjoy listening
to Warpaint, I must agree with woj's comments.  It's nice, but...
I mean it's nothing overly *amazing* as other comments on this
list suggest.  Now, try The Creatures FIRST lp called "Feast" (I think
that's what it is called).  Now _that's **amazing**!  IMVHO.
