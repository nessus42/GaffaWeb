Roy Sette (kl7@rahul.net) wrote:
: You've all heard comparisons of this singer or that with Kate.  They
: very aren't too accurate in most cases.  Like Tori vs. Kate, I mean
: *really*.  The differences are way more numerous than the similarities.

: But now I think I've found Kate's double.  Her name is Ana Torroja and
: she possesses the exact same thrilling soprano of Kate's early work.  Ana
: is the lead singer of a Spanish group named "Mecano."  Their music
: is very well produced.  So if you're adventurous and don't mind Spanish
: lyrics, give them a listen.  You won't be disappointed.

It's possible that you've found that similarity, but it's certainly
an exception.  I would be cautious in recommending Mecano outright,
because the majority of their stuff is not at all similar to Kate.

I live in Spain, so I hear Mecano all the time on the radio, and
I'm quite surprised to hear this comparison!

On the other hand, I don't think Mecano are bad, simply a little
bit too mainstream soft pop to be placed in the same league as
Kate.
