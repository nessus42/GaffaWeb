I saw October Project in concert last night at the Iron Horse Music
Hall (Northampton Mass).  What a great concert!  This is definitely
a group worth checking out.  The harmonies are wonderful, and the
lead singer (Mary) has a beautiful voice.  They said they will be
touring around the US, then going over to Europe.  For part of the
tour they will be appearing with Sarah McLachlan, but I don't know
when that will be.
