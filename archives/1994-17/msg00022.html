I have just bought the Australian release of the CD-single
for "eat the music", and have found it contains the following
tracks:
  Eat The Music
  Eat The Music(extended mix) 
  You Want Alchemy
  Shoedance(Red Shoes dance mix)

Unfortunately, the "extended mix" is the edited version(i.e. 
shorter than the album version), and not the 9.5 minute U.S.
extended version. 
It is supposed to be available in "scratch and sniff" format
as well, but I haven't seen this yet.
