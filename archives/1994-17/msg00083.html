Yes, there are books of kate lyrics!

	International Music Productions (IMP) 
	Southend Road, Woodford Green, Essex IG8 8HN, England, UK
	ISBN: 0-86175-413-1  order ref#:14305
	7.95(pounds) or about $18 US
	Title: Kate Bush Complete, EMI music Publishing (which I think 
can be ordered through Warner Bros.
	Contents: Lyrics and Music from the Kick Inside through to 
Experiment IV (also including: December will be magic again, Ne t'enfui 
pas, Passing through Air, the empty Bullring, Burning Bridge, Le baiser 
D'infant, Ran-tan Waltz, Warm and soothing, Under the Ivy, Not this time, 
and Night Scented Stock[!]) Also: a chronology, discography, videography 
and info on Homeground

AND, there is a book of music and lyrics for the Sensual world, but I 
don't have that info at hand - try your local music store if they sell 
sheet music, ask to see the catalogue

I hope this helps!  Cheers Bruce Gordon  (bruceg@u.washington.edu)
