Actually, I quite like the remix.  It's what a remix SHOULD be.  Most
remixes - and Kate's as guilty of this as anyone - are taking a song,
then taking it again with the vocals stripped, then moshing the two
together.  That way, a 3:15 song will have a 6:30 remix.

the result:  Booo-ooo-rrrr-ing!  (example:  The Eat The Music remix)
Kate's only three remixes I like are Cloudbusting (The Organon Mix)
and The Big Sky (Meteorlogical Mix).  Why?  For the most part, she
adds things that make them completely new songs.  Not just stretched
out and/or padded.  The third remix I like is Rubberband Girl, even
though it IS just a stretched out version of the original.  I have no
idea why, but I like it anyway.

That reminds me - and I'm surprised that a) I never thought of this
before, and B), this isn't a FAQ:  Who DOES those spoken voices
in the Big Sky remix?  The one saying "This cloud should be removed
immediately!" sounds JUST LIKE John Cleese.

Which brings us to now the FORTH Kate remix I like _ Shoedance.  This
is an utterly NEW song, it's SO re-worked and done over.  OK, so I like
the original better.  Much better.  But in terms of turn-the-volume-to-11
and-bounce-around-the-room, Shoedance fits the bill quite nicely, thank you.
And this is coming from someone who HATES anything that even comes close
to being in the neighborhood of even slightly resembling Techno.
