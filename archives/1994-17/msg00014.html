    Final Notice: Discount Tickets for "Miss Saigon" Available

  A few weeks ago I posted a message to some of these newsgroups offering
tickets to the Toronto production of "Miss Saigon" at 20% less than the box
office price. It elicited several comments which deserve to be answered.
My response is:
(1) The seats are in the center of the orchestra in rows B through H (the
    seats sometimes advertised for less are in the last two rows).
(2) The performance is on Tuesday, June 7 at 8:00 PM (it is not a matinee).
(3) The price of $78 is in Canadian dollars (about US$56).

  These tickets were obtained for attendees at the Supercomputing Symposium
'94 conference. Because fewer of them than expected were ordered, some of
the tickets are still available. If you are interested in seeing "Miss
Saigon" from the best seats in the theatre at a discount of 20%, just give
me a call. You can even use your credit card.
