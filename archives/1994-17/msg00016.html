Hi all,

    After all this time FINALLY the KaTe film will be shown in Sydney TONIGHT!
I've lost years off my life trying to organize this thing and I'll think VERY
seriously before I do something like it again. There'll be some 90 KaTe fans
there from various clubs (KBC, Homeground, r.m.g, etc) plus a heap of EMI
industry guests. I've got my KT shirt ready (it's covered in badges) I even
went out and got some cheap tennis shoes and dyed them red! :-) I'll let you
all know how things went tomorrow.

    As for the other states, well I'll be seeing EMI about them tonight BUT it
looks like I'll have to find a person willing to organize a theatre in each
state to show the movie. EMI will supply the film for free, but people will
have to pay for the screening. Oh well... :-/ I'll get onto this after I've
enjoyed this evening!
