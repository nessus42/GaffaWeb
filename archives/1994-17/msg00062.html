   Lyrics for all of Kates albums (and hundreds of others) are available
at Dave Datta's wonderful music archive, ftp.uwp.edu.

   If your machine has ftp access just type:

ftp ftp.uwp.edu

   The lyrics are in text files in the directory:

/pub/music/lyrics/b/bush.kate

   The names of the files are:

dont_give_up     lionheart.       red.shoes        the.whole.story
hounds.love      miscellaneous.   sensual.world
kick.inside      never.for.ever   the.dreaming

   If you don't have ftp access, you can retrieve these files via mail.
