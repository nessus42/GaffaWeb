Hi,
 
This is a nice story.
I don't really know the truth about it, because the man in the store was
telling a strange story, but this is what I heard the other day:
 
Apparently, EMI Holland has released "The red shoes" on CD-single a few
weeks ago, simultaneous with EMI UK. In a limited number of stores part 1 of
that CDsingle has indeed shown up. (The one with "You want alchemy" on it)
That's the reason why "Eat the music" is (still) not released in Holland.
EMI Holland quickly realised what they'd done wrong after contact with EMI
UK and the apparent release "Eat the music" that they noticed.
Anyway, the CD-singles, a 2-track and a 4-track will be in the stores from
May 3rd.
 
Or so the man told me.
 
He didn't know what would be on the CDsingles, but I'm pretty sure the
2track will feature "You want alchemy", whereas the 4-track will also
feature the "Red Shoes Dancxe mix" and the edited extended version of "Eat
the music". Can't wait!
 
A lot of people probably could care less about Holland, but the story above
is quite amusing, don'tchathink?
