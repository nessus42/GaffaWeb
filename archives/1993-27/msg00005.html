tjc50@juts.ccc.amdahl.com (Terry Carroll) sez:
>So does anyone else have an opinion on Tribe, and whether it would appeal to 
>a Kate Bush fan?

tribe might appeal to a KaTefan...depends if your musical tastes run into
the more rocky vein of tuneage that tribe dwells in. their major label 
debut, _abort_, is a fairly straight-forward rock album with strong female
vocals, percussion that is off the beaten track as far as standard rhythms
are concerned and offbeat song-writing which is both interesting and emotive.
their latest, _sleeper_, is a bit fuzzier in production terms, but still is
in the same general category. they come highly recommended from me, but i
can't say that all KaTe fans will dig them.
