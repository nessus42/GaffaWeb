As midnight brought us into the blessed day of July 30, I was sitting in  
my room with a dear friend consuming the beauty of "Moments of Pleasure."   
We were watching the Aspel&Co. performance provided by another dear  
friend-in-Kate.  I was driven to tears by the emotional content of the  
song and I reflected upon how many times her songs have so moved me in  
past years.  She has given us so much through her music.  I'm proud and  
honored to have Kate in my life.  So many moments of pleasure that I've  
had, came straight from her.

I wish to someday let her know just how much I appreciate her.  I'd also  
like to thank the friends I've made in rec.music.gaffa for sharing these  
moments with me.

Kate Jenkins.
