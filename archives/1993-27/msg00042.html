I have a catalogue of an American music mail order house
that stocks various books / posters / rare music etc.

They have the T shirt with the Aspects of the Sensual World
photo on it.

Here are the details on the T shirt from their
WINTER 91-92 catalogue (sorry I dont have a recent one!)

BTW they have lots of posters also!

-------------------------------------------------------
Kate Bush
XM-879  Face (from Sensual World) and logo
        red/black on white T shirt [L or XL]
-------------------------------------------------------

The code # for the T shirt is XM-879, it was priced
at $12 U.S back then, I have no idea how much more expensive
it is now.

NOTE: This code # MAY have altered as I have an old catalogue
      please quote the catlogue date as Winter 91-92

I strongly advise getting their catalogue - its very good
The only reason I have not purchased something from them
yet is because they insist on Airmail for O.S orders
which makes buying one T shirt from them VERY expensive - 
I estimated about $50 Australian.

Here is their mail order address (send a letter to them
asking for their catalogue to be sent to you)
