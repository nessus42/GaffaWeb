I don't recall anyone mentioning anything about it, but has anyone seen/
heard the Sinead O'Connor/Willie Nelson version of Don't Give Up?  I heard
about it a week or so ago, and caught the last bit of the video on Much-
Music this afternoon.. I have to say, not being a fan of either of the
aformentioned artists, I didn't like it.  There was a decided country twang
to the song which I don't think quite worked because of the delicate female
vocals.  Sinead was fine... it was Willie, I think, that did it.  Also 
shots of Willie looking sensitive/solemn in the video did not help.  
Although I have absolutely no intention of buying it, does anyone know where
this version came from? exactly who released it?

Jenna
