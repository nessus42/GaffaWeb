The new edition of "Q" magazine has a news item about the new single
(out of date info: "Eat the Music" on Aug 16) with an accompanying
colour photo. It looks to me like it's one from an album cover shoot:
full-length shot of KaTe in a black ballerina-style dress and red
ballet shoes, with her hair up, against a grey fabric background.
Oddly, what appears to be two extra arms (dark and muscular) are
visible behind KaTe, like a male dancer about to lift her by the
waist, but ther rest of the dancer doesn't seem to be visible...(must
be a pretty small guy 8-)
