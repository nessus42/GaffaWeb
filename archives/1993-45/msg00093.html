Well, I personally like `CotH' although I must say that it seems to be
a soundtrack more than a song. It feels like something is happening on
the screen during the dialog she doesn't want to distract us from.

Somebody remarked here that CotH seemed to be a follow-up to `The Big
Sky' looking inwards instead of out. I must say that it does make
sense, but upon looking closer, I think it is linked much more to
another song of HoL: `Hounds of Love' itself.

It is this same emotion: it's big, it's love, it's coming after ya and
you really don't want to face it. Keep running from it, keep wanting a
full report, it will hurt but maybe it won't. And you'll have to let
go.
