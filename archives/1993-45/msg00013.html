The Wedding List                 (from The Prince's Trust Rock Gala)

   "     "    "   "    "            Gary Numan best male award.)
Wow                                (Purple dress version)
Them Heavy People                  (Video)

*1   This one isn't in any of the usual collection sources. It's

*2 & *3 Excellent quality versions of these exist, but only in B&W, due
to the difficulty in translating SECAM to PAL or NTSC. A good quality
SECAM copy is at the *top* of our want list. This would be a wonderful
addition to the next tape tree.
