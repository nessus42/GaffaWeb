   As it is a bootleg set of videos, especially in the case of _The
Single File_ (a commercial release) feel totally free to make copies
of this guys tapes. The more copies you make, the better. 

   We will be doing a new tape tree, with the repaired Christmas 
special on it. The copy that we have is a 3/4" digital conversion
from a 1/2" PAL original. We also have the German documentary,
a 2nd generation SNL, and a pretty good copy (the best that 
I've seen in circulation) of the Dutch videos. I'm very interested
if this copy of the Dutch videos are better than the one on the
last tape tree. 

   All of the PAL stuff in our collection is digitally converted. 
If everyone can wait until the next tape tree, you can have this stuff
for the cost of a tape and postage.
