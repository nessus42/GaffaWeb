I don't know if the U.S. lyric sheet is the same as the Canadian, 
but on mine it appears that the only song out of order is Constellation 
of the Heart (if the song order is taken clockwise from the upper left).  
Obviously this is because it wouldn't fit under Moments o'P.  Haven't
considered playing the tracks out of order on side B.  Supposedly the
delay in the release was because Kate wanted to get everything just
right; I assume this would include the order of tracks.

IED is right, there are definitely hidden messages throughout.  (One of
the top 10 Bushy things an album can have!)  I'm still going nuts trying
to pinpoint some of them; also more than a couple musical &/or lyrical
allusions to previous albums (e.g. the noises after "Diving...into 
another moment" on MoP).

Patient listeners will be rewarded.  Infidels, beware!
