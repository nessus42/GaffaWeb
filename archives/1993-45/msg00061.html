From: uli@zoodle.robin.de (Ulrich Grepel)

Subject: German Charts, RbG up, no TRS yet
To: love-hounds@uunet.UU.NET

After two weeks in the Singles chart (#68, #81), Rubberband Girl did it
again, even a higher position than these two:


German top-100 singles charts from November 8, 1993:

...
65:   Kate Bush - Rubberband Girl
...


Probably the album helped the single to raise again. The album itself
has not yet appeared on the charts. This would have been the first
week where it would have been possible for it to appear, since
German charts are counted from Tuesdays 4pm to next Tuesday 4pm and
are published for the Monday AFTER that second Tuesday. There was
a Thursday, a Friday and a Saturday, all three not in every single
shop and not too well known (everyone said Nov. 1st), and a Monday
and a good half of a Tuesday to sell the album.

Next week! (hopefully)

Bye,
