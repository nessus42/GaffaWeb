I really only replied so that I could make some comments about this last
paragraph... let's get a few things straight :-) Prince is one of the most
gifted Musicians currently working today - he has evolved constantly, 
expoloring different aspects of music over the past 15 years - he has been a
major influence on a lot of people - his latest work is not at all 'yukky',
whatever that was intended to mean. The man is a genius.

So, when pairing two gifted people together I was hoping for something more.
I am *dissapointed* with the song - must be Lenny Henry's input :-) The
lyrics sound like something Prince casted off a couple of albums ago - take a
look at 'Diamonds & Pearls' - which was dredged up for use here. I believe that
they never actually met?? I would have to say that this is one of Prince's 
worst outings with female partners... I just don't think that Kate has got 
what Prince needs - also, do you think they slept together? Prince is known
for his *interaction* with his female counterparts...

The songs doesn't work for me.

Thanks for listening,
