Frank San Filippo asks:
>I just noticed in the FAQ a description of Kate singing "Brazil" on the 
>"Brazil" soundtrack-- is this the Terry Gilliam movie of the same name? I know 
>there was no Kate in the movie itself. Someone please e-mail or info about 
>this, I like that song.

   Yep, Kate sings the song _Brazil_. Her version was intended to be
used in the film, but for some unexplained reason, it wasn't included.
   Kate only did the vocal, not the music. The insturmental version
*does* appear in the film. Get the soundtrack if you can find it.
It was a fairly limited release.
