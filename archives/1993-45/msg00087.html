I never said that I was going to boycott the Cocteau Twins album, I
just said that I was going to buy it used. I still love the music,
I just won't be reading any more interviews with them. I can disagree
with an artist's politics, opinions, whatever, without letting it
interfere with my enjoyment of the music. But what's wrong with my
showing my disapproval in any way I see fit? Artists owe me nothing,
but then, I don't owe _them_ anything either.
