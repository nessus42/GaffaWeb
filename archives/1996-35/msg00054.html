1) the woman is hallucinating that she is being dunked, much like a supposed 
witch at a Salem trial -  "Confess to me girl!"

 -or my preferred explanation-

2) The woman is revisiting a <past life> where she died of drowning - in 
this case as an accused witch at a trial.  I sort of linked this song as 
being the first of a trio, with "Watching" representing her <current 
life/age> and "Jig of Life" representing her <future> pleading with her 
present.
