with my wife...

all potential mates...

1] Play some Kate Bush

the wheat from the chaff)

3] Make them sit through Terry Gilliam's utterly brilliant movie, Brazil.

not allergic to my three cats AND they could speak of a computer without
disdain, they were in...

And, my wife was the only one who ever passed all of the tests...
