Subject: Kate Bush meets Trio Bulgarka

The following article appears in this week's NME. I think all those who have
heard any of the Voix Bulgares discs or the Trio Bulgarka will be very
excited about the news that Kate is including Bulgarian voices on the new
album. There's even mention of a tour (well kinda).

******************************************************************************

		Down at the old Bul and Bush

	Len Brown - New Musical Express 12th November 1988
		Copyright IPC Magazines Ltd. 1988

Kate Bush and Yanka Rupkina sit on a bean-bag couch and sing to me,
Bulgarian style; Rupkina bleating high and joyous above Bush's
harmony.

Short but sweet, a brief musical meeting of East and West, it's the
sort of sensual aural treat every philanthropist would wish to share
with the World.  Except that the World would have been pushed to fit
in this studio broom cupboard off Upper Street, Islington.
