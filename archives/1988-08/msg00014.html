Subject: More interesting CD single finds....

i picked up a few more interesting CD singles in the past few days...

a-ha; "Stay on These Roads"; contains 7" of "SoTR", original mix of
"Soft Rains of April" (very good), "Take on Me", and 'Cry Wolf'.
didn't even know a-ha had released any singles off of the new CD.

Thomas Dolby; "Airhead", contains 7" and ep of 'Airhead', 7" of
'Hyperactive' and 'Budapest by blimp'

<say good-bye to capitalization... too much of a pain on this keyboard>

depeche mode; 'blasphemous rumours', contains ep of 'br' w/ a two
minute instrumental addition to the end, also has live recordings of
'somebody', 'two minute warning', ice machine', and 'everything
counts'

peter gabriel, 'sledgehammer', contains a remix of 'sledgehammer' w/a
two minute instrumental intro (incredible!), 'don't break this rhythm'
(excellent track-- mostly instrumental), 'i have the touch-- 1985
remix'

peter gabriel; 'biko', contains a live recording of 'biko', 'no more
apartheid' (instrumental written with Shankar and Little Steven, and
'i have the touch-- 1985 remix'

frankie goes to hollywood; 'highlights from warriors of the
wasteland'; contains one track.  It is a 21 minute remix of 'warriors
of the wasteland'

PIL; 'this is not a love song'; contains the original and remix of the
title song and two other tracks.

cure; "hot hot hot!!!'; contains extended remix and remix of title
song and 'hey you!!!' extended remix

metallica; 'creeping death/jump in the fire'; contains both EP's...
not a very good recording, but good material

metallica; 'harvester of sorrow'; contains the CD version of the title
track plus two covers; 'breadfan' and 'the prince'

if you want more info, mail me...
