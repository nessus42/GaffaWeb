Who was it who suggested (in this newsgroup) that one could have an,
er, "interesting" experience by listening to Kate Bush while allowing
white chocolate to dissolve slowly in one's mouth?

I finally tried it, albeit watered-down (Nestle's Alpine White
adulterated with almonds and _The Whole Story_ CD)...

...it IS awfully pleasant...

not hallucinatory, or anything, but still VERY nice...

Now, if you'll all excuse me, I'm going to go do it again. 8-)
