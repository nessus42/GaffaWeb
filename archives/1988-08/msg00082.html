Since I continue to have problems with having enough free time to
moderate Love-Hounds properly, in the near future I am probably going
to be returning Love-Hounds to autopilot.  This means that there may
be some duplicate postings in Love-Hounds Digests.  This is the reason
Love-Hounds was taken off of autopilot in the first place -- new
NetNews software caused old NetNews software to send multiple copies
of articles posted.  This problem has become less severe as time has
passed (though, it continues to be a problem), so it is now feasible
to put Love-Hounds back on autopilot.  I have also been reformatting
articles to look pretty.  With Love-Hounds on auto-pilot, you will
have to cope on your own with bozos who write their article on one
3,678 character-long line.  But at least there will be some traffic on
the mailing list, rather than just long pauses....

Your humble pseudo-moderator,
