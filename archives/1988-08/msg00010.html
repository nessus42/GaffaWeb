Before EMI's monumental blunder gets too much more repetition, let me
point out that the correct title is "Ne t'enfuis pas", with "enfuis"
one word.  The way it's written on the label of some versions of the
record is probably a confusion with "Ne t'en fais pas", which means
"Don't worry about it".
