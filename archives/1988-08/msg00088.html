I finally saw 'She's Having a Baby' last night (on video).  While it's
not a bad movie, I didn't really get into it until the end (of course)
when the flash backs occur while Kate is singing 'This Woman's Work'.
Beautiful moment and song.  During the rolling of the credits, there
are various celebrities and ordinary street people suggesting names
for a boy. Is Kate one of those people? I think she shows up right
after one of John Candy's many appearances and says the name
'Mesamurch' or something similiar, I'm not sure what she really says.
