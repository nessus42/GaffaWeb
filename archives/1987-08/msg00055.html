Love-Hounds news:
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
The first KompilaTion anthology tape of Love-Hounds'
preferred listening is underway, and will make its second stop with
contributor Mark Kat(e)souros. All L-Hs interested in adding
ten to twelve minutes or so of their recommended music, just
post their mailing address either to L-Hs or to Mark. Those
wanting more than the suggested time allotment might consider
contributing a second cassette to the pool. There are absolutely
no restrictions on the type or timeliness of the information
you put on the tape(s), of course -- anything goes, so throw down (!)
whatever you want. You can even put Kate Bush on it, but it
seems sort of superfluous -- even IED isn't doing that.
Look for it in your town soon!

-- Andrew
