Of interest to Peter Gabriel and KT fans, this 25 min 16 sec, CD contains:

1 Big Time (extended version)
2 Curtains
3 No Self Control
4 Across The River
5 Big Time (seven inch version)

where
1,2,5 p 1987
3 p 1980
4 p 1982

I believe that 2 is a new song?
Of course, this CD should have been titled "No Self Control".
The sound quality on 3 is wonderful, especially during KT's KEY contribution (which is not credited).
There is a slight but noticeable amount of hiss during the first few seconds.
