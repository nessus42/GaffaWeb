 Someone asked for a listing of the tracks on the new SMITHS album.
 Well, having picked up that particular piece of vinyl today, named
 _Louder than Bombs_, here goes:
 
 side 1
    is it really so strange?
    sheila take a bow
    shoplifters of the world unite
    sweet and tender hooligan
    half a person
    london
 
 side 2
    panic
    girl afraid
    shakespeare's sister
    william, it was really nothing
    you just haven't earned it yet, baby
    heaven knows i'm miserable now
 
 side 3
   ask
   golden lights
   oscillate wildly
   these things take time
   rubber ring
   back to the old house
 
 side 4
   hand in glove
   stretch out and wait
   please please please let me get what i want
   this night has opened my eyes
   unloveable
   asleep
 
 You may say, "hmmm I've heard some of these titles before".  Well
 that's because this two album set consists of lots of 12" singles
 packaged together with a few (I was told three, but don't bet the
 farm) new songs.  I haven't even ripped off the cellophane yet,
 so I can't comment on the contents...
