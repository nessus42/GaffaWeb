Gee.....I haven't seen much on the Usenet side of Love-Hounds of late.
Either someone cut or feed or stuff isn't getting thru or the list
itself is dead (hope the latter is not the case---my like wouldn't be
complete if I couldn't read IED messages and Wic flames!).

I was ecstatic today when I found a CD copy of Mike Oldfield's
Incantations!  I walked into my favorite import store and there it was!
Let's hear it for Virgin records who not only finally got this record
out on disc, but put the entire two album set onto ONE DISC!  My player
says "69 minutes".  I have been afraid that they would wimp out and put
it on two discs even though it could easily fit on one.  But they
didn't and I am now the proud owner of an Incantations CD (I'm
listening to it now---yow!).  So when's he coming out with a new one
(now that I've finally caught up)?
