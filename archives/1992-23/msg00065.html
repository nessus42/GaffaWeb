I was listening a couple of days ago to 98.9 KDBK San Francisco
(simulcast on 99.1 San Jose), and I heard "Running Up That Hill".
Also, a day later I heard "Solisbury Hill" by Peter Gabriel.  This
station recently switched formats.  Right now it seems to be a combination
of "Modern Rock" and "Album Rock", with some heavy metal as well.  Really
strange!  But they did play Kate.

Interestingly, many formats ago this same frequency was KKCY ("The City"),
and this was the first station I ever heard Kate on, back in 1987.  They
had an eclectic, free-form format back then.
