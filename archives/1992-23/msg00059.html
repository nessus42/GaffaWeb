Ed asked about which Jane S. to buy.  I would highly recommend
starting with her most recent, "Bound By the Beauty".  It is
the most accessable of her work as far as I can tell.  (I recently
acquired all her stuff, though I haven't yet found time to play
"No Borders Here".)  Her earlier stuff is just harder to listen
to, and I haven't really decided if I like it or not.  I was
fortunate to find her three middle releases on inexpensive vinyl
($6 ea), otherwise I'd probably be upset for spending so much on
something I had mixed feelings about ;-)

I have a great tape of a show broadcast on Canadian radio, which is
actually what I've listened the most.  It features her more recent
stuff, too.
