     Hi there!

     Information on Kate laserdiscs: "The Whole Story" is available on
     laserdisc, and it can be ordered from major laserdisc suppliers such
     as Sight & Sound in Waltham, MA, or Ken Crane's Laserdisc in
     California.

     In addition, "Live At The Hammersmith Odeon" is available as a
     Japanese import.  Expensive, but available.  I assume it's the
     one-hour version.

     "The Sensual World" (the videos) is also available as a 7-inch
     CD-video.  It's on sale in the discount bins of places like Tower
     Records for $10 to $15.
