Hi !!!

I just came back from London two hours ago and immediately got reading this
newsgrouop... Again I found some really great Kate stuff... Here are some
good record shops to look for...

Hanway Street (behind Virgin Megastore, 14 Oxford St.): On The Beat, Vinyl
Experience, Soho Records and former Rocks Off (don't remember the new
name).

Berwick Street (Soho): Reckless Records

Portobello Road (take Notting Hill gate tube): Intoxica, Plastic Passion
(actually on Blenheim Crescent)

That's not all but it's a good start !!!

Also buy the latest issue of Record Collector to check if there are record
fairs during your stay in London...

Last time I was in London I also took a trip to Welling and Plumstead to
see where East Wickham farm is and it was a great experience to walk on
Kate's home landscape. Take BR train from London Bridge or Charing Cross.
