Hello, Love-Hounds!

All this impassioned debate about whether Kate left a mistake in the 
newer version of "Wuthering Heights" has led me to wonder if there 
somehow mightn't be two different versions of the re-make.  People keep 
saying that her voice cracks or that the note is flat, but I just don't 
hear it - and I'm not without some knowledge of music. In fact, it has 
crossed my mind before that the same point in the original version might 
be a tad less than perfect (but not too much).  Yes, I know it's 
sacrilege; but, as has been pointed out, that word "Ca-thy" as sung in 
the original version *is* different from all the other recordings.  The 
question of interest for me is why did Kate choose to redo the vocals on WH?
