A week last Wednesday I owned not a single piece of music by Kate other
than a copy (should I be telling people that? :) of "The Kick Inside"
which an uncle of all people handed to me during the summer.

Now I've bought 5 of her albums and the "On Stage" live EP.

I'm not too sure what to think of it all.
Have I gone mad, obsessed, or have I just 'seen the light' ? :)

One question; why is there "Do" and then a little picture of a pair of
spectacles written on the run-off of the "Never for Ever" (vinyl) album
I've got?


There's really no need to say I haven't stopped listening to the albums
since I got them.?..... :)
