Hello Love-hounds,

KaTe's imagery is so vivid in some songs, and yet I find the image I have is
a little different than intended.

When I first heard "Hello Earth" I picked up on the hand held up high, but
not knowing the lyrics real well gave me a slightly different image. The
image I have is of KT standing on the moon, slight breeze pushing her hair
back (I know, there is no wind on the moon), she holds her hand up to block
the earth and moves her hand to the right saying peek-a-boo, then peeks
around to the left of her hand and again "peek-a-boo". Playing this "childs
game" with the earth is so lovely and profound that it has never left me.
Every now and then I will just think of the image during the day when I'm
not even listneing to the song. In other words, it just pops into my head
for no apparent reason.  I LOVE THIS IMAGE and have had it since the album
was first released. I know she is not standing on the moon, but the image is
so vivid and I treasure it so much that this "mistaken" image will not leave
me, nor do I want it to.

Have always wanted to share that. Thanks. The only friend I had that loves
KT died over a year ago. So until I joined this list I thought "I was the
only one." ;-) My current partner is slowly coming around. :-)

Bye for now.
Wayne
