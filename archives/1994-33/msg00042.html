I know nobody asked specifically, but here's some more lists:

FAVE KATE VIDEOS
 1) The Sensual World ==> Gorgeous Kate, gorgeous stage decoration,
gorgeous photography.  i think it's gorgeous.
 2) Sat in Your Lap ==> As tongue-in-cheek as I think she'll ever get. A
lot of fun and she actually looks like she's enjoying herself.
 3) Cloudbusting ==> Donald Sutherland, Kate in a fright wig, a great
song. How could it miss?
 4) This Woman's Work ==> It shouldn't work because one false move
could've screwed it, but Kate and Tim mcInnerny does a great job.
 5) Suspended in Gaffa ==> Superb one-woman show.

LEAST FAVE KATE VIDEOS
 1) Wow (Whole Story version) ==> Pointless, what other reason do I
need.
 2) Rubberband Girl (both versions) ==> The one looks like every other
rock video out there and the clips from TLTCTC made me dread seeing it.
The other one is fine except I hate the guy running after the wind
machine at the end... but that's just my view.
 3) The Man with the Child in His Eyes => I just don't like the
expression on her face.
 4) I like all of the other videos.

Not that you asked again, but my not-Kate Favourites are: Squeeze, Tori
Amos, Elvis Costello, Adrian Belew, David Bowie, Concrete Blonde, Belly,
The Housmartins/Beautiful South, Mussorgsky and any Russian composer.

Who is Happy Rhodes and how can I find out more about her?
