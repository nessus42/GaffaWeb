For those of you without usenet, don't have rec.music.tori-amos, or have a
slow news feed, here's the Press Release...


> Following is a copy of the press statement released by Atlantic Records
> today, which explains the condition which affected Tori during her show
> last night in Madison, WI:
>
> FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
> OCTOBER 5, 1994
>
>
> Atlantic recording artist Tori Amos left the stage last night during her
> performance at the Civic Center in Madison, WI.  She was taken to a local
> hospital, where a doctor diagnosed her condition as costrochondritis, an
> acute inflammation of the chest wall muscles and cartilage.  This
> condition affected her breathing, and caused chest pain for the singer.
>
> Doctors have given Ms. Amos a clean bill of health, and her current
> nationwide, headlining tour continues as scheduled, including tonight's
> show at the Riverside Theatre in Milwaukee, WI.  Touring in support of
> "UNDER THE PINK," her second RIAA gold solo album, Ms. Amos concludes her
> U.S. itinerary on October 25th, after which she will play dates in Canada,
> Japan, and Australia.
>
> Atlantic Records, 1994.
