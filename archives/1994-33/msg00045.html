Good evening.

Regarding the question someone asked about the attraction inherent in "Moments 
of Pleasure", I wanted to offer this:

MoP offers genuine emotion in a musical setting that is intelligently presented;
senitmental without being maudlin; meaningful while still being obscure.  It is 
an excellent example of Kate exploring her emotions musically without giving too
much of herself away.  

There are too few musicians that can approach this level of artistry.

Kate 100%,
