So, did anyone catch "Record Breakers" on BBC1 on Friday? I clean
forgot to set my VCR 8-(. According to the newspaper listing the
subject was Larry Adler - wouldn't be surprised if the sole
Kate-content was a clip from the "The Man I Love" video.

Ben Miller has already posted about KBC update 7, but just to
recap....

- And So is Love released at "end of October" (presumably the 31st?)
  in UK and Europe.

- UK formats are:

  - 7" (ASil/RbG(US)) pic disc in 12" sleeve with 60x40" poster.  5000 only.

  - MC, tracks as 7".

  - CD (ASiL/RbG(US)/EtM("US 12" mix")).

  - Limited ed. (5000) CD as above with "3 exclusive postcards".

- European formats undecided.

- TLTCTC video released in UK and Europe today (10th). US/Can on or
  around 18th.

moment). Interesting guy. Worth it just to hear "Watford Gap" 8-)
Check out http://www.helsinki.fi/~akoskine/royharper.html on the Web
for the tour dates.
