> Bands:
> 1. Cocteau Twins

Yup!!  IED again was responsible for my first CT listen.  _Treasure_ had
just been released and he played me the first song ("Ivo") and I said
that I didn't need to hear anymore, that I wanted every single song
they'd ever recorded up to that point.

> 2. The Cranes (Does anyone have any info on them? A brother & sister
> mystery act...The second album is brilliant.)

Both albums are, but yeah, I like the second one best too.  I don't know
much about them.  I saw the band in concert last year (it was great!) and
I waited outside for the longest time to meet Alison, but she had left
immediately after the concert.  A bunch of us stood around talking to the
rest of the band, and no one bothered to tell us that she had left.  On
the one hand, it was true that most people were waiting to meet her and
probably would have left if told, but on the other, the band *knew* we
were waiting and they knew she was gone, and I think they should have
said something.
