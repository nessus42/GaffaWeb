   Peter Manchester, the coordinator of the Tape Tree is buried in work at
the moment and probably cannot organize another round of tape distribution.
If any of the leaf nodes from the distribution wish to produce another
round, they are welcome to do so, but I'm not in the position to promise
that anyone will. The did quite a lot of work last time, all well
beyond the call of duty.

   So, if another "official" dubbing is unlikely, remember: this
collection is copylefted. ie: possessing a copy obligates one to make
futher copies. If you got a good quality copy you have a *holy
obligation* to make copies for others. 

   Additional dub sites are encouraged. S-VHS masters can be provided
to those willing to make at least six additional copies.
