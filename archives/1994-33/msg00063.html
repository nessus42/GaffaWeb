For those of you who are interested in the issue of copyright laws and 
how they apply to the internet, there's an interesting article in the 
Wall Street Journal, 10/10/94, p. B6 on this topic.

Their conclusions are 1) it's a mess, 2) no-one knows what part of what 
laws apply, for instance it could be interpreted that copyrighted lyrics 
are "on display" since they are not being sold, 3) no-one knows what to 
do or how the heck to do it.  The Internet is too big and complex to be 
monitored for breaches of copyright.
