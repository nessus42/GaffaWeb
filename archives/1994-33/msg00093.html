I had athought about these two topics.

We'd all like to see    TLTC in more general availability (and at a cheaper
price); equally too we'd like to have a complete set of the fruitopia commercials
and the longer "ad campaign overview theme music". I'd even like to have the
commercials in the format with the visual kalidescope images before the text
and voice overs were added.

So, how about writing Coke to sponsor a version of TLTC with the fruitopia
commercials (and related) on the front. He're an example of a film where we'd
actually *want* the commercials on the front. They could always distribute
the film via coupons inside six-packs of Coke if they wanted a closer tie.
The typical pricing for this type of promotion would be about $6.99/tape....perhaps
less with 'proof of purchase' stuff....

Any takers?
