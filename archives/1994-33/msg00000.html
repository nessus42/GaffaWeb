Dear LoveHounds,

	I really am getting sick of the flaming of Tori that goes on here at
the moment. As some of you will be aware, I have actually interviewed the lady
for the esteemed radio station listed below right, and found her to be wholly
genuine, utterly personable and individually talented, and not the cynical
rip-off merchant some people here seem to see her as.
	If anyone wishes to flame me (though I can't imagine why anyone would
do so to someone who is only expressing his own opinion!!), please do it via
private E-mail.
	Similarly, if anyone would like a copy of the transcript of my 
interview, drop me a line, but please be patient since it seems to have
vanished from my E-mail account. It'll be sent quite soon.

Cheers for now,

Steve
