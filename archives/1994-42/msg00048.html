I must be in the minority of one - I prefer "Lionheart" to all the other
albums, probably all the other albums put together. With the exception of
"Don't put your foot on the heartbrake", the songs are all non-noisy (cf her
later efforts). I suppose that I prefer the under-produced, simpler style of 
Kate to her more universally popular efforts. I still buy the new albums
when they come out, I just don't listen to them...

Sad to think that Lionheart is 15 years old, makes me feel old...
