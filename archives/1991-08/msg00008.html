I agree, STP is not the genesis/phil collins fluff that is currently all the
top40 rage.  It strikes me that the ep may mark the transition point from
the Steve Hackett days to the post-Hackett days.  The liner notes do not
say so, but I bet Inside And Out is a Hackett cut, whereas the other two
cuts represent some of the first Genesis stuff without Hackett.  They certainly
lack in the guitar department.  The "Out" part of Inside and Out is great!
