Julianne Dunphy asks if 'Morning Fog' invokes magical feelings when one
listens to it.

To this question, I must voice a resounding YES!  The Venerable One's music
always has such an affect on me.  But, The Ninth Wave is the work that has
THE most affect on me, with 'Waking the Witch' and 'The Morning Fog'.  ON
the conversation about 'Hounds of Love', that speaks to me like no other
song does, except for 'Love and Anger', and the aforementioned songs.


Take away the love and the anger...
