Subject: Spot The Pigeon

The EP was released in July 1977,and reached 14 in the UK charts.The 3 tracks
Match Of The Day,Pigeons and Inside & Out were outakes from 'Wind and Wuthering'
and were effectively Steve Hacketts last contribution to Genesis.The first 2
tracks are quite humourous,but nowhere near as funny as 'Harold The Barrel',
'Battle Of Epping Forest','Willow Farm' or 'Counting Out Time'.These 2 tracks
lie inbetween there best(ie Gabriel) stuff and recent stuff(ie Collins crap,he
should stick to just singing and drumming!).I would suggest buying it just for
the final track,which is worth the money.It is very much in the mould of a
true Genesis track.
