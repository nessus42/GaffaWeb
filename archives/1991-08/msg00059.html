Like I said in the title, I just HAVE to post this.
I played "This Woman's Work vol. 1 & 2" for a friend last weekend, and then 
at school this morning, he said that he just HAD to get everything she's 
ever recorded!  He said that over the weekend, everything he listened to was 
pale in comparison to Kate!  I don't think I've ever seen anyone become that 
strongly attracted to an artist in that short a time with just a sampling of 
stuff.
