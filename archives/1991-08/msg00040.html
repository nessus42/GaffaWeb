From: **ANDY** <sre017@cck.cov.ac.uk>

Subject: Comic Relief Red Nose Day 3 'The Stonker'

For all you interested 'Don't Give Up' was played in the back-ground when Tony
Robinson (Baldrick) did a report on all the troubles in Malawi for the latest
Comic Relief Day last Friday.Also later on in the night(about 1 in the morning)
they showed a compilation of comedians doing impressions of musicians.Included
was the 'Not The Nine O'Clock News' pisstake of Kate by Pamela Stephenson called
'Oh England My Leotard' set to the tune of 'Them Heavy People'.I think this was
first  shown in 1980,and is the only decent & funny impression of Kate ever done
"I like trucking,I like trucking and if you don't like trucking tough luck!!"
