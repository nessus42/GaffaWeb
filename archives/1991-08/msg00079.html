"Breaking No New Ground" is great..."Double Bummer" is even better...
Unfortunately, all the record stores around here are poorly stocked, and I 
was thinking of ordering the new bongwater stuff through Tower's 1-800 number
thingo.
Were my eyes deceiving me, or did I see bongwater on TWO compilation tribute 
albums?  Specifically, "The Bridge" (for Neil Young) and "Where the Pyramid 
Meets the Eye" (for Roky Erickson).
Another GREAT band on Shimmy Disc is King Missile...I got their "Mystical 
$hit / Fluting on the Hump" CD at Tower for $14.95!  That's a great buy for 
60+ minutes of hilariously funny music...
I suppose the 'band' Dogbowl was formed by Dogbowl himself, formerly of King 
Missile fame?  I need to check that out...
Many thanks for the person who posted the bongwater stuff...
