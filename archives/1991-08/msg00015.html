I think a letter writing campaign to advocate Happy as an opening 
act for Kate is a bad idea.

First of all, this will be an experience of a life time and I think it
should be a purely "Kate" experience.  I don't want it diluted with
_any_ opening act.

Second, Kate has a lot to do right now.   I think the idea of sending
her mail containing anything but pure joy at the idea of another tour
is a bad idea.  Touring has been too much to ask for a long time and
now you want more?

Please do something different if you want to advocate Happy.  If you
want to start a mail campaign to Kate it should be one of pure
encouragement.   It was suggested that more details on a tour might be
available early this year.  We've heard nothing official yet.  Encourage
Kate, don't distract her.
