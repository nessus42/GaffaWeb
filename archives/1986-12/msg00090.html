The Knights Templar exist today.  It is one of the many organizations
within Masonry.  There are two paths that a Freemason can take on his
way to becoming a Shriner and one is through the Scottish Rite (becoming
a member of a Commandry of Knights Templar), while the other is through
the York Rite, of which I know little.  I know this stuff because my
father belonged.  All sort of hokey but the Knights Templar have spiffy
outfits with swords and such.
