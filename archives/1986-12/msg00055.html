Subject: **The morning after**

Well, I awoke this morning in the back of my bus soon to realize that I
think I had fun at Doug's Katemas party last night.  Anyway, I would
like to thank Doug for hosting the thing and it was real neat meeting other
Love-Hounds (I think).  Since I was apparently decorticated for the
later part of the party, I am ashamed to say that I really have litle
recolection of the affair, and would like to apologize if I offended
anyone in my stupor.  I was correct, however, in my prediction that my
wife would enjoy the party, and I'm sure she'll remember her Dougelenna
bit  a long while.  As for the others of you whom I met, It was great
and we should do it again (next Katemas?).

At any rate, I finally got to hear the Net-Music tape (Greg, if you are
reading this please take the hint).  This is a must buy product.  As for the
Love-Hounds in the DC area, I am now more convinced than ever that we should
meet sometime during the APA convention.  Maybe I'll even be able to remember
the thing afterward. [Since half of the Navy scientists live in the DC area
we should hgave no trouble in providing transportation to Arlington].  Lets
try to do it.

Thanks again, Doug.
Jon
