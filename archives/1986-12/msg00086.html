Kate Bush's band back when she played in pubs and stuff, before she
had a record out, was called the "KT Bush Band".  Kate has no middle
name; KT means "Katie".  Hidden somewhere on every KB album is a KT
logo.  The logo originally came from a midieval organization called
the Knights Templar.  This logo can be found in Churches throughout
all of England (look Ma! religious symbolism!).

Yes, that was *the* Danielle Dax who played the wolfgirl in *The
Company of Wolves*.  You even get to see her naked, so run out and see
the flick!  (I bet Jon Rossi is wondering why Kate didn't play the
part....)
