Are you telling us that Cloudbusting or Big Stripey Lie can't be done live? 
You
gotta be kidding me!    ANY (sorry for yelling) song can be played live.  If
the
band is good enough and artistic enough, they can pull it off.  If you feel
that
Cloudbusting must be played in the same way as it is on the CD,  then go listen
to the CD.  What the point of going to see anm artist live if:
     1. The artist plays the songs exactly as they appear on disc?
     2. You expect anf want the artist to play it as they appear on disc?
*none* 

BTW, have you heard any bootlegs of Kate doing Egypt in 1979?   It's quite
different
than the version put on NfE and neither of the version have to do justice to
the
other.  They are different songs...yet they're the same.   Beautiful. 

This in incredible.   I don't understand why soooo many people can't or won't
see 
the beauty of a true live performance.  Perhaps a different arrangement of 
Cloudbusting would be most refreshing (piano solo performace, maybe?).  Big
Stripey Lie can certainly be played live without any trouble at all.    

Open your mind....open your heart....let the music in....let the weirdness in.

peace
