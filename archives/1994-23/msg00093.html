FRUITOPIA COMMENTS:

BTW, I find it pretty ironic that Kate did the music for the the
Fruitopia ads -- I've been seeing them on a regular basis on
television and in one particular movie theater here in Boca Raton, FL,
and I've told people repeatedly that I thought the music was "neat."
They're generally just a nice little rhythm loop -- with some chants
and yelps and such -- set to a somewhat irritating and
headache-producing kaleidosocpe pattern and particularly trite
"peace and love" slogans.

If this is what Gen X is supposed to be about, then I must be a very
mature (or immature...) 27 year old.  Ah, well.
