The 1994 tapetree is fantastic!  It's gotten me thinking about what I'd like
to see for the next tapetree (I hope there will be one).

My wish list includes:

The english special on the tour, which includes rehearsal footage.

The parodies of _Wuthering Heights_ and _Wow_ by Faith Brown.

The Japanese shows from 1978(?) with _Them Heavy People_ and the Beatle
covers.

_Wow_ on the Abba special.

The German tour documentary.

_Suspended in Gaffa_ on French tv.

The first _Wuthering Heights_ video in the red dress.



Anybody else have other ideas?

Lori K.
