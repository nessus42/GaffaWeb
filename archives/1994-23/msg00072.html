Hello,

Here is a small sample I made from an interview on a
picture disk with Kate. I use it on my Amiga 1200 as
sample for the sound-preferences. The sample is
uuencoded (original file length: 17244 bytes). It
is sampled at 19886 Hz.

Kate says: 'Uhum, execuse me'. That's exactly right
for this purpose, I think. Isn't it great to have
Kate asking for my attention when something important
happens on my system!

BTW, I also converted the huge 'Another Day' sample
to an 8-bits Amiga sample. It's a very nice song indeed.
If anyone out there likes to know how to do it, let
me know...

Greetings, Raymond Hoving.
