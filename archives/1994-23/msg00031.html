All you love-hounders who don't like Tori Amos discussion in this group,
consider looking at the CFV for a Tori Amos newsgroup. It can be found
in news.announce.newgroups, news.groups, alt.music.alternative, a.m.a.female,
rec.music.misc and the really-deep-thoughts mailing list.

All you r.m.gaffans who do like Tori Amos discussion: see above.

All you love-hounds who do not have access to usenet groups: mail me and
I'll send you a full copy of the CFV.

All who do just want to know how to vote: send email before August 25 to
voting@qualcomm.comm containing one of the lines:

      I vote YES on rec.music.tori-amos
      I vote NO on rec.music.tori-amos

Just one more bit of info: there's no gateway planned between r.m.t-a and rdt.
