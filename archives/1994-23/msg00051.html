Greetings Kate Fans!
I am so happy to have found this group. Someone just lent me his entire 
Bush collection on CD and i am in HEAVEN!!!
Anyways...i have a question...

While i was listening to her Red Shoes album on the song "Lily" (which of 
course i heard about ten times in the last 24 hours if not more) she does 
a magickal ritual called the LBRP or Lower Banishing RItual of the 
Pentagram. It is one of the most beautiful rituals that was ever made and 
a very good focusing ritual, and i have never heard any other person do 
that ritual on a CD before. Now as i listen to some of the rest of her 
stuff, i am noticing more and more and more magickal themes to them and a 
lot of metaphysical hints to some of her songs, and some that are quite 
magickal. Does anyone have any idea if Bush is currently doing any 
magickal workings? and if not, then why did she have those lyrics in her 
song?

PS: If anyone would like the LBRP i am willing to forward it via email.
