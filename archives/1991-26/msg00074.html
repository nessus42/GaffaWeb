According to a major record chain store in the UK,Kate's album is due this
October.This was in the Our Price store in Leamington Spa,where forthcoming
releases were listed on a poster.I enquired where they got the info, they said
they had just been told that Stevie Nicks (Greatest Hits),Annie Lennox and
Kate Bush were due to release albums around October time.
I would caution people not to read too much into this as release dates for
Kate's albums always have a habit of changing by as much as a year.Though let's
keep our fingers crossed.
