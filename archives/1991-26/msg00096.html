When I was in Olsson's buying _Fuaim_ I saw a copy of _Atlantic Realms_, but

been domestic.  I've seen _Magical Ring_ as a domestic, but that was most of

>The earlier stuff is comming out on Shanachie
>Records so far I've seen: In Concert, Fuaim, Clannad 2, Crann Ull and Dulaman 

This was what made me buy _Fuaim_.  I was out on the Great Residents Search
trying to find _Freak Show_ and either _The Speckless Sky_ or _The Walking_.
Olsson's had none of the three, but they had, much to my surprise, _Fuaim_.
The last time I saw _Fuaim_ at Olsson's was last August, and it was $24.
On Saturday I paid $15.  A pity that the album's only ~35 minutes.

I have this memory of also seeing a Clannad album dated 1990, as well as a 
CD single from the same time.  Anybody know anything about it?  This memory
could well be a figment; it's been known to happen.
