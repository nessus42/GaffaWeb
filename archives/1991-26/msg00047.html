It's not bad. It's basically a biography covering KaTe's life from
childhood to sometime in 1988 (when it was published) in varying depth.
For the most part, it's reasonably accurate and honest, although the
authors say some slightly strange things at some points. You don't get
many pages per dollar but there are plenty of photos, both studio and
candids including quite a few full-page colour ones (my favorite's the
one of KaTe in a woolly cardigan holding a tea-mug and looking as if
she has a mouthful of tea... it's the look in her eyes I think). It
covers the making of the Experiment IV video in some detail (lots of
colur pics of KaTe in the Sound Demon costume, for some reason).
There's a file in the L-H archive (called 'visual-doc-errors' or
something) written by IED listing all the thing the authors got wrong.
