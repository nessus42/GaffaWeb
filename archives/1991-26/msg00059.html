The new issue of the newsletter "Little Light" arrived today.  They've
improved the layout a bit and this issue looks a lot nicer.   One
interesting tidbit is this:

  "Kate has changed her plans on the upcoming album.  She is now
   saying that there will be a single by year's end.  This is changed
   from having an entire album this fall.  Here we go again!"

They don't site a source for this but it sure sounds likely.  We are
not seeing any of the signs of an imminent album release that I seem 
to recall from the summer prior to _The Sensual World_ release.  I'll 
have to check the archives to see just when these signs started showing 
up in earnest.

Love-Hounds is credited for a number of bits of information and listed
in the "Them Other Heavy People" section.  Ron Hill's objection to
their treatment of "Homeground" is printed in the letters section.
