my favorite Clannad (pron. KLAHN-ned, give or take) albums are:

Clannad in Concert -- stunning live irish 'trad', mostly sung in Gaeilge.
Magical Ring -- in between their trad and rock phases.  features the
		gorgeous "Harry's Game'.
Fuaim -- Eithne/Enya makes her presence felt.  mellow, a little 'jazzy'

all of the old ones (Clannad, Clannad 2, Crann Ull, and Dulaman) are
now available on CD from Shanachie Records -- they are all worth owning,
IMHO.  _Macalla_ (with a brief appearance from long-time fan Bono)
and _Sirius_ are well crafted but kinda dull.  _Legend_ is the sound-
track from the UK "Robin Hood" tv series -- as William Stoner says, 
they are rarely found here in the states.

some (rough) translations:

Clannad = clan, family 
Fuaim = sound
Crann Ull = apple tree
Macalla = echo
Dulaman = my dictionary says "channelled wrack"?!  the album cover
suggests a meaning of "stuff left at low tide", but i'm guessing...
