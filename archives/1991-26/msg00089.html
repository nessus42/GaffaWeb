Our source for obscure video in Columbus is Aardvark Video.  They've
been looking for a copy of the _The Single File_ laser disc for us for
a while now so a week or so ago they called and said that the VHS
video had been rereleased in Canada and wondered if we wanted a copy.
We were hoping for some confirmation from him that this was a legit
release and not just another sighting of the "Cello Video" bootleg 
before raising the hopes of Love-Hounds nationwide.  Hopefully this
"Pulse" item is verification.  I don't know why our source says
its from Canada, though.  The video is supposedly backordered right 
now.

This raises my hopes that _The Single File_ may soon be released on
Laser Disc in the US.
