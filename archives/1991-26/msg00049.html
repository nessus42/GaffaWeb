   It's true, IED didn't include the line "It's cold out here"
in his transcription of the lyrics of "GOoMH".  He had been alerted
to that before, but he just plain forgot to check it out. Now he
has, and sure enough, it's there. Thanks, Chris'n'Vickie, for
another neat new moment to put...

here! The addition goes into the next generation of "The Garden".

   A word on the two "biographies" of Kate Bush that
were mentioned in a recent enquiry by an Australian Love-Hound.
"Kate Bush: A Visual Documentary" is more recent than "The Secret
History of Kate Bush", not vice versa. "Visual Doc." is really
the best of all the unofficial Kate Bush publications, and is
more worth the $30A than any other Kate Bush book (except for
John Carder Bush's "Cathy"). "Secret History" is a silly piece
of nonsense purporting to trace Kate's ancestry, and IED does
not recommend it, except to KompletisT colleKTors.
   Happy Katemas to all!

-- Andrew Marvick (IED)
