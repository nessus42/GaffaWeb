Well I have the album Duluman which is all traditional/folky stuff
and it is wonderful...if you are into Celtic music you will enjoy
immensely.  It is mostly Maire (?? can't remember if that is her
name or not) singing ballads, but there are some instrumentals only
and there are of course some male dominated songs.  All in all I
think it's a fantastic album and worth a listen.  I don't know 
what it is like in relation to the other albums by Clannad, but
as I said if you are really into the traditional music sung in
Gaelic and English than you will enjoy.
