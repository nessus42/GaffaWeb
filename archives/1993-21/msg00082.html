THIS MAY BE A BIT LATE BUT I WANTED TO SAY THAT I TOTALLY DISAGREE
WITH MEREDITH,IN REFERENCE TO SARAH BRIGHTMAN.

SARAH MAY NOT HAVE THE BEST SINGING VOICE IN THE BUSINESS BUT HAVING
SEEN HER LIVE IN PHANTOM I THINK THAT SHE IS VERY TALENTED.

SHE GOT MARRIED TO LLOYD- WEBBER DURING PHANTOM BUT I'M SURE RECORD
COMPANIES WERE APPROACHING HER BEFORE SHE WAS ROMANTICALLY LINKED
WITH LLOYD-WEBBER.

MEREDITH LEAVE HER ALONE SHE HAS MORE TALENT IN HER LITTLE FINGER
THAN YOU COULD POSSIBLY HAVE.

WOLFMAN
