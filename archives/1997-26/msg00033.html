For everyone who bought the new commerative re-issue of The Hounds Of
Love, if you look closely at the pictures you'll notice that the cover
shot of KaTe with Bonnie and Clyde is not the same pose as the shot in

entirely different position.

By-the-bye, I'm glad I spent the bucks.  And this reissue is the first
time I enjoyed Big Sky. The EMI Manhattan version was too muddied.
