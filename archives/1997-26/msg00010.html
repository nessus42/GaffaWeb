I have been shocked and saddened by the death of Diana like so many
people. I am trying to think which Kate Bush song best sums up either
the spirit of Diana, or how I felt about her death. I keep coming back
to Dali on the bootlegs. I think it's the piano, which to me sounds so
melancholy yet hopeful at the same time. It's such a sad, sad melondy
and song. I would be interested to hear which KB song is appropriate for
Diana in the eyes of other gaffanatics.
