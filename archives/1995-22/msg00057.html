>1. best overall video

>2. best video "early years" (1978-1980)
Babooshka

>3. best video "glory years" (1981-1987)
Cloudbusting

>4. best video "quiet years" (1988-1994)
The Sensual World

>5. best hairdo
Suspended in Gaffa

>6. best costume
Babooshka

>7. best dance
Hammer Horror

>8. best special effects
Breathing

>9. worst video
"Moments of Pleasure" (clip from LCC-- how many times can 
one woman turn in a circle?)

>10. song you wished there was a video for
Night of the Swallow; Get Out of My House

>11. best head apparel (hat)
Army Dreamers

>12. favorite "guest" artist
Tim McInnery (This Woman's Work)

here for all that weird blurry stuff (I'm sure it has a name).  Very
distracting.

And how about "Best Appearence by Kate in Someone Else's
Video?"  Both versions of "Don't Give Up" would win hands-
down in my book.
