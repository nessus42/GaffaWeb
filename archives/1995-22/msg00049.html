Hi There,
A couple of years ago (3 to be exact:-) ) I bought "Hounds of Love" and 
discovered the B-side (if you can talk about B-sides of a CD!) that was 
supposed to be a soundtrack to a movie (?) or at least an extended 
musicvideo called "THe Ninth Wave" but I think Kate ever did this.

Since I had worked a little with moviemaking (mostly concerning sound) I 
became quite interested in this. 

I started to write a screenplay based on the soundtrack - just for fun at 
first, like a test of my screenwriting abilities - but as time passed I 
became more and more "hooked" to it. I just couldn't quit writing it, I 
had to finish it. And so I did - 18 months later (!).

I send it to Kate thruogh her friend Lisa at the Kate Bush Fanclub so I 
lnow that Kate received it. But after that....

Nothing.

So instead of gathering dust on my harddisk (!) I thought of sharing the 
screenplay with you guys. If, of course, you're interested. 

Maybe I could upload it to a web site? Does anyone have the email adress 
for the webmaster at "Experiment IV"?

Get in touch!
