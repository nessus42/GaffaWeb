        Cloudbusting Edition 2 is now finished.  Unfortunately my car blew 
up the other day so I don't have the money or transporation to mail it out 
to the people who are going to post it to the archives for me.  Hopefully I 
will be able to do so next monday.  I'll post a complete description after 
it is posted, as well as instructions on how non-FTPers can get it. 

        I had my first Kate Busy related dream last night.  Was it a dream 
of interviewing Kate about the songs she's never talked about?  Was it a 
romantic dream of me and her doing the dance from "Running Up That Hill"?  
No.  It was a dream about DEL PALMER!!  No Kate in it at all.  This sucks.  
In the dream a bunch of fans were at a convention and Del mentioned he was 
thinking of doing a Kate Bush Book.  I suggested that he do a book of 
reprints from the Kate Bush Club newsletter.  He mumbled something positive 
about that.  

        On a "Deeper Understanding" note, I saw "Lawnmower Man" the other 
day.  The first two thirds is pretty interesting, but it degenerates into a 
Standard Steven King Thriller by the end.  Unfortunately it doesn't show 
much knowledge about computers.
