Kate's performance at the Hammersmith (as well as all of the other
performances in this tour) were absolutely, positively done live --
not lip-synched!  On her E.P. there was definitely some touch-up of
the recording in the studio and probably some in the concert video
too.  Most of the instruments were played live in the concerts, but
there is definitely some stuff on tape (whale singing, for instance,
and the vocals for "Hammer Horror").
