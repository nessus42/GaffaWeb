> Just picked up Tori Amos' _Little_Earthquakes_ this Saturday.  I really
> do like it.  She is brutally honest, much like Kate's first two albums.

We got _Little Earthquakes_ a couple of weeks ago.  I 
immediately liked it.  There aren't many CDs that I want to
listen to repeatedly, but this is one!  I really like the simple 
arrangements on most of the songs.  Tori's lyrics are a nice 
contrast to Kate's softer style.  I agree that Kate's work is 
brutally honest, but as Kate has said, it has a definite
"English vibe".  Tori's songs are gritty and honest in an
American way.  This is not necessarily better, just perhaps
easier for me to relate to.  At the moment _Winter_ and
_Crucify_ are my favorite songs.  Interesting cover art too...

Dare I say it?  This CD is magic for me.  :-)
