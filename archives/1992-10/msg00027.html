Just to let everyone know (if you don't already) that Rocket Man is doing
really well on the Australian charts. It has been in the national charts for
8 weeks now and the last five weeks it was placed as follows
		5 weeks ago	-	No. 28
		4     "    	-	No. 26
		3     "		-	No. ?
		2     "		-	No. 12
		This week	-	No. 5  *****!!!!!****** Wow

I believe that this is one of her best Australian chart perfomances for
a Long Long Time, I could be wrong but I think the her only better charting
song would have been Wuthering Heights.  It's a pity that Rocket Man was 
written by someone else but I think that it is Kate's enormous talent that
has helped it chart so well.  I cant wait to see what position it is at next 
week, it would be great if it made it to No. 1!!!!
