Hairy frogs from Neptune forced woiccare@CLUTX.CLARKSON.EDU (Woj) to type:

>Oops, one more thing. Does anyone know anything about the bassist on 
>"the Big Sky" named Youth. I think I've heard of him, but can't place
>the name... 

As |>oug noted, Youth was in Killing Joke; he was their original bassist,
and left after their third album, I believe.  In any case, he definitely
left before they got really boring.  After that he formed a hard-funk 
band called Brilliant, of whom I've hear very little.  Love-hounds
might be interested in the instrumental album that Youth recorded with
Ben Watkins (of Everything But the Girl) a few years ago; it appeared
under their two names, I don't recall the album title (but there aren't
that many records in the Y section of your local used record stores, 
are there?)
