From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)

Subject: TSW featured yesterday on KITS


Yes indeed, as promised KITS did feature _The Sensual World_, along
with two other albums, on yesterday's "New Music Wednesday".  As I said
in an earlier posting, this month KITS is doing a sort of Best of
New Music Wednesday, featuring what they consider to be the
best releases of the last few months, as a guide for Christmas shopping.

They played "The Fog", "Deeper Understanding", "This Woman's Work",
and "Love And Anger" once each; "Heads We're Dancing" was played 
twice.
