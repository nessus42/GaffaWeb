I bought a CD at the weekend called "East Wind" by Andy Irvine & Davy
Spillane, containing Macedonian & Romanian music. Track 6 of said CD,
"Antice", contained a melody that sounded very familiar.... it is 90% the same
as the Uillean pipe melody on "The Sensual World", which is incidentally
played by .... Davy Spillane.

For info: Antice is a traditional melody, the CD was released in 1992 (3 years
after the release of TSW)

Nik
