I case no one else mentions this...

Wednesday night, during the late-night wrapup of the Olympics on CBS
(in the US), about 30 seconds of Rubberband Girl was played while
they showed part of the women's freestyle skiing event.  An inspired
choice, considering how the freestylers' legs rubberband up and down
at a furious rate to absorb the bumps.
