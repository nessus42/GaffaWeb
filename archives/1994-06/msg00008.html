The real version:

Scene:  Feudal Japan.
 
A young acolyte approaches the local Zen Master for the day's lesson.

"Master, what is the greatest acheivement in human history?  What is the
pinnacle of human creation?"
 
The Zen Master smiled serenely, and said, "That would be 'The Dreaming',
by Kate Bush."

The young acolyte then proceded to smack the Zen Master in the head with a
2 by 4.

When the Zen Master woke up, the young acolyte was holding a copy of 'Never
For Ever'.

The young acolyte smiled. "Thus endeth the lesson", he quipped.
