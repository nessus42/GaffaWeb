	Moving -- A collection of poems
		(Japanese title: KEITO BUSSHU SISHUU  MOVING)
	Shinko Music Publishing
	ISBN4-401-61329-5
	2,600 yen
	issued 1993.3.16

 o A5 size(not sure), 256 pages 
 o Lyrics of 79 songs of Kate Bush with side by side
	Jananese translations by Anri Kitazawa
 o album discograpy up to TWW box, quite simple
 o two page essay by the translator
 o Cover with Kate's photo 
 o some photos inside but none of them feature Kate

with no new information.
