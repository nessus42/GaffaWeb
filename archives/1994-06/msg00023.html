Simply put, TD is pure adrenaline Kate, going straight for the jugular.
Yes, her voice is purer on NFE.  But on TD we also get a "textured" sound
with Kate, thanks to the Fairlight.  This combination yields energetic 
explosive Kate.  Where NFE simply floats you away on clouds, LH leaves you
wanting more, TKI seems unpolished, HOL takes you on a roller coaster, and
TSW leaves you ??? TD blasts in to you with the raw power of Kate.  And yes,
there are songs (on TD) which do demonstrate her vocal qualities well.
