In Australia, there was a special limited edition of 'Bloodletting' which had
four additional live recordings:  'God is a bullet', 'The Sky is a Poisonous
Garden', 'Roses Grow' and 'Tomorrow, Wendy'.  I don't know if this the same
recordings from the above mentioned 'Caroline' single.  I don't think this
edition is available any more.  Anyway, the performances are great, especially
TSIAPG.

CB are the third artist I have heard that performs 'Little Wing'.  The others
are Sting (a very good version, I like it!) and, of course Jimmy!!
