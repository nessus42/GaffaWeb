The Cowboy Junkies "Trinity Sessions" was an all digital production
and I'd bet they didn't have a lot of $$ at that stage of their
career.

The liner notes say
	"A Digital R-Dat Recording using only a Calrec Ambisonuc Microphone"
and later
	"An MDI Production. Edited and Mastered Digitally"

The sound quality is superb to my ears, but I can't claim that they
are calibrated....
