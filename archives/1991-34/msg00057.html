Yowch!  Danielle Dax is wonderful.  "Dark Adapted Eye" is a
US compilation of her "Inky Bloaters" album and various 
singles.  Personally, I don't think there's a bad song in
the batch.  "Cat House" is a hysterically funny spoof on sex
kitten dance rock songs, and naturally, it became a dance hit.
"Bad Ms. M" a wonderful stab at Maggy Thatcher, and "Big Hollow
Man" a great satire on TV evangelists... with a beat!

I would think that folks who like Kate Bush for her wide musical
range and diverse lyrics would love Dax... especially those who
wish Ms. Bush would crank out a dance tune from time to time.

I saw Dax perform in Boston a couple of years ago, and she really
puts on a show.
