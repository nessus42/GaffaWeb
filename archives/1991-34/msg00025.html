No KaTe content this time, but I'm pretty sure there are a lot of other
folks out there that also enjoy k.d.lang, so I thought I'd mention that
there is a new laserdisc collection of her videos just out.  Stumbled
across it by accident the other night, and bought it immediately.  It's
great!

About 60 minutes of material.   20 songs, including both early rare stuff
and more recent works.  Plus her short but delightful acceptance speech
at the Canadian equivalent of the Grammies.  k.d. introduces all the songs
with her quirky, dry wit and always-up-front attitude.

Songs include "Pine & Stew", "Hanky Panky", "Don't Be a Lemming Polka",
"Turn Me Round", "Crying", "Trail of Broken Hearts", and "Pullin' Back
the Reins".  Really wonderful stuff.

Disc is titled "k.d.lang Harvest of Seven Years (Cropped and Chronicled)".
It's on Warner Reprise Video label.
