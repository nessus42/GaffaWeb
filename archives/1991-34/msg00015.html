With all this talk about "The Big Sky" (The KaTe song, the John
Wayne film, and the Montana nickname), I was futily trying to come
up with a Kate Bush-John Wayne connection (the best I could do was
"James and the Cold Gun").  But then I started thinking of one of
my favorite directors, Stanley Kubrick, and saw amazing parallels
there:

KaTe Song                   KuBrick Film
---------                   ------------
Army Dreamers               Paths of Glory
Infant Kiss                 Lolita
Breathing                   Dr. Strangelove
the entire Ninth Wave       2001 - A Space Odissey
Mother Stands for Comfort   A Clockwork Orange
Lionheart                   Barry Lyndon
Get Out of My House         The Shining
Pull Out the Pin            Full Metal Jacket

I think I may be on to something here...
