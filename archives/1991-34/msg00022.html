You listed "Live at Hammersmith", "The Whole Story", and "The 
Sensual World".  There is one other collection of videos:
"The Single File".  The videos on this collection that are not
on any of the above three are:

"Them Heavy People"
"Hammer Horror"
"Wow" (the original video, different from the one on "The Whole Story")
"Suspended in Gaffa"
"There Goes A Tenner"

Until recently if you wanted this collection you had to get the Japanese
import.  (Of course, the British import uses the wrong TV standard.)
This made it very expensive.  But just a few months ago it became 
available in Canada, and a few stores in the US (actually, only Tower
Records that I know of) are importing them from there.
