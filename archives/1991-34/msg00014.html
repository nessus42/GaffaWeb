Just a few more comments on Concrete Blonde:

I looked at the _Echo Park_ soundtrack this morning and discovered 2 things:

1) It was realeased in 1986 (CB's 1st album came out in '86).

2) Johnette recorded the song "Tomorrow's Gonna Be" with a group of musicians
who also were the backing band for some of the other singers on the album. This
implies a group of studio musicians and would explain why the song was not
done as Concrete Blonde.

I also just recalled that 2 CB songs appeared in the movie _Texas Chainsaw
Massacre 2_. The songs were "Your Haunted Head" and "Over Your Shoulder" 
(somewhat appropriate). Again, if anyone is interested, I have a couple
of live tapes and an interview that I would be willing to trade.

ObKT: I put in order for This Woman's Work. The guy said the English version 
was pretty hard to get now and that I would probably get the Japanese version 
for $140.
