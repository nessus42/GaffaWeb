Funny, a couple of months ago I was toying with the idea of sending an
April-fools item to gaffa that would be a news flash announcing Kate's
decision to make an album with niggers-with-attitude, along with a
forged statement from Kate saying that she was tired of all this
pretentious nonsense about self respect, creating music you believed

everywhere at once (or at least not for long) when your audience is
spread around the round globe.

Still, Kate working with Prince - time will tell.
