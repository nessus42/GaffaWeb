Kate & Miller's Outpost

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward J. Suranyi)
Date: Sun, 5 May 91 10:21:21 PDT
Subject: Kate & Miller's Outpost


Yesterday I went to Miller's Outpost to buy a pair of jeans.  They
were playing typical "Modern Rock" music from the loudspeakers,
like A Flock Of Seagulls and Elvis Costello.  At first I thought
it was Live-105, because it was so similar to the stuff they play,
but there were no commercials.

I was just leaving the store with my purchase when I thought I heard
something familiar, so I stepped back inside.  Sure enough, it was
"Love And Anger"!

I guess I was kind of staring into the distance listening, because
a clerk came up to me and said, "May I help you?  You look lost."

"No, I was just listening to the music.  It's really rare to hear
Kate Bush in a store!"

"We have the music on a tape," she said.

"Well, it's pretty good!"

"I'll tell my boss you liked it."
