Subject: KaTe in the UK

Many thanks to Scott Telford for passing on the news that "GLC" was being shown
again this weekend. In hopes that it would be broadcast in stereo I had my 
video setup ready and waiting, but it was not to be. :-(

Has there been any further progress on a UK Love-Hounds' KaTemas celebration?
For all their reputation of being so laid back, the Californians seem to have
got their act together pretty well already. :-) 

I didn't see any mention in the recent list of KaTe 'zines of "Pull of the
Bush". This was being sold to the queue outside the Hammersmith KonvenTion and 
I thought it was quite a good effort. So far as I know there have as yet been 
only two issues, and I was wondering how it was thought to rate compared to 
other 'zines people have seen. Any opinions out there?
