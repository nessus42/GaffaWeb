I think the idea of a printed love-hounds digest is good, but I think that
perhaps we have a larger quantity of interesting stuff then people here
suspect. I think that perhaps a good idea would be to compile a monthly "best
of" file containing perhaps a "news" page and then a couple of pages of the
most interesting messages.  One person could compile this, then perhaps
another could send it (or just the first page) to various places.  Then the
rest of us could print it out for any non-access people that we might know.  I
could also perhaps be destributed through The Big Sky or other services.  It
wouldn't have to included interviews (as those will, er... soon will, be
widely available through Cloudbusting / The Garden).  It could include things
like JCB's poetry, Kate's horiscope, and other Kate-related articles that are
not speficially interviews.  Its hard to believe that in a three months period
we wouldn't have nearly as much interesting stuff as appears in the fan mags. 
Remeber people pay big money to read this stuff, so its hard to imagine there
wouldn't be any interest. I would offer to make the file, as I make separate
"interesting" messages files anyway, but I am busy with a few other projects.
