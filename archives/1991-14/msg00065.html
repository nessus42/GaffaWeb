Since _The Sensual World_ was on Columbia, one would expect
it to have been available through the CBS club, and it was.
For a long time (about a year), it was listed in the bigger
ads such as appear in _Rolling Stone_ in the "Cutting Edge"
section.  In the newsletters, they were also offering _The
Whole Story_ and _Hounds Of Love_ for a while.  I don't
know if they still are.  I doubt it.

Back in 1985 I remember that _Hounds Of Love_ was offered
by a record club, but I don't remember which one.
