FLYING
------
        I think people have got the impression that I am carried on to a plane
screaming, at the moment, and have to be knoocked out so that I can cope with
the journey.  I don't like flying, but it's really not that bad, and I do fly
if I have to.  If there is another way of travelling, I quite often prefer to
go that way; but if you have to fly and it's the quickest way to get
somewhere, then I do. 

This is the only quote I currently have in on the subject, but there are
others, including a quote, (I think) in the raw footage of the Nightflight
interview (does anybody have this whole interview transcribed).  
 

And now a poetry break: 

He's only a heartbeat away, 
Remember today, to pray, pray 

Ron "My heart went a quiver" Hill
