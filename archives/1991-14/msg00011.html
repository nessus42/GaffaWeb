Well, the subject pretty much says it... heard on local radio 'modern rock
news' spot yesterday that Prince has lined up several directors already for
his next few videos, and among the names mentioned was... Kate Bush.
<jaw drops to floor with resounding ka-thunk!>

I only just discovered this newsgroup about 3 days ago, and have already
read all ~300 messages that our server still had around, and ordered two
of the boot CD's from Tom at C-Side.  What a wonderful treat to find so many
other KT lovers.  Bless you all.

(Sorry if the Prince video bit has been announced already - I really did read
each and every one of those messages, but after that many, my mind was reeling
more than a little... I don't recall anyone else mentioning it.)
