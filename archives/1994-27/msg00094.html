I'm sitting at work at 1:45am in the middle of a beautiful Labor Day
weekend, and I thought I'd take a small break and listen to some music.
So, I pop in TWW 2 and start with the live version of Them Heavy People.
Kate's voice is really beautiful live, and I once again ponder whether
Kate will ever tour again ( and maybe in the States this time ).  I realize
it's a rare chance, but I figure I'll do my best and write a letter to Kate
and beg her.  Does anyone know an address at Columbia-USA where I may
be able to write her?

-- Stephen
