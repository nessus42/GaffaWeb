BreakThrough was a short-lived but glorious Kate fanzine that was
published out of Winnipeg, Canada from 1983-1985(?).  It's crowning
achievment (besides letting a lot of American and Canadian Kate fans
know that they weren't alone out there) was the infamous "Bush-Con 84"
which was the very first Kate Bush fan convention in North America.

It's always nice to see old familiar names pop up!
