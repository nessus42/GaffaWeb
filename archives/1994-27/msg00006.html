Sarah McLachlan (sp?) was in concert in Salt Lake a couple of weeks ago
and did a little interview on the sponsoring station.  They asked her
about her career and she said that in her earlier days she was often
compared to Kate Bush.  At that time she didn't mind, but now she
doesn't like the comparison.  She doesn't like Kate's last couple of
albums and thinks that Kate has lost something she used to have.

I also noticed in my local cd exchange a box with Kate in some green
leotard on the cover, called something like Kate the Lionhearted.  Of
course I had to see what was in the box:  A picture book full of Kate
glossies and a Lionheart cd.  Cost was $55 US.  What's up with that?
