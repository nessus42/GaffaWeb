     On first listen, that first syllable of "Cathy" is off.  I thought it
was off too, but when you keep listening to it it's right.  It seems to be
an octave above the original version's.  Actually, she nailed it.  It's
so high compared to the previous and following syllables that it sounds off.
     It took a listen or two to get used to, but I do like it a lot better
than the original.  It sounds more like the Kate I'm used to.  I don't think
she would have let anything that bad pass by, especially something that she
should be able to notice more than most people.
