 Paul Boutin writes:

> 	"Once more into the breach, my friends!  Once more!"
>					- _Henry V_

A near unique example of a correct attribution, coupled
to a stunning misquotation. 

I don't know what to say! I really want to encourage the few people who give 
the authors their due. And, if we have the attribution, we can look up the 
quotation, and find out how wide of the mark it is. 

But there must be a limit!

The correct quotation is, of course:

"Once more unto the breach, dear friends, once more; ..."
