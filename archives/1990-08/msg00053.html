They are there, you just have to know where to look.  It's probably
easiest if you live near a major population center like Los Angeles or
New York or Boston.  Even out here in nowheresville the
import/specialty shop gets them, but that's because most of the people
that run the record stores are kate fanatics.  Main Street Records

I called them this morning to find out about the Love and Anger single
but they only have it on 7" right now.  I expect the CD to appear this
week.
