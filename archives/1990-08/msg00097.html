Subject: Another compilation

There is a new compilation album/CD out called the Brits Awards 90 or
something similar. It features The Sensual World by Kate Bush, as well
as tracks from most of the other nominees for Brits awards this year.

There was a trailer for Les Dogs last night. One saw Kate, (looking quite
beautiful) dressed in full white bridal gowns being kissed by Danny Peacock,
(at his most obnoxious) as a foul, disheveled, dirty groom. How she has to
suffer for art! The rest of the wedding party were arguing amongst themselves.
No words from Kate in the clip - full show tonight.
