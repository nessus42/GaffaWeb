Hi,
  I've been a long time reader of lovehound, but I have to admit, I rarely
post so if I screw up, I apologize.  On with the posting.
  The song that I first heard by Kate was _Running Up That Hill_, but the song
that really got me hooked on Kate would have to be _The Man With the Child In
His Eyes_.  When I first heard it, I melted.  I was more than overjoyed when
I got _The Kick Inside_.  I consider this to be one of her best album (only
beaten by _The Dreaming_ of course).
  Well, I guess I threw in my two cents worth.  Good luck with the poll.
