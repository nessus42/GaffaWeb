We've basically had three suggestions:
	1.	It goes with _Love_And_Anger_.
	2.	It goes with _The_Fog_.
	3.	It goes with neither.

May I suggest the other possibility:
	4.	It goes with both.

Reasonable reasons have been given for the laugh to belong to either track;
perhaps the laugh is a deliberate link between one song and the other.  KT
may have put some thought into ordering the tracks, rather than arranging
them randomly.

Now we have conclusive proof that the laugh is not *part of* _L&A_.  But it
is now also clear that KT does believe that the laugh *goes with* _L&A_ (it
does now, at any rate).  We have no proof as to whether or not it's got
anything to do with _The_Fog_.

Jon D's claim that he has now been proved right, and that the laugh does go
with _The_Fog_ is patently absurd.  [I've just added this paragraph so I can
get flamed.]

Steve.
