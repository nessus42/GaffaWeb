Kate on MuchMusic

On Wednesday, June 13 MuchMusic will be airing a one hour Kate Bush
feature.  MuchMusic ("The Nation's Music Station") is Canada's
equivalent to MTV, only MUCH better.  The show will be on at 9 P.M.
Mountain time.  (You can figure it out for your time zone).

The special will include an interview with Kate, live clips,
and "never before seen in North America" videos (A bit of _Army
Dreamers_ is shown in the promo).  Most avid Kate fans have probably
already seen the live and video clips.  The interview was conducted
recently in England, by Laurie Brown.  Most (or all) of the interview
has already been previewed on the weekly Canadian music show, _The
New Music_.  Laurie tends to be a reasonably well-informed and well-
researched interviewer, so she doesn't ask "the usual stuff".  She
asks Kate some questions about her recording technique, her voice,
touring and stage performance, dance, video, family, sound, and
Kate's new studio.

In Canada, MuchMusic is offered as a pay TV service.  Those of you
in the States should be able to pick it up if you have a satellite
dish, or access to one.  I don't think that it is scrambled to the
satellite.  Who knows, after you have compared MuchMusic to
Empty-V, you want to watch it instead!
