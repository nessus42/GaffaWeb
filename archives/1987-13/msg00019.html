Subject: The KompilaTion

     In hopes of seeing the KompilaTion tapes circulating as quickly as
possible, I have enlisted the aid of Mr. Mavrick and the map of the US
found in the front of my local phone book to break the distribution list
into several smaller groups - each of which should fill one C-90.  Each
sub-group is at most 7 names long - which works out to the earlier stated
average of 12 minutes per person.  I have grouped the names as best I
can to minimize the distance travelled from contributor to contributor.
     Once a tape is finished, the last person on the list should e-mail
me a note, and I'll reply with the name of someone on the "Appreciation"
list (contributors plus interested listeners) to send the tape to.  Hopefully
every tape will make it to everyone whose address I have (those sent to
IED through June 2nd).
