track "All Fall Down", on Ultravox's latest LP, U-VOX. It's probably
not at all co-incidental that Midge Ure is a huge and longtime
fan of Kate's, and that he named Hounds of Love the best album of
1985.

Finally, to Jamie: IED believes it's "Ms." Sakamoto, no? Thomas
Dolby's reference is to Akiko Yano, who sang back-up for him on
"Radio Silence", and who is married to Riuichi Sakamoto.
