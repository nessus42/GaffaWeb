According to the Tower throwaway "Pulse", Amnesty International is planning
on releasing a double album and book of the Secret Policeman's Ball and
a film to be released this fall "to be culled from live performances
by Kate Bush..."

Looks like Kate won't be the only pop star to have recorded with a 
traditional Irish band. Pulse also says that Mick Jaggar recorded
some tracks with members of The Chieftains. Perhaps Irish music
is the next "fad" (gag)...
