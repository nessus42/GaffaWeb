card. Please keep the signatures coming in! A. Marvick,

Henry Chai asked about Break-Through. IED is afraid that
he has no new word on the progress (or lack of same) with
what would be issue number 12. Robyn is all on her own now,
and at last word was planning to move out of Winnipeg. Her
own funds are far smaller than formerly...

IED apologizes for his error re Kate's awareness of Beatles.
She did say that she had only become aware of how incredible
they were "fairly recently," but this was obviously not a
very clear remark. IED would otherwise have been as sure as
any of you that Sgt. Pepper, Revolver and Abbey Road were huge
influences on Kate's music, and he is happy to see that that
may well in fact be true.

-- Andrew
