Last night's Grammy telecast was the worst piece of television I
have EVER seen. Shandling was the worst host in history. No timing
at all. There were more technical screw-ups than a video from your
local garage band, the musical numbers SUCKED, ESPECIALLY PG's. Even
Cirque De Soleil couldn't save that turkey. Whoever was responsible
for directing or writing the material should be shot many times,
and be left alive for public stoning. Only k.d. Lang's performance
was on par, and yes, I'm biased because she's Canadian. She deserved
that Grammy tho. I'm not complaining about who won(much), but the
show itself was pathetic.

Did anyone else feel this way?

I was especially miffed about KaTe not getting a lifetime achievement
award.It's about time she got one. :-)
