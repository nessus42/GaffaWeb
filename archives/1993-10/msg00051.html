yup. i'm a bit fonder of the eponymous release, but then i've listened
to that one a heckuva lot more than _rockabye_ (which came out just a

i've seen her perform once about two years ago and it was a great show.
bill frisell played guitar and wayne horvitz (her hubby) was in the band
as well that evening. see 'em if you can.

incidentially, you can also hear her play on the two new york composers
orchestra albums. pretty good stuff if you like that kind of avant jazz
classicism.
