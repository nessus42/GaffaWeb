   The phrase 'Rosabelle, believe' was the code phrase that 
Houdini worked out with his wife to send to her from the 
afterlife. Though Houdini's wife tried a myriad of mediums, 
none had the correct message. Well, apparently one did, but I do
believe it was shown that that medium and Mrs. Houdini had either
planned it out before, or she had accidentally informed him 
of the code phrase. 
   Rosabelle, of course, was just his wife's nickname, if I 
remember correctly.
