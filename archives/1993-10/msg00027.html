breasts slipmats"?  i was JOKING, really.  but i was in BPM this

out of a corset.  god knows why you'd want something like that on your
turntable...

>Know anything about the group Orbital? (not The Orb, as far as I can tell)

i know everything about orbital.  i shook their hands, felt their bald
heads and gave them copies of my single.  nice guys but their music is
a tad monotonous.  (they probably say the same about me.)

my favorite orbital tracks:

the moebius - plinky percussion and star trek: tng samples.
steel cube idolatry - lovely sampled female vocal wailing
halcyon - samples Opus III "it's a fine day" and actually does
          something COOL with it.  i'd guess this is the one that has
	  piqued your interest since it's the current single.

my housemate swears by "belfast" but i think it's too sappy.

the orb is dr alex paterson and thrash
orbital is paul hartnoll and phil hartnoll
