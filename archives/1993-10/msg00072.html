Graham Dombkins asked:
 
> Sorry for the non-KaTe question BUT does anybody know if 'Hourglass' can be
> found anywhere else but this video? I've had the vid for a while but I'd like
> to have this song on CD.
 
Yes, 'Hourglass' was the title track of a CD-single which was released in the
UK a few months after 'PastPresent' came out. I grabbed a copy at the time. I
seriously doubt it is still available, but it didn't chart so there may be
copies still lurking somewhere (though probably not in Australia).
 
The cover art of the single was a rather beautiful photo of Maire, so I chose
this as my item to get autographed when she played a very low key charity
concert right here in Cambridge about 18 months ago.
 
Geoff Parks
 
PS The new Clannad album is slated for an April release in the UK.
