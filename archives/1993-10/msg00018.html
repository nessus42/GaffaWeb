I don't know if anyone has answered this yet but here it goes:
  The music in Patriot Games and the car commercial was written and 
performed by an Irish group called Clannad. It was originally done under 
the title The Theme from Harry's Game. I believe this was a UK (or Irish) 
TV show pertaining to the IRA. The song is available on several CD's. 
1. Magical Ring by Clannad 1982 (European release only I think)
2. Anam by Clannad 1992 (US release only --- Anam 1989 UK doesn't have 
it)
3. the soundtrack to Patriot Games should also have it.

couple of Clannad albums from the 70's.
