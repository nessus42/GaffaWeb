My favourite listen right now is a CBC (Canadian Broadcasting Corporation)
program called Roots & Wings broadcast Sunday evenings at seven. If all
goes well I get to wash the dishes undisturbed for the whole hour.

R&W plays what is called "world music". Now I know Vartina isn't Swedish
but I heard them played here.

Peripheral Kate content: R&W recently played music from a collaboration
between Mystere de Voix Bulgaire and prominent African musicians.

They love feedback and they can be reached at their web page on the CBC
site.

http://www.radio.cbc.ca/radio/programs/performance/roots/roots.html

Perhaps if they knew more particulars about Eva Dahlgren - even if I knew
more - she might get some play over here!
