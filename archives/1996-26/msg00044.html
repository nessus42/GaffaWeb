How I began to like the music of Kate B.
----------------------------------------

Before nearly eight years I heard KaTe the first time in "Don't give up"
with Peter Gabriel.Her extraordinary voice made me curious.
A few weeks later I heard her in "Games without frontiers" and it 
happened that I read something about her in a magazine.
This made more curious.A little later her album "The sensual world"
came out and I bought it.The albums HoL,The whole Story,N4E,TKI,TD,
Lionheart and TRS followed in periods.
It was a bit difficult to get into that (her) kind of music-the first time
I heard TSW was quite frustrating :-).
Her songs are full of -sometimes quite crazy- ideas and the repertoire
of music instruments is widespread (Bouzouki,Digeridoo,..). 
Maybe that's the reason why I like her music.


So that's all folks.
