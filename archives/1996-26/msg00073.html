I too have this poster on my wall -- double matted, with 
plexiglass on the back (so you can see the large 'Maid Marion' 
shot on the reverse) and with a wooden frame, no less! It was 
about $200 to do, and I've heard a Kate fan or two tell me they 
thought the shot was "unflattering" but I obviously disagree.

I think her facial expression in this shot is so soothing. She 
looks as if she's been through some sort of emotional upheaval, 
but is still somehow offering solace -- to me! The warm glow of 
light emanates from about where her heart is. Kate as E.T.? All 
in all, a message of hope, despite what the song ASIL says.
