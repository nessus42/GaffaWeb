my first kate exposure was seeing the video for "suspended in gaffa" on mtv
back in 1982.  i didn't like it.

later, i heard "running up that hill" on the radio and thought it was cool.
then i saw a bunch o' videos on V66 (anybody from boston remember that?),
one of which was "the big sky."  i had to go buy hounds of love
immediately.  i didn't like it.  except for RutH and TBS.

then i started reading love-hounds (received at my university's computer as
"never for ever"!!) and i wondered what all these people were talking about,
re the 9th wave.  it never made any sense to me (although i liked playing
waking the witch on my radio show to freak out the audience).  so i went
back and started investigating and the rest is history.

here endeth the lesson.
