Okay, I suppose it would be silly to ask all of you when/how you
first heard Kate and then not to answer the question myself.

I first heard Kate from a friend in Vancouver in 1981.  The song that got
me was Babooshka.  I found the first album shortly thereafter in Montreal
but after buying Lionheart I was disappointed and didn't get another album 
until 1987 when I bought The Dreaming in London, England.  Then I was in love!
I then completed my collection and went back to Lionheart where I saw it 
entirely differently.  The Dreaming, however, is my favorite.

Andrea.
