_The Dreaming_ as a first KaTe album??  I wouldn't suggest it.  I bought _The
Dreaming_ first, on a suggestion from a friend, and almost got turned off to
KaTe by it.  It's a little deep, and a little heavy, to listen to as a
newcomer.  I got to like it, but only after buying _Hounds of Love_ and falling
for it immediately.

I would suggest that a KaTefan wannabe either buy _Hounds of Love_ first, or
buy the albums in chronological order.
