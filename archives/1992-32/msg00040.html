I thought that The Innocence Mission album UMBRELLA was fantastic.  It
is more like The Sundays or Cocteau Twins (Treasure) than Kate or Tori.
The lead singer sounds like Natalie Merchant on some of the songs,
drawing comparisons to 10,000 Maniacs.

It is worth a few listens to give this album a chance to grow on you.
I think that anyone who likes The Sundays will really like this album.
