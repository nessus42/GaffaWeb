> The Wishing Chair is kind of folksy/indie, In My Tribe is poppy but

Start with The Wishing Chair or In My Tribe or Hope Chest or
(if you can get it) Secrets of the I Ching (is that the right
title?).  All of these are incredibly excellent play again and
again and again items that everyone ought to have.
