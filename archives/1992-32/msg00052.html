Andrea> Gosh (!) it's been quite lately! .... What was the first KaTe
Andrea> song everybody heard an how/where did they hear it?

Long long ago, in a land far far away...

I must have heard Wuthering Heights when it first came out. However,
the first KaTe song I remember hearing was Them Heavy People, I saw it
on Top Of The Pops. 

The first record was the single with Lord Of The Reedy River as the
B-side (sorry I forget the A-side). The first album was Lionheart: the
cover captivated me but the contents were, as a whole, disappointing.

The divine dementation started with Hounds Of Love but The Dreaming is my
favourite album (Suspended In Gaffa, favourite song).

I was very dissapointed when I found out that John Peel (BBC Radio 1's
leading edge music DJ) didn't like KaTe...
