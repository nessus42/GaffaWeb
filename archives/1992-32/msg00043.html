I am not kyrlidis@athena.mit.edu (Angelos Kyrlidis).  I didn't say:
>Jon wants to remix happy! Is that a compliment or what? ;)

maybe.  maybe not...

so far in my professional career i've had to do two songs (three mixes) that
i found unbelievably distasteful.  ever heard of rozalla?  she's a terrible
lightweight pop dance music songstress (her big hit was "everybody's free to
feel good") and we had to churn out two mixes of "are you ready to fly" in a
few days.  churn was definitely the operative word here, cos that's what my
bowels were doing.  the record company sent us a tape of her vocal track
(and nothing else) with a sheet explaining that it would allow for "maximum
creativity" (read: "we're too cheap to give you the backing tracks.")  yeah
and monkeys might fly outta my butt.  but i digress...

so we sweated and slaved and they didn't even wind up using them.  we did
put one of them out on twitch, but it wasn't quite the same thrill.

on the other hand, epic is putting out a promo 12" of the new prefab sprout
track with our remix on it, and i'm getting a full credit despite the fact
that all i contributed was about 10 seconds worth of hi hat programming.
