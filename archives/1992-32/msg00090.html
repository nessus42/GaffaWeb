	Now you have. I heard the song _The Dreaming_ played on one of our local
alternative stations (Well, it wasn't called alternative then however). I've
always been interested in the folklore of the Dreaming and such so I bought the
tape when I came across it browsing in the store one day. I enjoyed it a lot
and began actively seeking her earlier work out after that.
	To be fair though this was awfully close to the time _Hounds of Love_
began to hit stateside, but it was _The Dreaming_ that got me hooked.
