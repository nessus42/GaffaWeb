     I think you'll find that the majority of suggestions for your first
     Kate album will be "The Dreaming" and "Hounds Of Love."  I would
     suggest "Hounds" first, as it's my favourite Kate album and one of
     my all-time albums period, ranking right up there with Peter
     Gabriel's third and fourth albums.  (She also makes heavy use of the
     famous Gabriel "gaseous cloud effect" on "Hounds.")  I was never the
     same after listening to "Hounds," and I found Kate's voice more
     appealing on that album than I did on her others - even on "The
     Dreaming."  It takes a little time to get used to Kate's screech-owl
     vocals, which are used in abundance in her earlier work.

     And anyways, I'm a sucker for drums - which are used aplenty in
     "Hounds."  That plus those stunning, spine-chilling strings, which
     are guaranteed to send a person into ecstasy during the climax of
     "Hello Earth."
