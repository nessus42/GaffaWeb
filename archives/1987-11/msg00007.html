Mark, I am glad to see that _your_ interpretation corresponds with mine!
One thing I would take exception with is the middle of the 2nd verse
where you have:
     "Too-lee-yay, too-lee-yo, too-lee-yay, too-lee-yo, too-lee-yoo", I say
     To keep me goin', to keep the shit away

It seems to make more sense to have it as "YOU keep me goin'" because she's
addressing most of the song to someone who makes her lose control of herself.
So, why wouldn't it be this same person who keeps her goin', who keeps the
shit away?
   Well, this is a pretty trivial point anyway.  Just thought I'd throw
in my opinion.  Lord knows we need SOMETHING to talk about!
