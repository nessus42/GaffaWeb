> What does "rot13" mean?

Rot13 is a technique for making text unreadable.  It simply takes each
character and rotates it by 13 positions.  For example an "A" would become an
"N", a "B" would become an "O", and so fourth, assuming that a copy of the
alphabet follows "Z".  It is nice because rot13(rot13(text)) = text, i.e. your
decoder and encoder are one in the same.

As far as the suggestion to rot13 all rec.music.gaffa, that wouldn't be a good
idea since many people receive it on machines where they have no rot13 decoder.
Now of course they can write one, or get someone else to, but why should they?
Nobody is forcing anyone to read anything.  I happen to think that the various
