Nope.  I saw the LP the other day.  Of course, they had to chop off the
song "Mermaid Smiled" in order to fit "Dear God" on, and to tie it loosely
into the "continuity concept" engineered by our dear friend Mr. Rundgren,
they had the ending note of "Dear God" fade and be overlapped by the opening
drum-clicks of "Dying".  Bastards.  Your best bet is to buy the original
"Skylarking" and then either the domestic 12" of "Dear God" or the import
12" of "Grass" (which has "Dear God" as one of the B-Sides).  The reason I
say this is because that way you also get the phenomenal b-side "Extrovert".
What a great tune!  Boppy, catchy, lyrically interesting.  Great stuff.
They even take a nasty jab at Rundgren in the lyrics:
      "No hidden message, nothing political..."
