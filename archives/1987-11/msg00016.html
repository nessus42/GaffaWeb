The new Thatcher on Acid LP "Curdled" is excellent.  It is
about the only "Positive Punk" style LP I've ever liked, so for those
of you who like that sort of thing, this is a good indicator of the
quality.  The LP is very political, yet not without its sense of humor
(i.e. the priceless found sound at the very end).  Song writing is 
excellent, good vocals (very British), fine playing.
