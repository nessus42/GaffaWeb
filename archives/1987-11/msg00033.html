In a way, you're right and you're wrong.  Although I know it's not what
you meant, it seems highly doubtful that Kate would be able to sing
most of her work if she were male.  Uh, make that a non-castrato male.
Yes, it's more than the music, even if you throw in the voice; it's the
message, the mindset expressed in the lyrics and the performance, the
rest of the picture beyond what can be lifelessly reprinted in a
paint-by-numbers guide.  I'm not quite hitting the right analogy
but suffice it to say that of the few KT addicts I know well, myself
included, almost all of them became hooked on Kate sight unseen.

Not by any means, of course, to suggest that her entrancing visage
might be a liability.  Oops, letting my stoic's guard down again.

Somebody asked if the domestic _The Dreaming_ was otay.  Mine sounds
fine, except that I have nothing to compare it to besides _TWS_, which
still sounds substandard to me.  Is it enough to say that there's still
less hiss on the domestic _TD_ than on the import _HoL_?
