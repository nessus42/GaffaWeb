Donna Henderson" <donna@math.uconn.edu> wrote:

> I have been getting alot of private e-mail regarding my prior post 
> regarding the love hounds collection and the KaTe-Topia video. 
>
> I am appealing to the founders of the collections:  Is there a 
> 'formal' way for one to request first generation (from masters) 
> copies?  There is ALOT of interest in these collections (rightfully 
> so, as they are EXTREMELY well done).

    I created The Love-Hounds Collection and sent S-VHS master
tapes to people who could make at least 5 copies each. In this way,
the first distribution covered over 350 people. I have no idea how
many copies are out there now. I'd be happy to make a new S-VHS
master set for anyone who will commit to making at least 5 copies
for anyone who asks. 

    The idea is to send two VHS tapes (good quality, no crap) in a
stamped, self-addressed padded envelope. The padded one goes
into a slightly larger envelope and sent to the dubber. In order to 
preserve the quality of futher generations we *insist* that copies
*ONLY* be made at the SP (2 hour) speed. This footage stuff is
so worthwhile that it would be a crime to save $4 by trying to 
cram it onto a single tape.

> Please advise, curious (and desparate) minds want to know.

    So, there you have it. If you (or anyone) is willing to devote
a few evenings dubbing tapes, you are welcome to an S-VHS
master set from the 3/4" masters.
