I'm only just in my 20's now since I'm 30 in August (I'm a Leo, same
as Kate). I have always loved Kate's music since she started,
although, in 1978 I was only 11 so I didn't have the funds to collect
any of her work. That started when I got my first job. 

In common with a lot of people, I seem to be surrounded by people who
can't see the magic in her music. I did a lot of my growing up
listening to all her albums. Her individual style influenced me quite
a bit.

I didn't like The Dreaming at first. I basically shelved it for a long
time, almost 2 years then when I played it again, I wondered what had
put me off it! Its great. I do tend to prefer her older stuff though,
Pre HOL.

Just in case anyones interested in my favourite tracks, they are:

This Womans Work.
Get Out Of My House
Army Dreamers
Hello Earth (I just love that chanting)
