Another one of those twenty somethings.

Me is 21 now and the first time I heard Kate was 9 years ago - I saw her
TOTP performance of RUTH (about 2 years after she actually erformed it I
guess).  It was instant bliss!

But I was living in India then, and had a hard time finding any of her stuff.

So I guess I seriously got into Kate here in the US four years ago.  I
started off with the Whole Story and slowly worked my way through all
her albums - almost backwards.  The last one I got was Lionheart - just
last year.  THE NINTH WAVE is my absolute favorite (- and so is
everything else by Kate!)

I'm still learning a lot about her music...

And as is the usual case, almost everyone around me cannot understand
this Kate (and Joni) addiction.
