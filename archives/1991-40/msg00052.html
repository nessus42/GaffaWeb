Annie Haslam was the lead singer for Renaissance (their second
incarnation).  Since that group broke up she's had a couple of solo
albums.  Actually, I think her work with Renaissance (one of my favorite
all-time groups) was FAR superior to anything she did by herself 
afterwards.  Check out Renaissance's albums _Scheherezade and Other
Stories_ or _A Song For All Seasons_, which are among my favorites of
their albums.
