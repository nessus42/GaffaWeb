The new issue of _Pulse!_ just came out (Nov. 1991), and two people
included Kate in their Desert Island Discs.

Scott Barker, of Tucson, listed:

"2. _The Sensual World_ -- Kate Bush.  Kate, if I may be so bold, *is*
the sensual world."

David Brookman, of Philadelphia, gave no comments with his list but included
_The Kick Inside_ as No. 3.

For the record, here are their other choices:

Barker's:

1. Fifth Symphony -- Gustav Mahler
3. Greatest Hits -- Marvin Gaye
4. Moonrise -- Kim Robertson
5. Rockin' in the Country -- Wanda Jackson
6. Eye -- Robyn Hitchcock
7. West Side Story -- Original Broadway Cast
8. Cold Hands -- Boss Hog
9. Kill City -- Iggy Pop and James Williamson
10. Sidewaulk -- Capercaillie

Brookman's:

1. Hot Rats -- Frank Zappa
2. Psychic Powerless -- Butthole Surfers
4. Funhouse -- The Stooges
5. Lola vs. Powerman and the Money Go Round part one -- The Kinks
6. God WEEN Satan ... The Oneness -- Ween
7. Meat Puppets II -- Meat Puppets
8. Vision Thing -- Sisters of Mercy
9. Scumdogs of the Universe -- GWAR
10. Bela Fleck and the Flecktones -- Bela Fleck and the Flecktones

I would say that despite the common mention of Kate, these two people have
very different musical tastes!
