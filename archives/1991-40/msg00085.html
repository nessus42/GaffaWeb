  I was working at WKDU, Drexel University's college station.  Someone called 
in a request for Babooshka.  To be honest, I hated it at first listening.  Then 
a friend evangelized at me and I listened to The Dreaming.  I soon became 
hooked, and even ran entire hour Kate sets.
  When I first turned my friend Mitch on to Kate, he also disliked it at first, 
and now he refers to her as "the Goddess".
  (This is why I started the thread on who loved her first and who hated her 
first.)
