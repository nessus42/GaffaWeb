What an amazing evening last night was!  I returned from seeing
Innocence Mission (oh rapture! oh bliss!) to hear on the radio that
the Aurora Borealis was putting on quite a show.  Jumped back into my
car and headed for the hills, where I found a perfectly dark open
field from which to view and listen to (yes, they make noise!) the
shimmering waves of light.  Truly thrilling!

My well-deserved sleep was filled with lovely dreams about KaTe
performing in a beautiful old house for a small group of friends
whilst we ate Indian bread.  I choose to blame the northern lights for
these images.

Jenn
