Kate was discussed and TRS reviewed in the New York Times "Arts & 
Leisure" Section on Sunday.  Page 24, article authored by Peter Galvin 
who is listed as being an associate editor at Interview Magazine.  Wonder 
if there will be an Interview article?

Although Kate by rights should have the entire Sunday New York Times 
devoted to an in-depth examination of her work, she shares this mere four 
column space with Tori Amos -- in a rather interesting comparative article.  
Of course the writer's conclusion was that Kate is older than Tori and 
has been at it longer so her career is more developed.  Pretty safe 
ground . . .

Karen Newcombe  kln@crl.com

P.S.  Most newstands carry the Sunday Times so you can very likely still 
get it.
