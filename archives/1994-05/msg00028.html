I think it was on when gaffa was down for two weeks.  NPR's Weekend
Edition had a review of TRS with snippets of tracks plus interview
pieces interspersed.  I have not seen it mentioned here so I thought I
would in case the official keepers of every word kate has spoken 
publically missed it.  It was broadcast sometime between 7 and 8am on 
a Saturday.  That's as much as I can tell you.

It didn't contain anything we haven't heard before.
