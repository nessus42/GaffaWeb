So far, these are who you can expect.  It would be nice if you could maybe
bring food or drink of your choice.  Contributions toward more food and 
drink (maybe), and phone calls to other parties will be happily accepted. 
We'll have some door prizes as well.  The pizza idea sounded good-I'll 
order some (meat and vegetarian) during the day.  I'd rather see more 
salads than potatoe and tortilla chips, if you don't mind. :-)  Please
bring all your Kate-related stuff-the more the merrier!  I have both
the LP and Japanese CD _This Woman's Work_ boxes already here.  Dress
Kate-like.  Wear your buttons and other Kate-related clothes.  Please note
that there will be NO other artists played at my party-not even Jane; not
even Happy.  It's Kate's day.  I'm looking forward to seeing old and new 
faces-it _is_ this that brings us together.  

Once again, my address is 567 Boxleaf Court, San Jose, CA 95117.  The 
festivities begin as soon as I wake up, and continue until we stop.  The 
phone number is (408)249-4664.  I really hope someone can get that phone
to the Chicago party!!  It'd be too bad not being able to talk with 
you guys.  If you had any doubts whatsoever, you will leave here convinced
that She really IS!  

Larry (L-H@cup.portal.COM)
