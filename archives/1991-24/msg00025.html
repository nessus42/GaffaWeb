I'm pleased to report that The Big Sky BBS seems to be serving a
useful role for Kate Bush fandom.   For a while now folks without
access to the Love-Hounds archives have been calling to pick up GIFs
and such.   Now people who have just heard of Love-Hounds have started
calling to check it out and often seek full access.  Normally I try to
suggest a nearby public access USENET site for people to contact to
get full access to Love-Hounds.  

Unfortunately, these sites are often still a toll call and the closest 
access is sometimes long distance.  At that point the only alternative 
I can suggest is that the person use Compuserve.  I'm not a Compuserve 
subscriber so I don't know exactly what's involved.

Could someone who has signed up for the mailing list through Compuserve
provide some "how-to" instructions that I can pass along to people
considering that option?  Some information about the charges involved
would also help them wiegh the costs involved.

Thanks in advance!
