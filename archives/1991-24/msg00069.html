Bob writes:
>  On the first day of KaTemas my true love gave to me
>  a box set of import CD's...

Which reminds me...on Monday, I went in to Olsson's (one of the fairly 
decent record (and book!) stores in the DC area) in Dupont Circle during
my lunch break and noticed, perched on a shelf above the CD's a familiarly-
sized black box in a plastic bag.  Yup...the Box Set...but it was the
Japanese version!  Quite a surprise to see a Set on a store shelf, more
surprising that it was right there by the CDs and not under lock and
key behind the cash registers where no-one could defile it.  The slip of
paper inside the plastic bag explained that it contained, "All 6 of Kate's
studio albums, along with 2 more CDs containing b-sides, the live EP, 
rarities and other great stuff."  And it could all be yours for $188.

And because it's the only thing I can think of off the top of my head
that scans correctly (and I'm too lazy to put any thought into this):

"On the fifth day of KaTemas my true love gave to me:  The Whole Story!"
