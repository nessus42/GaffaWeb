Hello,

I work for a non-commercial radio station here in Fresno CA, and once
a month we have time slot which is "up for grabs" for anyone at the
station to use as he likes.  The thing about this time slot is that it's
from midnight to 6AM on the last Friday night/Saturday morning of the
month--not exactly "prime time", but it's nice if you like to play hours
of your favorite music and can stay awake for it.

Well, you may see what's coming:  this month I put in a listing for
an all-Kate Bush evening (after all, Katemas is around the corner.)
I have now acquired all six studio albums (hey! my cheap-o $9.99 CD
of TKI actually has lyrics!  I'm amazed!  None of my other CDs do
except for TSW--oh, I digress...) so I can carry on until about 4:30AM.
After that...well, there's my problem...

If there is anyone out there who could provide me with extra Kate
music for this occasion, I'd be very grateful.  I'm not expecting anyone
to mail me their CDs, but a nice HIGH-grade cassette would be great.
We can do 1/2 track 1/4" tape too, 7.5 or 15 IPS.  We have DAT too.
I'll pay for postage and for tape (if you don't want it back) or
return postage (if you do.)  Basically, what's needed is anything not 
on the six albums (or AoTSW, which I have.)

This is rather late notice, I know, but I've been on vacation the past
week and so this is my first chance to sit down at my computer and
put forth this request.  In case you think I'm someone just looking for
a free tape, you can call me at our office M-F 9AM-noon at (209)233-2221.
The station is KFCF-FM, 88.1 MHz, Fresno, CA.  The show I'm doing starts
at midnight, Friday night, July 26th.  If you are anywhere in central
CA from Modesto to Bakersfield (or on a nearby mountain) you should be 
able to hear it.  Our address is:

KFCF-FM
P.O. Box 4364
Fresno, CA 93744

email replies to:

Brian.Clayton@ccfcc.fidonet.org, or
Brian.Clayton@ccfcc.csufres.csufresno.edu

Thanks!
