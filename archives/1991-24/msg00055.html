        Well, it's finished.  Cloudbusting Edition 1 is now done.  1.3
Megabytes of Koncentrated Kate.  I have put it in straight text format and on
a couple of IBM disks and will be mailing it to Jorn to post in the archives. 
Hopefully it will get to Jorn by the time he checks his mail on Friday so it
can be in everybodies hands before the Kate Birthday parties.  
        Since it's obviously in computer form, I hope to have many more
Editions.  All comments are appreciated!  I am sure I have made many minor
mistakes and probably a few major ones!  I'd like to make the next edition as
error-free as possible.  
        The quotes are still a bit more repetitive than I wouldv'e liked, but
I think it's best for me to take a break and get some outside opinions on it.
