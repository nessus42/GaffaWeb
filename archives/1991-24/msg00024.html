Hello Katefans.  I was paging through Beat Box on Oracle this morning
(for the confused, Oracle is a teletext magazine service, and Beat Box
is a music column therein) and the letters section contains the following
rather odd ditty.  Since it has a KT reference, I thought you might be
interested.  I don't think I am contravening any copyright by reprinting
this...

        "Oh insolent, misguided chart fan,
         Smoking as only Boo-Ya Tribe can,
         Kylie and Danni you're gonna get yours,
         Ganxsta Ridd, Chuck D gather round for the cause.

         I'm gonna go to a Kate Bush fan club,
         I'm gonna get me an Enemy sound-dub,
         Emerge with a force to kill with sound,
         The Sisters of Mercy will dig out you're [sic?] ground.

         CAPRI DRIVER'S CLUB, West Midlands."

Fairly clear references to Experiment IV here, I think!  I am not
prepared to comment on the rest of it :-).

Keep well,

Stephen
