There's another alternative to long distance phone calls.  It's called
"PC Pursuit" and it's a service of SprintNet (i.e. Telenet).  It
allows you to for somewhere around $30 a month to make unlimitted
offpeak SprintNet connections to dial-out modems in most major cities.
>From these dialout modems, you can then connect to the public-access
Unix computer of your choice.  I believe the phone number for PC
Pursuit is (800) 835-3638.
