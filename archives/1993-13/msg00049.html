I hope that IED has not lost his internet connection via Andy by now, but
here I go:

IED wonders if his interview LP "Just saying it... ...could even make it
happen" is just another copy of the very same old interview. I think
I should say "YES", even if my version of that vinyl is a picture disc.
At least the cover seems to be identical. The two pictures are: one of the
shots where Kate wears the pink dress and where there's skin visible all
over her chest (nooo, all over in vertical direction ;-) or ;-( - :-)). She 
holds her hands up in that picture. The other picture shows her with the thing 
around her neck that reminds of a butterfly. Oh - the whole disc is numbered
'Cloudbus 10' and made in England. Mine has no 'Cloudbusting' anywhere on
it (just 'Cloudbus'), and also no 'WOGAN 1986'. But the pics seem to be those
of Troy's disc. BTW: The company is called 'Discussion Records'.

I think it's my best-packaged Kate-interview-LP. (Four different pictures).
