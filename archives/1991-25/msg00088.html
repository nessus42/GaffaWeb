Vickie here. Since this is all fantasy anyway, I'd want all the albums
by the 10 artists, but if I were forced to choose...

1)  Kate Bush-All, please-I-couldn't-live-without-any-of-them!!
    Point-a-gun-to-my-head-and-pick-one-album-selection: The Dreaming

2)  Jane Siberry-All, please...    Gun-pick: The Walking
3)  Happy Rhodes-All, please...    G-pick: Ecto
4)  Peter Gabriel-All, please...   G-pick: PGIV (aka Security)
5)  Genesis-1969-1982              G-pick: The Lamb Lies Down On Broadway
6)  Dead Can Dance-All             G-pick: My own compilation tape 
7)  The Cocteau Twins-All          G-pick: Treasure
8)  Boiled In Lead-All             G-pick: Hotheads
9)  Camper Van Beethoven-All       G-pick: Their 2nd album, called II & III 
10) kd lang-All                    G-pick: Angel With A Lariat

Actually, I could live forever quite happily with just 1-5.
I'd want 6 & 7 to remind me what angels in heaven might sound like. 
8, 9 & 10 may be odd picks, but I'd desperately need them to put me in
a good mood, should my will flag. Dire circumstances couldn't keep a smile
off my face when listening to any of these.

Others I'd take with me if I could:

Kate-Rarities & B-sides & interviews compilation tapes

Plus, as many "Song" compilations as I could bring. There are lots of
songs I love that come from albums that I don't like as a whole.
Actually, I'd probably want to just bring tapes of my radio shows,
since my #1 Rule is "I never play anything I don't personally like"
and I've only broken that rule a few times in the 3 years on air.

Plus, A *VERY* rugged Walkman & headphone (backups appreciated) and a
lifetime supply of batteries! And how about a sand-proof VCR for...

This is way too hard. I'm exhausted!!
