Subject: Goldmine is out
To: love-hounds@eddie.mit.edu

The August 9 issue of Goldmine is on the stands.  In the upper left corner
of the cover there's a photo of KT squatting in a gorgeous red robe, and
across the top of the page the banner: "Kate Bush: For Collectors Only". 
US price is $2.50.

The article runs from page 14 to page 19, with seven familiar and one new
(to me) photos.  The author is Gillian G. Gaar (with "additional info" from
Tom Richards.).  It's all in paragraphs, no tables.

I spot a couple of errors (I think)-- it says Cathy by JCB is still
available, and that Vermorel's Secret History is o.o.p. (I saw it around
quite recently).

The issue also features "Yes".
