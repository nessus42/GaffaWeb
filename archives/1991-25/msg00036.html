My DIDS are as follows.  I probably would tire of even these 
after a short time, but they are the ones I couldn't do 
without.
 
1. Kate Compilation Tape - 90 mins of mixed albums and b-sides
   of course have to have the goddess 
 
2. Happy Compilation Tape - a little bit from each album
   have to have the holy ghost
 
3. Sugarcubes - Life's ToO GooD - to keep me insane
 
4. Split Enz - The Living Enz - 90 min. comp. of live
   zaniness and pure genius
 
5. This is Soca - if you're gonna be on an island have
   to have the best island party music
 
6. Sarah McLachlan - Touch - for relaxing entertainment
 
7. The Police - Synchronicity - what can I say I love this 
   album, and it follows me on all my adventures
 
8. 10000 Maniacs - Hope Chest - more island sounds
 
9. INXS - The Swing - my favorite old INXS album...love it
 
10.  B-52's - Yellow Album - Rock Lobster is the classic
     party song...and what better song to make you happy when
     you're stranded.
