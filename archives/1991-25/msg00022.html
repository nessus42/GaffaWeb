In article <1991Jul19.063618.7390@crash.cts.com> rhill@pnet01.cts.COM (Ronald Hill) writes:
> ... and Kate has always shunned having much of any image, but I think
> she is just about the only person around who portrays herself to be
> rouphy what she is.

I'm not sure I agree with this.  I think she is very, very careful in
the management of her publicity.  I recall this being discussed
over a year ago on Love-Hounds, and one of the things discussed was
how carefully thought out her promotional photographs were, particularly
the more recent ones.  I think her image is very carefully crafted, but
there is an equal amount of care taken to ensure that it isn't a *false*
image.  So, yes, she portrays herself as who she really is, but this
is by deliberate design, not accident.  Remember that she likes to
have control over all aspects of her career, and I don't think she would
skimp on her image.
