I actually like the idea of Love Hound desert island lists,
because I would be interested in what absolute staples in people's
musical diets I have never heard of and should.  Considering the
strength of the Kate Bush-bond, these lists are likely to be
enlightening.

Here, then, with the obvious caveats, are my indispensables.
Naturally, I cheated...
when there were artists with multiple DI-worthy albums, I listed them
in descending order.  If you are a purist, just delete all but the
first in these multiple listings.
I have also avoided (as much as possible)
greatest hits/compilations, etc., which I think
is important, since this is really a "greatest albums of rock era"
list, and as such should be pure albums, IMHO.

There are three sections here.  It is pretty self-explanatory.
If anyone sees something in this list they want more details on,
please do drop me a line... if they're indispensable to
my existence, maybe you'll like them too.
Here goes...
