A lot of nay-sayers here insist that Tori is not a pale imitation
of Kate.  Here is proof that is undeniable:

Tori - released album called "Under the Pink"
Kate - released album called "Under that Very Light Shade of Red"

Tori - founded organization called RAINN
Kate - founded organization called SNOWW

Tori - hit single:  Cornflake Girl
Kate - hit single:  Frosted Flakes Girl

Tori - puts out album every year
Kate - puts out album every year

Tori - tours often
Kate - tours often

Now, if THAT doesn't make a strong case, I don't know WHAT does!
