The line in "Wow" may not seem very positive but I never took it to be Kate
speaking as herself, but rather speaking as one of the characters in the
song.  It's a bitchy, backbiting sort of putdown from the sort of person
who would then go on to sarcastically say, "Ooh yeah, you're amazing / We
think you are really cool" and not mean a word of it.  One of the many
wonderful things tucked into that now-disowned video for "Wow" is Kate's
tiny pantomime of disapproval as she comes to that line, suggesting the
hidden meaning behind the words.  At least that's what I remember; it's
been a very long time since I last saw it...
