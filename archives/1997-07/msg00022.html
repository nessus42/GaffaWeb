Oh, and I just remembered another thread I wanted to reply to - the
"favorite songs off TRS" thread. My favorites are "You're the One",
"Moments of Pleasure" and "Song of Solomon", but I love them all... I
suppose "Big Stripey Lie" is my least favorite, but still a wonderful
song. After the touching story posted by Elizabeth, I plan on giving it a
closer listen! 

My story is related to "You're the One", though it's not quite so sad. In
short form, I had a relationship end, one that I thought would be the
"one" to last... No other breakup song comes as close to summing up my
feelings as Kate's... I still love the song - so beautiful and poignant
and true... In a strange way, the song makes me happy - it reminds me of
a beautiful relationship that, though it didn't last, gave me lots of
wonderful memories.
