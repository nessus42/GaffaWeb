Well.....the thing is......we're all kind of used to it now....four 
years between albums isn't that long, really.....sort of...five years 
could be a considered a bit tardy but four is pretty much on track....

The B-52's (average 4 years between albums), Xtc (closing up on 6

substandard material thrust at us evry 12 months.
