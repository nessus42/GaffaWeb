Wow, Meth, my "new digest format" doesn't run the messages together
at all--it separates each with a line and puts a multicolored
"Interoffice Memorandum" header on each one--takes up more space,
but other than that I can't complain. If the digests were very long
it would take significantly longer to page thru them in this format
(for me, anyway--not for you if yours are running together).  Sounds
like a software incompatibility thingie?  Ok, I here comes the
obiligatory KonTent--did y'all catch the lunar eclipse last night?
Put me in mind of "Hello Earth" which was my first "favorite" off of
HoL, which was my first KaTe album, and I STILL love it.  Our Kosmic
visitor Hale-Bopp was in great form, and Mars bright and right above
the moon, which turned rusty red and looked unusually spherical with
the binoculars--obviously it IS spherical, but it's usually too bright
to see the nuances of shape up there.  Oddly enough, I didn't have
Big Sky running thru my little brain, just Hello Earth.

cheers,
