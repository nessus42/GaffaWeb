I freaked out when I saw all those articles with "British Industry
Pornographic" awards, because just the night before I did this
"mystery set", which no one knew the theme of. (It's not about
British industrial pornographic music, in case you were wondering.
The connection is very unambiguous tho. Brad Goins, John Hogge and
Katherine Nichols also contributed to the set.)
