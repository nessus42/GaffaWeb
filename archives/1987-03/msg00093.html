You, sir, have the right to fill your record collection with sounds of
people making gobs of money. I however will fill my collection with music
by people who may *lose* money by putting out their own music, just because
they enjoy making music. You have the choice of supporting people who love
music, or people who love money. 

Just because a few people make top 40 shit to make money does not mean most
people are in music for the money. Jeeeeeez.
