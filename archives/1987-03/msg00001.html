About Jamie's surprise about IED's failure
to include the interpretation of "Hounds of
Love" as a description of Kate's relationship
with her fans:

is because it is WRONG.

Kate point blank whether it was accurate or not,
and received a very definite 'no'. Even this didn't

the transcript of his interview), but presumably
he has come to see reason since then.
 
There is, of course, absolutely NO tangible evidence in the lyrics
of the song for such an interpretation.
