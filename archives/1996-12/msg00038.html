 An architype of human potential - very nice. It is one of the greatest
 joys a feeling person can experience, to be exposed even fleetingly to
 the talents of those who rise so far above the masses as to seem truly
 superhuman; the dull earth of mediocre performers seeming to raise the
 brilliant stars even higher in the firmament.

 "She walks in beauty, like the night

  And all that's best of dark and bright
    Meet in her aspect and her eyes."
