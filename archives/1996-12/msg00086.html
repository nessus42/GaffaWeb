Hello dreamers,

> I *know* humans cannot fly

I can fly in my dreams, and I know it's not really possible in reality.
I also have a very technical background, so you cannot blame that.
Apparently, I've heard that flying in a dream means you're optimistic
and/or happy about events around you.

> maybe there's a nice English word for that profession.

My wife tells me "oneiromancer" was in the last Dune book to describe
people who are just this. It's in my dictionary (dated 1917):
	oneirocritic	n. Interpreter of dreams
	oneirology	n. The science of dreams
from "oneiros" Greek meaning "dream". Pronounced:
	on-ear-ology
I remember getting a book (for someone else) describing what things
meant in dreams. Some of them were strange. I wish I had a copy!
