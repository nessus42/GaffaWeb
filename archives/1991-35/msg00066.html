tracy robyn somervill (is there an e in the house?) says
that Happy Rhodes is repetitious and dull. !!

I just have to make a mention that i disagree quite wholeheartedly.

I am *not* making a comparison to Kate - I do prefer Kate overall to
Happy. Kate is still my favorite artist, but Happy is my second
favorite - and considering her music completely on it's own, I
wouldn't use "repetitious" or "dull" in describing it! What
albums/songs have you heard!? Her songs range from full and soothing
to sparse and errie,some are just plain odd, some are loud, some are
soft, some are screechy and some are loooow,some are very energetic,
some have amazing melodies, and i could go on, and on..

She's just *far* from repetitious.
