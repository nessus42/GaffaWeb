Hi!

I was listening to _Never For Ever_ this afternoon, for the first time in
a while.  And I realized that there really isn't much discussion about it
around here.  Not that there isn't stuff to bring out, IMO- hey, if the Gaffa
Wars are raging again (must be a new quarter :), why not dust off some old
issues (which may not be so old after all)?  So:

It's amazing the difference between _Lionheart_ and NFE, across the board.
Amazing how quickly one can slough off previous outside influences when one 
is even just co-producing, isn't it?

Two questions: 1. I noticed today the stuff behind "Infant Kiss" (like I
said, I don't listen to this one much).  What does she say behind "Ooh how
he frightens me/ When they whisper privately"?

	2. Anybody decipher the Latin in "Delius"?  After "Delius amat",
it gets pretty fuzzy (actually, this one has been bugging me for a while,
since I was in high school and wanted to play it for my Latin teacher, but
didn't dare until I knew what she was singing in case he asked me for the
translation! :).

Woj always says this is his favorite album (right?), and now I think I can
see how- I have no clue why I listen to this the least of all of them- today,
it struck me just how complex it is... wow. :)
