To end the debate once and for all:

Kate got the phrase "am I suspended in gaffa?" from the wall of the passage
connecting the dorms B and C in the Tunnels underneath the Butterfield Colleges
here at Wesleyan.  She sent her astral form to do some laundry in the
wonderfully spacious, comfortable, and economical laundry rooms down there, saw
this musing amidst the multicolored, multi-philisophical graffiti on the wall,
and decided to use it in her next song.  So there.
