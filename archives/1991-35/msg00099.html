OK, kids.  Here's directions on how to get to my place in case you're
coming to the Santa Cruz party this SUNDAY...  If you're not interested, hit
'n' now.

Find Route 1.  You can come down 880 to 17 to 1 from Oakland, or 280
to 17 to 1 from San Francisco, or you can grab 1 up in SF and enjoy
a beautiful trip down the coast.  Anyway, somehow you gotta get on 1
heading south.  Take the SOQUEL DRIVE exit.  Turn left at the top of
the exit ramp.  Turn right on 17th Ave (about one block down from the
exit).  Proceed on 17th through a stop sign, stop light, and second
stop light.  At the next stop sign (at Merrill Street) turn right.
Go to the end and look for somewhere to stash your car, cos you can't
park in my apartment complex.  Sorry.  Anyway, Merrill Street ends
perpendicular to 14th Ave.  Turn right on 14th.  At the end of the
street is Swan Lake Apartments (ugly name, but whaddya gonna do).  Walk
through the entrance, turn left, proceed to the end of the lot, go
down the path that leads between two buildings.  Turn right on the path.
Apartment 515 is on the back side of the building overlooking the woods.
That's where you want to be.

Here's a really primitive map of the relevant area.

Well, that was even more primitive than I hoped for, but it is somewhat
informational.  Just scream loud if you get lost, or call (408) 464-8758 and
beg for help.

Bring munchies and/or drinkables.

Festivities commence mid afternoonish, say 2 or 3pm and go until I get bored
and throw you all out.  Be there or don't.
