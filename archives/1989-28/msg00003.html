 To: Ed
 From: Andrew Marvick (IED)
 Subject: Party this weekend

     In honor of Julian West's party in New England this weekend
(Saturday, November 4th), IED will host a small, completely informal
(i.e. unplanned) open house for any and all Los Angeles-area Love-Hounds
(and other Kate Bush fans) who might care to attend. Anyone who wishes
to spend a few hours watching rare (and/or not-so-rare) Kate Bush video
material on good equipment in a quietly celebratory Kate Bush atmosphere
this Saturday evening is encouraged to telephone: (213) 474-5208
immediately.
     Those who cannot attend on such short notice should take heart:
IED will try to host a more carefully planned Kate Bush Bash in December
or perhaps at the new year, and more lead time will be given for that
party than for this one. So upstate Love-Hounds, you will be informed
in plenty of time to trek down here for a real IED party later on.
Meanwhile, anyone who wants to drop by Saturday evening, the address is:
     10499 Wilkins Avenue, Los Angeles, CA 90024. The house is a large
whitish Spanish-style building at the triple corner of Wilkins, Thayer
and Warner Avenues, located about a mile south of the UCLA campus,
between Santa Monica and Wilshire Boulevards. Please call first, both
to warn IED of your imminent descent upon his lair and to get more
precise directions.
     Meanwhile, IED wishes Julian the best of luck with his party
Saturday.

-- Andrew Marvick
