According to 120 Minutes last night, Skinny Puppy is no more.  The new
album is posthumous.  Ogre has left the band to join Ministry and will
be doing the vocals.  There is also a new Ministry record due out any
minute called *The Mind is a Terrible thing to Taste*.  This record
still has Al on vocals.
