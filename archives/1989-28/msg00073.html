Recent books mentioning Kate

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Sun, 5 Nov 89 18:47:24 PST
Subject: Recent books mentioning Kate


A couple of books that have just come out mention Kate.  There's 
a book that supposedly gives the address of everybody who's anybody
in the music business.  They list Kate, but they give the address as
EMI-Manhattan!  Good luck getting through to Kate that way.

Dave Marsh has a new book in which he lists his choices as the 
1,000 best rock singles in history.  Amazingly, one of the ones he
chose was "Don't Give Up", and he even talks about Kate's "angelic
soprano."  If this doesn't seem so astounding to you, you should 
realize that Mr. Marsh was one of the original Kate-haters of 1978.

That's it for now, folks!
