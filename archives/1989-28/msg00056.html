Andrew Marvick writes:

>>      Someone asked where the "KT" sign is hidden in _The_Whole_Story_ and
>> _The_Sensual_World_.

I haven't finished reading the latest batch of gaffa yet, so forgive
me if I'm reiterating.

To find the KT symbol, turn the cover upside down.  Find the rose petal
that touches the top (bottom) of the cover.  Move down to the one on the
right, then to the on on the left, then to the one on the right.  Look
along the top edge on the left of this petal and you should find it (it's
very faint).  I'm pretty sure it's not a natural part of the flower, but
has been added afterwards.

The thing that convinces me that this is it is that it looks like the
symbol, rather than just being similar to it.

Jim
