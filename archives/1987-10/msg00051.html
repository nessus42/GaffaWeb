I may have quite a few inaccuracies here and I apologise to any New Order
fans or people more knowledge than myself, but we Aussies have known about
New Order for a couple years now. They are a English New Wave band formed 
in the early 80's. They are best known down here for their single 
"Blue Monday" which I suggest you try and get a hold of. You may remember
them for their song "Shell-Shock" which was used in the "Pretty in Pink" sound-
track.
