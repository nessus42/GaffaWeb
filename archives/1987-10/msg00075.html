The original Buzzcocks were John Maher, Steve Diggle, Pete Shelley and
Howard Devoto. They release the "Spiral Scratch" EP on the New Hormones label.
Devoto subsequently left the band to form Magazine who had one notable hit
with "Shot by both sides". The Buzzcocks then released a host of excellent
singles on the UA label, beginning with "Orgasm Addict" and including "What do
I get", "I don't mind", "Ever fallen in love", "Promises", "Everybody's happy
nowadays" and "Love you more" ( my collection fades out here). Sadly, they
made little impact on the charts. I could include some of their stuff on the
compilation tape if there was sufficient interest. Talking of which ...

it's just a suggestion.
