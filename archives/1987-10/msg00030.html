Just a addendum on Psycodrama

From: hofmann@nrl-css.arpa (James B. Hofmann)
Date: Thu, 30 Apr 87 07:20:24 EDT
Subject: Just a addendum on Psycodrama


After lots of talk with the boys, I can't really say that they WANT to be
on "college" radio so I think the only way you'll hear them is if the 
radio stations have gotten their hands on the various compilations that
they are on.  (they told me the "Desperately Seeking Suicide" compilation 
is out with their Madonna cover).  I think Psycodrama's idea of exciting
radio is them w/help of the motorcycle white power brethern taking over
the station and killing every person who is either non-white, punk, gay
and/or Jewish.

As for the Happy Flowers, I'm sure they'd love to get on radio and all the
stations have to do (probably) is write their puny little record label
and request some of their puny records.  Return postage wouldn't hurt.  Tag
Bill or myself for the address.
