drowning in my own histamines (pollen count 121 today) and a peculiar
coincidence was brought to my attention today.

D5.  And sure enough, when I arrived in Baaaaahston, the locals told me

through Monday, too.  On the way back that night, I cycled through half
of Kate's albums.  To my utter surprise, I hear you guys up there got
dumped on with a foot of snow Tuesday.

Heh heh, guess it works up north after all...
