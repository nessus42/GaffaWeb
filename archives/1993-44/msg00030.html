I made a discovery within the last couple of days.  "Eat the Music",
while perhaps a little too repetitive if you listen to it with
full attention, is a great song for listening to while driving.
During traffic jams it's very relaxing, and when moving at
highway speeds it has about the same rhythm as the car's motion.
