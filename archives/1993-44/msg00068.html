   In a word, yes. The Trio Bukgarka is three Bulgarian women, Stoyanka
Boneva, Eva Georgeieva and the amazing Yanka Rupkina. They were
originally members of the Bulgarian State Women's Choir. An album
of Bulgarian choral music, "Le Mystre Des Voix Bulgares" was released
and became a sort of underground hit. Enough people with were bored
with western music to sell out several concert tours of the US.
   This music uses different structures than western vocal harmonies.
I'm not knowledgeable about music theory, but I've read that they use
3rds and 5ths. I guess it is something that you either like or not.
Personally, their vocals often move me to tears. Yanka in particular
is (IMO) one of the best singers on earth.
