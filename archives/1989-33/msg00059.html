The best source I've heard of for imports of this sort in the area is Reckless
Records in SF, however, this is not to say that they can't be found elsewhere.

TSW single was released before the album, but I found it about the same time
as the eventual album release, at Circular Motions in Cupertino.  I *just*
found TWW at Compact Disc Warehouse in Sunnyvale, and got one of the last
two copies (on display).  These two stores tend to get new-release imports,
later than some places in SF, and in lesser quantities, but they still get
them.  Unfortunately, the help generally don't know about them beforehand,
only after they've arrived and are selling like hotcakes.  Btw, I haven't
seen TSW single in the south Bay for several weeks.
