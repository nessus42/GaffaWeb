This morning I heard a side of the new (debut?) 10,000 Maniacs
record on KXLU and thought that you and your love dogs might
be interested.  I know absolutely nothing about the band 'cept
for this one listening, but they stuck me as S. Vega (but not

interesting song structure, musicanship but not virtuoso pomposity,
intelligent lyrics... or it could be that all female vocalists sound
the same to me.

So, check it out, if you want, if you care.  (I'd be interested to
know if I came close.)
