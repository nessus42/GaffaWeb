Extract from a letter-to-the-editor in the Nov. 1985 Keyboard Magazine:

"Let's face it, when it's all said and done and the smoke finally clears,
a few indisputable facts remain:  (1) Everyone has his own personal likes
and dislikes, and is hopefully free to listen to or play the instruments
and the music he prefers.  No one is going to hold a gun to Cliff's head
and force him to get it on with a Fairlight.  If Kate Bush wants to do
anything like that, she should feel free to call me anytime, seven days
a week, 24 hours a day..."
 
				John Ezra Scheirman
					Coos Bay, OR

The letter was in response to an article by Cliff Henricksen about how
electronic instruments lead to soulless performances.  It was a very good
letter.
