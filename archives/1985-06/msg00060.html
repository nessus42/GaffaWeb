I have Colourbox's self-titled LP (came out in August, I believe) and
I like it a lot.  It's incredibly DIVERSE - there's soul (a cover of
The Supremes' "You Keep Me Hanging On"), pop-funk ("Inside Informer"),
a 50's style slow-dance ballad ("The Moon Is Blue"), not to mention
all-out techno-dance music and a lovely piece for solo piano...in
other words, describing the Colourbox "sound" is nearly impossible!
My copy came with a free album which has some more experimental stuff
on it, different versions of LP tracks, and a B-side or two.  

"Fast Dump," by the way, is hardly representative of Colourbox.  Most
of their tracks feature their female vocalist.
