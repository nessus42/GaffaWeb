New Stuff from the wayside catalog

From: Tim Wicinski <wicinski@nrl-css.ARPA>
Date: Mon, 4 Nov 85 07:37:03 est
Subject: New Stuff from the wayside catalog

Just recieved saturday the update to the new wayside catalog. Inside,
bargains galore !!! They have four Joy Division Bootlegs they are
selling.  One is a three record box of three earlier eps. The other
three are live recordings from different places.  All are limited
edition (or so they say).  Also in the catalog was some old boots from
the Mothers of Invention.  Mostly, the Mothers albums were pressings of
sound board tapes from european shows from the late 60's. I plan on
checking one out.

tim
