******************************************************************
*  AN URGENT HOMEGROUND NEWSFLASH DIRECT FROM HOMEGROUND TOWERS  *
******************************************************************  
 
 
In the UK the first single from Kate's new album will be released 
on 16th August. The title is:
 
                        "Eat the Music"
 
It is said to be a Brazilian/latin type workout regarding music and 
food (stating the bleedin' obvious!). An extended version entitled:
 
                      "The Madagascan Mix"
 
will be available on the 12" picture disc and the CD single.
 
The B-side will be a track called:
 
                       "Big Stripy Lie"
 
a non-album track.
 
The first US single may be a track entitled:
 
                       "Rubber Band Girl"
 
another somewhat upbeat offering. We haven't heard that Columbia have 
finally made up their mind, though they may have by the time you read
this!
