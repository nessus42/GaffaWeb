Thanks, Mike.  To re-summarize, Mike repeatedly assured me that Chris was
offering to dub a master in his home, with Aspel and Rocketman, and that
it could be done in a matter of hours.  The truth that emerged after two
weeks of botched communication was that Chris couldn't do this at all,

Kansas City some weeks from now.  What a mess!

hopeful it will be the most wonderful thing you've ever gotten, and strongly

toplevel dubbers]
