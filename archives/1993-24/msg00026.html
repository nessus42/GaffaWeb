In the Globe and Mail (Canada's "National" [Toronto] Newspaper) a few days
ago there was an item saying that one of the principal dancers of the
National Ballet of Canada has been chosen to play the lead in a new

thereabouts....

ACK! Bad marketing on KB's part! If there is a stage musical AND a film AND

confused to say the least. Is it too late to persuade her to change it to
something else? e.g.
- Rubber Band Girl (a *great* song title)
- My Name Is Not Prince
- Me'n'Jeff'n'Eric

etc. etc.
