Subject: Is "Live at the Hammersmith" available in Hi Fi Video?

I guess the subject line says it all.  I'll probably end up buying
it anyway, but I'd like to know if there exists a VHS Hi-Fi version
of the Hammersmith Odeon video, or if the edition I've seen in stores
is really Hi-Fi even though it doesn't say so on the box.  If not, why
on earth not?  

I have the Hi-Fi video of The Whole Story - but it's disappointing. 
Many of the songs were recorded with a lot of hiss; the tape is full
of dropouts that sound like someone rubbing a balloon; and (I'm 
willing to argue about this!) many of the videos are embarassingly
crummy anyway.  My advice: stick to the audio version.  (Though it
might be worth the $25 for 'Wuthering Heights,' 'Cloudbusting,'
'Hounds of Love,' and 'The Big Sky' [not on the audio versions].)
