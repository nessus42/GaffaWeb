Subject: Kate in X10 bitmaps

I sent this to L-Hs once quite a while ago, but never saw it posted--so
excuse me if this is a repeat.  These are X10 bitmaps of a couple of
kate images I made from the last KBC newsletter--one from the front
cover and another from an artist's rendering.  They are only 80x79
pixels.  They can be used as include files in an X10 program or with the
X10 screensaver that was posted to USENET a while ago.  They can be
edited with the UNIX program bitmap(1).
