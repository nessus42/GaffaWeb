Yes, and tobacco is most definitely made of vegetarian
products. Alchoholic stuff is also made out of vegetarian
base products...

The reason to this rhetoric is that I and Carina often meet people who
think that to be a vegetarian is structly equal to be a pure-living
health phreek, which is NOT the case with us, and I suppose not with
KaTe either. You can be a vegetarian of different reasons; health or
otherwise. We are vegetarians of pure religious reasons (religion in
it's non-organized meaning). That we are feeling much better now than
we did four-five years ago (before becoming vegetarians), we just see
as a nice spin-off product.

So, I never saw Kate's smoking habit as anything remarkable in the
light of her vegetarianism, just a foolish thing to do in the long
run... (says the person who occasionally enjoys a pipe... :-)
