The routine was performed live at the show.  Kate wanted to perform
an all out dance routine for the song which meant that it would be
impossible to sing it properly.  She completely re-recorded the song
"live" to play as a backing for the dance routine and then during each
show performed the dance routine to the music with no pretense of singing 
(i.e. no lip syncing).  Evidently re-recording the music also helped
avoid any hassles with the musician's union.
