The Kate pages have been updated. New images, and more in the chronology.  
The address is in the .sig at the bottom. Many of the people who have 
registered with No Dead Trees have stopped their online service; changed 
addresses; or my server just can't reach your address.
  If you've registered with NDT, and didn't receive an e-mail notification of the 
update, let me know, and I'll get your address into the notification list again.
  As many of you know, I've begun imbedding images within the text instead 
of putting up purely image pages. This is the format of the new images. If you 
have problems finding the images, let me know and I'll send you the path.
  *An aside*
  I received a tape from a group called Arcanum. It's quite good. Some Kate 
influence, though I would say more Moody Blues.
  Also. If you can find Yuri (a Spanish diva) in your country, give her a listen. 
Though not influenced by Kate, she has a brilliance with lyrics, music and (on 
video) dance.
  *One other aside*
  Indian music (not American Indian) has some definite qualities that make it 
worthwhile for the average European or American. Try it.
