>I'm curious if anyone is familiar with the British band All About Eve,
>whose lead singer has similar musical leanings to Kate... They put out
>four albums that I'm aware of, along with a greatest hits and a live BBC
>album... since then, I've heard nothing of them...

>Does anyone know what became of them, or the lead singer, Julianne Regan?

Well, in short, they split up. Julianne has done very little since,
but a couple of weeks ago she released a new single "Mat's Prozac"
with her new band "Mice". An album is to follow shortly, but be
warned; the sound is quite different to AAE.

There is a mailing list for AAE discussion available, but at the
moment it's on the move to a new owner, I'll let you know the new
address when I have it (remind me if I forget!).

Incidentally, on a TV show some years ago, Julianne chose her
favourite videos. 'Cloudbusting' was one of them.
