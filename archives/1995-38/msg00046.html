hello everyone -

for the guy who was asking about the Butterfly Kisses' recording quality, 
it's not too bad.  it varies, though, because it was taken from different 
sources.  the worst would be the Saturday Nite Live recordings and old 
German concert recordings.  those have some clicks and pops, and it 
doesn't sound like it was from a good recording to begin with.  but the 
Amnesty version of RUTH, the Comic Relief tracks, and the studio version 
of Under the Ivy are all pretty nice.  

anyway, overall i'd recommend it, because it does have some neat stuff on 
there.
