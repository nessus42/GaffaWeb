I'm looking for a volunteer to do the
cloudbusting machine in VRML.  This is complex
so not for the faint of heart.  All
due credit will be posted with the work.
Other obvious KateSymbols are also
appreciated.  We've done the two KBC
symbols.  Those will be available for
reuse in other worlds.  Be sure to note
that these are trademarked symbols
and don't use them in any VRML scene
you intend to market for profit.

In fact, when using these worlds, make sure you
preserve any copyright information contained
within them.  This applies to photos,
recordings, MIDI transcriptions, etc.
The first world we list will have examples
of the kind of language I recommend
for this purpose.  Until the Federales
get this all straight (and they are working
on it), assume the regular international
copyright conventions apply and respect
them.
