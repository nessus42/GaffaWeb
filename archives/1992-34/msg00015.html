     Gabriel's "Sledgehammer" on MTV, and of course I watched it.  When

     award and he wasn't there to pick it up.  :)

     Then he also mentioned, in passing, that Gabriel will be guest-hosting
     their "120 Minutes" video show "some time in the next couple of
     weeks."

     I wonder if he has a say in the videos that will be played on that
     show?  If he does, then who knows which ones we might see?
