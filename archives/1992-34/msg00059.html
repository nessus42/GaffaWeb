For all who may have interest, the Tori Amos songbook is now available.  Quite
a nice package.  Several pictures (some are stunning), and notes from Tori on
each song (how she wrote it, what her inspiration was, etc.).  PLUS- (I just
glanced through the book, so I'm not 100% on this) it looks like ALL music is
included (even the bass line).  Maybe Kate can learn from this packaging.  The
book costs almost $20, though.
