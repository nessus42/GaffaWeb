There is a new CD service on HoloNet BBS that finds more than 50,000 CDs
that you can buy online.  I thought I'd check it out to see if Happy Rhodes
was available since I had read here that her CD's were really hard to get.
I was suprised to find out that at least two were, as well as Tori Amos, and
Kate CDs, some that I haven't seen in record stores.

You have to be a HoloNet member to get access to the service, which is
a minimum of $6.00/mo.  You can telnet to holonet.net or use one of their
national dialups to reach HoloNet (call 1-800-NET-HOLO with your modem
set to 8N1 to find the closest number to you).

The service is still in the experimental menu, but it is operational.
