Hi -

Not to start a flame-ware (who, ME?), but I thought "Bram Stoker's
Dracula" was lame at best.  Self-conscious artiness followed by camp
(actually, the camp parts were OK), followed by long periods of extreme
boredom.  Repeat cycle endlessly.

Now, if you want to check out a great (imnsho), see "Reservoir Dogs" -
Ultraviolent, ultra-cynical, nihilistic fun at it's best.  ObKate:  This
film has a GREAT soundtrack, entirely of the WORST non-disco songs of
the 70's, which quickly make you remember why you became a Kate/Alternative
music fan in the first place.
