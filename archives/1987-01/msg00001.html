Subject: Anyone heard of...


Does anyone know where I can contact this Vital Organs label that puts
out the new LP by Piglatin (obscure Bay area improv/noise band that
sounds like something I must have)? Or maybe the band itself? Thanks
a bunch...

BTW, new Wiring Department is out. Our friendly neighborhood cool record
store stacked it in the Spin box, so I almost didn't see it. An incredible
amount of stuff for $1, even tho the emphasis is on West Coast bands.
Totally shreds inferior and more expensive zines like Non-stop Banter,
not to mention Spin. This ish you get tons of reviews of obscure things
and excellent interviews with Lydia Lunch (catch the enmity between the
lines), Genesis P-Orridge, Michael Gira, Thurston M, Camper Van B, as well
as tons of obscure bands. Lots of great pictures too... it's good to know
what Scratch Acid really looks like, for instance. 

Bill
