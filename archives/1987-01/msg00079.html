If you're going into Boston for CDs, don't miss out on Newbury Comix- they have
better prices than BCD on lots of stuff (although BCD has a much better
selection, *plus* neat little lucite squares, but that's not too important).
Strawberry's also had some good stuff for not too much money, but
I was disappointed by the Coop because 1) most everything was overpriced and
2) there were salesmen yelling "we're out of the Bruce Springsteen set" every
five minutes.
Clancy said something about being from Rhode Island: on Thayer Street there is
a shop called "Tom's Tracks", above Taco Maker.  They have a lot of good CDs
(all three you mentioned already buying, as a start) and Tom will order stuff
for you.  Prices are very good, about the same as Newbury Comix.

-Jerhead
