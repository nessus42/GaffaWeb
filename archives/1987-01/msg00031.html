Somebody recommended this EP saying it was Godlike, etc. etc.   Well,  this
made me go out and get the aforementioned disk and I find it to be one of the
most boring records I've bought in years.  Yes it has touches of English-style
gloom (of which I am a huge fan of) but the songwriting really sucks as far as
I'm concerned.  I can definitely say that I have bought my last Dark Arts
release.
