For Pink Floyd Fans

From: nessus (Doug Alan)
Date: Sat, 17 Jan 87 02:29:13 EST
Subject: For Pink Floyd Fans

Roger Waters has done the soundtrack for a movie about nuclear war
called *When the Wind Blows*.  There is a soundtrack album out.  Another
movie, *Fuck Your Dreams, This Is Heaven* is dedicated to Syd Barrett
and the soundtrack albums contains three Syd Barrett covers by
Peter Principle (of Tuxedomoon), Minimal Compact, and Niki Mono &
Nikolas Klau.  The record also contains two Velvet Underground covers,
a Patti Smith cover, a Jefferson Airplane cover, and a Yardbirds
cover.  Due out in February or March is *Beyond the Wildwood*, an
album of 14 Syd Barrett covers.  For those who have been living in
a bread box, Pink Floyd is back in the studio with Rick Wright and
without Roger Waters.  Waters is sueing.
