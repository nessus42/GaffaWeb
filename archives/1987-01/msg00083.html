Just got my paycheck, and I figured, what better way to throw it away
than on yet another Kate Bush record?  So, trucking down to my local
record store, I find a three record set called "Kate Bush Live In
London 79-80" - it was a bit pricey, but hey, you only live once,
right?

Anyway, my main question is: has anybody heard this record?  if so,
what do you think and do you happen to know where it was recorded?
And can you tell me which songs are on which sides?  The labels of
the records simply say "THE WIZARDS QUEST - Continuing Saga Records"
and "Made In The USA"
So where did this come from?
