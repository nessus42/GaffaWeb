Announcing TRAJECTORY, the Veda Hille mailing list!

Veda Hille is a self-described "jazz angular pop beautiful" singer and
songwriter from Vancouver, British Columbia who is just starting to make a
name for herself outside of Canada.  This list has been created to provide
a forum for Veda's fans to discuss her work.  It is intended to be a place
to find out information about Veda's releases and gigs, exchange thoughts
and ideas, and get to know other fans from around the world.  

The list is available via loose-mail or in digest form.  (Loose-mail means
that every time a post is sent to the list, you will get it too.  Digest
form means that posts to the list are collected and sent out at regular
intervals as one chunk organized in one piece of mail.  If you are
concerned with controlling the amount of mail you receive, the Digest may
be the best choice for you.)

 To subscribe to the loose-mail version, send a note to majordomo@smoe.org
with

     subscribe trajectory

 in the body.

 To subscribe to the digest version, send a note to majordomo@smoe.org with

     subscribe trajectory-digest

 in the body.

For complete information, please see
<http://www.smoe.org/meth/trajectory.html>, or e-mail me, your humble
listowner, at owner-trajectory@smoe.org.

Hope to see lots of you on the list!
