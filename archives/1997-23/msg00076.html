To the person who inquired, Breaking Glass came out in 1982, according 
to this book I happen to possess, called New Women in Rock!

I was playing at a bookstore a couple of years ago (I'm an ecto/gaffa 
kind of singer/songwriter/pianist) and it happened to be behind the 
stage area, propped up on a shelf.  I immediately claimed it for my own.

It was published in 1982 and is the most amazing collection of women at 
that point in time:  Hazel O' Connor, Sheena Easton, B-52's, Annie 
Lennox, The Roches, Marianne Faithfull, Jayne (!) County, Cherry 
Vanilla, Joan Armatrading, The Raincoats, Nina Hagen, Lene Lovich, Patti 
Smith, and many many many more, including KATE!  

If enough people are interested and I have some free time, I could type 
up and post the article about Kate - it was written as she was working 
on her third album.  Just let me know...
