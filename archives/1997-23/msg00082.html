Without picking particular songs, I would find it interesting to hear
a KaTe interpretation of works by the following artists:

- Heart (How would she handle, for example, Baracuda?)
- Jimi Hendrix (?! - but The Watchtower comes to mind. Could KaTe do it
  without affectation?)
- Edith Piaf (God, I would love to hear a modernization of her stuff! I
  can hear Kate singing Rien, Je ne regrette rien (sp) in the back of
  my brain as I'm writing this.  And I haven't even heard Piaf's in
  nearly 30 years. Sooo many to choose from....)
- David Bowie (My wife and son like his voice, but I'm always wondering
  what someone like KaTe could do with his music)
- David Byrne (Betcha all the work it would take would be worth it)
