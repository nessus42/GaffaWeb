I agree that "The Whole Story" is a great place to start, but if you are
looking for her best studio album buy "Hounds of Love." IMHO, this is
the best album of all time, and definitely Kate's best one. All Music
Guide On-line gives an excellent review of it and is good reference for
the others as well.
