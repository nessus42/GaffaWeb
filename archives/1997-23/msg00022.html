 Utterly amazing, and awe inspiring. The 9th Wave brought chills back to me. 

The sound is far better than the original. Most of the instruments are clear now. The bass very penetrating. The drum tracks almost crystal clear, and the stereo separation is much improved. Almost sounds as warm and inviting as the old LP. 

Cheers to EMI! I was thoroughly impressed. Those who are complaining about it are nit-picking IMHO.
