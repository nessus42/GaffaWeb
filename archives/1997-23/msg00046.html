...What I always found particularly interesting (BTW I haven't seen any
of Peter Gabriel's videos since about 1987) is the contrast between
"Don't Give Up" and "Sledgehammer". The songs themselves have moods
almost 180 degrees apart--and I'm sure that was intentional; likewise,
the videos are almost 180 apart in style. The "hug" video was beautiful
in its minimalist simplicity (note, however, one thematic
difference--the video finishes on a note of hope, as the eclipse passes,
while this is not necessarily true of the song); "Sledgehammer" was a
big hit precisely because it was anything *but* minimalist.
