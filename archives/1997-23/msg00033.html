Hi all.....
	While I had a spare moment at work the other day, I checked through the
cover of Squiggle's 'Gold' album (the term 'squiggle' not meant to offend -
but I can't draw him.....) for the reference to KT, and couldn't find it
anywhere!! Consequently, my boss gave me much slack for believing
everything I hear on the net! He is always looking for a chance to slag the
net/newsgroups and KT - personally I believe he is afraid of 'deeper
understanding' - be it musically, or technology-wise.  :)

	I also listened (no - strained to hear) to KT's contribution on Squiggle's
'Emancipation'. Well. Well well.  :)

	Anyone heard Ani di Franco? There are several albums of hers - recently
made available here in NZ - and they're really cool. Okay, so I've only
listened to one...........She's done the cool thing and defied the dominant
record labels......Go Girl!!!...even still it's easier to get hold of her
work than it is to get Happy Rhoads!! 

	Good night all.....
