I just had an idea.  I know that several of us have personal web pages,
and I also know that many contain some Kate content.  Would anyone be
interested in starting a Kate Bush Webring so that we could all find out
a little more about the people we read posts from each day, as well as
provide another resource for Kate fans on the net (plus it will help
bump up our counters).  I'll admit that I just recently found out about
Webrings, so if you have no idea what I am talking about, go here:
http://www.webring.com/what.html

My desire to do this probably stems from those many years of thinking
that no one else had a clue who Kate Bush was and why she is special.
My page is in it's infancy with virtually no content, so I will be
adding the address to my sig file soon.

Anyway, that's the idea.  Anyone else interested?

BTW - Yesterday I posted that the latest Entertainment Weekly magazine
described the Jewel song "Foolish Games" as "pure Kate Bush".  Well...I
just heard the song on the radio, and I think the writer meant to say
"kind of 'Little Earthquakes' era Tori Amos".  Obviously it was just a
simple typo on his/her part. ;-)
