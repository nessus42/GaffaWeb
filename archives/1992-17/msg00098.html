Well, I saw this on television, and I have to admit, she is very good:  she
certainly puts a lot of drama into her songs.  I wouldn't call it the most
amazing performance I have ever seen (that goes to Christy Moore!!! - but how I
would love to see Nurset Fateh Ali Khan, live!) but it is certainly ONE of the
best.

I normally don't watch 'Tonight Live' because I can't stand Steve Vizard, but
on Fridays, comedian Richard Stubbs hosts the show and he is a treat!  What the
above forgot to mention that Stubbsy did have a little chat with Tori after the 
song:  apparently she has been touring since OCTOBER!  She strikes me as a very
serious person and is cautious when humour is introduced into the conversation.
(but then Stubbsy is a bit of a galah, and that usually puts any unaquainted
American off guard).

Alas, I think Tori is in Australia strictly for a promotional tour (and an
occasional sing).  If she'll have a concert here, I will definately go.
