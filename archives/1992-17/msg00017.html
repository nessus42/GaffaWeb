Hi!
 
        Then, of course, there is Dolly Deluxe, a duo of Swedish female 
singers who put out Rock vs Opera, which is a strange mix (litterally!) of  
Opera music, and Rock music. Very nice though...and the main singer is 
operacticaly trained...so she tends to "hit" notes, rather than just slide 
into them. They also were working on something called "Which Witch", which  
(if you can excuse the alliteration :-) ) is about the atrocities of the 
Church's Witch Genocide during the Middle Ages...and it is a Rock Opera! I  
have one interview they did on Much Music, along with the videos for "Queen 
of the Night/Satisfaction" off of Rock vs Opera, and "Eternally", from 
"Which Witch". They are definitely on  topic here, since they are extreamly 
influcenced by Kate Bush, and won a local radio station competition with a  
tape of 13 Kate Bush covers, and one original. So if anyone else has info on 
what  they are doing, and of a release for "Which Witch", I would muchly 
appreciate it!
