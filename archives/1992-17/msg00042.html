Actually, it's not as good as it sounds.  I haven't seen this on
lovehounds (I may have missed it, I've been busy lately), so I
thought I'd pass it on ...

I was reading through Beat Box, which is a tiny music-magazine on
one of the teletext services over here, and the following came
up.  Reproduced without permission.

	"Earthrise - The Rainforest Album, the official Earth
	Summit album, is released on June 1st.

	Out on the Polygram TV label, the album contains tracks
	from U2, REM, Queen and Seal, among others.

	It will also feature specially recorded tracks performed
	by a supergroup made up of such luminaries as Kim Wilde,
	Donna Summer, *KATE BUSH*, Brian May and members of XTC.
	All royalties will go to a number of environmental
	charities."

So, she's on an album, but it doesn't look like she's got a separate
track all to herself!  I guess I'll have to wait for KB-VII ...
