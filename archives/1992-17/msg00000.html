For so long I have been fascinated by female vocalists in the strongest way.
It probably started with Roberta Flack when I was just a wee one but I 
have been growing more and more one sided in my tastes in the last few 
years.


I must thank you all for introducing me to Tori Amos.  After hearing about
her so much on this news group I, in quick succession listend to, bought
and really listened to her album, and went to an excellent concert.  
(In San Deigo).  She is amazingly honest and expressive.
I was wondering if any of you knew of her summer plans in regards to
touring and appearences.  If I could catch another of her shows It would 
be great.  I did see the Dennis Miller appearence and thought it was 
good, esp. the "Silent all these Years" rendition.  But I did feel that 
it looked kind of rushed.  Also I was hoping that she would get some 
interview time as well

BTW has anyone heard of Julianna Hatfield, I realize that she is the 
antithesis of beautiful music, but she has some of the most honest 
and compelling song about the down side of love I've ever heard.
