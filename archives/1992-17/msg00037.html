Ok, so I'm still fairly new to this computer-y junk, at least this GREAT BIG
ALL AROUND THE WORLD computer-y stuff, but anyway.

I just had to write something to thank you people!

I'm a BIG BIG BIG Kate fan, so I was really axious to link up with this news
service and find out what I could.
And, well, I was really sorta' annoyed when I started reading to hear about
this Tori Amos person.  All I wanted was KaTe.  
Stories about KaTe.
The LIFE of KaTe.
Masses in honour of KaTe.
Masses OF KaTe.

But instead, it was this Tori person.

But I kept on reading, finding out a few nice new juicy things...
and getting more curious about this Tori person.

I usually don't watch M(t)TV, but I started to just to see if I could see this
woman sing.

At 12:54 pm the other day, I saw and heard the video for Silent All These
Years.
By 1:30 I had gone to Cat's Records, bought the tape (they didn't have the CD),
come back home, and was half-way through the first side!

THANK YOU!
Thankyou!
Thanks to EVERYONE who RAVED about this woman!

Voice like KaTe, words like Linda Paston, playing like the thoughts of God!!!



But I missed her in Atlanta?!?
Oh, cruel fate!!!
Is she coming near Knoxville anytime soon?!?


I think I'll listen to you people more often!
