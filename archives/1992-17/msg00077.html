Little Fluffy Clouds -> Pal Joey
Back Side Of The Moon -> Steve Hillage
Spanish Castles In Space -> Youth (yeah, the same one who played bass on
                   "The Big Sky" - how's that for tenuous Kate content?)
Huge Ever Growing Brain -> Cauty/Paterson
Perpetual Dawn -> The Orb

I don't know why the damn thing is called The Aubrey Mixes.  Really.
