Andy Marvick (IED) traditionally sends Kate flowers (white roses)
on her birthday.  Two years ago he asked love-hounds if they wanted
to contribute money, and get their names on the card.  Enough people
came through for IED to send five dozen roses!

confirmed that the flowers were sent, no reply from the Bush people
has ever come.

But if you want to organize such a gift this time, that would be
great.  IED would probably be able to tell you about costs.
