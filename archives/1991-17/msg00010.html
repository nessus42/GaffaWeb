From: barger@aristotle.ils.nwu.edu (Jorn Barger)

Subject: Happy news from Vickie

Happy and Kevin send thanks to all Love-Hounds for all the support!

I just picked up the Warpaint CD from the Rose Records on Davis in
Evanston-- priced at $14.99.

Vickie says Kevin says the following stations have copies of Warpaint (so
call up and request something):

KCRW Santa Monica (playing "Waking Up" a lot during the morning)
WEQX Manchester Vt (played at least once on their new-music show)
KLAX Berkeley
WXTN Philadelphia
WDET Detroit
WBCO Boulder
KITS San Francisco
WXRT Chicago (312-777-8881) (their powers-that-be will be giving it a trial
listen in two weeks)

soon:
KIRA Dallas
KGSR Austin
B-Side Records, Madison WI

The Tower deal is temporarily on hold, at Happy's behest, for strategic
reasons.

Vickie expects the lyrics to meander across eventually, and warns that she
found a typo in "One Alien" on Vol. 2: 'you' should be 'your'.  Make a note
if you plan to print out a copy.
