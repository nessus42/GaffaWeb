Here is a list of the GIFs that I have posted for Ken Saint John.
Ken can explain his scanning process better than I.  Most of these GIFs 
look like 320 x 400 in 256 colors to my machine although Ken says they
were scanned in 640x400 in 256 colors.  

I also have a whole set of pics from the boxed set and the _Cathy_ book
that Ken did but I seem to be having problems making uploads to the
archives right now.  When I I get the problem resolved I'll let you
know.

KBUSH1.GIF 

A young Kate Bush with a big smile.  This is in grey tones. 


KBUSH2.GIF

KBUSH3.GIF 

Picture of Kate Bush, her mom and two brothers.  Grey scale.


KBUSH4.GIF 

A close up of Kate Bush taken from the book _The Most Beautiful Women

those beautiful eyes!


KBUSH5.GIF

KBUSH6.GIF 

Color GIF of Kate Bush with her hair permed ala Lionheart.

KBUSH8.GIF

KBUSH11.GIF 

A pic of Kate Bush from the _Love and Anger_ video.


KBUSH12.GIF 

A series of four pics from Kate Bush's _Army Dreamers_ video.

KBUSH13.GIF 

4 shots from the "Experiment IV," video.  Kate getting into the van at

KBUSH14.GIF 

This is scenes from the "Don't Give Up (Version 1)" video from Peter
