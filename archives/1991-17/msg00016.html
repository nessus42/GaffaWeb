 I just picked up another picture interview album and am hoping someone
here can help me out with a question I have concerning it. This album
is titled "KaTe Bush-Abbey Roads Studio Interview", it was pressed in
England and has the number 1652 printed on side one in rather bold
type, the photo on side one is _very_ similar to the photo on my "TKI"
picture album, Kate is wareing a kind of emerald green outfit emerging
form a silver cylinder. Like I said very similar to the shot on "TKI"
but different. On side two we have KaTe sitting is a chair clutching
her knees, she has on a pink sweater and a string of what look to be
orchards around her neck. Both of these are lovely lovely photos.
 What I would really like to know is this. Who is the gentleman
conducting the interview? Aside from asking some of the more
intellectual questions I've ever heard presented to KaTe Our Lady Of
The Obsessed seemed to genuinely like him. My 2nd and final question
is if #1652 is run number anyone know how many of these albums were
pressed? Thanks in advance!
