        I got the "Rocket Man/Candle In The Wind" single!  (finally)  I
thought "Candle In The Wind" was better then "Rocket Man", though not quite up
to Kate's standards.  The encouraging news (for me) is that the latter song is
the better song,  which hopefully bodes well for the new album.  The
interesting thing is that with most of Kate's songs, I find it difficult to
listen to them in the background while doing something else.  Both of these
songs make good "atmosphere" music for me.  The noise at 0:58 on CiTW is quite
annoying though, especially on the instrumental version.   Aside from that
major flaw, they sound much cleaner and seem to have more space then the songs
from The Sensual World. 
        The cover photos, for those who don't know, are from the same sessions
as some of the photos from the latest, Sensual World era, issue of the KBC
newsletter.  I think they look great, but not as good as the one on the cover
of the newsletter, where she is "kissing" the camera.  :-) 

        Also got Neil Youngs live WELD video and his ARC album, which is
twenty-five minutes of pure feedback!  Now THAT'S Music!
