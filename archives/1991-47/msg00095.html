Absolutely!  The very first time I heard this track, I thought "that
reminds me of Marilyn Monroe".  For those of you who may not have heard
Marilyn Monroe sing, she had a very poignant (that's a kind word) singing
voice.  I'm sure Kate had this in mind when recording CitW, which is, after
all, a tribute to/requiem for MM (nee Norma Jean Baker).

BTW, late at night, last weekend, Rocket Man finally clicked with me;
before I quite liked it, now I love it.  I think I must have been in just
the right sort of aimless dreamy mood, and I turned on the player without
remembering what was in it.  Preconceptions are are hard thing to overcome.
