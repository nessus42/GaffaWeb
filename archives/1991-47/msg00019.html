>I'm also looking for info on a band called Single Gun Theory, on Nettwerk
>records. I tend to associate this label with Skinny Puppy and such dreck,
>but SGT had a very cool-sounding video on 120 Mins a while back. 

Single Gun Theory, last I heard, are on Nettwork, and recently released
a record, don't recall the name.  Their sound is remarkably consistent
between releases without becoming dreadfully boring.  Thus they avoid the
usual pitfalls of Nettwork artists (IMHO).  My favorite of theirs is from
a few years back, "Exorcise this Wasteland," a single and I think the title
of a very solid album.
