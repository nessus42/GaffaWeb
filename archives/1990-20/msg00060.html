WHOA!  I think this is a big mistake.  Kate (is it o.k. if I call her by
her first name?  She doesn't even know me!) created those albums the way
she wanted to.  If she added a section of music for the video version,
fine.  But that does not belong on the album.  Same goes for the first
item.  IF the extra music was originally excluded against Kate's wishes,
then that's another story.
I doubt this is the case.  Leave the existing albums alone.
Please do not suggest that they tamper with the albums, IED, it really
casts doubts on the validity of your whole letter.  Everything else you
say makes sense.

Well, just about everything.  I guess I would have indicated some sort
of preference.  For example, tracks from the Hammersmith concert would
be infinitely more preferable than her versions of the Beatles songs. 
Similarly, the
instrumental versions of existing songs are not as important as the
other b-sides.
(I just have this fear that they'll include some instrumentals and not
put "December Will Be..." on it.)

Those are my suggestions for the letter.
