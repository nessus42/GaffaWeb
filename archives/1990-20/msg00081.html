Guess who's got a new book out?  Fred and Judy Vermorel!  Yes,
the same folks who brought us _Kate Bush: Princess of Suburbia_ and
_The Secret History of Kate Bush (and the Strange Art of Pop)_ have
a new book, called _Fandemonium_.  Those of you who have read their
earlier works can imagine what this one's like.  Page after page of
weird things fans have done.  Fortunately, Kate is mentioned only once,
very briefly.  They have a paragraph about the guy who saved bath
water from Kate's outdoor overflow pipe.

In the "About The Authors", it says that the Vermorels "perpetrated
books about Kate Bush. . ."  I think "perpetrated" is an excellent
choice of word.
