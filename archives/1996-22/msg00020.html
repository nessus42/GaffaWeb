Her albums in the U.S. numbered 4 or 5, three on Warner/Reprise and 1 or 2
on Arista.  The first Arista, "Not A Little Girl Anymore" is by far the
most common, since it had a minor hit or two on it. It's a nice album, but
much more "pop" than her earlier records.  The Warner stuff, "Lark" and
"Fathoms Deep," as well as an anthology which name I can't remember at the
moment, are less common, but they had their fans at the time of release,
and should be able to be found in well-stocked used record stores.  I do
not know if there are any Linda Lewis CDs, from the US or UK--but I'd like
to find out!

Good luck!
