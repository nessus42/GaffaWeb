According to information by Simone Monetti on the 'Libertine
mailinglist' these will be the songs Mylene will perform during
her concerts.
California - Vertige - L'instant X - XXL - Rever - Tomber 7
fois - Et tournoie - Mylene s'en fout - Desenchantee -
Que mon coeur lache - Beyond my control - Sans
contrefacon - Douces - Tristana - Sans logique -
Libertine

Possibly she will sing two other songs during a concert.
Regrets will be sung (with Jean-Louis Murat) and a Kate Bush
cover *****The fog*****.

Simone's information also says that the concert video will be
released at the end of this year. It will be recorded at the 1st
June concert in Bercy Paris. 
===========================================

Mylene Farmer is best known for her excellent music videos. They are really,
really good. Very sensitive, fanciful and creative. And sexy! :-)
