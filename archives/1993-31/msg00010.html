looking for. She heard it frequently on the radio here in
Germany, but she does not know who the singer is.

that it is not Kate Bush. But I found it somehow similar
to songs of Kate. So I could imagine that people listening
to Kate Bush also have records of the female singer I am

least part of it) goes like this:

"...sometimes I turn into a raven, sometimes I'm a fox..."

Any help is appreciated. Thank you.

P.S. I tried this before on rec.music.misc, and the only suggestion
