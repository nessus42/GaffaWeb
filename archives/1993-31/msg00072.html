Oh....I forgot all about it...until now.

Willie Nelson was interviewed briefly on MovieTelevision....part of City TV's
entertainment dynasty here in T.O.   He was filming a movie...er ...acting in
one ....somewhere in B.C.  He was asked about his unlikely duet with Sinnead
O'Connor.  He said that it was an accidental.  Someone else was supposed to
sing the female lead....but that person backed out at the last moment.  They
had already booked the time and studio.....and somehow Sinnead was brought in.
I wonder who the original choice was?

This interview occurred some 3 or 4 weeks ago.  Sorry if someone else mentioned
it.
