>Monday, October 4, 1993
>_The_Line,_the_Cross,_the_Curve_

>(1993) dir Kate Bush w/ Kate Bush, Miranda Richardson, Lindsay Kemp,
>Stewart Arnold
>This new film from acclaimed musician/dancer Kate Bush is based upon
>the classic "The Red Shoes," and features six songs from Kate's
>upcoming Columbia Records release "The Red Shoes".

   I called the theatre and got the name of the booking agent. The agent
told me that Columbia "four-walled" the theatre (i.e. bought every seat
for promo purposes) and she was dealing with a man from Columbia. I
called the CBS guy and asked about getting the film at my local theatre.
His assistant said that they already have a Chicago showing planned,
and to call back Friday for the date and place. I called a friend who is
a booker for a theatre in Kansas City, and gave him the number.
