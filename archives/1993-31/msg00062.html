This month's Record Collector has a few ads mentioning some
interesting forthcoming foreign releases:

- Key Mail order of London lists a German "Eat the Music" CD-single
for L6.99

- Esprit of Kent list a Japanese CD3 of "Eat the Music" for L9.99, and
the Japanese release of the album on CD for L20.99

- Revolution of Middlesex have a Japanese "Eat the Music" (CD5 this
time), cat number TOCP-8014, release 17-9-93, for L10.99.

Looks like "Rubbberband Girl" is UK-only.
