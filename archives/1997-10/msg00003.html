Hello:

It's been a very long time since I've posted on this newsgroup--rare as 
it was back then--except for the sale of my Cathy Demos red vinyl (which, 
I'm sure, the present owner is enjoying immensely).

Well today, I went into a CD store, and had found what is, perhaps, a 
bootleg (never owned one before, but I'm sure it is). It's a CD called 
"Cathy's Home Demos." Forgive me if it may be old news, but it is to me.

It has 29 tracks lasting about 75 minutes--the first 24 are from her home 
demos back in 1975. It sounds as if they were lifted off vinyl and onto 
DAT tape. You may hear a few pops here and there, but for the most part 
this is quite, quite beautiful!

If anyone wishes more info on this, let me know. As I said, I had not 
been here in awhile--so this *may* be old news (though the jacket said 
"new" and "imported").
