The Man I Love is also a goody, but I'm trying to remember if she's done
any more covers.  My Lagan Love (another "traditional") and the Whole
Story version of "Wuthering Heights" if you want to be super-anoraky!

Oh, oh, oh, Candle in the Wind and the track from Common Ground (yet
another traditional - forgive me I can't remember how to spell it and I
don't want to insult anyone!), Let it Be, Long & Winding Road, She's
Leaving Home ...   I love it when Kate covers a track because it takes
her into the realm of another's art and it's always interesting to see
how she interprets it.

OK - my top five would be (at the moment).

1.	Rocket Man
2.	Mna mna .... (Common Ground, you know the one I mean)
3.	My Lagan Love
4.	The Man I Love
5.	Lord of the Reedy River
