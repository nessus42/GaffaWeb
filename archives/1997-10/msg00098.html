I'm originally from New York, but I moved to Jerusalem a year ago.  
I'm a 26-year-old male, and I've been a really big fan of Kate since 
I was in Junior High School, or something like that.  I first got 
into her music in around 1984 or so when I saw a couple of her videos 
on some obscure, short-lived UHF video station.  Since then, I've 
always loved her music.  Her albums are kinda like old friends for 
me.

Anyway, though, I was wondering if there are any other fans of hers 
living in my new country.  I hear Wuthering Heights on the radio here 
occasionally, and everyone seems to know who Kate Bush is (more so 
than in NY!) but I don't really know anyone who's very familiar with 
all of her work.  Is anyone else on this list from Israel, or at 
least in Israel?

Just a curiosity of mine, but if anyone on the list is in the 
neighborhood, drop me a line!
