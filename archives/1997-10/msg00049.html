Kate is working on her next album, since mid-1996.
Currently she's booked in at Abbey Road, the recordings 
there are expected to be finished in July.
A release this year seems possible. 

Musicians so far:
  David Gilmour (guit)
  Danny McIntosh (guit)
  John Giblin (bass)
  Stuart Elliot (dr)
  Manu Katche (dr)

Songs:
  Round In My Mirror
     with celtic instruments and a rhythm part by Stuart Elliot
  Open Your Sight
     features a long guitar solo by David Gilmour

David Gilmour said it's very different from her last album.
(Karen has promised to post a translation of the Gilmour
interview, so we'll soon know what he really said :-)
