Hi,

a while ago I posted a question about the inscription in the
runout groove space of the UK "The Kick Inside" LP, I didn't 
believe it was actually "Remember yourself".
Now I know for sure: two variants exist, 
copies of matrix# YAX-5389-3 have REMEMBER YOURSELF,
copies of # YAX-5389-2 have REMBER YOURSELF [sic!],
so whoever etched it in left out two characters.

Another thing that's AFAIK not yet reported, the message
from the Rubberband Girl 7" single: BACK TO LIFE

That was today's issue of "Things you really didn't want to know".
