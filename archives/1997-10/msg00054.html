Meanings of The Infant Kiss and Breathing?  Well, I suppose it is time to
reveal the hidden knowledge of these esoteric texts.  

The Infant Kiss is actually based on a secret Hermetic text, passed on
verbally from Master to Initiate for thousands of years, about the Healing
Kiss of the Sacred Infant -- not really an infant at all, but a
manifestation of . . . well, I may say no more without fears of hideous
reparations by those who protect such knowledge.  Just check out the ending
of 2001 A Space Odyssey.

Of course, Breathing is an entirely different story -- as we all know the
song is a thinly disguised message about the origins of humanity on an
ocean planet orbiting the star Altair.  Yes, it's true, man and dolphin
were once the same creature, brought to Earth by our interstellar
progenitors.  There are dozens of books on the subject. Just ask your local
FBI agent.
