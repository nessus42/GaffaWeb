Just some observations...

>      Q:  What does "Gaffa" mean?
>      A:  Duct tape. [see also 1.1.]

There is a cockney (London slang) usage for "Gaffa" (well that's how
it is pronouced, more than "Gaffer" - like "wanna" than "Want to")
which means "a boss/supervisor/manager".

>      Q:  When is Kate's next album coming out?
>      A:  Your guess is as good as mine.

I calculated Nov 97 (going by the release dates of the others - excluding
boxed sets, collections, etc).

>      Q:  Even at length?
>      A:  Yes.  Well, don't go TOO far - posting multi-megabyte discographies is
>          frowned on (and yes, people have done that).  Use your judgement.
>          [see also 1.1.]

As long as they're not magazine listing either!
