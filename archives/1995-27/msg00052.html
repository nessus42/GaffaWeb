Hello Eric:
 I spotted that disc about a year ago in a Chicago store. I thought $20 
 was a bit steep, so I let it go. later, on a friend's recommendation, 
 I started looking for it, and found a copy, last month, at the same store,
 but this time for $12! I scooped it up and have quite enjoyed it.

Some have the material has been transcribed into some of the various
Kate journals that I've encountered, sometimes refered to as a bootleg
CD interview.  The store I found it at is fairly meticulous about
not selling known bootlegs, though. As to rarity: it seems to be
hard to find, but rareness is usually in the eye of the collector,
to mis-quote somebody or other

Hope this helps.
