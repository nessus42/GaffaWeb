   On behalf of the entire TapeTree video crew, you are welcome.

   As difficult as it is to watch, it was even more painful in person.
We were "lucky" enough to be sitting on a couch in the control room
during the interview. Vickie and myself, Mike Weaver and a fellow Kate
Fan from Virgina and John Reimers were all biting our lips and sitting
on our collective hands to avoid running into the studio and throttling
the dippy "interviewer." This is as close as I have ever seen Kate get
to anger. 

   The saga of the Night Flight interview is a pretty good example of
the Bush Family view on publicity and privacy. Cynthia Friedland, the
producer of Night Flight sent John Reimers her 3/4" "window dub"
videotape. Naturally we were interested in getting a copy. John
Reimers had developed a friendship with Kate's brother John Carder
Bush. For some reason not clear to me, John felt compelled to ask
JCB if it was ok to make a copy of this tape for us. JCB responded
in the classic Bush Family manner "well, we would prefer that you
didn't." It wasn't until later, when a non-window dub (but much
lower quality) version showed up on tape trading lists that we were
able to get a copy for distribution.
