Okay, everyone.  I have been lurking around here for months, and I have learned
a lot and have come to appreciate Kate's music even more than I did before. 
But, I think we are losing sight of the point of the group.  We are here to talk
about Kate.  I understand, believe me, how frustrating it can be to feel like a
newbie, and I also understand how difficult it is to catch the flow of a
previously engaged conversation, but sometimes we just have to be patient.  If
there is someone around who knows more than you, just accept it and learn from
them.  I have.  And then we can all get back to arguing over which album is best
and remembering the first time we heard a Kate Bush song.
