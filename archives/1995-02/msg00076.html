So I've been trying to get Kate's home page for weeks.  I had it
a few months back, and one of our daughters wanted a KaTe Christmas.
Santa brought her three good albums, plus "The Line, etc...", but he
was *supposed* to bring her biographical and discography/videography
info too!

It's true, Santa was stunted by an Internet failure.

She had a merry one anyway, but if any of you swell persons would
be so kind as to email me any of the abovementioned documents I
would be ever-so-obliged.

BTW, I*M*HO, The Line et al, was an extremely well produced bit
(for what it was).  Kate is definitely somebody to be reckoned with, 
and it was just wonderful to see her on the screen after all this time.
Granted, it's continuity left room for desire, and there were a couple
places where I couldn't help but wonder if her alliance with Coca-Cola
and Fruitopia wasn't showing  :-)  but all in all it was most enjoyable,
and I'm glad to have a copy of it... er, I mean I'm glad our daughter
has a copy of it.

Please send me general KaTe files at my email address below.

Thanks in advance.
