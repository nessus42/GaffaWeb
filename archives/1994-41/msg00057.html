Hi all. Bit or an anorak posting.

I just added what I hope is the ability to send mpeg audio to the www
cloudbusting. If anyone out there is set up to access the web and play
mpeg audio if it's there, could they have a go at

	http://www.cogsci.ed.ac.uk/~rjc/hyper_cloud/music/cloudbusting.html

and click on the litle cassette icon. If your client can handle
mpeg-audio you should get 41Ksamples/sec 16 bit stereo (compressed
quite a lot, but still quite reasonable on my PC at home), if not you
should get boring 8K ulaw as ever.

Only the cloudbusting sample is there at the moment.

Anyway, I'd like to see what people think, if it works, what the
quality is like on something better than the Sun I have here etc.
