I bought Sandra Bernhard's `Excuses for bad behaviour part 1' the
other day, and it's so good I thought I'd pass on a
reccomendation. It's not stand-up stuff, it's an album of songs
(though I had to find it in the spoken-word section of the shop...).

Definitly worth finding a relative who doesn't know what to buy you
for christmas:-).

BTW, a warning to those Love Hounds with a phobia about mention of
religious subjects: there's a really good cover of `Sympathy for the
Devil'.
