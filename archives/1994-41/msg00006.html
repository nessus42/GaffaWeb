Hey, hang on a minute before this all gets totally out of hand. 
What exactly is your problem with the assertion that Chris'
opinion is as valid as those held by the Tarot guys?  What leads
you to interpret that sentence as demeaning?  I think you're
deliberately misinterpreting what Vickie meant.  If she had
emphasised the word "certainly", it could have led you to believe
she was putting down the Tarot advocates.  But, she goes out of
her way to state that she (and Chris), are not out to stop them. 
Unfortunately you forgot to include that part of her post.

If you look at Chris' original article, nowhere does he say the
Tarot project should be stopped.  All he does is point out why, in
his opinion, it is unlikely to be well received by KaTe.  That
seems like a pretty tolerant attitude to me. 

>I have said it before, and I will keep on repeating it as long as

Sorry, I don't agree at all.  *I* have found this group to be a
particularly warm and welcoming community, though I'm sure I'll
get flamed after this post.

Come on guys, lighten up - it's nearly Christmas.
