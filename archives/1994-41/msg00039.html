Hello there! Well, I've had my fair share of flames, and I presume that many 
people on this newsgroup & digest have too. Let's stop all this nonsense re:
Kate & tarot/arguments about insidious `Lily' influences etc., and just get 
back to the MUSIC!!!! I wish to start a thread (& you can call me an old
tart if you want) about the influence of fairy tales/Donovan/Roy Harper on
Kate. The last two I know pitifully little about (esp. Roy) bar that they
have had a profound influence on Kate's music. Personally I find albums such
as `Never Forever' quite MAGICAL, & I don't mean MAGICKAL (though if I did
what does it matter?) -- what do others think? Post & mail to your 
(collective) hearts' content, I want to know all!

Cheers

Ben
