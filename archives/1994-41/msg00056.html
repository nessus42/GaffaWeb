Um, I agree. I'd also like to point out that, despite all we try to know
about her, we know so _very_ little about the life and times of Kate. Yes,
I know that there is a "Lily" speaking on this track, but other than that,
this story of Lily's instruction to Kate in the Mystic Arts(tm) could be
cut from wholecloth. A fiction. A fantastic fabrication. For example, was
Kate ever married to Hary Houdini? She sung about it, it must be true! Was
Kate ever lost at sea? Was Kate ever impregnated by her brother? (At least
that is what the Kick Inside seems to be about...) Did she ever dance with
a Nazi in 1939? (Mind you, if any of those things are true, then we'd have
cause to worry about her. Me, I'm content with wishing she wouldn't smoke.)
