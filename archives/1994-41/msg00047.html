3:   My first Kate:  For me, the intro to Kate was a gradual thing: I was
inundated with Kate music for months by Mike Weaver (thanks Mike!), when
we were next door neighbors at Scott AFB.  Finally, it sunk in and I became
a Konvert, but too late for the Canadian BushCon, which I canceled out of
to my regret, and too late for Mike & I to really explore Kate music
together.  I guess the single song which really hooked me was Night of The
Swallow, although Babooshka and Army Dreamers and December Will Be Magic
Again and Man With the Child In His Eyes and Wuthering Heights had quite
a bit to do with it.    

Merry Christmas to all and a Happy New Year to all in rec.music.gaffa.
