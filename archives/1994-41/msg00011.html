12 Days of Katemas

Subject: 12 Days of Katemas

In the spirit of the holidays, I woke up with the following sugarplum 
vision dancing in my head:

On the 12th day of Katemas my Love Hounds gave to me --

Twelve sufis whirling,

Eleven clouds a' busting,

Ten Fruitopias spinning,

Nine waves a' roaring,

Eight Homeground coffees,

Seven Kate Bush albums,

Six big wheels at Howth Head,

FIVE WUTHERING HEIGHTS,

Four secret Experiments,

Three beeps for trouble,

Two Red Shoes,

and One Rubberband that won't hold me up.
