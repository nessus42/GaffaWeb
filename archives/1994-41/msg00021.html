In a previous article, kln@crl.com ("Karen L. Newcombe") says:

>
>I would like to suggest that if one of us questions the ideas or postings 
>of another Love Hound, that we send a private e-mail before posting 
>generally.  Some things should be hammered out in a public forum, other 
>questions could be straightened out easily through e-mail between two people.

Here's another:  When responding to a posting you find offensive, make sure
that your response is at least _one line shorter_ than the previous message
in the thread.  In time, when you are down to one-liners, decrease your
line-length by one word.  In this way, we can all look forward to the end of
disputes in finite, measurable lengths of time!

An observation: the lengthier a response to a "hot" topic is, and the more
impassioned the tone, the less sympathy people will tend to have for it.
