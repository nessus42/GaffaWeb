Kate gets publicity!

well, not much actually, but german music tv channel Viva had a short news 
clip in their videotex (page 115 yesterday, but it's gone already today):

Kate: Produzent begeistert

 Kate Bush liebt irische Frauen - zumindest in ihrem Liedgut.
Fuer den Sampler "Common Ground" nahm sie den traditionellen 
gaelischen Song "Mna Na Heireann" (Women Of Ireland) auf.
Es ist fuer Kate die erste Produktion seit drei Jahren.
Ebenfalls auf dem Album vertreten: U2, Elvis Costello, Crowded House.
 Produzent Donal Lunny war von der Zusammenarbeit mit Kate Bush 
begeistert. "Sie arbeitet so lange, bis alles perfekt im Kasten ist."

[clumsy translation follows:]

Kate: Producer enthusiastic

 Kate Bush loves Irish women - at least in her repertoire.
For the sampler "Common Ground" she recorded the traditional 
gaelic song "Mna Na Heireann" (Women Of Ireland).
It's the first production for Kate in three years.
[didn't she do "The Man I Love" in 1994?]
Also featured on the album: U2, Elvis Costello, Crowded House.
 Producer Donal Lunny was very enthusiastic about the cooperation
with Kate Bush: "She works until everything's perfect."
