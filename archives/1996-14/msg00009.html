Hi Violet--thanks for your input!  'twas quite helpful... until I was taking
some cold-medicine tonight for my nasty little head-cold.  As the burning
menthol liquid drizzled down my throat, it suddenly occured to me what the
Trio are actually singing.  I ran to my CD player, and cued up the CD to the
2:50 and 2:56 marks in "Deeper Understanding" and the 2:10 mark in "Rocket's
Tail" and upon a more concentrated/focused listening session, I discovered my
cold-medicine ephiphany was correct:  the Trio are singing "Oh, Nyquil oh
Nyquil."  I can deeply understand their desire to eternalize and worship a
remedy such as this.  Tomorrow, when I wake up from my Nyquil-induced
hangover, I will have forgotten this entire event, and will have the joy of
discovering it again!  :)

P.S.  I'm naming my first-born male child "Aquila"--if I catch anyone else
"stealing" this name, I will have to force you to eternally listen to the
three seconds (or so) of the Trio's cute little promotion for Nyquil.
