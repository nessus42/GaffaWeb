	I know others have already answered your question, so I'll
just add this bit of 'almost' information:

	I actually did the chapter search for MGM/UA's laser disc
release of that film, which involves 'breaking' the story into
suitable segments and then giving them titles. Those titles are then
printed on the back of the laser disc sleeve.

	I used "The Wedding List" for the scene where she's in the
airport and pulls out a list of addresses (I think...it's been awhile).
Anyway...this little homage to Kate didn't make it to the final laser
release. I'm not sure why, but I was edited! (grrrr.)

	But they used all my other stuff -- even the other 'pop'
reference I used to 'Basic Instinct.' I titled the scene where she
gets herself put in prison in order to get close to the last victim
"Basic Inmate."   :-)
