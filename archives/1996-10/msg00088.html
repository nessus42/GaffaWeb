Dear friends,

	This may have been discussed all ready, but the German band Dark
Orange does a cover of "Man With the Child In His Eyes" on the CD
maxi-single of "Sounds of Silence" (yes, a cover of the Paul Simon
song).  It was distrubuted in 1992 by Hyperium/Rough Trade records and
is available through darkwave in California. 
	Both covers are well done and rather pretty, the Paul Simon cover
being rather Cocteau Twins-ish, and the KaTe cover being rather
straight forward.
