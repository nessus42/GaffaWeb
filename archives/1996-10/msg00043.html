_wow_ is the disc that claims to be the amsterdam show from the "tour
of life", but it is, in fact, yet another bootleg made from the audio
track of the hammersmith video. i don't recall the sound quality being
bad, but it's certainly not worth the money if you can find the "live
at the hammersmith odeon" video.

correction on this!)
