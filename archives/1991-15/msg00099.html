Subject: Rainbirds 3rd album


Noticing woj's recommendation for the Rainbirds I would like to add some
information.

Apart from the two albums mentioned their third album has just been
released one or two weeks ago. It is called 'Two Faces' and it's
advertised as a new debut album because the band has changed.

Actually only Katharina Franck remained of the original group, which
could not have a great effect, as she was the one who wrote and sang
the songs (and played the guitar as well).

So Rainbirds today are only Katharina, and Ulrike Haage on keyboards.
And a bigger emphasis on keyboards is the only difference I've noticed
so far.

The album was recorded in Los Angeles.

KaTe-fans might be interested to hear that this album also contains an
Houdini song:   Ha! Ha! Houdini's Laughing.
