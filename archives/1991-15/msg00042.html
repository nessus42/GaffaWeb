This is not surprising, considering that Happy Rhodes' albums are
not even distributed in her home country of the USA!  Remember, people,
as yet SHE HAS NO RECORD CONTRACT.  So all you people trying to find
her albums in record stores can pretty much forget it for now.  A *very*
few stores, mostly near Happy's home of Albany, stock them.  An 
encouraging sign is that Rose Records in Chicago has aparently agreed
to stock _Warpaint_.  I guess they must be ordering copies directly
from Aural Gratification, because Happy has no distributor in the normal
sense.
