A quick note to Jeff Burka, who was describing the Gabriel discs
and what songs appeared on each:

   The second Gabriel album (the scratch album) is probably best
known for the tracks 'DIY' and 'On the Air.'  The song 'I Go
Swimming' (NOT Fishing! :) ) appears on the Plays Live album,
and not on the second album.
   Just wanted to set the record somewhat straight.
