Patrick Ryan asks:

>What does she mean by "Are we doing it, can we have it all now?"
>Sorry, I forget the song name <duck to avoid flames>.

This is pretty well addressed in that quote I sent a long a few days ago--
the question is being asked by someone who's in a purgatory-like state...
they've seen the face of God (or some other bright and shiny wonderful thing),
had it taken away, and been told "be good and we'll let you see it again..."
Much like the character in "Sat in Your Lap," the character in "SiG" is
impatient, not wanting to sit through the [punishment] before having it all.
