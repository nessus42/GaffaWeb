Hi Folks!


Finally, after three months, I have been recognized as "The Kate Bush Man"
here at my work. It took me some trouble: hanging a copy of a photo out of
Kate Bush Complete in my cubicle and adding the text "Kate Bush"; using
the sleeve photo of The Sensual World as a background for my X-Windows
environment (was: Kate on Cloudbuster); and making an A3 colour poster of
Stev0's phrase: "You're going to ask me if I'm going to tour, aren't you?
Well, go ahead, punk. Make my day! -- Kate Bush, holding .44 magnum to
reporter's face". But, finally, after three months, it worked!
