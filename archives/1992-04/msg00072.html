Hi all,

    I just thought that'd drop another line to you all about how well KT and RM 
seems to be doing here. A lot of the people whom I work with  have come to me 
and said how much they like the song. These are the kind of people who remember 
HER as "that strange woman who did WH" and how they "thought she was dead" or 
"married and age 50"... >:-( you know those types whom have never seen the 
light.

    So anyway, RM seems to be getting a lot of air play and now it turns out 
that one of our biggest current affairs programms, the Today Show (just like the 
US version), is holding a "Rocket Man Competion" !! They keep showing bits of 
the RM video (I think that I've seen most of it now, in 30 second spurts!) and 
saying how in conjuction with the new video clip by Kate Bush the Today Show is 
going to let somebody win a trip to the US to see a Shuttle launch.

- Graham
