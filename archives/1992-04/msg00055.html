Boston area Love-hounds looking for the RM/CitW CD single
can find it at HMV in Harvard Square.  It's #23 (if I remember
correctly; maybe #13) on their CD single chart and cost me $9.99+tax.

I'd have to say I was disappointed with _CitW_.
In particular, I was quite distracted by the synth noises.
Kate was way down in the mix, too.
I think the song is great, but a rearrangement and remix
would help it out considerably.
(Disclaimer:  I don't think I've ever heard Elton John's version.)

I seem to remember there being talk about a _Love and Anger_
CD single.  Was that only in the UK?  I have the _Aspects..._
single, and it's fabulous (except for some of the "choir"
parts), but I've never seen a _Love and Anger_ single.
