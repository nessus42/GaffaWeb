Subject: The Kick Inside & Moving 

To complete my collection of the 5 KT albums on CD, I have
only to buy _TKI_.  All the record stores around here have the
"Tammy Wynette" version (a.k.a. Treasury Series) -- I could
probably pick it up for $12-$16 (CDN).

But I really don't like the cover of that CD, and based on my
experiences with _The Dreaming_, it's likely missing the lyrics
and liner notes.

On a weekend excursion out of town I stumbled into a record store
with lots of obscure records and CDs, and lo and behold they had
the UK import version of _TKI_ -- for $30! (about $25 US).  Before
I splurge, would someone tell me if this is a complete rip-off?

In the vinyl dept. of the same store was a 2-record set called
(I believe) _Moving_ which seemed to be a live album.  I've never
seen or heard of this before.  Think it was on red vinyl.  This
was $60!  Can anyone tell me what this is?
