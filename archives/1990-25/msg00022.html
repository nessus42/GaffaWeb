Hello,

I'm back again, I'm sure this info is well known by now but I haven't
seen it in Gaffa so here it is.

According to KBC _TWW_ will be a limited edition, pressing will be
7000 album and cd and 4000 cassette. It will be released in the UK on the
22nd October priced @#64.99 (album/cassette) and @#94.99 (cd). As we already
know the extra tracks will not be available separately. Apparently the set will
be dedicated to the memory of Alan Murphy and Gary Hurst.

In anticipation,

Chris.
