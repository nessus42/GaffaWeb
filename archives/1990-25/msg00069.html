        USA Tour dates for Dave Stewart* & Barbara Gaskin

Oct
10    Boston-Harbor House
11    Long Island-007 Club
12    Woodstock, N.Y.-Bearsville
13    Wash DC-Bayou
14    Philly-Northstar
15    Pittsburg-Metropol
16    Cleveland-Empire
18    Detroit-Sam's Jams
19    Chicago-Schubas
20    Milwaukee-Phil Brothers
21    Madison-University of Wisconson
23    Minneapolis-Fine Line

That's it. This time they're only touring the East & N Midwest. No dates
at all South or West. According to the press release (read to me over the 
phone) they hope to do a longer tour sometime next year.

The line up will be:
Barbara Gaskin-vocals
Dave Stewart-keyboards
Andy Reynolds-guitars & keyboards
Drums will be programmed.

They will be doing at least 5 songs from the new album "The Big Idea"
and the rest will be past "Broken Records". ALL RIGHT!!! 

Make that:

Dave "NOT the same one who's in Eurythmics" Stewart & Barbara Gaskin
