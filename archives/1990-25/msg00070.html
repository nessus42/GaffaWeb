So I'm on Dave & Robert's shit lists because I called the Anthem
"stinky & stupid". Oh well darn!

Excuse me for not adding this:

* IMHO * the national anthem is stinky and stupid.

I don't like The Star Spangled Banner and I've NEVER liked TSSB.
Why does our national anthem have to glorify war? Why should I
revere a song that's about how swell it is that, after a night
of heavy fighting, old Betsy's still flapping proudly in the breeze?
Just never you mind all those dead bodies lying underneath it.
Yeah, a patriotic song all right! We're inches away from war with Iraq
and people seem awfully worried about superficial National Symbols now.

I've always liked America The Beautiful better (even with the reference
to God and the "fruited plains"). Our National Anthem should
glorify America's beauty. Lord knows we're mucking up the beauty and
if our schoolchildren and sports fans had to sing about "spacious skies
and amber waves of grain" all the time maybe they'd think more about
trying to keep America beautiful. That goes for all of us Yanks.

I shake my head in despair at America's problems, but we still have things
like The Grand Canyon, The Cascades, The Everglades, Flint Hills, the
Western coastline, Sedona, Arizona, Fall in New England, the Rocky Mtns,
Mesa Verde and about a million other wonderful places that should be the
main focus for America's National Anthem. TSSB is outdated and dull and
hard to sing. If I wanted a military song as an anthem I'd vote for
Paul Hardcastle's "19". Better yet, "Army Dreamers". 

For an environmental song as a national anthem, I vote for Jane's
"Bound By The Beauty". Ok, she's Canadian. So what! Does anybody
really know for sure that Francis Scott Key was an "American"?
Didn't we all come from somewhere else? Well, except for the Native
Americans, whose land we stole. Hey didn't they come across from Siberia?

Kate'n'Jane are my heros.
