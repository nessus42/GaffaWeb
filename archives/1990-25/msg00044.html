The lyrics server at Umass has Kate Bush lyrics...it should have most of her
albums (I haven't check lately).  The address is lyrics@umass.bitnet. Just
send a note without a subject line and the following in the body of the letter:
lyrics/author=Kate Bush/album = whichever album you're looking for (song can be
substituted for album).

To get a complete list of the albums they offer, just send the word album in
the body.  Add the parameters of author=kate bush, if you just want a list of
her albums offered.
NOTE: I dont' know how well this works from Internet to Bitnet, but it's worth
      a try!
