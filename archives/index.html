Love & Anger
The Love-Hounds Archives

Love-Hounds is the KaTe Bush mailing list. It has been in existence since 1985, making it one of the oldest music-oriented mailing lists on the Internet. For most of its life, Love-Hounds has also been gatewayed to the rec.music.gaffa newsgroup.

This archive collects all of the posts that have been made to Love-Hounds since its humble beginnings as mail.katebush. They are organized into blocks of 100 messages which are named by year. Thus, "1987-05" refers to messages 401-500 of 1987 -- roughly.
