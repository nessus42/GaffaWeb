> I've recently gotten into Kate Bush's music (and very glad that I did),

I think Madonna is a quite great artist and have half or two thirds of her

however, is Like a prayer, IMHO.

Yes, people here in Europe (or Sweden at least) doesn't seem to get as 
upset over her image as the Americans. But she was more popular some 
years ago, but I don't know if that has to do with the image or the music :-)
