For those interested there is a Roy Harper compilation in the UK imaginatively
titled 'An introduction to....'.This includes the Kate duet 'You-the game part
2-the two halves in flight' from the album 'The Unknown Soldier'.It's nice to
have this track on CD at last, my vinyl copy has seen better days!!
The catalogue number is HUCD017 and it is released by Science Friction records.
