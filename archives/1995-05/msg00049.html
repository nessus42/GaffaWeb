> WOW! on the 12" single version of "The Sesnual World" on side-A,
> the record has 2 grooves...so depending on where the stylus
> falls, you'll here either the regular version of "The Sensual
> World" or the instramental version!!!
> Are there any other vinyl records out there that have this double

Well, yes, there are, although no more with Kate.
The double groove records I have are "Popmusic" by M (12"), with on
the other groove a B-side song.
And "Good thing" by the Fine Young Cannibals on 10", featuring the
regular version with piano solo and an alternative version with sax
solo. If I remember correctly there should also be a 12" double
groove by the Blow Monkeys.
 
Double groove records are usually very rare, and as far as I know
these 3 (4) are the only ones ever made. But I could be wrong, if
anyone knows I'm wrong, please enlighten me. (I'm somewhat of a
record collector... :-)
