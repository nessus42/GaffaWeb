>If you're into some intense groove-toying, the performer Boyd Rice has

record _Penthouse_ (uh-oh--dare I say that around here?) & Pavement_.

never-ending grooove repeating the phrase "For a Very Long Time." Funy the
first time, and pretty irritating thereafter!
