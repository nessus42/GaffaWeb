In the absence of a new album and drying well of new TopiKs to discuss, I
proffer the following:

***The 1993 KaTe Bush Essay KonTest***

All you have to do is come up with a few paragraphs describing what you think
Kate is doing with herself these days, and what she's been doing with herself
for the past three some-odd years.
Let's face it, she can't have spent all this time locked in the studio... or
could she?

Post your entries here- as far as I know there are no judges, and prizes will
be along the lines of The Knowledge That You Have Done Something To
Positively Contribute To This Great List.  Points will be given for wit and
originality... go to it!!!
