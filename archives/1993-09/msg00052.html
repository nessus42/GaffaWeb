>Funny you should say that. I've heard that if you play "The Dreaming"
>backwards you can hear a voice that sounds like Peter Gabriel saying
>"Release fewer albums." Scary.

I laughed long and hard about this.  But then I got home and put on the Tori
Amos song "These Precious Things", and I thought I heard something in the
background.  So I got it down on Reel to Reel, played it backwards, and heard
her saying very distinctly: "There will be no 7, it's over now".  I guess we
know what that means.  Now I'm not laughing.  Could there be a conspiracy? 
Someone call Oliver Stone!
