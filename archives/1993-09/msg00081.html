Perfume Tree is a local band (Vancouver, BC, Canada) who have
one release (cassette and CD) called Dust... it's rather atmospheric,
bass heavy, lots of high, floating vocals. I wouldn't compare
them to DCD, they have a much more synthesizer-landscape
kind of sound, if you know what I mean. Their label is called
Zulu records; call (604) 738-3232 for information.
