whilst poking through the local used and rare record emporium, i noted (and
purchased) a new cd bootleg of Kate's entitled _butterfly kisses_. the first
sentence i uttered when i looked at it was "gee, it's not the hammersmith
audio track," (which got agood laugh out of the propieter of the store). in-
deed, it is not.

rather, it is a collection of oddball tv performances and a few tracks from 
the tour of life. i don't have the cd with me right here, but i'll post an
annotated track listing as soon as i am able to give it a listen and figure
out where all the tracks are from.

sound quality is as expected: not KrysTal Klear stereo, but fairly unmuddled
and certainly listenable (by my standards anyways).
