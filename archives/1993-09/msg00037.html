Well, I dunno if it's still in print, but here's all the usual info...

_ASPECTS OF THE SENUAL WORLD_ CDEP (1990)
Columbia Records US, 44K 73174

1. The Sensual World (LP Version)   3:56
2. Be Kind to My Mistakes (from the film _Castaway_)   3:03
3. I'm Still Waiting   4:25
4. Ken (from the Comic Strip film _G.L.C._)   3:48
5. The Sensual World (instrumental)   3:56


It was pretty easy to come by when it was originally released.  In fact,
I must confess that I'm not a total KateFanatic (i.e. I only own four of
her albums), and my wife (girlfriend at the time, actually) just picked
this up on a whim since she enjoyed the album so much - this leads me to
believe that this wasn't one of those releases that only die-hard fans
manage to track down.


On a somewhat releated note - has anyone here in r.m.g compiled a
complete discography of "offical" Kate releases, including release
dates, catalog numbers, track listings, etc.?  I haven't seen any
evidence of this in my couple of months of "lurking" around here.  I'm
just curious since I've undertaken similar projects for some of my
favorite bands (the Legendary Pink Dots, Skinny Puppy, and others), and
many 'netters have commented that they've found them pretty useful.
Obviously, as a "casual KateFan", I'm not offering to undertake a Kate
discog myself, but I'd be perfectly willing to offer any help I can
(such as Canadian catalog numbers, etc.).  Just an idea.
