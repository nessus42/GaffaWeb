Since Andy hasn't mentioned the traditional roses yet this year, can I 
propose a birthday gift for Kate that would really have to come from each 
of us, from our deepest levels of affection and respect and gratitude?

It's not even *that* much to ask:  just, suppose we all agree to police 
ourselves to the extent of *labelling our flames* in our Subjectlines as 
"FLAME", so that one simple killfile entry will serve for all time, to 
spare the Tenderhearted from the Terrible?  { :^)

So, when you're done venting your emotion, and you're looking at "Edit, 
Send, Abort" you take one deep breath more and choose 'Edit', and change: 

"Re: MoP is for Saps!"

into

"FLAME Re: MoP is for Saps!".


Is this little enough to give?
