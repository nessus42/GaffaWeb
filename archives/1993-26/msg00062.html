Be careful when buying these at swap meets, etc. At best, they are
manufacturer's appropriated (read stolen) overstock. Oftentimes,
they are reproductions or "fantasy" pieces. I don't mean to infer
that actual passes never reach the market, but these usually run
along the lines of the European posters that many record stores
sell for $10+ -- someone figures that a repro will be worth more
than the paper it's printed on...
