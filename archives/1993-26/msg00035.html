In article <9307270106.AA28640@kona.cc.mcgill.ca>, 
SHAWN@parasit.lan.mcgill.ca writes:
>     As a true Katefan, I am here to proclaim that, without a doubt,
> "Hounds of Love" was, is and forever will be Kate's best album. It is
> the best demonstration of the artistry and pure genius of the
> greatest chanteuse of our time.

I have to agree.

I've always thought of "The Dreaming" as Kate Bush's "Sgt. Pepper": a grand 
leap forward, and an astonishing breakthrough of demonstrating what is 
possible in music.

I've always thought of "Hounds of Love" as Kate Bush's "Abbey Road": using  
what was learned on the earlier album to produce a masterpiece.
