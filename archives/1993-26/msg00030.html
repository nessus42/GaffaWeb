Anyhow, for the completists (and for discography maintainers like me):

Prince - New Power Generation Radio Show 3LP set.
No order number at all. The set consists of three LPs with white labels (NPGR and
Side n, 1<=n<=6, printed on it). There's a runout groove message on each side,
'TONE BONE A' to 'TONE BONE F'. The LPs reside in white inner sleeves without
any trace of print on it. These inner sleeves each are to be found in a white
cardboard cover with a whole in the middle. No trace of color on these too.
(Now that's what I call bootlegger's fear.) Finally, the three LPs get company
by a full size b/w sheet of paper with 'PRINCE NEW POWER GENERATION RADIO SHOW 3LP
SET' printed in white on a b/w photo of Prince's head. His right eye is
hidden behind a scarf, the left eye is looking straight into the camera. The back
of this piece of paper is white again. The sound quality is remarkably good for
a bootleg, but remember that this was a radio show in 1989. (WHEN in 1989? Before
or after the middle of October?) Most important track is of course Love and Anger
appearing on Side 1.
