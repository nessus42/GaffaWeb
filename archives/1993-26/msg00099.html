Anybody know anything about a band called G.O.L.?  Sort of a cross
between The Orb, Cocteau Twins, Genesis & god knows what else.  Nice
dubby basslines, interesting rhythms & melodies, and a woman named
Antonia Reiner intoning unbelievably pompous lyrics over it all.  I
have no idea why I like it so much, but it's really infectious.

They have a track called "Angelica in Delirium" on Ambient Dub 1, and
"Soma Holiday" and "No Bounds" on Ambient Dub 2.
