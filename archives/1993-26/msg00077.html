Photo ideas:

1. Aspects of the Sensual World (sand-through-the-hands):
  This is my first-choice nomination.  I think Kate looks *amazing* in
this photo and it would look good from a distance.  (I do want a
t-shirt and poster of it.  Anyone know where to find them?)

2. Ninth Wave photo (on the back of HOL CD *booklet*, not the back of
the CD case):
  I agree this photo is also a great choice, but not the one from
TWW2.  I think her hand stands out too much in TWW2, especially from a
distance.  She looks much better in the original also, IMHO.  I find
the original to be much more visually _interesting_ and attractive.
It doesn't give itself away immediately.  When I look at TWW2 I right
away see a woman in a life vest in the water reaching out.  But
looking at the original, it takes longer to realize this... it looks
as if it could be some other situation... it holds my attention longer.


Phrase, Lyrics, etc:

1. "Love Hounds... Suspended in rec.music.gaffa"
  "Love Hounds" above photo and "Suspended in rec.music.gaffa" below,
or perhaps the latter only, above photo.  Maybe with the Ninth Wave
photo where she is "suspended" in water?  Love Hound (singular) might
even better signify that the wearer is a love hound.

2. Deeper Understanding
  Obvious choice since it's about computer communication/addiction,
but only some of it works for a shirt, I think.

Well I've never felt such pleasure.
Nothing else seemed to matter.
I neglected my bodily needs.
I did not eat, I did not sleep,
The intensity increasing,
'Til my family found me and intervened.
But I was lonely, I was lost,
Without my little black box.
I pick up the phone and go, Execute.
