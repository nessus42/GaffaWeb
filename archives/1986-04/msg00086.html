.....more sid stuff (does he never die?)......has anyone read "And I Don't
Want to Live This Life" by Deborah Spungen (you guessed it -- Nancy's mom)

least......not completely trash, but almost. The Chicago Sun Times called
it "An American nightmare". It's basically a biography of Nancy and how she
was always troubled from the day she was born................
