Summary: Best Kate Bush album

In my opinion, the best Kate Bush albums are 'Never for ever' and 'Ninth 
wave'.....fact!

The Ninth wave is excellent. After listening to it for almost 10 years, I 
still find it so interesting.It tells a story (to me) of a girl going through 
an emotional journey through life. She experiences comfort (listening to the 
radio), feeling trapped and persecuted (under ice), being punished during a 
medieval time in her nightmare (waking the witch), feeling the presence of an 
imaginary friend (watching you without me), wondering about her future (jig 
of life), travelling and feeling isolated from the rest of the world (Hello 
Earth) to waking up after a terrible nightmare having seen her whole life in 
dream and cherishing a new day (morning fog). It is wonderful and having 
listened to many other artists and CD's, no other musician has ever created 
such an imaginative piece of work. Kate Bush is a genious.
