Greetings Love-hounds,

   About a month ago a bought the bootleg video called "The Collection" 
(Not to be confused with the Lovehound Collection which we all know was a
beautiful piece of collaborative work from some of our more distinguished
Lovehounds).   Anyway, Ther are a few items of interest in this video.  
BTW, it is listed in the collector's guide on page 108...but the video
track list is inaccurate.  Obviously, the author only read the track list
off the cover).     Most of what's on this video is already available in
the Lovehounds Collection or Kateopia.  

Ok, here goes:


1.  The Wedding List  (Prince's Trust Concert)
       - yes, this is the piece where Kate's dress straps snap towards
         the end of the performance.       (picture qaulity:  B)

2.  The Man With The Child In His Eyes  &
    Them Heavy People   (Saturday Night Live)
       - picture quality:  C

3.  Them Heavy People     (Revolver TV show, UK, March 1978?)
       - Kate performs live and is wearing a Tuxedo.
       - pic. quality: D

4.  Babooshka   (Collard, FRANCE, 1980)
       - Kate mimes to a backdrop of a winter scenery.  She's wearing a
         green "sparkly" dress and a cape
       - Pic. Quality: D

5.  Suspended In Gaffa  (Champs Elysees, FRANCE, Oct. 1982)
       - Kate mimes to a backdrop of a Russian scenery.   She's wearing a
         grey dress with "puffy" shoulders".  There's a B&W picture of this
         performance in the book "The Whole Story" by Kerry Juby.  BTW, the 
         photo describes her as performing "Egypt". 
       - Pic. quality: D-

6.  December Will Be Magic Again   (Abba special, Dec. 1979?)
       - Kate mimes to the Bongo version of the song while sitting in a
         red chair.  
       - Pic. quality: C-  (actually, slightly better quality than on the
         Lovehounds Collection...at least the copy I have)

7.  Babooshka,  Interview, Delius   (Dr. Hook Special, March 1980)
       - this is on the LoveHounds Collection
       - picture quality: B

8.  Interview,  Sat In Your Lap video   (Razamatazz, Sept. 1982) 
       - can be found on the Lovehounds Collection
       - picture quality: C

9   Award presentation and Wow video excerpt  (BPI Awards, 1979?)
       - can be found on the Lovehounds Collection
       - picture quality: C

10. Them Heavy People video
       - swinging light version (official video)
       - can be found on the Single File
       - Pic. quality: C

11. Interview  (Delia Smith Cookery, Feb, 1980)
       - Can be found on the Lovehounds Collection
       - Pic. quality: C

12. Interview  (Russell Hardy Show, Nov. 1980)
       - can fe found on te Lovehounds Collection
       - Pic. quality: C
                 
13. Army Dreamers  (offical video)
       - can be found on the Single File and The Whole Story videos
       - Pic. quality: C

14. Breathing (official video)
       - can be found on the Single File and The Whole Story videos
       - Pic. quality: C

15. interview  (Desmond Morris Show, UK, unknown date)
       - Can be found on the Lovehounds Collection
       - Pic. quality: C

16. The Dreaming (offical video) 
       - can be found on the Single File and The Whole Story videos
       - Pic. quality: C

17. Interview  (Old grey Whistle Test, Sept. 1982)
       - can fe found on te Lovehounds Collection
       - Pic. quality: C

18. There Goes a Tenner  (Razamatazz, Sept. 1982)
       - Kate mimes on a stage with 2 dancers and a lot of kids.  She's wearing
         black pants & white t-shirt.  The 2 dancers are also wearing a black
         mask.  The choreography is slightly different from what is in the
         official video. 
         Here, we can see a 10 shilling note animated on the screen along with
a
								
"tenner" and a "fiver"   ...for those of you wondering what she's 
         singing about...
       - Pic. quality: C

19. Kate Bush Quiz Box (Music Box,  unknown date)
       - Kate does appear here.  It's only a promotional contest to win
         copies of Hounds Of Love
       - Pic. quality: C
    
20. Interview  (Countdown, dutch TV, Nov. 1985)
       - Kate is interviewed in english.  Ther are dutch subtitles.
       - Pic. quality: C


Total running time:  1:23

For those of you wondering,  I found this video at a used record store called
Second Coming  in Cambridge, boston, Mass.  (near Harvard square).  They did
have another one available for $15.00....I think...)  (I do not work for them) 


I'm willing to make the "new" stuff available for a future video collection
project (like the Lovehounds Collection or Kate-opia).  Just email me and we
can set something up.  :-)

peace
