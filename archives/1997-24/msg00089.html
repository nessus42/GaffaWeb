My tuppence on the artist & work & biography thang. Knowing more about
an artist (eg Kate) can make you understand better where they were
coming from when they produced a particular piece of art. Without
knowing about Van Gogh's madness, would "Starry Nights" have quite the
same meaning? You don't have to kow the "whole story" - (As Kate herself
once said, everyone's own interpretation of what her songs are about is
just as valid as her's) but it can help.
