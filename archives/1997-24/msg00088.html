>Ron Asked "BTW I have a LP verison of HOL that has the one additional
line--"And a
>big woof for Bonnie and Clyde". Any idea what that's all about?"

Apologies if this has been answered already - I'm just catching up on a
week's worth of Lovehound mail which seems to have arrived in one
splurge. Bonnie and Clyde were the two dogs Kate used in the cover pic
for HoL. She described in an interview once how it took all day to get
them to settle down (long walks, big feds etc). When they first tried to
take the shot Kate ended up with one dog's paw in her mouth and a
face-full of make-up that needed redoing. When the pic was finally
taken, one of the mutts actually fell asleep on her. Sweet.
