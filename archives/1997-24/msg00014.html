I was recently sorting through my ancient floppy-disk archive when I
came across some of my college Macintosh disks.

One of these had the "Deeper Understanding" computer beep on it - back
around 1990 or so I had sampled the beep from the song and filtered out
any "background noise" (ie, music :-) ) in an effort to reproduce the
same beep for my own machine.  If you've got a Mac, you can get the
sound file (29K) at http://www.tiac.net/users/rao/Gallery/Kate/

The sound quality isn't fantastic, but it's a fun system beep :-)
