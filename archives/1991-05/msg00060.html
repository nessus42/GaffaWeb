Well, I got LaH around Christmas, and it has the correct
title for "Don't Push ...", so unless that was a copy that had
been hanging around for some time (like well over a year), then
it looks as though its an error introduced on your reissue.

The copy I have is actually the second attempt I made at getting
LaH.  The first copy I bought, in late September, had the final
10 minutes missing!  It took me that long to get a full copy because
the music video outlets did not seem to stock Kate's videos much,
so it took me some hunting to find it.  Strangely, that changed
when "The Sensual World - The Video" was released - then the full
range appeared again.

I'm glad that I did get a proper copy, because (IMHO) the final
minutes, just before the credits, when Kate is finishing her
routine for "Wuthering Heights" and is jumping around waving
at the audience in extreme high spirits, is the most delightful
part of the video.

Well, that's it for this posting - no doubt it will be another
few months before the next one I make ... :-).
