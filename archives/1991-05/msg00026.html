Message from Vickie about the Live at Hammermith video

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Wed, 13 Feb 91 22:40:27 PST
Subject: Message from Vickie about the Live at Hammermith video


Vickie says she won't be able to post until this weekend, so she
asked me to post this for her.

Vickie works at the distributorship for Rose Records in Chicago.
She says that they got the _Live at Hammersmith_ video in last
Wednesday!  But did she tell us?  NOOOO!

In any case, Rose Records has 29 stores in the area, and all of them
got at least two copies.

Chris noticed something strange about the packaging:  It says
"PAL Colour".  It's not really PAL -- it's NTSC, the American
standard.  Chris figures they took the British packaging and
merely cut out all the EMI UK stuff and replaced it with EMI US
logos, but they didn't notice that one thing.
