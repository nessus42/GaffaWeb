I was looking at a map of San Francisco the other day and I
noticed that there is a Kate Street.  It's very small -- only
half a block long.  Unfortunately, it doesn't intersect
Bush Street.  Otherwise, the street sign at that corner might
disappear one day! :-)

(For all non-Bay Area people:  Bush is an important street in
San Francisco.)
