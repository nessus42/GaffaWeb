It's finished. I should be getting a preview copy any day now.
Happy and Kevin are pressing up CDs to send to radio stations and
record stores. I'll tell you when it's available to the public.
They will try to get it picked up by a major label. If that doesn't

be available via mail order. 

Chris wants me to mention that Happy called and told me that I'll

because of my efforts to help get her music out to people.
Glad to do it!!

I'll try to keep details up-to-date.
