> I only have "Birds of Passage" and like it a lot. Anyone know anything about
> the personnel in the band? Do they ever tour?

the band members are anneli marian drecker (vox, keys), geir jennsen (keys),
nils johanssen (guitars), luc van liesholt (horns), and michael delory (drums).
the first three are the creative forces in the band though (this is as of the
_birds of passage_ release).

here is a more or less complete discography:

1988:   Blank Sheets/Chaideinoi 7"
        White-Out Conditions/Maaligaabidaa 7"
1989:   Dreaming Girl (3 mixes) 12" (Nettwerk)
1990:   Birds Of Passage LP
		       CD with Look 3 (Nettwerk)
                       Lim.Ed. CD-Box with a CD-single with Oskorei
                               and Moligobido (CBS France)
        Birds Of Passage/Manupura 7"
        Birds Of Passage(2 mixes)/Glassmaker 12" (Nettwerk)
        A Shoulder To The Wheel 7" (Nettwerk)

All is released on Crammed Discs if nothing else is indicated.
