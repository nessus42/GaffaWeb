Chris, I love you.

Chris, never ever ever, ever ever ever...
please, for my mental health and well-being,
*EVER* answer or get involved in one of these
inane, stupid, ***HORRIBLE*** "witch" threads
again.

You have no idea how truly deeply it hurts me
to see people, who don't know you except from
your posts, pop up and call you names, judge
you unfairly, and generally show their
ignorance about you as a kind, gentle, caring,
supportive, funny, brilliant and wonderful person.

*ONLY* you know or care how this kind of thing
can affect me, and only *YOU* can do something
about it.

Let the rumours spread...who cares? Let the
witches have their silly little fantasies that
Kate is one of them...who cares?

Let it be...ignore them...who cares what
they say or think? Mutter "fuck it" under
your breath (or outloud, to me) the next
time you hear that inane rumour, then hit
delete. It's not worth it. My *pain* is not
worth it.

Who cares what they say about *you*?

I care, because I love you, and I can't take
their comments anymore. Please.

For me.

Vickie
