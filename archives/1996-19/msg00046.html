Dear Friends,

Just some info regarding Tori Amos...

Tickets for her concert, scheduled for June 21, 1996, went on sale at 9:00 am, 
Saturday, April 20, 1996.

Tickets were sold out in a few hours.

Tickets for a hastily added second showing for the same night after the first 
showing went on sale around 5:00 pm.

I think they sold out as well, but I wasn't paying ( or leasing ) attention.  I 
didn't buy tickets since I have yet to hear any tracks on 'Boys for Pele' which 
intrigued me enough.  Although, I must say I REALLY like 'Little Earthquakes' - 
enough so that it is in my personal high rotation section at work.
