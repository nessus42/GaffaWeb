The official Michelle Shocked website is at
http://www.shellshock.com

This site has tons of current info including her current touring
schedule, merchandise ordering, bio, discography, lyrics, etc.

Her latest album is "Kind-Hearted Woman" from 1994.  
It is currently "self-released" and is only sold at her shows or can be
mail ordered (address is somewhere at the above site).  However, it is
supposed to be "re-released" April 9 by Polygram and then be available
in stores.  The "re-released" version of the album is totally
re-recorded, but contains the same songs.
