Wayne asked about YWA. Unfortunately he forgot to include his praise. So I
add this for him:
"These two songs [YWA and SALD] are two of the greatest pieces of recorded
music ever, no, they are two of the greatest entities in the universe. Some
even think, they are the Universe!"

They are available for example on the following CD-singles:

You Want Alchemy
- Eat the Music, UK
- The Red Shoes, part one of 2CD set

Show A Little Devotion
- Rubberband Girl, US
- Moments Of Pleasure, UK, 4trk

Then, not to forget, there is "Shoedance" (the Red Shoes Dance Mix), a cool
10 min elaborated rhythm piece with voices from the film ("she tricked ya",
"My shoes perhaps?", "kinda Voodoo", "It's really happenin' to ya" etc..). 

For example on: 
- The Red Shoes, part two of 2CD set 
- Eat the Music, 4trk

These three songs on the TWW-3 CD are direct digital transfers from the
above singles and therefore really good quality, though unfortunately a
bootleg. Buy some of KaTe's albums and give them to your friends for a
present if you feel as a thief. :-)
More complete information is perhaps available from Uli or Scott?

Best wishes
    Wieland
