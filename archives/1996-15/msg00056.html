Oh yeah, and Debbie Gibson. We want lots of talk about Debbie Gibson. 
Really, I just always feel weird about saying "alternative" artists 
because it's such a meaningless word. Alternative to what? Some of the 
people called alternative are heavily in the mainstream. Others are not.

cases. Everything from Jane Siberry to Bjork to Kate to Alanis 
Morrissette gets called alternative at one time or another.

I think what we all mean is "women who don't play brainless commercial 
pop." As if we could agree on who is included in that definition.
