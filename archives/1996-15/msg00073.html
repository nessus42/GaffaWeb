There is a new home page up on the net that may be of interest to some 
of you. It is called "She's Got the Beat" and it regards women 
musicians, women in the music business, and women composers. Presently 
it has a bibliography, links to artist homepages, women's organizations, 
and a fledgling board for women musicians and composers to connect with 
one another. Musicians and interested parties (yes, including men!) of 
*all* genres are encouraged to visit and participate.
The url is at
