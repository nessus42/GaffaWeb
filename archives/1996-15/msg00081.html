This REALLY annoys me, no matter WHAT the brand or flavor.  There is just
something that sickens me about thinking I'm doing something healthy by
buying fruit juice, and then I read the label and that's not the case at

juice, I'd like it to actually BE fruit juice.  Otherwise, I may as well
just have some Kool-Aid.  Then, I would at least expect the sugar coma I'd
be in later in the day instead of being taken completely by surprise.
