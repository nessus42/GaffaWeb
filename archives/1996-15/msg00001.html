Ok, ok ok...!  But guys, it just isn't getting any better for me.  I
still get the lh digest (though I can now read gaffa, I find that the
digest is far more reliable and complete), but most days I have to
just delete it immediately and then empty the wastebasket, because my
school account is so short on disk space because of all the projects I
am caught up in.  Maybe if I live through this semester, I'll get a
life back....

I'm not completely asleep at the switch.  I am collaborating with Tina
and Paolo Pognant, who edit the Italian Kate Bush Fanzine, and who
will be bringing out an Official Italian Edition of The Lovehounds
Collection.  They can't use SVHS, but I have given them the Reference
Dubmaster I made from the first pass of SVHS Submaster #2, itself made
from the second pass of Chris Williams' SVHS master.  They are able to
produce the images for the boxes in color, so I have scanned those
afresh, and they will be translating the tracknotes into Italian.
They are able to do the NTSC->PAL transfer themselves.

Other than that, the only kb news I have is that I did find time
during intersession to play The Dreaming loud one night.  All in all,
pretty pitiful.

Since I'm delurking for a moment, thanks to Stev0 for the Kate page
reviews, and to all the new voices who are keeping the digest filled
up more than daily most of the time.  And Karen, Way! To! Go! on
getting your poem published!!

Also, Andy:  even as we speak I am in mid-semester in my course,
"taking their Jesus away."  I should add however that they get him
back.  Can't keep a good man down....
