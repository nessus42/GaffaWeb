Here is the list of artists people have sent me from Phase I.
If you came in late, the idea was to send me a list of artists
you like OTHER than KaTe, I would compile the following list,
and now you are requested to rate them.

---  DO NOT REPLY TO THIS UNTIL YOU READ THE FOLLOWING!  ---

1. Make sure you EMAIL your responses to ME: adams@bosco.berkeley.edu
   Depending on your news reader, "reply" may end up posting, since
   rec.music.gaffa is a moderated group.
2. DO NOT include comments about these groups with your reply.  I
   won't have time to read them and all I'm doing is making a tally.
3. Your response should contain one artist per line, followed by a
   colon and arbitrarily many spaces, and finally a number from 0
   to 10, representing how much you like that artist.  (zero means
   that you detest the artist - you would rather have several of your
   appendages ripped off than listen to them, ten means you worship
   the artist and that they can do no wrong.  I expect very few zeros
   and tens.)
4. If you are not familiar enough with the artist to pass judgment,
   PLEASE do not include a line for them in your response.  If you
   include an artist, I assume that you actually know something of
   their work.  So don't pan someone just because you have HEARD
   bad reviews of their work or because you aren't familiar with
   them.  Be fair.

At some point in the future I will post the results of the poll.  The
deadline for submitting your response is the end of May, so act now.
