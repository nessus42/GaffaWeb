Become Wings medly with Lisa Gerrard from Dead Can Dance. "Fyt" is the

Wolfgang Press (and maybe Modern English & Xmal Deutchland also, I
don';t have the credits with me). BTW, the song "fond affections" is
credited to Rema Rema, the band that evolved into the Renegade Sound
wave and... the Wolfgang Press.
