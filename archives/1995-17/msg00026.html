Check out the Net's OTHER indie music archive, the CME. With close to 100
full band pages housing reviews, bios, show dates, graphics, MPEG recorded
sound clips and full songs! In full operation since Oct. '94 the CME has
compiled a large database of Music Industry Contacts (MIC) available to
everyone on the Net. CME is also home to the net's sickest talker. Once
dedicated to music chat, now completely uncontrolled. So check it out if you
already haven't.
