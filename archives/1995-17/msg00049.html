Perhaps this is one of the more unusual sightings... even more so than
hearing Milla in a grocery store.  This is from the Ottawa Citizen, 
Tuesday, 30 May:
     As a Kate Bush ballad played, a Grade 8 boy was seduced by his
     female teacher in a sexual relationship that lasted six months,
     an Ottawa court has heard.
     "She used to play a tape by Kate Bush," a tearful, red-faced,
     27-year-old man, said in reference to a song by the British
     singer that he said referred to the "man and the boy."
     "She referred to me when the song was on and would tell me
     how I was similar and special, similar to the boy in the song."

Ever since I've read that (this morning) the best guess I've come up
with is "The Man with the Child in his Eyes"... but is that quite right?
