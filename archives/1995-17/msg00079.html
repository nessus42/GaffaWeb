In article <60.1681.5789.0C1E416D@canrem.com> you write:
>Is the excellent haunting music/vox that I hear in the London Life ads.
>on Canadian t.v. lately Jane Siberry?

I haven't seen the ads myself, but a member of the "siblings" mailing
list recently confirmed these are indeed Jane, singing something
off _When I Was A Boy_.

"Siblings" is a mailing list for discussion of Jane Siberry and her work.
Recent conversations have included her interviews in Mondo 2000 and
HotWired, the SIB-line newsletter, and other artists who have covered
her tunes.  To join, send mail to 
	majordomo@underground.irhe.upenn.edu
with the command
	subscribe siblings
as the body.

Paul Mallet has provided an excellent Jane Siberry web page at
	 http://malletp.psyc.queensu.ca/siberry/siberry.html
