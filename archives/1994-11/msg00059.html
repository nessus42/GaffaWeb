As Chris stated "England, My Leotard" is not a Kate *self*-parody.
However, it was not done by Faith Brown either. The track in question
was done by Pamela Stephenson on the legendary BBC comedy show "Not
The Nine O'Clock News". It appears on one of the highlight albums the
BBC subsequently released.

Faith Brown did indeed include a Kate impersonation in her act for a
while, but she didn't do "England, My Leotard".

If you like, I'll play you "England, My Leotard" when you and Vickie
visit in May - I have it on tape somewhere...
