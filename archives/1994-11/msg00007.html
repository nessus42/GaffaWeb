While listening to "You Want Alchemy" for about the 70th time the other 
night the phone rang and who should it be but IED.  He kindly pointed out 
to me that the secondary reason this song lingers in my mind (first 
reason being obvious) is that some lovely Debussy has been incorporated 
into it.

Don't ask me which composition, I can't remember off the top of my head, 
but IED hopes to attend the upcoming showing of TLTCTC at the UC Theatre 
in Berkeley (details posted a few days ago thank you CHAD), so you can 
ask him in person if you're there.

I'm planning on going Friday to the evening and late showings.  I'm about 
half way through a description and dialogue transcription for those of 
you who haven't been able to see it  - - but I've gotten fuzzy about some 
of the scenes.  Hope to get that cleared up at this next showing.

Not that it will matter much longer.  Though I have nothing to base this 
on but my intuition and persistent rumors (see Alan Watson's post 
yesterday - Hi Alan!) I think the video will be out shortly.  Notice how 
heavily the current single(s) is (are) oriented towards the film.  Notice 
how conveniently soon the KT Convention is.  Perhaps a significant 
announcement pending?

Speculatively anticipating the upcoming something,
