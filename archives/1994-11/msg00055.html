>    It will feature rarities, including non-album tracks and variant
>    remixes...as well as a hilarious self-parody by KaTe called
>    "England, My Leotard"! 

   It would be loads of fun for Kate to do a self-parody like this,
but she didn't. I believe the track in question is actually by Faith
Brown. Kate was on a talk show in the _Lionheart_ era, and the host
played Faith's parody of _Wow_ (she swung her arms around like Kate,
eventually winding up with arms 10' long.) The camera came back to Kate
saying "Amazing" in a rather droll voice. Faith also did _Wuthering
Heights_, tromping through the moors in army boots.

   The closest Kate has come to self-parody is the "woo-woooo-umm-humm"
at the end of "Do bears...?" with Rowan Atkinson.
