The German Eat The Music CD single (7243 8 81317 2 / F: PM 515, made in Holland)
contains the following tracks:

Kate Bush - Eat The Music (German Maxi)        24:39
----------------------------------------------------
   1) Eat The Music                             5:10
   2) Eat The Music (extended Version)          4:59
   3) You Want Alchemy                          4:22
   4) Shoedance (The Red Shoes Dance Mix)      10:06

Most important perhaps is that the extension of Eat The Music is exactly minus 11
seconds long. So it definitely is not the '12" version' that could be found on
the US version of the Eat The Music single. Go figure!

Anyone in desparate need of that CD?
