"I spoke to CBS/Sony in NY today about rumors of the video release of 
TLTCTC.  I was informed that a domestic [U.S] video release of the film
should be ready for distribution before Christmas of this year. I was also
informed that Kate will be appearing on an upcoming release by Davy Spillane
on an album titled _A Place Among the Stars_.  The track Kate is scheduled
to appear on is `Sexual Healing,' so be on the lookout for this release in 
the near future.  I aslo inquired about about any further domestic releases
for 1994 and was told that there are no plans for any more releases in the 
USA at this time.  I was informed that CBS/Sony USA is still trying to 
persuade Kate to tour again.  No plans have been made at this point, but 
the label is still trying their best to influence Kate to tour The Red Shoes.
We can only hope they accomplish their goal." [bla bla yeah right]
