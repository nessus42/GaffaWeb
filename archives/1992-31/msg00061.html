Ken wondered about Clannad's Sirius and why it sucked.

Here's the deal:  Clannad used to be a nice traditional Celtic band,
and recently they turned into a new-age-pap band.  Avoid Sirius and
the new one, Anam.  The older the better.  Macalla is decent
and available through Noteworthy.  If you want to see how they've
changed over time get Past Present.

Clannad Live is great if you can find it.
