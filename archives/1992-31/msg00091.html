Actually, there are a number of CDs with the Cathy Demos.  The two most
common, as far as I know, are the set "If You Could See Me Fly" and "Passing
Through Air" which has the demos spread over 2 CDs.  There are a few
other boots out there that compile everything on one CD.

Quality tends to be pretty mediocre.  On the pair I named above, the CDs 
were mastered from the vinyl EPs so they're quite noisy.
