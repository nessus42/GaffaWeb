        A couple of random ideas: 

        There is a new (to me) book out called Women in Rock. It has 
several pages on Kate.  it compares her to (I believe Debbie Harry of 
Blonde, saying that Kate was able to be move beyond the image of being 
a "product", wheras Debbie wasn't.  From what I read, Kate was treated 
in very flattering terms.  Towards the end of Kate's part it talks 
about how she has a small but devoted following here in the US.  :-) 

        Getting the Lionness at Heart book reminds me of what the world 
is most missing - a Kate Bush Coffeetable picture book!  The Beatles 
have about a zillion of these, but all we have is Cathy, the boxed 
sets, and "biographies".  What'd I like to see is a book kinda like the 
boxed set, but like about 100 pages long and including pictures from 
all parts of her life/career.  :-( 

        
        I've been thinking lately that perhaps we should think about 
changing the name of the "Katemas" parties.  I like the name KateFest 
myself.  The reason I think this might be a good idea is that many 
people might here the name "Katemas" and actually take it seriously (it 
is kinda meant as a joke, isn't it?  :-), and that those people might 
feel somewhat hesitant towards going to a party named that.  I myself 
have always felt somewhat uncomfortable with the name. 
        I don't mean to be presumptuos (?) about this, I just think 
it's something we n\might want to talk about, though I don't know how 
many others feel the same way. 


                I was also wondering about what people would think 
about have something like a REC.MUSIC.GAFFA.INFO newgroup.  What I mean 
by this is like how they have on the star trek newgroups, essentially a 
"very best of" for those who don't want to dredge through all the 
messages on love-hounds everyday.  What I would invision this having is 
just important announcements, interviews, detailed messages, etc and 
not have general chit-chat, flame wars, general discussion, etc.  
Maybe a message a day or around there.  
        The reason I think this might be usefull, is that it seems when 
people leave the newsgroup, they usually complain about there simply 
being too many messages to read-through.  My personal feeling is that 
we would never want to limit the messages on love-hounds at all (of 
course) but that a "best of" thing might within a year or so have as 
many readers as the newgroup itself does. 
        I don't know how this would be implemented - newgroup or 
mailing list, etc, - and I also don't know who would be in charge of 
picking the most important messages, but I do think that if it could be 
implimented and if someone were interested in running it, it could be 
very useful and a way of keeping a lot of people reading some of the 
stuff here.
