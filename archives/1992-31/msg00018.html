I finally got back on the list and really missed my daily dose of KaTe.

a two weeks ago on the radio program _the_Thistle_and_Shamrock_.  The show
was highlighting the work of piper Davey Spillane and a relatively recent
band he was working with (I can't recall the name, but if anyone knows it,

completely different arrangement with pretty cool rhythms.  I'm sure KaTe
did not play on it.  Anyone have more info on this?  I'm writing TTaS to learn
more.
