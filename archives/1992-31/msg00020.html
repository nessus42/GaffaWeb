Ha! I feel vindicated. Ron Hill quotes Kate B. as saying that "KITE is
actually a Bob Marley song". The first time I listened to it on a CD,
through headphones, I thought how reggae-ish it sounded and wondered why I
hadn't noticed this before. I mentioned it to someone else and he looked at
me like I was nuts. Now
I feel somewhat less out to lunch!

Maybe instead of the Trio Bulgarka Kate B. could get the I-Threes to sing
on her next record.....
