wcsanil@superior.ccs.carleton.ca (Anil Prasad) writes:

>I've been seeing references to Happy Rhodes left, right and centre
>lately, but haven't paid any attention to them (sorry.. but you
>can't read everything on usenet!).

>Can someone give me a quick summary about who/what Happy Rhodes is
>and what they/she/it is all about? For reference's sake, I am a
>big Bush/Amos/Vega/Tikaram/McLachlan fan... is HR something I need
>to check out?

Yes, absolutely. Not that everyone will like Happy, but she definitely
fits into the list you gave. She's much more obscure than those artists
mainly because she doesn't have a record contract and has always been
very independent. She has 5 albums out (paid for all of them herself,
with the help of her co-producer/SO Kevin Bartlett) and is working on
her 6th. She writes all her own music/lyrics.

Like Bush/Amos/Vega/Tikaram/McLachlan, she's hard to categorize and
label, so you'll excuse me if I don't even try to tell you what her
music sounds like. Whatever category those women fit into, put Happy
there too.

If you'd like further information, either subscribe to Ecto and/or
write to me. Thanks for asking about her!
