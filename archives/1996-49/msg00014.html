Grifone wrote:
>Is Kate Bush CD Live at Hammersmith Odeon a bootleg or official release. 
>If both exist tell me what's the difference
>Thanks

KaTe's 1979 *phenomenal* Hammersmith appearance has been bootlegged about a
zillion different times before it was finally "officially" released by EMI
in 1994.  It came out as part of the "CD & Video Double Pack" which
included the CD and a VHS copy of the concert as well (a real nice
package). It's EMI 7243 8 30065 2 6.  All other copies that I know of are
boots.
