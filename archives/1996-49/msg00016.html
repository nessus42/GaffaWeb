>Is Kate Bush CD Live at Hammersmith Odeon a bootleg or official release.

To the best of my knowledge, both of them exist. I have seen a lot of boots
around, in stores and catalogs. I remember one of them was called "Wow",
probably italian.
As far as I know, the official release comes bundled with the video. You can
find it at cdnow (www.cdnow.com)

I haven't heard the boots, but I've got the release and it sounds pretty
good. I strongly recommend it.

PS: I don't know which system you need, but I can trade you the NTSC video
from cdnow for my PAL copy ( It has only been played twice)  . In case you
need PAL, that is.
