I have to echo Sherrill's enthusiasm for this show.  I felt that the
Nightstage (Cambridge, MA) setting lent itself well to a more "intimate"
feel for the show.  Tori was able to converse with the people in
audience easily, and it seemed to put her more at ease.  Reading some
of the earlier reviews of her shows, I was expecting her to just bash
out the songs and not say much.  She actually talked quite a bit and
seemed to enjoy the crowd's reaction to what she was playing.  In 
fact, whe she burst into "Whole Lotta Love", the crowd thought that
was just great!  As pointed out before, "Smells Like Teen Spirit" is
a really silly song, especially when you can understand the lyrics!!!

Great show, especially since Tori had to enter the stage right in
front of where we were sitting.  We wished her a good show as she
entered and thanked her as she left, and she was very nice to say
"Thank you".   Overall, it was an impressive night to say the least!
