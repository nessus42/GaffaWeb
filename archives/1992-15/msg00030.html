The subject says it all.  When will Tori be performing in the San
Francisco area - Does anyone know?  I haven't heard *any* of her music
(I don't watch TV so I haven't seen her on MTV) but I would love to go
see her based on the reviews she's gotten here in Gaffa.  Even better
than playing in the SF area would be having her play in Santa Cruz -
but I guess thats wishful thinking...

<TANGENT>
speaking of people performing in Santa Cruz - I just saw John McLaughlin,
for the second time, here in SC.  He was playing with his usual percussionist
and a bass player.  The show was incredibly awesome, as would be expected.
The last three times I've seen him he's been playing an nylon strung acoustic
guitar that has special pickups so he can play it as a guitar synthesiser - 
it has a nice sound.
<END OF TANGENT>

while we're on the subject: has Happy Rhodes ever toured the west coast?
I'd like to see people from the bay area post concert dates for the
various artists who are discussed in this group...
