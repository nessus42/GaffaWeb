Just wanted to report that Tori's performance at Nightstage last
night was every bit as awesome as people have described & more.   Her 
voice is so powerful & she herself is just the coolest.  The crowd loved her!
I think that's probably the last time I'll get to see her for $8!  :(
(But it would be worth a whole lot more. :)

She played a similar set to what was described in Northampton (i.e. the 
whole album except "Girl", "Teen Spirit", "Angie", etc.) and also did some 
covers of Zeppelin!  Someone requested "Freebird"--> she just laughed.
Maybe next time...
