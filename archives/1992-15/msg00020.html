Hi Everyone, 

One thing noted by Dennis Miller was that Thursday's show at Bimbo's in
SF is Sarah's last one for this U.S. tour.  It starts at 8 and doors 
open at 7-I'll be meeting up there with Ed Suranyi and Karen Newcombe and
we'll get a table with a view.  Sarah played in Los Angeles tonight-whew,
what a time to be there, isn't it?  "Into the fire," for sure. Ugh.  She'll
definitely have a story to tell us at the show. 

I thought her performance was really good!  Yeah, it must put a little bit
of pressure to be introduced with a quote from some reviewer about being 
Canada's "answer" to Kate Bush.  What was the question, yeah?  Anyway, 
Sarah looked even nicer to me than she did on the Letterman show, and she
looked like she was enjoying this performance. I'm looking forward to 
seeing Sarah finally!  If any of you around the area can make it (if 
tickets remain,that is), look for us.  We'll be hooting for Sarah. 

(oh gaud, rev. jesse jackson just came on the TV-time to hit it)

Larry (L-H@cup.portal.COM)
