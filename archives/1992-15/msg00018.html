	I've been away from reading this group for a while, but I'm
	really pleased to see so much discussion about someone from 
	my home city, Halifax Nova Scotia, namely Sarah McLachlan.

	In fact, I went to her High School, unfortuneately about 2
	years ahead of her.  But, I listen to her music, and it ranks 
	high.  I'm glad to see the martimes producing new talent 
	obtaining international acclaim, like Anne Murray, but
	something a little more my speed/taste.  Everybody seems
	to come out of London England, or New York etc.  

	She is great, no matter where she came from, keep on
	listening !!

	Craig Hamm
