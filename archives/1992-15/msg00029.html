   To the poster who asked about Oleta Adams (Sorry, lost your posting), I
understand that she is B British and has been around for a while, doing  a lot
of session and backup vocal work. The Tears for Fears guys found her playing
clubs in the states and used her to add some (so they figured) much-needed soul
to the Seeds of Love Album, and I think Curt helped her get a record deal
to record the Circle of One album. Isn't her voice magnificent? As far as I 
know, the only recordings she has out are the aforementioned albums and
the track from the Two Rooms CD. I heard recently, however, that she is
recording a new album slated for release at the end of the year. As Tears
for Fears are no more, I'm not sure if Curt Smith/Roland Orzabal have any
involvement, although I wouldn't be surprised. 
 
Lastly, could anybody give me info on Loreena McKennit?
