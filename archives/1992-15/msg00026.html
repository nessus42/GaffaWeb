I know Oleta Adams released a song called "Get Here", I think shortly
after the Tears for Fears stint.  It mentioned various modes of 
transportation, then "I don't care how you get here, just get here if
you can."  I also saw an interview with her on Much Music, during
which she sang (unaccompanied!) Elton John's "Don't Let the Sun Go Down
On Me," but I don't think this is what wound up on "Two Rooms" (which I
haven't bought yet).
