her last release, _the visit_ was just released in america on warner
brothers records. there are three previous others, which can be gotten
from quinlan road productions in canada:

	quinlan road
	box 933
	stratford, ontario
	canada n5a 7m3

i do not have them so i can't supply you with names, but i believe that
they're available on cd for cdn$20 apeice. i have just received the new
release - it's another one of those etheriel female vocals pseudo-celtic
type deal, but i'm pretty fond of it already. i had been under the im-
pression that she was pretty popular amongst the neo-pagan contingent,
as well as science fiction fandom (i saw a number of her cds for sale in
a hucksters' room at boskone in february). that impression is pretty
valid after hearing _the visit_. she's quite a decent harpist to boot.
