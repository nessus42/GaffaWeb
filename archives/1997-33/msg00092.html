some other great Melbourne stores include:

Heartland Records - North Melbourne
	*goth, ethereal etc

Collectors Corner - Swanston St, Melb
	*lots of electro, ambient

Au Go Go - Little Bourke St, Melb, near Elizabeth St
	*alternative: punk, garage

Melbourne's got a great live music scene - check out some bands while you're 
down here. You might also want to visit some of the public radio stations - 
Melbourne's finest is PBS FM in St Kilda.
