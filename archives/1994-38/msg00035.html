That matches up with the result of MY investigation. I checked The Garden,
and my (and Kimmo Lindman's) compilation of all Kate Bush lyrics (that we

"The Lyrics of Kate Bush" that I have is basically a compilation made by
Andrew Marvick, with additions made my Kimmo Lindman. It has been edited,
and converted to Word for Windows (MS-Dos) format. It includes most lyrics 
Kate ever made until spring 1994. 

If you're interested in a copy, mail me. I can not guarantee that I have 
the time to send it to all who request it, and because it's a document
for Word, you'll need the "Timpani" font. It comes in zipped format, and
will be uuencoded.
