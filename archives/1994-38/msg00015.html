We have both of them. Here are the details:

WOMEN IN ROCK  (1986, Sony Video LP RO481VH)

The Belle Stars

The Belle Stars

Btw, these are all (on both tapes) live performances, not music videos.

Both of these were edited and produced by/at Trilion Video in London.
Producer for both was Phillip Goodhand-Tait.  These are Sony Video-America
releases, but they obviously originated in the UK, so they shouldn't be
hard to find in either place.

I've seen another compilation called "Women In Rock" that was totally
different from these.  That one featured really mainstream people like
Tina Turner (nothing against Tina) and we don't own it.  I just looked
at it once in the store to see if it was another Volume from this series.
It wasn't.  It didn't have anything in commmon.

I've never seen (or heard of) any other volumes after these two.
 
We bought them for Judie, but it was a nice suprise to get live Danielle Dax
too.  That's how I first heard of her.
