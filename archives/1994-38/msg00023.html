ATTENTION ALL BAY AREA RESIDENTS!!!

PAULA COLE IS COMING!!

If you haven't heard already, Paula Cole will be performing at the GREAT
AMERICAN MUSIC HALL on DECEMBER 9th.

THIS IS A SPECIAL SHOW!!!!

It is the first time ever that she has headlined a show.  Her previous
performances have all been in small clubs with other bands.  This time, not
only will she be the main attraction, but she'll also be performing in a
larger setting (TGAMH seats about 400 to be exact) which means...

MORE MUSIC!!!

The lucky attenders of this concert get to hear more material than before
and maybe even some NEW stuff.

Apparently this show will be "testing the waters" as to how big a crowd
Paula can draw.  So lets impress Imago Records by attending.  Bring a
friend!  Bring a your friend's friend!!   If you liked her album
"Harbinger", she's even better live (I believe previous postings can attest
to this.)

Tickets are $10.00 and it's general seating.  You can either call THE GREAT
AMERICAN MUSIC HALL itself, (415) 885-0750, or your local BASS TICKETRON
outlet.

Remember,  DECEMBER 9TH is the night.  GO!
