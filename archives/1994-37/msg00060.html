of Kate on TotP November 17 (?).  IED is confused by

mix-up is offered, namely that he was momentarily

these two songs as revealed in the movie.  IED had 
had no idea that "And So Is Love" could have had 
anything to do with the dear departed until he saw 
the bird placed on its velvet. . .
