But while I'm here, I thought I'd mention a couple of things:

I solved a long-running Lovehounds mystery!  You know that line
that no one can make out in "Do Bears..."?  Well, I figured it
out:

It's "Is DRINKIN' God-head?"

It fits.  All the phonemes are there (even if it sounds more like
"Drankin'"), and it fits perfectly with the character Rowan
Atkinson is playing.

Now, here's a new mystery for you English Lit types:
Listening to "December will be Magic", what connection does
Oscar Wilde have with Christmas?  O. Henry, Charles Dickens I
can obviously see if Kate used those as a literary referance.
But Oscar Wilde?  Is there some story of his ("The Importance
of Being Santa"?) I don't know about?

Oh, also those Web addresses I posted a couple of days ago -
You Webheads probably already realized this, but I made a few
typos in a couple of the addresses (Oh, big surprise there!);
basically it's dash-should-be-underscore and vice-versa stuff.
Still, those details are important...

Finally, it's kind of ironic.  Six months ago I had NO Kate
videos in my library (in fact, my entire video library was just
the Hitchhiker's Guide and some Max Headroom stuff); now I got
over 6 1/2 hours worth of stuff.  I realize that that's still
squat compared to most of you long-time collectors, but going from
0 to 6.5 since Katemas is rather impressive, I think.
