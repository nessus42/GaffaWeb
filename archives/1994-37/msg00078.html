I remember seeing some references to this Finnish band on this group
before, so I thought to mention that their 4th album "Aitara" is
released and entered the Finnish album charts at #33. The single
"Tumala" is getting lots of airplay on the radio, but I haven't heard
the rest of the album yet. The style seems to be a bit more "electric"
than on the previous albums, but the vocal sound is still the same.
