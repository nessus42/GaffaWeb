Chris here, Vickie forgot to mention Madonna's headset microphone. This
is one of Kate's most lasting contributions to the world of performance.
Honestly, to my knowledge, Kate invented it. No one had used one on stage
before the "Tour of Life", and the one used was custom built to Kate's 
specifications. It's a tribute to the skill of the sound mixer on the tour
that every concert wasn't awash with squealing feedback, as the microphone
used was ommni-directional. (If you don't know what that means, ask a friend
with a P.A. if he/she would use one on stage.) In the "Nationwide" T.V.
documentary, Kate described it as, "...like a telephone operators..".
   OK, she may not have actually built it herself, but it was her concept,
and in the modern world of committees and "time to market teams" and other
such nonsense, that's about all the "invention" that anyone can do.
