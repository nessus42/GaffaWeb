Thought you gaffans would be interested to know that "Wow" is used on
the soundtrack of the new film about Andy Warhol, _Superstar_.  It's
the second song in the film (after Blondie's "Heart of Glass" :-).

The movie hasn't been really released yet (I saw it at the
Smithsonian), but is due out in the fall.  I think a friend of mine
summed it by saying, "well, yes, it _is_ kind of superficial, but I
think that's the way Andy would have wanted it."
