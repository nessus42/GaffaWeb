I don't have the UK single here, but have listened to it a few times, 
and I think the versions of "Be Kind To My Mistakes" and "I'm Still

Seperate note... anyone else out there get Suzanne Vega's
"days of open Hand" yet? I've given it one complete listen so far,
and I'll refrain from reviewing it until I listen some more, but
if you like the first two, get it. It's more of the similar, but
not same.
