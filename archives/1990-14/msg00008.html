I am not sure if anyone has responded at this point in time since I am a bit
behind on my mail, but my sister and I once visited Rangely ME and the farm, 
ORGANON, where Reich "did all that stuff."  I would highly advised finding a
copy of the book, "Book of Dreams" by Peter Reich.  It is easy reading and
not really long.  It gives youa good background for "Cloudbusting."  For those
who can`t find a copy and live in the Boston-New England area at the Library
at Copely Square there is a copy that you can't check out but can read while 
there.  As for Organon, it is closed in the winter due to snow usually and it
is quite a site.  The lab is up behind the house which looks sorts of fifties-
ish.  It is now called Organon, the W. Reich museum.  Go see it if you can.
For those of you interested in Reich you ought to read "Man in the Trap" a book
by Ellsworth Baker M.D. about Reichs theories and practices.
