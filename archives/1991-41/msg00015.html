   Just one point to clarify. This was not a VHS cassette. It was a
"V-2000" type cassette. This short-lived format was somewhat popular
in the Netherlands and the UK, but was never introduced to the US.
 
  It used 1/2" tape but actually recorded 1/4" in each direction
when flipped over like an audio cassette. 

  Fortunately we had an old V-2000 VCR that I picked up for $10 from a
guy at the Fort Levanworth Army base, who had just returned from Germany
(I'm into collecting antique video equipment.) 

  Unfortunately the tape was erased by the X-Ray machine at the airport.
(Not the X-rays, mind you, but the intense magnetic field produced by
the machine's power supply.) Sorry, but we weren't able to make a backup
copy as I didn't have the right cable to go from the PAL V-2000 to the
PAL VHS.

   Sorry if everyone had gotten their hopes up. Maybe Kate will release it.
