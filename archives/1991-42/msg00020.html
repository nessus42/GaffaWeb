I became a Kate initiate in late '86.  I had seen "Sat In Your Lap" on         
Night Flight numerous times prior to that, but I just wrote it off as

had none of her own material until _TWS_.  All those lost years!               
                                                                               
My current favorites are EBTG, Enya, Siberry, Sara Hickman, and Gabriel        
to mention just a few.                                                         
                                                                               
I would like to answer the person who asked what Jane Siberry song was         
referred to on the new EBTG album.  It's "Hockey" from                         
_Bound_By_The_Beauty_ and the song it's referred to on _Worldwide_ is          
"Frozen River".  Do I win a prize?
