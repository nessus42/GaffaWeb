A Happy Rhodes Radio Special will air tomorrow (Tuesday, Nov. 12)

Ah, but only if you live in a few cities. However, I'm posting a list
of stations airing the show just in case someone who might be interested 
is a listener of one of these stations, or has friends who are listeners.

I've been talking about Happy Rhodes on the net for nearly 3 years now.
It's exciting for me to be able to pass this information along, and I
hope it will be of use to someone out there.

.........................................................................

The radio show is called "World Cafe" and originates from WXPN in Philly.
I am not sure if this entire 2-hour show is dedicated to Happy Rhodes, but
at least a good chunk of it will be. Happy's portion will consist of an
interview, and 3 songs recorded live in the studio. They are songs that
appear on Happy's albums, but were re-recorded especially for this interview.

Here's the line-up, some airtimes were not available.
