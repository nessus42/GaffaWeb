This reminds me of perhaps my funniest story about coming out of the
broom closet to someone:

Last Christmas, I saw a friend from school whom I hadn't seen in
years.  We'd known each other since the second grade, and weathered
lots of stuff together.  Any road, I told her about being a Witch, and
we talked about it, she asked me questions, et cetera.  Everything
seemed to be going well.

During the course of the conversation, we were driving around Houston,
and I had _The Dreaming_ in the tape player.  At some point, Alicia
said, "Are we listening to this because this woman's a Witch and there
are backward messages that you're supposed to be listening for?"

When I could breathe again I told her that that was not the case, but
that I'd have to be allowed to tell that story as the silliest
question to date I'd had on being a Witch... :)

At least she recognized the backwards part...
