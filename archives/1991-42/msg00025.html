Ed Suranyi asked whether I was referring to Sarah McLachlan's _Solace_
or her _Touch_ albums when I said that I wasn't able to find any
of her albums earlier this week when I went to Tower Records after
getting my root canal in Boston. The answer is: I don't know. I had
never heard of Sarah McLachlan, and I though I would poke around at
Tower to see what was there. All I found was one of those plastic
dividers in the domestic "Mc" section. I assume this means that
at one time they have had one or more of her albums, available
domestically. (Usually, imports are in a separate section.)
So. If albums reappear there, does a general concensus exist as
to the best album to start with? What's it like? What's going on?
Does Sarah McLachlan look like Marina Sirtis?

Hey, while we're talking beautiful singers, how 'bout
Julia Fordham? The cover of "Porcelain" makes me get all sweaty.
