Thread the third:
Re: Theodore.  I did NOT buy it just for Kate (Hell, I never bought the
soundtrack to "She's Having a Baby", and I know I made the right choice).
I also like the Psych Furs, O Positive, and a few other bands on it.  And
yes, I got turned onto a couple of bands by it (most noticably Poi Dog Pondering),
but overall, if I had to do it over again, I would NOT have bought it.  There
are NO "Great" cuts on it (this is NOT IMHO.  This is a scientific FACT!), and
the lame ones far outweigh the good ones (3rd Bass?  Gimmie a break!  OK, they
may be the best "White Rap" group, but that is like saying "the most honest
lawyer" or "most compassionate Republican".  Not quite an oxymoron, but veddy
veddy rare.
