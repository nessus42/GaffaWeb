In article <c3iu02bN03Of01@JUTS.ccc.amdahl.com> tjc50@JUTS.ccc.amdahl.com (Terry Carroll) writes:
> 1) Where in the Bay area can I get the Kate Bush boxed set?

It's been a long time since I looked, but I think Rough Trade in
San Francisco had some left in at least one format.  You can also
ask at Rasputins and Streetlight Records -- all of these have had it
in the past.

> 2) How much should I expect to pay?

If you buy it locally, it will probably be fairly expensive.  Unless
prices have been lowered greatly lately, it will be $150 to $200.  You
can get it cheaper via mail order.

>Also, what are the CDs in there?  I heard there are 8, and I'm only aware of 6
>Kate Albums:
>
> The Kick Inside
> Never For Ever
> Lionheart
> The Dreaming
> Running Up That Hill

That's the name of a song.  The album is Hounds Of Love

> The Sensual World
> (not counting The Whole Story compilation)
>
>What are the other two?

The other two CDs or cassettes (three LPs) are only available as part
of the box set, except when unscrupulous dealers split the box set up.
They contain B-sides, alternative mixes, and other non-LP cuts.
