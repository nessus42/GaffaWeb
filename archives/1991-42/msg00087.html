The December issue of "Q" has a small snippet about KaTe in the news
pages. It says the album is progressing after some earlier "sticky
moments", whatever that means. Also the only Kate-Prince connection
they can find (apart from the mention in "Diamonds & Pearls") is that
KaTe went to one of his shows at Wembley last year...

There's also a quite amusing interview with Enya in it.
