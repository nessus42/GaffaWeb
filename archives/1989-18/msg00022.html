Subject: Kate in Q

The October edition of Q magazie comes with a photographic
supplement on songwriters. Kate Bush is featured with a photo
of her seated at the piano (fashionably attired in flip-flops!)
which might be described as arty or out-of-focus depending on
your aesthetic viewpoint. The legend reads " Kate Bush arrived
in 1978 with Wuthering Heights and a precocious repertoire of
songs whose other-worldly oddness and melodic ingenuity were
entirely detached from the jagged new wave modes then prevailing."

Neil
