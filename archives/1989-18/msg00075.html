hi love-pups,

i would've written sooner, but my sides were still aching
from corey's tribute to bob dylan.  i'm taking up a collection this 
month to send corey Bob Dylan Complete for christmas.  make your 
contributions now!  if there's enough leftover, we can send him a
lifesize poster as well.

after corresponding with devoTed hounds from both the northwest and
east coast, it became clear that it's time to plan for the
ALBUM RELEASE KATE BASH.  it sounds like targeted cities so far are
boston and seattle, with a california site still to be determined.
i ofcourse, offer my humble abode for kate hysteria, but ied
mentioned something about hosting it in l.a. (any thoughts, andrew?).
carpools anyone?

other odds n' ends:  while listening to "the infant kiss",  i found
a particular phrase that piqued my interest.  while she is
without-a-doubt singing to a little boy, she sings, "ooh, he scares
me, there's a man behind those eyes".  in the song "the man with
the child in his eyes", the opposite concept is expressed.  i find
the boy/man integration to be a rather intriguing theme.  however, 
i never would've thought about it much, without kate's unique lyrics.
