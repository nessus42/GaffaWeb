Not sure about your spelling, however, see if you can get a copy of a UK
radio series called "Now that is what I call Mali". It is a documentary
about a trip to Mali to find Mory Cante (sp?), and Ali [ something I 
can't spell like Faqu-turi ]. It was made by the BBC about 10 months ago,
and has been broadcast here  on Radio 4 at least twice. Unfortunately I never 
bothered to tape it. 
Try contacting the "Duty Office" of the BBC in London, to see if they 
can help, either with a copy of the program, or with album numbers so
you can get the records.
