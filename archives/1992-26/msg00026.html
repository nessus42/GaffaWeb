While I'm working on my rebuttal to Vickie and my TORT of response to IED 
 :- ) , I thought it paramount to step on this thread immediately!!!!

INTERGALACTIC (IGG) does not sell bootlegs! (This in itself will cost me 
lots of business :-)  Given, a few items have crossed the pages of  the 
INTERGALACTIC GAZETTE over the years, but as a rule they were pieces of 
merit that surfaced in collections purchased. (And be forewarned: Maura 
does have agood memory  for those who would point a condeming finger and 
have also called looking for certain forbotten titles!!!)

Furthermore - although 'someone' was selling the John Riemer tape, it was 
NEVER  IGG. In addition, inspite of the implications in Vickie's post, 
IGG DOES NOT sell reproduced tapes. If you have ever seen such a listing 
in an IGG catalog, please correct me.

Furthermore, IGG was formed and exists to promote Kate Bush, the artist 
and singer/songwriter. We do not carry items that do not promote her 
career, artistically or commercially.

A recent bootleg surfaced that contained B-sides of Kate material. Some 
ot this material was available on Kate still has in print. Some of the 
material was drawn from CHARITY PIECES that Kate had contributed to.  
Worse yet, the piece was released on several colors of vinyl. COLLECT 
THEM ALL! IGG never did carry this shoddy shoddy piece of work and never 
will.

I agree that there are items out there that do Kate's career harm.

But please, don't ever accuse IGG of knowingly selling such items.
