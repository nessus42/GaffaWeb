I've recently discovered that Gered Mankowitz is having an exhibition of 
his work in London between Sept. 14 and Sept. 27. The title of the show 
is "Images" and will include work from his stint as the Rolling Stones' 
photographer as well as photos of KATE, Elizabeth Taylor, Gary Glitter, 
Bing Crosby and more. The exhibit is at Cromwell & Ward Gallery, 40 Dean 
Street, London W1. Also Record Collector magazine plans on having an 
article about Mankowitz's views of rock photography over the years along 
with a discussion of his work with many artists such as Jagger, Hendrix 
and KATE BUSH. Keep an eye out. If anyone goes to this exhibit I would be 
obliged if they could leave me a message as to what it was like. 
Unfortunately it's impossible for me to get to London by the 27th. Thanks 
again,
