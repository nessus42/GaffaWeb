Last night, my housemate, my friend Pat, and myself were working on promos
for our campus radio station, which currently can only be heard on campus,
and I managed to use some of KaTe's music in there.

1) Wuthering Heights (Original) - The guitar solo at the end of the song
was used as a general promo for the station.

2) The Jig of Life - We did a promo for our (Pat and I) Saturday Night
using the instrumental part.

3) CloudBusting (Organon Mix) - This was also used as a general promo,
using the drum part in the beginning, and fading with "I still dream".
Our Operations manager wondered how we did that. ;)
