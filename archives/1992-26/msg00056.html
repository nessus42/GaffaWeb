Yes I know this is not Kate stuff, but its sort of related!..

Here in Adelaide, earlier this year, we had a Womadelaide Festival,
having musicians from all round the globe. Yes, this is the WOMAD
Peter Gabriel is involved in.

I heard yesterday that Peter Gabriel might be part of the Womadelaide Festival
next year seeing this year's was such a success. Its a MAYBE at the moment, 
but I hope he will come!

Also, Peter Gabriel's single has been released here (released 2 weeks back), 
the album is to be released here on October 15th.

The last song on the single is a speaker breaker..and I thought Enigma was the
best to test subwoofers! 

The video for 'Diggin in the Dirt' is amazing, well worth a look see.. especially
the time lapse stuff. It makes alot of sense after me hearing he got divorced 
recently and had many sessions with a shrink!


Lastly..

	Any word when Kate's new album is to be released?

	Any word if the tour is still going to happen?


Thanks!
