35 votes were tallyed, and we had people from around the world send their
votes in.  From what I understand, this may be the first poll on the
albums from here, but I am not sure. If there is, I would like to see them
if I can.  Other people added other votes in, such as 'Another Day' and
the Cathy Demos. Since I did not have them readily on CD, they were not
counted in.  I also want to thank all the people who noticed my mistakes
with titles of certain songs. I did that all by memory, and thought I did
a very good job.
