>Well, I'll admit I have given away dubs of entire albums, but *rarely*,

blah blah blah, excuses excuses.  over 200 lines of it, no less!  why can't
you just say, "i did it and i'm proud, so go sit on a middle digit and rotate
til you squeal like a pig on a honeymoon!"

this all reminds me of a rather apt quote on the back of some independent
artist's album: "home taping is killing the music industry.  keep up the
good work!"

now that my very first piece of official copyrighted recorded product is
shipping, i feel i have some perspective on this issue.  i would be upset if
somebody duped my record, but on the whole i'd rather have people hearing
the music than paying for it.  both would be nice, but i'd settle for just
getting it out and about.  after you've had the pleasure of seeing 5000
people jumping up and down to Your Song, financial considerations seem less
important.  i wonder if i'd feel the same if i had actually taken the
financial risk in getting the record pressed.
