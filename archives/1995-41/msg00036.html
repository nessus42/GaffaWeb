   The 2nd lead vocal ("..the eye of wonder, the eye that sees, the "I"
that loves you...") is, in fact, Lenny Henry. "The Self-Obssesed Wanker 
Formerly Known as Prince" (thanks Stev0!) did background vocals but only 
"...of all the people in the world..." is clearly him. The rest of the 
"...why should I love you..." fade-out is Kate doing a Prince-like call
and response.

  One interesting note: Squiggle is credited simply as "Prince." Nice
to know that he only insists on that nonsense to annoy the press.

  One other thing: personally Lenny Henry isn't (to my ears) imitating 
Prince on this track. He's imitating the sort of chorus that Steely Dan
often used, particulary on the album _Aja_, and in particular the track
_Peg_. The vocal arrangement sounds like a homage to the "...it's
your favorite foreign movie..." chorus. Kate has often mentioned _Peg_
as a one of her favorite songs.
