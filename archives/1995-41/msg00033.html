Check this out music lovers....
________________________________________________
WHAT
A live Internet broadcast is happening this New Year's Eve
at Moe’s Mo’Roc’N Café in Seattle beginning Sunday, Dec. 31
at 10pm.

The featured bands are:
1) The Presidents of the United States of America
2) The Posies
3) Super Deluxe

The show is being put on by SPRY, CompuServe's Internet
Division and I-music.
________________________________________________

HOW TO "GET THERE" ON THE INTERNET

Fat chance getting one of the 400 lottery tickets to the show.
You can get there via 3 Web sites for FREE:
1) http://www.spry.com
2) http://www.imusic.com
3) http://www.compuserve.com

________________________________________________

WHAT YOU NEED TO MAKE IT HAPPEN

Internet users with a PC-compatible or Apple Macintosh computer
with a soundcard, Web browser and a 14.4 KBPS modem (28.8 KBPS
preferred) will be able to listen to the concert.

Each Internet site (any of the 3 above) will have a link to download a 
free version of Streamworks™ audio decoder software from Xing 
Technologies that enables users to listen to the live audio feed from the club. 

________________________________________________

DIG IT!
What a great way to see a free concert!  It should be hot.
Apparently, the owner of Moe’s Mo’Roc’N Café said, "We 
haven’t had this high of a demand for tickets at Moe’s since 
Neil Young and Pearl Jam played together in June."

This is definitely the beginning of a future wave of bringing
music to people. ENJOY!!!
