Since Marvick's own letter was full of personal attacks, I need not
scruple to point out that his own pretense and stupidity have once
again been his downfall.  My query was followed by the classic USENET
smiley-face symbol, :-), indicating that it was a joke.  This fact
would have been evident to even the dullest of intellects, even without
the symbolic reminder, but not, it seems, to Marvick, whose capacity
for outrage so greatly outstrips his capacity for thought.

Since the rest of his typically long-winded and flatulent posting is at
the same microcephalic level, I see no reason to respond to it in
full.  (Or should I really explain in full the meaning of an idiom like
"Kate can't dance", explaining as if to a child that it does not mean
she can't pick up her feet, but that she is lacking the talent
generally considered neccessary to a professional performer?  What
would be the point of responding to such moronic drivel?)
