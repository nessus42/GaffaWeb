- About Kate's quick hop to France :

    She has so far been featured in :
    - The 20:00 news on Channel 5 (La Cinq) -> interview
    - "Top 50" (Canal Plus) -> interview + TWW
    - "Giga" (Antenne 2) -> interview + various excerpts (TSW, TBS, ...)

    She's scheduled to appear in "Champs-Elysees" (Antenne 2) this saturday
    at 20:30. This show was taped four weeks ago - I don't know yet what
    Kate will do, but I'll be sure to tape it too.

    Oh, for those of you who are suddenly thrilled at the idea of more KaTe
    stuff : I haven't heard any "real" news (except the Stivell/tour bit
    I already posted) - the interviews hardly ever got beyond "TSW was my
    most female album" - and the French use SECAM, not PAL or NTSC.


- Now something for you cover analysts :

    The "Breathing" single cover has an interesting set of illustrations on
    the back - three 1" * 1" BW 'pictures' on the right side, aligned
    vertically, of Kate (?) dancing. Well, in the third one, she looks
    surprisingly like the letter 'D', although the first two don't evoke
    anything special at first sight. Does anybody have more info on that ?
    (the whole cover is B&W, the front is almost completely black and has a
     mushroom in the lower left corner.)

That's all folks - Kimon.
