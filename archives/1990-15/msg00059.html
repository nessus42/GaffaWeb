A Shameless Plug for the Sinead O'Connor Mailing List

From: mcb@largo.ig.com (Michael C. Berch)
Date: Wed, 16 May 90 17:41:56 -0700
Subject: A Shameless Plug for the Sinead O'Connor Mailing List

I just thought I'd mention that JUMP IN THE RIVER, The Sinead O'Connor
Mailing List, is alive and well after about two months of existence.
We have about 90 direct recipients (plus a couple of local redistribution
lists), in 7 different countries, and there have been about 120
messages in two months, including a couple of album reviews, the
lyrics (which are not printed on the album notes) from THE LION AND
THE COBRA, and several concert reviews from Sindead's current tour.

The list is undigested and unmoderated.

To join, please send mail to 

	       jump-in-the-river-request@presto.ig.com

or, for those averse to so much typing,

		      jitr-request@presto.ig.com

For those unable to use domain addresses, these addresses can be reached
via UUCP using the path:

	     {apple,ames,rutgers}!bionet!ig!jitr-request

or for UUNET subscribers only:

		   uunet!presto.ig.com!jitr-request

When you request a subscription, please provide an unambiguous mail
address; we prefer domain addresses, but can handle UUCP paths.  If
you site is in the UUCP maps, site!user is fine; otherwise please
provide a path from a well-known site.  BITNET subscribers are
welcome; we may arrange direct BITNET LISTSERV access at a future
time; for now, BITNET subscriptions will be mailed directly.  Site
redistribution lists are welcome as well.

Please also include your name, so we know who is behind such evocative
addresses as GLRF3908HJK@HUGEVM.MASSIVE.EDU and so forth.
