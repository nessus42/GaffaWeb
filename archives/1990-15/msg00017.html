I just bought the intervew CD you wrote about a month ago or so.

It's got some interesting stuff about songs on the two albums named in
the interviews...and the money I paid for the CD was worth the appreciation
I now have for "Houdini"!

The first interview is in a restaurant, and is pretty noisy, with lots of
background noise.  On the other hand, you get to hear Kate talk with
her mouth full <grin>

The second interview sounds as if it were made in a very large room and is
pretty well done.

I paid $17.00 for the album, which is a lot higher than 6 pounds, and I 
consider it worthwhile.  

The interviews last a total of roughly 40 minutes.

They've not yet been transcribed and posted to Gaffa.
