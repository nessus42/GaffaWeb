Hello there Jon--
  Just a note regarding the Young Ones skit you were referring to the
other day; the title of the particular skit is "Cash", not "Bambi".
In the "Bambi" episode, our fellows go to the laundrette and eventually
wind up at University Challenge.  "Cash" finds poor Vyvyan as an unwed
mother (?) :), and Neil is forced to join the police force in order to
raise money for the little waif.
  As an aside, one of my favorite Young Ones episodes is the one where
they have the big party at their house (the episode is called "Interesting",
I think).  Definitely some great stuff from "those witty Brits".
  Happy viewing.

                                           All the best,
