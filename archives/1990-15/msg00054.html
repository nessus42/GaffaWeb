Hello everyone.  A short while ago I posted an article in which, among
other things, I asked about an interview picture disc CD I had found (the
catalogue number is CBAK 4011).  I have bought the CD and have transcribed
the first of the two interviews on it, and Jeffry C. Burka has very kindly
proofread it for me.  However, there are a few spots on the recording
where neither of us could make out clearly what was said.  Before posting
the transcript to the Net, therefore, I would like to throw a few questions
into the group so that the garbled patches may be corrected.  Here goes:

1) Who directed the video that accompanied the original single release of
_The Dreaming_, and where (ie, which studio) was it shot?

2) The interview (which primarily concerned the _The Dreaming_ album, BTW)
cites Rolf Harris as an influence, and the interviewer mentions a particular
piece of work (presumably by Rolf) as an example.  The name of this
work sounds like "Sun Arise".  Does anyone know what this might be?

3) On _Houdini_, Kate's singing teacher, Gordon Farrell (sp?) is credited,
and she said this was because he contributed a vocal to the track.  What
precisely is this vocal?

Thanks in advance,

Stephen
