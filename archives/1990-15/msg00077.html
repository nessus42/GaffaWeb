Hey,

henry_burdett_messenger@cup.portal.com wrote:
>Has anybody heard anything about Fripp & Fripp?

I think their official name for a while was Sunday all over the World,
according to the liner note on Fripp's 3 or so articles in Guitar Player
last year.  The League started touring again a couple of months ago, or
so it seems.  I think they perform randomly, although Fripp said the
current touring group is in the "3-year guitar craft course for becoming
professional musicians".  I presume that means that they will be
recording music.  Sunday might be an aspect of this phase, although I
think it too may just be something random.  The League's most recent
live music is quite simply amazing compared to the League tape of '88,
which in turn is quite amazing compared to their "Live!" recording.

- Brian
