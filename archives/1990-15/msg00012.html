At first I thought you were talking about Amherst, NY or something,
but you're definitely describing Amherst Mass.  And you didn't stop in
and visit while you were in town!  Some nerve.

The store in question is Al-Bums and it totally sucks.  They have got
some bootleg albums, and they have a nice bootleg Wuthering Heights
poster as well for cheap, but other than that, feh!

The store y'all wanna visit if you're in Western Mass is Main Street
Records in Northampton, just a short hop down Route 9.  They have lots
more Kate stuff, including all the import CD singles you could ever
want.  Not to mention videos, books, maybe posters...  They also have
a new branch called Son Of Main Street which has rare stuff but I've
not been there yet.  I have enough problems getting the non-rare stuff
I want, like the new Foetus disc...
