Hi everyone!  Once again, I went on a post-paycheck Kate binge...
actually, this time I only bought one poster.  It's the "Love and
Anger" poster with the color picture of Kate and the ballet dancers--
and the picture itself is on about a quarter of the poster, at the
bottom left-hand corner!  The rest of the poster (and it's like
5'x3') is just plain black.  It's strange, but neat.  Has anyone else
seen this one?  I hadn't until this afternoon.  I think those people
in the stores know when I get paid...  :-)

I've had some requests for the lyrics I formatted.  I haven't
forgotten!  They're in the process of being converted from MS Word to
LaTex, so it'll be a little longer.  But I will send them to anybody
who asked (or asks) me.  I'll be changing e-mail addresses in about a
week (new job, hurray!), so things may be a little frotzed for a bit.

Off the subject of KT: Sinead O'Connor is coming to Austin.  What's
the public opinion?  Would it be worth it?  I still haven't let myself
off for missing Jane Siberry. :-(
