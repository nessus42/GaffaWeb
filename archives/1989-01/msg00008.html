Small notes:

        - `City Slab Horror' was reissued with a slightly different front cover
          than the original. The back cover and track listing is the same.

        - The Nettwerk `Dead Eyes Opened' EP in no way, shape or form resembles
          the original Ink `Dead Eyes Opened' EP (other than the fact that both
          have versions of the title track on them, and they aren't even the
          same)

Other than the presumed existance of the `City Slab Horror' CD (which I've
never seen) and the cat. # of the `Bulkhead' CD (which I've seen but
did not buy, as it's a compilation), this all comes from my collection, so
all the info. is accurate.  Any further fleshing out of this info. wanted,
e-mail me (Don't bore the list) ...
