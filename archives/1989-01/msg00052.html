_December..._ out of print

From: "Joe Habermann" <haberman@umn-cs.cs.umn.edu>
Date: Tue, 10 Jan 89 00:39:09 CST
Subject: _December..._ out of print

I've tried to order it a couple of times, but have been told that the
12" with _December_Will_Be_Magic_Again_ is out of print.  (Also on this
record are WH and Exp. IV, methinks.)

Anyway, I've pretty much exhausted the local import and collector shops
here in the Twin Cities.  As I am more of a music consumer than a
collector, I wouldn't know of any good way of tracking down music as
rare as that is.  Are there many copies of this work floating about?
If anyone locally can secure a new copy of this this I'd surely
appreciate it and make it worth your trouble.  However, don't try to
pull a "Chuck" and ask $300 for it.  (That was his name, wasn't it?)

By the way, I believe someone back a while asked about the numbering on
the back of the latest R.E.M. album.  That is, 1-2-3-R-5...  I just
heard the band say on "Rockline" that it was just an idea of Michael's
designed to catch the eye.

Oh, well.
