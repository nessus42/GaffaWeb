William LeFebvre mentioned that he likes the Dr. Calculus track, "Full
Of Love" on the "She's Having A Baby" soundtrack.  Has anybody else
noticed that it's a remake of their 1986 single "Perfume From Spain"
(also on the Dr. Calculus album "Designer Beatnik")?  They basically
wrote new lyrics to the song and remixed it.  It's very disorienting
to hear the new version after becoming used to the original!
