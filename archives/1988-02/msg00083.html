      [	I don't know a whole lot, but I'll tell you what I do.  She's

	contact with Robert Fripp might result in some good

	ran around nude as a female werewolf in the wonderful movie
	*In the Company of Wolves*.  She sometimes designs her her own
	strange electronic instruments.  She's put out a few really
	good solo albums, most of which were recorded in her own
	four-track studio.  -- |>oug ]
