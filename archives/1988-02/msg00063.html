Subject: Singles

I just bought the Singles collection for $20...The guy said someone
had sold it to him used, and he thought $20 was a fair price.  I
agreed, but then I would have thought $30, $40, or $50 was a fair
price also.  $100 is too much, though.  The recordings are quite good;
I'm used to CD's, but this wasn't much worse.  My only complaint is
the lack of B-sides; out of the 12 records, I was hoping for 12 new
songs.  However, I only heard about 4-5 that I know are on no album of
Kate's.  Question: Does Kate have many other songs that can only be
purchased as B-sides, or on albums like "She's Having...?"  If so,
where can they be found?  I thought this addiction would stop when I
bought all the CD's, but it hasn't.

Last question:  Any latest speculations on a new album?
