>Yes, the question is, who IS Swami Ananda Nagara?  He is credited with
>producing the Dukes of Stratosphear's _25 O'Clock_ and a couple of
>songs off of The Woodentops' _Well Well Well..._.
>    -- John

Swami Anand Nagara is another name for John Leckie, who you may recall
produced XTC's first two albums.  Apparently he is also a real swami
(I wonder what it takes to become one) and so when it says (on the
back cover of 25 O'Clock) "Produced with love by John Leckie, Swami
Anand Nagara and the Dukes" the first two names refer to the same
person.
