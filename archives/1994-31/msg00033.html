What can I say??  I'm overwhelmed and rather touched by the support
you guys have extended to a less than 100% Kate fan.  I have yet
to see all the messages posted on this thread.  Due to strange
delays between articles being posted and arriving on my machine I
often get to see replys before receiving the original article. 
It's clear from the posts I have seen that you consider me a fan
to be nurtured rather than cast out!!

I rather liked Mikael's suggestion to buy nothing but Kate's work. 
Nice try, but I'm afraid I like others as much as I like Kate
(Tori Amos, Amy X Neuburg, Indigo Girls etc), and I also want to try
*new* stuff (Sarah McLachlan, Happy Rhodes.)

I *will* however, make sure I get at least another one Kate album
the very next time I'm in a music store.  From the "Rate Kate"
thread, it would appear I have little choice but to get "The
Hounds of Love" (nice one Connie - that thread should increase the
volume of postings on r.m.g)

Thanks for your support and indulgence - you really are one of the
nicest bunch of people around.
