   As near as we can tell, the three photos were intended to go
together: the sand (or ashes or soil) the flower (from the album
cover) and the peach. Possibly to express the cycle of life, or
simply to show three sensual textures, letting sand flow through
your fingers, the aroma, beauty and softness of a flower and the 
fuzzy texture and the taste of a peach. But they never appeared
together as far as I know.
