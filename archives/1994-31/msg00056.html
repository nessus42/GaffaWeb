At the risk of making Karen paranoid about more Kate in newspapers :)....

the Globe and Mail (Toronto) on Saturday/24 had a full-page feature on
Larry Adler and the new record, complete with a picture of Kate and the
Great Man. 

She is on the left, resting her head on his chest, with him on the
right. Sorry to say that she looks very puffy and tired, even in
comparison to a guy in his 80s :)

In the story itself he talks about the record but not about Kate
specifically - mostly about Sting and Meatloaf (who he likes!). He says
generally though that he was very surprised at the good quality of the
performances, since he didn't know a lot about the rock stars of today.
