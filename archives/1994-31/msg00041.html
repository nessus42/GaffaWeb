> I posted this response to a query a couple of months ago and got no
> enlightenment about the origin or value of these boxed sets.  Now that
> everyone's back from the summer, could someone give us the scoop here?
[...]
> I'm curious what the "word" is on these, too. Seems clear Kate had nothing
> to do with their production.  They seem awfully well-constructed for
> "bootleg" type materials.  I think I paid a similar price, something over
> $50 each.

They`re neither official nor bootleg - merely "unofficial". The CDs
are regular EMI releases but everything else is produced by UFO Books.
The photos they used are all, as far as I know, by Gered Mankowitz or
other freelance photographers. The "Lioness at Heart" one is IMHO much
better produced than the latter one, which has a basically random
assortment of photos. I can't remember exactly what they went for when
they were released, something on the order of L20-25. Don't know how
easier they are to pick up now.
