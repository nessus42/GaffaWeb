Hey this is a good thread I will even de-lurk to join this one.

5 favorite albums
1. Hounds of Love - I became a fan with this one. Though I only listen to 
		    it once or twice a year. Since I listened to it a lot 
		    durring a bad time in my life when I got the album.
		    So I only  listen to it when I want to be in a 
		    melanchoy mood. It has nothing to do with the album, 
		    it just brings back a lot of memories.

2. The Dreaming - Just a good album to listen to.

3. The Sensual World- Only a few highlights, but taken as a whole is 
		      pretty good.

4. The Kick Inside - If only for Wouthering Heights a fine album, and I 
		     do like The Man with a Child in his Eyes as well.

5. The Red Shoes - As a whole not great, but I love Moments of Pleasure 
		   and like Top of the City and You're the One.

Special Honarable Mention Aspects of the Sensual World.

Favorite Songs.
1. Wuthering Heights
2. Moments of Pleasure
3. Hounds of Love
4. Suspended in Gaffa
5. Cloudbusting

These are the Kate songs and albums that mean the most to me.
