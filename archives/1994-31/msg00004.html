1. The Dreaming  - I still say WOW! when I listen to this
    MASTERPIECE!!!!!!!!! 
2. Hounds Of Love - Would've been number 1 if the whole
    album was about The Ninth Wave...(She should've spent
    her time and money on making a moving about this 
    instead of TLTCTC....oh well)
3. Never For Ever - Hints of what was to come
4. The Red Shoes - Lily, MoP, TRS....yummy! the rest is 
    so-so to me....
5. Lionheart - this was my first Kate album.....I love it....
    many people dislike it...but we mustn't forget that it
    was made in a very short period of time...all things
    considered, it's a pretty good album.
6. The Kick Inside - Still one of the alltime best debut
    album, period. (altho, it hasn't aged very well, IMHO)
7. The Sensual World - Apart from The Fog and TWW, there
    isn't much here....quite a letdown after HoL, IMHO.

thank you for your support ;-)
