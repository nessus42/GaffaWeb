1) "hounds of love"  it's the first kb disc i ever bought... 

2) "red shoes"  though kate has my deepest sympathies, finally there is a 
real deep element of emotion that i don't think she's shown before. some 
of the more up tunes ("eat the music" for example) seem a bit forced in 
comparison...

3) "the dreaming"  definitely her most intellectual work..

4) "lionheart"  fun to hear her getting a bit more settled into a 
distinctive style..

5) "kick inside"  god, she was so innocent!

6) "never for ever"  i was so disappointed to find out that she smoked 
cigarettes.. but this is still such a neat cd.. always hear something new 
when i listen..

7) "sensual world"  it's not that i don't like this one... it's just kind 
of ...well, boring... and i really don't like the videos... 

now, what about everyone's favorite b-side??

i vote "under the ivy" cause it always makes me cry...
