		Here's my 2 cents:

1.  Hounds of Love - The #1 album of all time, no question
2.  The Dreaming - Still gives me chills
3.  The Sensual World - My first!
4.  The Red Shoes - Poppy but classy
5.  Never For Ever - Has its high points
6.  The Kick Inside - So-so
7.  Lionheart - Blah - Not even 'Coffee Homeground' could save this one

		Top 5 Tracks

1.  Running up That Hill
2.  Get Out of My House
3.  Waking the Witch
4.  Deeper Understanding
5.  Song of Solomon

		Bottom 5 Tracks

1.  Rocket's Tail
2.  You're the One
3.  Night Scented Stock
4.  Walk Straight Down the Middle
5. Reaching Out

		5 Best Non-Album (Demos not included)

1.  Under the Ivy
2.  Ne T'En Fui Pas
3.  Ran Tan Waltz
4.  Hounds (Alternative)
5.  Not This Time/Passing Through Air
