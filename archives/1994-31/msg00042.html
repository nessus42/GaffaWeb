Hi!

This is an Announcement.

On Saturday, October 8, 1994 from 2:00 PM until whenever, woj and Meredith
will be hosting an OcTorifest/ectogathering/belated KaTemas/housewarming party
at their humble abode in the Old Schoolhouse #6 in Boonton, NJ, USA.  All who
are willing to make the trek into the wilds of Northern New Jersey are invited!

There will be food, drink (perhaps of the fuzzy blue variety ;), good company,
and of course tons o' music, videos, and rarities by our favorite artists (Tori,
Happy, KaTe, Sarah, and many more).  Bring acoustic instruments if you have
them, and there can be live music, too!  We might even have a couple of door
prizes, who knows?

Please RSVP before October 6 if you'd like to come.  Send a message with
"Party" as the subject line to meth@delphi.com, and make sure you cc woj
at woj@remus.rutgers.edu.  If you'd like to bring food and/or drink you're
more than welcome - let us know in your message.  Overnight accommodations
are available but limited, so please let us know that, too.  Directions will
be sent to all responders posthaste.

Looking forward to seeing you all on the 8th...

Meredith and woj
