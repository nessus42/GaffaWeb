> YKTR has been reissued and includes additional songs such as Happy Workers
> from the Toys soundtrack.

No    it     hasn't!  That is *not* a reissue.  It is a bootleg.
There's a difference.  For some of you it may be a matter of semantics,
but "reissue" implies an official release.  There are a lot of reasons 
that that implication should be avoided.  For one thing, an actual 
reissue of YKTR would send entirely the wrong message about how 
Tori feels about that album.  There is not, and probably never 
will be an actual reissue of YKTR.  

As someone pointed out, Atlantic could probably reissue the album if
Tori ever leaves the label but since Tori's contract evidently calls 
for something like five more albums, she's with Atlantic for the
forseeable future.
