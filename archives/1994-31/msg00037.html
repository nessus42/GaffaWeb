My top 7 albums are;

1) The Red Shoes; I don't really know why, but I keep coming back to this
   one, I guess I just gotta keep dancing!

2) Hounds of Love; What can I say? I love it!

3) The Sensual World; For some reason 'Deeper Understanding' has a familiar
   tone to it, could it have anything to do with me writing this at 2am
   local time?

4) & 5) Never For Ever and The Kick Inside ; These have some of my favourite
   songs.

6) Lionheart; Quite a few good songs here and a few better ones to spruce
   things up too.

7) The Dreaming; I haven't quite got into this one yet, I'll just go and try
   it for the 200'th time and try again...


8) any album by any other artist.
 
My top five non-album tracks are;

1) My Lagan Love
2) Ken
3) The Handsome Cabin Boy
4) Under The Ivy
5) You Want Alchemy

All above, IMHO.
