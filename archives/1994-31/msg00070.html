So, for my fist posting back on L-H, I will follow along.

1. Hounds of Love - I love both sides. The Ninth wave is incredible.
2. The Dreaming - Get out of My House. Night of the Swallow. Houdini...
3. The Sensual World - This album will always bring back memories of my first
                       true love. I actually love EVERY song on this album.
                       Rockets Tail and This Womans Work top every thing.
4. Never For Ever / The Kick Inside - I like these albums, but they don't
                                      "effect" me the way the others do.
6. Lionheart - Hammer Horror. The rest of the album is ok. (this was my first
               Kate CD.... but the last album bought (before TRS)).
7. The Red Shoes - Maybe if I listened to it more....

Top songs:
1. This Woman's Work
2. Under Ice
3. Get Out of My House
4. Running Up That Hill
5. Rocket's Tail

Bottom Five:
1. Ken
2. That Prince song (not that I don't like Prince.... the song just sucks)
3. uh... That's about it...

Well, that was exciting.
