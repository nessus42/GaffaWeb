Kate is God; we all know that.  But I have frequently heard of interesting, new
musicians via this newsgroup, and thought I'd return the favor.  I recently saw
the Indigo Girls in D.C., and before them, a guy named "Darden Smith" took the
stage, and played some of the best music I've heard since Kate's last album.  I
went out and got his recent CD, "Trouble No More."  It is absolutely fantastic,
and I *strongly* recommend it!  Every cut is energetic, intelligent, and just
plain great!  When I'm not suspended in gaffa, I'm "rockin'" w/Darden.  I'd be
interested in hearing any other "hound" opinions on this great new artist...

P.S.  The Indigo Girls, while still no Kate, were also *great* as usual.
