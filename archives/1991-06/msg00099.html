Argh! Despite TV Times listing, no Kate on SNL

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Wed, 27 Feb 91 13:27:14 PST
Subject: Argh! Despite TV Times listing, no Kate on SNL


Last night, I noticed the following TV Times listing for
11:00 pm on channel 20, an independent station in San Francisco:

The Best of Saturday Night Live: Eric Idle; Kate Bush

Now I know that when repeats of this show are broadcast, the Kate
parts are usually cut out.  But I figured that since they mentioned
her in the listing, this time they'd leave her in.  No such luck!

Well, at least I now have a very good copy of the ending credits for
that show, with Kate on stage with the Not Ready For Prime Time Players.
Jane Curtin and Larraine Newman give Kate hugs, and Eric Idle gives her a
kiss.

This is so frustrating!
