I've been reading rec.music.gaffa for some time now but have only recently
become rabidly Katefected. 8) Anyway, as this is my first post, I'll have to
ask you all to bear with me and my simplistic questions. I just bought TKI,
(bloody hard to find it, too) and noticed a fairly early publishing date.
Is this Kate's first album? Also, who is IED? Also, How many albums has
She come out with? Is there any way I could satisfy the completist in me and
get them all in one (or two) fell swoops? I notice a fair amount of discussion
about "the boxed set." Is this the coveted complete collection? Anyway, I'm
sure there are many more shy newcomers like myself who lie awake at night
wondering at the answers to these and other questions. If you guys think
the answers are worth posting to the Net, I'm sure others would be interested,
otherwise, e-mail is fine.

Yours in Dreaming,
