Subject: terry gilliam is a lovehound???

Terry Gilliam is thanked on the HoL credits because he was going to have
made the videos for it, and possibly even have done a long-form vid for
"The Ninth Wave" (Now wouldn't that have been TOO F--KING AWESOME FOR
WORDS?  YOW!)  Unfortunately, none of these events came to pass.  The
video for "Running Up That Hill" was directed by David Garfath, who worked
with Gilliam, and the video for "Cloudbusting" was directed by Julian
Doyle, who is also a long-time Gilliam associate.  Gilliam himself was
involved with the storyboarding for "Cloudbusting," I believe.

Of course, "Hounds of Love" and "The Big Sky" videos were done by Kate
herself.
