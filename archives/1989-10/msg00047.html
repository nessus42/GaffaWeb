As far as I recall, after seeing the Dallas video on TV and by recalling
my own French pronunciation abilities, Jarre is one syllable. If there was
an accent on the ending 'e', then it would indeed be two.

If you want another album to go for, then these are what are available:-

Oxygene
Magnetic Fields
Equinoxe
Zoolook
Rendezvous
Revolutions

The Concerts in China
The Essential Jarre
J.M. Jarre Live in Houston

I think that the next one to go for would be Oxygene, which has some well
known pieces on it (been used in lots of movies etc.), or possibly Rendezvous.
I think my favoutrite has been Zoolook - a blow out of an album.

In the compilations, The Concerts in China are highly interesting.
