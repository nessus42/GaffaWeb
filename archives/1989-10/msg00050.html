Subject: CALL FOR DISCUSSION: rec.music.dylan
Newsgroups: rec.music.folk, rec.music.makers, rec.music.gaffa, rec.music.dementia
Organization: Penn State University - Center for Academic Computing
Sender: netnews@ms.uky.edu


This is a call for discussion for a potential rec.music group that would
discuss the music of Bob Dylan.  Discussions could cover anything relating
to Dylan, including an analysis of his lyrics, information about recordings
and tours, reviews of his work, and whatever else the group found
worthwhile.

I am willing to put in whatever time and effort is necessary to moderate
the group.

ANY DISCUSSION OF THIS PROPOSAL MUST BE CONDUCTED IN NEWS.GROUPS.
All opinions would be appreciated.
