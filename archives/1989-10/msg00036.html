Due to popular demand (Ok, so I am lazy and can't be botherrred to work
out mail paths to all these people ).

Here She is for X windows.

Once again, she is a little low-resolution.

This is Icon sized, however I have no idea how to go about seting an
icon in X ( I am a very basic X user ).

Cut at the dotted line ( I think getting rid of all extra lines is
important ).

Then at the next one further down ( remove that line too ).

The first is Her, suitable for 

	xsetroot -bitmap kate.bm

the second is a mask to make Her your cursor! A bit big but looks really
wierd haviong Her face float around the screen

	xsetroot -cursor kate.bm kate.mask.

Ps,
	I don't know why the two files are such radically different
	formats, they seem to work though.

Pps,
	I have a version that is full screen size for a background, but
	it is _huge_, so I am not happy about mailing it around. I may
	be able to get the program which generates it to Dan who has
	the images and so have it available form there.
	


Enjoy!
