Subject: The Stranglers

There's a new CD from the Stranglers available (from the U.K.).  It's
a Radio 1 Sessions disc, featuring BBC recordings of "The Man They Love
to Hate", "Nuclear Device / Genetix", and "Down in the Sewer".  It was
recorded in 1982, and it's pretty good.  Fairly close to the originals,
but with some twists.
