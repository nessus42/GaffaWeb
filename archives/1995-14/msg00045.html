Hey folks --

Saw "While You Were Sleeping" last night (_really_ sweet, in a warm-fuzzy 
sort of way...), and the previews were preceded by a long version of the 
new Frutopia ad -- same music (with indeciferable lyrics), and a whole 
lot of "some people are like this... because... some people do that...  
because..." style messages for about 60 seconds.  Same eye-straining 
kaleidoscope backgrounds, of course.  The long version (unlike the new TV 
spot) was not for a specific flavor (I'm pretty sure the new TV ad is for 
the iced teas, right?) but for the entire product line.  

Man, I must be starved for new Kate, if a 60 second rhythm loop with a 
few impossible to understand, heavily reverbed gibberish vocals from her 
majesty is enough to get me so excited!

-- Gregg
