A few weeks ago, I asked for advice here about obtaining CDs that could
duplicate the music on my five Hounds of Love-era 12-inch singles. Many of
you offered input. I appreciated that and thanked you all individually. I
have ordered the appropriate CDs. As a result, I no longer have a need for
the 12-inch singles, so I would like to sell them.

These are all the original picture-sleeve 12-inch singles issued in the
U.K. I bought them as imports when I worked at a Florida record store. At
the most, they have been played once or twice; some have never been played
(although none are still sealed). The sleeves and vinyl are all in mint or
mint-minus condition (visual grading), and they have been handled as
little as possible over the past decade.

Here are the contents:

12 KB 1--Running Up That Hill, Under the Ivy, Running Up That Hill
(Instrumental)

12 KB 2--Cloudbusting (The Organon Re-mix), Burning Bridge, My Lagan Love

12 KB 3--Hounds of Love (Alternative), Jig of Life, The Handsome Cabin Boy

12 KB 4--The Big Sky (Meteorological Mix), Not This Time, The Morning Fog

12 KB 5--Experiment IV (12" Mix), Wuthering Heights (New Vocal), December
Will Be Magic Again

I will sell each disc for the best offer I receive by noon a week from
today--Friday, May 5. The sale prices will include shipping. Foreign
orders are fine, but payment must be by money order in U.S. funds. I also
reserve the right to wait for personal checks to clear. You may respond to
this email address if you like or my regular internet address, 
rasalas@startribune.com.

Thank you.
