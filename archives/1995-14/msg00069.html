Whoever posted the note the other day about the Fruitopia commercials 
airing on the east coast gave me an idea:

If you are watching evening TV in eastern or cental time zones and you 
see a Fruitopia ad on a national broadcast that will air later in the 
Pacific time zone, run in and post it.  

Those of us in the Pacific zone can then set our VCRs in advance, and 
hopefully capture these elusive things when they turn up.  This might be 
the best way we'll be able to compile a full set of them.

I will volunteer to check rec.music.gaffa every evening at 6:45 PM which 
will give easterners until 8:45 to post any commercials that showed up.  
If someone else (or better yet, two people) would volunteer to be backups 
for this system we stand a better chance.

Comments?  Ideas?  Laughter?  

Karen  kln@crl.com  Who ever thought time zones could be an advantage?
