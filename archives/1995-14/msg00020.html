The fourth edition of the "Goldmine Guide to Collectible Record Albums"
lists, in the late additions section under Hendrix, EMI-America 17171 (DJ)
Band of Gypsies 2. It describes it as a prototype pressing to test the
marble vinyl for HOL, and says the label credits Kate, but the disc plays
one side of BOG2 on both sides. No info about number of copies,
transactions, or prices.
