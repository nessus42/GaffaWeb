out Larry Adler was interviewed in the Sky News studio and the video

                                    " When we made the video 
together ,the one you just showed a bit of ,I had gout in my foot and 
whenever I had to go to my bedroom, Kate would escort me like a 
nurse, and I just was so charmed by her ."
