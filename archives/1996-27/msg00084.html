Unless Bryan Dongray or Mr. Wentz object,
I am adding an audio link to my KateWorlds
page to let people listen to Bryan's
fine midi renditions of kate's songs
as they cruise the VRML worlds.  I will
be adding a new world shortly, a Talosian
Spaceport, which because it is not textured
(that is, all VRML with one background
texture) should load much more quickly.
You will still need the Live3D plugin
or Netscape 3.0.  The VRML world is
about to move on to the next version of
the language, VRML 2.0 which is a fully
animatible 3D language.  I will be converting
the KateWorlds to this as time permits
and beginning to add more motion and
more interesting worlds.

Damm, this is soooo much fun!  The tools
we are getting are just sooo cool!

I used wavs of Kate, but they are just
TOO DAMM BIG for the web.  OTH, if
you link to them on the harddrive or
a CD-ROM, they work very nicely.

Albums are about to get a whole lot
more fun.  ;-)
