first, apologize's if this was posted back in Oct '95 - 

I was going through old stacks of Q magazine & back in issue 109, Q gave
away a complilation cassette entitled "Drive!, the Ultimate Crusin' Tape".
 the reason i bring this to r.m.g is that on the tape, track 3 actually,
is RUTH! I thought i might share with you all what Q had to say about RUTH
in their little guide to the tape:

"Aside frome her breakthrough effort, Wuthuring Heights, this
intoxicatingly rhythmic beauty from Aug '85 was Bush's most successful
single, as well as her most significant, signalling a new complexity and
maturity for the already prodigous talent.  According to the astonishing
lyric, she wished to swap places with no less than God.  Pushing things a
tad, even for Kate.  A wizardette, a true star."

i like the wizardette part myself.... :)
