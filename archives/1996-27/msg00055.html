> I was able to acquire a radio station promo of Sarah Brightman singing "Love

> Webber's musical 'Aspects of Love'.  Quite good.  I've only heard a bit of

You might try Tower's ordering 800 number for a $21 import 
called 'Surrender; The Unexpected Songs' in which she sings 
seventeen Lloyd Webber pieces including 'Piano' ('Memory' in 
Italian) and some others from Sunset Boulevard, Cats, Phantom, 
and a haunting requiem called 'Pie Jesu.' Sorry, I don't have 
the number on me, but any Tower would be able to give it to you.

Her vocal ability has grown a great deal since "The Songs That 
Got Away" and despite the fact that Lloyd Webber's footprints 
are all over this CD, it's very impressive and I recommend it.
