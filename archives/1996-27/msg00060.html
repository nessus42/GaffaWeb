Love-hounds ,


I  have one recent review you might not have come across :

The UK magazine Keyboard Review in its' July issue ran a review of 
the album , and  the reviewer felt that the 
album failed to make much of an impression " till track 8 
........Elvis Costelloe... when the magic really starts .....and Kate 
Bush, of all people, singing in gaelic ,with a stirring string and 
harp accompaniment ...from here the album never looks back "

As I've said before DJ's in Ireland especially seemed to comment on Kate's track 
on the week of release, intrigued that she sang in Irish and 
commenting after playing the track " and that would you believe was 
Kate Bush ". Common Ground entered the Irish album charts at No.6 
,peaked at No.5 and is still in the top ten as I write .
