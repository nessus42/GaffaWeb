bruceha@microsoft.com (Bruce Hale) sez:
>What's in the Cocteau Twins box set that's not available on 
>the regular CDs? What CDs are in the box set?

there are ten cd singles in the box set. they include the lullabies ep,
the peppermint pig ep, the sunburst and snowblind ep (which can also be
found attached to the head over heels album), the spangle maker 12",
the aikea guinea ep, the tiny dynamine ep, the echoes in a shallow bay
ep (the latter two eps are also available combined onto one cd), the
love's easy tears ep, the iceblink luck single and a single with four
rare tracks including "dials," "crushed" (taken from the _lonely is an
eyesore_ compilation), "the high monkey-monk" (from the gigantic 2
compilation) and "oomingmak" (this is an alternative version of a song
from _victorialand_).
