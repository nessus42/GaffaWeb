Well, I was quite shocked to see a big stack of "The Red Shoes" in my local
World of Music (Munich) last Thursday.  And for only DM 24,99, not the usual DM
36,99.  ???  I was simply there to collect information about the new Sarah
McLachlan album/single.  No luck there.  First impressions of TRS:
1. Rubberband Girl - growing on me
2. And So Is Love - like it
3. Eat The Music - still hate it
4. Moments Of Pleasure - stunning
5. The Song Of Solomon - like it
6. Lily - like it
7. The Red Shoes - like it
8. Top Of The City - it's okay
9. Constellation Of The Heart - it's okay
10. Big Stripey Lie - love it, always did
11. Why Should I Love You? - bleck!
12. You're The One - it's okay.

Happy listening, guys.
Johnny
