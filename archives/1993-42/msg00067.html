I left on my lunch to pick up TRS.  I fought traffic.  I fought parking.
I bought two copies, one for a friend and one for myself.  Then I
had to deal with traffic and parking on the way back.  Nevertheless,
I was happy.  That is until I opened up my TRS and found inside:

PEARL JAM   

Aaaaaaaaahhhhhh!!!!!!!!!!

It might have been the album "Ten", I'm not sure, since at that point my
vision went blurry and I felt sick.  Put it on the CD player and sure
enough -- Pearl Jam, both copies.

I immediately drove back to the store.  The clerk went 7 for 7 on the
Peal Jam before he found two that actually had TRS inside.

I am pissed,
