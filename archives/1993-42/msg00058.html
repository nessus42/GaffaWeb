Leafing through my copy of Radio Times (TV Guide to you US Gaffans) a HMV 
Christmas Guide fell out. Along with all the usual stuff that appears in these
things such as what CD's to buy your loved ones for Christmas there appeared
a Kate Bush competion.

Basically the prize is a personalised EMI Kate Bush Gold Disc plus 10 CD's of
The Red Shoes for the lucky runners-up. The address for the competition is below 
Kate Bush Comp
Dept Seven
Marketing Leaders Ltd
PO Box 144
Plymouth
PL1 1AX
United Kingdom

The answers have to be in by 7th Jan 1994.
What is the question I hear you all ask, well here it is.

What was the title of Kate Bush's first number one hit?

A real hard one there, I guess this information is of no use to non UK Gaffans
but it didn't say anything about overseas people not entering. Apologies to
everyone if this is a boring message but the prize does look pretty impressive
so I'd thought I'd share the information. As a postscript in another part of
the magazine they mention Moments Of Pleasure as being the next single?
