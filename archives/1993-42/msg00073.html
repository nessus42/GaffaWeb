Aren't they kind?

From: "D.M.Woodhead" <D.M.Woodhead@lut.ac.uk>
Date: Wed, 3 Nov 93 0:51:31 GMT
Subject: Aren't they kind?
To: Love-Hounds@uunet.UU.NET (Kate-Mailing)

While buying Future Music and Q magazine yesterday at WHSmith's in 
Loughborough (for the KaTe articles), the shop assistant told me to go to the
record department and ask about any KaTe merchandise they had spare. Off I
trundled upstairs and to my immense surprise, they GAVE me (totally free) a
poster, the 12" LP sleeve (what had they done with the record?) and the
promise that they will give me the complete promotional display from the shop
window when it's taken down next week. Aren't they kind?
