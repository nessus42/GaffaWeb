just came from the Las Vegas Tower release party.  

seems that there were only 3 of us there.  rather a dismal
turnout I must say (it was a last minute deal).  BUT because
the turnout was so bad....  we are all owners of videos, 
buttons, posters, and t-shirts (I've a short sleeved one and 
now I also want, I know this is greedy ; ), the long sleeve 
turtleneck)!!!   

I understand that there was a lithograph to be raffled but I
didn't see it.  (got there a half hour after it started but was
told they gave away a copy of RBG 12").

I'm going back tomorrow morning to see the manager, if I can 
get some more buttons (the cover) I'd be happy to send them out
(like I did w/ the flyers) to those that didn't have the chance 
to get one.  I'll post tomorrow *if* I get more button & how many
I'll be able to send out.

hope the SF one went better...

suzanne
