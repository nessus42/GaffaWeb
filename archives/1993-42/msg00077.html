dunno if there is an official single release of rbg planned (though it
would seem likely that columbia would release at least one other single
if trs is supposed to be the album that will break KaTe in the states)
but there is a promotional single for rbg. i found a copy in a cutout
bin monday for $3. here are the vitals:

kate bush - "rubberband girl" (columbia csk 5504)

	1. rubberband girl (edit)	4:07
	2. rubberband girl		4:42

the single is packaged in a regular jewel case. the photo on the insert
is the KaraTe KaTe photo. the insert is a flat sheet bearing the lyrics
to the song and a small picture of the album cover (in black and white)
on the reverse side. the cd is a light blue screen of the photo that is
on the rbg single released everywhere outside of the states.

i haven't listened to the edit closely enough to note the differences
between it and the album version. if anyone cares, i'll post it whenever
i get around to listening that closely.
