Goes without saying I love it :-)

The Trio are wonderful again, Prince kicks and Eric is
unobjectionable. Wish she'd find a new violinist.

To early to have much of an opinion on the tracks except the ones I've
heard before. RBG keeps growing on me, MoP is still magical. The only
really weak track is EtM, which I can only assume is an attempt to keep
KT an obscure cult artist in the US.

On the whole it seems to be more like the early albums than the more
recent stuff. An example of perfection in the art of writing damn good
songs. 

Now, for the important questions:

	Are those her feet?

	Has anyone found the KT yet?
