There's one other memorable Lenska performance, and that's in the
BBC Radiophonic Workshop's production of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the
Galaxy --- Ms. Lenska played an ill-fated trio of talented clone
archeologists, named Lintilla.  The cloning machine had apparently
become out of sync', such that it began the next Lintilla before it was
halfway through with the previous one.  "This meant that it could never
be shut off without committing murder.  Lawyers for the company attempted
various ways of re-defining murder, including different ways of spelling
it, but to no effect.  Fortunately, a solution has been found, but it
is rather unpleasant, and ...." so the narrator doesn't reveal it until
 the plot requires it...
