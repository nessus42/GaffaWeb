   Hi all!

  I was shopping at the mall yesterday, and while I was browsing around in
a certain store, I finally realized what the background music was. "Rocket
Man"! Boy, was I surprised! I asked the fellow at the register about it, and
he said it was a tape sent out to various stores. Name of the store? Gap Kids,
of all places! (Yes, I am a shrimp, I can wear a kids' large in certain items.)
Anyway, the fellow asked me why I was so excited about this particular song, so
I explained who KaTe was, what the song was, etc. in hopes of "further
enlightening the masses", as it were. None of the yuppie-type fiends working in
there had ever heard of her. Ah, well, their loss.....
