   Cynthia- I'm sorry I was a rude little rag. Nobody's perfect, I guess.

other people's in many ways, that apology felt good. 

   HINT FOR THE DAY: Try it kids, you'll LIKE it!

We're all human. We all have our right to agree or disagree, and to express
such thoughts and opinions as well. I just wish we could all be ADULT,
RESPONSIBLE, and tactful when we do it. I, for one, will try to do so in
the future.
