Did anyone see the David Letterman 10th Anniversary Special?
There was a big music number.  Emmylou Harris, Roseanne Cash,
Michelle Shocked, Nanci Griffith plus another lady or two
whose names I didn't catch, were there.  A group of women that
totaled humongous talent, and they were relegated to singing
backup to Bob Dylan, whose performance was shockingly bad.  I
mean, he sounded unintelligible.  I know, some may say he
always did, but I could make out his words in the past.  This
was awful.  Have there been any reports of Dylan having
something wrong, like maybe Parkinson's Disease or something?
He appeared on a music awards show last year, and was really
bad then too.  

Oh, Carole King was there on piano, also.  All that talent,
wasted essentially.  

Later,
