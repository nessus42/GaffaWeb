Dear Hounds,

   I've been reading Love-Hounds for months but this is the first thing
I've ever contributed..... Here goes...

  I went shopping along Oxford Street last Saturday ( to see what the HMV sale
had to offer ) and I was looking at a CD picture disk of an interview with
the Beatles. The CD was made by a firm called 'talkback' ( or something
like that ) but anyway, I couldn't help noticing that on the back of the
CD was a list of other interviews in the series. Among the list was ( you
guessed it ) an interview with Kate Bush. I searched the store so see if
they had it, and asked the assistant if they had any out the back, but had
no luck.

  I was wondering if any of you Love-Hounds have every heard of this CD
and, if any of you should happen to have a copy, then could you tell me
about it. ( i.e. How long ago was the interview done? what do they talk about?
and basically is it worth forking out 8.95 for it? ) 

  Further more, in the CD singles section of the store I managed to pick
up a (slightly battered) copy of The Sensual World CD single for a mere
50p !!!

  ( come along HMV - she's worth more than that !? )

  Alan.
