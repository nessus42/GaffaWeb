Most favourite KT song?

I still don't know what my most favourite one is, but the following make good
chances:

 - Get Out Of My House
 - Breating
 - Breating live
 - Houdini
 - Hello Earth
 - Night Of The Swallow
 - Love And Anger
 - The Fog

Most Favourite Album: The Dreaming
This is the most extreme album. That means: few people like it a lot.

The song that made me a KT fan: I dunno: Probably Get Out Of My House!
   I think it's the mixture of a desperate woman screaming and a woman who
is very certain of herself (somewhere in the middle of the song). On this
song Kate seems to have got rid of all brakes: she lets herself go. Just
great! [I wonder if this is proper English] Also the end of the song, when
she sings against the male voice (I don't know by head who it is). The sound
is just filling the whole room ... wonderful.
