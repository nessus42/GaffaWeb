	Well GG Allen is up to his old tricks again, eh? For those who aren't
familiar with his "work" his old bands were

	1. GG Allen and the Scum Fucks
	2. GG Allen and the Texas Nazi's

	Mr. Allen was using the first band before his prison stay for raping a
14 year old girl on stage during one of his shows. One might ask WHY a 14 year
old girl was at one of his shows in a bar, but that didn't seem to occur to the
authorities. He has been plagued by the authorities for years, it seems they
know when he's coming and they just wait for him to do something like the above
incidents. Maybe he should modify his act slightly if he would like to avoid
additional prison time, but then again if he smart enough to put out his own 
albums he would have thought about this already.
	Well I'll have to update my mental archives on GG Allen to include the
Murder Junkies. His music is actually not that bad on vinyl if you like
hardcore, but I'd stay WAAAAAYYYYY back at one of his performances if I went to
one. He'd have to be playing NEXT to my house for me to go, so I don't think
I'll be seeing him anytime in the near future.
