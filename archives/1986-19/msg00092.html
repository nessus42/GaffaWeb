News update:

Kate's LP The Whole Story entered the Billboard album
charts in the December 20th issue at No.158. In the latest
issue (December 27th), it has risen thirty-nine places
to No. 119. In Cashbox (Dec. 20th) it entered at No. 110,
and has now risen (Dec. 27th) to No. 90.
In the Sunset Blvd. branch of Tower Records, the LP is the
eighth bestselling album in the store this week.
