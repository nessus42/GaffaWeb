For those few out there like me who are neurotically obsessed with
obtaining two copies of every Kate Bush related item, there is
a limited edition poster sleeve version of the British 7-inch single
of "Don't Give Up".  The poster is pretty boring though.  It's a
blurry, low-rez still form the video.  Peter and Kate are hugging, and
we only see Kate's back.  But think about it... how often do we get a
picture of Kate's back?
