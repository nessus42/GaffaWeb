On the 23rd of March 1995, TAFKAP played an aftershow gig at the Emporium 
club in London. Kate was there & by a complete stroke of luck, so was I. 
He also thanks her in the sleevenotes to " The Gold Experience " album, 
although as far as i know she doesn,t appear on it. We had to wait until 
" Emancipation " for her vocals. I think from this we can assume they 
have actually met, so maybe their working relationship is closer than we 
thought, it's lasted at least 3 years.
