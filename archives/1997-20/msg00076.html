Just so everyone knows, I've put up lyrics of what I hear
on The Early Years recording. I happened to do this totally
separately to Wieland's posting, sorry I'm way behind in
reading LH.

transcribe what they think they hear and then compare, so
they do not get influenced by what someone else heard.
That way we should get a good approximation to what is
really sung. I guess I'll be working with Wieland to get
these right over the next few weeks.
