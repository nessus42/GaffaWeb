To me "Under The Ivy" is very simple and direct and beautiful. It is a shy
contemplative girl telling a boy her secret, the place she goes to get
away from everyone and meditate and  be her true self, and she is showing
how much she cares for him by telling him her secret place where he can
find her, away from the party.
