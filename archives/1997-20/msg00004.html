PROJECT LO are releasing there 2nd CD "Black Canvas" with special guest
Happy Rhodes. Happy sings two songs on the release including a version of
Mercy Street by Peter Gabriel. She will also be performing with the band
live later on this year.
Here are some comments about the first release "Dabblings In Darkness".

An instrumental blend of acoustic guitar, electronic keys, jazz and
ambiance, creating texture with a pulse from guitarist Bon Lozaga ( Gong )
and keyboardist Jim Loretangeli. Among the many guests are bassists Percy
Jones (Brand X), Gerald Veasley (Zawinul Syndicate), violinist Caryn Lin,
and drummers Pierre Moerlen (Gong) and Vic Stevens.

"Black Canvas" features Bon Lozaga, Happy Rhodes, bassist Percy Jones, &
violinist Caryn Lin.


 “...I could recommend the album solely because of “Sand”, a captivating
piece combining urgent, glistening keyboards and guitar with sorrowful,
long- lined soprano sax”

“...the music creates a mood and lets you fill in the details
imaginatively.”
“...Lozaga captures the spirit of Ralph Towner, John McLaughlin and of
