The Tori Amos Bootleg discography is up and running and now easily
accessible on the web! I keep it on my Tori home page, which is:

http://world.std.com/~fuzzbox/tori.html

It's available in either plain text form or netscape-enchanced form,
and there's also an index, that has the CDs in order chronologically,
alphabetically, by recording location, and cross-referenced...

Have fun and tell all your friends!
