If I recall, the story is that Kate walked into the Bush family den 
during the television broadcast of WUTHERING HEIGHTS just 
in time to see Cathy come through the window--doesn't she say, "glass and 
blood everywhere. . ." or something like that?  Anyhow. . .

I'm fascinated by the influence of televised films, particularly 
Hammer films, on Kate's body of work.  Last night, the cable channel ENCORE showed the Hammer film, THE DEVIL'S BRIDE, aka THE 
DEVIL RIDES OUT.  During the narrative, M. le Duc de Richlieau, played by 
Christopher Lee, recites the prayer found in "Lily" or, at least, a 
slight variation on it.  Also, there is a shot of Tanith--Nike [Arrighi 
or Aggirhi] pursued by Rex--can't remember the actors name--which is a 
dead ringer for the shot of Kate pursued by The Man With the Hole in His 
Chin from the video for HoL.  The film will be rebroadcast, again, tonight.

So make of this what you will.  Thanks for reading.

Keith
