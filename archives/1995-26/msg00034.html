Excellent idea -- but perhaps the Tori fans on rec.music.toris-amos
had an even more excellent one. Every week they post an RFAQ -- REALLY
Frequently Asked Questions -- with a 'byline' of "Read this before
posting." The RFAQ is much shorter than the FAQ, (in our case, only
about, umm, a GAZILLION pages shorter) and would answer the oft "when
does she tour next?" "what's the best album to get for a newbie?" and
"why do you all talk about Fruitopia so much?"

The RFAQ could contain URL's to various Katesites or directions to the
FAQ. I know this is *borrowing* from r.m.t-a, but, hey, they were
borne of r.m.g.

See ya...or, rather...read ya!
