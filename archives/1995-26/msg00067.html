On Mon, 14 Aug 1995, Keith DeWeese x422 wrote:

> During the narrative, M. le Duc de Richlieau, played by Christopher 
> Lee, recites the prayer found in "Lily" or, at least, a slight 
> variation on it.

Which one? The spoken prayer at the beginning, or the ritual in the lyrics?

The first prayer, "O Thou, who giveth sustenance to the Universe...", is 
taken from the Charge of the Goddess - a very beautiful Wiccan piece of 
prayer, apparently written by Tuscany witches.

The second ritual, "Gabriel before me...", is taken from the Banishing 
Ritual of the Lesser Pentagram, which I believe was written by Aleister 
Crowley.

Hope this helps!
