Forgive me if this is a little off topic:

Yesterday I was at Target picking up school supplies
for the kiddies and happened to wander by the CD display.
They had one of those preview things where you can listen
to the disks before you buy, and Bjork's new one was in there,
so I fired it up.  I got to hear about 20 seconds of the first track
before the wife dragged me back over to assist with selecting
the notebooks and stuff.  I liked the 20 seconds or so that I
heard - strong, sort of Bonhamish drum beat with Bjork doing
whatever she does over it.  Any Bjork fans on the list who could
give me a review of the entire CD, and if it's worth buying or not?
Email would be fine.

Again, sorry for the off-topicness, but a guy's gotta' do something
between K8 releases!

(Also wanted to mention that I tried Fruitopia for the first time last
week.  The one I tried was a lot like Hawaiian Punch.  Not bad,
but definitely sugar coma material.....)
