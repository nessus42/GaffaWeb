Subject: Tori Amos Bootleg discography now available on the Web
Newsgroups: rec.music.tori-amos, rec.music.gaffa, alt.music.ecto, alt.music.alternative.female, rec.music.misc, rec.music.info, alt.music.bootlegs
Organization: Department Of Redundancy Department

In case I don't happen to post it regularly, the Tori Amos Bootleg
discography is now easily available on the Web. I keep it at my
home page, which is: http://world.std.com/~fuzzbox/fuzzbox.html

Follow the links to my Tori page, which is actually the most visible link.

It's available as plain-text, or fancy Netscape versions, and there's
also a wonderful index (thanks John Underwood!) that has the CDs in 
alphabetical order, chronological by year, and also listed by country 
of recording. There's also a fully cross-referenced guide, to prevent 
you getting ripped off by buying duplicate CDs. You can reach the 
index directly by using this URL: http://world.std.com/~fuzzbox/itabd.html

Enjoy it, and let me know if you have any comments!
