Okay, new survey:

	Which single cover is your favorite and why? Which album 
cover? For those who don't have the older singles, they are displayed
on the inner cover of 'The Whole Story.'
----------------------------------------------------------------------


	My fave single is for Wuthering Heights -- because it was the
first single, and really established her theatrical nature. The
expression on her face is far more sensuous than on the similar pic
which adorned The Kick Inside album cover and it must've really set
her apart from the typical positioning of women artists in the late
seventies. Ne T'Enfuis Pas would be my second, just 'cuz she looks so
good.

	My fave album cover is The Dreaming...also 'cuz she looks so
good. I love that it references the song Houdini, while piquing your
interest on just what the hell she's doing with that guy and the key
in her mouth! The sepia photo, tinted with oranges and golds was a
nice touch, and it fits the mood of what I think is her earthiest
album.
