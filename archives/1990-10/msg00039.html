This is the VOTE TALLY for the creation of rec.radio.noncomm, a new newsgroup
to provide a forum for the discussion of noncommercial radio
among broadcasters and listeners.

Voting ended March 13, 1990.

YES : 143
NO  :  30

By Usenet Group Creation guidelines, rec.radio.noncomm may be created.
A mass acknowledgement is at the end of this article.  Errors may be 
corrected until March 24 by sending corrections 
to me.  Some votes (all yes) were received after the deadline and
not counted.  If there are no objections from the 'YES' people,
the word 'noncommercial' will be completely spelled out.
I abbreviated because of my programming habits.

NAME:    rec.radio.noncomm(ercial)
CHARTER: unmoderated discussion of noncommercial radio.
Some topics to discuss are:
- listener opinion
- noncommercial AM/FM radio of the college, community, and NPR variety
- carrier current and cable based radio
- do trade journals manipulate or aid?
- upcoming conventions such as the Nat'l Assoc. of Broadcasters/BEA
  meeting in Atlanta and the Intercollegiate Broadcasters Society in NY
- the newly released "Public Radio in the 1990s" report by The Public
  Radio Expansion Task Force
- the FCC and its regulations
- obtaining and managing noncommercial grants
- programming sources
