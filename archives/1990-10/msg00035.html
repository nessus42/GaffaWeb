My advice to anyone looking to purchase the singles in the U.S. is
that if you don't absolutely need *everything*, then get the domestic
"Aspects" CD.  It's less money ($6 versus $25-30), it comes on one
disc instead of three, you'll get everything except two songs (one of
which ("The Confrontation") is an uninteresting instrumental, the
other ("One Last Look...") a one-minute long instrumental).
