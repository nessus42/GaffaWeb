There is a mail-order company in the UK which does, among other things,
posters and photos of Kate Bush. They are called Push Posters and can be
found on 041 951 4460 (a UK phone number), or write to them at PO Box
327, Clydebank, Dumbartonshire, G81 3HE, Scotland. They have an
(un-illustated) catalogue which can be got by sending them two 1st class
stamps (for UK folk): they will do international mail order, though I
don't know the details.
