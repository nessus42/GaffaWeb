best bet for americans still jonesin' for a box set is to get it via
Canada - the conversion rate in US/CDN dollars will save you quite a
bit.  i had a Canadian love-hound purchase my set for me for CDN $130,
which worked out to something like US $100.  i then sold all my
now-duplicate US CDs and made about $20 off them.  so, total cost of
box set: $80!
