Yes, that was me.

> Could someone set me straight on this bit o' KaTrivia?

Yes, I can. She really does. There are three 'proofs': a picture in the
wonderful 'A Visual Documentary' book (preparations for the 'Experiment IV'
video) and two videos, 'Kate Bush on Tour' (a video aired on April 3 1979
that shows Kate and co rehearsing, planning, training and performing the
tour) and 'Musical Chairs' (interview with Kate and Keef MacMillian about
the making of the 'Wow' video). 


> Disturbed and shaken,

Oh yes, and it's even in the FAQ: We all hope she stops smoking.

matter.
