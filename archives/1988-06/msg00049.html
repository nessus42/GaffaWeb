Andrew Marwick wanted me to spread some more info about Ultravox.
Well, Andrew, here are the answers to your questions, as far as I know
what's going on:

1. I have absolutely no idea what has become of Ultravox.

2. In 1986, Billy Currie said that he was going to make a
   solo-album, but I have not heard anything yet.
   I have no idea what Chris Cross is up to, but I would be
   very interested to get to know it. Anybody out there who
   can help us?

3. There are no plans for a new HELDEN-album now. Warren Cann
   is going to starr in a movie called THE HARLEY BOYS, together
   with Billy Idol and Dave Vanian. The movie is all about two
   motorcycle gangs that are rivals. I don't know when this
   movie will come. The other part of HELDEN, Hans Zimmer, wrote
   the theme for a TV-series on Brithis cable/sattelite channel
   SuperChannel, called GOING FOR GOLD. 

5. I haven't heard anything about John Foxx for ages, so I
   don't know if we will get another solo-album from him. 

Kari "I wish that I could have spent the rest of my life with Midge Ure, but
      Annabelle Giles got in my way"
