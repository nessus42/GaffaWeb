Oh...

You know, it's just a hunch, but I don't think that these methods
would work very well.  The idea, when introducing a new concept to
anyone, is to put _your_self in _their_ shoes, not the other way
around.  You can't just assume that someone will understand, you have
to teach.  It's a process, not an event.  It's a path, not a door.
Fucking etc.

I'm all for crazy passion abandon intensity etc., but it's not some sort
of contest to see who can "take it" loudest purest whatever.

Join the Marines.  "Kate Bush : We're looking for a few good men"
