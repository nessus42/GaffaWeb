Yes, they are hideously expensive.  _TWS_ is much more manageable via
Canada-- a friend of mine got his for around $25-$20.  But we couldn't
find _TSF_ for less than $100 anywhere.  My friend rented a VCR,
_TSF_, and the Hammersmith Odeon video, and hooked the entire thing up

illegal, but so are all of his bootleg KaTe albums.
