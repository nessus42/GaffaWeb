after seeing the Peter Gabriel "Don't give Up" video with her (yum!!)
would also like to see her live somewhere in the USA. My SO thinks I'm
crazy, but I've got a proposal perhaps |>oug can handle:

interesting underbelly scene.

Think about it folks, |>oug could get it fixed-up I bet and we'd all
win...
