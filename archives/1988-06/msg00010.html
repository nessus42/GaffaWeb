Thanks to the two people who have responded to my cry for help in the
wilds of South Carolina.  I'm pursuing one of these leads and still
trying to flush out a store here that can get their greedy hands on
this disc.  In the process, I've stumbled across something strange.

At the local franchise of CD Warehouse, they let me browse through a
catalog called "CD International" that lists all kinds of discs
available from the US, Great Britain, West Germany and Japan.  Sure
enough, they didn't list _Daft_.  But then I noticed that they DID
list _Who's Afraid... ?_ as being available from Great Britain -- with
a catalog number of EMI ZCIDQ-2.  This sounds suspiciously like the
catalog number for _Daft_ that one intrepid net.reader posted with the
complete listing of tracks on the disc.  Am I hallucinating?  Can
someone fill me in?
