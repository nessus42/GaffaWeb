Subject: Computer Music
Distribution: aus
Newsgroups: aus.general, rec.music.gaffa, rec.music.makers
Organization: Comp Sci, Melbourne Uni, Australia

The Symposium of the International Musicological Society and
Festival of Music will be held in Melbourne from 28 August
to 2nd September. Themes of the symposium are:
	(a) music since c. 1960
	(b) Cultural interaction through music
	(c) Analogy, Relations between Musical and Non-musical Structutres

A session entitled "Changing Concepts of Musical Structure
in the Development of Computer and Experimental Music" will be
held on 2nd September, 8.45 - 11.00am, at the
Victorian College of the Arts, with papers by James Dashow, Andrew Czink
and Larry Polansky. There are also sessions on Post-Modernist Composition
and Analogy and Metaphor in theory Building.

As well, there will be 2 concerts of computer music by leading
Australian and international composers at the VCA, room 113, on:

	Wednesday 31st August, 8.15pm, and

	Friday 2nd September, 1pm.

for further details, contact Margaret Kartomi, Dept. of Music
Monash University, ph. (03) 565 4000, or myself.
