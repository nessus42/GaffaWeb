I hope by "letter" you meant email, 'cause I've forgotten which keys to
push to generate that other stuff (unless perhaps there's a tape directly
involved).

As one who wasn't around for the original Kompilation Tapes' take-off,
I'd very much like to participate in their rebirth.  There's nothing
I like more than imposing my musical tastes on unseen others.

My only suggestion is that the geographically-imposed distribution not be
too closely limited.  I don't see that an extra day or three's time spent
in the mail is going to cause much propagation delay, especially since
experience has shown that the greatest hindrance to the whole scheme is
individual lethargy.
