For Sun users, (and those ambitious enough to use the raster bitmap
conversion programs) there are some compressed Sun rasterfiles bearing
the cover of The Whole Story available via anonymous ftp to
antares.cs.umn.edu (128.101.233.1) which include:

(1) kb.highres.Z - 1600x1280x1 dithered image 
	I sometimes use this for my high-res 'suntools' background.

(2) kb.reg.Z - 1152x900x1 dithered image
	Mediocre.

(3) kb.8bit.Z - 1152x1280x8 image 
	Pretty nice.

If anyone else has better ones, please let me (us) know.
