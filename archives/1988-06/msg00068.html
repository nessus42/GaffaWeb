Gary Dare -- re: Danielle Dax

People are indeed mentioned Ms. Dax in the same breath with the all
holy Kate person.  I am one of those persons.  Dax writes & produces
her own songs (Just Like Kate), and has an insanely wild voice (Just
Like Kate).  I don't know what else to tell you other than get off
your butt and buy something by her!  If you consider yourself an
avant-garde kind of guy, pick up "Pop Eyes" or "Jesus Egg That Wept"
(both available on a single compact disc entitled "Up Amongst The
Golden Spires")!  "Jesus Egg" may be a better choice since it is a tad
more accessible, and is an EP, so is cheaper.  If you just want some
straight out massacred rock and roll, then "Inky Bloaters" is your
baby.  She goes from mutated rock through mutated country & western.
No kidding!  I don't know if I'd call her a "low budget" Kate.  I
might, though, since she used to record on 4-track equipment only.
Just get some and listen and you will UNDERSTAND the fundamental
UNIQUENESS of her work!  It's great stuff.  Especially "Flashback"
played at MAXIMUM CONCEIVABLE VOLUME!!!  Here's a quick discography
(probably missing something somewhere)

(LPs)  Pop Eyes, Inky Bloaters
(CDs)  Up Amongst The Golden Spires, Inky Bloaters, The Chemical Wedding
(EPs)  Jesus Egg That Wept, The Janice Long Session
(12" singles)  Fizzing - Human - Bomb, Big Hollow Man, Where The Flies
               Are, Cat-House, Yummer Yummer Man

Enjoy...
