Pebble Mill is the name of the BBC's Birmingham site.
Various programmes from there have used the name.
Much of the Pebble Mill output has been daytime chat,
for example "Pebble Mill at One" which was a chat show
feature programme which went out at one o-'clock.
"Saturday Night at the Mill" was obviously an evening
programme. The wall behind the presenters seating area
was/is glass and so viewers could see outside quite
easily, and from this tell whether it was a daytime
programme or one made in the evening...
