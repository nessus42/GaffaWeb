Well, I just got done reading my very first 150 messages on r/m/g...and I
must say I'm quite taken aback. Here I thought I was an avid Kate fan, only
to get on here and here about videos, books, tours, singles, etc. that I
had no unearthly idea existed. (that's "hear," btw ;) ) At this moment I 
feel very small and insignificant. 
   I thought that owning all her albums was a nifty accomplishment, but 
this.....wow....
   
  I just want everyone here to understand that even though I classify 
myself as a Kate-worshipper (not in the literal sense, but you know
what I mean), my pitiful knowledge is nothing compared to what the rest
of you seem to know. So if I post any really stupid questions or make
an erroneous assumption, please don't flame me too badly.
  I'm here to learn. So teach me.
