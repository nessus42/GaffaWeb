It was beautiful.  Abosolutely a dazzling visual experience.  I
don't think Kate intended for it to be so much a straight
narrative as a ninth wave in film, i.e. images related by a 
common theme or story. I also think due to its phantasmagoric
nature much of the acting was purposefully melodramatic for
effect.  I think Kate would actually make a good actress if she
was being 100% serious, but this is a fairy tale and is
supposed to have and element of exaggeration.  Ah, well, just
my $0.02.

Mary-Helen
