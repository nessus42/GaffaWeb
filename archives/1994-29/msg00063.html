Perhaps I can dispell a little confusion here.  "Pebble Mill" was (is?)
the name of the building in Birmingham (England) containing the BBC's
Midlands regional studios.  The many programs that were filmed there
included both "Saturday Night at the Mill" and "Pebble Mill at One".
"Pebble Mill at One" was broadcast (at 1pm:-) on weekdays (possibly only
in the Midlands area?) and usually had a featured musical guest within a
general chat show format.  Unfortunately I don't remember Kate appearing
on it, but I was very likely at school at the time (and, dare I suggest,
PDFM probably was as well:-).

[Incidentally, about the most interesting thing about "Pebble Mill at
One" was the innocent passers-by who could be seen walking past through
the full-length windows behind the presenter:-)]
