Lance recalls a February 1978 appearance 
by Kate Bush on "Pebble Mill", in which she 
performed "Wuthering Heights".  IED's records 
indicate that this cannot be correct.  (His 
source is Peter FitzGerald-Morris's videologue, 
the best source IED knows of.)  The programme 
was then called "Saturday Night at the Mill", 
it aired February 25, 1978, and Kate did 
perform, but not "Wuthering Heights".  Rather, 
she did "Moving" and "Them Heavy People".  
Btw, this was not the same performance
of "THP" that Kate fans usually see in 
collections -- that was from a May 20, 1978 
edition of "Revolver".  IED has yet to see a 
copy of the "Saturday Night at the Mill" show 
from 2/25/78.

IED found the McLachlan brief fascinating, 
and wants to assure anyone who's interested 
that IED has never (to the best of his recollection) 
stalked, sued or threatened Kate Bush.  His 
obsession (for such it undoubtedly is!) is 
with Kate Bush's work, not with her person.  
He cannot, however, vouch for that Marvick nut. . .
