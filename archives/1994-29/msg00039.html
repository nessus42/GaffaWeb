Nothing earth-shattering in the latest missive from the KBC, just...

 - Live at Hammersmith Odeon video/CD: *will* be released in Europe
(date uncertain), plans for US NTSC release unknown as yet.

 - TLTCTC released on video in October.

 - "Skyscraping" still available from "major record shops". Anybody
seen this in a major record shop? I don't know whether they'd put it
under "Rock & Pop" or "World Music" but I can't find it under either
at any of the big shops here.
