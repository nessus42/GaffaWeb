  Finally! I had been keeping an eye on the "Personality" series, and
it seemed like every woman in popular music had been the subject except
Kate. Kate has been on the cover of a comic before; Issues #1 & #3 of
a 3 part series from Innovation Publishing called "Media Starr.

  The contents have nothing to do with Kate, but the paintings are nice.
The cover paintings are by Scott Rockwell, and the contents are by
Dennis Duarte, Allen Curtis, John Statema and Tom Vincent.

  A friend of ours was an avid comic collector, and had one of two
books with Kate graffiti on the walls in various scenes. Maybe there
is a Kate fan or two doing lettering. 

   BTW, did John Howard ever go anywhere? He was a old correspondent
of ours, and an pretty good comic artist, (although his renditions of
Kate had an unfortunate tendency to look like she was cast out of
concrete.)
