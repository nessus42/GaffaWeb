  Well, HWD is the awful fear of being deceived.  I know I'd be pretty shaken
up if I realised that the charming person I met the night before was Hitler.
As for TWW, for me, it's probably the most emotional song of all.  It reminds
me a bit of "All the Love", because they both deal with not sharing your
emotions and desires fully with your partner.  If you've seen "She's Having
A Baby", you know what I mean.  That scene with TWW is absolutely powerful.
