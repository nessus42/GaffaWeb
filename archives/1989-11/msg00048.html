idiots!". This looks like the Rushdie issue. "He doesn't like Kate! Death to
the infidel!" Grow up Doug!  Kate Bush is not your "run-of-the-mill" artist.

time trying to defend your (fanatical) tastes, but just tell the person that
Kate is not "eveyone's cup of tea", and suggest they look elsewhere for
critical opinions, and leave it at that.

I would also offer that it would be better that this group become "unmoderated"
. Then at least people could use the "R" key and reply (or flame) privately 
without being sent out for everyone to see (in the heat of the moment).
It's a royal pain to manually type in a path, and I would rather see "R"
work, for all the current benefits we are supposidly receiving by being a
"moderated" group.
