After reading the nth letter on this subject,
I had a brainstorm.  Why don't we include some
extra cash for KB herself, say an extra $3.50
to make the cost an even $10.  That way, we'd
get our tapes, Kate would get paid, and I'd 
venture that %35 is more than she makes on
her regular album sales anyway.  The only people
who would lose are the lower-than-lawyer 
bootleggers.  Yeah, I know, there are 
one thousand reasons why this scheme won't
work.  Just an idea.
