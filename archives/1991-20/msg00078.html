I'm sure not all the votes are in, but the ratio has been pretty stable for
a couple of days: not quite 2-1 in favor of Happy (23-14 just now-- 62%).
Slightly more evenly divided (17-12, 59%) for _losing_ the List of Musical
Lists.

Vickie actually loves the idea of Happy having her own list, but doesn't
want to see her banished entirely, I'm sure-- just using the list for long/
obscure info.

I have sent the following query to the Musical list-server:
>The readers of rec.music.gaffa have voted (tentatively, at least) to 
>unsubscribe from the Musical List.  I trust this is no problem?  Can 
>individuals without net access subscribe individually?  What other 
>newsgroups do you regularly post to?  Is it possible we could 
>subscribe to a service that only reported _changes_ to the list?

And I've added this paragraph to the monthly post:
>Katefans are generally the nicest people you could hope to meet, but 
>r.m.g. does erupt fairly regularly into classic Usenet flamewar- 
>behavior, most recently on the question of whether Katenews was being 
>unfairly swamped by discussion of Happy Rhodes.  Newcomers can rest 
>assured that you are welcome to participate, and that if you feel 
>ignored or incinerated you can safely assume it's nothing personal, and 
>persist until you feel accepted... for you will be.
