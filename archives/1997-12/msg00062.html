to those for whom i dubbed the kateopia kollection,

i am sorry that the copies i sent you deteriorated in quality partway 
through. the opening segments looked good and i didn't watch them all 
the way through before mailing them out. now i've learned that the 
signal deteriorates and the tapes are all but worthless. evidently, my 
copies are too many generations old to dub from.

please send me your addresses and i will send back the $7 you each 
sent me for the tapes and postage. and carole blakely--i found out the 
copies weren't viewable before i sent your tape; that's why i never 
sent it. i am sending you a check today.

again, i apologize. anyone else out there want to dub a few copies of 
the kollection for these poor folks? let me know and i'll pass on 
their identities to you....
