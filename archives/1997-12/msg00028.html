It's amazing what you can make yourself "hear" in things - it's not
backwards apparently. This was the focus of a long running KBC

confused by repetition (I think it sometimes plays as "We, we let the
wierdness in")

themes in Kate's work - be open to ideas, experiences etc. Listen,
learn, I'll be running up that hill;  can I have it all now?;Give me the
karma mama; Them heavy people....  etc etc
