Hi all!  Well, this  is a bit of a potporri.  

First, the latest music news in Orlando.

It seems last Monday, at a downtown "progressive, underground
punk" club called Club Space Fish, a group known as GG Wells
and the Murder Junkies, proceeded to urinate and defacate
onstage, and then began throwing the ... poo poo at the
audience.  They were arrested, the club was temporarily shut
down.   Apparently, in 1989, the same guys were arrested in
Dallas for the same type thing.  THe news had a video snippet.
It was very nice.   One of the band members claimed that his
body was atemple, and all his fluids were sacred. 
Neat.  I'm so glad I was doing laundry instead.

Saw the Addams family.  Thought the characterizations were
Mah-velous, ANgelica Huston is the BEST, and all the other
cast members were great as well.  My only criticism, if there
is to be a sequel, they need a much better script.  This one
was cute, but the movie was carried by great performances, not
great plot.  Not terrible plot either, but not great.
Overall, very amusing, quite entertaining, and great acting.

Ok.  Lessee, I'll be back to Hound and Love you on Monday,
December 9.  In the meantime, you may fight over the following
offer.  I currently have the Happy Rhodes cd Warpaint.
Listened to it, here's what I think.
Ms. Rhodes has a lovely, rich alto voice.  Loved her voice.
The tunes were well matched to the lyrics, in fact I liked
some of the music more than the lyrics, though usually I dwell
on lyrics.  Her lyrics were obviously haeartfelt, but not my
cup of tea.  I grew quickly weary of what struck me a lyrical
histrionics.  I felt like I was listening to Joni Mitchell on
acid at times.  I think I would have preferred this music
several years back when I was having certain kinds of
problems.   But hey, it ain't my cup of tea, maybe it'll be
yours.
If you want, I'll send Warpaint to you, just let me know.
Hey, for free, it's surely worth a try.

Meantime, since I'm no longer playing it, I have found that it
makes an excellent kitty litter turd scraper.
