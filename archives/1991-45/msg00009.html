Hmmm.  About "Carribean Blue" I might agree with this.  Over all, though,
I think _Shepherd Moons_ is a bit starker than the previous two albums.  
Maybe it's just my imagination.  There are fewer wall-o-sounds, though.

what she's saying).  

My current opininion is that _Shepherd Moons_ isn't quite as good as her
previous 2 albums--but that's not to say I don't love it.
