Angelos Kyrlidis in Pulse magazine!

From: ed@wente.llnl.gov (Ed Suranyi)

Subject: Angelos Kyrlidis in Pulse magazine!
To: love-hounds@eddie.mit.edu


Our friend Angelos Kyrlidis got his DID letter published in the latest
Pulse magazine (Dec. 1991)!  The headline, given by the editors, is
"Hi, Ecto?!", so you know it's good.  Here's part of it:

"1. _Warpaint_ -- Happy Rhodes.  In one word: brilliant! Hi ecto!

"2. _The Dreaming_ -- Kate Bush.  Obligatory DID.  Hi to all love-hounds."

Could someone who subscribes to ecto mention this to them, if they don't
already know about it?

For the record, here are his other Desert Island Discs:

3. Passage in Time -- Dead Can Dance
4. Peter Gabriel -- Peter Gabriel (Third Album)
5. Dirty Mind -- Prince
6. The Lion and the Cobra -- Sinead O'Connor
7. The Stiff Years -- Lene Lovich
8. A Night at the Opera -- Queen
9. Doolittle -- The Pixies
10. Meno Ektos -- Eleftheria Arvanitaki

Not only that, but someone else lists TD as well!  Philippe Gillard of
San Francisco lists _The Dreaming_ as No. 5 on his list.  He also includes:

1. Les Marquises -- Jacques Brel
2. The Final Cut -- Pink Floyd
3. Strip -- Adam Ant
4. Terre de France -- Julien Clerc
6. The Beatles -- The Beatles
7. Blue Bell Knoll -- Cocteau Twins
8. Flasi Allarmi -- Alice
9. A Kiss in the Dreamhouse -- Siouxsie and the Banshees
10. Dvorak: Symphony From the New World
