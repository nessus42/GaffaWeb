A guy by the name of Jogle just posted the results of a poll in
rec.music.misc.  He asked people to list their favorite albums
of the year.

Well, by a HUGE margin, the top two albums were _Passion_ by
Peter Gabriel and _The Sensual World_ by Kate Bush.  No other
album even came close to those two.
