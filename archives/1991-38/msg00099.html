J. Fingerle posted:
>> I always assumed "In Excelsis Dio", figuring IED and U2 (see _Gloria_)
>> meant Ronnie James Dio. 

   I guess everyone forgot the obvious Kate and Ronnie James Dio connection;
that Jimmy Bain, one of the legion of bassists on _The Dreaming_, was the
bass player in Dio. There seems to be little overlap between Kate fans and
Heavy Metal fans, but one of our old correspondents from California, Wayne
Henderson, has a dream of seeing Kate and Ronnie James Dio singing a duet.
Oh well....at least he wouldn't tower over her.

>> had a few minor hits.

and Vickie asked:
> Are you thinking of The Cockney Rebels? Some of whom were on _The Kick

  I remember both. I think _Rejects_ was an intentional slam at _Rebels_.
"Steve Harley and Cockney Rebel" never had any hits in the US. My older
brother had a good collection of his stuff and I quite was quite fond of
some of it. Harley was the third artist in the Bill Duffield memorial
concert with Kate and Peter Gabriel.
