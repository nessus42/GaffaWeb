I picked up this cd a few days ago and have really fallen in love
with it. Her voice is similar in range to Kates (including the
occasional excursion into the upper registers). Do be warned, most
of the songs are in Japanese. This wasn't a problem for me, but some
people don't like to buy vocals that they can't understand. I am
learning Japanese and found her clean enunciation very approachable.
If anything I was disappointed that they only had the lyrics in
translation into English (anybody know where the originals can be
obtained?).

Her songs have a Zen-like simplicity in that she paints an "image"
in brief sentances. She comes across as a very beautiful spirit,
the type of personality I could fall as deeply in love with (though
Kate has prior claim).
