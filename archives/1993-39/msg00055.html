I've heard the ROSE CRONICLES EP. It's got a few strong things on it and a
few over-indulgent head scratchers. They don't really seem to know what they
want to be when they grow up. Each song is rendered in a different style. The
ones that work for me are the couple that have a Cocteaus/Sarah McLachlan
feel to them. I definitely think the band has potential and would much rather
be listening to them than The Cranberries. (No offense to Cranberries' fans,
just not my cup of tea).
