I just wanted to mention that for Innocence Mission fans who saw
Suddenly, Tammy (a Lancaster band as well)...they released an album
on Spin Art records. I really like it. It doesnt do their live
performances justice however, but nevertheless its a nice album
filled with songs from their tours with IM. I even recognize some of
the same set list orders.

been recording but I havent heard a release date. Anyone hear

called "Patience". I'll be there to buy that one the day it comes
out. I dont know if this was their 2nd album that was printed or not
(on their previous smaller label). any info would be helpful.
