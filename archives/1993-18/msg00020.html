  The second ever U.S. Kate Bush Convention to take place in Boston on
Saturday, May 22nd.  The Boston KT party will take place from 11:00AM
to 5:00PM in Convention Hall C of the 57 Park Plaza at Park Square.
This hotel is located at 200 Stuart Street, downtown Boston and should
not be confused with "The Park Plaza" hotel.  Come for contests,
displays, collectables, videos of rare performances, interviews, etc..
Admission is $5.00 at the door or $4.00 for tickets ordered in advance
at: 3061 Hudson Dr., Cuyahoga Falls OH  44221.  Ticket holders also
receive $1.00 off admission to see One and Spy Mission Pineapple
appearing at Club III.
