two omissions on his "discography."  Both would go in the "non-LP" category,

Rowan Atkinson called "Do Bears . . ."  This is supposed to be humorous, but
it's a one-joke song:  the old "threaten to say a naughty word" joke.  (e.g.
"Do bears sha-la-la-la in the woods?")
