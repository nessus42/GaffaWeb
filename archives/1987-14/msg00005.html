Oh, and re: Trina's comment about XTC selling out by splicing "Dear God" into
Skylarking.  That was almost certainly Geffen's or Virgin's doing; they get
periodically desperate to see XTC have a big hit (thus the initial choice of
having them be produced by Todd Rundgren (passive voice intentional there)).
I don't think the band really care about hit singles ... if they did they
wouldn't release relatively uncommercial songs like "Grass" and "Wonderland"
as singles.  Why hasn't "Earn Enough For Us" been released as an A-side yet?

Oh and by the way: Sonic Youth are still god.  "Sister" doesn't have quite
the power and coherence of "Bad Moon Rising" or "EVOL", but it's one baad
groovy slab nonetheless.
