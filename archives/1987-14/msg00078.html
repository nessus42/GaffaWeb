the two tracks by dead can dance are "frontier" and "the protagonist"
"frontier" was released earlier (as a single, i believe, it says in
the promotional booklet that comes with the limited edition) ... "the
protagonist" is a new song, a very haunting (in my opinion) instrumental.

since i'm not a kate bush fan ("horrors!  sacrilege!" scream all the 
love-hounds) ... i have to have some music that i favour the most ...
4ad is that music ... along with the usual cure/new order/bauhaus lines
of music (these include other incarnations of the bands, like joy division,
tones on tail, the glove, etc.)

don't get me wrong, however ... i agree that kate bush is an incredible
vocalist ... but none of the stuff that i've heard runs with my tastes
in music ... but these tastes can change ... and sometimes do ...
