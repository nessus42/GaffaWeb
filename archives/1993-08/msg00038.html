Ed, I think you are referring to the "Aspects of the Sensual World" CD5
that came out a few months after TSW.  Basically, it combined the b-sides
of the British TSW singles, minus a track or two (The short piano
instrumental, "One Last Look Around the House Before We Go" was one of
the missing tracks).  If you hunt a little, you can probably still find
this.  Hope this helped.
