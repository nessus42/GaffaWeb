I woul like to personally thank everyone who replied to my questions about
rec.music.synth.  In direct response to my last posting, Rich Salz did some
tinkering with his mailer and has set up an automatic relay for echoing
the stuff over to ARPA.  He has also set up a mail address for 'direct
deposit' to the newsgroup from .ARPA locations.  The thing has been working
correctly for several days now, in both directions (hey, I've beta-tested
things for a shorter time).  At any rate, any DOD techno-junkies who want
to directly receive rec.music.synth should post a request for inclusion
to the mailing list directly to Rich Salz (RS@MIRROR.UUCP).  The address
for posting to rec.music.synth is (REC-MUSIC-SYNTH@MIRROR.UUCP).  I
know from personal mail conversation that Jim Hofmann has wanted direct
access to that particular newsgroup for a long time now.  People don't
know it but Hofboyy is secretly an electronics hobyist and stays up late
at night reading the latest issue of the IEEE journal.  And everybody in
netland thought he was just a good ole Joe.

John
