Jim Hoffman mentioned a release of ten psychic TV live albums ("pseudo bootlegs"


Actually, these ten are going to be 10 in a series of 23 that are supposed to
be released on the 23rd day of each month for the next 23 months.  The 
first one was released in November (LiVE In tOKyo), I believe the following
release was titled "Live in Paris".  I only own the live in Tokyo LP
and it's mostly their pop song material (with an awesome, distorted version
of "We Love You" by the Stones.).  I know somebody who got the next one
who said it was a lot more noise oriented.

If you wonder how I know about the 23 album releases, it explained all of this
on the back of the first album.
