WEIRD is a new mailing list that a few friends and I have started up here...
We have been concentrating on cutups, short stories, strange arguments, and
some other stuff, all with a certain degree of warpage.

Here's how to get on the Weird list:
Send a posting.  You will be on the list for the rest of the current
month, and for the next month.  When the next month rolls around, you
will have to send another posting if you want to be on for the month after
that one.  In other words, you have to keep posting in order to remain on
the list.
The list is mildly moderated.
The address of the list is WEIRDDST@BROWNVM.
The address of the moderator is JEREMY@BROWNVM. (That's me.)
(BROWNVM is on BITNET, so for some the address will be WEIRDDST@BROWNVM.BITNET)
