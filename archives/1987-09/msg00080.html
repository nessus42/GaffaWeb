It was interesting to see that this video was finally going to be
released here. I was thinking about that last week when I saw an item
in the L.A. Times announcing that HBO/Cannon Home Video (formerly
Thorn/EMI/Cannon/HBO video) was changing its name HBO Home Video
following HBO's buyout of Cannon's share of the operation. As I recall,
the previous hang-up was over who owned world rights when Globus and Golan
of Cannon were running the show. Some of the current delay may be a result
of some hasty repackaging to reflect the new corporate identity.
Now, do you think this will ever show up on HBO? nah...
