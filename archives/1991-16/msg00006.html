   Jorn tells of a recent dream of his about KaTe.  I've had one too.
   When I first started getting into KaTe, when all I'd heard was HoL and I'd
never heard of this newsgroup, I had a dream about KaTe.  I met her at a bus
stop and she was wearing the gown from the TNW cover.  I invited her to a
party, and she came.  When I introduced her to my friends at the party, I
called her "Kate <some non-Bush name I forgot>" so nobody would know who she
was.  My roommate Marty (who owned the HoL CD I'd been listening to) said
something like "Wow you've got a beautiful voice.  Do you sing?" and KaTe got
all bashful and started saying things like "No, not really..." but I
interrupted and said that yes, she did sing and I asked her to sing.  She then
broke into Cloudbusting (or was it The Morning Fog?).
				-Bill (wms@sam.cs.cmu.edu)
