In article <"910519022142.76004.2670.DHA24-1"@CompuServe.COM> you write:
>3.  Why on "This Woman's Work, Vol. I" is track 14: "Walk Straight Down
>The Middle" on there?  It already is on the Sensual World CD.  What's
>special about it?

Nothing.  It's just that WSDTM was originally designed as the B-side
for "The Sensual World".  Then they decided to make it a bonus track
on the CD and cassette.  It's not on the LP.  Putting the song twice
on the CD and cassette box sets could be considered an error, but it
has to go on the LP version of TWW, Vol. I, because it isn't anywhere
else.  Maybe they thought it would be more convenient to have the CD and
LP versions of this album have the same track listing.
