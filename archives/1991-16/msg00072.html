Another Happy-Kate fan

From: "Albert W. Philipsen" <awphili@cs.vu.nl>
Date: Tue, 21 May 91 16:23:09 MET DST
Subject: Another Happy-Kate fan
Organization: Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam

Hello everyone, this is my first posting to rec.music.gaffa. I have
been reading this newsgroup for half a year now. Because of all the
raving about Happy Rhodes, I ordered her CD "Warpaint."

I am very impressed by her music. I must have listened to the CD about
fifteen times in the last week. Her voice is absolutely stunning, the
lyrics are so inspiring.

Thanks to everyone who posted something about her, especially Vickie
Mapes, for encouraging me to invest in this very talented artist. I
have already ordered all the tapes.

I think I am the person from The Netherlands who was accidentally sent
two copies of the CD. I received a letter from the postal service
telling me that another package from Aural Gratification had arrived.
They needed my permission to open it, because a "green customs sticker"
was missing. (The other copy of the CD arrived without any trouble.)

Albert Philipsen
