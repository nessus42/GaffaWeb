Kate in Music Mart in Goldmine

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward J. Suranyi)
Date: Fri, 17 May 91 17:16:48 PDT
Subject: Kate in Music Mart in Goldmine


A while ago the record collector's magazine _Goldmine_ started a
publication called _Music Mart_.  This was basically a large
classified ads section, with people offering to buy or sell music-
related merchandise.  Aparently it didn't do very well, because
in the May 31 issue of _Goldmine_ it is announced that _Music Mart_
is to be incorporated into its parent magazine, as a greatly expanded
classified ads section of _Goldmine_.

Anyway, the point is that at the front of the new Music Mart section of
_Goldmine_, there's a classification index.  One of the ad categories,
with number 5085, is "Kate Bush".  This is interesting, because neither
_Goldmine_'s old classified section, nor _Music Mart_ when it was
separate, used to have a special section for Kate.

Of the four ads in this week's issue, three want stuff, and one wants
to sell stuff.
