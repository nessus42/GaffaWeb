I had the pleasure of booking the band when I was working for KSJS heavily.. 
they had the same booking agants as the band that they were touring with, 
Caterwaul.. For those who don't know about how Arson Garden are in concert, 
they are very good.. The lead singer has a style that is reminecent of a 
cross between  the Throwing Muses and Sonic Youth.. They were very very good 
and I hope you all support them, because they have a lot of potential.. 
Also, as for Caterwaul, they are the greatest group of people that you'd 
want to meet.. They're led by Betsy Martin, who has a natural gift.. A voice 
that is unlike any out there.. The guitarist Mark has a sound that is as 
wide as the grand canyon, and has the same beauty to it as a flower in the 
desert. I suggest that those who haven't heard open their wallets and then 
their ears.. They are out on IRS records and Arson Garden, although hard to 
get, is available on Community 3 records out of Brooklyn, NY.. For other 
fans of Caterwaul, I give you this as a gift.. not the home phone # (don't 
want to piss them off..) but an address.. Caterwaul, P.O. Box 6121, Burbank, 
CA 91510. I think that's right.. the card is a bit worn.. :)
