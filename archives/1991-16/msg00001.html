I read today on the news page of the "Beat Box" section of the
Oracle-C4 teletext service (teletext is a sort of non-interactive
bulletin board transmitted with each TV channel we have here) that two
directors have been signed up for Prince's new videos (Spike somebody and
somebody else) but that KaTe was "unconfirmed".

I really can't see why She would do it:

a) She doesn't need the money (I wouldn't have thought so anyway 8^)
b) It would delay the new album (oh no!)
c) It would delay The SeKond Koming (OH NO!) 
d) I don't know if She would actually want to do someone else's vid. I mean,
Prince's style is just *so* different...
