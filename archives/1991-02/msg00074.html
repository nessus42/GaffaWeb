Subject: _Ne T'enfuis pas_ Lyrics


Someone asked for the lyrics to _Ne T'enfuis pas_.   Here they are 
courtesy of Andrew Marvick.  Someone hoped that the title meant "My
Next Release Won't Cost $200."   Far from it, this song alone is 
proof that it's worth that much.  Here's another way to look at it: if
you bought the set only for the B-sides and you paid $180 for it, that's
$90 per CD.  That's six times the normal price for a CD.  I don't know
about you folks but I listen to my Kate CDs at least six times more
often than any other CD! :-)

------
