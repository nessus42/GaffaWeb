Well, when you kinda look at Ian Curtis' lyrics, it seems a bit inevitable.
However, soon before Ian decided to commit suicide, his girlfriend broke up 
their relationship; this is probably the event that pushed him over the 
edge.
I think Joy Division was probably the most important band of the post-punk 
era...Ian Curtis' lyrics were really angst ridden and very depressing...
I'd recommend all their stuff...a good start is "Substance" which is a 
collection of singles and other hard to find stuff...get it on CD so you can 
get the 7 bonus songs.
The remaining members of Joy Division formed New Order...actually, the early 
stuff by New Order sounded a lot like Joy Division, but they strayed away 
from that, basing their sound on synthesizers instead of guitars.
