Subject: US vs. UK videotape


Someone asked whether the US videotape of the Sensual World has the
interview on the British videotape.  The US videotape does not
have the entire half-hour interview.  Instead, it has short segments
(maybe a couple minutes long each) before each video.

Incidentally, this was an American interview.  It was first shown
on the American cable channel VH-1 back in December of 1989.
