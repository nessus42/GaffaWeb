I noticed in that same Can'n mag I referred to earlier (music express)
an add for rock biographies.  Under books, they list two Kate items

	Kate Bush: Visual....$26.95
	Kate Bush............$14.95

That's all they say.

The place is Monarch Books, 5000 Dufferin St., Downsview, Ontario M3H5T5

Hope this is helpful.  Good luck!!!
