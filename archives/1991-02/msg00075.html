Early Music discussion list formed

From: GONTER%AWIWUW11.BITNET@mitvma.mit.edu (Gerhard Gonter)
Date: Wed, 23 Jan 91 22:10:02 WUT
Subject: Early Music discussion list formed

There is a new discussion list for early music:

* Topics:
*   Anything about EARLY MUSIC (medieval, renaissance etc.)
*   including comments/questions about
*   a) (new) records
*   b) books
*   c) performances
*   d) song texts & translations
*   e) encoding early music scores in electronic form
*   f) concert/festival announcements

If you want to subscribe to this list, send the following command
to LISTSERV@AEARN.BITNET in the body of a mail:
   SUBSCRIBE EARLYM-L Your Full Name

Contributions should be sent to EARLYM-L@AEARN.BITNET.

You can obtain a list of files, which are available from this list
by sending the following command to LISTSERV@AEARN.BITNET
in the body of a mail:
   INDEX EARLYM-L

For further information about this all you can contact me
at GONTER@AWIWUW11.BITNET.

*peace*, Gerhard Gonter                        <GONTER@AWIWUW11.BITNET>
