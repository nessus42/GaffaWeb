The KateWorlds and Homeground Towers VRML 
sites get a nice mention in the VRMLSite online-zine 
this month.  It's at http://www.vrmlsite.com 
Robert St John was interviewing VRML artists 
and I came up on the list.  Rob is complementary 
about the worlds.  For any of you interested in 
hacking VRML, this is a good site to visit.  I 
am writing a tutorial on VRML 1.0 for next month's
edition.  I will probably extend it into 2.0 for 
animation.

For those who worked on the apparently defunct 
Enhanced CD-ROM for Common Ground:  the 
Isle of Jeenix VRML world was built to house 
Kate's contribution to that CD.   It works nicely 
on my hard disk, but the wav file even in MS ADPCM 
4-bit mono is still a little under a meg, so too big 
for the WWW.   Someone should consider an 
enhanced CDROM made up of contributions from 
artists who are fans of the primeArtiste.  Sony?
Kate has an unusual amount of support out 
here in the art community.

Kate Bush received a write-in vote today
for a state appellate court judgeship in 
Alabama.  She couldn't do worse than the bozos 
who run the show now.  She might do considerably 
better.

len
