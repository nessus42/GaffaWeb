Chris Sweet wrote:

>MTV europe has confirmed that Kate will appear on the song
>"My Computer" on Prince's upcoming 3 cd set, Emancipation, to
>be released Nov 19th. The song was completed via swapping
>tapes...they never actually met in the studio. Supposedly the
>song is about Prince's hobby of surfing the net on AOL. In fact
>the song opens with AOL's greeting "You've got mail" and closes
>with AOL's "Goodbye."

Urr.  I'll have to find someone to tape the song for me -- or hope against
hope that it's released as a single -- because I'm not about to shell out
for a three CD set by "(unreproduceable glyph)" when I haven't cared for
his last several single ones, just to hear one song.  I can hardly believe
it, but someone's finally found a way to keep me from rushing out and
buying a new Kate song!

Should we assume that this song is an artifact of the same long-ago tape
exchange which produced "Why Should I Love You?"  Or do we have any reason
to suppose that Kate and "(unreproduceable glyph)" have resumed contact
since then?  Given what we know about their "difficult" collaboration I
would tend to assume the former, barring any definite word to the contrary.
