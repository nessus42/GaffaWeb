Just for the record, Kate's track from the 
film "The Magician of Lublin" is not an instrumental.
She did the guest lead-vocal of a song composed by
Maurice Jarre (lyrics by someone else IED can't recall
the name of offhand, sorry), which unfortunately is
heard only in the middle of the movie while Alan 
Arkin is yelling inconsiderately over it!  There has
never been a commercial release of the soundtrack,
not even of the instrumental reprise of the melody
which can be heard during the end titles of the film,
not even as part of an anthology of the film music of 
Jarre.  :^(
