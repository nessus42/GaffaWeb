On Thu, 18 Nov 1993 at 09:51:27 -0500 jbj@daimi.aau.dk (Jens B{k J|rgensen) wrote:
> Does anybody know if Fumbling Towards Ecstasy is released in Europe?
> The record shops I've asked here in Denmark have never heard of it.

No.  Sorry.  "Fumbling Towards Ecstasy" is currently only released in
Canada.  The last I heard it was not due to be released anywhere else
(not even in the US) before January 1994.  This is a great shame as
it's an excellent album.

> Is Sarah McLachlan's CD's distributed in Europe at all?

"Solace" and "Touch" are both available in Europe (well, here in the
UK at least) on the Arista label.  I've only ever seen the live album
as an import of the Canadian Nettwerk release.
