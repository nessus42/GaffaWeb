 The wait is finally over - I now have TRS!

 Listening to a new Kate Album is something akin to a religious 
 experience.
 
 I really like this new album. And I like EAT THE MUSIC.. what are you
 guys going on about ?.. I think its a great single track and I see it 
 as something you can really get down and boogie to.
 
 I look forward to the Australian screening of "The Line, The Cross,
 The Curve".
 
 I see this as her most accessible album to date, and that is a good thing 
 when it comes to attracting new Kate fans. Hopefully we may see the older
 albums rise back up the charts once more.

 Thats my 5c worth.
