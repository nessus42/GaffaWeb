In an earlier message I suggested that The Line, The Cross and The Curve
would not be out on video till next year. Actually, in the programme notes
the EMI publicity suggests it would be available later this year.

However, when I contacted Novercia a guy told me that they were not
releasing it on video yet and were looking for other outlets - presumably
a cinema run. He said he might be able to give me more details in a
couple of weeks.
