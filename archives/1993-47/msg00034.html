The good thing about charts is that there are so many of them, here's
a couple of selected items from the "Rumba" magazine:
- Moments Of Pleasure enters Rumba's Top 50 list at #49, the list is
  based on combination of record sales (album and single), radio airplay
  on national and local stations and disco playlists.
- Eat The Music is #12 on Radiomafia's (Finnish Broadcasting Co.'s
  rock channel, and don't ask me about the name!) airplay chart, and
  it hasn't even been released as a single here
