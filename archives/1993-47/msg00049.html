OK, I've found the glitches and phone ring in question.

The phone ring I hear most clearly is in Constellation of the Heart,
at 2:53 into the song. It's actually the funky organ sound that's
behind the song, but it hits just the right tone there. It's possible
that KaTe uses the organ other places, and that's why you hear it more
than one place.

The Glitch in Moment of Passion that I hear is arround 2:26. It's sort
of like a high pitched squeak between the words 'moment's giving'.
There's another one at the end of that sentence too, and that wholed
damn phrase is 'contrasty', for the lack of a better word.

Can anyone confirm my times?

I now love the album. I didn't at first. The trick, for me, was to
listen to it over and over. You know you have been converted when you

I Love You". If you start to actually like WSILU, just concentrate on
lyrics like 'The L of the lips...The V of the Velvet...The E of the
Elvis" :-)
