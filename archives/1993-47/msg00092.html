>Am I alone in thinking that Never For Ever is KaTe's best album?

Not only do I firmly believe NFE to be KaTe's best, I also think it's probably 
one of the two or three best albums of all time.  Every song is a joy (yes,
"Violin", too!) and the emotionality, range, and originality are simply
unbelievable.  My favorite criterion for a "great album" is that it's one
of a kind.  I can't say I've ever heard an album remotely like NFE (musically).
TKI, TD, HOL are all solid albums, but I don't find their musical ideas all
that original (see: Elton John, Peter Gabriel, mid-80's British MTV music -
in that order).  NFE is also the only Kate album I've loved on first listen
as well the one that made me an obsessed fan.  

JMTC (just my 2c).
