I would say my favorite is The Dreaming. Don't know why. Maybe the posture,
the intregate juxtaposition of ideas, the key. And, of course, it's
building on the idea of the whole theme of the many songs (the "magical"
key of Houddini).
I must say that the feeling I get just looking at this cover is definetly
something of a mystery to me. All subjective. Hardly understood. Maybe it's
KaTe's charm working once again. I recognize it as a kind of neoromantic
artistic style, though, and know that's probably why I do like it that
much. To say the truth, it's also the first CD I was ever offered by a
girlfriend of mine :). 

Of course, I only own all the official albums (as well as video tapes) and
singles strarting with The Sensual World (those I could get in France or
nearby Germany).
