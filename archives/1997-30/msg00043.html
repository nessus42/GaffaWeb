My favorite album cover is The Dreaming by far, but am I the only one
who thinks The Red Shoes cover is really cool?  If she had put this out
without her name on the cover, I bet I'd still know it was a Kate album
at first sight.  And her "Ophelia" pose on Hounds of Love is my favorite
picture of her, so I'll say that's my vote for best back cover. 

Favorite singles covers: Babooshka, Wuthering Heights(UK)
12" single:  Alternative Hounds of Love(UK)   
Video:  The Sensual World, Running Up That Hill, and while I wouldn't
say it's her best, I'm fond of Hounds of Love-it strikes me as romantic
and very British.     
Promo poster:  Hounds of Love (the one with the tassled hat)
