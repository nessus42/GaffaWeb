My favorite cover is the Moments of Pleasure single (UK release)........  
.......there's something about not only the use of the vivid colors on the
cover--stopping just shy of being boldly garish, yet somehow maintaining a
beauty and spirited brilliance--but also how it captures Kate's vivaciousness
as she dances over the skulls and bones of the people in the blazing
fire...not to mention the look on her face and how she appears to be
rejoicing the moment.  I know we've all seen it before in TLTC&TC, but they
picked the *perfect* clip for the cover!

If I can't vote for any old cover and have to vote for an album
cover...change it to The Dreaming.  It's absolutely brilliant.

And as for her worst cover....definitely the Sensual World.  It's just too
Silence of the Lambs-ish.  It has always reminded me of the unpleasant
advertisements in which the lower portion of Jodi Foster's face is hidden by
a giant moth.  Eww.

As always, it's just my opinion, take it with a grain of salt...

~~~Samantha
