Has anybody heard Bjork's recent album "Homogenic"?
It's absolutely genious, my favourite at the moment
is "Joga". The German mag "Musikexpress" wrote
the following lines in their review: (sorry guys, this comes in
German cause I cannot translate it properly):

"... Und selbst wenn das singulaere Talent einer
Kate Bush in den intimsten Momenten ihrer
besten Platten kurz zu sich selbst fand - ruhig
und von kuehler Majestaet - dann war das ein
Scheissdreck gegen diesen mineralischen Monolith
aus Geist und Seele ..."

It means something like: Kate Bush's best
works are worth a shit compared to Bjorks
new album. Hey, don't kill me, it's not
my opinion!!! But I think Kate Fans should
check out "Homogenic" - it is wonderful.
It somehow reminds me on "The Dreaming"
and "Hounds Of Love". Beautiful!!!
