This little bit of information came across in the National Midnight Star (Rush mailing list) today:

CamMan123@aol.com spewed into the cosmic void that is Cyberspace:
Seeing that Alex and Geddy are both big fans of Massive Attack and Bjork, maybe it would work to hire their producer, Nellee Hooper, onto their next project. Its not as if Nellee would not be the right man for the job, he just produced the most recent song for The Smashing Pumpkins. Unfortunately, I don't think this would happen. As far as I know, Nellee Hooper is
producing the new Kate Bush CD, and it just doesn't seem like something that would happen.  Too bad though, the man is brilliant and everything he works on is simply amazing.

[Some stuff deleted]

Another note on Bjork; her new album, 'Homogenic', came out yesterday.  Brilliant. Simply *brilliant*. Gives me chills listening to it. However, I cannot fully recommend it to the uninitiated and unacquainted, since this album is easily her darkest and most challenging. She easily risks
alienating some of her fans with it. I think its kind of funny that this album came out with pretty much no publicity or backing from the recrod company; I guess they figured that now they can rely on her core group of fans to carry this record, they can get away with not spending any money on promotion or advertising. Sounds kind of familiar....

[End quoted material]

I pretty much dig Bjork, but I have yet to hear any of the new stuff.  Sounds like he could be describing "The Dreaming"... or "Boys For Pele".
