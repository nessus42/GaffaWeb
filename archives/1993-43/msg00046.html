Subject: Kate played on L.A. NPR station

My mother, who knows what a big Kate fan I am and who therefore pays
attention to what she hears about her, told me just now that L.A.
radio station KCRW played a new Kate song on its "Morning Becomes
Eclectic" show.  She couldn't tell me the name of the song, but she
thought it was pretty nice.  KCRW is an NPR affiliate, but I don't
think that radio show is network-wide.
