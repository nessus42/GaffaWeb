Based on what I've seen on this newsgroup so far, I think I'm in
a minority on this issue, but I LOVE the song "Constellation
of the Heart".  I liked it on first listening, and I like it
more each time.  I think it's got vocal twists similar to those
of "Running Up That Hill", a song that gives me chills even after
I've heard it ten zillion times.

Some people have compared it to "Why Should I Love You", in the sense
that these songs are both throwaways, and not very significant.
I think there's a HUGE difference.  CotH is GREAT.  WSILY is still
only so-so, though with time I might get to like it more.
