Hi John!

> i know that we are in the midst of "red shoes mania" (tm :-), but
> i have a few questions about an older Kate album, the aforementioned
> butterfly kisses. i picked this up recently, and i was wondering
> if anyone has info about it. namely, i'd like to know where and when
> the songs were recorded.

That's easy, though I don't know all the years from memory:

- Wuthering Heights: 1978, either Bio's Bahnhof or Top Of The Pops (must check)
- The Wedding List: 1982, July 21, The Prince's Trust Gala
- The Man With The Child In His Eyes: 1978, December 9, Saturday Night Live
- Them Heavy People: 1978, December 9, Saturday Night Live
- Under The Ivy: 1986, March, The Tubes for the 25th anniversary of Abbey Road
- Breathing: 1986, March, The Comic Relief benefiz shows
- Do Bears Sh... In The Woods?: 1986, March, The Comic Relief benefiz shows
- Running Up That Hill: 1987, autumn, Secret Policeman's Third Ball, w/ DaveGilm.
- Moving: 1978, June 18, Japan, Seventh Tokyo Song Festival ('Budokan')
- The Long & Winding Road: 1978, June 23, Japan, Sound in S
- She's Leaving Home: 1978, June 23, Japan, Sound in S
- Room For The Life: 1979, In Concert (German TV special)
- Strange Phenomena: 1979, In Concert (German TV special)
- Violin: 1979, In Concert (German TV special)
- In The Warm Room: 1979, In Concert (German TV special)
- Kite: 1979, In Concert (German TV special)
- Wuthering Heights: 1979, In Concert (German TV special)

Bye,

Uli
