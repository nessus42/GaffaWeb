never mind all this red shoes biz... check out "pure, impure" by
seefeel.  they do have a female singer (natch) but her voice is used
as an instrument, and i don't even mean that in the cocteau twins
sense.

the band is really into the "sound sculpture" thing, with lots of
guitar samples & loops & effects all swirling around in a beautiful
maelstrom of organized noise.  volume magazine (which has a feature on
them in issue 7) called them a dub version of my bloody valentine.
could be, could be.

personally, i think that if the aphex twin's remix of "time to find me
(afx fast mix)" doesn't convince you, then you're beyond hope.
