Hee hee hee -- sorry.  This has happened to me a couple of times -
once with Cheap Trick "Live at Budokan" (stop snickering at me)
turning into Louis Armstrong, once with David Bowie "Hunky Dory"
turning out to be "Pinups".  In both cases, however, the labels on
the tape and CD (respectively) said that they were what they were
supposed to be, and not what they actually were, so it does no good
just to open them in the store - you have to get them to play them
for you if you are concerned about this (if you live way far away,
for example).
BTW, I kept the Armstrong, but traded the faulty "Pinups" 'cause I
already had it.  -- Ottis
