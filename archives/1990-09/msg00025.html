According to (the distributor) Schwartz Brothers' weekly flyer (dated
12 March--bear in mind that I'm typing this on 9 March), there is an
upcoming CD-single release entitled "Aspects Of the Sensual World". 
It is described as a "five-track 5" disc".  Its release date is listed
simply as "ASAP".

Sorry I can't get any more detailed information than that, but that's
the most information I've found so far.  A couple of weeks ago, I was
told that there would be a five-inch single for "The Sensual World", with
no announced shipping date and no information about additional tracks.

My guess is that the track listing will be :

(1) The Sensual World
	(since it's the new CBS single)
(2) Love And Anger
	(since they didn't get a CD-single release for it)
(3) This Woman's Work
	(since they probably won't release this as a single)
and, since CBS has to play catch-up on using existing tracks:
(4) Be Kind To My Mistakes
(5) I'm Still Waiting

Call me pessimistic, but I really don't expect *any* new material on this
disc.  I even expect the cover art to be the "sand through the fingers"
picture. . . .
