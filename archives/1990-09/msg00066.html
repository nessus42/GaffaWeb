Well, gee, this business about having fans was real nice while it lasted but
flattery won't get you out of this one Michael.
No, I'm not going to flame you. After all, I wouldn't yell at a deaf
person for not hearing something that I hear.

I *DO* get that magic Kate Bushy feeling all over when I listen to most
of The Sensual World. Some songs hit me right away. Some took a few listens.
Some took many listenings. One, to be honest, has not hit me at all yet
(Heads We're Dancing, in case anyone's interested), though there are things
within that song that I do like.

I'm NOT one of these people who absolutely ADORES *every single little*
thing that Kate does. There are actually Kate songs that I detest. Only
a couple though (these are not on TSW).
There are other songs, on previous albums, that, if they disappeared
from the albums, I wouldn't miss them. Only a few though.
Even those "I-could-do-without-them" songs have interesting things within
them that I can hear and appreciate.

    The Dreaming is the only album in which I * LOVE * EVERY * SONG *!! 
         It's Kate's magnum opus. Her greatest accomplishment.
                     It's a MASTERPIECE!

Re your reaction to TSW: I'm tempted to bestow my condolences. IF your
ability to feel Kate's magic has been smothered by tons of expectations
and comparisons, then I am truly sorry for you. IF feeling Kate's magic
will only take more listenings with an open mind, then go for it.

DON'T BLAME KATE OR THE SONGS ON THE SENSUAL WORLD! 

I've been a hard-core fan since 1981. If *I* can feel the magic, it's there!
Waiting to be heard. Waiting for those with open ears, open minds and 
open hearts. Waiting for you maybe?

Yes, I have put my asbestos suit on, preparing for those of you who can't
feel the magic of TSW and who are positive it has to be Kate's fault.
