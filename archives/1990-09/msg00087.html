Here's the news from the March 17 issue of _Billboard_:

The album is down to #87, from #76 last week.  Note that this is
the 20th week that the album is in the charts.

_The Sensual World: The Videos_ stays at #30 for its second week
on the Videocassette Sales chart.  It enters the Music Videocassette
Sales chart at #10, this week.  (The latter chart only appears every
other week; that's why it didn't show up last week.)

The order of music videocassetes on the regular Videocassette
Sales chart is different from the order on the Music Videocassette
Sales chart.  Anyone have any idea why?
