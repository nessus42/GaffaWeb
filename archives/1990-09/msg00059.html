Starting on April 1st, I am changing jobs.  I will be working at the
Media Lab here at MIT.  Unfortunately, I cannot bring my computer
GAFFA.MIT.EDU with me.  I will eventually acquire a computer there, I
am sure, but unless someone volunteers to run Love-Hounds, the mailing
list and rec.music.gaffa newsgroup may be down for an unknown period
of time.

In order to run Love-Hounds, you should have a Unix machine which you
can use to run the mailing list, you should know how to make a digest,
and you should know how to feed mail directly into
"/usr/lib/sendmail".  Any volunteers?
