   In a recent post, Robert Jude Kudla said something to the order
of (and I'm paraphrasing)-

   "Why does everyone think there's something wrong with KaTe writing
gay/lesbian songs?" (sorry, Robert, if I screwed that up!)

and caring enough to write about it! Hooray for KaTe! (As Larry so often
sez,    She Really IS!)
