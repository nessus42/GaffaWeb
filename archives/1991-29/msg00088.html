Randy writes:

>Which set me to wondering: what, if anything, has Kate ever said about
>her metaphysical beliefs?  She seems sympathetic to Pagans and Witches
>(viz. "Waking the Witch"), but has she ever come out and specifically
>stated what she does or doesn't believe in in that domain?


Here's a clip from the HoL-era interview on the CD picture-interview-disc
CBAK 4011:


I:  You talk about energies quite a lot.  Do you subscribe to
a particular theory or religion or anything like that.

K:  (laughing)  Subscribe to a monthly manual.

I:  You know what I mean.

K:  No, I don't, really.  But I do think certain things help,
like I think trying to be positive about things is a very
helpful thing.  It can just stop you getting down, and to try
and enjoy things if you can.  But I don't have any kind of
religious beliefs, really.



Perhaps Ron can come up with a better quote from Cloudbusting; I just
happened to remember this bit and it's one of the few interviews I 
actually have handy...
