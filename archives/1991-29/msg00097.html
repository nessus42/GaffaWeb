  Kate _was_ scheduled to appear on Letterman in 1984, on the last night
she was in New York. She begged off, our guess is that she watched the show.

been a pleasant experience. It is interesting to note that the day before,
she appeared on NBC's "Live At Five" which, as every _Late Night_ viewer
knows is broadcast fron the studio across the hall. One wonders if they met?
