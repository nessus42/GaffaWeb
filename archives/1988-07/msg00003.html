While everyone is waiting for the new Kate album, there is a new
Cocteau Twin's album due out in September to make the time go more
quickly. The Twins made a rare TV appearance (well in video form) on
the Channel 4 programme Wired last weekend, playing a track from the
new album: and excellent it sounded too.  Liz Fraser's voice was in
fine form.  Neil.
