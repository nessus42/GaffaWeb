	I assume you mean Our Lady's Image, since their can't possibly
be more than One Lady, The One and Only.
	Anyways, I have the same image, that was digitized with a high
S/N CCD camera, as well as several other images of our goddess,
including two (kludgey, but not bad) color images taken from two
magazine articles (Keyboard and Musician (I think)). (The reason I say
kludgey is that the CCD camera is only monochrome, so the color image
was constructed with filters and the color balance is not perfect, but
pretty good...)
	Unfortunately they are not currently in raster(5) format,
although it should be easy enough to put them in that format, but they
are in a very raw binary form (more or less simple byte stream), for
which I have programs that can display on Sun's, Ikonas frame buffers
and Data Translation frame buffers. It should be trivial to convert
them to any other format.
	If anyone wants them (or requests others to be digitized),
just let me know. I could make them available via FTP.
