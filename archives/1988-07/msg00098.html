     Anyone wishing to apply for admittance to the WSI is invited
     to take the following entrance examination. All the answers
     have appeared in Love-Hounds during the past twelve months.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------

 1. The first drew one, the other flew like one.

 2. Despite doing this, she would seem out of place on a basketball court

 3. His performances were touching, in other words.

 4. Later form of a giant-killer's diminutive, perhaps.

 5. She was inspired by 007 in the first case, but couldn't return the
    favour in the second.

 6. Two suggestions of lubrication.

 7. A pair of wizards suggested the first; wanderlust
    suggested the second.

 8. He "hit that note and let it float."

 9. One was a kid musician, the other blew away, but neither was a goat.

10. The car temperature on a trip to the mountains, and the effect of
    driving there.
