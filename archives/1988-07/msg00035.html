Kate Bush Scrapbook: a table of contents

Subject: Kate Bush Scrapbook: a table of contents

     Here is a list of everything that's included in the new bootleg
book "Kate Bush With Love". The book is printed entirely in
black-and-white.

Reviews:
^^^^^^^^
  "The Kick Inside"(w/Karla Bonoff debut LP), Bob Woffinden, 2/78
  "The Kick Inside", Sandy Robertson, 3/78
  "The Kick Inside", H.D., 3/78
  "Lionheart", Dave McCullough, 11/78

  "Wow," 2 reviews, 4/79
  "On Stage," 2 reviews, 9/79
  "Never For Ever", Paul Du Noyer, 9/80
  "Never For Ever", Ronnie Gurr, 9/80
  "Never For Ever", Tony Mitchell, 10/80
  "The Dreaming" (LP), Rose Rouse, 9/82
  "The Dreaming" (LP), Leyla Sanai, 9/82
  "The Secret History of Kate Bush" (Vermorel), by Kate Zeserson, 4/83
  "The Single File" (box set), Tony Mitchell, 2/84
  "Krufts Original", review of Hounds of Love, Jane Solanas, 9/85
  "Puppy Love", review of Laserium debut of Hounds of Love, 9-10/85
  "Cloudbusting" single, 10/85
  "Hounds of Love" album, 10/85
  "The Bush of Ghosts," brief piece on "Hounds of Love" album, MM, 12/85
  "Bush Fire," long review of "Hounds of Love" album, Mick Wall, 10/85

  "Katherine the Great," review of "The Whole Story" LP, Roger Holland, 11/86
