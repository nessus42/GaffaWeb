The Hounds of Love videos had yet to be made when The Single

Depends. There have been several printings of TWS in the US. I 
have a copy which offers a look at all the single and 
album covers in color. Another offers them only in B & W. And a 
third is a single page printed only on one side -- no 
information within at all. The only liner notes on any of these 
reveals chart positions of the songs and albums on TWS.

it's probably Canada.
