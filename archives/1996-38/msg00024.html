There's Moviestar Europe, located in Hamburg/Germany, but with a UK Web address:
http://cobweb.wintermute.co.uk/moviestar_europe/

I ordered four quite unusual CDs (non-Kate) there in July, and am still 
waiting for one of them (Henry Cow - Legend).

and a company named "German Music Express", but located in the Netherlands:
http://www.musicexpress.com/
they have all KB albums, the Japanese TWW-box, most videotapes, the Video-CD
and the Bushtucker album. I didn't deal with them so far.
