Wow! That's indeed a Japanese catalog number. And it's the exact catalog number that appears on the TKI CD that's in the Japanese This Woman's Work boxed set. But that CD, insert and booklet clearly say "Made in Japan".
The other CDP number is in the boxed set version as well. It's the regular
international catalog number from EMI. Is the CD tray in the jewel case
white? For the boxed set ones is. Another thing that distinguishes the
Japanese TWW issues is a small circled "1" on one of the spines - is it
there? Is there any Kanji writing there? On the back of the Japanese TWW
one there's "KATE BUSH BOX (1)" as well. So the TWW ones are clearly
distinguishable, at least the Japanese ones.

I do not know any regular Japanese TKI CD, only the boxed set one, so I don't
know the catalog number of it. You should ask on love-hounds. Your CD
definitely looks like a German-Japanese collaboration. Maybe someone with a
regular Japanese CD can help.
