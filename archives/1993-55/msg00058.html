>Just a quick note on getting RhodeSongs in the mail today.

Great compilation album.  The closest things to RhodeSongs are Rhodes I and II.
And yes, Happy does both the really low, and the really suspiciously Kate-like
highs.  My personal theory is that Happy has a time machine, and goes back
to visit Kate circa Never For Ever and gets her to record the higher vocals.
One of my worries is that Happy's highs are *so* Kate-like that she could be
dismissed as a Kate sound-alike or wanna-be (not a bad thing in itself, but
to some, a strike against originality (Not me!  I have them all!  No flames!)).
