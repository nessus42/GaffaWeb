P.S.  What exchange rate would the currency be converted?  What date would
      this exchange rate be determined?  See your cardmember agreement for
      details.  Depending on the credit card, you often get a better
      exchange rate through the credit card than you could get as an
      individual.  This is because the credit card companies can transact
      on the spot & futures currency markets.
