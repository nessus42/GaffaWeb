Well, these WERE the major record stores in Amsterdam you visited
(heck, I know: it's WAY to little), but what the clerk told you is
true. The TWW discs have been available separately practically
since the box's release back in november 1991. In the beginning a
friend of mine reported these boxes were indeed broken up, but as
time went on these discs have been available from EMI separately,
well, in the Netherlands anyway. The music library offers these
discs as well, and _not_ the box set (...as they obviously had the
previous discs already). So, to answer the question briefly: yes,
TWW part 1 & 2 are available separately in most stores in the
Netherlands, tho only the "really big ones" stock them. As a result
there are _no_ booklets available separately. (But the TWW box set
is rather cheap at Fame anyway..., well, cheaper at least than when
I bought it :-(  )
