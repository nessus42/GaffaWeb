No, there isn't a sudden collaboration that none of us knew about,
but it struck me recently that they would make a perfect musical
match, especially if Mike could get back to his early stuff.  Things
like Ommadawn (must really get that on CD) spring to mind.

track) and several tracks on TWW.

Who else has thought of this or agrees.  Maybe we could even get the
suggestion to either artist, problem is that KaTe's with EMI and
Mike's with Virgin (still? - think so) and you know how those dudes
can be :-)
