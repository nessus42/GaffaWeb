Okay here's a quick update.

TLTCTC still on for Friday Feb. 25 one showing only at 9:30 PM.  Tickets 
are firstcome firstserved.  The program for the theatre's Jan/Feb/March 
schedule says on the inside:

"New directors" are often expereinced filmmakers, or talented 
entertainers using the medium of film to continue their life's work.  
British pop diva Kate Bush makes her directorial debut with a snappy 
variation onf the Michael Powell classic <The Red Shoes> entitled <The 
Line, The Cross, The Curve> and starring Bush and Miranda Richardson.

I am unable to find out if there is a separate program for the Women's 
Film Festival.

Bye!  Oh, almost forgot, there are several new books out on Rock/Pop 
music that mention Kate.  One is "Rock & Roll Collectibles" which has a 
photo of Kate's handwritten lyrics for Wuthering Heights.

Another one is a guide to Rock Magazines which lists all interviews.  
Covers a wide number of magazines for the past 20 or 25 years.

Bye again.
