>Frankly, I'm surprised she chose Zappa at all!  Heck, most of his

"Most of his stuff"?  Meaning no offense, but how much of his stuff do
you know?  The only generalization *I* can make about "most of Frank
Zappa's stuff" is that it is _hard to play_. He and the folks he
worked with were capable of incredible discipline when it came to
"putting the eyebrows on" a performance.

"Opaque melodies that would BUG most people," to quote Don Van Vliet.
KTB has been mentioning Zappa and Beefheart for years.  They're both a
lot more *original* than the refried pop sludge that oozes from
commercial radio's sphincters (and that goes for the slime from your
video, too).  ;)  _Trout Mask Replica_ (Beefheart) *still* sounds
like it could have been recorded last week.  There's power in that.

>Well, I enjoy Overnite too, so there!
