But, people have favorite albums for different reasons. The Dreaming is my
favorite (ok, so far) musically, but it will always be my very favorite
album for other reasons also.  The album came out 3 months after my mother
had died and it was very important in helping me to focus on something
other than my grief. It and Peter Gabriel's "Security" were what snapped
me out of a miserable fog. They were such intricate and complex albums
and gave me something interesting to *do*, getting to know them and 
trying to figure them out.  Beyond that, the song "All the Love" just
completely blew me away, because of the lyrics, coming to me when they
did. You can imagine, I imagine.

I have deep, deep, deep emotional attachments to TD that I don't have
with any other Kate album, as much as I love them and as wonderful as
I think they are. *That's* why it's my favorite, and that's why it 
always will be.
