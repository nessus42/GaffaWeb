This album is called "Again" and is made from old songs re-recorded. Kate
participates on one of them(backing vocals and keyboards). If you have 
trouble to find it I can give you the complete reference(I think Alan is
well distributed in the US now).

Speaking of Alan Stivell I saw him in concert a couple of weeks ago (I hoped
for a godly appearance but it didn't happen:-). It was a good concert.
I met a guy there who told me that Kate and Alan have recorded enough stuff
to make a complete LP, that they wanted to publish it but EMI veto-ed against
it (I doubt this, knowing the artistic freedom kate has in EMI,  she must 
have thought the songs weren't good enough).
The same guy told me that copies of the Kate/Gabriel sessions were in circulation
among fans. Anyone here have them ?
