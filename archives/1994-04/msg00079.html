---- IMHO mode on ----

I'm not "in denial" about *anything* wrt Kate. TRS isn't my favorite
Kate album...never will be. "The Dreaming" holds that spot, and always
will. I accept TRS for what it is, and love it as such. I'm not one
to hold Kate to any expectations *I* might have for what she's *supposed*
to do. Rather, I accept her output for what it *is*. "Disappointment"
isn't in my Kate vocabulary. 

Kate's God, always will be.
