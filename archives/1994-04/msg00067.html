After waiting two months for this album from the first time I heard it was
going to be released, I have to say that I am not let down.  It has a bit
more variety than Little Earthquakes, and that makes it pretty interesting.

I am on my second listening to it now, and it is only getting better.  As
to people's comments about the fact they thought God was Kate Bush at first,
I did not even think anything but Tori.  It is the same style as she used on
Happy Workers on the soundtrack Toys.  In style I mean the pitch of her voice.

Anyway, the wait was worth it.  Go buy it.
