From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)

Subject: The biggest Konvention camera screw up of them all!


I was just talking to Peter Fitzgerald-Morris (of _Homeground_) on the
telephone.  Thanks to Neil, he now gets printouts of Love-Hounds.  He
said he was amused by our stories of cameras malfunctioning, but his
own beats all of ours.

You see, someone did not connect something up correctly, so the official
video of the Konvention did not come out at all!  Peter said Kate was
particularly annoyed because she wanted to see all the live acts.

Isn't that awful?
