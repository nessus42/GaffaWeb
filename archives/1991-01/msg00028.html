I AM JAZZED.  I had sent my pictures through th third time cause I wanted to
get the best repoduction possible and she was still quite white on a lot of
them.  I unexpectantly got them back today (its a Sunday and they aren't
supposed to put them out on Sunday, where I work there is a photo lab) and
they came out MUCH BETTER than I thought.  They are roughly simular to the
pictures in the Homeground expect most of them are slightly futher back and
less grainy (and of course COLOR). THe background screen and the rest of the
stage isalso darker.  Compared to my original set, her face is much clearer. 
The only drawback is that the rest of her body is slightly darker than the
original set (which was just about perfect) but the trade off is worth it.  I
would up there rating from SO-SOto PRETTY GOOD maybe even REALLY GOOD (in my
humble opinion).  I am quite happy about this as I was slighly worried that
people might be disatisfied with them, but not now. NOTE: the pictures of the
other performers aren't quite as good as the HOMEGROUND ones but thats
secondary.  I have been told that Peter from Homeground wants the pic of all
of us stuffed in the car, so those of you wondering about the LOVE-HOUNDS
reference in the currentHOMEGROUND now know what thats about.  Am going to
send him a set.  
    I am rambling! (trying to type fast as it costs me about 1.50 an hour to
be on here (local-long distance bill)).  I am going to send the pictures
though tommorrow (19 sets!).  This means they should be done about the 21rst
of January.  Barring any more screw-ups from the photo place (I hope they
don't screw up 1,900 pictures set!) I will send them that same day.  Here is
