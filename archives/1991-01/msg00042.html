From: **Love-Hound** <sre017%cck.cov.ac.uk@mitvma.mit.edu>

Subject: Tour news hits the press!!

In today's Melody Maker the following was written under the heading 'Kate to
tour UK?'.......
Kate Bush is planning a tour of the UK to coincide with the release of her new
LP.Bush,who is renown for taking a long time over her albums('The Sensual World'
was four years in the making),is said to be working 'very quickly' on her latest
 project,and is considering a tour to coincide with its release.
    An EMI records spokesman said:'Most unusually for Kate,she's working very
fast at the moment.She hasn't been under any pressure from the record company,so
 it's really up to her when the album comes out.She's thinking about touring
although she hasn't totally committed herself.If she does tour,it will be at
 thwe time of the LP release,maybe at the end of this year or sometime in 1992.'
