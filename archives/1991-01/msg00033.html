Kate was played on KITS yesterday as part of "What's The Connection?"
The three songs were:

Kate Bush -- "The Sensual World"
XTC -- "Senses Working Overtime"
Talking Heads -- "Life During Wartime"

This was an easy one.  The answer was "sense".  (The third song is from
an album called _Stop Making Sense_.)  It becomes slightly more difficult
(but only slightly) when you know that they didn't say what the titles of
the songs were until someone came up with the correct answer.
