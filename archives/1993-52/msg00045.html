> So that's why you're always selling Happy, huh? :)

When I discovered Happy, in 1988, very few people outside Albany, NY
had heard her music. I played her on my radio show, bought numerous
tapes to give away, told everybody I met about her, and in general,
tried to spread the music. Not for any personal gain (I know you 
were kidding) but because I love Happy's music, lyrics and voice
so much. Happy appreciated my efforts, and thanked me on "Warpaint."
Please remember (or know) that those credits were written after I
started talking about her on rmg, but *before* interest in her really
became widespread here. The release of "Warpaint" on CD is what
prompted more and more gaffa folks to try her out, which led to
the creation of Ecto, which led to her re-releasing her 1st 4
cassettes on CD (if you get the earlier CDs, check those credits
for "Thanks to the Ectophiles") which will, of course, lead to 
Worldwide Domination... :-)
