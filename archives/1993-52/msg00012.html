back in 1985. We never got in line then though, knowing it was hopeless. We
just milled around and met other fans, including |>oug. We didn't go this

certainly worth the amount we spent, so I'm not bitching. But it did
leave us tapped out for Kate's New York appearance. Now, if there had
been a concert involved, we would have *sold* things to be able to be
there!

Anyway, thanks for the nice report (I just read it to Andy/IED over the
phone) and the nice personal sentiments. Congratulations on getting
in and getting just a good "watching" spot!
