As others said, the windows were covered up last night at the CFNY
studio in downtown Toronto.  According to the people there this was a
condition imposed by the record company.

I'm not sure whether it was the petition we signed outside, or a clever
trick by CFNY to get around the record company rules, but Kate ripped down
the papers herself near the end of the interview.

Then we all went a little bit crazy.

Richard.
