Glen Davis asks if the "Home For Christmas" song on the US release of
"Rubberband Girl" is the traditional one or a new one.  Well, it's a new one,
written by Kate.  It's very pretty, but rather slight (weighing in at a measly
1:45 -- I know, quality, not quantity).  It's sort of old-fashioned.  Here are
the lyrics for a little taste:

You know that I'll be waiting
To hear your footsteps saying
That you'll be coming home for Christmas

Please say you won't forget me
That every moment's empty
But only 'til you're coming home for Christmas

If only I had wings
Then I would fly to you
Through all the snowy weather
We'd be together

No one makes me feel the way you do

--Trombone solo--

You know that I'll be waiting
To hear your footsteps saying
That you'll be coming home
Home for Christmas

-Sam
