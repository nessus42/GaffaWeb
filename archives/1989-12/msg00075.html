It would seem that Gary Numan has signed with IRS Records here in the
U.S.  There is a new compilation of fairly recent Numan material called
_New Metal Rhythm_ or something like that.  It is definitely different
than his recent _Metal Rhythm_ album, and has at least one piece ("My
Dying Machine") from _Berserker_.  I'm not sure about the other cuts, as
I didn't buy a copy.
