    To promote the policy of using e-mail for correspondence not likely to
    be of general interest, rather than USENET, I would like to remind
    posters to 'moderated' newsgroups ( such as rec.music.gaffa ) that,
    because their postings do not go directly to the net, it is important to
    include in your .signature ( or somewhere ) an adequate path for e-mail
    correspondence, as the Really-From address of person@site may well not
    be enough, and the full path to your site is not usually retained on the
    posting. By 'adequate' I mean as far as a USENET backbone site, or a
    major educational / industrial / government establishment whose node
    name will be recognised by the major relay sites around the world. This
    is most especially important for correspondence between different
    countries. Thank you.
