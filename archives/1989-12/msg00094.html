Larry said that the book he read said (breathe) that BMI logss are sent
   once a week from radio stations!  It ain't true!

The stations where I have worked, both professional large-market stations, did
   their BMI logging twice a year.  (Oh, maybe it is four times, but sure ain't
      weekly!)

The last time I did logging, though, I did play Kate Bush, but if she's not a
   BMI artist, it didn't do her any good... well, I tried.
