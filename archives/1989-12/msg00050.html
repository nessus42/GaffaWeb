I'm fairly certain that there is simply some confusion going on here.  There
is indeed a boxed set of Kate Bush singles, but they're all 7" vinyl (most
45's).  20 bucks, on a very good day, isn't unreasonably low for a used KT
boxed set.  Another tipoff: usually, condition (mint/good/fair etc) is
mentioned/discussed relating to vinyl, not CD's.  CD's (not their packaging)
are basically either mint or poor.  (Yes this *is* over-generalizing).
