Southern California Katemas Bush Bash preliminary announcement

From: IED0DXM%OAC.UCLA.EDU@mitvma.mit.edu
Date: Fri, 15 Jun 90 14:15 PDT
Subject: Southern California Katemas Bush Bash preliminary announcement

 To: Love-Hounds
 From: Andrew Marvick (IED)
 Subject: Southern California Katemas Bush Bash preliminary announcement

   Details are not entirely set yet, and directions are forthcoming,
but for everyone's information IED will be hosting a Katemas Bush Bash
on Saturday, July 28, 1990, at his home in West Los Angeles. Anyone in
Love-Hounds, and anyone else who is sincerely interested in seeing
some of Kate's best and rarest videos and meeting other Kate Bush
fans, is welcome. IED will provide hours and hours of video material,
including all five official Kate Bush laser-disks and dozens of rare and
unusual television appearances both vintage and recent. Refreshments will
also be made available, as well as a possible modest doorprize or two.
   The reason the party has been set for Saturday instead of Katemas Eve
(Sunday) is that IED understands a group of Kate fans intend to head out
to Disneyland together on Sunday, and it was thought that such an outing
should not impose time restriKTions on the video party.
   So IED hopes to see many of you there on Saturday. Specific times
and directions to the house will appear in Love-Hounds sometime next
week.

                 It is this that brings us together...

-- Andrew Marvick
