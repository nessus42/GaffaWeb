I've placed 3 orders from midnight, and 2 have arrived  with no prob's,
the third is due real soon; I'll let you know.

You can call 'em if you have a credit card, and they'll send you a catalog
if you mail 'em a buck, or it's free with an order (ask for it).

Their selection is good, their prices are on the high side!

I got a nice list of kate stuff from

do would be to get on his list, or call him with a specific request.
