Secrets of the I-Ching was re-released without the Maniacs permission
in 1988 (I think, I'll have to check) by Albert Garzon (he mixed the
album in 83 when it was recorded and must have had a copy of the master).
so apparently when they got popular he saw a chance to make money and
re-released it, the maniacs sued him.  I was lucky enough to have been
in Boston soon after the album was released and got a copy (the re-issue
that is).  I have never heard anything about it being re-released officially.
I hope it does get done there are some good tracks on it.  Some of them
were redone on the wishing chair: My Mother the War, Grey Victory, Daktari
(with slightly different vox and guitar parts) and Tension makes a Tangle,
there may be a couple of other that I'm forgetting.

As for Human Conflict #5 I have searched for almost two years now with
no luck :-(
