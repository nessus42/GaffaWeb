The Nymphs, Celebrity Skin, Painted Willy, fIREHOSE
4/17
Anti-Club
Hollywood

Two reasons for reviewing this show.  First of all, The Nymphs and
Painted Willy were the True indulgent dull-jam nightmare.  PW are
touring the great american southwest next month.  Avoid except under
fire.  Second, Celebrity Skin are very inventive, Robyn Hitchcock
influenced, somewhat crafty guys with definite off-planet tendencies. 
Even without former Germ Don Bolles on drums (didn't show) they were
an impressive unit.
