Subject: After the party...

Well, the second West Coast Love-Hounds party has come and gone, and Erik
Olson, Paul Traina, Peter Alfke and his mythical "friends", and Nancy
Everson's fictional "brother" never did appear, despite
the fact that they had all indicated their interest. No doubt they feel
they have some kind of excuse, but as far as IED is concerned, their
names are the blackest and foulest of mud.

Fortunately, a healthy quorum (IED's current word) did manage
to attend, and all impressed their host by their extensive knowledge
of the subjeKT. Thanks to Joe Castle, non-L-H Kate fan extraordinaire,
for the industrial-quality transfers of some of the unofficial video.
Let's do it again sometime -- at somebody else's place!

-- Andrew
