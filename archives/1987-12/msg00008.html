What do you want to know?

It is a double album set on the Giorno Poetry Systems Label.  It has a
side of John Giorno, a side of William Burroughs, a side of Laurie
Anderson, and a side containing 3 concentric spirals each of which has
a shorter piece or pieces by each of them individually.

Burroughs reads from several of his works.

Giorno "performs" his "poetry".  He has to be heard to be believed.

Anderson plays several selections.  It is interesting to hear her
earlier sound.  When you don't have the money for ton$ of $ynthe$izer$
you get a different sound.  It is nice to hear that her talent doesn't
live in the hardware.

Recommended to serious Anderson fans.  Might be hard to find.

If you want more information, drop me a line.
