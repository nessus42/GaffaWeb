appear as a posting on this board.  I hope no one was irritated by
the fact that I DON'T REALLY KNOW WHAT I AM DOING!

I'll ask mommy to hold my hand.

Enough of that, I've noticed some of you expressing difficulty getting
your wives or husbands interested in Kate Bush.
Well, I have no difficulty doing this whatsoever because -
I DON'T HAVE A WIFE! I DON'T HAVE KIDS! I'M ONLY SEVENTEEN!
HAHAHAHAHHAHHAHAHha-ha. ha. heh. 

Sorry, I got carried away.  Please don't take what I just said as implying that
there's actually anything particularly GOOD about being unmarried and seventeen,
because I don't really have any say in the matter, and that's just not at
all what I was saying.

Ignore at will.
