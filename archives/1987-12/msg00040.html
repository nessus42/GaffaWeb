Re Siberry, yes there is more, as a matter of fact. She
has actually released three LPs. The first was on a small

Dave, as far as IED knows, all editions of the Kick Inside CD
omit the original JCB/DP back cover art work, including the German
pressing which he owns. Maybe the Japanese pressing is different,
but IED has never got a good enough look at one of those to be sure.
You're right -- it is a pain about the new Dreaming back cover --
that solitary line "explained" the front cover even as it suggested
further mysteries.

achunter, you ask a familiar question. What is to be done for the
growing multitude of fans who desperately need to see the "unofficial"
Kate video, yet don't know how to go about it? The problem is getting
out of hand. That's why IED has been negotiating with certain
collectors in hopes of getting them to hurry up and get their video
organized, for the purposes of possible editing, transfer and distrib.
However, this remains a pipe dream for the time being. Usually
something is expected in return for rare Kate video, anyway, since
collecting and copying time, as well as costs, can be a pain.
Intergalactic Garage does offer a 2-hour compilation of
Kate video for about $35.00.
