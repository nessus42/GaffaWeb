Someone earlier mentioned the Clannad song used as the background music in a Volkswagen   commercial.  At first, I thought that the singer was Loreena McKennitt; it wasn't, but
I'd still like to mention her.  KaTe fans with a Celtic bent, take note.  If you can
find a copy of Loreena McKennitt's album "The Visit" at your local record store,
by all means pick it up!  It's a truly *beautiful* album.  It might be mislabeled
as "rock", so look around a little.
