     Rolling Stone (March issue w/Natalie Merchant) mentioned Kate is quoted in
Gillian Gare's book, "She's A Rebel:The History of Women in Rock and Roll." The
Kate quote, "The media just promoted me as a female body." Nothing new here 
but, perhaps there's more of Kate in the book? Has anyone read it yet? Others 
mentioned- Yoko Ono, Sinead, Debbie Harry, Joni Mitchell, and Patti Smith.
Unfortunately, Alison Moyet, Rosanne Cash, and Maria McKee are overlooked in
the book.
