I was also puzzled about this, but I found the answer in the Laurie Anderson
concert I went to on March 31st in Boston.

These clips are parts of the monologue that Laurie uses to introduce some songs
or are just part of her show. Having heard at least 4 of those monologues, I
was extremely bored at those parts of the show. I could say that if it wasn't
for the excellent performance of the title song "Strange Angels" from her
latest album the concert would have been a terrible disappointment for me.

And for all you Sinead O Connor fans. Watch out for her shows... The ones in
Boston sold out without getting any promo from the press. Whoever was listening
to the radio got tickets. The rest of us can pray for additional shows.

If any of you have any concert dates please post them here or e-mail them 
to the Sinead mailing list at: jitr@presto.ig.com
