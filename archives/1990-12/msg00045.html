RE:Uncle Bonzai
Unfortunately, I belive they have broken up.  The last recording I saw
from them was a re-release of their song "Men and Women" on the
Windham Hill new folk artist compliation titled "Legacy" (I highly
recommend it).  The male singer from the group has just started his
own band called "The Electric Bonzai Band" (despite the fact he
contuinues to play acoustic guitar in it).  The one cut I have from
them is on the Christine Lavin "make-out music" (her term, not mine)
compilation cassette called "On a Winter's Night", I believe.  It is a
great album, but I am not sure how well distributed it is.  I got my
copy at a local folk club in Boston.  If you are interested, I can
probably get an address to write for info on it.
