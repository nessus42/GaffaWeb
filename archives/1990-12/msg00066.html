Here's the info from the April 7 issue of _Billboard_:

Album down to #136 (114 last week)
Video up to #28 (35 last week) on the videocassette sales chart.  It

VH-1 has added the video for "This Woman's Work"!  Guess I should start

information is now completely integrated into the Pop Albums Chart.

Incidentally, _Cash Box_, which rarely agrees with _Billboard_, shows
_The Sensual World_ at number 107 this week.  Kate traditionally does
better on their chart -- don't ask me why.
