Anybody own and/or have access to this soundtrack?  Could you either tape it
or let us know where we can pick up a copy for ourselves?  Send replies through
e-mail either to me or to epollatsek@eagle.wesleyan.edu.  And if the show ever
goes up in your area, check it out- it's really thought-provoking and the
music is great!!!
