The concert was great! I just went to the one in Ottawa, Canada.
It takes a while to get really started, since she plays her new
material in the beginning, and the really rollicking pieces
don't come off too well with only a piano and guitar accompanying.
But her older material at the end of the concert really brings
the house down. A definite plus for this concert is the new
material she plays. You won't be able to hear it anywhere else
but at the concert. There were at least four new songs.
