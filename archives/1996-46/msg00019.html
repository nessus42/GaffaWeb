     I'm new to this group and have been listening in on the 
discussions.  First off I'd just like to say that the Kate vs. Tori 
bit is getting really annoying really fast.  Just my opinion, but I 
call 'em like I see 'em.
     About Kate's early videos . . . when you consider what "videos" 
were in the late 70's-early 80's, pre-MTV, Kate's were pretty unique 
and refreshing.  Someone mentioned Duran Duran--check out how 
primitive and goofy "Hungry Like the Wolf" looks in 1996.  DD's visual 
brilliance really came of age around '83;  If Kate's '81-82 
"Dreaming"-era videos weren't on par, the "HOL" stuff in '85 
unquestionably made up for it.
     Concerning the Love-Hounds video collection, I'd be interested to 
know what's on it.  I already have a copy of the Xmas TV special w/ 
PG, but I'm sure there's probably plenty that I'm lacking (anyone have 
the RUTH "Wogan" video that was shown here in the US as THE video?)
