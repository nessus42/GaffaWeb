Aloha everyone,

There have been a lot of inquires about the video collection of Kate's TV
performances since Chris W. mentioned them in one of his recent posts.  In
the spirit of the season I hereby offer to make copies of the Love-hound
Collection (two tapes) and the 'Katetopia' (one tape) video to any and all
persons wishing copies.  North Amercian Television Standard format only. 
My appologies to all the Fans in Europe and Asia who need the PAL format,
I cannot help you.  This is a not-for-profit deal but it will cost you
postage and the price of the video tapes.  If you are interested in
obtaining copies e-mail me at the following address:
braffet@hawaii.edu
