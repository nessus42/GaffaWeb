Check out the current issue of "The War Against Silence" for a review of
_This Fire_, the new Paula Cole album.  The preamble contains a very
interesting (and, imho, spot-on) meditation on Kate's influence on the
artists that have come after her.  The article is at
http://www.furia.com/twas/twas0096.html .

(There are also interesting musings on Tori Amos, Sarah McLachlan and Peter
Gabriel here, which should make the article all the more appealing to
gaffans...)
