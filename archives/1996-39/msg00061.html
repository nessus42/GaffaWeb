Speaking as a HUGE admirer of Kates' sixth album, I wonder if anyone 
else agrees that the pleasures of The Sensual World are best felt at 
this time of the year as we approach Autumn . The Sensual World, 
Never Be Mine, This Womans Work, The Fog, Deeper Understanding and 
Reaching Out are all KATE CLASSICS (IMHO) , I wasn't expecting Hounds 
Of Love Part 2 back in 1989, and Kate delivered something altogether 
different but equally absorbing .... that's what she's always done ,
and will continue to do . It's what makes her great .
