Jane Siberry is wonderful and you should all at least give her a try. Three
of her albums are very close to Kate's in their level of creativity and
individuality, IMO. Her style is different than Kates but there are
similarities. The 3 albums are: "No Borders Here", "The Speckless Sky", and
"The Walking". She has a great voice and uses it in a very unique way in her
backing vocals too. Many beautiful touching songs (You don't Need, The Taxi
Ride). All 3 of these albums are very well produced and ,as with Kate's, you
will be hearing things years from now that you had never noticed before.

If you want to know more about Jane Siberry you can certainly email me.
