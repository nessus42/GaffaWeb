For those of you who never bought the box set (TWW), go to 
the CDnow web page right now,http://CDnow.com, and order volumes I and II 
of the B sides! The sound quality is very good (i.e. if they are 
bootlegs, and I actually don't think they are, they are of excellent 
quality). They contain all the songs from the 2 B-side CD's on TWW, plus 
some extras (Rocket Man, Candle in the Wind, Rubberband Girl (12")).

I've been waiting YEARS to get these B-side albums without having to pay 
for the whole box set, and am at peace with myself for finally having 
just about every KB single out there. 

CDnow also lists a TWW vol.III, which contains B side singles released 
after The Sensual World.

I'm not sure how this co0mpany managed to do this, but bless them.

KB for the next Pope, or emperor of the planet (solar system, universe?).
