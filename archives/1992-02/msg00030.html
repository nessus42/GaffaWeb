deadman@garnet.berkeley.edu (Ben Haller) writes:
/More information please!  Are her four albums currently out on CD,
/from QRP or elsewhere?  What label did she sign to, and are any
/release plans, new albums, etc. announced?  I've been looking for
/this stuff for a while now!
 
 1. CDs: I've checked at HMV, which I know has outlets in the US,
 and the price is $C 14.00 or less for each of the four CDs. The
 rights are as per my previous posting (QRP, QRP, QRP, WEA). The
 address for WEA Canada is: Warner Music Canada Ltd., 1810
 Birchmount Road, Scarborough, Ontario, Canada, M1P 2J1 -- but
 you would probably do better to find your local branch and let
 them deal with the shipping hassles (after you check HMV (-: ).
 
 2. We're awaiting the arrival of her latest newsletter detailing
 tour plans (rumor: she may be touring the northern states).
 Since she has just released a new album (_The Visit_) within the
 last six months, and the promotional material and touring for it
 are still happening, one would not expect a new album just yet.
 I will post the pertinent data when it arrives.
 
 I do hope you get a chance to see her live. I first got to know
 her music when she played in the St. Lawrence Market in downtown
 Toronto, and even in that less-than-ideal setting, she put on a
 wonderful performance. On stage she's magic!
