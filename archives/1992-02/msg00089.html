pictures. I opened a Japanese version at work the other day (advantage of

machine" in KEN. Hope it helps.
   As for Kates Latin lyrics...the ones in Delius are grammatically 
incorrect. "Delius amat Deus...." Deus is nominative and there is no 
accusitive which is what 3rd person singular amat needs.
