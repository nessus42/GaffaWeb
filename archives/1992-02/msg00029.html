She has another great album from a couple years back, called 
_Y Kant Tori Read_.  (There's no artist name indicated on that

under "Y").  The Kate influence is very strong; "Kate Bush with
a knife" seems an apt description to me.  I like it a lot.

album. 

I'm glad to see she's released another one.  Hopefully she'll find
greater recognition in the U.K. than she has here in the U.S.
