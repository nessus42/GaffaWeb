as vickie mentioned, big hat has been signed to c'est la mort records.

what she didn't mention was that their first release on clm will be out
in february and is called _shimmer_. luckily though, i got a copy of it
today in the mail...only have had the time to listen to it once so far,
but i got a few impressions from it: more arty than hugo largo (didn't
think that was possible!), somewhat uneven musically, but i really like
yvonne bruner's vocals.

one thing i don't know: do they have any previously released material
besides the tracks on _hearts lust in limbo_? if so, where can i get
my grubby hands on it?
