NMDS order numbers...

From: rosen@ucbarpa.Berkeley.EDU (Rob Rosen)
Date: Sun, 16 Nov 86 16:11:19 PST
Subject: NMDS order numbers...


	I have a favour to ask of some of you.  I once had a copy of the
New Music Distribution Service catalog (the orange one, with the artwork
depicting little pins dancing around).  I found a couple of albums I wanted
to buy but before I got a chance to buy them my roommate, thinking that the
catalog was some sort of perverted sex manual, threw it out.

	I just got the update of the catalog but it doesn't have the order
numbers for the albums I wanted to buy.  So if one or two of you who have
the catalog could send me the numbers for all the LPs/EPs by a band called
OTHER MUSIC (described as a sort of psuedo-gamelan Bay Area-based band),
I'd be a happy man.  I also need the prices, since I forgot what they were.

	I wouldn't be doing this except for the fact that I can't find any
Other Music LPs around here, even given that I live in Berkeley, an acknow-
ledged mecca for alternative music.

	Oh yeah.  If any of you have actually HEARD anything by Other Music,
drop me a note (or send it to L-H).  I'd like to have some idea of what they
sound like other than NMDS's description.

	thanks

	%%Rob
