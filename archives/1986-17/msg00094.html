Yep, I bought it the other day.  It's just called "Camper Van Beethoven
Pitch-A-Tent Records/Rough Trade".  It not great *overall*, but is worth
buying for the excellent, almost authentic sounding cover of Pink Floyd's
Interstellar Overdrive.  Also on it is "Stairway to Heavan (sic)" which
sounds in no way like the beloved Led Zeppelin tune.  There's some good
psychadelic sounding guitar work on side 1 by Chadbourne, and Soviet Spys
swim upstream disguised as trout.  The album is diverse as expected, with
some ethnic flavor here, some country flavor there.

Your choice.
