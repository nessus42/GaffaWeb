  The New Trouser Press Record Guide lists a single album, "Colossal
Youth", on Rough Trade from 1980.  Personnel:  Alison Statton on vocals,
Philip Moxham bass, Stuart Moxham guitar & organ.  They are from
Cardiff, Wales.  Statton left and formed a band called Weekend, the
Moxhams formed a band called Gist which has one album ("Embrace the
Herd", 1983, Rough Trade).
