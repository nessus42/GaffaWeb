> Please!  Someone tell me what I should invest in to have a working
> knowledge of Miss Kate Bush.

Well, in a few days a compilation record of her music throughout the
ages should hit the stores.  It is entitled *The Whole Story* and this
should give you a sampling of her entire career.  Of course, most of
her best stuff is not on this album.  If you want her best record,
get *The Dreaming*.  Be forwarned, however, that this is also her least
accessible album.  If you want something more accessible, get her most
recent real album, *Hounds of Love*.
