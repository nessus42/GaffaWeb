Just got my hands on a tape from a little outfit called
Underground Cinema, straight from Boise, Idaho.  These
folks have journeyed down to L.A. to check out Life In the
Big City, and met up with our gang somehow.  They have a
three song cassette.  At worst I'll call them a strongly
TG/Chris & Cosey influenced band (femme vox, mostly electronix)
at best I'll say it's damn fucking amazing that anybody in
East Armpit USA is doing something like this.  Supposedly
there's a `big Industrial scene' in Boise ... I'll bet there
hasn't been anything like this since SPK hit Lawrenceville, Kansas
(sp?) in 1982 ...
