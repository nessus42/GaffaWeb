The last word on J.D.-bashing

From: Susanne E Trowbridge <ins_aset%jhunix.BITNET@WISCVM.WISC.EDU>
Date: Mon, 10 Nov 86 18:59:12 EST
Subject: The last word on J.D.-bashing

Okay, so my best friend's a rock critic.  I'm tired of having to defend
him from hofmann and wicinski, so you may consider this the last word on
the subject, from J.D. himself.  Now can we pleeeease go back to our
regularly scheduled discussions on Kate and good new music?

******************************************************************
To Wicinski and Hofmann:

Having finally seen the puerile blatherings of Messrs. Wicinski
and Hofmann, I must say that, offensive as the level of discourse
often is (is Mr. Wicinski's fascination with "sucking dick"
reflective of some deeply repressed desire, or is it simply an
attempt to emulate street language?), the discussion itself is
rather amusing.  After all, why would the two feel so compelled
to continually lambaste my reviews unless, deep down, they
believed that

A) I know more about music, on all levels, than they; or
B) I am usually right.

I therefore suggest that they lay off Sue, and confine their
whingeing to the "Post"'s letters page, like real men.

-J.D. Considine
******************************************************************
