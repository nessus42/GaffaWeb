Is this still around?  This is one of the biggest fraud-stories floating 
around on the net.  Neiman-Marcus does not have a secret chocolate 
chip cookie recipe, and whoever originally sent this message was probably 
trying to share his or her cookie recipe.  I remember when this message 
floated around at Colorado State's English Department a year and a half 
ago.  Sad to say, the recipe wasn't even that good, in my opinion.  I 
read an article last January about how this was all a hoax.
