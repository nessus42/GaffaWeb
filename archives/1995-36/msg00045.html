Hello friends, 

taking advantage of a lovehound's generosity, I had the great pleasure to
get a tape of Jon Drukman's "Kate-Rape" and I like it very much. I would
like to contact him. Is there anyone on this list, who knows, what he's
doing at the moment and knows his actual email?

BTW, I have two VERY important questions:

1. On the "Never for Ever" LP you can find the inscriptions     
   A: "Do" 
   B: "Mi".
   Is there any KaTe relation?
 
  Do you know, that in the runout groove of the Roy Harper LP "The Unknown
Soldier" (1980) is scratched: "To Kate with love Roy" ?

2. Is there anyone on this list, who can rebuild the cover of the
   "Collectors Guide" in his room?
   At the bottom you can see a blue (sky) "Wuthering Heights" sleeve.  
   What's that?

BWT2: I had forgotten the place of the KT-symbol on HoL. I only remembered
it to be on the back cover. It took me ca. 2 min to find it. It is well
hidden. Give it a try!

BTW3: A while ago I asked about the "German version of RUTH". Do you
remember? Now I found a Doug Alan mail in the archive, that he asked Lisa
at the KBC about this and the answer was: No, there is no such version.

BTW4: A name mentioned very seldom on this list is Mike Oldfield. I think,
he's a music-genius, he's unique. For me he represents beauty in music. 
What, if he and KaTe would do music together? 
Maybe they thought about it, but turned it down, because they
know, the result would be soooo incredibly beautiful, that all humans would
die at once (and the earth would explode in a great boom-bang)?

"Dreams are." (Happy Rhodes)
