Hi Lovehounds,

Just thought I'd clear up this meteorological stuff...

In the song, the voice refers to Cumulus Castellus,  generally
"Castellatus" or the more modern usage "Castellanus" is the
description of a cloud which is exhibiting vertical growth in
the form of turrets.

Usually we refer to "altocumulus castellanus", which is a middle level
cloud (approx 8000 to 20000 ft above sea level) seen in the morning
in the form of a line with series of verically developing cumulus
cells.  This indicates an unstable atmosphere with the potential
for thunderstorm development later in the day.
