Hi Folks!


Okay, my tone-setting was not very diplomatical, but I thought this was
the only way people would notice my request.  How unfortunate, I started
a flame war. Sorry for that.

But I still cannot understand how the people who prefer Tori Amos talk in
this newsgroup can think that they're right.  Isn't it rightness that has
to protect the individual instead of democracy protecting the majority?

However, Jon Drukman is right: I try to read this information via the
Love-Hounds mailing list and not via news or notes. So, can I get a little
more of deeper understanding here?

Furthermore, I DO like to hear what other KT fans think of other artists, such
as Tori Amos, Peter Gabriel, Utah Saints covers (yeah, I know it now, Vickie,
thanks), the Cure, Talk Talk etc, but this does not include concert dates
and concert ticket availabilities and especially not in the US (though I know
this newsgroup is mainly read by US citizens).


Hope this will settle down the discussion.  At least it will be my last
contribution to it.

Ben.
