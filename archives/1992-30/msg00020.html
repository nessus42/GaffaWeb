I accidentally deleted Larry's post before I could answer it, so thanks
Mike, for giving me another chance. _Passion_ is up there, and it's one
of my favorites. I suppose that's the trouble with "ranking" albums...
Something has to go near the bottom. In cases like Peter and Kate and
Happy and Jane, artists who have multiple albums, all of which I love,
the favorites are the ones that mean a little something extra to me.
The albums at the bottom of the list are the ones I just don't listen to
as often, even though I love them dearly.

Kate            Peter              Happy               Jane

Dreaming        Security           Ecto                Walking
TKI             PGIII              VI                  Speckless Sky

That doesn't mean I dislike Lionheart, PGII, VII & JS, I think they're
all wonderful albums, but they always seem to get ranked last.
(Yeah, I separate the 2 sides of HoL, it's my list :-) )
The albums in the middle can undergo the "whatever I'm listening to..."
syndrome, but the first and last ones never seem to change.

But yeah, Larry, I did forget about Passion. Thanks for reminding me.

It isn't that I think that Peter will never top Security or that
Kate will never top TD....wait, I lost my train of thought, because...

TD.  (IMHO, of course & as usual)
