received the latest little light 'zine from the aathp over the weekend.
and another nice issue it is too. features reviews of cloudbusting, the
early years (is that the correct title?) portfolio (someone here asked
about this recently, so yes, it does exist), sarah mchlachlan and tori
amos. articles on roy harper, katealikes and a fictionalized story about
aathp's forthcoming boston KT parTy (hey - i thought that's what the name
of greg's 1992 KaTemas party was). various KaTenews, letters and the
usual artwork (which has improved finally). also word that tracy robyn
(spelled it right!) sommerville will have a new issue of still breathing
soon and a note to lonestar lionheart kevin hendryx to let us all know
what's up after the words he had with love-hounds (remember that little
event?).

all in all, a decent 'zine. send them $10 for a year's sub (four issues).
for that price, it's more than worth it.
