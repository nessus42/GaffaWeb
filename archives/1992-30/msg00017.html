> I didn't carefully read all articles lately (mainly because so much non-KT
> stuff in between), so I missed what all those new box sets are about, such
> as Never For Ever and Lionheart. Can anyone tell me:

> 	- where they come from (is it an official release);

UFO Books, aka UFO Records.

> 	- what is in them;

bog-standard CD, large softbound book of photos (48pp), postcard,
monochrome poster, T-shirt. Some Lionheart boxes didn't have T-shirts,
some NF boxes don't have CDs.

> 	- whether there is new music material in them;

Nope.

> 	- where to obtain them;

Mostly mail-order specialists (never seen one in a shop)

> 	- how much they cost.

Here, about L28 (NF, no CD) to L35 (NF, with CD). The Lionheart ones
are getting hard to find now.
