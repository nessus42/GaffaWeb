i thought that the live cd ep that people have mentioned was recorded at
a performance in toronto, not on cfny. at least, that's what the program
seller at the club bene show told us when we asked if there were any
forthcoming releases.

other releases that sarah mchlachlan has out are all singles. from the
nettwerk catalog (these are the cd catalog numbers, some vinyl is avail-
able too):

w2-3023   vox (extended remix)/vox (instrumental dub)/into the fire (ex-
          tended mix)

w2-3035   steaming (radio mix)/steaming (dance mix)/solsbury hill (live)

w2-3056   path of thorns (radio)/path of thorns (lp)/shelter (bass/violin
          mix - featuring jocelyn lanois of martha and the muffins and
          crash vegas on bass)

w2-3063   into the fire/sad clown (cbc)/black (cbc) (the two cbc songs
          are acoustic and orchestrated and are taken from a live cbc
          performance on brave new waves)

w2-3065   drawn to the rhythm/gloomy sunday (live)/drawn to the rhythm
          (live acoustic)

w2-6313/4 live ep (limited version supposed to have a reprint of the tour
          program included in the jewel booklet)

i've got the into the fire and the vox singles and am waiting for nettwerk
to send me the rest of them (separate packages for large orders, you know;
also finally ordered the first single gun theory cd which i've been meaning
to do for a few years now).

woj
