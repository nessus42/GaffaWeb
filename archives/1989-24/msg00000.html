MTV should get on the ball!

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Mon, 16 Oct 89 22:41:46 PDT
Subject: MTV should get on the ball!


I was just speaking to Vickie Mapes a few minutes ago.  She said
the new album wasn't mentioned at all on tonight's MTV new music news,
and usually albums are announced on this show the day before their
release.  She said if they don't mention it tomorrow, every one of 
us should write or telephone MTV to complain -- after all, Kate is
a major artist, and the release of the new album should be mentioned,
at least.

So if anybody knows how to get in touch with MTV in any way, please let
us know.  Andrew Marvick mentioned knowing somebody there, but he's
currently unavailable.

Thanks.  Everybody, your support is needed!
