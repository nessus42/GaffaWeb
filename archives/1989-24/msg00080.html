I am bummed, I called the local radio station to request something off
of TSW, I figgered I would do my part to help the world hear THE MUSIC.
Well, first I got a "Who is KB" from the DJ, "if you want to request
that, call the program director" so, next morning I called  the
director, he said "Well, I like KB but we are a top forty station, she
doesn't fit our format" Hrmmph, not good enough for me, I said "so
what, play it anyway" and he responded "well, CBS isn't promoting her
new album as 'top forty' and they didn't send us a copy of the album or
the single" I said "but..." he said "well, sorry. even though CBS has
the rights, they aren't publicizing the album as top forty"

I am bummed but I am glad I don't listen to the radio. I am especially
glad I don't have to hear about new music from the radio either.
