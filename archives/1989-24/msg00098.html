Among the first things I did upon arriving in Boston was to head for the
Coop in Kendall Square and burst through the record department turnstiles
screaming "Where iS iT? Where iS iT?" 

I tarried long enough to pick up a case of blank tapes, and as I bent over
to collect them I heard the unmistakeable sound of bells...

Yes indeed, TSW was playing _as_I_bought_ TSW.  The cashier confirmed that
stock was moving fast.
