Just my one little comment about The Sensual World...

I think THIS album, more than any other Kate album, needs to be played
LOUD!! As soon as I got the album last Saturday, I stuck the CD in and
selected Rocket's Tail, which everyone on Love Hounds was raving about.
10 seconds into the song, I HAD to stop it, turn up the volume, and start
it again. LOVE IT!! I don't know WHAT you people have against Reaching
Out. I love that almost as much. I'm still digesting though. :-)


  -Mike
