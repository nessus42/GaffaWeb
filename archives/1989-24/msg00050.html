Much to my surprise the CD made it's debut @ my local Strawberries "on sale"
at $11.99. At this store most new CD's are $14-$15 and the ones they're trying
to liquidate are $9.99. (I'm 50 miles outside Boston, so there aren't any 
"price-wars" to speak of) So I was pleased that they didn't seem to be 
"milking" what might be considered the "cult following" (must have at ANY price
when it first comes out). Matter of fact her "oldies" are going for more at 
the same store! (classics?)

The CD was rather prominately displayed on the "new-release" display rack,
as well as being available under "Kate Bush" section. Maybe this price is part
of a scheme by CBS to get more people to "try Kate", but then again I've seen 
no videos yet (the new way to push records). I'd be curious if this was a local
Stawberries "loss leader" or if other chains (Tower, etc) have done similar 
relative price reductions.

just an observation,

-Bob T.
