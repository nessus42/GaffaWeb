I've not heard BK but have TWLW.  For the most part the recording quality is
pretty awful.  But its still worth having if you're a "live fan" such as
myself.  Its probably worth it just for the version of Breathing on it.  But
be warned, there are three blank spots on the cd.  Two during Wedding List and
one during, (I think, if I remember correctly) Ran Tan Waltz.  Of course all 3
of the above songs are on BK also.  The specialty on TWLW is the live version
of Don't Give Up. 

Good luck,
