	Thanks to all who have written in response to the Summer 1994 Tapetree
announcement I managed to lash together before disappearing to the Outer Banks
two weeks ago.  Sorry for this bulk acknowledgement; give me a day or two to
get unpacked, debrief with Tom Estes and Chris Williams, and I'll get back to
everyone who's written me.  I've got two weeks' worth of Digests to peruse, 
also.

	Last weekend on Ocracoke, I happened to tune in last year's MTV summer
version of "House of Style" (they played three or four years' worth of them in
a row one afternoon) and I caught the bit on the Canadian model who shaves her
head, who remarks that she likes Kate Bush along with Nine Inch Nails and a
couple of others I couldn't make out in the mumble.  It was a feature piece on
younger models, and I thought this was enough of a credit to her I would
remember her name; but no...

	Suffice to say it was a welcome reminder that Kate is everywhere.
