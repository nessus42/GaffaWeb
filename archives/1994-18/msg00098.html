Peter Chow from Brighton adds:

The recording of "Under The Ivy" is most likely one from a special 200th show
edition of a UK music tv programme called "The Tube". The show featured Kate
performing the song 'recorded live' at Abbey Road studio with just her and a
piano. Footage of the performance was intercut with bits of the video for
RUTH. The show also featured the UK tv premiere of "The Big Sky" video.
