TRS producKTs in Hong Kong

Subject: TRS producKTs in Hong Kong

Organization: UTCC Public Access

After suffering from gaffa-withdrawal for 3 weeks, I'm finally back from
Hong Kong.  While there, I was perusing a local Chinese newspaper one day
when my eyes were drawn to the words "Kate Bush".  The article was about 5 
record stores in Hong Kong, one of which specializing in limited editions
and box sets.  The photo accompanying the article was of 3 TRS shoe boxes -
the official heart-shaped one, the shoe-box shaped one with a T shirt and
a pin, and the other box-shaped one with a teeny pair of red ballet shoes.

So I made a bee-line for the place.  The two fake boxes were HK $500 each
(approx. US $70) and the official one $800 (US $120).  Since I'm not
a collector, I passed up the chance to own one, but Les (here in Toronto)
is now the proud owner of # 395 of the official box!

Both UK and Japanese pressings were available for TRS as well as Tori's
new album and singles, the Japanese pressings being about 40% more expensive.
I also saw Sarah McLachlan and Loreena McKennit discs.  The only thing I 
saw with Jane Siberry on it was "Summer in the Yukon".  Alas, no Happy !-)
