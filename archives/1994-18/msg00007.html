Hello all!

Apologizes if this is old news, but I just read the following in the latest
issue (6-3-94) of the southern California edition of BAM (Bay Area Musician)
in "The Biz" column...

"...A 45-minute film directed by and starring Kate Bush will premiere July
1st-2nd in Los Angeles at the NuArt Theater.  _The Line, The Cross, The
Curve_ is based on Bush's _The Red Shoes_ and had its U.S. bow at the
Berkeley Women's Film Festival earlier this year.  The Bush film will be
shown with Laurie Anderson's _Home Of The Brave_..."

Yippee for all us smog-puppies! :-)  If anyone finds out more details, please
post!
