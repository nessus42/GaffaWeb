particular song.  He thought that FEAR was one of the best bands ever
and hung out with Derf Scratch quite a bit.  It was Belushi who talked

FEAR's thrashing fans grabbed the mike and yelled "Fuck New York! Fuck
New York!"--still one of my favorite SNL's
