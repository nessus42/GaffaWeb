Feederz, Foundation: 2 F*cking great albums

  • Subject: Feederz, Foundation: 2 F*cking great albums
  • From: hogge@p.cs.uiuc.edu (John Hogge)
  • Date: Mon, 23 Mar 87 01:37:46 CST
Well well!  We do have quite an interesting album here with Feederz' 
"Teachers in Space".  We have some thrashing fast punk, some poetic vocals 
over post-punk dissonance (really NICE word collages), some fine little
melodies, and some great found-sounds (the Intro, from some movies perhaps?).
All very social-concious.  This album is WELL worth having, and as a bonus,
the cover art is a great momentum from the space shuttle explosion.  OH Yeah,
you also get the passage from Network (I believe) culminating in the
statement: "I'm as mad as hell, and I'm not going to take this anymore!!!".

How does this one compare to Feederz other album?
---------------
New EP from Foundation called "Voyage" on Fartblossom Enterprizes (POBox 818
Pomona CA 91769).  This is a band from DC I believe.  Great Husker Du
Zen-Arcade style fast melodic psychedelic thrash whatever, with some nice
metalic guitar leads here and there and strong songs.  Side 2 is my favorite,
but side 3 is strong too.  ORDER NOW!  

--John
