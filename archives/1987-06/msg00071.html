Actually Keith Haring kind of appears in Diamond... his drawings (now
incredibly trendy) are all over the album cover. Diamanda Galas' recent
releases are supposedly related to the events surrounding AIDS. Coil has
released a 12" cover of Tainted Love with proceeds going to AIDS
research.
 
> BTW - the album in question _A Diamond..._ also happens to be a
> great compilation.  I'll try to post a review in the near future.
> I'd also like to hear other folks opinions of any Dial-A-Poet
> compilations; so far I've liked _A Diamond..._ best but I'm

Most of the Dial-a-Poet comps are more heavily into spoken stuff than
music. I've heard snippets from the Nova Convention (the Allen Ginsburg
piece on punk rock is hilarious, but the folk singing sucks), the one
with Leroy Jones/Amiri Baraka, Burroughs, Giorno, Jim Carroll etc. and
a few others. Great if you like this type of material, otherwise much
more sedate than Diamond.
