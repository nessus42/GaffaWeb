I got a response from the KBC today! Only a week and a bit after I sent
a request for subs info. Not quite as fast as the Homegrinders, but
getting there 8^)

According to the application form, the joining fee is now L5 for UK, L6
for Europe and surface mail outside Europe, and L10 for airmail. They
also accept dollars: $26 surface or $36 air. The token renewal fee is
now L1 UK, L1.50 surface and L2.50 air. The "interim leaflets" are
still being promised. The KaTephoto is a whopping big 10"x8", but it
doesn't say it's signed 8^(
