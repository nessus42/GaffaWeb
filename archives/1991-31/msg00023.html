From: ed@wente.llnl.gov (Ed Suranyi)

Subject: Sarah McLachlan out on Sept. 10
To: love-hounds@EDDIE.MIT.EDU


I just read that Sarah McLachlan's new album, _Solace_, will finally
be released in the US on Sept. 10.  I got my copy in Canada, where
it's been out a long time already.  It's really good, I think.  If
you liked her first album, you'll want this one.

On the same day, four Cocteau Twins albums that were previously
available only on import will become domestically available.
