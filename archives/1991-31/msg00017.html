Someone was talking about the new Toni Childs video.  Her first album,
UNION was fantastic.  She has a deep, rich voice.  Her concert - at least
in Portland, Oregon was packed.  Her new album is called House of Hope.
I have never heard any of her music on the radio - nor on the video
channels - I was lucky to discover her via our college experimental radio
station, KBVR at Oregon State.  If there is a video of hers from this new
album, great, she deserves some publicity.
