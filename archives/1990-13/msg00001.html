As most of you will know by now, the new album 'Days of Open Hand'
will come out on April 16th. At the same date a single/CD-single
'Book of Dreams' will be released. The songs on the new album are:

   * Tired of sleeping        * Those whole girls (run in grace)
   * Men in a war             * Room of the street (was Cuba)
   * Rusted pipe              * Big space
   * Book of dreams           * Predictions
   * Institution green        * Fifty-fifty chance
                              * Pilgrimage
