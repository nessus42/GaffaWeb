Here's a track listing of this CD EP:

1. "The Sensual World" (LP version)
2. "Be Kind To My Mistakes" (version from the "This Woman's Work" single)
3. "I'm Still Waiting"
4. "Ken"
5. "The Sensual World" (instrumental mix)

Most of the big record stores around here had at least a few copies of
it.  It's a regular release from Columbia.  Catalog number:
Columbia 44K 73174.

Since this is a current release from a large record company, any place
that does mail order should be able to get it for you.  I would be
surprised if there was really no place in the Philadelphia area to 
obtain this.
