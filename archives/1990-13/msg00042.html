>I've never heard any of the alternative mixes (aside from Jon's
>creations) and I know some of them have agreed-upon names.  For
>Cloudbusting, the version played adds some echo to Kate's voice towards
>the beginning.  What's this mix called?

That would be the Orgonon Mix.  Identified incorrectly on at least
one release as the Meteorological Mix but don't you believe it.  The
Meteorological Mix is the remix of "The Big Sky" (with all those
quotes about "that cloud looks like richard nixon" etc ad infinitum)

I guess for the record we should mention that the RUTH 12" is called
"extended version", HOL 12" is "alternative hounds", Experiment IV 12"
is "extended version."  There are a few "special single mixes" for
some TSW songs but who really cares...

Incidentally, while visiting friends in Boston to see The Residents,
we came up with about 800 appellations for my remix of "Love and
Anger" most of which were far more amusing than "Southerly Wind Mix."
My version of RUTH has become known variously as "the chipmunk mix" or
"katemas 88 mix"... take your pick
