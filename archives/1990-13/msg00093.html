Subject: Mandela concert

In case anyone was wondering ... Kate did not turn up to perform
with Peter Gabriel at the Nelson Mandela concert. Tracy Chapman
shared the vocals on "Don't Give Up" with Peter. She seemed very
nervous but it worked OK. Rumours that Kate did not turn up
because she had OD on Easter Eggs were unconfirmed :-)

Neil
P.S. it was a good relaxed concert. Pleasant if not great music but
in a good cause so who is complaining.
