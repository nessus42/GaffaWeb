As I promised last week, here is a list of what was shown last week
at the U of MN St. Paul student center theater as part of their
progressive rock on video series.  For those of you in the Minneapolis
area, the shows are every Tuesday at 7:00 in the U of Mn St. Paul
campus student center theater.   Admission is free. The shows run until 
around 10:15.  The shows will be going on for 7 more weeks (early Genesis is 
next weeks show).  Call the student center for more information.

They cut some of the videos/performances from some of the interview shows
if we had already seen the video once, so don't depend on the above list
for a complete listing as to what was shown on any of those shows.

I did catch a few KT symbols.  One in Army Dreamers and one in
The Big Sky.  I also think that there might be one in Suspended In Gaffa.
Anyone know of any others?  It was kind of hard to look for them, not being 
able to rewind and all (and I kept getting caught up in watching them!).  
I didn't even think to start looking until I saw the one in Army Dreamers 
(it was pretty obvious).
