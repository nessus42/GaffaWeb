Yes!  Please post more about it.  I can probably live without Foetus if
it sounds anything like the name implies to me, but I'd love to know
what else is on the compilation.  I've been in 'compilation mode' lately
after just picking up 'Zang Tumb Tuum Sampled' and 'One Pound Ninety
Nine -- The State of Things (Beggars Banquet)'.  The latter introduced
me to such wonders as 'Haunted When The Minutes Drag' by Love and Rockets
(which then prompted me to run out and buy their album, but I havn't had
time to hear it all yet), and 'Perambulator' by Icicle Works (which
doesn't appear to be on their new album), as well as the Ramones'
classic(?) 'Bonzo Goes To Bitburg'.  The former has a great version
of Born To Run done by Frankie Goes to Hollywood, as well as a couple of
cuts I like by Propaganda (anyone know anything else about them??),
also Andrew Poppy (again, anyone know about him?)), Art of Noise and
others.  These compilation sort of things seem to be a great way to get
introduced to new music, especially since there is not a whole lot of it
around on the radio.

a double single by them with two versions of 'Just Like Honey' (at the
