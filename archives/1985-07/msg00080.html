Press entry is this fantastic 12" dance mix a friend in Washington

ought to talk about other music until he floods our senses with his

mostly because he made the acquaintance of an EMI rep at a Roger Miller
show ... I think he lied about the size of love-hounds but praise duh
Lawd that the guy fell for it.

he is waiting for some nude shots of her ...
