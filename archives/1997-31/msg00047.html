Hi everybody,

just got the latest KBC newsletter (18) today - is this a kind of bad joke?
Instead of any news concerning Kate just some rubbish about
Paddy Bush - is this a Paddy Bush Fanclub or what? They should
have served their ink and paper (and our postage) for something really
interesting.
I am very annoyed. What do you think about the KBC's work? I think other
fanclubs
do a much better job than they do. Why don't we discuss this here?

By the way, Kate's warm words did not touch me this time - a very
poor "Thankyou" for all the fans. But of course she's too busy these
days - the new album will be released soon (just in about three years or
something).

Thilo & Beate
