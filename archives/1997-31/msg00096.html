I found a short article about Bjork and the Sugarcubes way back from 1989.
Each bandmember choose their "Single Of The Week".
Bjork's favourite was "The Sensual World":

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------
Bjork's single of the week:
Kate Bush - The Sensual World (EMI)
(Two years (?) beavering away quietly this is Kate's first work since "The
Hounds Of Love" LP).

Bjork: "I used to be a big Kate Bush fan when I was about 12, when her
first record came out.
But I was really disappointed with "Hounds Of Love"!
It's not enough really to just listen to this once. I like it."
...
Chart prediction: I don't know. People here in this country like her, don't
they? 15, maybe".
