You know its worth cycling home eight miles (and 900' vertically) when
you open the mail box and there's Kate Bush, or at least her picture on the
front of Option magazine (Mar/Apr 1990 No. 31 5th Anniversary Issue).
Inside the article, an interview by Maria Montgomery Sarnoff with
Kate and also Del Palmer, is five sides long with four photos, two colour ones
in a wood (one full page), both mainly Kate with a hint of trees in the
background, one (b/w) from the L&A video (I think) and one other
of Kate sitting and looking at the camera with her hand out (b/w).

The interview mentions fanzines, Bushcon and Katemas but doesn't mention
rec.music.gaffa :-(
