POLL: The song that got you into Kate

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Tue, 27 Feb 90 10:55:42 PST
Subject: POLL: The song that got you into Kate


I have an idea.  We've been talking lately about the songs that first
got us into Kate.  I'd like to take a poll.  Simply MAIL me (do NOT
post) your choice as the one song that first made you interested in Kate.
This is not necessarily the song that you now consider your favorite.

Sometimes this is a tricky choice.  In my case, I first became aware
of Kate when I heard "Wuthering Heights", because I had been a big
Pat Benetar fan.  However, the first song I heard that made me desire
to hear more was "Them Heavy People", about twenty minutes later.
So I'd say it was the latter that first got me into her.

I will post a summary in a little while.
