   First, I'd like to clear up some things with Julian, though I imagine
he has my message by now.  1) I am a male, not a female.  2) Azhrarn was
just a calling card, not something expected to be talked about on
love-hounds. 3) Like most people, I too make mistakes, and 4) Just for
you, I'll stop using Azhrarn, since I don't think it belongs on
Love-hounds.
   Thank God that's over.  Now, I'd like to mention something about
Vickie and Chris.  Someone, no names,  implied that reading a
chris'n'vickie post was something wrong.  Personally, I think their
posts are extremely interesting and a valuable asset to this board.  If
you're going to make a sarcastic comment, remember that people on
computers can't hear or interpret your tone of voice.  All we see are
words, which very often can be misinterpretted.  Perhaps the use of a 
:-) could save alot of misunderstandings and hurt feelings.  Chris and
Vickie, you have my wholehearted support to post whenever and whatever
you want, since I feel quite comfortable in either of you being able to
judge what deserves to be posted.  Keep up with the excellent posts!

					K. Bernatz
