Subject: Kate GIF/IFF Files Via Dial-up


If you're seeking GIF or IFF files of Kate you want to call
the "KAOS" BBS system in California.  Ken Saint-John is one of the
Sysops there and he has a nice collection of Kate pics that you can
download from there.

Ken not only scans the pics but he has also done some creative things
with a few of them.  His scanned pic of just Kate's eyes (I think taken 
from the "Babooshka" poster) will hypnotize you.

If you're interested call the BBS at (805) 399-1765.  If you leave a
note for the sysop telling him that you heard about this here and that
you're interested in Kate pics, he'll give you access ASAP.  While
you're on there drop a note to Ken Saint-John.  He can listen in here
but can't post yet.  Ken's a long time Kate fan and he'll be glad to
hear from you.
