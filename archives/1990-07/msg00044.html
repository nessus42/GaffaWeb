Hi folks,

	As I was the one who posted way back when the track titles for
	the chronicle discs, I figured I should let people know what is
	going on.

	At present, all three volumes actually do exit, they are
	released as a limited edition, I don't know how many copies
	there are. US customs is not letting any of them into the
	states for re-sale. (As far as I know) If you happen to know
	someone in Japan who can mail you a copy, that is probably the
	only way to get them.

	Chronicle 2 track listings were changed. One of the members of
	the band decided that he didn't want one of the songs on one of
	the sets. I don't know which song it is but there is a
	difference from the original announcement.

Good luck on finding the sets, I don't have them either so I am looking
alongside the rest of the world...
