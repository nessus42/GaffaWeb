Hi, I wandered into 'missing link' here in Melbourne today & copped
sight of an LP from a new icelandic group called 'Ham'. After listening
to the 1st track I decided it was definitely not my type of music.

A cross between Mudhoney/Tad/My bloody valentine & gorillas on heat!

A better new group was one called 'risaedlan'.  I haven't a clue
where they are from, but their style is similar to the new Sugarcubes LP,
& the only pressing they have is a 4 track 12" with no title (or english
writing).  I was going to buy it, but then the price - $A18! - no thanks,
not at that price!

Anyone else know any more?
