My introduction to KaTe was sort of amusing.  While hanging around
woj's dorm room, I took notice of the numerous KT posters adorning
walls, ceilings, doors, roommates, etc.  So I said, naively, "Lemme
borrow a couple of her tapes....I'll check her out." 

Well, I won't go into my various thought processes of the following
day; suffice to say, 24 hours later I showed up on his doorstep, 
bearing a sign which read:

			SEND
			MORE
			KATE
			BUSH

SNL fans will get the in-reference.

Eric
