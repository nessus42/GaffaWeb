Do you have Snapple in the UK?  If not, you can substitute any
ordinary fruit juice for this, and you Yanks can use Snapple.

OK, now take your Snapple (1 bottle) and/or juice (1/2 liter),
and add a cup of sugar.  This is what your average Fruitopia is like.

light" and ice tea flavors are actually pretty good (they've finally
toned down the sweeteners).  I don't think ice tea is legal outside
America, though...
