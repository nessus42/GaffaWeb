No, the Kate Collectibles book was never updated.  The author, who 
participates on the aol Kate board sometimes, said (if I remember 
correctly, pardon me for loosly quoting from memories of a post from a 
year ago) that the sales of the book were steady, but not high enough for 
the publishing company to feel they could spend the $$ on a new edition yet.

It may happen in the future.  I still see this book for sale at San 
Francisco record stores, so I assume they still have not sold out the 
first printing.

Perhaps a letter campaign to the publishing company requesting a 
corrected and updated version would provoke some thought on their part 
about future editions.
