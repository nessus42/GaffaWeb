One man's treasure is another's trash, I guess, but I only semi-liked
this film which is more the story of a girl torn between her dance
career and her love of her boyfriend. The 'red shoes' appear in the
ballet within the film -- the familiar possessive footwear storyline
that Kate uses -- and these segments are quite colorful and visually
appealing. They echo the main storyline, which is rather overacted
and drab. I wouldn't plop down $100 for it...watch your TV guide (esp.
for American Movie Channel, TNT or some other 'old' movie network) and
eventually it will reappear.
