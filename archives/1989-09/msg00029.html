> and that added quality may be worth waiting for. So--the floor is

I would prefer to wait until the demo recordings become more widely
available.  The original obsKuriTies tape was interesting for a glimpse
into the early Kate music, but quite frankly the audio quality is too
poor for me to truly enjoy the music.

If the duplicating service idea gets vetoed, I would volunteer to do VHS
HiFi or other tape duplicating again.  However, I would really like to
be sure that my work is going into a product that other people will be
guaranteed to enjoy.  I would rather go through the work to produce a
relatively high-quality copy of the bootlegs than produce yet another
generation of a microphone copy of a tape of the demos.

In summary, I think that we should wait to see if the collection comes
out in a higher fidelity edition, then try to arrange for a commercial
duplicating service to handle the copies.

				--M
