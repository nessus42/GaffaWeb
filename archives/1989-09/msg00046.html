Laura,

"Another Day" was written by Roy Harper, the English bluesman who has
been putting out great, quirky, and generally unheralded music for the
past thirty years.  Not suprisingly, KT has expressed a lot of admiration
for Harper's work.  The KT interview with the British DJ that Andrew 
Marvick reprinted a couple of months ago (sorry, I didn't save it, so 
I can't provide many details) has Kate playing a couple of Roy Harper 
songs during her slot as "guest DJ."  It's not too surprising that she 
also recorded one of his songs.  

By the way, I just bought "It'll End in Tears" this past weekend - 
largely on your recommendations and comments in rec.music.gaffa and
rec.music.misc.  I'm not at all disappointed.  It's a great album.  
Thanks for the recommendation.
