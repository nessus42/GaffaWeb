        Gentle Giant's Power and The Glory has just been re-released
on CD and cassette (I am not sure about LP - I haven't seen any -
could be one of the first albums to not have an LP release (except for
the original release, of course).

        It is "digitally remastered" on both formats.  The cassette is
recorded with Dolby HX (which continually adjusts the bias to the
correct amount), and at my local store, only costs $3.44 new, as
opposed to $10.99 for the CD (which is still very cheap for a Gentle
Giant CD!).

	So, if you haven't heard this album, you could try the
cassette at very little cost.

	By the way, this release is on Capitol Greenline Series CDs -
which (if my sources are correct) is even less expensive than
Capitol's budget Starline Series CDs.  You might find it as low as
$8.99.
