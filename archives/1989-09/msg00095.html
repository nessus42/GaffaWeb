>Has anyone got any idea how much postage is from the US to here?
>
>If there are a reasonable number of us would IED be willing to do rather
>more for us ( ie buy the stamps! ) since it would be much harder for us
>to work with the US postal service. 

There are things called International Postal Reply Coupons.  You can
take your padded envelope and sample tape (for weighing purposes) down
to your local Post Office and ask them how many IRQ's to buy in order
to send said envelope back from the U.S.  (Just remember to take the
tape out before sending it to IED :-)  I've done the same from the
U.S., it's a fairly straightforward process.
