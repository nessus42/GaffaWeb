Kyle D. Henriksen

From: David I. Dalva <dave@TIS.COM>
Date: Mon, 29 May 89 12:32:39 -0400
Subject: Kyle D. Henriksen

This note is intended to inform those who have not yet heard that Kyle
D. Henriksen was killed in an automobile accident on May 21, 1989 while
returning from preparations for a bicycle race in Newhall, CA.  An avid
bicyclist, he was greatly loved by his family, friends, and
co-workers.

I am attempting to notify his e-mail correspondents that I am aware of,
and am soliciting donations to a fund that is being set up in his
name.  The most proper disbursement of the fund is currently being
determined by his family and friends.  Those who with to make a
donation may mail a check payable to the "Kyle Henriksen Memorial Fund"
to

		Kyle Henriksen Memorial Fund
		c/o David I. Dalva
		Trusted Information Systems, Inc.
		3060 Washington Road
		Glenwood, MD  21738

Thank you,
