Subject: Classic Rock Mail Digest



	It's finally here!!  I have managed to piece it together in my spare
time (how I found any is beyond me).  The details follow.  I don't know what
to expect in the way of content, but I'd like to see a few Yes, Jethro Tull,
Todd Rundgren, Gentle Giant, Van der Graaf Generator, and King Crimson
discussions.  Please send any subscriptions to:

	gibraltar-request@smiley.mitre.org  (DON'T forget the "-request")

List:  gibraltar@smiley.mitre.org
Coordinator:  Michael Lamoureux
Format:  Psuedo-moderated digest (Not unlike Love-Hounds)
Charter:  For the discussion of classic rock/progressive rock/art rock/(any 
    other redundant label for this type of music).  This does not limit 
    discussions to bands which are played on classic rock radio stations.  
    Anything arguably "classic" is welcome.

	I also have a list of 20 or so music related mailing lists in the
above format if anyone wants a copy (part of the Gibraltar welcome message).
