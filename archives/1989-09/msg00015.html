  BOOT CDs du jour:

    
    PRINCE 'The Black Album'
    (Neutral Zone Records, France)
    62 min.	
    AAD
    Sound Quality: Excellent (9/10)	   
    Source: Original Black Album tape/cd, ** Live in Germany Video

    Track Listing:
       Le Grind
       Cindy C	       
       Dead On It
       When 2 R in Love
       Bob George
       Superfunkicalifragisexi 
       2 Nigs United for West Compton
       Rock Hard in a Funky Place
       When Doves Cry ** (not listed on disc)
       Purple Rain **
       1999 **
       Alphabet St. **

    Of the three 'Black Album' cds that I have heard this is by far the 
    best sounding.  It was taken from a very good source - most likely 
    a promo-tape or a cd to tape transfer since the sound is not 'perfect'.
    The other two 'Black Album' cds were taken off records and contain
    *very* annoying 'pops' and 'clicks' - look for the Neutral Zone label
    (it is on the side and back).
    The live tracks are the remaining tracks from the 'White Album' concert.
