Berton, thank you so much for the info and descriptions of Sarah

Amazing enoguh, i caught the end of Vox in a Sears store here in NJ
about a month ago! I was AMAZED! It was very nice. 

god, i'd love to see the rest of her videos.

Joe was in canada this weekedn and got me Solace, and so far 
I really love it.

It makes me cry - maybe it's just appropriate in my life just now? But
i also think it's very good and very emotional. It's certianyl
different as you describe, the smooth flowing ethereal vocals are gone
and everything now seems a little more down-to-earth, straghtforward,
and sometimes simpler/harsher/clearer. if that makes any sense.

in any case, while it's quite different, I like it on it's own an
awful lot already (4 listens so far, 1 of them on headphones).

I read the lyrics through before I even heard the music, and they
alone made me cry a little.

Into the Fire shoudl make a good single, i'd like to see it released
here..
