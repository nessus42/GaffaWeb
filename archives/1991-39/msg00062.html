As I was driving home from the record store with my copy of Two Rooms,
I heard Rocket Man on the radio -- KITS San Francisco.  I should have
known that if any (commercial) station would play that song, it would
be that one.

I like most of it.  I think the reggae-type stuff works well.  Towards
the end I get the feeling Kate couldn't figure out how to end it, though.
