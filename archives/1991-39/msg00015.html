Have been lurking for awhile without posting but I thought I'd just comment
on Kate's cover of "Rocket Man".  Just heard it on the radio a few hours ago
and I must say that it's a great version.  Though hearing those lyrics
sung by Kate instead of Elton is a little disconcerting.

Also wanted to inject that I noticed a Happy Rhodes ad in CD Review.  It was
one of those 1/30th page mini-ads.  The picture is apparently the cover of
the album "Warpaint" with the following text:

Happy Rhodes: Warpaint
For those who are drawn to the progressive sophistication of Peter Gabriel,
Kate Bush, or Laurie Anderson, Warpaint is a must!  The fifth disc from this
gifted vocalist/composer, Rhodes continues to enchant and challenge with
haunting imagery, vocal brilliance, and an emotional honesty second to none.
$13.99 plus $2 P&H (NY residents add 7% sales tax).  Aural Gratification.
PO Box 8658. Academy Station, Albany, NY  12208.

Aural Gratification AGCD0017

I kinda wonder what kind of response do you expect from an 1/30th page ad?
