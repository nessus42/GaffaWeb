Just got my copy of _Two_Rooms_, but it's too early to make a judgement
on "Rocket Man" -- I love the sound, but the beat is disconcerting, as
is hearing KaTe singing the lyrics.

At the same time, I got the soundtrack to _The_Commitments_, and it is
incredible -- if you like the type of music at all, you'll love this
album.  "Try a Little Tenderness" is unbelievable.
