While on the subject of "What to Play for Halloween", several years ago
(Halloween 1985 to be exact), a housemate at the time wanted to throw
a (heh-heh) killer halloween party, and asked yours truly to provide the
music.  I still listen to these tapes I made.  Here's a partial list
of contents:

The Cramps - Googoomuck
Kate Bush - Get Out of My House
The Damned - Grimly Fiendish
Dead Kennedys - Halloween
Depeche Mode - Blasphemous Rumors
Human League - Circus of Death
Jonathan Richman - Party in the Woods
David Bowie - Scary Monsters
The Lords of the New Church - Like a Virgin
Roxy Music - Manifesto
The Clash - Straight to Hell
Bauhaus - Bela Legosi's Dead
Pajama Slave Dancers - Scoutmasters from Hell
The Stranglers - No Mercy
Nico - The End

Sends shivers down yer spine, eh?
The list of music I did that year for a Christmas radio show was almost
identical.
