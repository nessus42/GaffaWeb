	Bruce Cockburn (pronounced Co-burn) starts the first part of his world 
tour on Nov 1, He is known for hits like "Wondering Where The Lions Are" and 
"If I Had A Rocket Launcher" and "If A Tree Falls". His new album is called 
NOTHING BUT A BURNING LIGHT and the single is called "Dream Like Mine". The 
album is produced by T. Bone Burnett and appearing on the album are Jackson 
Browne, Booker T., Sam Phillips and Jim Keltner.

	Here are the East Coast dates for Bruce Cockburn and Band. Opening will
be Sam Phillips.
