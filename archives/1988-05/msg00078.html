> Alf (Yazoo)...  How are Alf's solo albums?  I have both Yazoo
> albums, and think they are excellent, but have never heard Alf solo.

Alf's first album "Alf" is pretty good.  It's worth buying for "Love
Resurrection", "Honey for the Bees" and "Invisible".  However, I don't
think it is as good as her Yazoo albums.

Her second album, "Raindancing", is most definitely avoidable.  "Weak
in the Presence of Beauty" and one other song (whose name I can't
recall) are the only two worth listening to...the rest is filler.
