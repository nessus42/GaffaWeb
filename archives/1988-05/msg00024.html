I don't know if this has been mentioned before, but when I bought the
CD of The Kick Inside, the oustide box had the Tammy Wynette cover
printed on it, but when I opened it up I found that the CD itself had
the original UK cover. Just imagine my surprise and elation (at the
time, I had no idea there were different covers floating around). Talk
about a Kick Inside! ( sorry, couldn't resist ).
