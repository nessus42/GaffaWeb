Colourbox has a very varied sound, if you can't give their music a
chance, expecting something like HOT DOGGY all the time, of course you
will think the album is lame.

The Horses Fucking CD is reggae-ish...  whilst the other CD is
extremely pop.  if you are looking for something with the sound of HOT
DOGGY, then get the OTHER CD.  you will probably like the songs EDIT
THE DRAGON, and JUST GIVE EM' WHISKY.
