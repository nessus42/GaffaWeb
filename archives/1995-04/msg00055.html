It appears that the Sony pictures are in the JPEG format. What you need
on your PC is a viewer program that can display this type of image.
There are several available as shareware at ftp sites on the net. Two
that I have used are 2SHOW and LVIEW. 
(When you download the images you might want to give them a name ending
in .JPG so that you can keep track of the format - other sites use .GIF
format for images.)  PKUNZIP only works on files ending in .ZIP that have
been created with PKZIP.
