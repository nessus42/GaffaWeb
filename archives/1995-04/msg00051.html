On the original Experiment IV KaTe WWW Page, there was a page
listing the history of the (then-nonexistant) Lovehounds shirt.
I was (am, actually) going to put a sub-page on this on my
Lovehounds Shirt page (http://www.chaco.com/~stev0/lhshirt.html),
which caused me to dip into the archives, a sport I rarely do
since it's so scary.

What I found amazed me so much that I had to report it in public.
The day a Lovehounds T-shirt was suggested:  July 19, 1986

The very first person who suggested said shirt:

None other than our very own IED!

Take a bow, Andy!  (So what if your idea took eight years to
become reality...)

Also, another reason I was archive-dipping was to see if I
could find my very first post.  I was aware of Lovehounds from
day one (since I was good friends then with a couple of folks
who knew I was a KateFan(tm) in a Big Way, and they were also
good friends with some guy in Boston named Doug (that's how
he spelled it back then) who had this idea...)
