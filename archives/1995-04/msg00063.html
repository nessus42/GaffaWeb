uli@zoodle.robin.de (Ulrich Grepel), of all people, asks:

> Just watched a Kate video again, and re-found something that did escape
> me with that one. This something inspired a good quiz question:
> 
> - how many Kate Bush videos feature some artificial snow/glitter kind of stuff?

Easy!  All of 'em!  That's her trademark, like Hitchcock's trademark
of a brief non-verbal walk-on, or Russ Meyer's trademark of women
with Very Large Breasts.  Admittedly, you can't SEE the glitter/snow
in a lot of the videos, but the stagehands were throwing it around
backstage, and "It really looked marvelous, even though you couldn't
see it on camera.  It leant such a festive mood." (From an interview
in Trouser Press last year)
