Regarding the method jer thought of a while back for removing the vocals
centered in the stereo image:

> [jer:] Has anybody ever tried this?  Will it work?

Yes, it does sort of work.  There are are even boxes you can buy that do
just this.  But you needn't bother for "Suspended In Gaffa".  I'll tell
you what the whispers say.  There are three, one for each chorus.  In
order, they are

	(1) I caught a glimpse of a god, all shining and bright.

	(2) We all have a dream, maybe.

	(3) Mother, where are the angels?  I'm scared of the changes.
