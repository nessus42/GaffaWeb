Unfortunately, the chorus from "Hello Earth" was not original with
Hounds of Love.  It was used in the 1979 Werner Herzog film "Nosferatu,
the Vampyre".  I'm not sure who originally wrote it, the music in
Nosferatu was credited to Wagner, Gounod, and Popul Vuh.
