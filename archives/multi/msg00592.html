Here is a Robyn Hitchcock discography:

The Soft Boys
-------------

Give It To The Soft Boys (EP, 1977)

(I Want To Be An) Anglepoise Lamp/ Fatman's Son (1978)

Near The Soft Boys (EP, 1980)

I Wanna Destroy You/ I'm An Old Pervert (12 inch? 1980)

Only The Stones Remain/ The Asking Tree (1981)

Love Poisoning/ When I Was A Kid (1982)

He's A Reptile/ Song No. 4 (1983)

Wading Through A Ventilator (12 inch EP, 1984)

A Can Of Bees (LP, 1980) -- There are three different versions of this
	album.  Each has a slightly different collection of songs.

Underwater Moonlight (LP, 1980)

Two Halves For The Price Of One (LP, 1981)

Invisible Hits (LP, 1983)

Live At The Portland Arms (Cassette only, 1983)

Robyn Hitchcock
---------------

The Man Who Invented Himself/ Dancing On God's Thumb (1981) -- Comes
	with free flexidisk.

America/ It was The Night/ How Do You Work This Thing (1982)

52 Stations (promo flexi EP with other artists, 1982)

Nightride To Trinidad/ Kingdom Of Love/ Midnight Fish (12 inch EP, 1982)

Eaten By Her Own Dinner/ Listening To The Higsons/ Dr. Sticky (1982)

The Bells Of Rhymney/ Falling Leaves/ Winter Love/ The Bones In The
	Ground (12 inch EP, 1984)

Heaven/ Dwarfbeat/ Some Body (12 inch EP, 1985)

Heaven (One sided promo 7 inch, 1985)

Black Snake Diamond Role (LP, 1981)

Groovy Decay (LP, 1982)

I Often Dream Of Trains (LP, 1984)

Fegmania (LP, 1985)

Gotta Get This Hen Out (Live LP, 1985)

Let There Be More Darkness (track on "Don't Let The Hope Close Down
	compilation LP, 1984)
