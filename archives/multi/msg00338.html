I now have the official lyrics to "Under The Ivy".  I got them almost
all right.  Unfortunately, I got one very critical word wrong.

	UNDER THE IVY

	It wouldn't take me long
	To tell you how to find it
	To tell you where we'll meet.
	This little girl inside me
	Is retreating to her favourite place.
	Go into the garden
	Go under the ivy
	Under the leaves
	Away from the party.
	Go right to the rose
	Go right to the white rose
	    (for me)
	I sit here in the thunder
	The green on the grey
	I feel it all around me
	And it's not easy for me
	To give away a secret -- it's not safe
	But go into the garden
	  go under the ivy
	  Under the leaves
	  Away from the party
	  Go right to the rose
	  Go right to the white rose
	      (for me)
	It wouldn't take me long to tell you how to find it.
