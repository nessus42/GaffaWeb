This is from net.music (finally I will be able to get one of my very
very favorite Kate Bush songs ("Breathing") on CD! (All of my other very
very favorite songs are on "The Dreaming")):

From: jcp@osiris.UUCP (Jody Patilla)
Newsgroups: net.music
Subject: Greenpeace album/CD
Posted: Fri Aug 30 23:26:33 1985

	A&M is releasing an album for Greenpeace, all the royalties
for which will go to the Greenpeace organisation. The album, called
"Greenpeace", is a compilation of songs donated by various artists.
One track,  "Push and Shove", was written especially for the project;
the other tracks have been released before.
	The tracks are as follows, with artists listed in alpha order:

Kate Bush: "Breathing"
Depeche Mode: "Blasphemous Rumours"
Thomas Dolby: "Windpower"
Eurythmics: "No Fear, No Hate, No Pain, No Broken Hearts"
Peter Gabriel: "Shock the Monkey"
George Harrison: "Save the World"
Heaven 17: "Let's All Make a Bomb"
Howard Jones: "Equality"
Kajagoogoo: "Turn Your Back on Me"
Nik Kershaw: "Human Racing"
Madness: "Wings of a Dove"
Hazel O'Connor and Chris Thompson: "Push and Shove"
The Pretenders: "Show Me"
Queen: "Is This the World We Created ?"
Roger Taylor: "Killing Time"
Tears for Fears: "Mad World"
