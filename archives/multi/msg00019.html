Here's part of a letter I just received from Jim Hofmann that KB fans
might find interesting:

From: Jim Hofmann <hofmann@AMSAA.ARPA>
Subject:  MORE KATE ON WHFS!!!!

I can't believe all the Kate I am hearing [on WHFS, a progressive
commercial radio station in MD -- Doug] .... I must rush out and buy
"Lionheart" and the single - "Sat in Your Lap" - with that Donavan song
I mentioned yesterday [they played "Lord of The Reedy River", the B-side
to "Sat In Your Lap" -- Doug].  Listening to Kate is so-o-o relaxing but
still stimulating (i.e. NOT DOCTOR OFFICE) but still relaxing but still
stimulating but ... (you know) Hardcore is for the Weekends.  Kate is
for the Weeknights and morning and afternoons.  Oooh, I swoon. "Get out
of my House" is my fave from THE DREAMING ... with "Sat in ..." second
and suspended in GAFFA 3rd... the rest are good, too but those are my
favorites.  Does it sound like I'm rambling it cause I am.  Now if only
HFS would play more BEET FARMERS and OFFENDERS and more obscure stuff, I
wouldn't twiddle with the dial so much.

I hear you are moderating a kate bush (et al.) group.  I like
to be on it only if there will be info on other music, too, like
you said.  However, I'm afraid I won't be able to contribute
much since I am commited to writing for music-flamers.  

					Auf Weidersehn

						Jim, the Mu
