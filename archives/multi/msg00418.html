"Urgh, A Music War" will be on the USA network tonight at 1 a.m. and
Saturday at 5 a.m. and 11 p.m.  This is a compilation of concert music
videos from the likes of Klaus Nomi, Gary Numan, Echo and the Bunnymen,
the Fleshtones, the Cramps, etc.  There's a lot of drek, but a few
shining moments (also a good video from Gang of Four).  It was released
in 1980 (a great year for music).
