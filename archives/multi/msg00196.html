Today in the Boston Globe (affectionately known as The Glob)
there was a music review of the Cocteau Twins, who were at
the Paradise on Monday night.  In reviewing their concert,
Jim Sullivan said the following of the Cocteau Twins' lead
singer, Elizabeth Fraser:

       "She reminded one of Kate Bush: breathy one
	moment, tortured the next, ecstatic another..."

Like I said, you just can't get away from it now!
