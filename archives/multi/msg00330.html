> OK.  I give up.  Just *how* does one listen to albums (or CD's...)
> backwards?  (I need to know about all these evil things that I am
> being programed with!  :-) )

Well, you don't play your CDs backwards unless you transfer them first
to reel to reel tape.  You listen to albums backwards by using a spare
copy of the album, an old stylus, and preferably an old cartridge (I'm
too lazy for the last one and I only have one cartridge, and I managed
to not mangle my copy of "The Dreaming" too badly, either.)  You then
unplug the record player and turn the platter backwards by hand.

Have fun!
