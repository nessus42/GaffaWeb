Also, I think a "noise" Kate makes with her voice that might
send demonic chills up spineless people's backs (and chills of
pleasure up ours, right?) is a noise that Doug says sounds like
she's "gargling with razor blades".  For instance: GET OUTTA MY
...HOUSE...  or  I LOVE LIFE, I LOVE LIFE, I LOVE LIIIIIIIIFE...

You've just GOT to admit that the first time you heard these, you
said, "oh GEEEEZZZ, what was THAT?"  Debby Boone she's not.
