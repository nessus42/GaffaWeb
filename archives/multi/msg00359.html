I just got some back issues of the KBC newsletter (unfortunately, they
are out of many of them), and here's some interesting trivia from the
first couple (from 1979):

Kate had not read "Wuthering Heights" at the time she wrote the song.

"Wow" was Kate's try at a Pink Floyd song.  "Kite" is a Bob Marley song.
"Don't Push Your Foot On The Heartbrake" is a Patti Smith song.

			"compacted awareness .. gems flattening ..
			 long streams of resin tools"

			 Doug


P.S.  On Night Flight, they cut out "Wow" from "Kate Bush Live" to make
room for more commercials.  Oh well.  It's the most expendible song
anyway....
