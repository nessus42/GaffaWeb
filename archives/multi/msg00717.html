There is a *wonderful* interview with Kate in the 21 Nov 85 issue of Hot
Press (Kate is the cover story).  Unfortunately, it is an Irish music
paper, so it may be a bit difficult to find in the U.S.  I picked up
some copies while England.  Apparently a lot of KB fans didn't like the
interview (there's some hate mail in the 5 Dec 85 issue) because the
interviewer asks some unusual and awkward questions that force Kate to
reveal some of her weaknesses.  But I thought that was great, because
the interviewer is clearly a fan and because it shows a lot about about
how she thinks, and indicates that to a large extent she manipulates
images on a more unconscious level than one might think.

I also got a bunch of really nice photos of Kate that I took at the
Tower Records in-store.  Too bad I can't include them in this message.
