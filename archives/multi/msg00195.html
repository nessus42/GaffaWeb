I think it's sort of in the middle of her two poles.  Her early stuff
is all love ballads, and although they are diverse, the style is much
the same.  Then there's The Dreaming, which is nothing like anything.
RUTH strikes me as being a neat combination of both of those artistic
types...no one can deny RUTH is a powerful love song, but the intricate
rhythms remind me of stuff on The Dreaming.  Anybody else feel the
same way?  I can't wait to get Hounds of Love!!!
