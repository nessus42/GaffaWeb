Question about SHRIEKBACK show, and two recommendations

  • Subject: Question about SHRIEKBACK show, and two recommendations
  • From: hall%beta.DEC@decwrl.ARPA (Dan Hall)
  • Date: Wednesday, 9 Oct 1985 07:44:12-PDT

I saw SHRIEKBACK, ADVENTURE SET and BLUE HOLLOW Saturday night at
the Channel in Boston.  SHRIEKBACK were dynamite!  It's been too
long since I've seen such a high energy band.  From the first note
they played to the last, it was go! go! go!  They played most of
the new album _Oil and Gold_, and a few tunes from _Care_ but I
don't remember hearing anything from _Jam Science_.  If anyone else
saw them there or elsewhere, can you tell me who the two guitarists
were, who was playing keyboards opposite Barry Andrews, and what are
the names of the two backing vocalists?  

BTW, this was also the first time I saw ADVENTURE SET live.  I was
very impressed.  I didn't realise until then that I have heard two
of their tunes, "Great White Way" and "Blue is for Boys" many many
times on WBCN and WFNX.  Highly recommended!

My recommendation on _BLUE HOLLOW_ is to avoid them.

Dan
