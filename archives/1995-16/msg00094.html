BMG Music Service (yea, yea. I know...) puts three "10 Spot" lists
on the back of each catalog, where they promote albums having some
common connection, such as "recorded live", soundtracks, "contains
a reference to Bob Dylan" (I don't think they've actually done
that one, yet!), etc.

In their latest, under the guise of "Duets", they list PG's _So_,
emphasizing "Don't Give Up (w/ Kate Bush)".

Also, in the special focus insert on "the women in music", they
only have _TWS_ by Her Highness.  A question of licensing, I guess.
