Wow (to borrow a phrase), that's just how it was with me, except I came
in on the show late and missed the first song.  I was 15 at the time. 
Years later I came across "The Whole Story" in a record store and
thought, geez this woman looks familiar.  It wasn't until after I had
backtracked and bought The Kick Inside that I realized that haunting
woman on SNL many years ago was indeed Kate Bush.
