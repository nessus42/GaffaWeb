Hi, all...This is a request for somewhat unusual information, I guess, but:

performance of all of Kate's THE NINTH WAVE, being the second part of the 
HOUNDS OF LOVE album.  I'm a double-major in Theater Studies and Music

not even an admission charge).
So...The question is...Whom should I get in touch with in order to
