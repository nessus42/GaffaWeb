On Sun, 6 Jul 1997, Ariusz Nowak wrote:

> I just bought the kind of curiosity I think. 
> It's an russian bootleg (or maybe it's not a bootleg?) that contains 
> TKI and LH on one CD.
> It would be nothing special, but the albums are recorded in the 
> wrong order (LH first) and the last song (The Kick Inside) is
> not included (due to lack of time I think).

I wondered if that thing existed.. there was a show on CNN about illegal
mass CD burning in Bulgaria, where there are no laws against burning
CDs and selling them cheap without paying the copyright holders... anyway,
the camera swept over a shelf of such CDs, and my eyes immediatly caught
a picture of Kate Bush on a CD cover, only the title was "Lionheart & The
Kick Inside". I thought I must be delirious or something.. guess I wasn't.
