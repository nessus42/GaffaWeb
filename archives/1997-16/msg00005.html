Hoi,

First of all I'm 27 (almost 28, 4th of July) and my first contact with
Kate is ofcourse Wuthering Heights. I heard it on the radio but the
title was to complicated to remember (I was 8 at the time).

Later on  I saw a clip on Toppop of a woman dancin' and singin' in a
sort of hut. I immediately liked her and the song. But unfortunately
Toppop showed a very short segment of the clip (it was on the
Tipparade and not on the actually Hitparade). I missed the credits so
I watched Toppop every week since, but the clip never showed up again
(it never reached the hitparade in Holland I think).

Later on a very good friend of mine introduced me with Kate with the
Hounds of Love and I was sold. I bought everything of her. I
recognized the song Suspended in Gaffa. And when I bought the Single
File video everything fell into its place.

Most favourite song at the moment is (it depends in which mood I am):
This Woman's Work because I'm going to be a godfather within 7 days.
HOORAY.
