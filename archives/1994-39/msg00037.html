   Well, Kate already made a wonderful video for _Hounds of Love_ and
I'm not a big enough fool to make a video to directly compete with Kate.
But I have considered using _Curse of The Demon_, _The Haunting_ and
_The Shining_ to make a video to _Get Out of My House_. I now have the
morphing, warping and image manipulation software to do the human/mule
transformation. Now I just have to write Kate and Paddy asking for
footage of them making "Hee-Haw" noises.

   Actually the most likely future video is one made from the film
_Hanussen_ for _Heads Were Dancing_. I want to do this to prove my
friend Kim's theory. I just found this film on Laserdisc, so I should be
able to make a really good quality video.

   Has anyone else made videos for some of Kate's songs? Shortly after
the Konvention someone (I forget who, sorry) posted that he'd made a
pair of videos for the competition. It would be great to include them
on the next tape-tree.
