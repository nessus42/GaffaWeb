I'd like to take this opportunity to extend to the Love-Hounds an apology 
for a recent display of my sometimes petty nature.  To make the story 
short, on Monday, November 28, while reading in soc.motss, I happened on 
a posting regarding the WIRED ad for \ X U.  From what I could 
understand, the poster assumed the film to be "positively unwatchable."  
Immediately, I raised my hackles and replied with a bitchy statement 
regarding the poster's assumptions; furthermore, to make matters worse, I 
made reference to the poster's other postings in the group.  Later, I 
received a colorful reply from the poster, which I forwarded to the 
Love-Hounds.  I'm apologizing for cluttering rec.music.gaffa with such 
nonsense.  Please accept my apology and be assured that I will not use 
this forum for playing "tit-for-tat" again.  Thanks.

Keith
