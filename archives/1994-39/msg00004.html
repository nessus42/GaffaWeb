> What was the first song of hers that you heard, that caused you to get
> interested in her music?

One day I heard Running Up that Hill on the radio but didn't hear who 
this song was from, (I didn't have a clue at that time who Kate Bush was.)
I kept hoping that I'd hear it again sometime and catch the artist's name.

Well... my local Alternative radio station had a show on at that time 
(still does) that featured aspects and artists of Alternative and
related music, and one day I found that they were featuring an artist
named Kate Bush. I listened to her music that day and automatically fell
in love with it.
