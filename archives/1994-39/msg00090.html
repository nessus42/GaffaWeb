IED's pre-cursor (Andrew Marvick) was just 20
in December 1977 when he first heard "Wuthering Heights"
over Radio Luxembourg while living in Florence during
his junior year abroad. The reception was terrible, the
radio a tinny portable mono-speaker job, but it was
enough to completely screw up his whole life for good.

The performance with Pete Townsend on guitar was indeed
"The Wedding List" from the Prince's Trust Gala Concert (1982).
Other musicians in the band were Phil Collins, Mick Karn,
Gary Brooker and Midge Ure.

-- Andrew Marvick (IED)
