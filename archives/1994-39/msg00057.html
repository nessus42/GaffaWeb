I just saw a message I sent about three weeks ago turn up in the digest 
yesterday.  Very Strange.

Just got the video of TMIL thanks to Lagan Love on aol.  Not terribly 
exciting.  They have Kate sort of meandering around behind some people 
playing instruments.  She has her arms folded and is wearing a sort of 
leather smoking jacket.  The few close ups of her face have a glaring 
spotlight in them that makes it hard to see her.

Lagan Love also taped the U.S. ETM and RBG videos (so now I have really 
good copies) and interestingly enough, the ETM video is a shade different 
from the MTV broadcast version.  It begins with Lindsey Kemp yelling into 
the snow "She's trying to keep me from helping you!  Sing for your 
smile!" in what looks like an outtake from the movie.  Then the video 
begins.  It ends with a long shot of the shoes which I don't remember 
from MTV.  

The promo video has that loop sequence of ETM, Lindsey Kemp and RBG, but 
I can't remember if Kemp is clearly saying anything in that version of 
the tape, which was the giveaway at the TRS release drawings everywhere 
but in S.F.  Grrr.  Can anyone clarify whether there are actually three 
versions of ETM with slightly different beginnings?  1) MTV, 2) Promo 
Video Tape, 3) Lagan love's VeeJay copy.
