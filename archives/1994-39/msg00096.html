images
>>of Kate and Kate-related stuff?  My PC needs a facelift!  Thanks for your

If you can get on the WWW, there's a page with WAV files (mostly quite 
large though, 2MB minimum) called "Kate Bush Musical Extravaganza", address 
is http://actor.cs.vt.edu/~wentz/index.html. If you're using something like 
Netscape or Winweb, you'll be able to search for "Kate Bush" and get there. 
There's also "Experiment IV" page, which contains info on stuff like the 
KBC/Homeground Convention on 8th May this year, Kate FAQ (frequently asked 
questions), discography, details on the Cloudbusting project, plus the 
Gaffa T-Shirt project. Then there's "Lionheart", but I've never got through 
to it.

Back to your original question, trying FTPing cs.uwp.edu. There are 
pictures for practically anyone you can think of, including lots of Kate. 
I've grabbed a few myself recently, and they're not half bad.
