Here's the news from Billboard, Dec. 11.

First, the bad news:

The album "The Red Shoes" is down to no. 117 on the album chart.

Next, the reasonable news:

"Rubberband Girl" stays at no. 7 for a second week on the Modern Rock
Tracks chart.

Finally, the good news:

MTV has added R.G. to its regular rotation.  What level of rotation
won't be listed until next week.
