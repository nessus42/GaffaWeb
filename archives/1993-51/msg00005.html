   Every contact that *I've* had, and several contacts that friends have
had, have been uniformly negative. He put enormous pressure on Dale
Sommerville to give him a copy of the Breakthrough mailing list, he
pressured John Reimers to give him a copy of the unedited "Night Flight"
interview, calling him names on the front page of his mailing list.

started chatting, that Kate Bush fans were suckers who would pay
practically any price for stuff. I have never had a single positive
contact with him.

>He clearly likes Kate Bush's music, but you are too prejudiced to see

>visiting England and was kind enough to give me advice on where to

>Kate Bush collectibles and showed me which stores to go to.  He did
>this at his loss because with me nabbing many of the Kate Bush
>collectibles there were fewer for him.

   Your positive impression of him is due to your experience with him,
colored by the slack that one is likely to give someone that you have
met in person. I have never met AB or spoken to him on the phone.
I base my impression of him on his actions. If he is the Kate fan
you make him out to be, why has he never posted a single non-commercial
word to gaffa (other than one response to a flame)? Why hasn't he shared
this amazing storehouse of knowledge that you insist he has, rather
than simply using love-hounds and rec.music.gaffa as a postage-free
mailing list in obnoxious violation of nettiqite?

>>       This long-time fan has never seen any proof that this is not so.
