On Wed, 8 Dec 1993, Mary C Barnes wrote:

> Has anyone on this list listened to a Norwegian band called Bel Canto?  I just
> heard a '92 tape called "Shimmering, Warm and Bright" which sounds like its
> name, sort of an electro-folksy Cocteau sound with a lead singer who reminds
> me of Sarah McLachlan or Liz Fraser..a very pretty voice she's got.  Are there
> more albums and if so, which is the best?   MaryB
> 
Mary,
     I love Bel Canto!  Their first album is called _White-out 
conditions_ and is somewhat difficult to find.  (At least it was
for me.)  Their second album is called _Birds of passage_ and is,
in my opinion, their best.  If you liked _Shimmering..._ then
find _Birds..._  For their first two albums they were a trio.
_Shimmering warm & bright_ is their 3rd and most recent album,
and they have pared down to a duo.  I don't know about any
plans for a new album.  Enjoy!

Michael
