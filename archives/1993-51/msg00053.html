I would suggest a trip to Seymour Street downtown between Pender and
Dunsmuir.  A&B Sound has their main store here and a large "Sam the Record
Man" is in the same block.  Prices downtown are generally good.  You might
also try Track Records or Odyssey Imports also in the same block!

Best of Luck,
