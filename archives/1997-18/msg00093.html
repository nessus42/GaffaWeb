TAFKAP took a part of that song, made loop and filled the other 47 tracks
of his recorder-monster with several instruments and voices.

had to look for what they can actually use.

between Kate and Prince -- different from the work with Peter Gabriel
or Nigel Kennedy or the numerous other artists, who visited the
studio.
