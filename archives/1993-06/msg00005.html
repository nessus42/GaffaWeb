Which is actually relatively reasonable.  I don't know what things are like
in your area, but here in DC I usually pay between $18 and $23 for import
CDs.  TWW with 8 CDs would run ~$160 or more.  Never mind the fact that two
of the discs are unavailable elsewhere (and _definitely_ worth having for
the person who hasn't been collecting 7" and 12" singles from the start), 
everything's wrapped up in a wonderful box, there's a great (if tiny) book
of photos, *AND* you get stickers...

Admittedly, I can't complain because I got my box almost 2 year ago for
~$130...
