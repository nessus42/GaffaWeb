MTV-Europe uses Kate in a trailer!!!

My experiences of Thursday, January 7, 1993:

I didn't believe my eyes when I watched MTV: They have a little trailer
called 'The four seasons of MTV'. They play Vivaldi in extremely low fidelity
and show several video clips in a black-and-white fashion with disturbances
like in a film of the twenties. And - one of those clips in the 'Spring'
season, only visible for about one second - is Kate Bush wearing an umbrella,
fresh out of The Big Sky. Finally, after that Paul King said what to expect
in the late night edition of MTV Greatest Hits. About them was Kate again,
I looked into MTV Text and found: Cloud Busting (their spelling). Today they
play several videos where some other stars are featuring, and so they thought
of Donald Sutherland. AT LEAST THEY HAVE NOT FORGOTTEN HER!

Bye for now,

Uli

P.S.: They showed the 4 seasons clip again, so watch out!
