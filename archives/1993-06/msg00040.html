> Re the discussion on how or how not to introduce people to Kate Bush:

> So if the person you are trying to convert likes "modern music"/alternative

    Might be a good solution, but I only know a few people who like loud
    music.

    Personnaly, I've started to like Kate's songs when I first heard her on
    TV6 (french musical television, now dead), showing a live concert.

    The first album I bought was : 'The Whole Story'. I was knowing many of
    the songs in this compilation, and I started to buy the 1st real album,
    and latest at this date : 'Hounds of Love'. It was the Initiation.

    Everyone know that after Initiation comes Faith ;^) I've converted a
    friend (just a little bit, sadly :-( ) by doing a small animation on
    my computer and playing 'Wuthering Heights', really magic !
