Larry@apple writes:

>In addition, I wonder if anyone else thought that the opening theme
>song sounded *very* much like a KaTe KomposiTion?

I assume you're talking about "The Closing of the Year" (as opposed to
the opening song of the movie/soundtrack which is actually excerpted from
Tchaikovsky).  Just the other day I was playing the album for a friend,
and as soon as "The Closing of the Year" started, he looked over and 
asked, "Kate?"  He's a non-rabid fan with maye 4 of her albums.


On a vaguely related note, I heard Bel Canto's "Unicorn" on the radio
this morning and was dumbstruck trying to figure out who it might be.  It
sounded (to my bleary morning ears) a _lot_ like KaTe though I was quite
certain it wasn't.  It was very odd that I didn't recognize it, though,
as I have (and massively overplay) Bel Canto's first two albums, and I've
heard "Unicorn" before.
