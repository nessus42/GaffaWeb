All of the red copies of Hole that I've seen include an EP in the same
sleeve as the LP.  The extra tracks (on those copies I know of) aren't

domestic LP without the EP included.  By the way, I read a recent 
interview with where he said he's completed his contractual agreement
(five LP's) with SB and is not going to work with them anymore, since
he dislikes most everyone there.
