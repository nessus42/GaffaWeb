Hello to all fans of Kate!

As noted here in Love Hounds, MTV also played "Love And Anger" 11/6
on the Now Hear This! show. (6:30 and 11:00 pm CST).  This time, I was
prepared for and taped both (in NTSC stereo, Beta 1S Super Beta HiFi).

WARNING TO ANYONE WHO TAPED THE "WORLD PREMIERE"!!!
MTV chopped the end of the video!!!  I was intensively mad, and the only
thing that kept me from calling the cable company and having them remove the
entire cable hook-up was the 11:00 pm rebroadcast.  This was thankfully NOT
cut (or MTV AND my cable company would have gotten SERIOUS abuse!).

This will probably be the video played on 120 minutes next Sunday.  (11:00
pm - 1:00 am CST Sunday Night).  They have made no mention at all of any
other videos from The Sensual World.

Has anyone else noticed that one (and only one!) of the short band clips,
the snare drum is hit on the ONE and THREE (not TWO and FOUR!) beats AND IS
NOT PROPERLY SYNCED WITH THE AUDIO!!!  (Which is on TWO and FOUR!).
In all other shots, the snare sounds when it is hit.  This is unusual for a
Kate video!!!
