If you're reading this then I am sure you have the following items:  :-)
	1. Kate Bush _The_Sensual_World_ LP cover
	2. Laurie Anderson _Strange_Angels_ CD cardboard package

Place the Laurie Anderson (Mapplethorpe) cover over Kate's face on the LP.
(Note to Kate purists: I *know* it's wrong to cover Kate's image, but you
don't have to leave it covered. At least I'm not *burning* anything :-)  )
Line up the faces to form one composite - the eyes make a good guide.
Squint.
Now you will see one or more of:
	a: Kate Anderson
	b: Laurie Bush
	c: Kate with a big nose ?
Hey, I didn't promise anything.
