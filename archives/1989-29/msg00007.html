An Internet mailing list called SIBERRY (Jane Siberry fans) is now
operating.

about Jane Siberry and her music.  Reviews, rumors, concert info, you
name it.  Membership is open to everyone.  The list is set for automatic
distribution, so anything you submit will be automatically rebroadcast
to everyone.
