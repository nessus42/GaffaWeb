The Nov. 11 _Cash Box_ shows the album climbing to #49  -- 
exactly the same as _Billboard_.  It also shows it climbing to
#7 in the Alternative Music albums chart, though they STILL call
the album _Pop Singer_ there!!!!!

I haven't seen a review of the album in this magazine.  I might have 
missed it.  But they did have a review of Laurie Anderson's new 
album, and one line struck me.  The reviewer said that on this album she
sounded less like a second grade teacher, "and more like, say, Kate Bush."
