Okay, folks:
Pretend for a minute that Kate has decided to record an album of songs
written entirely by other people.

Which songs would you like to hear her sing?

My list:

Jumpin' Jack Flash (Stones)
I'm Down (Beatles)
A Foggy Day in London Town (Who wrote this?)
Tropical Hot Dog Night (Capt. Beefheart)
Strictly Genteel (Frank Zappa)
Stormy Weather
White Christmas
The Lady Don't Mind (Talking Heads)
Tightrope (Leon Russell)
My Pink Half of the Drainpipe (Bonzo Dog Band)

Now, there's an album.

song you'd like to hear them sing.
