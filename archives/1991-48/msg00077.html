The set on Wogan was much the same as the band scenes in the video
except that Davey and his pipes were nowhere to be seen - I would've
thought Paddy could've stepped into the breach if Davey wasn't
available. The photographic direction was poor and KaTe didn't seem as
relaxed as I've seen her before. Kate wiggled and strummed her way
through the choruses with her ukelele(?), but a bit slower than in the
video.
