I'm in Dallas and my discs are in Houston,

two albums.  I think they are "Two Nice Girls," and
"Claire loved Olivia."  These guys are great!
"For the Inauguration" is hilarious...

My brothers went to high school with Gretchen at
HSPVA in Houston.  David saw her at their 10 year
reunion last summer and related the story of how
they had won a healthy block of pro studio time
in a "Sweet Jane" contest in Austin.  This version
of "Sweet Jane" is the one that is on the first
album, and this how they were able to cut some great
demos and subsequently get a contract...

I defy any human being to listen to a Gretchen Philips
song and not break out in an ear-to-ear grin for at
least a moment or two.

Anyway, Laurie Freelove was on the first album, but there
was a new name on the second...  I think they are actually
the same person, but am not sure.

A hearty endorsement from here!
