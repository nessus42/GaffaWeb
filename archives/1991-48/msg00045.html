I sure do!  I was fortunate enough to catch her on both passes she made through
Portland, OR (late 1988, early '89).  The first one was particularly memorable
because, after witnessing her captivating performance, I got to meet her!  We 
just noticed a group of people waiting near the side door by the stage and 
figured, why not?  Once they let us backstage I managed to (haltingly) tell her how great I thought her music and performance were and politely ask her for an 
autograph.  A really charming, talented lady.
