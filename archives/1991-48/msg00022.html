Finally shelled out the clams for the new single ($12.95 in Raleigh,
NC...Ouch!), and lo and behold, I like it.

I mean, I really like Rocket Man.  Yep, the guy who doesn't like 
The Ninth Wave or The Sensual World got bowled over by an
Elton John cover.  I thought the arrangement was nice and
sparse, and the performance was very good.

Candle in the Wind didn't do anything for me though... I don't hear
the passion in Kate's version that comes through in the original.

Ya win some and ya lose some.
