>Is this the same Laurie Freelove that is/was (?) a member of
>Two Nice Girls?  

yup - was. i've got to agree with vickie on this one: _smells like truth_
is an excellent release: a mysterious and sonic album that is deep and 
ensnaring.

i saw her perform at jc dobbs a few weeks ago and it was an excellent show.
the music was even further stripped down than the album: laurie on acoustic
guitar with three others playing electric guitar, percussion and a lyricon.
very textured music and precise performance.

on a related note, i was not terribly impressed with the new one from 
single gun theory. a few songs struck me as defintely worthwhile, but alot
of it seemed pretty fluffy, even for dance music. hmmm.

i also found the second release from devils weirlding scimitars, _worry
dolls_. this band is something in the soft guitar rock stuff ala 10,000
maniacs and the children. catchy and decent.
