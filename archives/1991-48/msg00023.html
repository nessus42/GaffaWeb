Stephen "Jokey" Golden (golden@eniac.seas.upenn.EDU) spouted:
>Anyone else have Katedreams to share?

Yeah!  Years ago when all I knew of KaTe was the HoL CD, I had a dream one
night where  I met Kate at a bus stop.  She was dressed just like the back
cover (you know, the TNW cover) but she was dry.  I recognized her and asked
her if she wanted to come to a party.  She said sure (in the voice from
"Mother Stands for Comfort"...I'd never heard her speak then).  When we got
to the party I introduced her as "Kate <some other non-Bush name>" to everybody
for no reason I can remember.  My friend Marty then said "Oh, your voice sounds
like you sing.  Do you?"  Then she got all bashful and said something like
"Well, a little...not really..." but I said "Oh, come on, you do too!  Sing!"
so she started singing "RUTH".  Pretty nifty dream.
				-Bill (wms@iwarp.intel.com)
