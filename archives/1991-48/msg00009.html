Did anyone else wonder about the significance of Terry Wogan's final
words before introducing the guest following KaTe?

He roughly said (paraphrased from memory!):
"I know someone who'll be particularly pleased to see KaTe and that's Hanna.
She always watches the show, particularly when KaTe is on. Hello Hanna
and get well soon!"

Who else could it be but KaTe's mum? Let's hope all is well at East Wickham 
Farm.
