Subject: In KaTeness



Well I wasn't going to write how i discovered KaTe but what the heck!
I first heard of her about 4-5 years ago after reading Wuthering Heights.  
Someone mentioned that an English musician had done a song WH.  Of course
at the time I payed no attention to this!  Then a couple years later I
finally heard WH....wow i thought this is weird...but I loved it!  The
next song I heard was There Goes A Tenner, again I though how strange, but
I loved her story telling....I was in a trance....I found myself haunting
the music stores....I managed to snatch up all the albums in less than 2
weeks.  At one point I had KaTe in my car and in both stereos in the house.
Needless to say I am banned from listening to her in the house except when
my mother isnt around....hmmm????  Thanks to several friends I now have
a fairly extensive collection of b-sides etc. I have also successfully
converted several people into being KaTe fanaTics.  Anyway enuf driveling!!!
My Personal Favorites....
Favi song: There Goes A Tenner (sentimental)  Jig of Life (otherwise)
Favi album: don't have one LOVE them all!!!
Favi B-side: Ne tenfuis Pas (blew two speakers listening to this one)
Favi video: KaTe doing Oh England in Live at Hammersmith (the look on her face
is just incredible, and the emotion.....almost wanna cry)

Harg
