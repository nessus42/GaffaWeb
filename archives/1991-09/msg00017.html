I'm not sure about "The Whole Story", but I've bought bunches of stuff in 
Korea only to find that stuff has been left out (which has left me somewhat 
angry)
Specifically, "Hey Joe" was left out of my copy of Jimi Hendrix's "Live at 
Winterland", two tracks were left off my copy of "Love" by the Cult, "This 
is the Picture" was left off "So" by PG...
Some of the stuff was transferred pretty well (like "Live at Winterland") 
while other stuff was poorly transferred (like my Korean copy of "Tubular 
Bells")

So, my advice would be to stay away from the Korean stuff.
