Kirstin writes:

>Needless to say I am banned from listening to her in the house except when
>my mother isnt around....hmmm????

Would you believe that for a period of 2 months, my father owned more
KaTe CDs than I did??

I had him listen to NFE not long after I bought it; he loved it, and went
out and bought his own copy.  Later that year, I bought him HoL for his
birthday, though I still hadn't bought myself anything beside NFE (I had
HoL and TKI on semi-permanent loan from a friend...).  It was another two
months before I bought my second CD, _Lionheart_.

Of course, Dad never got around to buying anything other then the two he had.
He was rather pleased when I bought the Box Set and gave him my copies of
TKI, LH, and TSW.

The family that listens to KaTe together stays together....
