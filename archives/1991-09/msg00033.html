I got one of The TRio Bulgarka's albums- The Forest is crying-  and it
it really cool!  It's in the vein of gypsy music, so most people won't
like it-  then again, most people don't like KaTe (%^(...  It's made
for rainy afternoons and as mood music late at night.  I can't under-
stand what they're saying, so I make my own stories to go along with
each song-  There is one song that really moves me- the way these three
women meld their voices knocks me on my keister every time!  
   If you go to a record store that has a decent import/collectable
section, you probably can find this one without much trouble.  Oh, the
album is on Hannibal records, and if anyone wants their address just
E-mail me and I'll send it to ya!
