Depends on what you consider "REALLY new".  Michael Cretu and Sandra and
Frank Peterson have all worked together before but not as Enigma; I think
the only project where they were *all* present before was Sandra's album
PAINTINGS IN YELLOW from last year but Cretu and Sandra are married and
Cretu's produced every album of hers that I've seen.  He also produces
Hubert KaH and has a few solo albums of his own out; I haven't heard any
of his solo work but the Sandra and KaH and Enigma all share the same
production sound though Sandra and KaH are more obviously dance-oriented.
