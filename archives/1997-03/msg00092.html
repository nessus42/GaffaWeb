Some Irish news !A real blast from the past this time.
On the Sunday just before Christmas Day , RTE 1 television screened a 
programme of Delia Smiths Cookery Course filmed in (I think), late 
1979 and of course featuring Kate chatting to Delia about Vegetarian 
cooking !!!
I had known about the existence of this programme and often wondered 
about it . I was very lucky to grab a tape and record it . A 5 minute 
slot was filmed with Kate and Delia sitting outside a very summery 
East Wickham farm with a spread of foods in bowls & plates before 
them . Kate was so charming and pleasant and shy and all the words 
which always describe her !The food was prepared by her John Carders 
wife (Kates' sister-in-law).
I recently found mention of Kate and 3 of her  recipes in the accompanying 
Cookery course book in a friends house .....
Also , on the Satellite TV station NBC Superchannel last week there 
was a feature on the show "The Ticket" about the launch of the 
War-Child exhibition .Brian Eno, Bob Geldof and several others were 
interviewed in the gallery but I only caught the end of this feature 
and I definitely saw Kate in the background wearing a long light 
brown coat "mingling" with a group of people !
