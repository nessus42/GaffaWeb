Hi!

I saw the show, too, and didn't find the performance very good. I
think KB's voice is very hard to imitate, even more her special way of
phrasing the words.

I found the gestures of the lookalike also a bit exaggerated, it
seemed unnatural.

And I was angry about the lil pic they showed from Kate. It was an old
picture, which showed Kate with wide open eyes and open mouth looking
a lil bit insane. I think this portrays an image of Kate being a witch
like, more or less weired singer with a high pitched voice.

An image that Kate has proven to be wrong during the past years!!

I think Tina Turner was the right choice!! :-)
