To duplicate a line I once wrote in a letter to Kate (about a year ago):

(typical explanation to [other] people who wonder what the continuing
fascination is):
'Whenever I buy a new Kate Bush album, I have to play it about 6 or 7 
times, and then it's just starting to "go in" ...'

Note about TD: get it on tape so you can start & stop to transcribe the
whole lyrics. Takes about a day to do. The record sleeve blurb is
difficult to read & not exactly identical. The words in TD are very
important. If you haven't quite heard all the words while its playing,
you're seriously missing out.
