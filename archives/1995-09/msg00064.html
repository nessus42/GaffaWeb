Hello...

I've been thinking that it would be quite a match if Kate were to do the 
soundtrack for Anne Rice's The Witching Hour, which David Geffen and Warner
Brothers owns the film rights to...Kate and Anne have actually quite alot 
in common - love the same books, movies, prose...and explore and use the 
same religious images (mmm yes..a variety)..and basically have shared 
inspirations.

The Witching Hour, for those that havent read it, isnt a Wiccan based story.

I was listening to The Ninth Wave and reading one of Anne's books once 
and I was really startled to find a deep artistic connection between the 
two of them.

Anyway...I got Anne Rice Kate's work (she hadnt heard her) and if anyone 
has a line into Kate...get her The Witching Hour.

I'd really love to see Kate do the soundtrack for the film when it is made.

Oh..and after watching HoL video that KT directed, I think that the film
The 39 Steps was an inspiration...Twas about two innocent people in love
in handcuffs being chased across the moors...Hitchcock directed.

Lemme know your thoughts....

Anne Rice Newsgroup FYI is alt.books.anne-rice

I have this nagging thought that KT listeners and Anne Rice readers could 
stay up til the wee hours conversing away...
