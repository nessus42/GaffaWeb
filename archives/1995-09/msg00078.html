  I'm sure that many of you are, at least perifferally, aware of the 
collectable Card Game craze a la _Magic the Gathering_.  Well there is one 
out called _On the Edge_ based on a roleplaying game by the name of _Over 
the Edge_.  It turns out that in this game there is a singer/performer 
character named Karla Sommers who is a religious cult figure (with her 
own church and everything). In a mail-list discussion, the designer
was asked what kind of music Karla sang. His response was that a good 
overview of Karla's music could be found in the album "The Whole Story" 
but that her best album was still "Hounds of Love"...
  Go figure...
