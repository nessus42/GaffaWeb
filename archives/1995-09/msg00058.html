Hi all!  I was watching the show "4 on the Floor" this morning on VH-1.  
The show is basically a weekly panel discussion on current events in the 
pop music industry.   One segment today invloved critiques of Annie 
Lennox's new song and video "No More 'I Love You's' "  One of the 
panelists stated that he thought the video was too much like a Kate Bush 
video, and therefore not original enough.  My boyfriend, Mike, (who is 
definitely NOT a big KTFan) mentioned that it was most likely a 
concentrated effort on Annie's part to pay tribute to Kate, of whom Annie 
is obviously a big fan (see Love-Hounds Collection clip with Ms Lennox 
for point in fact).

Anyhow, just wanted to say how thrilled I was to hear Kate's name as 
something of an innovator in video, as we all know...

She Really Is!!
