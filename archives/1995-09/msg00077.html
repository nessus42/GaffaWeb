Oh well :-)  Oh, before continuing, I would like to apologize to both 
Lagan Love and K8BUSH for spotlighting them unnecessarily like I did on my
original rant against AOL.  I didn't need to make it personal in any way..
They didn't flame me at all; I just felt guilty afterwards.  How's the old
saying go, something like Think Before You Post, Dummie... 

That said, I actually had the occasion to sample a friend of mine's AOL 
account over the weekend, and I came away impressed!  It is very 
easy to get onto the 'net, to FTP stuff, and do other utilities that 
other mailers are very cumbersome about.  I got into the "chat mode" thing 
for a while. I think this facility can be put to productive use in business.
Unfortunately, this evening, the group that I ended up in included a couple of 
the types of "kids" who give AOL its bad reputation. But all in all, AOL 
isn't really as bad as I've been led to believe.
