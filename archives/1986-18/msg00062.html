Subject: Scratch Acid

Here's one band I like alot: Scratch Acid, their EP and LP.  This stuff
is great, high-energy, skillfully executed (and recorded) metal-influenced
punk--or perhaps punk-influenced metal...or maybe it's hardcore-influenced
metal...whatever--with fine lyrics & vocals.

My questions are:
  1. Do we all agree this is great stuff?
  2. What similar metal-influenced bands do this well?

Personally I couldn't care what the answer to #1 is, but if you can't
answer #1 for yourself, best go listen to them so you can agree with the
rest of us <smiley face>.  As to #2, best answer it or forever acquiesce
in the claim that Scratch Acid rules.

--John
