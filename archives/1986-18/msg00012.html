I have been an avid reader of this group for some time now, but have yet
to see a Kate Bush discography posted.  If one exists and I missed it, I
would appreciate it if someone could send me a copy.  Or, if one has never
been posted, perhaps some good soul could whip one up.  I'm sure a lot of
us would like to see it.

Also... did anyone from the Philly area make it to the WYSP rock convention?
(23 Nov)  A few venors had some Kate stuff, including many 7 inch imports
(of which I bought "The Deaming", "RUTH", and "The Big Sky" picture disk),
bootlegs (I got one recorded at the NY Paladium), and (my best find) a
Kate video compilation which includes "The Single File", and the RUTH and
Cloudbusting videos.  The video tape was copied from a 3 inch (or is it
3.5?) master and is surprisingly sharp.
