From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Fri, 30 Mar 90 23:14:52 PST
Subject: Kate on P-M MTV again


I caught the video for "The Sensual World" on Post-Modern MTV again
last night.  (They seem to have changed the scheduled time for
P-M again; this was at about 1:20 am.)  Beforehand, they showed yet
another tiny piece of interview.  Kate said:

     "I don't now spend more time promoting than creating, but I have
to be careful that I'm not inaccessible totally to people because,
obviously, when you've made an album you want people to know it's
out there so that if people want to hear it, they can."

Pardon the punctuation, but Kate said all this with no pauses that
could conceivably be breaks between sentences.
