Vickie here. I left the studio last week without playing half of what
I had with me. I decided to have a part 2 of "Filmsongs".
I picked the songs and the order of play but Chris took over the
DJing duties because I had to work der bingo game (keeps the station on
the air & that's no lie!).
I repeated three songs. Kate because it's one of my all-time favorite songs,
and the other two because a turntable was giving me problems last week
(something about the anti-skating something or other) and it caused them
to skip. I hate that. I hate it when good songs get ruined, even if it's
not my fault. 
btw, I did watch the Academy Awards after all and was pleased for the most
part. This year they (argh!) did (argh!) let the nominated songs be sung
(ARRRRRGH! ARRRRRGH! ARRRRRGH!)
Good for Kim Basinger!
