I found a Canadian CD5 single of her song Steaming which includes
"Steaming" (radio version), "Steaming" (dance version), and "Solsbury
Hill" (Live).  The last song is great (not as good as the original,
but that goes without saying, IMHO).  I don't think I found out about
Sarah from this newsgroup and I don't remember if she's been mentioned
here.  In case she hasn't, if you like The Innocence Mission I think
you'll like Sarah's album _Touch_.  If you haven't picked up _The
Innocence Mission_ yet and you read this newsgroup, shame on you....
go do it right this minute!!  I mean it!!!  Good stuff.
