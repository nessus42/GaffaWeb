Subject: Kate Books


Wax Trax, Chcago's premier import etc store, has been stocking Kate books
lately, so I've been able to add 3 to my collection of 1:
The Secret History of KB by Vermorel
KB- a visual documentary by Cann & Mayes
KB Complete (EMI Music)
KB--the whole story by Juby (hardbound)

Of the lot, the visual documentary is priceless, the lyrics-collection is nice,
the hardbound seems pretty vacuous, and Vermorel of course is tres bizarre.

I'm wondering if "Cathy" is still available from Kindlight, and what is the
going rate?

And I'm wondering if anyone would support me in an effort to compile an
annotated lyrics file, with song backgrounds etc? (I have come very late
to Kate-ism, so have few resources from albums 1 to 4...)

--Jorn
