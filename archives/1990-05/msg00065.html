I just have to say that this was the first time I had seen 
these videos other than the clips from the VH-1 special.  
My personal favorite is This Woman's Work.

	Why? => The song by itself gave my goosebumps and caused the hair
	        on my forearms to stand up.  But the video ... 
	        The TWW video made me CRY. No other video/song ever did that
		to me before.  KaTe packed some emotional images into the 
		song and the video reinforces them. An arrow straight into
		my soul.
		
		I must admit to a disclaimer though: although some
		people might say that I am not very emotional, that's
		pretty much an Armor-All exterior (part of my defensive 
		armor) but underneath I think I'm a soft touch and I was 
		ALONE when I watched the video after all.
