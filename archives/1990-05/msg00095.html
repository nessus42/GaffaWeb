Subject: Love and Anger

EMI have just sent out a note to members of the KBC, re Love and Anger.

  EMI records are pleased to announce the release of the third
  single from the Sensual World album "Love and Anger".

  "Love and Anger" will be released on the 26th February on 7", 12",
  cassette single and a limited edition 7" gatefold which includes
  extra pages featuring colour photos taken from the "Love and Anger"
  video. A CD single will be released on 5th March.

  The 7" and cassette singles are backed with a previously unreleases
  song, "Ken", from the Comic Strip film "G.L.C." (due for transmission
  on Thursday 16th February [sic]) and the 12" and CD versions also
  feature two extra unreleased tracks, "The Confrontation" and "One
  last look around the house before we go ...".

  The video, directed by Kate, can be seen on ITV's The Chart Show
  on Saturday 24th February and Kate herself can be seen in her acting
  debut for the Comic Strip on Thursday March 8th in an episode called
  "Les Dogs".

So that's three (count 'em) new Kate tracks about to hit the streets.

Enjoy.
