Excuse me, gang, but this seems like the place to ask: does anyone
know what Steve Hillage is doing these days? His last credits
on albums have been as a producer ("Children of the Night" by Nash
the Slash, "Bankstatement" by Tony Banks, Cock Robin (ugh), etc.),
and I haven't seen an album of his own stuff since 1983. My partial 
discography includes:

Fish Rising, L, Motivation Radio, Live Herald, Aura, Green, Open, 
Rainbow Dome Musick, For To Next/And Not Or, Shimmer(7" EP). That
only includes his solo stuff, and no Gong, Khan, etc.

Have I missed any? Has he recorded anything since For To Next and 
And Not Or, which were 1983 or so?
