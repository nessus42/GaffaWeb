There's a review of The Red Shoes in Seventeen magazine (the 
December issue):

"Ask any female alternative singer for her list of role models and
Ms. Bush's name will top it (or appear just under Patti Smith's).
On her latest, enlivened by her clear, yearning voice, gorgeous
melodies, and lyrics that keep you guessing, this Bush burns."

Rating: four out of four.
