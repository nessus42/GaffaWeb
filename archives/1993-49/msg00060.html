Rockworld is a magazine from England that is also availlable in a German issue.
They (the English one) made a poster special recently, it seems to be the
third one this year since it's No. 03/93. I don't know when it appeared, but
I found several copies of it in an international magazine store in Frankfurt
recently.

The poster special are loose posters folded twice into something that's
magazine-sized. This issue has the subtitle 'Girls, Girls, Girls, Girls',
as all of the posters are showing some women. These are: Annie Lennox, Cher,
Debbie Harrry, Bjoerk, Madonna, Kim Wilde, Doro (from Warlock), Kate Bush,
4 Non Blondes (the gal with the hat), Janet Jackson, Ace Of Base (all 4, not
only the women), Whitney Houston, Army Of Lovers (all of them again), Leila K,
Curve, and Tina Turner (only half the size, there has to be room for the title).
