Anayway, let's start again:

(Note: All the rest is IMHO)

I don't quite agree with your statement, I think we shouldn't expect
KT to do the same kind of music in every album. What I like about her
is the constant change in her music: I have never seen an artist that
had so much variety in voice, style and instruments. And even if I like
Irish traditionnal music very much, I wouldn't like her sticking to
it, as she wouldn't, in that case, have introduced marvels as the Trio
Bulgarka or all the exotic instruments played by her brother.

BTW, I think it's the first time I write to this list, so let me introduce
myself: I am a 22 year old student at the computer graphics lab in Lille,
France. I have been a KT fan since 1984, I recently discovered Tori Amos,
and, like many people, I'd like to hear some of Happy Rhodes'. And, of course,
I'm waiting for the new album.
