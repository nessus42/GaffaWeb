Hi, thanks for posting this and letting us know. I'm glad you like her.
What record store did your wife find it? Do you know if the store where
that came from had any others? Most record store that carry Happy just
tend to get Warpaint. If they do have others, I'd be interested in
knowing which ones. If they have them all...hmmm, that's a tough one.
Maybe Vol. I, so you can hear where she started. Maybe Ecto, which
came right before Warpaint. I love them all, it's hard to tell someone
"you have to get this" because that implies "but don't get that" :-)
and Happy is one of the rare artists (along with, of course, Kate)
who always makes (IMHO) great albums. I love her entire catalog.

Thanks again. If you want a (an...which is it supposed to be?) FAQ,
let me know.

Happy Birthday!
