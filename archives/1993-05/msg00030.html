Just got a note from the KBC this morning. There is "no projected
release date" for KBVII but only "some time in 1993". More excitingly,
the Comic Strip film to be shown on BBC2 on Xmas Eve(?) "features a new
song by Kate called 'Home for Christmas'" !!! (as far as I remember, this
film is about Xmas turkeys getting their revenge or something like
that...)


Also got Homeground 46 yesterday (just in time for the Winter
Solstice!). They hope to hold a KBC/Homeground Convention next year
(yay!). Also, Peter now has an account at exnet.co.uk, a public access
Usenet system in London - though I suspect his address should be
"s0pdfm@exnet.co.uk" and not "pdfm@exnet.co.uk" as printed in
Homeground (the guy who runs it (hi Damon!) insists on putting an "s0"
in everybody's username for no good reason 8^) .
