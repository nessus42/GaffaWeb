Well, on our recent honeymoon to New York City and San Francisco, my wife 
and I were on a quest to find as many Happy CD's as possible, since we 
both fell in love with the 5 songs of her's that a friend had put on a 
mixed tape for me.  In NYC, the Tower Records in the Village had a section 
for her stuff (I think it was in the "H" section, tho'), but it was empty. 
The uptown Tower, however, had two copies of "Rhodes II" and a copy of 
"Ecto".  No "Warpaint".  We got the two that were there, and both are 
wonderful.  I'll be writing to Aural Gratification soon to order her other 
three discs.

And speaking of writing... has anyone else out there written to Happy 
through A.G.?  Does she reply to "fan" mail?  I'm just curious, since the 
only other person that I've sent "fan" mail to is Edward Ka-Spel of the 
Legendary Pink Dots, and he replied to all the mail I sent.
