Hello again.

My versions of "Rock vs. Opera"(1986), "Which Witch"(1987-13 tracks), and
"Which Witch paa Slottsfjellet"(1990-32 tracks) are all released on a label
called NB! Records (Nota Bene Records). I think it is a norwegian 
label. It may well be that their records are distributed abroad
(that is outside of Norway) by a larger label ??

The two first have definitely been released on CD (since I have them
myself), the last one I think is released on CD also, but I own the LP
version so I'm not completely sure.

This thing about using/not using the Dolie de Luxe name is a bit complicated.
Both girls have repeatedly stated that they don't want to use the name
because it kind of links them to a 'pop' image. But when the time comes to
releasing records, they use it anyway. The reason, I guess, being that this
is the name people recognize (??) There are some indications that they are
gradually dropping the name: It is not included on the "Which Witch paa
Slottsfjellet" LP-cover; only on the inside sleeves. And they have stated
that the Rock Opera will definitely NOT be linked to this name when
performed in London.

How did you come across the video to "Eternally"?? I didn't know it had
ever been shown outside of Norway...
