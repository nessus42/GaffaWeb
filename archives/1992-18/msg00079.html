Brief note:  Got the 1st 4 Happy Rhodes CDs in the mailbox on Saturday.
Having no idea what to expect, I must say that I'm very pleasantly
surprised by her 1st two (all I've listened to).  At times you really
would swear it's KaTe singing, and a young Kate at that on the 1st
CD.  The only things I don't really care for are the ghastly covers
(like some bad paintings on "Night Gallery") and all the inner deamon
stuff doesn't really impress me.

The production on the 1st two CDs is pretty spare, some cuts with
only voice and guitar or voice and synth.  That I like.
