Sheet music books are still available for _TSW_ and _HOL_.  
Both are very nicely done with B&W and some color pictures.  The
_HOL_ book has a pull-out color poster included.  I ordered 
both of these books from a local sheet music store.

There was a also book of sheet music published for _The Whole 
Story_, but it is now out of print. This is still available from 
our public library, so you might want to check there first.
