The new season of that *most* erudite and educational television show,
Mystery Science Theater 3000, has begun!  i am, by design, currently
without the necessary viewing apperatus (like, no TV, no cable, no
time), but i hear from the ever-vigilent Vickie Mapes that KaTe was
mentioned in the very first episode!!  apparently, our heros went over
a long list of "Things that the Space Program has brought to the
general public," and KaTe was in there somewhere with Velcro and Tang
;) Vickie assures me that she and Chris replayed the segment several
times, and that they (the MST3000ites) quite definitely said "Kate
Bush."

wow, KaTe is really going places now!  maybe now that she is getting
some *real* attention, she will be encouraged to tour!
