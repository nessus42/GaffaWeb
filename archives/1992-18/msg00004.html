	I recently purchased this mini-CD.  I was told that It's been around for
quite a few months.  My question is:  

Is this evidence of KaTe's new style that will be on the new album?

The tracks are very basic, with some very enjoyable flavours in them.  But they
are VERY likable.  I wonder if the new album will be a return to the days when
all she had was the KTBush band...  I'd welcome this, my favorite songs were
those that had a unique turn of phrase, but weren't so complexly layered that
you had to fight with the song.  Maybe another concept piece would be nice.

Why doesn't one of you blokes in ye olde UK sneak into her studio and 
five-finger discount a demo or two???  This suspense in waiting for the
"promised new album and tour" is making me go crazy!!!
