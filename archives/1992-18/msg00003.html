Okay, I just saw the Tori Amos video: Silent All These Years.

Now, I'm trying to keep an open mind, but it seemed to me to be a wierd
combination of a 10,00 Maniacs and Edie Brickell video.  In essence:  Even
though I love 10,000 Maniacs and tolerate Edie, I immensely DISLIKED this
video.  I thought it too disjointed and way too simplistic to like.  I guess
that I should hear the rest of the album to be truly informed, but I am one
of the few that do not like this song...

And her in the crate I see as plagiarism of Kate's "Kick Inside" cover...

I'll reserve judgement until I hear more material, but for now I'm going back
to my Happy Rhodes tape...
