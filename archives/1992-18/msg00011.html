jdi@Franz.COM (John D. Irwin) writes:

>Speaking of Talk Talk, does anyone know if they've done anything recently?
>TCOS is one of my all time favourite albums, but their followup album was
>way too weird for me.  (The one with the tree on it, I haven't looked at or
>listened to it in so long I forget the title).

They have a new album out, "Laughing Stock". But if you didn't like
"Spirit of Eden" you probably won't like "Laughing Stock". 

And I must say, if you didn't like "Spirit of Eden", it's probably
becuase you expected it to be like other Talk Talk albums. It's an
*incredible* album, and i very strongly suggest that you forget
entirely that it's Talk Talk, and pretend it's some new thing, and
give it a listen or two.
