A lot of people, both here and elsewhere, have been talking about how
much Tori Amos sounds like Kate.  The other day someone dear to me said
much the same thing, so I decided to get the album and decide for myself.
It's a great album, but she sounds nothing like Kate, either in voice
or style, though it is obvious that Kate was an influence.

That evening I had some people around and one of them wanted to change the
music.  He took Little Earthquakes off the CD and asked me if it had its
own case or should he put it in the boxed set.

Is it possible to be too attuned to Kate?
