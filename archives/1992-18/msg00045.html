If anybody's wondering what KaTe's involvement was, they used the
beginning of "Hello Earth" during the title sequence (shots of Earth
from space and speeded-up aerial views of mountains and lakes zooming
past), and the the end of the same track later in the program.  Since
the musical director for the show was Michael Kamen, this was only to
be expected 8^).

Btw, I've read it's to be shown in 130 different countries, so
presumably the USA will be one of them! It's also a very disturbing
account of what's happening to the environment. Recommmended.
