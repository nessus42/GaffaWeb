...I told you all about a new CD/photo book box set that was
advertised in Record Collector? Well it finally arrived today(!)

It's called "Kate Bush: A Lioness at Heart", it's been put together by
and outfit called UFO Books, and it comprises a nice big 12"x12" box,
a big glossy 48-page book with TKI/Lh-era photo session shots by Gered
Mankowitz and a chronology of KaTe's career during 1978/79, a
bog-standard Lionheart CD, a postcard of the cover photo (a
particularly nice green leotard shot) and a small monochrome poster of
the (in)famous pink leotard photo.

The book is very nicely produced and designed. The photos include ones
from the green & pink leotard sessions, the Kate-in-a-box sessions, the
Lioheart cover sessions and more obscure ones. They're not all in
colour, and I think maybe more should have been, for the price.

No sign of the "certificate of authenticity signed by Gered Mankowitz"
tho'.

It cost L31.49 (extortion, I know, but I've stopped worrying about
all the money I spend on KaTestuff now 8^) inc. UK p&p from
