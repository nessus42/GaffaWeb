A quest: grrrl seeks other grrrls to jam with

Joan Galt is looking for some other grrrls to jam with, somewhere in
the greater Washington DC/Baltimore/Frederick MD area.  Joan, who is
in her 30's, likes to sing and play her strat, so anything that works
with that would be of interest.  Grrrls of all colors, ages and types
are welcome to respond (agnostic and cynical tendencies preferred).
Note: this is strictly a musical thang!

Co-jammer requirements:

Whacked-out sense of humor
No commercial aspirations--it's just for fun (but who knows?)
Environmentally-oriented--especially ticked-off vegans & vegetarians
Must be willing to be totally fragrance-free for sessions (allergies)
Art or design background a plus--no lawyers, please

Joan's musical likes:

Jane Siberry
Kate Bush
Peter Gabriel
Brian Eno
Portishead
Alanis Morissette
Crash Test Dummies
Ofra Haza
Thomas Dolby before he cut his hair off
Erik Satie

All interested parties are urged to write with location, talents (if
any) and outlook on music, culture, life and the state of the
world...

This offer good for a limited time only, so act now!
