Just re-listening last night to the demos I have (in the bath I 
thouroughly recommend it!) I noticed something on the track, the nane 
for which I have:
I don't See Why I Shouldn't (Pick The Rare Flower)

Towards the end of the track, when the piano is really kicking
thumping away after the last of the lyrics, I can hear what sounds 
like a cup and saucer rattling in the background.
Presumably this was on top of the piano, and the vibrations from the 
piano caused it to rattle. At the end of the track Kate obviously 
stands up very abruptly to turn off the tape recorder as she knocks 
the afore mentioned tea cup.

Da da my bit of trivia for you !!
