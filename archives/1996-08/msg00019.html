I am glad someone mentions it again. And yes, "Kate Bush Covered", the
compilation 'Mr Mike' is referring to is up and running. I wish I could
repost this message every few days, but then I feel people would grow
accustomed to the message and then just skip it. And that is of course not
my intention.

A progress report can be followed on the 'Kate Bush Covered' webpage
(http://ourworld.compuserve.com/homepages/marcel/katebush ), where even two 
MIDI files that will possibly be included can be downloaded.

I am continually on the lookout for talented people who want to try and
record their own version of one or more Kate Bush songs, and if you, dear
reader, feel that you can record something to contribute to this project,
please don't hesitate to e-mail me. The project will hopefully end by the
end of this year, but knowing the pace of Kate Bush I doubt that her fans
will work any faster. 

So prove me wrong, then!
