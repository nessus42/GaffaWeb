DALI

WOW! Yeah! Violet! That's superb!!! It fits nearly perfect! And it makes
much  more sense than anything else. So far the best suggestion, I think.
It directs one to a  straightforward interpretation.

face, laughing about this...

woman)". It is  "The Ascension of the holy Cecily", but I can't think that
KaTe had this in mind.
