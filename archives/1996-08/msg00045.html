Ah, Wieland, you read my mind. No sooner did I post my request for a
comprehensive list of the Demo songtitles than I found it already posted.

My Demo story... the demos are the first KB I ever aquired... for a
roommate many years ago. I liked them so much I got myself a copy as well
some time later. Lilly (the roommate) had been a Kate fan for years and
was stunned to hear the demos - I hadn't heard of Kate until Lil
introduced me to her music, and the demo songs remain to this day my
favorite Kate tape! I'm sure the lady herself would not be entirely
thrilled to know that there are fans who like the old demo tapes as much
or more than her "finished" material, but I guess for me it's a nostalgia
thing.

So again, thank you Wieland, for reading my mind!
