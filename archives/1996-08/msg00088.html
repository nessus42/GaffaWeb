To kerry white:  The copy of the Prince's Rock Trust
I have was actually rented from a vid store and
copied for me, so, once upon a time, it must
have been commercially available.  Some hunting
in the racks might turn one up.  It's worth it because
even though Kate only gets one song, and some
other worthless types get two or three, she
does an excellent job of it and you get to see
her shine when the pressure is on and things
go wrong.  She's also quite beautiful in the bit.

It also includes the longest most boring speech
ever delivered by Bonnie Prince Charlie.  After this,
you will understand what he sees in Camilla.  Joan
Armatrading does a tune, they have a local contest
winner, and lots of Phil Collins' drum work.  The fretless
bass player (can't remember his name, but I think
he worked with Midge Ure once, although that
I can't confirm) is outstanding.
