I find it all quite amusing that Tori gets a bad press in this newsgroup. The converse isn't true in r.m.t-a. 
Perhaps this says something more about KT fans than the undeniable musical talent of Tori Amos? 
Is it that we some of us here resent Tori's popularity built largely by a very supportive record company 
and her own commitment to making sure her music gets heard? EastWest/Warners back her worldwide, 
do EMI back Kate to the same extent? Tori tours extensively also and courts the media. Her schedule 
over the last couple of weeks has been very busy, including forfilling commitments both sides of the 
Atlantic. Does Kate do the same? What do we as fans get? A Radio 1 interview every 4 years, a handful of 
magazine articles and a occasional appearance on TOTP (or Michael Aspel hahaha!).
I love Kates music, it means a lot to me personally and has been the source of great strength. I experience 
many emotions whilst listening to her music; the same, for me,  is true, of Tori, Rickie Lee Jones, Suzanne 
Vega, Joni Mitchell, Beverley Craven, all to varying degrees and intensities (especially Tori).

As for the copying arguement, anyone ever noticed how the start of 'Precious Things' sounds likes a Rolf 
Harris record?
