Okay, let's get serious, folks, and go back to the roots. *I'd* put HOL
first, TD second, but as for the third, shall we consider TKI? I mean,
after all, it *does* have "Wuthering Heights" (hello) on it, and " Them
Heavy People" and "The Man With the Child in His Eyes" and songs that a
long time ago got some of us into KT in the first place.

Of course, I would also posit Volume 1 of the B sides from This Woman's
Work--it being an incredible assortment of her lesser-known, and yet
greatly meritorious, work, but alas, it wasn't recorded conceptually as an
entire "album." But it still knocks my socks off. 

A merry Katemas to all.
