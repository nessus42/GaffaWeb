Hello!  What prompted interest in "3rd Best"?  Did a favorite Olympian
win a bronze?  Here are my standings (for today anyway):

1st:  HoL      
2nd:  TD
Both amazing, perfect albums. Choosing the best on any given day
depend on whether one wants to see a movie or read a book.
3rd:  TRS
4th:  NFE
Misunderstood newcomer gets a slight edge over the sentimental crowd
pleaser. Actually kind of similar; TRS could be NFE revisited, a
little wiser and sadder.
5th: 3 way tie-TKI, LH, TSW. Some weak cuts, but most songs are
beautiful- the title tracks,Moving, Oh England..,TWW, Rocket's Tail.
