>
>I just recently bought the Love and Anger Single, and I was wondering
>where "The Confrontation" and "One More ..." come from?  I mean,
>they sound somewhat like incidental music.  Did Kate make this for
>a show?
>
	Simply, yes.  'The Confrontation', 'One last look around the House
before we go', and 'Ken' were all written for the Comic Strip's film 'G.L.C.',
a parody of the Greater London Council when it was led by 'Red' Ken
Livingstone.
