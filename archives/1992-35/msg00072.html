Somebody (don't remember who, sorry...) said that Michael Stipe joined 10,000
Maniacs onstage for "A Campfire Song."  I thought I'd mention that Michael
sings on that track on the album that it's on and that Natalie and Michael are
friends (for who didn't know and care :) ).  You're lucky, I didn't even get to
see them.  :(

On a Kate related note: the Community Theater here used a couple of Enya songs
and "This Woman's Work" by Kate as part of the music either before or during
the intermission of their production of _Close Ties_.  I meant to mention this
a while ago (ie: when I found out) but got side-tracked (for a long time I
guess...:) ).  Oh well, better late than never  :)  (I hope :) )
