Hi,

Just something I noticed when I played "December will be magic again" off
the Moments of pleasure CD-single.
It sounded different than I remembered it... and at the end my suspicion was
proved right: the fade-out is earlier than on other versions! I checked it
with the This woman's work CD, and that one is the 'original' version off
the 7". 
One giveaway of this 'Moments-of-pleasure-version' is the typical
Christmassy tambourinish sounds being faded simultaneously with everything
else where on the original version it lingers on for about a second more.
Also, Kate is still singing on the final second of the 'MoP-version'.
I leave it up to people with better Hifi-systems to notice any other mixing
differences, as I am sure there must be. It just sounds differently!
Did anyone notice this earlier, or am I really on to something new????
