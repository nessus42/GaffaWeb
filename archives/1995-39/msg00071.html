Well, there was a fellow named Jesus, about Kate's age
at the time of His death. Some say His, though youthful,
was the face of God.  Dozens of other young-looking 
deities come to mind, too.

Nevertheless, IED shares Peter's sentiments.  As one of a depressingly small
number of fans who believe that Kate looks BETTER in those infamous "Q"
photos than in most of the earlier images, IED looks forward to seeing the
new "unflattering" snapshot which Steve generously offers.  II'sHO, a mistake
some fans tend to make is in thinking that one's appreciation of Kate's face
increases only in proportion to its "prettiness", rather than to its
revelation of her artistic, human (and divine) CHARACTER. The latter virtue
is, and always has been, more beautiful -- and of more vital interest to this
fan, anyway -- than the fleeting and, on the whole, futile charms of youth,
Kately or otherwise.

-- Andrew Marvick (IED)
