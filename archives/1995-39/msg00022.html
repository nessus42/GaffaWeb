Having done a fair share of laying gaffer tape in my time, I have
to say the first thing that sprang to my mind on hearing this song
was not thick black tape but some Middle Eastern bastion of Islam,
somewhat akin to Mecca, Jedda, or such like. Being suspended in
Gaffa sounded more like one of the trials of Sinbad than anything.
This is, of course, just one man's fancy.
