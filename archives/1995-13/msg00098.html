Although its difficult for most Americans to understand, Kate was a 
phenomenon in London at the time the Penthouse Kate Simmons schtick came 
out. This foto layout was intentionally designed to give Penthouse men a 
girl as remarkable as our Kate. Singing/Songwriting/Dancing and high 
boots and so on.

What I'm saying is that the Kate Simmons rumour didn't get started 
because Ms Simmons looked like our Kate, but rather because someone at 
Penthouse intentionally created a bio from Ms Simmons that paralleled 
the resume of our Kate.

It should also be noted that HomeGround has previous reported that I own 
a warehouse lot of this issue of Penthouse....alas...if only life were 
as sweet as the imaginations of the press!
