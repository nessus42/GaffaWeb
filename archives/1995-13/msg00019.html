As mentioned before, she does have 3 albums.  The tracks on the first
one are as follows:

 ** On the way down from the Moon Palace **

1. on the way down from the Moon Palace
2. Guessing Game ( or the Music Business )
3. Blue Monday
4. calling..    
5. Hangin' With a Dead Man
6. Screaming Angels Dancing In Your Garden
7. Riding My Bike
8. simply tony
9. Dig My Own Grave
10. Cry Baby
11. bye bye little doggie       
12. The Other One
13. dark irie

the title track is instrumental, as I recall.  The words are
included!!!

It is, as the cover states, "written, performed, and produced by the
EMOTIONAL WENCH LISA GERMANO".  

The little poem on the back:

in the arms of another
no thought to move
no fear of falling
he holds you up
here on the Moon Palace
you live through his light
and yours 
is fading


It is a stunning album, and quite a bit different from the other two.
