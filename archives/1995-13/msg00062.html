I just saw Nanci Griffith last night (4/17) in New York and was mesmerized
by the end of the show.  Understand, I like Nanci Griffith but never called
myself a big fan until last night; she's a great songwriter, storyteller,
and performer...and she had a lot of FUN.

The only personal bit of information I can share is that (according to her
last night) she is computer illiterate and is amazed at how plugged-in and
online savvy her band is (especially guitar tech, Bruce Pearson...oops,
should his name be kept a secret!?).

Anyway, wonderful range and presence throughout her show.

Peace,

Mike
