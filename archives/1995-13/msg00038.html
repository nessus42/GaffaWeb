There were ten commercials last year, nine of which featured different pieces
of Kate Bush music. Several of these
"old" Kate Fruitopia spots are now being aired again, along with
one confirmed "new" spot -- the one we saw during the Oscars.
IED recently reported wrongly that he had seen a different 
new commercial for a drink called "Inner Light". Thanks to Kimiad we now know
IED got this wrong -- and a second look has shown
this to be the same ad as the one that finishes with a shot of 
the words Iced Tea. This ad doesn't make any more specific reference to a
particular flavor, as far as IED can tell. 

The other ads -- at least those recently reported about in Love-Hounds --
with different pieces of music, are from the 
older collection. So far, then, there is still only one 
confirmed new ad.
