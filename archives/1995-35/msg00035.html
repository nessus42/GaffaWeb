Hi, this is Norman.  First of all, a special apology for accidently 
misspelling Bryan Dongray's last name.  And also, the Japanese lyrics are 
a bit off in a couple of places.  I do use my ears.  And Ron told me 
about Kate Bush Complete.  I have a sneaking suspicion that book's hard 
to find.  (One of those things to get, though, such as the Lovehounds and 
Total Tape Integration video tapes.)

     And speaking of rarities, it appears that CDNow has discontinued 
selling imports that are really bootlegs.  That means you have to find 
other means to get the Kate Bush Home Demos CD.  (I'll bring the catalog 
number with me when next time I come up here.)  There was a separate 
studio demos CD's that I believe contained earlier outtakes of some of 
her later stuff (but I'm not sure.  CDNow hasn't put in details about 
most of their imports as of yet.)  Anyway, I thought for those of you who 
accessed CDNow and was confused, I apologize.  

                                                     Stormin' Norman
