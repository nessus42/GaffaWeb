Unfortunately, time has come for me to part from my beloved and very rare

shoe box with the album picture on the box, and it includes:
- the CD (of course)
- the video of the RS track
- a beautiful promotional fountain pen
- biographical papers including information of the record
- a promo-picture of KT

The value of the box is not so much based on the stuff thats in it, but
