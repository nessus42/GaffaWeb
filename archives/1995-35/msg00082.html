> -- In the past few years, Loreena McKennitt's haunting fusion of the ancient
> and modern has attracted a steadily expanding audience. She follows her last
> album, The Mask And Mirror (which is approaching gold status) with Seeds Of
> Love--A Short Collection For The Winter Season, a five-song EP of holiday
> songs.
> -- Of the five tracks here, "Coventry Carol" features Loreena's musical
> setting of words from a fifteenth-century pageant, while "God Rest Ye Merry,
> Gentlemen" and "Good King Wenceslas" are stunning reinterpretations of two
> yuletide favorites. "Snow" and "Seeds Of Love" find Loreena matching a
> hundred-year-old poem and a traditional folk lyric with her own spectral
> music.
> -- Loreena will support Seeds Of Love with a nationally syndicated radio
> broadcast followed by a live on-line question-and-answer session (a la the
> recent Elvis Costello concert, which was carried live by more than 200 radio
> stations nationwide). Count on extensive publicity surrounding the
> performance.
> -- Loreena has build an extremely solid U.S. following, and her albums are in
> continuous demand as new listeners discover the extraordinary depth and
> quality of her music. Count on a strong grass-roots demand for this EP, which
> should help to broaden Loreena's audience still further.
