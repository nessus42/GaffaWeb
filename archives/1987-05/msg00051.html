Well, Pat, you may try the Pontiac Brothers to see where at least
one member of The Gun Club, Ward Dotson, ended up.  They now have
two rock and rolling albums out on Frontier records.  In interviews,
Ward portrays Jeffry Lee Pierce as an insufferable crybaby, Momma's
boy that never properly compensated his fellow band members. Whenever
Ward tries to get money due him  Jeffrey's Mom intervenes and makes
sure Jeffrey's poor psyche isn't damaged.  Some of the other members
went on to form an La post-punk type band - is it Thin "White Rope?
I dunno - Jeffrey hasn't done anything as far as I know except
sit back and enjoy the acclaim of past works.

James "I don't know Wicinski" Hofmann
