I had this dream last night

From: J. Peter Alfke <alfke@csvax.caltech.edu>
Date: Fri, 06 Mar 87 12:44:05 -0800
Subject: I had this dream last night

I had this dream last night where I was sitting with about 20 people
on a walkway around this tower in the middle of a desert, and we were 
all playing percussion instruments and laying down this awesome 20-piece
rhythm jam.  I was playing one of those flattish Mylar balloons and it
had a great tom sound.  I just now realized that it probably wouldn't sound
like that in real life.  Too bad.  Anyhow, that was my dream.

Oh yeah.  There's this sort of interlude between "Society Is a Hole" and
"I Love Her All the Time" that sounds unlike anything else on the album
(Bad Moon Rising).  Is it early SYouth, or some other band or what?  It's
definitely a piece of a longer song.

I thought we had some Soft Boys / Robyn Hitchcock gurus here ... could
someone post discography-type info?
