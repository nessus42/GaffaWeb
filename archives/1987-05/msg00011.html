Subject: Urgh! Aaagghhhh!!!! Weeeee!!!!!! Yeah!!!



just celebrating over finding two relatively hard to find
records at BARGAIN (below $4) prices.  Well, they
were very, very, very, very slightly used (probably Considine promos...)
but man, oh, man...

1) Golden Paliminos (1st LP) - this does not wank.  Though, it makes
for some good dishwashing music, it's also headphone city.  I swear
the singer from Killdozer must have listened to this at
least once.

2) Antetiem - 1st LP on Homestead (HMS 023).  Get this for "Good Kirk/
Bad Kirk" - still this is the un-angular type of stuff you probably
won't see in Earle's fave list. But if you like folky, type of stuff - 
kinda how Fetchin' Bones would sound if they didn't wank, get this.
Also, "Music From Elba" - their latest is okay.  Sounds like X taken
to a reciprocal power.  Hah.  Try and make sense outta that allegory, mates.

Jim
