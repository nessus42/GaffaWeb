I believe Re/Search is still around (does Allyn Hardyck know?) They just
take their time putting stuff out. All their stuff is probably worth
getting.

There was an issue of RAW late last year with a new MAUS instalment that's
not in the MAUS book. Spiegleman said something stupid in the editorial
about the lateness that's not worth repeating. It's a pretty decent issue,
with a good 1-page strip from Richard Sala who's quickly becoming one
of my favorites. Sala's new '86-87 stuff is much better than his '84 book
Night Drive. Borges or Kafka in comic form.

If you have problems getting RAW, they do mail order. Or you can move
to a hep town like Champaign. :-)
