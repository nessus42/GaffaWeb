Tesla play hard rock.  Bad hard rock.  Avoid at all costs unless
you're into stuff like Ratt and Bon Jovi.

Record cover of the month -- Prince, dressed in drag, on the cover
of his new 12".  He makes quite a pretty girl.

CD of the month -- Front 242's "Back Catalogue," containing 70
minutes of music (!) and selections from their first 2 albums
and 2 EPs.  If only more of my favorite groups did this...v.cost-
effective.

Boo, hiss, and feh -- the Siouxsie LP.

--Sue
