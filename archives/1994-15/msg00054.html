	I think woj is more accurate in saying that the phrase about "there 
being some love-hounds in attendance" came _after_ the list of countries from 
which fans were known to be coming, hence _after_ the US was mentioned, but not 
necessarily "in connection with" as Evan Walsh correctly remembers me saying.  
That was definitely the impression made on me, but is an interpretation, not 
the naked fact.

	Briefly on the love-in-the-title Quiz question:  when Andy Marvick was 
out Saturday, IED ruled that "Alternative Hounds of Love" *is* an 8th correct 
answer.  This was after we looked at the UK 12" vinyl and saw that title on the 
disk itself.
