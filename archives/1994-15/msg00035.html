Hi all,

    I don't know how many remember my message last year about organising the
TLTCTC to be shown here in Oz. Well I got some 400 names from
gaffa/Homeground/KBC/plus others and then EMI ducked for cover and it was never
shown in Feb. this year, like they promised. Well I have just got off the phone
with EMI NSW and IT'S FINALLY ON!!! I'll be getting a whole heap of invites
that I'll post out to NSW people soon. As for the other states, Qld and WA will
be informing me soon of their dates so I can get them out. SA is on holiday
today so I'll hassle them tomorrow and Vic., well, here's the BAD news.... THEY
have already shown it there!!!! You have no idea how piss-off I was when I
heard that. Apparently they did an 'industry only' showing and didn't tell
anyone else! >:-(

    The GOOD news is that after I had got through with them then NSW EMI rang
them and did the same, they'll show it again BUT I need somebody in Vic.
willing to help organise it all. So... any takers??? Reply ASAP.
