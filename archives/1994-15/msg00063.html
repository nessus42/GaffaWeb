gmeij@psy.vu.nl (Gerard Meij) writes:

   A few days ago I bought the CD singles Eat The Music and Moments of 
   Pleassure. Both covered a song that was not on the album and as a real 
   Kate-fan I just had to have them. I was surprised by the song You want 
   Alchemy. I think it is one of Kate's best songs and it is my opinion 
   that this song should have been on the album The Red Shoes. It is better 
   than Lily of Big Stripey Lie. Don't you agree?

I agree that YWA is a good song. I don't agree that it should have replaced
Lily or BSL on TRS as they are my favourite tracks on the album - closely
followed by the title track and MoP. Now, if you had suggested replacing
Eat The Music or Why Should I Love You or the last (the title of which
eludes me), I would have agreed!

Geoff
