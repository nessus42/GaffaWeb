I forgot to include a couple of things in my last posting.

1)  During my phone conversation with Mark Hamilton of KITS, he told
me that he had the _New Musical Express_ with Kate on the cover right
in front of him that very instant.  He said he found the interview
interesting.

2)  Tower Records, at least, now knows about the album.  Through mail
messages from others as well as my own checking, the Mountain View,
San Mateo, and Berkeley stores are all aware of it, so I assume the
whole chain is.  The San Mateo store has a nice Upcoming Releases 
board; _The Sensual World_ is listed plain as day on it.  October
17 is the day!
