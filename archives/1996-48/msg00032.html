You seem to be confused. Rumours, the classic Fleetwood Mac album was
released some years back. While it was "rumoured" at the time that both
Stevie Nicks and Christine McVie were really Kate Bush, the rumors
proved unfounded. It was eventually shown, as I'm sure you all know,
that Stevie Nicks is really Bob Dylan and Christine McVie is Rod
Stewart. And I suspect that there is truth to the rumor that they were
involved with helping John Lennon dispose of Paul McCartney's body and
suppressing media coverage after the automobile accident. The lookalike
we now know as Paul McCartney is -- amazing how these things come full
circle -- Kate Bush's first cousin on her father's side! Sadly, it seems
to reinforce the theory that Kate inherited her musical ability from her
mother's side of the family.

The release date for Kate's new album was posted last week along with
her tour schedule. Check the archives.
