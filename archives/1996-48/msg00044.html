A funny addendum to this: about 8 years ago, I designed a T-Shirt in Zapf
Chancery Typeface (the hounds of love typeface) that said, "KATE BUSH
HOUNDS OF LOVE '85 ROAD CREW."

I came up with a list of cities and silk-screened them on the back of the
shirt. 

Whenever I wore it and somebody who knew who Kate was would see it, they'd 
go absolutely nuts, thinking they missed a tour. I made up wild stories
about being on the road with Kate (usually they involved
impossible-to-honor requests from Ms. Bush, pertaining to an elaborate
stage show which I made up every time I told the story). 

I really screwed up a couple of people's reality with that thing. I wish I
still had it.
