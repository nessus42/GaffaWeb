My personal theory on the cover art of The Red Shoes album--and I'm 
sure this will be controversial--is that it features the feet instead 
of a Kate facial picture because it is called The Red Shoes (get it, 
shoes)  just as the Hounds of Love featured Kate in bed with two dogs 
(get it hounds, love).  But this is my opinion, and is in no way 
authoritative.
