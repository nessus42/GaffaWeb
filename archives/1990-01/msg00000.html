    WARNING!! YET ANOTHER SUSPENDED IN GAFFA PLAYLIST COMING UP!!

This was last night's year/decade end 'god-I-hate-"Best Of"-lists' show.
"Best Of" lists only reflect two things; Sales/Popularity or
Personal Opinion of the person writing up the list. Take Rolling
Stone (please), how can anybody take seriously a list that excludes
Kate or Jane? Obviously, people who've never heard of Kate or Jane!
On tonight's show I was very honest in calling it "*My* Personal
Favorite *ARTISTS* of the 80s"(female-wise, of course.)
These are artists who are consistent. Everything they do is great (IMHO).
I would reccommend ANY album by all of these artists (of course,
some of the artists only have 1 album.) I could have played just
about any song from any of these artist's albums. The songs played
have no significance. I picked them at ramdom, something I could
only do on THIS particular show.
