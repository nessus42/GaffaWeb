I think this qualifies for the "Only in LA" file.  There is a new product
out called Dancing Flowers.  They are basically plastic daisies that move
to sound.  On my way through LAX on the Friday before Christmas I saw
3 of these on display in the gift shop.  The radio next to them was play-
ing "Running Up That Hill."  Now you may feel jaded and think that you've
seen everything, but you haven't really lived until you've seen three
plastic flowers boogeying to the wonderous voice of Our Lady!!
