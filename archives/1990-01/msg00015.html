   Ok, the .gaffa readers probably know this already, but the .misc readers
   probably don't.  I'm cross-posting to increase the interest of getting
   readers over to .gaffa.

   Another CD single from "The Sensual World" is out: "This Woman's Work."
   It joins "The Senusal World" 5" CD single.  Does anyone know of other
   CD singles from the album?  

   Here are the particulars:

   "This Woman's Work," 5" CD single, EMI Records, Cat #. CD EM 119, 1989.
      Track 1 - "This Woman's Work (Single Mix)"  Time: 3:34
      Track 2 - "Be Kind to My Mistakes"  Time: 3:03
      Track 3 - "I'm Still Waiting"  Time: 4:28
      (Tracks 2 and 3 are not found on "The Sensual World")

   "The Sensual World," 5" CD single, EMI Records, Cat. CD EM 102, 1989
      Track 1 - "The Sensual World"  Time: 4:01
      Track 2 - "The Sensual World (Instrumental Version)"  Time: 3:57
      Track 3 - "Walk Straight Down the Middle"  Time: 3:47
      (Track 2 is not found on "The Sensual World")
   
   Just thought you'd like to know...  And, it pays to shop around... One
   store had "This Woman's Work" for $19.98, and another had it for $8.98.
   Just a small difference, hmmm?

   Oh, I also found a picture, interview CD with Bush talking about
   "Love Hounds" and "The Dreaming."  I didn't buy it...yet.  Can anyone
   tell me if there is any music or really useful material on it?
   I know, I know... Everything Kate has to say is important.  I was
   just asking! ;-)

   Enjoy,
