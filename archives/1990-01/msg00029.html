Subject: Sarah McLachan

Hi love-hounds, hope you all had a happy Christmas and New Year.

Anyway, while I was at home for Christmas (in Dublin) I was pottering
around a record store when I spotted the album "Touch" by Sarah McLachan.
Having heard good things about her a number of months ago on gaffa, I
decided to buy the album and was quite pleased with it. Its not a classic
of our time or anything but I like it a lot especially the song "Vox".

I know next to nothing about this woman so I would be interested in any
info that anybody has on her e.g where she's from, how popular she is in
North America (or anywhere else), whether she has released "Vox" as a
single (I think it could do really well over here in Europe), any plans
for touring, what she is like live etc. etc. All information welcome.

Thanks

Declan

p.s  Is it my imagination or has this group been renamed rec.music.ied :-)
Seems like there's more discussion of IED than Kate at times !

Glad to see that all the back-biting which characterised this news group
during the month of December seems to have died down though. I do hope
everyone can get back to healthy and stimulating discussion of real music
now.
