Kate was featured in a BBC 2 programme on New Year's Eve called
"Eighties" which was a rock review of the decade with highlights
from the most outstanding performances on BBC TV over the past 10 years.
The showed an excerpt from Kate's performance of "Running Up That Hill"
from the Wogan show probably only about 40 secs worth, but some artists
only got about 10 seconds.

>From: chris@world.std.com (Chris'n'Vickie of Kansas City)
>Subject: Ladyslipper catalog listing.
>MARY COUGHLIN-Tired & Emotional
>A thoroughly unusual debut album from an Irish woman who has developed
>quite a buzz, even a cult following, among music fans.
>I only have the LP but I think it's available on CD now.

It's good to hear someone promoting Mary Coughlin. I strongly urge
Love-hounds to check her out. Great for late night listening. There
is a second album called "Under the Influence" which includes a great
version of "Ride On". Both albums highly recommended. Both are also
available on CD - at least over here. It has been a long time since
the second album came out so maybe there is another one due.
