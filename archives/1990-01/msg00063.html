The bronze writing on some American CD longboxes is distinctly brighter
than others, now.  I noticed this today when I saw a large stack of
_The Sensual World_ CD's at a record store.  Look for yourself; you
can't miss it.  It's easiest to see if you look at the edge of a large
stack; that is likely to have both printings.

The new, brighter writing is much easier to read.  Does anyone know if
in fact there has been a second pressing?
