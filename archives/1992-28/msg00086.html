I would like to take a stab at an approximation of the phonetic
reversal of "We let the weirdness in."

An approximate reversal of 'we' could sound like 'you'.
'let' versus 'tell'. 'the' versus 'oth', as in 'other'.
'weirdness' versus 'sendry-oo' or 'sundry-you'.
'in' versus 'ni', as in 'nick'.
Now, all backwards, it might sound like, 'niss-endriu-oth-tell-you'.
Of course, vocal inflections are important, too.

Oh well...
