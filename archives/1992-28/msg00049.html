But speaking of acts that have used Kate to further their careers, I
happened to notice tonight at my local 7-11 the October issue of _DJ Times_.
The cover noted an interview with the Utah Saints, so I went into Kate Scan
Mode, and read a quote from band member Tim Garbutt (I may misremember his
name) regarding liberal use of sampling by groups of other's works.  The
interviewer asked why they used the Kate Bush sample in their recent big 
song.  Here's what Garbutt had to say to that: 

"By using Kate Bush samples, we're getting a nice cross-section of people
 buying our ravers." 

Wow, what a really deeep answer...  I had occasion to hear this thing the 
other day, and I must confess that I do not intend to become a part of this
cross-section.  :-)  Gawd, some of the stuff that makes it these days...
