A brief nit-picky note to Uli:

In a recent posting, you noted "We let the weirdness in" as being
the last line in "Sat in Your Lap."  Actually, if my memory serves,
these are in fact the last words spoken at the very end of "Leave
It Open."  The last words of SiYL are "...across the water," as
the last in the series of something like "across the desert, across
the elements, across the water..."

Like I said, nit-picky.  But hope this helps both you and the person
you were posting to. :)
