> Just a few questions regarding "Another Day":

> Who wrote it?
> When and where was it performed?

> Thanks!

Roy Harper wrote the song and originally performed it.  It is on one
of his albums (*Fast, Baroque, and Ugly*? I don't recall right now the
exact title.)  Several people have covered it, including Kate Bush and
Peter Gabriel, who performed a duet version of it on Kate's Xmas
special in 1979.  The 4AD supergroup This Mortal Coil also covered it
on the album *It'll End in Tears*, Liz Frazer doing the vocals.
