Friends, 

Having digital cable radio is kind of nice, especially here in
the musical wasteland of NE Indiana.  Even heard one from TSW.  Anyway,
I record it at night and listen to it at work during the day (Hey, I'm
not selling them or passing them to anyone, so don't case me about
any infringements!  :-) ) and there was this most excellent song
that really hit me.  Being cable radio, there was no way to find
out who it was.  I'm hoping someone will recognize the first few lines.
I have my suspicions ...  Here they are (as far as
translated correctly...):

Guard my soul if you can feel it
Hold me close while I'm still breathing
Don't hide away
Gaze upon the stars at midnight...

Anyone?  Anyone?
Thanks
