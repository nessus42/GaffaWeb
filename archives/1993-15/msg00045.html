>I can't help with your questions but I can tell you about the song
>"Another Day".  Another Day was written (and orginally performed) by Roy
>Harper.

   *Ding*! Correct! Roy Harper did indeed write _Another Day_ 

>I think (and I'm probably wrong) that Kate and Peter performed Another
>Day on UK television (chat?) show once.  I also believe that it was
>recorded for a possible single release but the idea was latter shelved.

    The Kate and Peter version was preformed on Kate's TV special "Kate"
on Dec. 28th, 1979. Kate and Peter were seated at a breakfast table, 
singing their parts, while the picture on the wall was Kate and Peter
acting out the emotional aspects of the song. The special was an hour long
and Peter also did _Here Comes The Flood_. This video can be considered
the cornerstone of a Kate collection after the commercially available 
videotapes.
