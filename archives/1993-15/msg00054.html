I know of two books, both by the English SF author Graham Joyce, which
were inspired by Kate songs. The final chapter of "Dreamside" has the
characters on a frozen lake, with something "under the ice - it's
moving!" - ring any bells to anyone?

The latest book, "Dark Sister" (a witchcrafty, feminist story) was
written after listening to (the author says) a KT track with
blackbirds in (he'd also had a blackbird stare through the window at
him). When I suggested the title was "Waking the Witch", he went
white...

Anyway... Does anyone else know of any KT inspired art/books etc?
