Anyone who cares to (and so affirm her sacred intellectual and emotional
autonomy) is invited to join me this Friday night, April 23, long-distance
vibes-only, for the first Warmroom "Dreaming seance".  Beginning as
precisely as possible at midnight eastern, 11 central, 10 mountain, or 9
on the Aquarian coast, start up your favorite media-format of TD, as loud
as your headphones or neighbors allow.  (I recommend analog audio-tape, as
likely the best sensitivity for vibes. :^)

Listen, as attentively and emotionally as you know how, so that if the
media is, really, at all sensitive, you will be transmitting to it in
*clear synchrony*.  Visualize the many varieties of consciousness and
levels of spiritual compassion sharing this communion across the
continent, or beyond.  Pray for mutual tolerance, reconciliation and
understanding.  And see what happens...

*Feel* ya there...
