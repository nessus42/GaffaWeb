Hello all! 

   A few days ago, I mentioed that I happen to have a copy of a song 
called Just Another Day sung by Kate and Peter Gabriel.  Well, I've 
since been flooded with mail (Thank you all *very* much!) and to wit, 
have learned many things.  Among the trivia is the fact that the song 
was written and originally performed by Roy Harper.  My question to the 
group now is: which Harper album is it on ?  To the best of my ability 
to say, not very many of his albums are available on CDs. (at least not 
available in the USA.)  Actually, now that I think about it, the only 
one I've seen is called "Once".  If it's going to become necessary for 
me to order the CD with "Another Day" from overseas, I'd like to know 
which one it's on first... 



 Also, does anyone have any idea how Kate feels about bootlegs ?  Some 
artists have very strong feelings, others are much more indifferent.  
I'm very curious which side of the coin she falls on (and how 
strongly). 


                       Scott "Eskimo Spy" Frank
