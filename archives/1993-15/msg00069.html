In article <C5rs8G.7vH@umassd.edu> you write:
>Ok. I had asked this quite a while ago.. The Wogan one of Running up that Hill 
>is WHICH one? I know of only two. The dancing/faces one and the sabbath/
>preaching one..

The Wogan performance, which was used as the video by MTV most of the 
time, was what you call the "sabbath/preaching" one.  The real video
was the "dancing/faces" one.

>And hey, I only have MTV since a few weeks ago and aside from the vids
>on 'The whole story' +the RUTH sabbath/preaching one, I have not
>seen any other vids of Kate.

"The Sensual World: The Videos" is a collection of three videos from
the album of the same name.  It is available if you look for it.

Also, there are some videos that were in her earlier collection
"The Single File" that are not in "The Whole Story":  "Them Heavy
People", the original "Wow", "Hammer Horror", "Suspended in Gaffa",
and "There Goes a Tenner".

There are some odd bits for various TV shows, like videos for
"Delius" and "December Will Be Magic Again".
