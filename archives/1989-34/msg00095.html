Woj's recommendation of AREA is seconded by me...  I just picked up
_Between_Purple_and_Pink_ and it's great.  They do have at least one
other record besides _The_Perfect_Dream_ but the title eludes me for
the moment.

A correction - Doctor Death's Volume 3 is not really "just released" -
it has been available since February 1989.  I got mine just a few
months ago, though.

And one more C'est La Mort recommendation - the group Heavenly Bodies.
Apparently it incorporates some ex-Dead Can Dance personnel.  It
sounded very like your typical floaty 4AD kind of thing.  Their track
on Doctor Death's 3 ("Rains On Me") is excellent.
