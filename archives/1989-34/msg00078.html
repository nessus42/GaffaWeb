I'm going home to Winnipeg for a few days, so I'm looking forward to
getting and hearing this album.  I first came across her at previews
for a Toronto musical "The Living Legend" in 1986 (a friend was the
music director for the ill-fated production -- lasted less than a
week).  She had a great voice, but I didn't think of her as rock act
potential.  Her voice was well suited to theatre and cabaret, and I
believe she did make the rounds of piano bars and lounges in T.O.
