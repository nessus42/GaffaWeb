'This Woman's Work' video

From: Kari Haug <kari@sensual.wa.com>
Date: Thu, 21 Dec 89 23:32:28 PST
Subject: 'This Woman's Work' video

						Oslo, 3. Dec. 1989
	Dear Steve,
It's 8 in the night.  I just turned on the TV to watch UK TOP 30.  I
turned it on about 2 minutes too late, they were in the middle of a
Kate Bush video -- "THIS WOMAN'S WORK" !  I saw about 3/4 of it and of
course I freaked completely out.  I'm not exactly sure what was really
going on in that video -- I main in a hospital waiting room, and Kate
(his wife, I guess) was the one who had been grought to hospital.  I
don't know if she was pregnant.  In the last part of the fideo, you
see something -- that man and Kate in a restaurant, suddenly Kate
fainted, dragged the whole table cloth and everything with her ass she
fell off the chair.  The man carried her out to a car, and drove very
fast through the dark, rainy night, I guess he drove right to the
hospital.  I don't really know if Kate died or not, because they cut
the video right before the end, but from what I saw of it, I get the
impression that she (or the baby/or both?) died, but I'm not at all
sure.  I hope to see it again soon, since it has now entered the
UK Top 30 (it was no. 30, new entry, this week) I guess I'll keep
watching the Top 30 in the coming weeks.  I can't believe this -- now
it's commercials on Super Channel -- so I turned over to MTV, right in
the middle of "Running Up That Hill" !!!  You see, MTV is running a
"TOP 100 songs of the 80's" tonight, and "Running Up That Hill" was on
no. 32.  Great, this is the Kate Bush night!  So, what I said to you
on the phone, about "This Woman's Work" being released as single, was
right!!  I think they're very aware of the time of year for that
release, that song has potential to become a really popular Christmas
single!  Oh, yippee, this was really fantastic.  I'll keep you
informed about how it does in the charts from now on.  Yah!
	[some stuff deleted]
I can't think of much more to say, seeing that new kate video (and
also "Running Up That Hill") made me forget everything else!
	[some more stuff deleted]
						Kari
