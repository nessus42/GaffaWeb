By the time you read this PDFM will probably have mailed it,but I'll
send it anyway!
The new uk single 'The Red Shoes' will be released on 5th April backed
with 'You Want Alchemy' and will be available on 7",cassette and CD.
The Cd will also contain 2 other tracks- the video mix of Cloudbusting
and the single mix of This Woman's Work.The following week another CD will be released.The main track will be 'Shoedance', an extended dance
mix.The other 2 tracks on this will be The Big Sky single mix and
RUTH 12".The single in Europe and Australasia will be "Eat The Music"
which should be released on the 5th as well.Canada has already released
'And So Is Love'
Other info:-
Video will be shown on the Chart show on 2nd April.
Kate is appearing on the Larry Adler/Gershwin album celebrating Larry
Adler's 80 th birthday.Kate has already recorded the track 'The Man I
Love',though there is no set release date yet.
Andy Semple
