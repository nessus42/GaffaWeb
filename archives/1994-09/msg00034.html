Tori just finished a live interview at CFNY here in Toronto.  She was asked a
direct question about vocal similarities between herself and Kate Bush.
I don't have time to transcribe that part of the interview but if no one else

(...not that I'm claiming Tori is a ripoff).....well....actually those people
are certainly free to stick to their guns but Tori certainly recognizes some
similarities!
