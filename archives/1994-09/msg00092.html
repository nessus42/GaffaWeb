Hi Cyndy, I can send you a Happy/Ecto FAQ if no one else does. I'd rather
offer first just in case you get 2, 3, 5 others via e-mail :).

Have you seen any Happy CDs for sale in stores near you? An answer to your 
question would pretty much depend on what's available to you, though you
could always mail order directly from Happy herself if you haven't seen
anything in stores.  I suppose I'd recommend _RhodeSongs_ since it's
a compilation and will give you an idea of how she sounds throughout
her career. I love all her albums, so I'm unable to give an unbiased
recommendation between her other albums.

As long as I'm talking about Happy...

For those people interested in Happy but can't, for whatever reason, 
handle the mailing list Ecto, she's on the verge of going into the
studio to record her 7th studio album, and it's due to be released
in August. I'll post again when a release date is set.
