I posted the following message while UUNET was in crush and failed to find it 
on digest. 



Here is a list of Japanese magazines that feature Kate,
excluding disc reviews of tRS and some shorter ones.

Many of them will be of no interest outside Japan because they all 
are in Japanese and many are translations from some original articles.
Photos also seems to be taken from original articles or promotional items.
But I wonder Del's interview may be original to S&R Mag. because
the interviewer is credited "Rittoh Music Europe".
At least it's different from "Future Music" inteview, mainly on production,
composition, guest musicians, recording equipment, of tRS.
If this article turn out to be an original one,
I'll make a summary of the interview.
Can anyone identify this point?

last part of the interview goes something like;
	BJ: "So you [Kate and Del] have no problem in your cooperation."
	DP: "Well, there can be some problems, in fact, when I 
		tryed listening the Hounds of Love recently, I went up to
		the console and remixed some part I didn't like."

Another interest will be on scores of RBG and EtM,
transcriptions from the CD.
I was surprised that RBG is played virtually in one chord only.

--
List:

Keyboard Magazine 1993.12
  - band score of RBG

Keyboard Magazine 1994.3
  - Kate on cover photo (Kate's face in red color)
  - Kate's interview in 4 color pages (translation from VOX)
  - piano and vocal score of MoP (transcribed)

Sound & Recording Magazine 1993.12
  - Kate on cover (fruits on white dress)
  - Del's interview by Brian Jacobs in 4 color pages 

Rockin'on 1994.2
  - article on Kate and the Red Shoes in 4 color pages
	(copyrighted by Simon Reynolds/S.I.N./Orion Press)

Music Magazine 1993.12
  - fetures Kate's history, review of tRS, article on her relationship with 
    musicians of various fields, in 13 pages total
