i had heard word of the impending release of this bootleg cd but was
hesitant to post details until i had something concrete. i still have
not seen a copy of this. the owner of a record store nearby had
informed me that red robin was planning on releasing a cathy demo disc
but he had little solid info and i was a bit surprised that red robin
would release such a disc. red robin is a label that seems to have some
links with folks from georgia in the united states.  the guy who runs
red robin apparently also is friends with peter buck of r.e.m. since
they have released a number of cd bootlegs by them of shows that were
taken from peter's collection of older r.e.m. recordings.
