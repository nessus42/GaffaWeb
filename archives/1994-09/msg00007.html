sarah
=====

....
excited around here. Also, i think it's the next issue of Time Magazine,
but apparently there will be a piece on sarah. someone let me know when you
see it. in canada, 'hold on' has been climbing the much music charts, with
it now up to number 13 on their top 30.

sarah will also be on television twice in the next few days...march 25th on
mtv's alternative nation and on the today show on the 25th, as well. on the
today show she'll be entertaining bryant and katie with a solo version of
"possession." as far as we know, the time is still between 8:30 and 9:00. i
also have just been given word that westwood one record a sarah show last
year and they are broadcasting it this week. different times in different
markets, so check out when and where.

and...melody has done a fantastic update of the sarah discography. it is
available on the bbs, via ftp mail  through the bbs, or you can ask for it
here. it's worth having if you are a sarah fan. thanks, melody. and don't
forget about the sarah mailing list that some people who have nothing to do
with nettwerk started up...you can subscribe to it at:
'fte-request@yoyo.cc.monash.edu.au' include 'subscribe' in the message
body.

rose chronicles
===============

our newest little stars are doing quite well at this time. they have a big
vancouver show lined up on march 27 at the vancouver east cultural centre.
it's an all ages show, as well. the band are also shooting a video at the
end of the month, but the name of the song escapes me. and i'm not in the
office right now. it might be "dwelling". it might be "glide". you don't
expect me to know everything, do you?
