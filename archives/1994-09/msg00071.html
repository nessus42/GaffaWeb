Michael Powell and Emeric Pressburger's film _The Red Shoes_, which as
most know was inspiration for Kate, is going to be on Bravo again
tonight, March 30th at 10pm EST.  (Bravo is a US cable/satellite
station with only one feed, as far as I know, so it's on at 7pm PST.)
The _South Bank Show_ about Michael Powell was on last Sunday and
maybe they'll re-run it this week if you want to try to see it.  The
show is re-run a lot and my guide never says who will be on.
