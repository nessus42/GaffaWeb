On Tuesday February 22, I called the Herbst Theatre in S.F. only 
to be told they sold out within hours of going on sale on 
Sunday the 20th.  I was quite dissapointed, but just to be sure, 
I went to my local Tower Records later that afternoon only to 
discover that tickets were STILL AVAILABLE through BASS, (Bay 
Area Seating Sevice - a local ticket distributor).  I got two 
great (8th row) seats to her show.  I can only guess that you 
(living in Wash.) called the theatre directly and were told the 
same as I; "No more tickets."  You should try calling the BASS 
ticket number in S.F. at (415) 762-BASS.  I just called and they 
said there were still seats available.

As for a second session, I doubt it.
