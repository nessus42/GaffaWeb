FRIDAY THE 25TH HAS FINALLY ARRIVED!

UC Theatre in Berkeley, 9:30 PM for The Line, The Cross, The Curve.

Love-hounds arriving early can meet Karen Newcombe and Chad Williams 
among others at 6:30 PM in front of the theatre.

Love-hounds will stake out territory at 6:45 in the Round Table Pizza 
restaurant directly across from the theatre.  Thanks to Chad for scoping 
out the area this afternoon.  From there we can observe the status of the 
line and also eat (eat?  who can eat on a night like this?)

Those of you driving from Maine, Florida, and San Jose will find there is
little parking.  Chad observed that there is a metered, 24-hour lot behind
the McDonald's near the theatre.  Otherwise it is street parking. 
Remember to pray to Squat for those parking spots! 
                                                                                                                
The theatre has given two contradictory messages regarding when tickets go
on sale: they told me 2:30 PM and told Brad Baker 45 mins. before the
show. 

Mod Lang Records has a flyer available advertising the film.  Anyone
volunteer to stop over there and bring us all a stack? 

See you tomorrow night! 

Karen Newcombe  kln@crl.com

Thanks Chad for the reconnaisance.
