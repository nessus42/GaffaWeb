In article 5@CompuServe.COM, 100276.2176@CompuServe.COM (Marcel Rijs) writes:
> ..._but_ Adam Curry is also the man who started his "career" some
> 10 years ago as John Holden on an Amsterdam illegal radiostation,
> and a few years later, in 1985, interviewed Kate Bush in England
> for Dutch television. The moron wasn't even prepared: he 'forgot'
> that Kate had made not 3 but 4 albums before Hounds of Love, and
> assumed she had only one brother, which is quite stupid.
> I mean, when interviewing someone, you have to prepare yourself,
> right?
> ...

Actually, when introducing MTV's premier of the video for "Love and Anger,"
Mr. Curry brought up this interview and what an impression it had made on him.
He was apparently still relatively new at that sort of thing, and Kate sent a
big Bentley limo, then met him at the gate (to the farm) personally.  He was
quite overwhelmed--or so he gave that impression--and seemed to be quite
taken with Kate herself.

I'm no raging fan of MTV (although The State is very well done), or of Adam
himself, but I think his work on setting-up and maintaining the MTV.com WWW
server has been excellent.  I wouldn't be quite so quick to write him off as
the typical MTV looks-no-thought pinhead.

James
