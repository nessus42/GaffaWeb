Hi Everyone!

I just have to post so I can enthuse about my latest acquisition:
a shiny new set of TWW!  After touring (nearly) all the record
shops in London and being assured it had been deleted, my favourite
shop (The Vinyl Experience, W1) suddenly got a whole load of Kate
stuff, including the Red Shoe Box, signed drawings etc.

My life is now more complete - after losing my rather truncated
tape of B-sides I can now listen to "Not this Time" at full volume
on CD: I love it not for the content but the joyful, all-sing-together
feel which kept me smiling through my final exams (oops, what a
giveaway ...)

If my brother's reading this: NAH NA NA NAH NAH !!
