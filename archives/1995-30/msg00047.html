Hi again,

Yet another time I am asking for your attention to be drawn on "Kate Bush
Covered", the project on and around the Internet I am coordinating. I have
set this project up a few months ago and if I got the info right, some
people around the world are busy recording their covers as we speak.

So what's it all about?

1. You are a fan of (or admirer of, or maniac, or totally in love with) Kate
Bush and her music. 
2. You feel that you are capable of making some music yourself
3. You are determined to cover a Kate Bush song someday (i.e. this year)
4. You are willing to send it to me
5. You have an (analogue) audio tape and a few dollars to spend.
6. You live on Earth.
7. All of the above, yet it concerns not you but someone you know

...if you feel you are resembling at least 4 points of the above, then you
can email me, and be part of the "Kate Bush Covered". I will need at least
20 contributors, so I will keep bugging this list until I have as many! ;-)

I thank you in advance for contributing, and if that is not what you're
planning to do, I thank you for the 60 seconds you spent on reading this.
