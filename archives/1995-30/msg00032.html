So, Kate's building a great big ol' house, eh?  Figures...

Here in the South Bay, we have a great big ol' house called
the Winchester Mystery House.  It was owned by a woman named
Sarah Winchester (widow of the Winchester Rifle guy, had tons
o' bucks because of it); seems she felt guilty about all the
folks being gunned down by the Winchester Rifle.  A psychic
in Boston told her to move to San Jose, California and build
a house, and never stop building.  As a result, it is now
the house with the most rooms in the world (check your book
of World Records if you don't believe me!)

My point?  I'm pretty sure Kate is going to try to out-do
Old Sarah.  I agree with Chris - don't expect any new album
before the millenium is over.
