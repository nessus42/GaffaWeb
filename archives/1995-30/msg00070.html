From: ccsdra@bath.ac.uk (Dave Allum)
Date: Sun, 24 Sep 1995 13:27:49 GMT

WEA will be releasing Enya's long-awaited new album on November 27th (although
this may possibly be brought forward to the 21st).

It is to be called _The_Meaning_Of_Trees_ and will contain eleven new
tracks. The first single, _Anywhere_Is_, will be available from
November 6th.

Many thanks to Martin Craig for this great news which he got directly from
Rob Dickens.
