Hi!

First the good news:

3SAT (German/Austrian/Swiss satellite/cable TV channel) program:

Monday, October 9th, 20:15 - 21:00 (CET):


              Kate Bush: The Line, The Cross and the Curve

              (Videomaerchen, GB 1993
               Gesamtkunstwerk aus Musik, Tanz u. Pantomime)
              [Video fairy tale, GB 1993
               all-in-one work of art made out of music, dance and mime]

[I'm curious wether they'll show the original, the original plus subtitles,
or even a completely translated version...]


And then the even better news:

3SAT program:

Monday, October 9th, 22:30 - 0:00 (CET):


              Bio's Bahnhof

              (Musik und Gespraeche mit Alfred Biolek
               Zu Gast: Kate Bush, Folies Parisiennes, Renate Goebel,
               Hubert und Kaethe Heyne, Vicky Leandros, Udo Lindenberg
               und anderen [SIC! - should say 'andere'])

              (Nostalgischer Rueckblick auf das Jahr 1978: Damals empfing
               Alfred Biolek Stars wie Kate Bush, Udo Lindenberg und Vicky
               Leandros zum Plausch)

              [Music and talk with Alfred Biolek
               Guests: Kate Bush, Folies Parisiennes, Renate Goebel,
               Hubert and Kaethe Heyne, Vicky Leandros, Udo Lindenberg
               and others]

              [Nostalgic look back to the year 1978: at that time Alfred
               Biolek received stars like Kate Bush, Udo Lindenberg and
               Vicky Leandros for a cosy chat]

Remember that this was the very very first TV appearance of Kate Bush
anywhere at all. And let's hope they'll show both titles and the interview
inbetween, because one of the songs (Wuthering Heights) with a part of
the interview was rebroadcast a while ago, but the other song and the
rest of the interview wasn't (as far as I know), so these aren't available
in too good quality.

Bye,
