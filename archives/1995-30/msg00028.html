The Lemon Kittens CDs were rereleased on Biter of Thorpe which is 
probably either her or Karl Blake's own label--no one on the World 
Serpent list seems to know for sure.  It's distributed by World Serpent 
Distribution which is based in London (don't have the address handy right 
now though) but appears to be more popular on the continent and in 
America.  In England, I don't know where to get WSD CDs, but I'd assume 
some place like Tower or a Virgin Megastore would either carry them or be 
able to order them.  For those in America who are reading this, 
Soleilmoon distributes their whole catalog and does mail order.  Their 
email is Soleilmoon@aol.com.  Hope this was of some help!
