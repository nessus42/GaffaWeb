No violent wave crashes here
no thunder from the starry deep
just a silence captured long
in the heart of the poet
who has written you song
gone unanswered, an eternity
of waiting at the edge of ocean
where once you strayed awhile
where often you stayed until
all the words of heaven
ran out into the open sea.
