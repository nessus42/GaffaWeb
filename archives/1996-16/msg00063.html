> Love-Hounds! It seems that KaTe finally gave in my begging!

I can confirm this! Kate is sitting in my living room right now (Chris,
aren't you sorry you're in Kansas City? Makes up for that time that
Kate called you while I was at work!) and she said it's true!

She was especially pleased when I told her that my favorite demos
were "Davy," "Frightened Eyes" and "Disbelieving Angel" because
those are her favorites too. She says they'll be the first three
tracks.

I've been showing Kate all the web pages, and she's very pleased,
though a bit embarrassed. I told her all about the upcoming
(as yet unnamed) Gaffaweb, and she said to say thanks to everyone
involved in the project.

She says (yeah, she's looking over my shoulder) to say a special
hi to |>oug, IED, Uli, Karen, Wieland, Stev0 and Chris (she says she
still watches "The Infant kiss" video quite often when she's
showing it to friends) and she still thinks we're all quite mad
(she's laughing...she'll never live that down) but thanks us
for supporting her.

Vickie (the lucky one...)
