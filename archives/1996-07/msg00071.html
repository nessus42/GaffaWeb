The Kate Shocked Concerts begin at the Kate Bush Web Page. Though 
still experimental (there is a known bug on the Rocket Man Concert 
for PC users (double speed sound)), these Concerts should provide 
the Kate fan with a bit pleasure and wonder.
  As Stev0 has pointed out, the pages at No Dead Trees are 
Netscape-happy, and the Shocked Concerts are no different: You'll 
need Netscape 2.0 and the Shockwave plug-in to view the Concerts. 
They are large -- between 260k and 400k, but we believe they're 
worth it.
  In addition, the site has undergone numerous changes, upgrades and 
updates. More demos, more images and more fun. The main page has 
been reorganized so that it downloads quicker, and many of the 
images have been moved to The Gallery.
  Stop by No Dead Trees, and have fun at the Concert.
