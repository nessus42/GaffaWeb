song so why would she smile?  And how would you like it if someone
dismissed Kate's performance just because of a "dent"?

>A great success for those who think Kurt Cobain was a poet and his

>Why DO people compare Tori to Kate?  Well, there is the

Huh?  A different female artist with a similar voice is what appeals
her to Lovehounds, I guess.  And Kate has attributes that make
Toriphiles love her.  That and she's a strong artistic songwriter and
piano player.  She has a new album out so it's time for the big
discussions.  She's more distinguishly different from Kate than Happy
Rhodes (and we're too awed by Happy Rhode's vocal range to give her a
hard time.  :)

I don't really flame as play around.  But I don't see what's the
necessity of placing Kate and Tori on a hierarchal plane.
