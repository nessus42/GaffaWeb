"Camilla"?  On THE RADIO!!?!?!?  Have you people gone utterly ***DAFT***!?!?
Bad enough this whole obsKuriTies mess, the thingy in "Homeground", etc.,
etc., etc...PLAYING IT ON THE RADIO IS STUPID!!!  Why don't we just
all take out billboards in our home towns proclaiming ownership of illegal
copies of Kate Bush's early demos?  Kansas City, or no if this kind

going to get all self-righteous (and quite rightly so I'm afraid this
time) and get *EVERYBODY* in trouble with EMI.  No matter how good the 
intention, how noble the cause, PLAYING IT ON THE RADIO IS ***STUPID***!!!!!

I'd love to know how that one was finessed in the playlists for BMI
(EMI Music Publishing Limited and Kate Bush Music Limited are both
affiliated with BMI, which collects the money for airplay for them -
BMI charges radio stations and distributes the appropriate royalties
based on the station logs that are turned in by the station).
