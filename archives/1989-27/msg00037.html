Well. the first two albums are pretty obvious, really...on "Never For
Ever," the KT is on the inside, and on "The Dreaming," it is on the front
cover. After "The Dreaming," things start to get tough: The KT's are no
longer out in the open; now they are disguised as other things. On "Hounds
of Love," it's on the back cover. As for "The Sensual World," we decided,
upon examining the CD carton cover, that Angie's initial spotting was a
false alarm; our suspicions now center upon an ambiguous shadow near her
hairline.
