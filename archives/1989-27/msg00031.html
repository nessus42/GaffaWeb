In a recent article, radlein!ray@uunet.UU.NET (Ray Radlein) writes:
>
>Yep - "Happy Christmas" is what mine says....speaking of runout grooves,
>does anyone have a complete list of Kate messages from runout grooves? I
>recall "Life is Love" (or vice-versa) from "Army Dreamers," and "Why don't
>we all breathe the same air - OK?" from "Breathing" (and "Happy Anniversary
>to the P's" [?] from "The Empty Bullring"), but what others are there? I
>also remember that my "Hammersmith Odeon" 7" EP *doesn't* have any
>messages; any reason why not?
>

This is getting further and further astray from the music, but it is
moderately interesting, so I'll fan it a little more:

On _Sat_in_your_Lap_ b/w _Lord_of_the_Reedy_River_ (1981 English 7"),
side A says "Well Done J.B.  1st Dan".  Side B says "Thank You Donovan"
(Donovan wrote LotRR.)

And some slight corrections for _Breathing_ (1980 English 7").  The A side
says simply "WE ALL SHARE THE SAME AIR", and the B side spells "anniversary"
as "ANNIVERARY" (no "s").
