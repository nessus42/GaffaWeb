News, Oct. 30

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)
Date: Mon, 30 Oct 89 10:53:36 PST
Subject: News, Oct. 30


1) On the back page of yesterday's (Oct. 29) Datebook section of
the _San Francisco Chronicle_, Rainbow Records is advertising a
sale.  _The Sensual World_ is one of the featured albums.

2) "Holden Caulfield" of KFJC had his first show today since the
album was released.  In honor of it he played a long Kate set:

"The Sensual World",
"Oh To Be In Love",
"Coffee Homeground",
"Babooshka",
"Suspended In Gaffa",
"Experiment IV",
and
"Love And Anger".

That's it for now, folks.
