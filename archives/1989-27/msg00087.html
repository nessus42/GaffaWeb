I'm not much of a historian, but I got the impression that he wasn't the type.
The "seducing Germany" hypothesis sounds pretty good.  The year makes me
think it actually refers to Hitler, but I doubt it actually refers to a
girl dancing with him and not recognizing him.  More like people
listening to him and being "seduced" by his ideas, and later (after the
war) realizing "who" he really was.
I don't see how it has anything to do with recent mass murderers--it
just doesn't have that kind of feeling to me, and it doesn't have a
resolution that makes any sense.  Dancing with a murderer and realizing
it the next day doesn't make a very Kate song without something more
interesting happening.  With Hitler it sounds more like the kind of
thing She likes to do.
