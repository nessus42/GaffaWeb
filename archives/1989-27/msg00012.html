Ominous

From: Michael Mendelson <mendel@cs.uiuc.edu>
Date: Mon, 30 Oct 89 23:56:46 -0600
Subject: Ominous

Last week I received my oversized KT poster from Kansas City.
It was *well* worth the wait.  I put this baby up on my wall and
it *literally* dominates the entire room!  It's as if Kate
is right there, "watching you without me."  I recommend this
poster to anyone who has been ambivalent about getting it.  
Thankfully, she looks great in this one... certainly much younger
than she really is.  I had a bunch of friends over Saturday night
and we all commiserated over how terrible she looks on the NME cover.
On NME, she looks like a mother!  Weathered and worn.  

Ahhh, the magic of photography...
