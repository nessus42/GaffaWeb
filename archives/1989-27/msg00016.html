I have long since deciphered the "message" in _The_Ninth_Wave_'s
_Watching_You_Without_Me_. I shall submit my evidence for inspection

HEINOUSLY BOLD STATEMENT #3:

_The_Sensual_World_ is a continuation/follow-up/sequel to _The_
_Ninth_Wave_. I list for you just a few of the "similarities" found
in text and image. Most, but not all, references are of course pulled
completely out of context for the sole purpose of supporting this
heinously bold statement and could not possibly withstand even
the most fleeting of scrutiny. Editorial and commentary are
enclosed in [] brackets. _TNW_ references come first; _TSW_
references follow. Tongue held firmly in cheek, I start with the
obvious:
