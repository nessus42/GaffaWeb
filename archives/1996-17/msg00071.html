   It is a re-recording of *exactly* that. This is in the FAQ. Here's the 
relevant part:

    Hounds of Love:
        The beginning ("It's in the trees!  It's coming!") is from the 1957
        UK horror movie "Night of the Demons" (US title: "Curse Of The Demon",
        original title: "Casting The Runes"), though Kate re-recorded the bit,
        because she couldn't get enough quality from the original.

  Something not in the FAQ is the connection that this film continued to have
on Kate's work. As noted above, the working title was "Casting The Runes".
It's been years since I've seen this film, but I recall symbols on paper 
having great power. This concept was incorporated into Kate's film
"The Line, The Cross & The Curve" where Kate's character is tricked into
drawing the three symbols in the title on paper and giving them to Miranda
Richardson's character.

>>I wonder if there are any other MRJ references in the work.
