Hello friends,

today I dreamt two unknown Kate Bush demo songs!! This 
is not a joke. I think this was stimulated by Marcel's 
new demo song titles. 
I dreamt of a tape, which I got from an (unknown!) person 
and I played it to friends the first time. It 
started with the end of an unknown song and I was blown 
away. I couldn't believe it, but it was definitely KaTe's 
voice! Then came Frightened Eyes (I dreamt this song 
exactly as it is, which means it is stored somewhere in 
my brain!) and then came another new song. A very 
haunting melody with KaTe singing like a bird over it. 
VERY beautiful! I have heard this song and it was 
definitely KaTe Bush! Really! 
Then I woke up! :-(
This is the truth. This was my dream! Nobody (including 
me) will ever hear this song again!! Can you believe 
that? 
Oh my..  

Now my question is: Where did this song come from? Is it 
somewhere in the "air" around us? Are there 
KATE BUSH SONGS that even Kate Bush doesn't know? Is 
this possible? 

What do you think? 
What can we do? 

Best wishes
    Wieland
