The American copyright organization BMI has their song database on WWW at
http://bmi.com/. I had a look, but couldn't find anything that isn't on the
official albums. However, some of the songs are listed under multiple titles
or alternative spellings of the title. Another interesting fact is that they
seem to consider Catherine Bush to be KaTe's real name (try a song title
search on any of KaTe's songs).

The British PRS also has a WWW page at http://www.prs.co.uk/, but they don't
have an on-line database.
