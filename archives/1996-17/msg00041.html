It took me a little while to figure out your abbreviations. Call me silly 
but HOL and TSW are on the same album. I like TSW quite a bit but I think 
my favourite is still The Dreaming. I don't like Red Shoes which makes 
this a completely sacreligious comment on this newsgroup. I see that we 
have a lot of artists that we like in common. What's with mr symbol man, 
or should I call him, tafkap? I like Lloyd Cole. Have any recomendations 
on his solo work? I have to agree with you on Amos, liked her earlier 
work. The new stuff does not impress me. Jane Siberry is quite good, have 
her last 4. A local artist is Loreena McKennit. She lives in Stratford 
which is a hop, skip and jump from Waterloo, Canada. She is quite good. 
Her music quite a mixture: mostly harp, Celtic and Arabic rhythms. She 
likes to sing about mystical topics. I recommend her. Enough of my 
ramblings welcome aboard.
I never introduced myself either. As you can see from the bottom, I go to 
school. I have been listening to Kate since her 3rd album which was the 
same time I got in Peter Gabriel. I unfortunately do not have any money 
to invest in memoribilia and only have her studio stuff on cd plus some 
of her videos and interveiws taped off of muchmusic-Canada. I have a live 
ep on record, I think it's called Live at Hammersmith but I can't 
remember. I had to leave my records in Ottawa. I should say that Red 
Shoes is the only exception. I was not moved to buy this one.
Before I go, you should check out Laurie Anderson, she's really cool!
