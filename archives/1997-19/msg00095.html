So tell us what you thought!

I liked it. It's a bit like a Prince ballad with M's falsetto 
and the lush instrumentation (there's even some staccato harp), 
but it's quite beautiful.

And his devotion to Kate is quite obvious -- his intro to the 
song is:

"This next song we're about to do is a song written and produced 
on an album that came out I think seven or eight years ago, an 
artist by the name of Kate Bush. I don't know if you all are 
familiar with her (some applause and shouts) but she is the 
bomb. Truly."

Cool, huh?
