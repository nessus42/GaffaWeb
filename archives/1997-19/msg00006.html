The Homeground 15th/Kate's Birthday thingy
=================================

This is an informal gathering at the Avebury stone circle, Wiltshire,
England
on Sunday 27th July, the nearest Sunday to Kat'e birthday

No organised events, just a chance to meet people and get together.

Meet at the obelisk stone, or rather the concrete pillar that marks where
it was, at 1.00 pm.

See you there.


HOMEGROUND SUBSCRIPTIONS
=========================

HOMEGROUND is the oldest established Kate Bush fanzine now in production.
It was started in 1982 and now, as then, is run by three Kate Bush fans
independently of any official fan organisation or record company. It has
readers in the UK, in most countries of Europe, in the United States and
Canada, South America, in Australia, New Zealand and Japan. Our aim is to
provide a service of news and information about Kate and her music, and 
to provide a platform for review and discussion of Kate's work on a world
-wide basis.

Our aim has been to publish four times a year, supplemented (for 
subscribers) by HOMEGROUND UPDATE issued when there is urgent news.

Subscription rates (inclusive of postage and airmail to distant places
are:

IMPORTANT: Please make all payments to HOMEGROUND FANZINE and in British
currency if at all possible. It is prohibitively expensive for us to deal
with cheques or money orders in non-British currency. If you cannot pay
in British currency please send local currency notes (i.e. dollars, lire,
yen) remembering to include an extra 10% to meet our conversion costs. 
The current US dollar equivalent of a one year subscription is about $28.

Some back issues are available at the following prices inclusive of
