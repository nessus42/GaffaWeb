What's so special about Katemas 1996?  The official unveiling of Gaffaweb!

Gaffaweb will become available on August 3rd at your local Katemas party.  (Time 
to be determined by Bill Wisner.)

Designed with love by Kate's Love Hounds, this site includes many fascinating 
features, including a discography with information on each album and single, a 
videography which strives to distinguish one Wuthering Heights TV appearance 
from another, a dictionary of Kate Bush terms, and many beautiful pages treating 
of varied subjects dear to Kate Bush fans.

Gaffaweb, a work in continual growth, will be an ever-changing resource for Kate 
Bush fans worldwide.  

Prepare for Gaffaweb!  Interfacing to a video terminal near you on August 8th.
