I really like Tears For Fears and have liked them for years and years and
years.  About 6 years ago I bought Sowing the Seeds of Love with a gift
certificate that I got for Christmas.  It sat on my shelf for the first
couple of years without being fully listened too, because I only really
knew one song on it and I already had that EP.  Anyhow, one day I just
decided to listen to it...as background noise.  Then I kept listening to
it, and one day I layed down with my headphones on and listened to it
while I read all the lyrics.  I thought it was so awesome...still do. Now
it's one of my favourite all-time albums...and, I am still discovering
knew things about it...like on vacation a month ago I realised that all
the songs are connected...(took me long enough!)
