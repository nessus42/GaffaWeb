I also with to offer my thanks to the folks who put together and
distributed the Total Tape Integration video.  Being a devout fan of
Kate Bush, I remember reading in _The Garden_ about her various
appearances on TV, and thinking, 'Wouldn't it be great to somehow pop
back in time to see that stuff.'  I was particularly intrigued by her
performing Beatles songs live in Japan, and I also had wondered about
what she said while introducing her film at the London Film Festival
(not much!).  But it was fantastic to see the clips of Rocket Man, the
Man I Love, etc. as well.

It must have taken a hell of a lot of work to bring together those
clips.  And the title animation and interspersed images were absolutely
beautiful!  This video was manna from heaven!

Again, thanks
James
