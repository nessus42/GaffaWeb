And the answer is...YES!

I don't know if it's rare or not...I think it's still in print, 
though I don't see it often (and I CD shop a lot!) But the point 
is, there is a Kate song you don't have...and it's now available 
to you...so what again was the question?

Okay, seriously, the song Brazil is a lovely standard -- far 
more tuneful and moving IMHO than The Man I Love. There are a 
few versions of it on the soundtrack -- and unfortunately Kate's 
was not actually in the otherwise brilliant film. Also 
unfortunately, the production of Kate's version could have been 
a tad better...her voice level is a bit low. I can't remember 
who produced it, but I don't think it was Her Kateness.
