 One of the first things I did when I got myself (*months* ago!) online
 was search for a KT newsgroup. I noticed rec.music.gaffa (wondering
 what exactly "gaffa" meant; my only knowledge of it was as in "Suspended
 in Gaffa") but it was too cryptic for me; I ended up DL-ing some
 newsgroup concerning a heavy metal band called "Bush".

 at night on top of a lonely hill in the middle of nowhere,
 preferably in a cold breeze.)?

 Red Shoes" and its listing is as follows:

	1) Shoedance (The Red Shoes Dance Mix)

	3) Running Up That Hill (12" version)
