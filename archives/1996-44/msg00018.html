Yep, Mr. Rees is most certainly correct.  This brings up an interesting 
idea somewhat related to the Dreaming Plug:

If someone were to pump Kate's CD's through a compressor, some soft, 
difficult to discern, constantly debated snippets of Kate-logic might 
come leaping out at us begging for our attention!  I hadn't realized this 
until the original poster mentioned that Lily's spoken part was much 
louder.  Of course it would be - that's what compression does.  Most 
people don't have access to compressors, so this probably hasn't been 
tried before.  Might be worth a shot just to see what kind of difference 
it makes, if any.
