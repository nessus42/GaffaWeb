Got it!

From: katefans@world.std.com (Chris'n'Vickie of Chicago)
Date: Mon, 5 Nov 90 03:39:14 -0500
Subject: Got it!

Chris went up to the Record Exchange in Evanston (there you go C. Ray)
yesterday and brought home the beautiful Album Box Set! It now has
a very special place on our shelves. Our turntable is broken so we can't
even listen to the rarities, but that can wait till we get the CD set
anyway. More than likely these albums will never get played. The box
will just sit up on the shelf and look pretty. Sigh...that's a lot of
money for something that will do nothing but gather dust...sigh.

Excuse me while I go look at the booklet...the thing that makes it
all worth the price we paid.

OK...I've looked at the booklet and I feel better now!

Talked to Peter Morris yesterday and he said that the album set is
very hard to find in England. One shop he went to only carried the
CD set. He was told that he was the 5th person who had asked for
the albums and that the manager was upset that they'd decided to only
carry the CDs.

Sounds like the albums may very well become true collector's items.
