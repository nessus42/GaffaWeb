the TWW cursor.

	#define tww_x_hot 10
	#define tww_y_hot 17

BTW, the boxed set is amazing. I have no idea how I survived so long
without My Lagan Love and the Metreological Mix of TBS. Sell the
children; if you don't have children sell someone else's or put the cat
to work in the local chinese restaurant. Is that real (as oposed to
sampled) didjeridoo in the opening of TBS? If so who is it, is the
Harris person on _three_ good tracks? At any rate it is amazing turned
up _REALY_ loud, don't do this at home kiddies, Richard has specially
trained stunt flat mates who already think he is round the twist. And
the pictures, lots of images of the second most gorgeous female
hominid known to science...

Sorry, I'm starting to ramble, just put it down to my loosing contact
with the ground.
