Kate in AmigaWorld!

From: greendog@max.physics.sunysb.edu (Michael D Fischer)

Subject: Kate in AmigaWorld!


While flipping through the December issue of AmigaWorld, I saw an
advertisement for a new graphics program... one of the sample
pictures they used to show off the program's graphics was a drawing
of Kate holding a rose, very similar to the _TSW_ cover!  I don't
have the issue here, so I can't tell you who drew it or on what
page it was... the artist's name was mentioned though.  Take a look.
It really surprised me when I saw it!
