Someone who thought that I might be interested mentioned a sort of
Broadway type production called _Riverdance_ that had been released on
video.  It features Irish music and dance.  I bought the video tape and
it is excellent!  The music was composed by Bill Whelan and features
Davy Spillane--both of which have contributed to Kate Bush's music! 
:-)  _Riverdance_ is also available on CD for anyone interested.
