Veering even further off-topic, a British sci-fi novel of the 1950's
was called "Blast Off At Woomera" - but when an American edition was
published it was re-titled "Blast Off at 03:00" - presumably the
publishers felt there was a lack of awareness in the USA as to the
location of the town.
