list member in Hawaii!) and they are so wonderful!  I have just scores of
questions as I watch... I wonder how all these were acquired, and applaud the
effort that must have went into compiling and editing these tapes... wow!

the guitar in the chair with the candle is a tribute to her guitarist that
died? (you probably discussed this ages ago, but I am a newbie - can't help

interview!  She has a lot more patience than I do!

Well, since these were probably discussed ad infinatum when they were first
distributed, I will sign off now.  Thank you again, everyone who worked on
them - they're great!
