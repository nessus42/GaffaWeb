It was "Les Dogs".  The Radio Times described this short film thus - "At the
point of death in a motor accident, businessman Victor relives a ten-year
affair with a beautiful bride", which describes this rather surreal film as
best as anything.  Despite the fact that she says almost nothing in the
entire film, the RT gave Kate top billing.
