Kevin queried -

> Has anyone heard of a song from the seventies called "Mirrors" I discovered 
> it on a Seventies compilation album, and it sounds very much like Kates early 
> stuff, I forget who's singing it, but it's very similar in style.

Yup, "Mirrors" (with its December Will Be Magic Again-foretelling 
percussion) dates from 1978 (what *was* in the ol' aqua that year ?) 
and sits, in truly beauteous company, as track six on Sally Oldfield 
(Mike's sister and erstwhile musical collaborator)'s splendid solo LP 
debut : ~"Water Bearer"~. This *partly* Tolkein-inspired aural tonic, 
showcases an awesome talent, and is currently available as a budget
CD release from Castle Communications.

Anyone who has more information about Sally and her work, I know that 
she is far more prolific than Kate,  (why is it so hard to uncover material ? 
Does she live in Germany ?) ***PLEASE*** get in touch with me...............
