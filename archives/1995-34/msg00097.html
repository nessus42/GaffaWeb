                 HOMEGROUND SUBSCRIPTIONS

HOMEGROUND is the oldest established Kate Bush fanzine now
in production. It was started in 1982 and now, as then, is
run by three Kate Bush fans independently of any official
fan organisation or record company. It has readers in the UK,
in most countries of Europe, in the United States and Canada,
South America, in Australia, New Zealand and Japan. Our aim
is to provide a service of news and information about Kate
and her music, and to provide a platform for review and
discussion of Kate's work on a world-wide basis.

HOMEGROUND is usually published four times a year, and
supplemented (for subscribers) by HOMEGROUND UPDATE issued
on those rare occaisions when there is urgent news.

Okay, enough of that. I see some surprised reactions at my recent
posts. Actually as the old-stagers will know I've been reading this
group in one way or another since shortly after |>oug set it up. At
the begining various people were good enough to send me regular piles
of print-out. When we achieved a modem, about 2 (?) years ago, I
started reading direct, and posted new information as and when it came
to hand. Recent there hasn't been much, and my internet links have
been dodgy, hence no obvious signs of my presence. Within the last
week or so I've mastered offline mail reading and upgraded the modem
from 2400 (on good days) to 9600 (the most my XT will stand without
the coal boiler exploding). I've the chance therefore to join in a
bit.

Things are fraught here at the Towers at the moment. Krys and myself
have been pursuing IVF treatment for infertility, and are just at the
point of seeing if the first course has worked. At the moment it
appears not. Sadly. We shall go round again. Sorry if that stuff is
personal and irrelevant to the list. It's occupying much of our
thinking time at the moment.
