Hello friends,

Maybe there is now some confusion about KBC newsletters and updates?
I differentiate between
KBC-Mags and (actual) Newsletters (updates). I know, the Mags are also called
"Newsletters" and HAD been newsletters in the good old times. These early
Newsletters evolved into high quality colour Mags, but unfortunately the
quality Q and publishing frequence P went Q = k*1/P or Q = k*exp(-P)?. So
far I am aware of 24 magazines (see below), which are called "bible" under
KateFans (tm). To announce real News, the KBC created a new form of
"Newsletters". You send in an SAE + IRC and receive a short photocopied
letter with news, if there are any. At first these were sent out monthly,
but this is no longer the case. Again unfortunately, P goes down but now
without Q rising. I received the last Newsletter in May 1995. It was No.
10, so I'm waiting for No. 11. Maybe Christmas?

I expect, the following list will now appear several times on this list,
but up to now it's not on my news:rec.music.gaffa.
