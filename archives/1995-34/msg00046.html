    Let's look at it this way:  who here thinks of The Red Shoes as Kate's
absolute best work ever?  Seeing as she doesn't really tour, we have
little to base opinions on except for the recorded works themselves.  If
that's what Sarah was basing that statement upon, I'd have to partially
agree.  However, Sarah's statement is admittedly a little too absolute; I
think Kate is evolving, rather than losing her edge.
    As for the "who is she to judge" statement, that's far too subjective
a matter to even speculate upon.  All I can say is, Sarah McLachlan is no
more or less qualified to judge Kate's music than any of the rest of us.
