_The Spiral Dance_ by Starhawk: _Wicca for the Solitary Practitioner_ by 
Scott Cunningham; _Drawing Down the Moon_ by Margot Adler. Um, I don't 
think KaTe's ever been associated with a particular brand of Wicca, or

from a widely-known occult ritual called the Lesser Banishing Ritual of 
the Pentagram. Except, of course, she gets the angels the wrong way round.
