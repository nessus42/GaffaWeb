Hello Lovehounds, and to Ronny, who asked if TLTCTC (The Line, The 
Cross, and The Curve) was worth ordering?

     Order TODAY!  I just got my copy yesterday.  Watched it twice last 
night!  Is Kate a genius or what?!  I was just mesmerized by the film.  
And Miranda Richardson . . . If the movie, Now Voyager is ever redone, 
she's perfect to be the new Mrs. Danvers!  Wow, does she do evil so well! 
 There are so many artistic and clever touches.  Let alone, the "type" of 
film changes all the time, from a seemingly video album in the beginning 
with Kate doing some clever dance steps (with the help of her partner) of 
"Rubberband Girl" and then . . . well, I shouldn't spoil it for you!  You 
have to go out and get a copy!  

      Now, once again.  Can someone explain to me what's wrong with MTV? 
 They should be having a Kate Bush weekend, showing this film, concert 
footage, interviews, and mesmerizing everybody.  She'd make Beavis and 
Butthead silent for once!  But if they ever do show her, it's relegated 
to 120 Minutes, shown once a month, if at all, until the song is way old. 
 Of course, MTV isn't that much nicer to Tori or Sarah Maclachlan or 
anyone of similar nature.

     There were many enigmatic images and symbols shown in TLTCTC.  One 
I'd like to bring into discussion (if it was talked to death, let me 
know.  I can read the archives) was that black bird in the "And So Is 
Love" sequence.  I'll say nothing more so Ronny can get the video and see 
what I'm talking about.
