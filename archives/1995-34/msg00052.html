Homeground may not have known about this house-moving 
development at the outset, but they seem to know more than 
any of us do now -- their cautiously worded confirmation of
the report is testimony to their unique position, and the
almost certainly sensitive implications which probably attend
that position.  What's more to the point, the information 
they do relay can be relied on -- they are a highly reputable 
news source for all matters Kate Bushological, and for this
virtue (as for their many others) they deserve our praise and respect.
