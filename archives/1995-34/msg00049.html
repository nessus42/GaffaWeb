  Hello, Just a quick note to newer KaTe collectors:*Beware!*
  I once sent off for a boot vinyl of "LIVE KATE OUTTAKES: 1986".  It was 
the songs from Hammersmith-odion that weren't on ON STAGE and was 
*pressed* in 1986.  I sent for ANGELS ON EARTH "Kate and Peter G Live in 
Europe 1980".  It was the soundtrack to the x-mas special. Britain in 
Europe and they lied about the date.  About 10 years ago I sent $20 to 
get a vid tape of x-mas and 'chat' show stuff.  It was a tape of 95% of 
the vol. 1  Love-Hounds Collection  but about 4 gen and fuzzy and w/ no 
documentation.  I also got a fairly cheap 7" red vinyl LIVE IN JAPAN that 
has a great cover, so *sometimes* ads can be a good source.  
  Caveat emptor, y'all!
