I can't say Never Be Mine is her best song, but it does have a very special
place to me
"Their setting fire to the cornfields as you're taking me home" is a scene
that I think would be perfect for a movie, especially considering the very
dramatic images I've seen after harvest in Illinois where there is a lot of
burning (reminds my especially of sunset in this situation). I've even
centered part of a story I've written around that sensation, the time I got
to witness this was a few years ago. I was on my way up to my sister's
farm. Coming north from Florida means a drastic climate change that
reminded me that it was Autumn. Something about that first evening that
feels like autumn...I dunno, it effects me deeply.

Ok, I know that wasn't the question that was asked, and I wasn't the person
it was directed at, but there it is.

I need to go take my medicine anyway
