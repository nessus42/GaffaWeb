Heather Russell wrote:
> 
> >What single line of a Kate song (vocally) has caused the most intense reaction? The biggest chill to roll through you as if  threatening never to let go.
> 

For me it's in "All the Love", at 3:21 into the track, when she sings
the line "All the love" for the last time right before the ansaphone
messages start.  She takes the vowel in "love" up, and then finishes
with this heartwrenching descending glissando.  It always gives me a
twinge right behind my solar plexus.

But then, that whole song always wrecks me.
