very subjective opinions.  When you amplify them by being Passionate about
Music, it's silly to expect anyone else to agree with you, and wonderful
when they do.
   People who feel the way you do about Kate Bush are rare, as they should
be.  Something unquantifyable, yet desirable, would be lost if they were
commonplace.
