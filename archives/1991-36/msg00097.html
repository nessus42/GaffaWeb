Chico Hablas is a complete screaming guitar monster when he's working
with Yello, which is weird because his solo stuff is almost unbearably
new-agey (well, unbearable if you spin up the CD expecting 45 minutes
of "Si Senor The Hairy Grill" -- it's actually quite good otherwise :-).

Now Carlos Peron, on the other hand . . . he's quite the Boris-wannabe
on GOLD FOR IRON but he's pretty good at it.

Listening to BABY on a $40,000 stereo system is about as close to touching
the face of God as you can get without dying.  The Yello sound survives
closer scrutiny than any sound I've ever heard, ever . . . yes, Boris is
a master of the mixing desk.  How the fuck does he *do* that?
