That said, let me try to explain why I made the post in the first
place.  The synthesizer is a special instrument. It can be used as an
instrument in its own right, or it can be used as a substitute for
others. Some people view it as a goal in itself to use synthesizers
for everything. I.e. I have a sampler where the blurb extols the
natural sound of the synthesized guitars, drums and saxophones. On the
other hand, there are some who regard the syntesizer as evil
regardless of how it is put to use (heavy metal fans often come in
this category). When I saw Vicky's post, this black-white view of
synthesizers sort of clicked in. Yeah, I know, thinking that Vicky
should be thinking along either of these lines is silly, knowing how
broadminded Vicky is. Sorry about that.

I'll admit one other thing: I have a record with Altiplano (Chilenean
group, plays a lot of flutes etc.). On one of the songs they use all
sorts of weird instruments to make the sound of rain, wind, and
bird-calls. While I don't think my enjoyment of their music would be
any less if they used a synthesizer, my admiration for the production
would be considerably less.
