Hmm...my recommendation for most "etheral" jane tune would be 
"Vladimir, Vladimir" off of The Speckless Sky.  What I'd recommend you starting
with is either TSS or The Walking...neither of them are etheral all the way
through (ala enya or sarah m) but both are amazing works.  the walking is the

give them a chance you'll likely want the whole set (there are 5 currently, 
with a sixth do out shortly after the new year. :) :) :))
