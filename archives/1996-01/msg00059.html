Experiment IV

http://www.aitec.edu.au/ExpIV/

This used to be the best Kate page by far.  WordStar also used to be
the best word processor.  This is what's known in the biz as a
"cobweb site".  It hasn't been updated since late July, and it looks like
it's going to updated next right around the same time Kate tours again.

It has a nice HTML FAQ - going back to May of '94.  It has a couple
of out-of-date links, and a rather unintentionally funny page about
an aborted Lovehounds T-shirt project (before one was actually
made).  And, quite frankly, it doesn't have much else.

God knows its heart is in the right place, and if it were updated, it would
still 
be THE source for the Serious Web-Browsing KateFan, but as of now, it's 
more of a curiosity for people interested in Lovehounds history (and there's 
even a better source for THAT!)
