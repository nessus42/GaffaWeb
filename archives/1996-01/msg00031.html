About the album-engravings.. Well, quite a lot of Kate's original records
have these engraved messages.. Like:

Wuthering Heights - 'Remember the whales'
Man With The Child - 'The child hides in the light'
Hammer Horror - 'We're all playing a hunch'
Breathing - 'We all share the same air'
The Empty Bullring - 'Happy anniversary to the p's'
Army Dreamers - 'Life is to love'
December - 'Happy Christmas'
Sat in your lap - 'Well done JB 1st dan'
Lord Of The Reedy River - 'Thank you Donovan'
The Dreaming - 'For Rolf'
Cloudbusting - 'For Peeps'
Hounds of love - 'Woof'

The Kick Inside - 'Remember yourself'
Lionheart - 'Hope you like it'

    These are just a few I noticed.. I wonder if anyone knows of any other
etchings? This "up yours, ugly"-thing has me curious, though.. Just who is
ugly and what did he do?
