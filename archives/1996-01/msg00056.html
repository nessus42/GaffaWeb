Eugene, it's correct, there is ONLY 5 copies.  I managed to get my
hands on 2 of the five from Rockaway Records about 6 years ago.  What
they are are 5 different splattered (multicolored) discs without an
photo LP sleeve.  These were strictly promotional.  The 9 tracks on the
LPs are music by Jimi Hendrix.  The only thing in relation to Kate is
the LP label of "The Dreaming"...They each cost me $250.00. 
Unfortunately, they have been sold to 2 die-hard collectors in the
Pennsylvania and New York areas when I was in financial dire straits. 
In the Illustrated Collectors guide, some of the material were from my
collection that I sold to Tom Richards of Clearwater, Fl.

All I can say is that at least had the pleasure of owning some of the
rarest Kate collectibles around.

And if you ever order rare collectibles by mail/UPS or sell by
mail...be sure to insure your merchandise and have proof of value.  UPS
lost my order for the Yugoslavian "The Kick Inside" LP, and that
hurt..$125 down the drain.

Good Luck on your collecting.
