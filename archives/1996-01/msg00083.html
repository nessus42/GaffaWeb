Called "A Lovehounds Page", brought to us by David Benson.

http://www.acy.digex.net/~dobenson/kate.html

There are pages out there that have more info, and there are
pages (most notably "Lionheart", with its stills from the "Rocket
Man" video, among other things) that have images this one
doesn't.

But in terms of sheer estetics, this page can not be beat.
Dozens of beautiful images.  Dozens and dozens of them
of Our Muse.  Normally, I am one who likes female vocalists
for their voice; whether or not they are physically attractive
is entirely irrelevant.  But even I have to admit some of these
pictures - I have NO idea where David got some of them - are
quite stunning.

Also, if you look hard enough, you can find a very-recently
started under construction site here where he's adding lyrics
(including the DEMOS!), and another HTMLized version of
The Garden.  Like I said, this is already the best LOOKING
Kate Site on The Web; once everything is in place, this very
well could be the Best Site on The Web, period.

The ONLY complaint - and it's rather minor - I have is that
there should be better names for the pages (instead of "Kate1",
"Kate2") with uninformative names like six "Another Kate Page"
links (for example, if I were labeling them, I would call the links
"Kate live at the BBC"; "Images from the Tour of Life Booklet", 
etc.)  But this is the most consistantly worked on Kate Site,
always improving, so THAT will probably be fixed in the Very Near
Future, too.

Oh, one other warning:  This site is VERY Netscape-happy, but
don't let that scare you.
