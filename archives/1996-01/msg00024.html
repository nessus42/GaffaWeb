Looking at the video list where this occurs I see there are two:
	The Lovehounds Collection	TLHC
	The Collection (a bootleg)	TC
I don't own "TC", but "TLHC" has a recording of her from the ABBA
Winter Snowtime Special broadcast 22 Dec 79 (BBC1), recorded 18 Feb 79,
I understand.

THLC is a most excellent collection, it is 2 2hr tapes, and for the
price of postage and a tape, or something good to trade, you should be
able to get from a local friendly LoveHound. I see you're in the UK.
Sorry, I'm in MN, USA. Just a little impractical.
Watch out on video copies, the quality falls off rapidly, so if you can
contact someone who has as close to master copy, you'll be better off.

Credits to TLHC:

a blank tape. I mean WELL worth it, although only the true hardened
fan would repeatly see the uneditted Night Flight Interview.
