Kuyper Hoffman <kuyper@iplan.co.za> wrote:

> One other good thing I got out of this is EMI's catalogue number for the
> album, they have it as "EMI EMD 1047".  I am not very familiar with the
> numbering scheme, what would the EMD mean, my RbG CD single is CDEM280,
> just as Scott Telford said it would be.

EMI UK adapted this style of catalog number in 1989, though there are
probably still "rogue" releases that do it differently.  12" singles are
"12EM <x>", CD-singles are "CDEM <x>", and CD albums are "EMD <x>".
Parlophone has its own scheme that *seems* to be continuously-numbered back
to the early '80s ("12R <x>", etc).
