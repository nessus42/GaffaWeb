Hi!

While working on my KaTe discography again I found an item that's more weird
than any other item in my colleKTion. I do have a copy of the "don't give up"
UK poster sleeve 7" single. It was sealed until 10 minutes ago. I carefully
opened it (it's still virgin vinyl, though 're-sealed' with the sticker and the
plastic bag it came in) and to my very big surprise I found TWO vinyl singles
in there. Two copies of the single in one sleeve. They still sticked together
a bit, so I presume I was the first one who broke the seal... Now who's going to 

believe me this? Has anyone else found this kind of surprise?

Bye,
