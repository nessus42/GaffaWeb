This type of question came up in alt.fan.frank-zappa a few months
ago, with a twist: it was phrased as "what do you usually have in
you CD player besides FZ?" That was easily answered.  Now, my
problem here is that I have no Kate Bush on CD, and I hardly ever
listen to LPs anymore (the last three records I listened to WERE
Kate, though), which is why I'm desperately seeking the boxed set.
By the way, I want to thank those people who responded to my post
yesterday ... I now know what to look for, and that there is not a
new set out, just a re-release of the old one, which I will soon
have IhopeIhopeIhope.  Anyway, here is what I listen to besides
Kate.  Beyond the first two, no order of preference, so I'll go
alphabetically:

David Bowie        -- almost always ...
