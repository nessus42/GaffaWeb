I talked to Peter Morris (in Kent, England) on the phone today.  He
runs "Homeground: The International Kate Bush Fanzine".  Joe Turner's
"friend" who claims that Kate performed "Sat In Your Lap" and "The Big
Sky" at the Saturday Comic Relief show has his head up Madonna's
wazoo!  She didn't.  Every night she performed "Breathing" alone at an
electric piano, and sung in the comedy duet.  The Comic Relief show
broadcast on TV did not contain "Breathing", but it did contain the
duet.  The Comic Relief record does in fact contain both "Breathing"
and the duet.

Peter also told me how he got to be in the video for "The Big Sky".
They called him up to ask him if he could get together 200 fans who
would like to be in the video and play an audience.  Peter and David
Cross (another "Homeground" editor) and someone else got to play
airoplane pilots in the video!  Kate directed the whole thing.
