Subject: Cocteau Twins (who else?) on CD

Newest acquisition:

	Cocteau Twins	"Treasure"	CAD 412 CD

Slight hiss, and the fade-in to "Ivo" seems a bit sudden, but maybe that's
only because I had the volume up so much :-)   Also maybe a bit trebly.

My record-shopping companion picked up the Bauhaus collection, so I'll report
his impressions sometime.

@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@@

I've put "Victorialand" away and am now only listening to it once every few
days.  It leaves me in a very wrung-out, spacey mood ... like coming down
from a trip.  Call me weird, but I felt the album was becoming dangerous.
Not that it isn't stupendous, but like some other things it should be done
only rarely and with forethought.
