Subject: Today, V66 -- tomorrow, zee vorld!!

As I was sitting down to a leisurely late dinner (~10:30pm), I flicked on
WVJV-TV, channel 66 in Boston. I expected some cheap rock videos to help me
digest my food, but what did I get?

A HALF-HOUR OF KATE BUSH VIDEOS!!

Yes, beleive it or die, they showed:

	Running Up That Hill (real version)
	The Man with the Child in his Eyes
	Cloudbusting
	Hounds of Love
	Sat In Your Lap (British version w/lips in sync)

Weird, eh? Almost as strange as seeing the video for "Moments in Love" on a
cable station at 1:00am...
