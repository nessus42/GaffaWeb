 Hi all,

 It's too hard to determine the most favorite songs of Kate Bush.
 The following is my choice. But I think,if I choose the most favorite next 
month,or have choosed last month, the choice would be somewhat different.

(1)"Moving":this is the first Kate's song  I listened to almost twenty years ago.
(2)"Cloudbusting":I was impressed by the video featuring short-haired Kate and 
Eliot Gould. The tune is elegant. And as for lyrics, there're some attractive
enigma for me.
(3)"Dreaming":I feel aweful. It's somewhat sharmanistic.
(4)"Army Dreamers":It represent the cynical and socio-critical side of Kate.
(5)"Breathing":I found in this tune somewhat sexual connotations.
(6)"The Morning Fog":Everytime I get low, I listen to it. It gives me some
positivity.
(7)"Babooshka":It tells me a form of extreme human communication.
(8)"Kite"
(9)"Don't Give up": This is not a song by Kate. But first time I heard this
tune, I found a new aspect of Kate(her approach to the blues).
(10)"Eat the Music":I like Brazilian music!
