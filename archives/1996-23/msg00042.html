Yesterday, the director of a play I'm in, wanted to do some improvisation
so that we could get into character.  However, he needed some music (he
didn't have any with him at the time).  "Ah-ha", I thought.  "Here is my
chance!"  I grabbed my pesonal stereo and produced forth The Red Shoes.
"Oh yeah", he said "I like Kate Bush too!"
The entire cast of the play ended up doing an hours worth of inprovisation
with The Red Shoes playing in the background.  Great!

Okay, here are my favourite songs:-             and my favourite albums:-

(1) Hello Earth                               (1) Hounds of Love
(2) And Dream of Sheep                        (2) Never for Ever
(3) Egypt                                     (3) Lionheart
(4) Fullhouse                                 (4) The Sensual World
(5) Be Kind to My Mistakes                    (5) The Red Shoes
                                              (6) The Dreaming
(6) The Wedding List                          (7) The Kick Inside
(7) Top of the City                           (8) The Whole Story
(8) Kashka From Baghdad
(9) You're the One
(10) Get Out of My House

Don't ask me why I like them.  I just think they're great.

Dave

P.S  I used to love "Moments of Pleasure" and then one day it just really
got on my nerves.  Whenever I listen to it now, it grates on me.
