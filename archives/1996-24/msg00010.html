I love best and worst lists, but think I'm failing the quiz here--
can't seem to cram 5 into either category.  My best try--
1.  Cloudbusting--the first Kate song for me, and I loved the video.
We in Arizona have a soft spot for Wilhelm Reich, too--he was concerned
about groundwater pollution in Tucson 30 years before anybody else (
the military was flying planes thru radioactive clouds following bomb
tests, then washing the planes in Tucson, letting the watter trickle
into the ground).
2.  Love and Anger--when I was really, really depressed a few years
ago, it just kept rattling thru my head (if you can't tell your
sister, if you can't tell a priest, can you tell it to your heart)
till I got help.
3.  The 9th Wave.  All of it.  (see? I can't follow the rules on this)
4.  The Dreaming.
4.  The Dreaming (if I did that twice it was my usenet reader program,
sorry.  Because "the pull of the bush" is something we have around here
too--the native spiritual connection to the land, the desert).
5.  Them Heavy People
6.  The Kick Inside

Worst:
1.  Between a Man and a Woman
2. Heads We're Dancing
3. Top of the City
4.  Why should I love you
5.  You're the One
(hey!  I found five!  not fair, though, I'd should give "Lionheart"
another listen...)

cheers,
Kelley
