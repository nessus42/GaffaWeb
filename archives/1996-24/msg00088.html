Actually, it's only moderated so that the rec.music.gaffa newsgroup can
be gatewayed to the Love-Hounds mailing list, ie. postings to the
newsgroup are forwarded to the mailing list, and messages to the mailing
list are posted to the newsgroup. No actual moderation happens. I'm not
aware of any other newsgroups that work like this, but r.m.g/L-H has
been like this since, ooh, way back.
