G'day all!

Graham here coming out of lurk mode. Well tonight was great fun. The 
national Australian youth radio network Triple J did a THREE HOUR radio 
special on KaTe! 8-) Lots of cool KaTe music, snippets of interviews over 
the years. A quiz (I got most of the questions right!! I wish I was on 
it). I did get to talk live for a few mins about my own KaTe experiences 
as did Richard Frost about his KaTe web site. They even played KaTe's NEW 
song!! 8-) from the Common Ground album.

There was an interesting story from one person about KaTe shopping in 
Harods with Enya!? Did anyone else hear it? What was it all about??
