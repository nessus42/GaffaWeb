From: john@tessi.com (John Zimmer)

Subject: latest Clannad album finally out

Clannad's _Anam_ was finally released here on Tuesday.  It includes 
(unnecessarily, imho) "Harry's Game" -- "from the motion picture _Patriot 
Games_" -- and the Bono duet from _Macalla_, "In a Lifetime".  I can only 
figure the beancounters in charge made the decision to include them, since 
these are readily available on previous albums.  Were these songs on the 
UK/European versions?

The rest of the album is new material.  I've only heard part of it so far, 
but that has been quite good.  More when I've listened to the rest.
