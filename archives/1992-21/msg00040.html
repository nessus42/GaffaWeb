>
>        Anybody who understands what this means should let us know!

   According to our informants, Madame Maria Nanky, Enrico Baratta
(whose activities were chronicled in the KBC newsletter) and others were
charactors invented by Kate and her brother Paddy. It's an in-joke by Kate.
She probably wrote a song called "Poor Old Flea" (an undiscovered demo)
long ago and did an awful version of it and blamed Madame Nanky.

 
>        _Wuthering_Heights_ by Jah Wobble (supposed to be crap!!). 

    The artist is "_Jah Wertzel_ (Morgan Fisher)" *not* Jah Wobble, and
it's _not_ crap, just very, very strange. Imagine a bizarrely accented 
man's voice singing:

   AOW-hut on the WHILE-E, WhinD-e M'whores
   W'he'd ROLL 'n fael in Ghheenn.

   ...over a backing track played by musicians sight-reading sixth
generation xeroxes of the sheet music, on the insturments they have never
played before.

>        And there was an abysmal Reggae-inflected _Wuthering_Heights_ 
> around 1981, group unknown by IED, and not recommended.  (May be the same
> as the one above).

    I don't know what to call it, but it's not Reggae.
