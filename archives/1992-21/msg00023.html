...is now out. Nothing too exciting (but a very nice cover).
Apparently two different covers of "The man with the child in his
eyes" were released in May by a rave outfit called Inside Moves and
somebody called Brigid Boden. As can be expected, both are said to be
awful.

Rumours of Nige Kennedy and the Trio working on KB7. (It's
beginning to sound like TSW all over again!).

The Hair of the Hound video, Lionheart on vinyl, and the vinyl and
cassette editions of This Woman's Work have been deleted.

Also a report on the Homeground 10th Birthday party. Nev & Jon went,
but KaTe didn't (whew 8^), though she sent a message and some signed
merchandise for prizes.
