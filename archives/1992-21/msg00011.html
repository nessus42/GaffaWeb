a non-whispy Suzanne Vega.  She also writes music in a slightly Siberry-esque 
fashion.

I bought her albums because I thought she was Anna Pigalle, who I played a
few times years ago when I had a radio show (KDVS, Davis, CA).  It was
immediately obvious that I had not found the right "Anna", but I'm happy
with my find nonetheless.
