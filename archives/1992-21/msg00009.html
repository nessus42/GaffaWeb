Paul Gillingwater wrote:                                                       
>A pleasant surprise here in NZ.  HER voice was heard singing ``don't          
>give up on us... etc'' as part of a TV ad promoting the United Nations        
>High Commission for Refugees.                                                 
>                                                                              
>I believe this was taken from a duet sung with some male voice (whose

I have seen the same thing here in the States.  I think it was                 
specifically in Florida.  It was a PSA for a runaway hotline and the           
line Kate was singing was from "Don't Give Up".                                
                                                                               
"When times get rough, you can fall back on us,                                
 Don't give up.  Please don't give up."                                        
                                                                               
As long as it takes to sing that is about how long the PSA was.  It had        
the hotline's number superimposed over an image of a runaway cowering in       
a dark alley on a very rainy night.                                            
                                                                               
It really freaked me out the first time I heard it.  Never thought I           
would hear her voice on TV aside from videos.
