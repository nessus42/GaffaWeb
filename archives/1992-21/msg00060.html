			KaTemas at Wollongong, Australia.
			--------------------------------

What follows is the text from the note I sent around my Lab.
-------------------------------------

Yes, we'll be celebrating KaTemas (Kate Bush's birthday) again this year. 
As good as excuse as any for people who like nice music to stuff themselves 
silly and get pleasantly sozzled whilst listening to it.

Where: Stephen's house - 2/49 Gilmore St. West Wollongong.
Phone 263778.

When: Saturday the 1st of August, from lunchtime (1 PM) 'til whenever??? 
(KaTe's birthday is actually Thursday July 30th).

A BBQ will be provided for people who wish to burn things and attempt to 
eat them. I'll provide some soft drink and juice - anything alcoholic 
you bring yourself. Also, bring along any sort of interesting munchies.

Music will be provided - between Graham and I we have enough Kate, Tori, 
Sinead etc to survive - but if you think you have some nice music we 
haven't heard before - bring that too. Last years hit was Yothu Yindi! 
Special treat this year is a video from the 1990 KT convention as well as 
photos from Tori Amos' gig in Sydney a few months back.

Feel free to bring lots of friends along (although telling me how many 
beforehand could be useful - but not really necessary).

See yas there. Stephen.
