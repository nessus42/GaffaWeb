Is anyone else bothered by EMI suddenly acting like one of those bootleg 
outfits?  You know, the ones who issue the same recordings over and over 
in different packages trying to cash in on Kate?  

While I think TRS CD Single is lovely, I think Kate must have a stockpile 
of songs other than previously released ones they can draw on.  Obvious 
attempt to promote other albums in catalogue.

And splitting the two versions of TRS into two CDs in two separate weeks 
REALLY bugs me.  I work in marketing and don't like to see this sort of 
cheesy crap associated with God and Her Music!

Whew!  There, I feel better now . . . but you can bet I'll write to EMI 
and KBC and gently express my discomfort . . .
