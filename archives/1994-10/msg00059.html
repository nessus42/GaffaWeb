                      -------------------
                           Tori Amos
                      Bootleg Discography
                      -------------------

This is a discography listing all available CD bootlegs
By Tori Amos. Most bootlegs are live recordings, usually
acquired by various illegitimate means or radio 
broadcasts. Because I don't have a definitive concert
list for Tori, I am assembling this discography in 
alphabetical order by name. If I am able to get a
tour itenerary, I will reassemble this discography to
respect the chronological order in which the shows were
performed.

This is the first version, which will just include track
listings as they appear on the CD. Later updated versions
will include reviews of the quality of the CD, track
listings with corrections to truly reflect what is on the
CD, and times of songs, as well as total times.

If You have any CDs that are not included here, please
e-mail me at fuzzbox@world.std.com with all relevant
information. Also e-mail me if you have any other 
questions about any of these CDs.
                                    -Jason
