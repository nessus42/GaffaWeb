common knowledge, but.. let's see, shall we?
 
I just discovered that Kate is one of the guest artists on
a collection of Gershwin tracks to be issued in June.

artists of interest contributing to the album. It seems to
be a project of Larry Adler, the mouth-organ player
(definitely not harmonica, he's most insistent on that point)
with George Martin producing.
