Got the second part of the TRS single today (anybody know when it was
supposed to be released? Monday?). The cover has a new b&w photo of
Kate against a fuzzy background of rather odd male faces. Strangely,
the catalogue number is the same as part 1: CDEM316. I don't think
anybody's mentioned the video yet: it looks like a straight segment of
TLTCTC starting with Miranda Richardson's character offering the shoes
to Kate - as usual The Chart Show cut it off in the middle, just after
Kate bursts through the mirror. Probably the most appealing of the
three videos IMHO.
