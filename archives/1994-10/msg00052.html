Or at least it is if you live in the Bay Area (again!?!) or Boston.
I got this information from America On-Line (Yes, I'm a member and no,
I'm not ashamed of that fact!).

The following is the un-edited message left in the Kate Bush folder there;
where the person writing it got their information, I don't know, so I
don't know how true it is, so someone out there may want to positively
confirm/deny this:

---------------------START OF MESSAGE----------------------------------


Subj:  The Line, The Cross & The Curve
Date:  94-04-05 02:34:12 EDT
From:  BraddyBrad

I'm glad to see that Kate's folder is BACK.

This is the update on Kate:

1. The film, "The Line, The Cross & The Curve" will be showing on April 22-23
(make sure to verify dates) at the University Theater in Berkeley, CA.  It
will be showing with Laurie Anderson's film, "Home of the Brave."

2.  "The Line, The Cross & The Curve" has been accepted at the Boston
International 
Women's Film Festival, and will be showing on April 29th at the Brattle
Theater in Boston at 10:00 PM.

3. The Florida Film Festival is interested in Kate's film, and they will
probably show it during their festival.  The date is sometime in June, to be
determined.

4. I believe the record company is arranging some other screenings, but they
are not 100% set yet.

---------------------------- END OF MESSAGE ------------------------------

TLTCTC with Home of the Brave?!?  Wow!  I'm there!

Also, I think I'll correct the author of this - there is no "and" in the
film title (I was making that mistake awhile before someone corrected me,
too).
