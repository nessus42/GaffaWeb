I live in Sacramento, so I don't really know much about Davis stores.  Tower
may be selling it for less, but I don't know for sure.  Might want to
give them a call.  The Beat will be showing it at 6:30 and 8:00 pm.  
Suppossed to be on a "big screen" TV.  There will also (according to 
a flyer I got today from them) be "Kate Bush UK rarities.  Import item
door prizes."  (I assume that means they are giving away an import CD
single or two, but I'm not sure of what the door prizes are.)  The Beat
is a fairly strong supporter of Kate in general - they always have several
Kate rareties in stock, and seem to really like her music.

(There is a bus that goes to Sac. [the 42] and it's only a dollar one way
and let's off within reasonable walking distance of the Beat - well, 
reasonable for me at least.)
