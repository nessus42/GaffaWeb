Karen L. Newcombe <kln@crl.com> writes (about Saturday night's Euro VH-1 KB 
interview):

> Peter Manchester, don't you have some kind of airwave swallowing radar
> dish that can pick this thing up?

Don't I wish!  No, I am not satisfactorily ambient in electromagnetic space.  
In 1985 I rigged up what I called my "Kate Bush Observatory," an rf amplified 
UHF TV antenna that allowed me to pick up the late great U-68 from New Jersey 
(this was an MTV alternative that played a lot of world music video, including 
all of the HoL KB videos immediately upon release), but I am not presently in 
orbit.

I fear we are dependent on our esteemed colleagues in the UK and Europe to 
cue up tape and capture this thing for some future tapetree.  I sorrow to say 
in addition that I'll bet this is nothing new.  But I would share your joy--and 
frustration--it indeed it is a new interview, and all we can do is hear about 
it.
