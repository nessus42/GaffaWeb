west coast Katemas party a few years back IED obtained many laughs at
my expense with a creative presentation of my interview with Kate.

interview with Kate, are utterly beyond even the slightest reproach,
it can easily be seen that such events could happen to others, who no
matter how virtuous and lacking in foibles, could never reach the
incredible standards set by yours truly, and are thus infinitely more
attractive targets for humor's pointed arrows.
