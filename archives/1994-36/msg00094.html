Hi..
Sorry if this has already been mentioned, but Kate was featured on the
Sound FX program (on the FX network) last Thursday night.  They have a
segment on the show called "Viewer's Choice" where people write in to the
show and recommend music that they like.  Occasionally, they phone the
people who write in and talk to them live on the air.  This is what
happened Thursday.

Anyway, someone had written in and recommended Kate.  They played the "Red
Shoes" video (!!!) and then talked to the guy who wrote in to the show. 
He was very enthusiastic about Kate and said that she is the greatest
artist that has ever recorded!  

It was great hearing someone rave about Kate on national television! 
Maybe it will prompt some people out there to give Kate a listen...

Charles
