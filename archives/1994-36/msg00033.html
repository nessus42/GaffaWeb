Free TLTCTC screening in Encinitas, CA.

From: Lisa Arzt <Lisa_Arzt@sonymusic.com>
Date: 1 Nov 94 13:25:00 EDT
Subject: Free TLTCTC screening in Encinitas, CA.
To: Love-Hounds <Love-Hounds@uunet.UU.NET>
Content-Type: Text/Plain

Hi all!

There is another FREE screening of TLTCTC happening that I'm happy to pass on 
information about!

It's this Saturday, Nov. 5th, in Encinitas (forgive me if this is spelled 
wrong), which I'm told is about 20 miles north of San Diego. It's at the La 
Paloma theater, and is at 9:30 pm. It's free for everyone who comes, and I'm 
told there will be giveaways happening, as well as an opportunity to buy the 
video of TLTCTC at a "special sale price" at this screening, courtesy of Lou's 
Records. There should be an ad running in the San Diego Reader this week if you 
want more details. Look for Ernest from Sony & say hi if you go by.

Ernie is setting up more CA. screenings, and will pass on that information as 
well when they're set, so stay tuned for further details!

Lisa
