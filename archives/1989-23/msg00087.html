KT interviews on radio/TV

Subject: KT interviews on radio/TV

I stumbled across Kate on the radio on Saturday.  I unfortunately missed the
beginning of the interview (did any other UK love-hounds catch this
programme) but heard six songs from TSW, listed below.

	Never Be Mine
	Rocket's Tail
	The Fog
	Heads We're Dancing
	This Woman's Work
	Deeper Understanding

The interview was quite revealing, and I will attempt a transcription, but it
may take a little time (it lasted 50 minutes).  As well as explaining the
songs, she answered such questions as

	a)	Why are the Trio Bulgarika featured on the album?
	b)	Is KT going to tour?
	c)	What does KT think of computers?

Stay tuned to rec.music.gaffa for the first episode...

KT was also on TV this weekend, in a programme called "Rapido".  They were
slightly less generous, with about 10 seconds of TSW (and a bit of "Hounds Of
Love").  The interview didn't reveal too many facts either (unless you would
like to know that Rocket's_Tail will feature Dave Gilmour from Pink Floyd as
the rocket).
