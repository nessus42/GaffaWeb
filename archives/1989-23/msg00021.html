Actually, I'm also one of those who didn't like _The Dreaming_ at first.
I had all of her albums but _TD_ (including _HOL_).  One day I bought
the mini-LP, which contains "Sat in Your Lap" and "Suspended in Gaffa".
I loved SIG on first listening, and I kind of liked SIYL, though not as
much.  The funny thing is that when I played SIG for the first time, I 
could swear that I had heard that song before!  This is one of the greatest
mysteries of my Kate-fandom.  I know I heard that song before I bought
the mini-LP, yet I can't think of where I might have heard it.

Anyway, after hearing those two songs I decided to get the whole album.
I'm afraid to say that I was quite disappointed.  The songs were just
*too* strange.

However -- after listening to it five times I started figuring it out, and
after ten times I loved it.  It's still not strictly my favorite album.  I
actually don't have one.  My favorite is always the one I'm currently 
listening to.  So, when I'm playing _TD_, it becomes my favorite!  Lately,
_The Sensual World_ has been my favorite a lot of the time.

I have a friend here who had _Never For Ever_ and _Hounds Of Love_, but
none of her other albums.  I lent him _TD_ once, and he loved it
immediately.  So it affects different people differently.
