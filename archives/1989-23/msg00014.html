Anyway, as promised _The Sensual World_ was featured on yesterday's
"New Music Wednesday" on KITS.  They played "Deeper Understanding"
once, "Love and Anger" twice, and "Heads We're Dancing" three times.
(Conceivably, they could have played more when I wasn't listening).
Steve Masters called HWD "excellent".  So much for your theory that 
this song is too controversial for radio, IED.  Actually, I don't 
find it controversial at all.  Frightening, yes.  But something
frightening is not controversial unless not everybody agrees that 
it *is* frightening.  I can be pretty sure that that's not the
case here.
