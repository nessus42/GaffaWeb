_Running Up That Hill_ was used as the theme music for a British
children's TV drama called "Running Scared."  This is currently
being played on the ABC at 5:30pm weekdays, for the next week or
so.  The credits feature two hands holding and parting in a manner
similar to that at the end of the _RUTH_ clip, so I assume the
music wasn't just chosen because it sounds good.
