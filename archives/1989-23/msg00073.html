I saw at the bookstore was from *June* 1989.  I just can't wait for them to
get the October issue (and I mean I canNOT wait...).  Also, they're more

their waist.  Well, almost. I doubt if they'll do a good job with the
interview.

(by Ken Pyne):  a grouchy looking man is sitting on a chair.  Two woman
sit on a sofa behind him, and you also see a dog cowering behind the sofa.
Wife says to second woman : "Yes he's such a sadist - he even buys 
Kate Bush records just to annoy the dog."
