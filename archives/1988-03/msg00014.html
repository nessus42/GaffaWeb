Subject: Hello Earth....

Hello fellow Kate fans!
  Having only been on the LH mailing list for a few days, I haven't
had much opportunity to get to know any of you better.  However, I
have enjoyed very much reading some of your notes to one another, and
it rather surprises me how IN DEPTH you all are to Kate's music.  Call
me shallow, but, while I also attempt to pull apart her lyrics and
analyze them, I can't see what the point is in going in SO deep that
you're listening to Kate for analysis only and not for pleasure.
  Sorry, guys.  Don't get me wrong...I have also spent many hours
listening to such goodies as "Lagan Love" and "TWW" and trying to find
just what the fair MS. Kate is getting at, what she's striving for.
But I also have been known to just sit and FEEL, without an analytical
bone in my body, and let that be good enough for me.
  Comments?  Am _I_, a newcomer, a bimbo for making such statements?
I look forward to hearing from any of you, and getting to know more
about this whole network of Bush fans.
  'Til next time.....go to sleep, little Earth.....
                                                         -- Deb
