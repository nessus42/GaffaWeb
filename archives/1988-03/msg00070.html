Sorry about that, but I had to tell you all the UNBEFUCKINGLIEVABLY
GOOD NEWS (pardon my english).  The new TD album is out, and it is
available in quality music shoppes everywhere!  I picked it up on CD
not more than 20 minutes ago and haven't even gotten to my CD player
to tell you if it's good or anything like that.  Note that the compact
disc features a "special bonus track" which is nothing more than "May
The Cube Be With You".  The track listing is as follows: The Key To
Her Ferrari, Airhead, Hot Sauce, Pulp Culture, My Brain Is Like A
Sieve, The Ability To Swing, Budapest by Blimp and May The Cube Be
With You.

It is on EMI/Manhattan Records, catalog # E2-7-48075-2.  Buy it now!

The cover art is hilarious - exactly what you'd expect given the
title, and the lyrics look nifty from what I can tell.  Dolby has got
an entirely new band for this outing, and they are billed as "The Lost
Toy People".  Expect an in depth, lengthy, boring beyond belief and
completely subjective review of this new slab o' plastic within the
week.

I am indebted to love-hounds Julian West, MarK T. Ganzer, and Nancy
Everson for providing info about this.  Pick up a copy of the April
issue of Music Technology - it features a big piece on TMDR.
