I must agree with Doug's response to your admission that Hounds of
Love left you lacking.  Well, all I can tell you is....listen again...
and again!!  Like yourself, I'm a BIG Genesis and Gabriel fan, but I
also have more than enough room for the likes of the fair Ms. Kate.

usually open my notes to you)

Give Kate another listen, if not "Hounds", then another album.  I
didn't become a fan of hers overnight either, but boy, am I glad that
I gave her a chance.

  Good luck...good listening.
