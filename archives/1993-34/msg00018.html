In a recent listing of 'live import' (wink wink nudge nudge) CDs
that I came across, there is a Kate CD in the 'new releases'
section called "This Woman's Live Work (T.V '78-'87)" Does
anyone out there have any info on this disc? Sounds like
it may be a rehash of "Butterfly Kisses" (which covers the
same period), but you never know...

My two cents (why not?): 'Rubberband Girl' and 'Big Stripy Lie'
are excellent, while 'Eat The Music' is kind of disappointing...
its not bad, but its not anything to get that excited over.
(all IMHO, of course). The 'three chord monotony' factor
really sets in on the 12" version of EtM.....
