got stuck after writing the first 10 seconds of the song, and couldn't 
figure out a way to continue it. So Del said, "Honey, I know what to do. 
Just leave it to me." KaTe left, and Del just kept running the 10 seconds 
over and over again, ad infinitum. When KaTe heard it, she got so angry 
that she refused to credit Del on the CD single.
