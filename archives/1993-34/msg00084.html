I have a copy of this disc. It certainly doesn't duplicate the
track listing on "Butterfly Kisses" although there is some
overlap. I'll try to remember to post the exact tracking listing
tomorrow. For me, the most memorable thing on the disc is an
audience recording of Peter Gabriel doing "Don't Give Up" live
on which KaTe makes an unannounced guest appearance. When she
starts singing for the first time there is complete bedlam...
