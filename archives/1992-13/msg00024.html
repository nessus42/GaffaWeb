Announcing the Sarah McLachlan Boston 4/6/92 Tape Tree
******************************************************

Sarah McLachlan performed Monday April 6th at the Charles Playhouse in
Boston. The concert was FM broadcast on WFNX. A friend of mine taped
the concert on a Sony 75ES DAT recorder and made a DAT for me. The 
recording is very good except for some FM interference. It gets bad on
side B, and particularly on Path of Thorns. The concert fits perfectly on 
a 90 minute tape. I am offering to run a tape tree to distribute this tape 
to anybody on love-hounds that is interested. 

The setlist (for a 90 minute cassette):

Side A:				Side B:
Drawn to the Rhythm		I Will Not Forget You
Back Door Man			The Path of Thorns (Terms)
Home				Into the Fire
Lost				Steaming
Shelter				Encore: Vox
Black				Gloomy Sunday
Out of the Shadows
Ben's Song


The tree will be set-up such that preference is given to people that have good
tape-dubbing capabilites and who are willing to make a number of copies. If
you don't have anything to trade, you will still be able to obtain the tape by 
sending a blank and the cost of the return postage to the person above you on
the tree. If you are interested in getting a DAT copy be sure and let me know.
I will accept sign-ups for a week or two and then make up a tree.
