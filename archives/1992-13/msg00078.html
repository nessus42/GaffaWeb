The only one that I'm aware of is Pat Benatar's version of "Wuthering Heights"
on her -Crimes of Passion- album.  Although I do like a lot of Pat's early
work, she probably should never have touched this tune.  Her "Wuthering
Heights" is pretty standard MOR formula (ugh!), complete with plodding rhythms
and a really dull guitar solo in the middle.  She's no vocal slouch, but she
doesn't seem able to invest the song with much personality.  And a Kate Bush
song performed with minimal personality is, IMHO, a travesty.
