        I don't think anyone's mentioned what HOMEGROUND has to say about 
the new album: The word at the moment is that the 'September-ish' target for 
the release of the new album still holds.  HOMEGROUND is told that the album 
is more or less finished, with a few bits and pieces to be done.  There is 
as yet no word on the first single. 


        They don't seem to be aware however that Eric Clapton himself has 
confirmed that he worked with Kate, though.  :-) 


        Jill asked about IED.  Andrew Marvick is in San Jose (or is it San 
Fransisco?) in a new apartment.  He says that he is going to maybe get a 
modem sometime soon and try to get back on.  He didn't seem to understand 
about how there are public sites for Love-Hounds, but I think I got him to 
understand.   Earlier, he told me that IED is currently in a box.  :-) 

        A friend of mine told me he saw in the local paper a review of Tori 
Amos.  It said something like "Kate Bush should sue her for copyright 
infrignment."  :-)
