A friend of mine just received HoL on CD from her mostly non-Kate-
appreciative husband.  She let me listen to some of it (I only have

horror!")  Is this usual?  Even my cassette has the lyrics!

I too anxiously await a new album from Kate Bush, but perhaps talk of
a tour and speculation as to when she might release the album should
be postponed till she's had a suitable time to grieve her loss.  (I
know, what ELSE are we going to talk about besides Tori Amos?)
