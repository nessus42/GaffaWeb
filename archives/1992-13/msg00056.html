        Andy asked about "Carnival songs".  To me the early demo song 
_Nevertheless,_You'll_Do_ always sounded carnival-like.  I think the 
carnival influence is most important when it comes to the _tour_, especially 
on songs like _Strange_Phenomena_ and the German documentary 
_Room_For_The_Life_.   The thing about _Coffee_Homeground_ is that it's one 
of only three songs that were written between the first album and the 
second, so maybe the "carnival" atmosphere of it was partially inspired by 
the carnival like existance that Kate was living at the time.

        Phred asked about the secret message in _Leave_It_Open_.  The funny 
thing about it is that from the very first time I heard the song, it always 
was very clear to me that she said "We let the weirdness in."  As a matter 
of fact, that was my favorite line from the whole album for years, kinda 
jumps out at you.  It wasn't until years later that I realized that it was 
supposed to be "secret".

importantly, if they mention the interview book and if so how much (USA).  
THanks.
