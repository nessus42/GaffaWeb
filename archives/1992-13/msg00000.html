This is my first posting to Love-Hounds, hope it makes it.  

To John from Portland asking about Tori Amos tickets in Seattle - I 
tried to reply, but received a message back from another John saying I 
had the wrong person.

Anyway, I called the Backstage, and tickets are $7 advance, $9 at the 
door, and should be available now at Ticketmonster, $2 extra at outlets 
and even more over the phone (1-206-628-0888).  Reply if you need more 
info.  I will probably be going alone and wouldn't mind meeting someone 
there.

As for Kate - any rumors of Katemas plans in Seattle?

Kate is one artist my whole family likes.  A few years back, I was 
picking my daughter up from day care - she couldn't have been more than 
5 at the time.  When we got in the car, she wanted to hear some Kate 
Bush, so she asked, "Dad, do you have any Kate Bush's in the car?"  

Only in my dreams.

Mark Hanson
