(I tried to post an announcement like this before, but it apparantly got
caught in the crash that occurred a couple of weeks ago.  Anyway...)

"siblings" is a new mailing list, for the discussion of Jane Siberry and her
works.  It is *very* new, and some of the things you expect from a music
mailing list (FAQLs, discographies, etc.) are not yet in place.  (Mostly,
I'm hampered by the fact that cs.uwp.edu still won't allow new uploads.
The stuff there still references the old siberry list, which seems to have
spontaneously combusted at least 6 months ago.)

The list itself has been functioning without mishap since March 4th.  To
subscribe, send a mail message to:

majordomo@underground.irhe.upenn.edu

No subject line is necessary, and all you need to put in the text of the
message is "subscribe siblings".  Your name or your e-mail address is not
required (in fact, they would probably foul up the works and delay your
subscription).

Assuming all goes well, you'll get back a welcome notice and a brief intro.

And even if you don't want to subscribe, if you have anything to contribute
to a Jane Siberry FAQL, discography, or just trivia, you can mail it
directly to me at the address below.

Hope to see you on the list!

Brian Gerstel
