>Questions: just where exactly does Rowan Atkinson show up in Kate's
>repertoire. I'm embarrassed but I can't find it. Do I need a magnifying glass?

   They did a wonderful duet called "Do bears...?" on a Comic Relief
(UK) show. 

>Has anyone out there seen Lindsay Kemp live? I saw his production of
>Flowers in Toronto (1978?) and it changed my life. Never forgotten it. I
>also found an amzing book on him and his troupe. I would love to know more
>recent things about him...

   You will be pleased to know that it changed Kate's life as well. She
credits "Flowers" as one of the most important experiences of her life
at that time. It moved her to learn dance and mime.

   Most recently Lindsey starred in Kate's film "The Line, The Cross,
The Curve." I've not seen him to many places, but he had a nice bit
as the Innkeeper in the film "The Wicker Man." I used to own, but lost
a Penthouse magazine that had a several page feature on Lindsey and his
troupe.
