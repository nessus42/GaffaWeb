Hi...
It seems like the folks at EMI UK have had a difficult time getting their act
together!
I'm not sure if this has been posted yet or not, but it looks like the plans
to release "And So Is Love" as the next single have been scrapped.  Since the
video for the song was seen on MTV, maybe it is still going to be a single in
the US, but I don't know for sure..
Anyway, the new UK single is "The Red Shoes" and it's going to be released as
a regular CD single, and a limited edition CD single with a different track
listing.  I don't know what the tracks are, but I can't wait to find out...  
I think the release date for the singles is April 5th, but I'm not completely
sure of that.
It's about time!!!
