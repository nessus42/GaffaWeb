Went to see Sarah McLachlan in concert last night. Simply put: impeccable
sound; impeccable lighting; impeccable musicians; impeccable backing vocalist
(Camille Henderson - kept up with Sarah note for note). Sarah was of course
excellent as well, and quite charming as she reacted to the ovations she
received with a " what - for me?!" giggle. I was surprised, having heard only
the accolades about her voice, at how accomplished a guitarist and pianist she
was. Several members of her band also doubled on more than one instrument. 

If you get a chance to see her, don't pass it up!

Alan D.
