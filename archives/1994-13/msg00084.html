well you may have heard about the break in here, but not all news is bad.
set your VCR's or stay up late, because sarah is taking over the ed
sullivan theatre on Thursday, May 5 as she goes on the big show with Dave
and Paul. that's right, Sarah McLachlan on David Letterman Thursday, May 5.
she'll be performing "Possession" with members of her band and the CBS
Orchestra (can't wait to see Paul on keyboards for this). pass the word
around, let everyone know, don't miss it.

any other news...well probably, but whatever it is can wait for now. see
you later.

Lane and Cathy.
