>RIDLEY@ZASU.SPRL.UMICH.EDU writes:


>I have these CDs at home. They are by this female singer named Kate Bush (or
>something like that.) Maybe you all have heard of her? I know that not very
>many of my friends have. I thought it would be interesting to talk about her

>1. The Hounds of Love.

This is her best album in my opinion. The b-side is fantastic.
Whenever I hear Under Ice I realize that my heart is not completly
made of stone. 
I love it when an artist make theme-albums. It gives us something more
than just the music. Back in the "good old days" when I played in Rock- and 
Blues-band, we played two covers, and made them part of a thread by writing
a third one to connect them. It was pretty facinating doing it, and see our
fans liking it. KaTe must have enjoyed putting *that* album together, more 
than any other album.

Although I am displeaesed by KaTe's performance on The Red Shoes,
I consider Big Stripey Lie among her top ten. I haven't had the opportunity
to see TLTCTC, but I still wonder... I don't think I'm going to like it that 
much. The songs on the album has too week connections to eachother IMHO 
to make a good video/movie.
