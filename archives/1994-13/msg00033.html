To whom it may concern:

About a year ago, the college where I work got access to the Internet, and 
my brother told me about this neat Kate Bush discussion group (that's you, 
love-hounds).  Well, I subscribed as soon as I could, looking forward to all 
sorts of neat news and gossip about Kate.  Imagine my surprise when, 
instead, I found myself literally inside this jorn/chris/vickie thing.  I 
have this to say about that:
              FUCK OFF, ALL THREE OF YOU!!!
  The next time I get the urge to hear some children fight, I'll go across 
the hall to the campus Day Care facility.
Not that I am cowardly and cannot face the replies that this post will 
undoubtedly inspire, but I personally have better things to do with my 
limited mail-reading time.  I am going to unsubscribe from this list.  I 
will, however, keep the e-mail addresses of the few sane, adult individuals 
I encountered through this group.
