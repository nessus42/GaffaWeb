I have these CDs at home. They are by this female singer named Kate Bush (or
something like that.) Maybe you all have heard of her? I know that not very
many of my friends have. I thought it would be interesting to talk about her
for once...

So, why do I love Kate Bush?

1. She's a fox (just kidding...)

1. The Hounds of Love.

  Running Up That Hill was probably the first song I ever heard by her and
  it still is one of my all time favorites. It is so sad. I love the
  realization that you are always alone. No matter how close you are to
  someone, you are alone.

2. Love and Anger

  It is so true to my first love and I.

3. Emotion

  She gives so much.


I just thought that I could motivate a little talk instead of all this 
bickering that is going on.
