*  AN URGENT HOMEGROUND NEWSFLASH DIRECT FROM HOMEGROUND TOWERS  *
******************************************************************  
 
 
"Though they weren't the proper colour for mourning, she had no others"
 
Hans Christian Anderson - "The Red Shoes"
 
 
My friends,
 
It's 12.43  am, and this afternoon I drove the 240 miles back from
our HOMEGROUND Wuthering Hike IV. Some 30 of us gathered at Top 
Withins, the ruin on Haworth Moor that it is thought inspired in
Emily Bronte the idea of Wuthering Heights.
 
I have just got back from the London Weekend Television Studios,
where with Dave and Krys we were in the audience for "Aspel and
Co."
 
I'm knackered, but I just wanted to share with you my first reactions. 
Think of "This Woman's Work" and step up the emotional power by a factor 
of ten. his song is emotion incarnate. It is power incarnate. It is indeed
a roll call of those people Kate has loved and lost. If this is a pointer 
to the album, then we shall be overwhelmed.
 
More when I'm conscious.
