Well there I was, sitting at home watching the ice skating special on "Mobil
Sports" when I suddenly heard the opening notes of "This Woman's Work". Sure
enough one of the performers (Rosalyn Sumners (sp first name?)) was skating
to KaTe. It took me half a minute or so to remember to start watching the
skating :)

Anyhow this made me wonder if any other of Kate's music has been used in a
similar fashion. I think that powerful effects could be created with a
combination of some of her songs, and a talented dancer (or pair). In 
particular I thought that a truely riveting dance could be set to "Waking the 
Witch" (although the ending might get a bit repetitive perhaps)

Any thoughts?

Philip
