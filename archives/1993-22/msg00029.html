Jerome, I'd worry a bit about that mysterious swelling... ;^)  (My Ulaw'd 
Moments3 is only 2Mb.)

The clarity is truly fantastical, tho, and KT's voice is in ****fine****
form!

This ain't Rocket's Tail, everyone should realize, the intensity is real
but in an *old fashioned* emotional way...

I risked the wrath of my sysadmin and ftpmailed it.  There's a much quicker
ftpmail service than the DEC one, but it will break moments3.au into *45*
messages.

Send the following message to BITFTP@pucc.Princeton.EDU [capitalization
not important]

ftp ftp.uu.net uuencode
cd  /tmp
ls -l  [so you can see if you named it wrong]
get [whichever version you want, some may be too large for this service]
quit

Thanks to Jerome for the help converting au to Mac!  I got my segments
somewhat scrambled, but it's still sheer bliss....
