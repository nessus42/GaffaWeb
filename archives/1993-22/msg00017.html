While most of the Love-hounds here spent June 20th anxiously awaiting
Kate's TV appearance, I spent it at the Worcester Centrum,
Massachusetts, where I saw PETER GABRIEL IN CONCERT!

And here's why I mentioned it here:

During the intermission between the opening act (a French group called
Papa Wemba) and Gabriel's show, they played a few songs over the
speaker.  One of them was "The Sensual World."

Alas, Peter did not play any Kate-related songs this time - "No Self
Control" or "Don't Give Up."

Check out the Gabriel mailing list for more info on the Angel Gabriel
and his "Secret World" tour.
