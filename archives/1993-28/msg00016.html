>Speaking of Don't Give Up, the song was played on a local television station
>here in Orlando, Florida as part of a public service announcement.  ONLY
>Kate was heard:  "When times get rough, you can still count on us, don't give

Speaking of it again, that same part was also used in an episode of
MTV's new Real World.

Apparently unlike a few KT fans, I really like the song, maybe cause
it and RUTH were how I first found out about Kate.  I first heard
RUTH, which was enough to make me want to buy something, but a week
after I heard RUTH, SO was released.  When I realized or found out (I
don't remember) that it was Kate on So...  well, before long I had all
of Kate's stuff!  First artist I had all the CDs of.  I was lucky to
have heard RUTH on the radio at all here in Tucson.
