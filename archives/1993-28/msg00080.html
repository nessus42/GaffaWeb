Well - there's always more. But first the Tori Amos mailing list address
is really-deep-thoughts@gradient.cis.upenn.edu, to subscribe etc. send
a note to really-deep-thoughts-request@gradient.cis.upenn.edu. The
really-deep-thoughts part can be abbreviated to rdt in both addresses.

Now to the basic discography (or the part that's in my possesion, as
I said there's always more, and I skip all non-CD stuff):

Albums:
Y Kan't Tori Read
Little Earthquakes
A Kiss On The Glass (live bootleg with audience recorded audio, i.e. bad audio)

Singles/promos:
Crucify (US promo, Crucify remix)
Crucify (US, Crucify remix, Winter, Angie, Smells Like Teen Spirit, Thank You)
Crucify (Germany, Crucify, Here in my Head, Mary, Crucify (LP version))
Crucify (UK ltd. ed, Little Earthquakes (live), Crucify (live), Precious Things
    (live), Mother (live))
Winter (US promo, Winter (Edit), Winter (LP version))
Winter (UK ltd. ed, Winter, Angie, Smells Like Teen Spirit, Thank You)
Winter (UK, Winter, The Pool, Take To The Sky, Sweet Dreams)
Winter (Germany, Winter, The Pool, Smells Like Teen Spirit)
Silent All These Years (Germany, Silent All These Years, Upside Down, Me And
    A Gun, Thoughts)
Silent All These Years (UK ltd. ed, Silent All These Years, Ode To The Banana
    King (Part One), Song For Eric, Happy Phantom (live))
China (Germany, China, Sugar, Flying Dutchman, Humpty Dumpty)


There's also a promo single from the Y Kan't Tori Read album, and there surely
are many more CD singles from the Little Earthquakes album. And there's vinyl
and video and cassettes.
