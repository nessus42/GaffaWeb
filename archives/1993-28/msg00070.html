   No, no. The reference is to the "fetish shoes" Kate wore in the 
_Die Efteling_ video to _Strange Phenomena_, not the thigh-high numbers
she wore in _Moving_. The poster in question was shot by John Bush
at Die Eftling (a TV appearance, not a "fan club" thing) and the poster
was one of a set sold on the tour. We have three, "Moving", "Lakeside"
and "Tour". 

   Anyway, the shoes in question are mongo platforms with a kind-of
"reversed heel," wider at the bottom than the top. In the Kate video
this is best seen when Kate sings "...you pick up a paper, you read a
name..." wearing a red sequined tail coat.
