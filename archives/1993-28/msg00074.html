One thing in the new Homeground I don't think anybody mentioned was the
newsbit about the Red Shoes film. If I remember rightly it said it would
comprise of 40mins of musical footage and 20mins of dramatic footage -

she is about the same age as KaTe, I would hazard a guess that she
plays the main protagonist and that KaTe has concentrated on the
production/direction, perhaps with a only a few bit parts like in the
Experiment IV video. It would be odd to have a KaTe Bush video without
KaTe in it....
