  I know I'm being wildly optimistic, but...

  I'm lucky enough to be going on a holiday in England for a week and a
half (August 12 to 21).  I know that Kate doesn't play live very much,
but as I never thought I'd ever be on the same continent as her I haven't

coming out I have this faint glimmer of hope that there might actually be
a show somewhere, sometime when I'm over there.  I'm willing to give my
right arm to go (although I'd prefer to pay cash), so if anyone knows
one way or another (or could direct me to tickets?!) I'd be eternally
grateful.
