Newsflash August 1993/3
 
It's not quite official yet, but HOMEGROUND has been given the 
go-ahead to start planning for the next Kate Bush Convention.
 
Because of Kate's promotional schedule, including her trip to the
US, dates before Christmas are precluded, and the planning dates 
will be in late January or early February 1994.
 
The venue will be central London, and we have a number of locations
in mind.
 
We'll keep you all posted as details are settled.
