Just to add my two cents, I'd say English Settlement (the 2-disc
Virgin version) is XTC's PG3/Sgt Pepper, so far.  I'm surprised at the
mentions of Mummer and The Big Express as being some of their best
work, as I leaned more towards the early (D&W, BS, Go2) stuff.

Peter and Joe both mentioned most of XTC's stuff, but didn't mention a
couple of things (just trying to be complete) .. one is an EP that
came out right on the heels (within a month or two, I think) of
English Settlement, called "5 Senses."  It's got stuff on it that
doesn't appear on ES ... similar in style and quality.  I don't
recall if any of the songs made it onto the Beeswax LP.

The other is a couple of 12" discs that came out with funny lines all
over the jacket cover ... it sounded pretty experimental, lotsa
African riddums and such.  Andy Partridge does Brian Eno.
