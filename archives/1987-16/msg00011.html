Subject: throwing muses album

The new Throwing Muses album seems to be out.  At least WFNX has it.  They
played the whole thing (side 2 is just one song) Sunday night on the Boston
Rocks show.  I think the dj said it was on Sire records domestically.  I
was working while I was listening to the show, so I didn't listen very
closely, but it sounded like Throwing Muses.  I liked it, though a lot of
the songs sounded familiar for a minute or two, and I will probably buy it
when I lift my compact disc embargo.

- Nancy
