I just got back from seeing "The Line, The Cross, The Curve" here in
Seattle.  It will show again today at 4:50, 7:00, and 9:05 at the Varsity,
so if you're in Seattle and missed the posts to LoveHounds about it or
forgot, there may still be a chance if you're reading this on October 1. 

Anyway... there was a stack of flyers in the lobby saying that TLTCTC 
will be released "at a video store near you" on October 25.

I think I would sum up the movie by saying, "The music was exquisite and
Miranda Richardson was terrific--what a great music video."  I wish it 
had been more Spielbergian.  Or maybe Ridley Scott.  Hmmm.
