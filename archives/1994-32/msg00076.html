Hi all,

This is a reminder that woj-n-meth are hosting a party (a.k.a. OcTorifest,
Belated KaTemas, ectogather, housewarming, excuse for a party) this Saturday,
October 8, 1994 from 2:00 PM until whenever at our humble abode in Boonton,
New Jersey, USA.  Boonton is located just north of Morristown on I-287, and
is accessible via mass transit from New York City and other area locales.

If you'd like to attend, please send a note to both myself and woj at the
addresses below with the subject line "PARTY".  Please RSVP by Wednesday,

food, drink, goodies of the musical/video/rarity variety to show off, or
acoustic instruments for musicmaking, please feel free!

We will be hanging out, listening to the music we love (by Tori, Kate, Sarah,
Happy, and many many others), watching videos, and eating way too much for
our own good.  You're all cordially invited to join us if you can make it.
