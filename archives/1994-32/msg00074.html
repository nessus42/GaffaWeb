In article <2E8DBA46@ALBPIG.CHO.GE.COM> "Saunders, Jonathan" <Saunders@albpig.cho.ge.com> writes:

>I'm still waffling on getting the (UK or Japanese) TWW boxed set. If I buy 
>that instead of each album individually, will I miss out on the album cover 
>art and the thrill of searching for the KT symbols?

>Anyone willing to list the pro's and con's of buying that over the original 
>albums?

I bought the UK box when it first came out.  It had/has the stickers, the 
photo book, and 6 CDs plus TWW vol 1 and 2.  The CDs are the same UK CDs you 
can buy seperately, thus the album covers are the same.  (Of course, as you 
know, CDs are smaller than record albums, so...)
 
I have not seen TWW 1 & 2 available apart from the boxed set.  If you try hard 
enough, though, you could probably find them.

So, imho, you don't miss out on anything by getting the box... you get more.
