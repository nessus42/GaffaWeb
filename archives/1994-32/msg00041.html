>Um, I'd love to have TWW, but I know it's gonna be $$expensive$$,
>especially since I beleive it would only be available in this country as

>getting the CD version of TWW to Billings, Montana, USA? Tanx.

This seems to come up every couple of months or so. For those of us U.S.ers, its
not a hassle to get the box set (and its better than buying those bootlegs of
the TWW 1 and 2).  Just call any of the big record stores in Canada (like HMV,
A & B sound, Sam the Record Man), order it and it'll be in your hands in about
a week for about $100 U.S. There's 8 cds in it so that's not a bad deal, plus
its worth every penny.  If you shop around for sale you can get it even
cheaper; I got mine for $75 which included the shipping and everything, but it
did have a damaged box lid.

best of luck,
Glen Davis
