...okay, a couple of months behind everyone else :) 
A little TV watching finally paid off. But did they have to be during
the Miss America pageant? [hides face in shame]

Anyhow, I saw two different ones, and I really, really liked the
music. I don't know that I could have identified it as Kate-music
if I didn't know in advance, but it was certainly miles above the
usual commercial soundtrack goop - very different and creative.

That said, I was utterly bored to tears with the pseudo-60s graphics and
the ever so new and brilliant (sarcasm mode on) idea of imposing
profound statements of text over the pictures. Ooh, they must have
sat up all night thinking that one up! :P Suffice to say I don't
think I'll actually be DRINKING Fruitopia any time soon, but I
admire their taste in soundtrack composers.

Cheers, Fiona
