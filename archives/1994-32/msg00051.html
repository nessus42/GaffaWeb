Another late contribution - a great way to fill in time waiting for a program
to finish running.

The albums:

1. Hounds Of Love - no question
2. The Dreaming - would be top but for "There Goes A Tenner", which I hate
3. The Sensual World - contains my two favourite Kate songs (see below)
4. Never Forever
5. The Kick Inside
6. Lionheart
7. The Red Shoes - some great tracks but a lot of uninspired, boring drivel

Top 5 songs:

1. The Sensual World - hmmmm yes!
2. Rocket's Tail
3. Cloudbusting
4. Breathing
5. Under The Ivy

Bottom 5 songs:

1. Why Should I Love You
2. There Goes A Tenner
3. Eat The Music
4. And So Is Love
5. Constellation Of The Heart

Top 5 Videos:

1. Cloudbusting
2. Experiment IV
3. The Sensual World
4. Army Dreamers
5. Suspended In Gaffa
