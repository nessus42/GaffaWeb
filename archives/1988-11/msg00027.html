I've just bought Music for Films III on Opal Records.  It features
Brian/Roger Eno, Harold Budd, Laraaji, Daniel Lanois and a few others.
(The Harold Budd track is a remix of Balthus Bemused By Color from the
White Arcades album (also on Opal).

The title of the album indicates that there _must_ have been a Music for
Films I and II preceeding it.  Now Music for Films I is obviously Brian
Eno's Music for Films on Editions EG.  Has anyone heard of a Music for
Films II?  Or is that supposed to be Apollo:Atmospheres and Soundtracks
(also on Editions EG)?
