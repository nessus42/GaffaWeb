Gaffians all,

Although I hardly ever read gaffa any more, I turn to the more knowledgeable
amongst you now in search of a definitive answer about Kate's private life.

In general I detest speculation and gossip about people's sexuality,
but in this case I would like to know.  A colleague of mine at work
swears that Kate Bush is a lesbian, or at least bisexual.  She cites
as evidence conversations that she personally had with Kate some years
ago, along with another woman called Gaye, who apparently ran an 
Australian Kate Bush fan club.

Among the list of people Kate has supposedly slept with are Annie Lennox
and Stevie Nicks, something that strikes me as particularly unlikely.

Is there any evidence that these claims are true?

(No, this has nothing to do with April Fool's day)

Martin
