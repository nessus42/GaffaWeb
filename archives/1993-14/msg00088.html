In article <martin.734258500@marsh>:
>In general I detest speculation and gossip about people's sexuality,
>but in this case I would like to know.  A colleague of mine at work
>swears that Kate Bush is a lesbian, or at least bisexual.  She cites
>as evidence conversations that she personally had with Kate some years
>ago, along with another woman called Gaye, who apparently ran an 
>Australian Kate Bush fan club.
>
>Among the list of people Kate has supposedly slept with are Annie Lennox
>and Stevie Nicks, something that strikes me as particularly unlikely.
>
>Martin

Well, anything is possible, but this strikes me as extremely unlikely.
Kate has had a long-term, well-publicized relationship with her bassist,
Del Palmer, for perhaps fifteen years now.  It's true that some of her
songs deal with gay issues, but that's as far as it goes -- at least
according to all those whom I trust.  Many of her songs are
clearly heterosexual, according to Kate herself (e.g. "Running Up
That Hill").  Incidentally, I'm pretty sure Kate has met Annie
Lennox, but I have my doubts whether she's ever met Stevie Nicks.
