I have just bought Rasputina's "Thank's For The Ether"
and I find it just great . The girls are really talented and
in some songs they remind me of Kate . Especially the
track "Crybabies".
Many thanks to Rennee Rosen who introduced us
to them . I fully disagree with Kelley Hays-Gilpin , who ,
in my opinion didn't listen to the album carefully because
it's sure not an easy listening one . Of course everyone is
allowed to have his likes and dislikes but I think that the
three girls really worth it .
