Dear everyone,

In the absence of our beloved KaTe it is nice to know that there will be
new Kate-related material out on CD soon. Before the end of this year I
will release Kate Bush Covered, a 70-minute Compact Disc with covers of
Kate Bush songs by some very talented fans. The CD will have at least 20
tracks, ranging from the debut single "Wuthering heights" to "Lily" from
the most recent album. 

RealAudio samples from these songs can be downloaded and listened to from
the Kate Bush Covered Homepage at http://www.bart.nl/~mfgr/katebush.htm
You can also find details on how to order the Kate Bush Covered CD there.

This is a non-profit project operated by one Kate Bush fan. No bootlegging,
and no ripoff! 

Thanks for your time.

Kind regards,
