Yiannis,

Briefly, Happy Rhodes has released the following albums:

   Rhodes Volume I (1986)
   Rhodes Volume II (1986)
   Rearmament (1986)
   Ecto (1987)
   Warpaint (1991)
   Equipoise (1993)
   RhodeSongs (1993 compilation)
   Building the Colossus (1994)
   The Keep (1995)

All are released on Happy's own "Aural Gratification" label.

For more information, including availability of CDs, see:

   http://www.tela.bc.ca/ecto/
   http://miso.wwa.com/~vickie/happy/

I would also reccomend that you take a look at the Happy Rhodes entry
in the Ectophiles' Guide to Good Music at:

   http://www.ecto.org/ectoguide/

and consider joining the Ecto mailing list at:

   ecto@ecto.org
 
but unfortunately the whole ecto.org domain seems to be down these
days.  If you would like a Happy Rhodes sampler tape, please contact
the undersigned.

Hope that helps.
