Big Country in Cambridge, MA

From: jdo@world.std.com (Jack D OLeary)

Subject: Big Country in Cambridge, MA
Keywords: Big Country, The Seer
Newsgroups: rec.music.gaffa
Organization: The World Public Access UNIX, Brookline, MA

Probably only of peripheral interest to Kate fans, but the Scottish
band Big Country is playing a FREE concert at the Cambridgeside
Galleria Mall in Cambridge, MA on Saturday, Sept. 11, at 3 PM (?).

Kate sang on a song of theirs called "The Seer", on an album of the
same name.  These guys play Celtic heavy metal, if you can believe it
(though their newer stuff sounds more like Springsteen or something).

I'll be there!

Does anyone know where I should post this to reach the most potential 
Big Country fans?  Please drop me a note.
