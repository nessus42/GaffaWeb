as melodic and all-around interesting (to me) as, say, "The Speckless Sky",

album.  I can't imagine why this has been deleted.  I would expect it to
reappear soon, considering that her new one is doing well.  But if I
ever want to pick this up on CD, I suppose I can always make the two-
hour drive to Toronto (one of the little benefits of living in Buffalo!)
I'm sure she doesn't get deleted in Canada.
