Here ya go, East coasters:

Sept 04 - Chicago, IL        Abbey's Pub
Sept 05 - Chicago, IL        St. Xavier's University (Irish Fest)
Sept 06 - Boston, MA         Kells
Sept 08 - New York City      Tramps
Sept 09 - N Hampton, MA      Iron Horse
Sept 11 - Philadelphia, PA   Commodore Barry Irish Center
Sept 12 - Buffalo, NY        Unconfirmed, venue unknown
Sept 16 - New Orleans, LA    Tipitinos
Sept 19 - Pawling, NY        Towne Crier

She might also be on Mountain Stage on Sunday the 19th, but it's 
unconfirmed.

The booking agent gave me these dates and venues off the top of his head,
so I'm not sure if all the spellings are correct. Anyone planning to go
(and yes, you should go) should call the venue for specific details.

What a wonderful show it was! Please go to this show, if you're in the
general area of one of them. Mary's voice is in top form, it wraps itself
around you and won't let go. Dark, smoky, wry Irish vocals. Mary Coughlin
is from Ireland, but she doesn't sing "Irish" music. She's more akin to
Billie Holiday and June Tabor. She can take any song written and sing it
with her Irish voice and wry style and make the song completely her own.
She sings everyone from Cole Porter to Elvis Costello and never ever sings
a bad song. I was so excited to be at this show because I've been such a
big fan for so many years, and I wasn't disappointed at all. She also tells
great stories about some of the songs.

Vickie
