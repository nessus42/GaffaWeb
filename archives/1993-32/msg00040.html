KROQ (Los Angeles) just completed a "Flashback 500" of their all-time
top requested songs.  KROQ is one of the more adventurous mainstream radio
stations in our area.  They were the first to play people like Blondie,
Dead Kennedys, Bangles, etc.  Anyway, Running Up That Hill was #221 on
the list.  Not bad, considering that (a) radio is programmed for the teen
audience and (b) most Americans are illiterate ;-)
