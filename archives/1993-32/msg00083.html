Hi all!

    In less then 24 hours I'll have my own copy of RBG!! It's like waiting for
Christmas! :-) I'll let you all know what I think when I get my hands on my
copy. I've just got off the phone with EMI Australia who tell me the official
release of the first KaTe single RBG wont be for another 2 weeks here in
Australia (funny thing that, since she always seems to chart better here then
anywhere else in the world) and that it'll be followed up by the single release
of ETM! Hey, it looks like we get both here!! :-) The actual album TRS wont be
released here until late October, early November. They didn't even know she had
done a film so couldn't tell me about that. What I -can- say is that they have
quite a few demo singles for release to almost every radio station in and
around Sydney!! 8-) It looks like they plan to give this one plenty of
publicity! 8-)
