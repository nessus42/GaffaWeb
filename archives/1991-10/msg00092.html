This song is from a group of several dozen Kate demos that started
circulating among the fans a couple of years ago.  They were all 
recorded before Kate's first album came out, and they didn't end up
on any albums.  They've become somewhat more available lately --
you can get them on a set of EPs called the Cathy Demos, a set of
LPs called Cathy's Album I and II, or on a couple of CDs that have just
come out: _Passing Through Air_ and _If You Could See Me Fly_.  Or you
could get a copy of the tape, sometimes called _Fiddle_, that made the
fan rounds a while ago.  All are bootlegs, of course.

These are WELL WORTH HAVING for any serious Kate fan.
