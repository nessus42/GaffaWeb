Subject: convention announcement

Just wanted to drop in and let you know that the American Association
of Them Heavy People is sponsoring a Kate Bush Fan Convention in
Middleburg Heights, Ohio, on APRIL 6, so you will have to make
plans in a hurry. Kate, Paddy and Del were all invited, but we
wont know if they are coming until just before the convention.
I am AATHP's new computer librarian, and you can contact me
for details or call Bill at (216) 923-8977.
AATHP also does a newsletter called Little Light. This comes
out of Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio (not Rhode Island, that's Little
Earth). We are a somewhat more serious journal and cover her
art and music. We have well placed contacts at EMI and Columbia,
and the newsletter staff is almost wholly comprised of
professional writers, including a friend of Kate's. This isn't
yet available any way except by mail, and the address is:
Little Light, PO Box 221, Cuyahoga Falls, Ohio 44222. Sample
issues run $2.50, and a four issue subscription is $10.00.
If you want more information let me know.
By the way the convention is at the Signature Inn at I-71 and
Bagely Road.
