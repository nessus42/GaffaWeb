This is the so-called "Mini-LP", and at one time it was quite easy
to find in the US.  It's been getting harder lately.  It came out
around 1983 when EMI-America was trying to build on the stir that
_The Dreaming_ had created in certain circles.  The Canadian version
contains six songs, the extra one being "Ne T'en Fuis Pas".
