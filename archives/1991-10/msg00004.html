Here are some interpretations given by a friend of mine (Gargantua):

 The angelic being in the video for Experiment IV is a Muse. (makes sense)

 The line "mother will hide the murderer" in "Mother Stands for Comfort" 
refers to an abortion...(????...I'm not too sure about this one...anyone 
care to shed any light?)

Recently, I saw a two-CD collection of the best of the Golden 
Palominos...I'm pretty sure that it contains ALL of their three newest 
albums, "Visions of Excess", "Blast of Silence", and "A Dead Horse"...
So, any Syd Straw fans should probably check this out...the price was pretty 
good; less than the cost of two regular CD's...
