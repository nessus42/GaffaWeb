Not much, at least in the US.  The only Kate LaserDisc to have been
released here is _The Sensual World: The Videos_, which Image released
as an 8 inch disc.  If you find a place that carries Japanese imports,
you can get _Live at Hammersmith_, _The Single File_, _Hair of the Hound_
(a collection of the four videos from _Hounds of Love_), and _The
Whole Story_.
