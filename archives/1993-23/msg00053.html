KaTemass Party to be held in Wollongong
=======================================

Date : 31 July 93
Time : Starts after 1:00pm
Where : Stephens Place
        2/49 Gilmore Street
        West Wollongong

        Gilmore Street runs directly off the top end of Crown Street. There'll
be lots of piccies out the front and we'll be around the back where all the
noise(music!!) is coming from! Oh.. BTW It's only about a 2K walk from the
Wollongong station. You can either walk from there or we'll pick you up (given
some notice).

   All you need to bring is your own meat (for the BBQ) your own grog and if
you need to crash over for the night, a sleeping bag! We'll supply the great
music/video's (unless you've got something interesting to bring), other KaTe
stuff, munchies, strange instruments to play, a KaTemass Tree, a suprise!, and
whatever else comes to mind.

   See ya there!
