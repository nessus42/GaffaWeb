>i'm sure Kate uses him for his guitar-playing talent.

I am a big (and very knowledgeable) Prince collector and I can almost assure
you that he has written, yes written, a song for her.  He did three songs
for her, one of which was "With This Tear" which ended up on Celine
Dion's CD.  He may or may not play any instruments on the track,
depending on how much of his version Kate ends up using as backing. 
(Celine ended up using her own arrangement I believe...I don't own the CD).

Incidentally, I don't know how many of you are aware of this, but Prince
did a one-off radio show in MPLS called the NPG Radio Show in 1989.  During
his stint as DJ he played a remixed (allegedly by him) version of Love and
Anger.  It was pretty interesting.
