hiya, everyone!

yes, there are KaTe fans in Deep-East-n-Dahrlin' Texas!

but, i haven't had the money or opportunities to buy all the goodies, 
and out here, there just ain't a whole helluvalotta rare KT stuff 
available *heavy sigh*.

i've been a KT fan since around 1980, and heavier since 1985, when i 
rediscovered her on the "cloudbusting" video on VH1's contemporary 
jazz show that was on late sunday nights. or was that 1986?

before i begin my list of questions (i'm sure several are FAQs, but 
hey, i haven't found the KTB FAQ list yet) i'd like to share with 
y'all what i have in my KT collection:

ok, first of all, as you notice i don't have *Never for Ever* YET!
but i've been looking to find an EMI-America cd of it.

they do that (cut-out the lyrics) with all Amer --> Manhattan cds, 
and did the EMI-America version of *The Dreaming* have a lyric 
booklet???

has anyone translated what the australian ab. says at the end of "The 
Dreaming"?  and what is it?

e-mail them to me? (esp the chorus)

I remember when TSW first came out and i just didn't have the $$ to 
get it... so i couldn't... then i noticed they changed the catalogue 
number on the box, even the copper tint changed slightly. does anyone 
have an orig. US cd and can you tell me the orig. cat number and the 
dif. of the old and new? (like, is Columbia printed the same size as 
the title, like on *Aspects...* on the spine of the jewel-case? new 
one Columbia is half-size).

as you can see, the only b-sides i have are from the *Aspects...* and 
LaA, and TWW cd singles.  what are the other b-sides (can someone 
list these with their A-sides, too?) and is there a legal cd released 
somewhere with some of these on it?  i DON'T have the $$$$ for the 
box set...even if someone offered one for cheap!
