>ok, first of all, as you notice i don't have *Never for Ever* YET!

JUST BUY IT!  NfE was the album that really got me into KaTe and it
contains my single favorite grouping-of-three:  "The Infant Kiss," "Night-
Scented Stock," and "Army Dreamers".

It's going to be impossible to find NfE with lyrics in the liner notes unless
you buy an important.  Don't put it off!

>some lyrics i can't grasp from "Suspended in Gaffa": anyone care to 
>e-mail them to me? (esp the chorus)

Which part were you looking for?  The chorus is pretty simple:

"Suddenly my feet are feet of mud 
 It all goes slow-mo
 I don't know why I'm crying
 Am I suspended in gaffa?
 Not until I'm ready for you
 Not until I'm ready for you
 Can I have it all?"

The part that most folks can't figure out are KaTe's whispered lyrics:

I caught a glimpse of a God, all shiny and bright

We all have a dream...maybe

Mother, where are the angels?  I'm scared of the changes.
