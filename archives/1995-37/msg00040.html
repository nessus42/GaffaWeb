>I recieved a prompt : login

OK, if you entered the right port number (4201, not
4021), you would have been given instructions on what
to do on screen.  Here's the lowdown:

Type CREATE <name> <password>
(No, DON'T type the <'s or >'s!) where <name> is
what you want your name to be and <password> is
what you want your password to be.

Then the next time you log in, type:
CONNECT <name> <password>.

FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS on what to do
before doing this:
