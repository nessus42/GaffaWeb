"Rocket Man", the Cd single is available in Canada through a CD 
distribution company/store called CD plus.  It is in their catalog, 
which is very extensive...lots of Tori, Jane, Laurie, everything, 
including my fave: BULGARIAN SINGING!!!!! Trio Bulgarka, Le Mystere 
se Voix Bulagres, oy vay, so much stuff....By the way, anyone who 
loved the Trio Bulgarka should check out the duet of Kronos Quartet 
and the mongloian throat singers of Tuva.  They are able to prouduce 
two or more notes in their throat at the same time....It is amazing.  
The song "Kongerei" is avalible on the disc "Night Prayers" 
(Elektra), and amazingly enough, the disc is avaliable in, of all 
places, COLUMBIA HOUSE!
