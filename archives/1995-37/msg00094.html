The EDLOS, the Bad Boys of A Cappella, will be performing all over

album, A CAPPELLA COUNTRY; we hope you enjoy listening to it as much as we
enjoyed making the music. Pulled out our Christmas album the other day and
gave it a "spin", it's still a kick to hear as well!
Blessings and peace to your house from ours.
