iRock, the Internet's only true Internet Wave music radio station has
added the unreleased The Spin Doctor's, "Miss America" to the playlist
in Show #15. It's available as on-demand audio stream programming via 
VocalTec's IWave Player on iRock Internet Music Radio.  

Tune in and hear it! - http://www.iRock.Com

iRock plays an eclectic mix of progressive, alternative and wacky
music.  The bottom line is that iRock plays excellent music that
you won't hear on broadcast radio!

"How is iRock different from any other on-air, broadcast radio
station and why would anyone want to listen to radio via their
computer?," are the two questions most often asked.  "The answer's 
easy," replies G. J., head muckity-muck and CyberDJ at iRock, "we 
play the good music that traditional radio is afraid to play... and 
I'm really funny.  Plus, it's really cool to listen to radio over the 
Internet.  Anyone can listen to an AM/FM radio in their house or car, 
but try listening to iRock via your laptop on the beach with a 
cellular modem.  Dude-Man...you'll be an instant geek-babe magnet!"
