----------
This is the official T-shirt for Lovehounds /rec.music.gaffa, the Kate Bush
mailing list/Newsgroup. 

[Three links of pictures of the front, the back, and a close-up of the front
are here]

For those of you who can't see graphics, the shirt is light tan with a
picture of the cover of The Dreaming, with the words "Suspended in
rec.music.gaffa" over it in dark brown. 

On the back, on top is typed love-hounds@uunet.uu.net and under that, in
Kate's own writing (really!) is: 

To everyone at "Love Hounds"
Thanks for all your support
      With love,
      Kate Bush
      XX
