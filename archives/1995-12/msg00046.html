I own an 40 minute (+) interview disc with Kate.  It is called "The 
Conversation Disc Series.  I can't find any other company name on it.  
The disc packaging is yellow with a circa 1980's photo of Kate in a 
white shirt with lots of make up.  The same photograph is on the disc.  
The Disc says "Kate Bush" "The Conversation Disc Series" and 
"Manufactured in England" "ABCD 012"  I purchased it about 5 - 6 years 
ago at a record shop in Southern Calfiornia.

The 2 page booklet attached has only info about CD quality, but it also 
says "All interviews in this series are of no less than 40 minutes.  
These interviews are not available in any other format." "Strictly 
limited edition of 2500" and "Made in England".

I have no idea what I paid for it or what it is worth.  I didn't see it 
listed in the FAQ and have been meaning to e-mail the FAQ-master ;) with 
the info, but just haven't gotten around to it.

The interview is lengthy and goes into a lot of stuff, like her 
relationships and her house and recording studio.  It also goes into her 
childhood and dance background.
