From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)

Subject: KITS album countdown


I finally got in the mail the list for KITS' Labor Day countdown
of the "Top 105 Albums of All Time."  This countdown was based on
listeners votes.

I was disappointed to see that only one Kate album got on the list.
It was _The Sensual World_, which was number 99.  I was really surprised
that _Hounds of Love_ didn't make it.  Oh well.
