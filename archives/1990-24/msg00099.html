The latest issue of _Goldmine_ (Oct. 5) mentions the box set in its "Grapevine"
column.  Here is the item, quoted in full:  "EMI U.K. will release a Kate Bush
boxed set including her six studio albums plus two CDs of rarities."

In an ad, Christopher Chatman, who traffics largely in gold and platinum record
awards, offers the following item:

"KATE BUSH 'Hounds Of Love' In-house CD Award -- Presented to
    Music Industry Person.....................................$700
(Note: This is the only U.S. Kate Bush award known and it was
    given simply for sales and market expansion into the U.S.)"

Anybody know anything about this?
