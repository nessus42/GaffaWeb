Subject: Looks like I'm going!


I thought I wouldn't be able to go to the convention, but I think now
that I can!  My problem was lack of time.  Most airlines have a fare
of something over $500 round-trip if you stay a minimum of six days,
which I can't.  Without the minimum stay, the fare jumps to $1300
round-trip!  But it turns out that Continental has a $700 round-trip
fare if you stay over a weekend -- perfect for me.  I'm so excited!
