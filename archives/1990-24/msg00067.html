I don't know Hammersmith all that well.  I've been to a few concerts
at the Odeon, and driven through it once or twice (traffic is an

Coming from Heathrow simply take the underground (Piccadilly Line) to
Hammersmith.  From Gatwick, take the train to Victoria, then the
underground (District Line) to Hammersmith.  There are several different
exits from the underground.  I don't think you can get to the north side
of the Broadway from the District/Piccadilly Lines.  There is definitely
an exit on the south side (on the corner where the -O- is on the map).
The Hammersmith and City Line has a separate station.

Hammersmith Palais is a short walk up Shepherd's Bush Road, on the left.
I can't remember exactly how far it is (I don't think it's as close as my
map implies).  There are several other small roads not shown above.

Traffic congestion is apalling, despite the A4 flyover (which runs above
Talgarth Road) since Hammersmith is a sort of crossing-point of several
major routes.  Most roads are one-way.

Hope this is of some use.  See you all there!
