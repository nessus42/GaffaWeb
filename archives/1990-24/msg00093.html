>>I just have a quick question: What is "Hair of the Hound"?  If you can't 
>>believe that I've never heard of it before, let me explain that in order to 
>>get "The Whole Story" around here, you have to order it.  It's not available 
>>in any of the record stores off-the-shelf.  Laurie Anderson's "Home of the 
>>Brave" is not available at all, on order or off-the-shelf.

>  Suggestion - move somewhere near a decent record shop!!! :-)

Actually, "The Whole Story" video is getting hard to find in 
America.  I haven't seen it anywhere in about six months.  Even
Tower Records, which in the past has had quite a few copies at
some stores, hasn't had any lately (and I've looked in five local
branches of that chain).  I'm pretty sure it's still in print, so
you should be able to order it, but stores seem to have run out.

As for "Hair of the Hound", that was *never* available in America,
except as a Japanese import.  (Of course, the British import won't
play on American equipment.)
