From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)

Subject: Other things from the Oct. _Q_ magazine


I've been telling you about the KT Bush Band picture that comes with the
latest issue of _Q_.  In the magazine itself are a couple of things:

There's a bunch of pictures of rock stars with their parents.  One is of Kate 
with her mother Hannah and her brother Paddy.  It's a tiny picture, and 
besides, I've seen it before.  (I think it's in the _Visual Documentary_.)

There's a review of The Cocteau Twin's new album, _Heaven or Las Vegas_.
It's a rave, but I just wanted to quote one sentence:  "They must be doing
something right because Heaven Or Las Vegas is their finest hour yet -- 10
exquisite moments that make Kate Bush -- their only possible comparison --
sound as airborne as Motorhead."
