I was watching 120 Minutes on MTV Sunday night.  As I sat down to a song I
did not recognize, I saw a flash of Kate from Cloudbusting.  Upon closer
examination, I noticed that the video was interlaced with clips from the
Cloudbusting video and that the one line being song over and over was Kate
singing "I just know something good is gonna happen".  The song was called
"Something Good", and I can't remember the artists name.  Not bad.
