I found yet another Kate Mention - this time in the oh-too-hip
British Comic/Music mag "Deadline".  (It's worth picking up just for
a couple of my faves, "Milk & Cheese" - Dairy products gone bad,
and "Tank Girl", sort of a female Mad Max, but with much more violence.)

Anyway, they were interviewing the band "Spiritualised" (half the time
spelt "Spiritualized") (No, I never heard of them, either).  A quote
from them: "We'd quite like to work with Kate Bush; not because we're
big fans or anything, but purely in terms of the sort of sound she gets,
the feel of the things she does."

I wouldn't have even bothered to read the interview, but on the Splash
Page, in big letters:

"We'ld quite like to work with Kate Bush..."

THAT is going on office wall.

- Stev0 the fanboy
