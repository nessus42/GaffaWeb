Tori Amos will be a guest on Love Line tonight (10 pm PDT, 9/8) on the 
radio station KROQ (106.7) in Los Angeles.  I caught the end of an 
interview with her on another station's morning talk show last week.  
She played keyboard and sang 'Precious Things' and 'Leather'.  She was quite 
talkative and discussed some of the motivations of her songs.  She also
had an amusing anecdote about 'Leather':  the Mustang Ranch in Nevada plays
'Leather' in the lounge where their customers wait.  That must put them 
in a strange frame of mind.

Did anyone see Tori at any of the Los Angeles or Coachhouse shows?  They
were all sold out by the time I went for tickets.
