I've had good, bad and indifferent service from both.  C-Side
usually has better prices, sometimes by quite a bit.  IGG takes
plastic, C-Side doesn't.  Neither of them has everything.

If you don't mind waiting for your check to go through or paying
COD, I'd recommend calling C-Side first.  But if Tom doesn't have
what you want or you can't live with the terms, you can call IGG in 
good conscience.  In my experience, IGG is not the Evil Incarnate
that they have been reported to be here.  Allan Balliet (of IGG)
runs a business and happens to be a Kate fan, Tom Richards is a
Kate fan who happens to run a business.

goodies, which they both seem to be scarfing up as quickly as 
possible)
