Subject: DJ lists Kate as a favorite

KITS San Francisco ("Live-105") has a weekly ad in the San Francisco
Bay Guardian.  In this week's issue (Sept. 2), they ask some of their
DJ's to name their favorite artists.  Mark Hamilton lists Kate, tied
for no. 5 on his list.  (His show is from 10 am to 2 pm, weekdays.)

I already knew that Mark Hamilton is a Kate fan, from having spoken to
him, but it's nice to see it confirmed in print.
