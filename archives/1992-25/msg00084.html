Well, it's SORT OF a Bush single... actually, it has generous samples
of Bush.  And I'm not talking about the Utah Saints "Something Good."

I'm not even talking about Kate.

I'm talking about that slimebag in the Oval Office.  A San Francisco
band, "A Thousand Points of Light" has a great new techno-dance song
called "Read My Lips".  I personally can not stand techno-dance
(My motto:  Disco STILL sucks!), but this is a great piece.  They took
various sound bites of George's, and re-edited them to make him say
what he means (eg, "We have to protect obsolete weapons systems - and
you pay the bill!")

And you thought all the musical Bushes were on THAT side of the pond.
