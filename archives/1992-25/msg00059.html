Hello, and Welcome to the All-Out Kate Bush Music Poll.  There are alot
of songs in general which we all know if we like or not.  I figured it
would be a good idea to see exactly where each song ranks among Kate's
material.  The material covered by this poll are all the albums, "the
Whole Story", the 2 B-side CDs, the "Rocket Man" single, and her
duet with Peter Gabriel, "Don't Give Up".

The voting will be done under a simple system. How songs will be ranked
will be determined by average score each song has. Place a number 1 thru 10
(1 being horrible to 10 being top of the line) on the left hand side
of each song/album. Here is an example.

8  The Kick Inside 
10 Lionheart
8  Never ForEver

It is pretty simple to do. Voting will end on September 14, 1992. Results
will be posted on September 16, 1992.

Send all replies to v129j6ed@ubvms.cc.buffalo.edu

Remember, KaTe is watchin' you.
