I'm not trying to start a flame war, but I have to say this...please
check with Tom at C-Side and order from him if you can, instead of
Intergalactic Garage. Tom is a genuine fan, and always goes out of
his way to help other fans. He's in the "business" for the love of
Kate and her music, not just to make money. The IG guy, on the other 
hand, thinks Kate Bush fans are "weird" and deals in Kate just
because he knows that they'll spend money.

1) yes, I know we (some of us anyway) *are* "weird" but that's
   beside the point. I wear my weirdness as a badge of honor, he uses
   the term in a derogatory manner. There's a difference.

2) nothing wrong with free enterprise, but I feel that fans should 
   try and support other fans whenever possible.
