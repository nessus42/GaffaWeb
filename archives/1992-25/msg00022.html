        Thanks to Chris for clarifying the Guns and Roses Kate-like 
tatto thing.  The video that show's the "Kate-like" tattoo is none 
other then the video for one of my favorite 80s songs, Sweet Child "O" 
Mine.  It can be seen towards the end of the song in the "where do we 
go now" section where Axel is wearing a sleaveless shirt.  It's on his 
right arm on the top and looks an awful lot like 78/79/80 Kate.  You 
can see it quite clearly, but its difficult to say for sure if it's 
her.
