Subject: Sarah McLachlan profile on MusiquePlus

Sorry for posting this so late...

MusiquePlus will be broadcasting a profile of Sarah McLachlan's videos at
Wed Sep 2 21h EDT. It will be repeated at Thu Sep 3 at 5h and 13h.
The usual format for their profile show "Rock en bulle" is to play 15 to 20
minutes of videos with short excerpts of interviews in between.

I'm not sure how useful this information will be. Since MusiquePlus is
generally only available on cable in Quebec, you will need a satellite dish
to see it elsewhere. I have heard that MP is on one of the Anik satellites
(E1?) and that it was an unscrambled signal.

OBKaTe: I saw a broadcast of "Jeux sans frontieres" yesterday on TV5. Peter
Gabriel's song of the same name is very accurate description of it. It is a
game show where teams of people from different countries wear silly costumes
and compete in silly games. To people in the US, the American Gladiators show
is comparable. I can't believe I wasted my time watching this show.
