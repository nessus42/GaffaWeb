     Leonard Maltin's Movie and Video Guide is considered the definititve
     movie guide for many film buffs (available in most bookstores).
     Here's what it says about "Curse Of The Demon:"

     "Curse Of The Demon (1958-British) 83m. ***1/2 [out of a possible
     ****] Director: Jacques Tourneur.  Dana Andrews, Peggy Cummins,
     Niall MacGinnis, Maurice Denham, Athene Seyler.  Andrews is a
     stuffy cynical psychologist who doesn't believe that a series of
     deaths have been caused by an ancient curse, but film convinces
     audiences right off the bat and never lets up.  Charles Bennett
     and producer Hal E. Chester adaptated Montague R. James' story
     "Casting The Runes."  Exceptional shocker, originally called
     NIGHT OF THE DEMON, and running 95m. in British version.  Available
     on video."

     Sounds like something that Love-Hounds could put right next to
     "Wuthering Heights" on their list of Kate-related movies!
