Some more info: His name was Frederick Delius. His long time protege, Eric
Fenby, wrote extensively about his mentor, and is the person to whom
"Delius" in the song says, "In B, Fenby."

Kate appeared with Fenby on a TV show (Russell Harty) and performed the
song--Fenby appeared amused, and said Delius would have appreciated it.

What I've heard of Frederick Delius is quite good--very emotional and
melodic. I believe his Second Symphony (don't quote me on this) is a good
place to start. I heard it on classical radio and was very impressed.
