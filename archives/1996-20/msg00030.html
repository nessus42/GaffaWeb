Commiserations to UK Lovehound Steve Thomas on just failing
to make the finals of University Challenge with the Warwick
University team. Despite a slow start they very nearly caught
a strong Imperial College team. Even the Red Shoes sweatshirt
(from the Convention) that Steve was wearing for the event
was not enough to ensure victory. Unfortunately, there were
no Kate songs in this round and Warwick did not fare too
well on operatic arias. Anyway, it was great to hear them answer
Toxteth O'Grady USA to one question.
