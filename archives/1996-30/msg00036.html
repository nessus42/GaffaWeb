San Francisco Katemas '96

From: wisner@gryphon.com (Bill Wisner)
Date: 14 Jul 1996 11:48:23 -0700
Subject: San Francisco Katemas '96

The 1996 San Francisco Katemas gathering will be held Saturday, August 3,
at my house in Half Moon Bay.

At 7:30pm on August 3, Sarah McLachlan, Suzanne Vega and Paula Cole will be
playing at the Berkeley Greek Theater.  Tickets are $35 and $25 and there
are reportedly still some to be had.  You can order them from BASS at
+1 408 998 2277.

Since several attendees (myself included) will be attending this concert, the
Katemas festivities will start at noon.

Katemas is an annual gathering of Kate Bush fans in honor of Kate's birthday.
Expect to find friendly chat, Kate rarities (video, audio and print) and very
probably another cutthroat round of Kate Jeopardy.

A barbecue will be operational and ham-, turkey- and veggie-burgers will be
provided.  Guests are asked to bring other sorts of food, drinks and snacks.

All are invited; just please RSVP to wisner@gryphon.com with the number of
people you'll be bringing so we can lay in appropriate quantities of grub.
Directions will be sent upon receipt.
