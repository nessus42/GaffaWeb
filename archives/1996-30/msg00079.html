	I believe these are the bootlegs of the 'Extra Track' 
CDs from the "This Woman's Work" 8 CD boxed set...well, two of 
them anyway. "This Woman's Work" was released in 1990 and 
contains Kate's commercial releases from "The Kick Inside" 
through "The Sensual World" as well as two CDs of off-album 
tracks, b-sides, extended mixes and a few live tracks (from 
Hammersmith).

	These two extra CDs are available as bootlegs -- and the 
bootlegs actually contain three additional tracks that aren't on 
the originals. A third and fourth bootleg CDs were added, which 
have no corresponding 'original' from the boxed set. TWW III 
contains 10 tracks, most of which are b-sides to "The Red 
Shoes'" singles. TWW Live contains about 17 tracks of 'Live' 
performances, most of which were taken from TV shows and benefit 
concerts. A few are from Hammersmith, but not many.

	Here's the track listing for TWW III:

Cloudbusting (video mix)
Eat The Music (Extended Mix which is actually shorter)
You Want Alchemy
Moments of Pleasure (Instrumental)
Shoedance (Red Shoes Dance Mix)
Home For Christmas
Show A Little Devotion
Candle In The Wind (Instrumntal)
Rubberband Girl (12-inch)
Don't Give Up
