"Backsides" has the following tracks:

1) December Will Be Magic Again
2) Warm and Soothing
3) Ran Tan Waltz
4) Fullhouse (??..same as on "Lionheart")
5) The Empty Bullring
6) Burning Bridge
7) Not This Time
8) The Handsome Cabin Boy
9) Under the Ivy
10) The Big Sky (Meteorlogical Mix)

All of the cuts are taken from vinyl singles, not the bonus CDs in the
boxed set. Since "Backsides" is incomplete (and has inferior sound), I
would pass on it and pick up the "This Womans Work - Extended Edition" CDs
instead. These discs are boots copied directly from the CDs in the box
with a few bonus tracks added (Rocket Man, Candle In the Wind, Rubberband
Girl (extended version)).
