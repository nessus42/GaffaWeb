Ok, now that the people responsible have mentioned the existence of the
second Love-Hounds KolleKTion, I can publicly say THANK YOU to everyone
who put it together and got it to all the KaTemas party hosts in plenty
of time for party viewing!!!  Even though we had a *very* small group,

In other news, in the true spirit of the KaTemas season I have KonverTed
someone, specifically my boss, who asked me the other day to introduce her
to some new music.  I gave her a bunch of tapes and CDs from artists ranging
from KaTe to Sarah McLachlan to David Sylvian to John and Mary, and of them
all she totally flipped for KaTe (TSW, if anyone's keeping score) and thought
Sarah was pretty damn nifty, too.  I'm going to let her borrow HoL, TRS and
TKI tomorrow, and see what she says.  I don't think I'm ever going to see my
TSW tape again, but that's okay, I have several copies on CD. :>
