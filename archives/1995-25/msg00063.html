Until Kate breaks down and transfers the original tapes to CD (which 
is hardly likely, as I hear she thinks of this work as juvenile), Home
Demos is what we're stuck with. And it's not all that bad. I have it
as well as 'Shrubberies,' and although I think they're from different
sources (I think I hear a scratch, or 'thumping' on a track of Home
Demos that isn't on the same track of Shrubberies), they're pretty
darn close in quality. Or lack thereof.

BTW, 'Shrubberies' contains only 26 tracks, compared to Home Demos' 
29. It comes in a gatefold cardboard jacket instead of a jewel box.
And although there are more pictures, they're of the 'candid' variety
(i.e. not great of Kate) and are very small. Among the 'missing'
tracks are: 'Scares Me Silly,' and the second version of 'Babooshka.'
I don't remember what the other two are (yes, two, as Shrubberies has
a demo of 'Moving' that Home Demos doesn't.)

Before selling it back, contact John Teo at

teocs@merlion.singnet.com.sg

I know he'd buy it from you.
