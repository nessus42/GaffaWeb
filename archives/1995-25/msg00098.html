OK, some of these were guesses and of course can be way out, I've
also not listened to the whole CD while comparing the words with
what is shown in the wordsheet I have (which may also be out of date).

I hope this helps out and others benefit from my observations,
it might help since I was brought up in the same part of England
as Kate (about 20-30 miles away), although I often think she has
more of an Essex accent really (no insults intended), not Kent.
Hmm, the accent is close, maybe her parents were from Essex, or
had some London in them. I don't mind really, I quite like accent
variations, they really fancinate me.
