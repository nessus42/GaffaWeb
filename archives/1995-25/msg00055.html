Well, really!   Even suggesting that Mar....hang on.....MARMITE could 
possibly be bad is tantamount to heresy!

My name is Will and I'm a MARMITE-0holic.    I'm convinced that once 
tasted even the Gods would forsake Ambrosia (although they make 
lovely Rice Pudding and Custard) and Nectar in favour of this 
fabulous yeasty spread!

On bread and butter, toast and with cheesy things, to enrich and 
enliven soups, gravies and stocks.....MARMITE is the best thing 
ever!   It full of the B vitamins and has plenty of iron so it's 
really good for your blood and excellant as a vitamin supplement for 
pregnant women (although frankly I'd like to see a pregnant man 
once in a while)   You can have it as a drink - in fact I once saw in 
a recipe book written by Monks that they drink it not only with hot 
water but with hot milk, too!   I have to admit that this doesn't 
really appeal to me but that's monks for you - trying to be like 
God/ess, I guess........
