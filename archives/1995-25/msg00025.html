jigs and reels during the reception, but we started our ceremony with
a live performance of *Under the Ivy* sung by Chrissie, the best
friend of my wife's who is also a classically trained singer.  During
the ceremony we stood under a trellis convered with ivy and white
roses.  It was all I could do to prevent bursting into tears during
the song.
