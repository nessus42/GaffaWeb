I'm not sure if we're talking about the same record but I'm lucky to
have a vinyl album called Back Sides. I found it in London already 
in 1990. The contents are: 

A1. December Will Be Magic Again
A2. Warm And Soothing
A3. Ran Tan Waltz
A4. Full House
A5. The Empty Bullring
B1. Burning Bridges
B2. Not This Time
B3. The Handsome Cabin Boy
B4. Under The Ivy
B5. The Big Sky (Metorological Mix)

The versions are alike the commercial releases, so it's not worth
buying this album if you have the particular singles or This Woman's
Work boxed set.
