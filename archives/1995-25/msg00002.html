Hi Everyone, 

May I third or fourth the positive remarks posted by others about the SF 
party.  I was able to stop in for a while, about halfway thru the debut
of the new tape.  Boy, IED, what a bang-up job.  John and Karen, your 
contributions are not overlooked; you all did quite a great job on this 
tape-it will sit proudly next to my vol. 1 set. 

I agree about the Jeapordy game-whooo! I had to leave before "final" 
jaepordy (never could spell that word), but I was battle-worn even by 
then.  Our team was holding up well considering we were outmanned by 
our humble(?) psycho-modeerator helping the other team every chance he 
got just so he could watch Jessica get mad. ;-)  It was hot, and I don't
just mean the record 99 degrees setting a new record for SF. :-) 

Lots of new stuff on this volume 2 of the Love-Hounds series.  Well, not 
"new" new stuff; with Kate you get what you can get.  Even a cleaner "Mrs.
Mopp" dub of the "Army Dreamers" performance (why am I not surprised that 
this would end up on any tape to do with Andy :-)? I dug the raw footage
of the last Kon-poor Kate; the way people were following her every move
makes me appreciate her reluctance to do much publicly anymore. 

I treasure each Katemas as an opportunity to renew old aquaintences, see 
old friends, meet new ones.  This was no exception.  Nice to see you all 
again- Karen, "natalie merchant", Jessica(!), all the old faces.  I hear
talk of next year's bay area party being in Half Moon Bay!! Coooooool! We
can go look for the ninth wave or something... 

Larry Hernandez -San Jose CA (larry@hal.com)
