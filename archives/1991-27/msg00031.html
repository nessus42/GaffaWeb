Just went to the record store to pick up Innocence Mission's
_Umbrella_.  "Sat In Your Lap" was playing when I entered the store.
Of course, then I had to stick around a bit to find out whether it was
_The Dreaming_ or _The Whole Story_.  It was the latter, which meant I
had to stick around just a bit longer to listen to "Experiment IV",
which I don't hear often.

To add to the serendipity, I was wearing plaid...
