Apparently, yes, official.  If it sells, there's gotta be sequels.  (And
when I checked, I found I'd misremembered the price for the Revolver
box-- only $70, so not unthinkably pricey.)

>    Then you write: "Larry H. mentioned in May that a new can was

> What do you mean, "can"? A CD in a can? On what label? Is this
> official Kate Bush material? What do you mean, 'new material'?
> Only guest-vocals by Kate on other artists' records? Do you refer
> to Larry Hernandez, or some other Larry H.?

I can't picture the can business, myself!  It's 'unofficial' but I'm
pretty sure new to the 'unofficial' cd market.  The Comic Relief stuff
I think is also supposed to be on there.  Larry Hernandez posted about
it back in May or so, but it's been shelved and revived since then.
Somehow it's all supposed to be packaged in a can.

(I saw a new CD of the Cathy Demos in WaxTrax yesterday for $38.  It
appeared to include all the good stuff except Organic Acid on one CD.
I forget the title, but the cover art was a drawing of Kate, not a photo.)
