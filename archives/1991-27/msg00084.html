A conversation at Tower Video.

From: ed@wente.llnl.gov (Ed Suranyi)
Date: Fri, 2 Aug 1991 18:50:02 -0800
Subject: A conversation at Tower Video.
To: love-hounds@eddie.mit.edu


I went to the local Tower Video store to find out about the alleged
release of _The Single File_ in the US.  I went up to the clerk, pointed
at the line in the new releases section of _Pulse!_ magazine, and said,
"Where is it?!"

The clerk said, "It's not out yet.  I forget what the street date is,
but it's supposed to come out later this month."

I said, "Is it REALLY coming out here?  It's been available in England
for many years but has never come out in the US.  I'm a little skeptical
that it will finally be released."

So the clerk said, "It will DEFINITELY be released here soon.  I
personally ordered several copies for this store!"

So I guess it really is coming out!
