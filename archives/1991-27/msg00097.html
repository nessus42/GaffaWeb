Actually, it's still in print in the UK.  I bought an import copy of the
CD out at school about 6 months ago, and have seen another 4 or 5
copies at various stores since then.  If you're a fan of Gabriel, I can't
recommend it highly enough...and I paid $18 for the ~35 minute album!
The liner notes say, "WARNING:  This record contains re-cycled material
and no lyrics."  The track listing says where many of the musical themes
first appeared (for instance, "Close Up" is a beautiful, slow version of part
of "Family Snapshot" and "The Heat" is based around "Rhythm of the Heat.")
However, not all of the (Gabriel-penned) sources are listed, so you get to
try to figure out just where you heard that little bit before!

If it's any help, the album is on Charisma/Virgin, catalog # "CAS CD 1167"
