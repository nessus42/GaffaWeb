> and the infamous Japanese videos, which are a scream.  I am probably going
> to hang up my Kate parodying gloves after having seen what those Japanese
> folks did to "Them Heavy People."  For those not blessed enough to have seen

A nearly unwatchable copy of this great moment in Kate's career was
among our Katemas viewing as well.  Every time we see this we laugh 
until bladder control becomes a very dicey thing.  This must have been 
a pivotal point in Kate's career.  She never did anything quiet that
silly again until the 153rd identical lip synch of _Running Up That
Hill_.

If I were her I'd call out the goon squad to retrieve every single
copy of this thing.  Keep the home demos, keep the b-sides bootlegs,
just fork over the Japanese video or we'll epoxy your shins to the 
wainscotting and feed you lentils until you explode.
