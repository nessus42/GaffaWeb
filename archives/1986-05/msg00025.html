THANK YOU,  I HAVE BROKEN ENGLISH, DANGEROUS AQUAINTENCES(SP.?) AND A 
CHILD'S ADVENTURE. 

BROKEN ENGLISH- SCORNED WOMAN WITH A CHIP ON HER SHOLDER.

DANGEROUS AQUAINTENCES- REFLECTING ON HER PAST. LOTS OF PAIN, LITTLE JOY.

A CHILD'S ADVENTURE- BATTLES WITH DRUGS AND ALCOHOL.

ALL ARE CAPIVATING MASTERPIECES.  HER VOICE IS ENOUGH TO MAKE YOU WANT TO TURN IT OFF, BUT IF YOU SAY WITH IT ...YOU WILL SOON BE RAPPED UP IN THE LYRICS AND THE HARSHNESS OF HER VOICE AS PAIN.

PEOPLE LOVE TO MOURN WITH THE LIKES OF MARIANNE AND MORRISSY FROM THE SMITHS.
