New York City area = New York City, North Jersey, Eastern
Pennsylvania, sothern Connecticut...

In case you live there, and don't know about U68 (channel 68)...
It's an alternative video channel to MTV, and also broadcasts in
stereo...and best of all FREE!
PLus, they play more progressive videos than MTV...
like, The Cure, Jesus and the Mary Chain, SIouxsie and the
Banshees, PiL, Psychedelic Furs, New Order, The BOngos,
The CUlt, Echo and the Bunnymen, and LOTS of Kate Bush!
