No Subject

Bowie's "Absolute Beginners" is #1. Sigue Sigue Sputnik drop 6 spots to
#13 with "Love Missile F1-11". "Peter Gunn" comes in at #24 from nowhere,
the Pet Shop Boys' "Love Comes Quickly" comes up 8 notches to #23. New Order's
"Shell Shock" is at #25, also from nowhere. "Hounds of Love" drops from
#24 to #46, and "Marlene on the Wall" from Suzanne Vega pops in at #48. At
the bottom of the list, "C'mon C'mon", from the Bronski Beat, at #50.

As for LP's... "Brothers in Arms" is still #1. "Hounds of Love" is sitting
pretty at #4, sandwiched between "No Jacket Required" and ELO's "Balance of
Power". Talk Talk's latest, "The Colour of Spring", is in the #9 position
with a new single due out soon... Depeche Mode's new album "Black Celebration"
comes into the chart at #22, with the first single "Stripped" coming in at
#5, up from #7. Red Lorry Yellow Lorry's album "Paint Your Wagon" jumps into
#48 its first week in the charts. The Damned have their album "Damned But Not
Forgotten" drop to #49 from #46. At the bottom, "Alone" by Nana Mouskouri.
