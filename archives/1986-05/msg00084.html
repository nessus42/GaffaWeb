In followup to discussions concerning HoL, I must confess that I love the LP.
If Kate is mentally masturbating, I hope she continues to do so in the future.

100%.  There is a lot more to music than harmony and other traditional
partitions.  Texture and sounds go back in my past a long way.  Emotions are
another thing which I believe make up the bulk of music appreciation.  It takes
only an ape with a stick to beat out an odd rythm, it takes human intelect to
be able to do it again, knowing that it was important the first time.  If 
everyone sounded like the Rolling Stones we wouldn't ever be able to say
"Wild Horses was different..."
