for the time: pretty horrid.  This is the domestic I'm talking about

are OK, none of them are going to win any engineering awards.  The
domestic version of The Whole Story is apparently terrible and should
be avoided at all costs (sadly an impossibility if one wants
Experiment IV and the new Wuthering Heights - you could always try to
have someone mail you the import from Britain, I suppose)
