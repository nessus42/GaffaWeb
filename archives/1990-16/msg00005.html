Deb Wentorf recently posted a (very) descriptive list of the clips on
the video tape Kate_Bush: The_Collection.  While the majority of the
clips were not familiar, the first clip was known to me.  This performance
of The_Wedding_List comes from the Prince's Trust Concert (21 July 1982).
If memory serves, Kate only performed Running_Up_That_Hill and Let_It_Be
at the Secret Policeman's Third Ball.

By the way, anyone lucky enough to have seen Kate's performance of
The_Wedding_List (available on the U.S. video release of the Prince's
Trust Concert) knows that Kate has nerves of steel.  When her straps
snapped, Kate didn't even flinch.  Just the slightest hint of a smile
and she carried on with the song without missing a beat.
