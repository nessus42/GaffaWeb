Just discovered 'Back Sides' in one of Edinburgh's 2nd-hand record
shops. It cost me a tenner. Anyways, I got it home and I was playing it
while I was shaving (wet shave). Then Ran Tan Waltz came on ... Anyone
who has never tried to shave while listening to this song is advised not
to try it or they will stand accused of attempted suicide. Anyone who
can control their muscle-movements while this song is playing is dead.
Not that I've never heard RTW before but I've just never shaved to it
until now.

Slowly recovering in The Edinburgh Royal Infirmary For Facial
Reconstruction (where some would say I should have been long ago) ...
