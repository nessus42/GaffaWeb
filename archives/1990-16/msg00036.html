Because of the feedback I received from my inquiry on a Genesis
and Peter Gabriel mailing list, I will be responsible for initiating
one.   Information contained in the mailings will pertain to members
of Genesis past and present.   Lyrics to ALL songs will be available,
as well as a section on swapping bootleg albums, information, lyrical
interpretations, and anything else we can think of.  

I need an idea of the volume of people that will be involved.  If you
are interested, please e-mail me for confirmation.  The first "news-
letter" will be released within two weeks....

Thanks.. John.
