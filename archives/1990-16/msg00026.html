Well it is blindingly obvious if you are British but I guess not to
those overseas. Actually, Gryf Rhys Jones looks remarkably like the real
Bamber in the Young Ones episode. The programme University Challenge
ran for something like 20 years and only recently came off the air.
I believe BG was the host throughout that period. Although there were never
any shows quite as outrageous as Scumbag Vs Footlights, I have heard that
LSE caused uproar during one appearance in the sixties and there were always
a few students who couldn't avoid the odd childish joke or two which weren't
edited out. Trivia note - Clive James represented his Cambridge college
also in the 60s. Was there ever a US version of the show?

No I have never heard of another Bamber either Julian. Though there is
another Gascoigne who is going to become famous this summer as the star
of the World Cup ( don't mention US 1 England 0 :-( )
