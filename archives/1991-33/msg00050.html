Ohhh, I am getting tired of reading postings flaming the above
message!  I think the original poster has a point: if an amateur
musician and no recording contract can produce a well recorded,
engineered, and mixed CD, why can't a professional musician with her
own studio do this?  This is a valid question, IMHO.

Yes, it is saying something negative about Ms. Bush.  From what I have
read here, the criticism seems well deserved and it would seem there
is a general opinion that "The Sensual World" was not recorded all
that well.  I could be missing something here, but why are there so
many replies to the above message bashing the original poster?

I do *not* read the above to say that Happy Rhodes is better than Kate
Bush.  In fact, there is no allusion in the above sentences that even
comes close to stating that Happy Rhodes music is good.  It could be
pure dreck (pure supposition here, I have never heard any Happy
Rhodes).  *All* the above message states is that the "_Warpaint_ CD is
superbly recorded, engineered, and mixed" and that "The Sensual World"
has "below par" recording quality.

Chill out!  As Monty Python has said: "Nobody expects the Spanish
Inquisition!"  (Actually, I should probably chill out right about now
too. ;-)
