If you're a Gilmour completest be sure to pick up Warren Zevon's  
_Transverse City_.  It's not as good as _Sentimental Hygiene_ but its
best track, _Run Straight Down_, features Gilmour's guitar work to
excellent effect.  Gilmour's talents are used much more effectively
than on _Love and Anger_ and perhaps even as well as on _Rocket's Tail_.
It's sort of an bitter, mournful wail drifting across an ecologically 
devastated futurescape.

Speaking of Zevon, _Mr. Bad Example_ should be arriving soon.
