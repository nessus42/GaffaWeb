KaTe's last live performance was... well, if you want to stretch it a bit, the
little song she sang (to the tune of "My Lagan Love") at the KonvenTion last
November 17th.  At the same Kon she announced plans "to play a few shows at the
end of next year", meaning '91, which of course haven't surfaced- but, in true
KaTe fashion, plans seem to be put off, and will happen sometime... and in true
KaTefan fashion, we're anxiously awaiting news!  :)

Incidentally, if you, Chris (or anyone else who may ever be in the area) can
pick it up, Switzerland's state-run radio Programm 3 plays lots of
"alternative" music, or did when I was there in January.  On New Year's Day
they did a show of "The 350 best songs of all time", which was of course QUITE
subjective, but apparently compiled by listener response.  I heard lots of
KaTe, Pink Floyd, Franz Zappa, Bowie, OMD, even some Cocteau Twins... it was
way cool, especially since I hadn't heard that sort of thing on the radio in
months!!!  Also while I was in the listening area, they played "Iceblink Luck"
to death and other neat-o things you just wouldn't expect to hear on a
state-run radio station...

Thought that might make you feel better, Chris!  If you can pick up a Programm
3 station (this was in the German-speaking part of Switzerland, but the country
isn't *that* big), check it out!  :)
