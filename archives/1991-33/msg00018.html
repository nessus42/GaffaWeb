the actual title is _back sides_ and it contains a number of b-sides
from the 7" and 12" singles. sound quality is reasonablly good, but

new or rare on it. 

the track list: december will be magic/warm and soothing/ran tan waltz/
full house [why this is here is beyond me]/the empty building [sic]/
burning bridge/not this time/the handsome cabin boy/under the ivy/the
big sky (meteorological mix)

as you can see, there is no real incentive to purchase it, so save your
pennies.
