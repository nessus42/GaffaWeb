Peter Fitzgerald-Morris called me last week and said that, it's certain
that no single will be released by the end of the year. The album is
still being worked on, but "very near completion" though with Kate that
still means months more work yet to be done. The tour is still on, but
details (tour and album) are locked up tight and will remain so for a
while yet. That's all the Kate news he had.

Predictions? I'll gaze into my Crystal Orb and say that we should look
for the album next fall and the tour by the end of 1992. Hope I'm wrong
and it's sooner, but....

Peter called about a non-Kate matter, but I asked him about the next
issue of Homeground. He and Krys hopw to have at least 2 more issues
by the end of the year. They have been replacing the plumbing and their
house has been torn up since February! 

David Cross has been working on establishing an alternative distributing
company, and Peter said the first single released by the company will be
Karen Childerstone's version of "Under The Ivy" which she sang at the
Konvention last year.
