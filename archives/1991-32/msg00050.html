In article <4-mn+9d@rpi.edu> prince@aix.rpi.edu (Ron Buckmire) writes:
>What do people think I should get next, "The Dreaming", or "Hounds Of Love"
>and are they really necessary if I have her two best albums already anyway?

If your basic idea is that The Sensual World beats The Whole Story, then
go with Hounds of Love...most of the extremities that make The Dreaming
interesting for me have been sanded off in Hounds of Love and streamlined,
which is what I assume attracts you about The Sensual World.  The Dreaming
is the only Bush I'd ever own, personally. Heck, if you're into old stuff,
why not just spring for the rerelease of Low or Heroes instead?

I'm sure that the legion of Bushpersons will descend upon you in droves
now. Just thought you might like an opinion from a non slobbering type.
The Dreaming really is an interesting recording, even for nonbelievers
like myself.
