Just a small Kate story for you all...I was talking to one of my old high
school friends the other day and found out that she has an uncle who has
met Kate Bush.  He (my friend's uncle) is an opera singer who is studying
in Belgium under a well-known, highly-respected opera teacher.  Apparently
he was at an opera singer party one time, and who was there but Kate
herself!  I don't know if he actually conversed with her, but I know that
he had at least *some* contact with her.  Does anyone know if she commonly
"hangs out" those in the opera world?  I just thought I'd pass this little
story on to you all...
