A note to Carol Jean Swanson:
  If you're still looking for suggestions as to music to play for
your wedding, here's mine:  "With This Love," from Peter Gabriel's
_Passion_ (the soundtrack to _The Last Temptation of Christ_.)  It's
entirely instrumental, and it's *lovely*.  In fact, if the soundtrack
had been available when *I* got married in August of '87, I would
have seriously considered using the piece for my processional.
  Give it a listen and see what you think.  Whatever music you decide
on, best wishes to you and your future husband!  :)
