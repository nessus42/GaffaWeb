> BUT...all that being said, it IS worth it for 'Under the Ivy' 'The Man
> With The Child' 'Them Heavy People' and a different version of
> 'Wuthering,' none of which are available on CD elsewhere. Anyone know
> how to get the 'alternate live' version of 'Egypt?'

There are basically two different alternate 'Egypt's: the one from 'Kate'
(Christmas Special), and the one(s) from the tour.

'Kate' (Christmas Special) bootlegs:

Live In Europe 79 & 80 (2LP)
Live In Europe 79 & 80 (3LP, = 2LP plus Paris concert)
Moving (2LP)
Wow (2LP)
- (2LP picture discs, otherwise these, Wow, Moving, and the 2LP set Live... do
         have the same contents: the Christmas Special and the Hammersmith
         Odeon soundtrack)
Angels on Earth (CD)
This Woman's Live Work (CD)

Tour bootlegs including Egypt:

Live In Europe 79 & 80 (3LP) (Paris concert)
Dreamtime (3LP) (London Palladium concert)
Live In Bristol England 1979 (2LP) (Bristol concert)
Live In Manchester April 10th 1979 (2LP) (Manchester concert)
Temple Of Truth (1LP) (unknown concert)

I don't really want to listen to the vinyl live bootlegs to figure out if all of
them have truly an alternate version of Egypt on them, sorry... (sound quality
is so bad on all of them).
