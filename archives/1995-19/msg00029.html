This is a Kate Bush forum.  Discussion of other artists who are of
interest to Kate Bush fans is allowed, within reason.  When the volume
of such discussion becomes overwhelming, typically a new forum is
created.  Most of us are happiest when the discussion of other artists
takes the form of helpful pointers -- "if you like Lionheart check
out the new album by..." -- or when the artist being discussed does
have some direct connection with Kate (Peter Gabriel, Roy Harper).

Tori Amos has her own forum.  (really-deep-thoughts@gradient.cis.upenn.edu)
That is the appropriate venue for any serious discussion of Tori Amos.
Really love Tori Amos?  Great.  Go to RDT and join the love-in.  Really
hate Tori Amos?  Great.  Go to RDT and let *them* flame you to a crisp.
Whatever your opinion of Tori Amos and her work, this is not the correct
place to talk about it.

I am not going to attempt to prevent anyone from discussing any topic
they feel like broaching in this forum.  Community pressure does so
more effectively than I will ever be able to.  I am not laying down the
law here; I am offering a suggestion.  Remember why we're here: not to
carry on crusades against music we don't like, but to celebrate the music
we all love.

Thank you.

w.
