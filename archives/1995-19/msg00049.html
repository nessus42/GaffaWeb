Has anyone tried the Sara McLachlan CD/Multimedia disc?

It's great.  You get a 20minute quicktime movie on the

first track and 8 regular songs follow.  This is the best

of both worlds because you can play tracks 2 - 9 on your

regular music CD player.  Or you can play the first 20min

track on your PC.



Why can't Kate kick out something like this rather than

those funky CD-I discs?



The only complaint I have is that it's a quicktime movie.

Quicktime is great for Mac's but definitely isn't the norm

for Intel based systems.  It's especially troublesome on OS/2!
