The Save The Earth Foundation Celebrity Memorabilia Auction Opens 6-15-95
on
the World Wide Web http://www.commerce.com/save_earth 

Save The Earth Foundation kicked off their music memorabilia auction on
April, 22 and based on the auctions success and its popularity, "Save The
Earth" will continue it on a ongoing basis. We will offer 10 to 20 pieces
from a selection of hundreds of autographed items to be sold during a
monthly
auction.  

 The following is list of the auction items for sale this month:

The Eagles, autographed poster  
Jerry Garcia signed artwork
Grateful Dead Poster from Mardi Gras 1995
Hotel Trident San Francisco, "Wyland Suite" one night stay 
Billy Joel, autographed poster   
Alice Cooper, autographed poster   
Ozzy Osborne, autographed poster  
Lou Reed, autographed poster   
Gloria Estefan, autographed poster   
Guns 'n' Roses, autographed poster   
Red Hot Chili Peppers, autographed poster   
The David Singer Collection of 90 posters 

Internet users can view full color images of the items to be auctioned
online, check current bids and enter their own bids online. Internet users
can find auction information on the World Wide Web at
http://www.commerce.com/save_earth 

Proceeds are used to address urgent regional, national and global issues
in
air and water pollution, toxic and hazardous waste and damage to sensitive
ecological systems.  The Foundation is non-political in nature, awarding
grants to institutions of higher learning rather than trying to pass
specific
legislation or elect candidates.     

On behalf of The Save The Earth Foundation, thank you. If you do not have
web
access we can help place your bids.
