to reveal a few things about myself in order to stop the vicious and
hurtful rumours that I have read about myself on this 'BBOARD':

   important areas!
2. Yes! I have had my hair cut very short for several years now! It is
   also permed and bleached!
3. Yes! My once dear friend, Dell Palmer, has disassociated himself
   from me since I told him about my sex-change! I don't know how he
   never noticed!
4. Yes! I am a Seiko spy in Fujitsu! I am trying to convince Elvis
   that he would be better off at Seiko!
