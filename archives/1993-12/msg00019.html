Right before the big storm hit, I made a quick trip down to NYC where I 
picked up a bootleg that I don't remember being mentioned before.
It's called Butterfly Kisses ( Butterfly Kiss on the CD ) that features
live Kate performances.  These include ( from memory ) two versions
of Wuthering Heights ( probably from the German tv shows in 1978 ), 
Moving, The Long and WInding Road and She's Leaving Home from the Japanese
show "Sound in S", The Wedding List from Prince's Trust Gala Concert,
Breathing and Do Bears Sh.. from the Comic Relief Show, Running Up
That Hill from The Secret Policeman's Third Ball, Under the Ivy from
"The Tube", Them Heavy People and The Man With... from Saturday Night
Live, and I think Kite, Strange Phenomenon, and a few more tracks.

Of course, stuck in my house for a few days with this new gem
wasn't such a bad thing!  My personal impression was that the sound
quality was good ( but I'm no real audiophile ) and I would recommend
it to anyone looking for such performances.  Since the performance
locations were not listed on the CD ( I guessed the above from 
the Garden ) can anyone tell me if my guesses were right, and
where the other tracks come from?  Thanks a bunch...
