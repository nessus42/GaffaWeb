By the way (IED, who hasn't been around for a long while, will
probably be repeating something already reported long ago in
Love-Hounds), IED came across another new (?) interview LP in a New
York store the other day.  It's called "Just Saying It (Could Even
Make it Happen)", and its front cover shows a nice photo of Kate at the 
'87 BPI's (IED refers to the ceremony at which Kate was dragooned 
into handing Peter Gabriel an award), wearing a high-collared black
evening dress, with her hair pinned up.  On the back is a color photo
from Kate's appearance at the Laserium in 1985 for the premiere of the
"Hounds of Love" LP (where she was accompanied by Del Palmer for the
first time in a social context). The vinyl in the copy that IED saw
was a peculiar sort of purplish-Pepto-Bismolish opaque color.
Unfortunately, IED doesn't know what interview is on the album, as he
does not have access to a turntable, but it is identified as an
interview from "1985-86", so it may very well be THAT same old
interview once again. Does anyone know more?

-- Andrew Marvick (IED)
