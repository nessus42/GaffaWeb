One of the things which infuriated me when I first got my
"Two Rooms" disk was reading Sinead's quote inside the insert
which said "I can't believe no-one did 'Candle in thhe Wind.'"
My immediate reaction was, "Why are you complaining that no
one did it?  If you felt it should have been done, why didn't
*YOU* do it.

Now I've heard that Kate did it as the B-side to the CD-single
of Rocketman.  Does anyone know more about this?  I need it.
I need MORE KATE!!

In other news, I think I've answered my own post regarding
Sting while researching this post.  Sting did the song 
sequentially after Kate's 'Rocket Man' on the 'Two Rooms' disk.
As I flipped throught the liner-note book looking for Sinead's
quote there was our KATE, and directly across the page was Fade,
err.. I mean Sting.

So while Kate technically never collaborated on a song with
Sting (that I know of), someone *could* say they had worked together (on the
same album).  I don't think the previous poster
realized Kate & Sting hadn't worked together on a song.
