Last week I got a copy of "Great Pop Things" by Colin Morton and Chuck
Death (published by Penguin). It is a compilation of their comic strips by
the same
name that run in New Musical Express. IMHO it's hysterically funny. But
just to warn you, there is a strip about Kate B. in it that I fell on the
floor laughing
about but more reverent types might take offense to. 

Pick up on it.
