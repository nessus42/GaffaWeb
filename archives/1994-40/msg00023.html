I have vague memories of seeing Wuthering Heights on TOTP when I was about
nine years old, but it's only a vague memory. The first song that really
stuck in my mind was 'The Man With The Child In His Eyes'.    

It wasn't until The Whole Story came out that everything clicked. I bought the album for my mum as a christmas present; she didn't listen to it at all so I justborrowed it for a while (well a couple of years.)
