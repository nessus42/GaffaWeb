My first KaTesong?  The Wuthering Heights new vocal.

My dearest she'enedra Jennifer said, "Have you ever heard of Kate Bush?"

I admitted I had not.

She smiled, and put on "The Whole Story".  By her claims, all I did was 
listen and breathe until the side ended.  Then I just flipped the tape 
and listend to the other side.

I don;t remember...I was entranced.

What has this brought me?  Well, some poverty (finding "This WOman's 
Work" AND the CD Singles for Eat The Music, Rubberband Girl and Moments 
of Pleasure at the same store at the same time...and eating naught but 
Ramen for a month).  And a great deal of joy...and some deep thinking.
