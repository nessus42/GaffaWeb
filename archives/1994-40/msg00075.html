To start off with, this is my first time posting here (& I'm new to 
listservs/news groups to begin with), so if I breach any rules of 
netiquette, please forgive me (and tell me what I did wrong so that I can 
avoid it in the future.  Thanx!  Constructive criticism is never taken as 
an offence.)

I was wondering about the status of the Cathy demos as regards to 
copywright--I know that legally Kate should retain the rights to them 
even though she hasn't published them (at least, according to American 
copywright laws as I understand them--I'm no lawyer, so don't quote me on 
that.)  The reason I'm wondering is that I am a musician (trying to start 
a band sometime next year--know anyone in the Lawrence, KS area, by the 
way?) and I wondered how I'd get permission to cover one of these 
songs--would I have to write to Kate &/or her lawyers first?  (I have a 
feeling that she wouldn't approve since she's not so fond of these songs.)

At the moment this is of a more hypothetical "future reference" 
nature--I'm not signed to a label or anything.  But if I'd have no chance 
of ever releasing it, I don't want to waste my time working on it (except 
maybe for my own pleasure :-) )
