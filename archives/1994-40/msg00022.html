Hey, I was walking past the clothing department in Fred Meyer, a
department store chain in the Northwestern United States, and heard a
rather distinct voice.  In an effort to be as hip as more stylish stores,
Fred Meyer installed video monitors in their clothing department, too. 
I've never stopped to see what videos they were showing until today, when
I heard Kate say "man I love".  The piano, the leather jacket--I had not
seen this video.  (I confess, I also haven't paid attention to where I can
find "Man I Love", either).  I stood transfixed until they segued into a
Fuente video.
