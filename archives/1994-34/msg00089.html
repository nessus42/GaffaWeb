   I'd not seen anyone else suggesting including _The Line, The Cross
& The Curve_, so if it was someone else's dumb idea, forgive me for
jumping on your case. But I felt I had to be pretty forceful to kill
this in the bud. Copying TLTCTC would be the one sure way to get Kate
really pissed off at us. It would be *directly* ripping her off and
would serve no promotional purpose at all. 

   Even if it was your original idea, let me apologise for jumping on
you, and simply suggest that I wish you'd considered a few things
before posting:

   * There are people reading this with a domain name of sonymusic.com.
     These people would be seriously remiss if they didn't relate
     a plan to bootleg currently available commercial product on a
     worldwide basis to the interested parties at Sony. 

   * As the editor and originating point of the previous and future
     video collections, I'd be the obvious person to send threatening
     legal letters to. I don't want any of these. So I have to cover
     my own ass, which is a bit more exposed than yours. I still
     feel that "no fucking way" accuratly summed up my feelings.
     A "cease and desist" order would tend to put a damper on the
     possibility of future tape trees.

   * Please consider suggestions in the larger context of posting
     a message to thousands of other other people. I understand
     that the suggestion was made innocently, but please, we are
     very exposed here, doing things that are fairly gray.
