Having seen but a few of the Frutopia commercials I was struck by the
similarity between them and the add for Birdseye's frozen veggies.
Frozen veggies are constantly in motion, though not in kaleidoscopic
motion, all to backing music with a distinct Celtic lilt.  Does Birdeye
have any connections with Frutopia?  Has Kate lent her talents to another
advertising campaign?  Or are Birdseye just copying Frutopia's lead?
