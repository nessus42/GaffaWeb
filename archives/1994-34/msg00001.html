2.1. Who is Happy Rhodes?
  

     Also available is the IRC channel #ecto where at most times you can find
     some Ectophiles and #Ectophiles.
[see you there]

     Please note that Happy prefers it if you buy the albums in a normal shop.

[well, no more signed CDs. Happy doesn't have time for this anymore. Sniff...]
  

4.2. Tours

     1995:  Wanted to tour with just her and a piano, but was told that this
            would fuel those frequent comparisons between her and Tori Amos.

[I found out why there won't be a tour in 1995 either...]
  

     Early in November the next set of singles will appear, again in the UK
     only: And So Is Love. Two CD singles (regular and a box with prints) and a
     7" in a picture sleeve will appear.

[To US subscribers: go hunting those import shops!]

     A good short description of the contents of Kate's new 43 minute film
     The Line, The Cross & The Curve are the lyrics to the title song of The
     Red Shoes. The film will finally appear on video: UK and US is scheduled
     for October, Japan for December. All three releases will be available on
     VHS and LaserDisc.

[a little bit better (though not yet exact) release dates, and I finally looked
onto the clock while watching my video of this one for the umptiest time...
(thanks again, Kimmo!)]

4.4. Basic Discography/Videography

     Albums and other major collections:

     The Cathy Demos (30 early songs, ca. 1975-77???, a MUST for every fan)
        Five of these are demos of songs that later appeared on albums.
        They are available on various bootlegs. (Anybody have an early 80's
        album called THE EARLY YEARS?).

[again, I finally counted them]

     Also soon to appear: the US dance remix of Rubberband Girl which was also
     played at the Convention. It is said to be not very different from
     previous stuff. (Haven't heard it yet.) It will appear as a b-side for
     And So Is Love.

[yeah!]
