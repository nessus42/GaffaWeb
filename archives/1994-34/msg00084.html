I am now back here in Love-Hounds after 3 weeks of absense due to my 
transfer in my conpany.  My mail address has changed from 
sozo@crl.mei.co.jp to yamamoto@tvrl.mei.co.jp .

I think I must report that TLTCTC was aired on Japanese TV recently.
It was in a 50 minutes program (Oct. 18, 20:00 - 20:50) on JSB 
(informally called `Wowow') which is a payed BS channel.
Currently I'm not paying for the station and all I could see was
a scrambled picture without sounds.
I must wait to receive a parcel from my wife's friend, to whom I 
asked to record the program.

The program was with a full TLTCTC (already with Japanese caption,
I wonder why Toshiba must wait until December) followed by two clips.
First was Experiment IV, the second one was a clip I first look,
Kate with black glasses and grasping mike stand.  Is that the
notorious MTV version of Rubber Band Girl?
The program was with no interviews, no narration.
