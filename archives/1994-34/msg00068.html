Just a weak moment...don't mind me

I feel a little silly sharing this, but I was sitting in my office just now 
listening to TRS and "Moments of Pleasure" started playing.  I decided to stop 
what I was doing and really listen to the song--I've played the album many 
times and I always thought MoP a standout track, but I think this was maybe the 
first time I *really* let it sink in and wash over me.  

All I can say is--well, I don't know that any adjectives I can come up with 
would do it justice.   And having read on r.m.g. about the people and events 
that inspired the song--I'm quite shaken and I feel like I've just read 
someone's diary.  I am actually having a little bit of a physical reaction to 
it--a little flushed and disoriented, and yes, I did have to choke back a tear 
or two.   I feel grateful when I come to a song I've heard a lot with a sudden 
new intensity, but I must say that this has got me a little worried.  It 
reminds me not a small amount of how Paul McCartney's song "Here Today" (his 
song for/about John Lennon) affected me for a good year after the "Tug Of War" 
album came out.  It still gets to me sometimes; over ten years later.  

How wonderful that Kate is willing to share this beautiful, incredibly personal 
and moving song with us.  I feel most fortunate, really.

Sorry for the interruption; just my $.02 for today.  Thanks for reading.

--Connie
