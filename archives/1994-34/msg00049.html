I keep hearing about the UK single 'And So Is Love' and that it includes
the US mix of Rubberband Girl.  

I've heard a few Kate Remixes, I don't think there are all that many  
available, but if the RG US mix is the 7 minute one that comes on the US  
RG single, then I would say that its a pretty disappointing mix.  The idea  
of RG going on for 7 minutes isn't such a bad idea, but something about  
this particular mix just doesn't click for me.  Perhaps it is too much  
like the unmixed version extended a bit.  I'm not sure.
 
I did however enjoy 'shoedance' and the other remixes that appeared on the  
imported (from the UK) Red Shoes singles, so I don't think all Kate  
remixes are bad, just that the RG mix was disappointing.

One other question, I recently picked up an alternative music sampler  
which had 'Be Kind to my Mistakes' and a few weeks later, I found 'Aspects  
of the Sensual World' which, as you probably know, is more or less the CD  
single for TSW.  My question is, are either of these rare finds?  I'm sure  
they were readily available at one time, but I suspect they are a bit more  
difficult to come by these days.
