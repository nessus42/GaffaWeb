Subject: It's in the trees!

Video Alert!  Curse of the Demon, which is, if I remember correctly, the 
movie with the line that begins HoL, will be shown on cable station 
Cinemax on July 2nd.  

CD Alert: Alain Stivell's latest album has finally appeared at Tower 
Records in San Francisco.  As you remember, this album features Kate on 
bv's and production on the song Kimiad.  The CD is not in the bin, but on 
display above the bin if you go there looking for it.  They also carry 
the soundtrack to Brazil with Kate if you haven't found it anywhere.

Wonderful!: Sheila Chandra's new album - The Zen Kiss - has also appeared 
and as all of her work is an incredible masterpiece.  Sheila continues to 
explore the space of the voice -- every time I listen to one of her 
albums there are a few moments where the hair stands up on the back of my 
neck.  Her talent is unsurpassed.  Right up there with Kate in my book.  
Not for the faint of heart.

That's my report!

Karen kln@crl.com
