I would be very surprised if this store in Gothenburg would succeed
in importing this video from a country where the video hasn't even
been available. I don't know, but it seems to me that this store is
making you happy with a dead sparrow (as we put it here). As far as
availability goes, the video hasn't been released anywhere, and it
won't be until late this year, if at all this year. So they told me
at Fame, Hollands biggest music store, a few weeks ago. They keep
a close look on Kate, and have gotten hold of many rare items over
recent months, and had ordered the TLTCTC video, which of course
drew my attention.
 
> I don't recall the name of the store right now, but if there are

a few weeks after that...
