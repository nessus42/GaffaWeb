Got a mailing from Key Mail Order (big record dealer that advertises
in Record Collector) yesterday, telling me I was a Kate Bush Priority
Customer or some such and offering the Australian scratch'n'sniff Eat
the Music CD-single, but also a new release (think somebody alluded to
this recently) of "Live at a Hammersmith Odeon", comprising PAL VHS
video and CD of the soundtrack. Unfortunately, the track listing looks
identical to the original video. The price was about L16 I think. I
*assume* this is a kosher EMI release...pity they didn't have more
imagination...
