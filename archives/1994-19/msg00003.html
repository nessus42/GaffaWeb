The answer to life, the universe, and everything is 42.  For a long
time no one knew what the question was, though.  Deep Thought computed
the answer, but he had to build a new computer (The Earth) to
determine what the question (of life, the universe, and everything)
is.  Unfortunately, the Vogans destroyed The Earth a few minutes
before the result was due, in order to make room for a hyperspace
bypass.

At some time later, however, the characters hypothesize that the
question must be buried in Arthur Dent's mind, ready to plop out with
a little prodding.  With the help of some Scrabble pieces, they
determine that the question is "What is six times nine?"

I think that this is a bit off-topic, however.
