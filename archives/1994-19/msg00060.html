"The Line, The Cross, The Curve" will be playing at The NUART Theatre 
located at 11272 Santa Monica Blvd in West Los Angeles (310) 478-6379.

Saturday screenings are at 2:00, 4:45, 7:30, and 10:15.

I'm going to the 2:00 screening on Saturday. Maybe I'll see some other 
fans there.
