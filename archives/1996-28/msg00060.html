According to the Radio Times (29 June - 5 July) a new series of
radio programmes starts on BBC Radio 2 at 1000pm on Thursday
4th July. It is called "I Write the Songs"-  Don Black presents
a 6-part series in which he enjoys and interprets the songs of a diverse
collection of singer/songwriters. 1: Kate Bush.

There is a sidebar with a small photo of Kate and this text: "Kate Bush
first came to the attention of the British public with a hit inspired by her
love for the Emily Bronte novel Wuthering Heights. In this new series,
lyricist Don Black will be asking his fellow practicioners what has
inspired their lyrics, what they mean to them and how they have been
interpreted by other performers".
