Nettwerk Productions 93.002 Sarah/Ginger Tour Dates

Hi, just a quick posting from the friendly people at Nettwerk. We are going
to ocassionaly post major announcement to newsgroups, but for the most part
we send info out to whomever wants it through email. To get on our emailing
list, just send us a note and youll get whatever we send out, usually
something once every week to two weeks. Coming out next week will be the
latest version of the Nettwerk Newsletter and the mail order catalogue.

Here's the schedule for the Sarah McLachlan/Ginger tour of Canada. For our
American friends don't expect anything until February or March of 1994.
Europe after that. Ah, it's good to be Canadian.
