>From article <9309300938.AA05307@ginkgo>, by nbc@inf.rl.ac.uk:
>> I just rang up a big record dealer in the UK and asked whether they

> there are ongoing attempts to 
>do away with the sales of used CDs

I don't think the ban has anything to do with the controversy over
used CDs, but rather is a manifestation of restrictions on parallel
imports that have been around for a long time.  Those restrictions
have in many cases not been enforced, and I often wish they would be
removed entirely, but they are most likely the source of the problem.
