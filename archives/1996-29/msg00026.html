Hey all you lovehounds,

	Kate has just earned her highest accolade yet. She was 
the "hardest question on the board" on a gameshow called "Debt." 
No, I'd never heard of it either, but whilst rambling through 
the channels sometime last week, I happened on this cheesy copy 
of "Jeopardy" and almost immediately heard the words 'Rubberband 
Girl.'
	The clue was a 'Daily Double'-kind of thing -- and fell 
under the category of 'Bush.' The host referred to it as the 
hardest question on the board --  which wasn't saying much. The 
clue was: "This 'Rubberband Girl' sang 'Don't Give Up' with 
Peter Gabriel."
	I admit this wouldn't be brain surgery for anyone who's 
ever heard of her...but that's a small number in the U.S., so I 
was truly amazed when one of the contestants got it.
	Now back to your regularly scheduled programming.

	Robb
