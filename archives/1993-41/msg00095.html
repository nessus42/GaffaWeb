People have many times mentioned the resemblance between Kate Bush and
Mary Steenburgen (as well as Anne Archer).  One of my favourite
movies, probably for sentimental reasons, is Time After Time (1979),
starring Mary Steenburgen and Malcolm McDowell.  I saw it when quite
young and was mesmerized by her, and not just by her beauty (IMHO), so
it's not surprising that Kate has the same effect on me.  Anyway, in
this movie she's playing a liberated career woman.  She comes out of a
theatre showing "Exorcist IV" and says "I hate all that violence!  Did
you ever see 'Red Shoes'?  I really got off on that...... careers."
(As has been mentioned, the Red Shoes movie is about a ballet dancer.)
