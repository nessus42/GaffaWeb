OK all you little waves . . . I couldn't resist stopping by Tower S.F. 
last night to see if they are setting up for Monday.  The litho for the 
drawing is on display. 

Tower's December Magazine has mysteriously showed up a month early.  Page 
21 says of Kate:
"Kate Bush's new record, The Red Shoes, is to be acoompanied by a 50 
minute film to be premiered IN 40 U.S. CITIES.  The film, which features 
six tracks from her new release, stars Bush, along with actress Miranda 
Richardson and choreographer Lindsey Kemp.  The record features 
appearances by Nigel Kennedy, Eric Clapton, Jeff Beck and Prince."

Coming soon to a city near you!
