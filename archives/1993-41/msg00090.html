"Very" illegal?  Try telling that to Blockbusters, That's Rentertainment,

transactions, and naturally the credit card number is a part of that
transaction.  You may be confused with the laws in some states that

they receive.

I'd be far more concerned about the increased use of the Internet for
commercial postings than whether IGG keeps a file of credit card 
numbers.
