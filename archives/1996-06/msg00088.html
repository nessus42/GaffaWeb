T H E   K A T E   B U S H   D E M O S

        T H E   L Y R I C S

[first transcribed by IED (Andrew B. Marvick), later on improved and corrected 
by various love-hounds (e.g. Bryan Dongray and Scott Telford).] 

We should all try to use the Phoenix broadcast titles, because they seem to be 
correct. At least no inconsistencies have been found. IED's titles are given in 
brackets. 

Everybody is invited and encouraged to listen carefully again to the difficult 
passages. Some are almost inaudible. 

I went through the complete set several times and corrected  or inserted a
word here and there and tried to sort out the wrong suggestions. 
It's surprising how much we have correct now!
In a month or so I will put the complete and up-to-date demo-lyrics into the
LH-archive. 

What follows are only the main problematic passages: Listen again! 
Give it another try!  Bryan! Scott! 

Enjoy!
Wieland
