PS Despite my previous low score for TA BfP, I really quite like
the track "Hey Jupiter", not quite sure on the meaning yet, but
we still have similar discussions about some KaTe tracks!

interview with "Procul Harem" about "A Whiter Shade of Pale", which

just seemed to fit together nicely, nothing hidden, just good words".
