Hmm.  I loved the Gershwin.  Kate's voice was
a perfect fit for that style.  Good control, good delivery,
clear as a bell to a southerly wind.  Very like
the late twenties and thirties movies when they
used sopranos a lot because it was a range
and timbre the microphones of the day could
reproduce.

Yeah, she has a dent.  It is noticeable on the 
MTV Sensual World interview.  It is probably
the result from the wear in the early years
when everyone just HAD to kiss her.  Lucky sods.

I don't know about the Ian Anderson story.  I
have the Prince's Trust Concert and the strap
on the dress broke as she sang.  The dress is too
small by about a size and she filled it in thought
provoking ways.  To keep her left
wonderfulness from becoming more wonderful,
she grabbed the strap cradling the mic cord, 
hung on to the mic and didn't miss a note.  
The smile at the end with the reddening face 
is one of the best shots of her I've ever seen
of Kate.  She was perfectly composed (and
no doubt, well orchestrated)....

Tori who?
