Dear Chris Hansen,
I'm glad that you can fall "in love with Kate's voice all over again."
Personally, I don't think "The Man I Love" was well sung.  Her voice
has changed throughout the years.  For a torch song like this, I'd much
prefer Ella Fitzgerald, or even Dorothy L'amour for god's sake!  Kate's
voice just couldn't sing in that style effectively.  The instruments
outdoes her this time.  Nevertheless, I was glad to experience 
anything new by Kate.

Sincerely,
