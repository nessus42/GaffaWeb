To: love-hounds <love-hounds@uunet.uu.net>

While reading through today's (7/27) digest, I was thinking about something 
that had been posted a couple of days ago to the Love-Hounds list which sort of 
ties in with something going on on the RDT (Tori Amos) list.   Someone had 
asked about Kate covers by other bands--it interested me because I regularly 
perform "Running Up That Hill" (a popular choice, apparently!) during my sets.  
The reason RDT came to mind was because an enterprising subscriber (my 
apologies, I can't remember his name although I'm very sure he subscribes to 
this list as well) is putting together a compilation of Tori covers performed 
by members of RDT.   

I would imagine there must be more than a fair number of musicians out there in 
Love-Hounds land: has anyone thought about putting together a "homemade" tape 
of Kate covers as performed by various Love-Hounds?   Just curious...I thought 
it was a great way to fuel the devotional fires as well as spark some 
creativity.  

--Connie
