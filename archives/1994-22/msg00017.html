Ah, Lawrence.  Just left there two months ago after five years of
listening and playing music locally, and, oh yes, there was school
too.  A great town for music, quite a bit of diversity although you
have to keep your eyes open to find it among the masses of grunge
and thrash bands.

The guitarist and drummer from For Against (actually from Lincoln
Nebraska, another pretty cool music town in the Midwest) are currently
in a band called "The Millions", somewhat early 80's feel but
with greatly improved vocals... I can't remember her name but she's
got a cool voice.  A friend and I were wondering if she might be Kate Bush
influenced (so maybe this _does_ belong here!?).  If there are any
die hard For Against fans out there, I have the address of the
guitarist...
