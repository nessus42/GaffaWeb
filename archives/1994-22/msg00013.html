G'day all!

    Well wasn't all that tapetree stuff fun!! :-) I'd just like to add my voice
to everyone else's in thanking the "shakers and movers" of the whole thing. You
did good guys!! :-) I don't think I'll *ever* get over that 'Walking the Witch'
quote from the Night Flight interview. All I could think during that whole
thing was "Run NOW Kate!! Quick!! Run RUN!!" No wonder she hates interviews if
they turn out like that.

    Anyway, I may have just got my hands on something new!! Has anybody else
here heard of the 'Kate Bush: The Whole Story 94' CD-ROM?? It's a two CD set
that contains all of the clips form 'The Whole Story' vid and 'The Single File'
plus all of her other 'modern' vids from TSW and TRS all in MPEG format! I
havn't got it in my hot little hand yet but it's put aside for me in Sydney by
my music pusher. Cool! :-) I'll let you know what it's like when I get it.
