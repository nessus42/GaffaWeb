My thanks for that thought. HOMEGROUND 52 has just gone to the printers,
slightly delayed by the fact that our very close friend Paul Gill has
just had a collapsed lung and had to be rushed to hospital in Southampton.
As it never rains till it pours, the father of his wife our dear friend Pami, 
has just had a heart attack. At the same time they have just had an offer
for their property (been trying to sell for 18 mths!) etc etc......just
the background of an average HG putting together time!

Glad to hear all the Katemas events went well. We hear that a goodly crowd of
fans went round to Eat Wickham Farm bearing gifts, and another crowd met
atop Glastonbury Tor.

I hear that Kate's version of "Sexual Healing" may not after all appear
on the Davy Spillane album, and therefore may not get any sort of release
for the time being. 

Good wishes to all!!
