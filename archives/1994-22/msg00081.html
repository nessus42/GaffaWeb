I have been a Kate fan since 1982, was introduced to her music by
another female fan, and we are both more than just casual fans.  By this
I mean we tend to collect everything we can get our hands on.  Kate's
music means something very personal to me and her appeal (to me) has nothing
to do with her sexuality.


In fact, I know more female Kate fans than male Kate fans.  Must be a
geographic thing!
