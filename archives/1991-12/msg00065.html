To whom it may concern, probably our moderator:

I discovered the LHD through something called  "List of lists" that was 
ftp'd from nisc.sri.com: netinfo/interest-group.

The description for the Love-Hounds was as follows:

   This is a mailing list for the discussion of Kate Bush's
   music (and any otherr artisitic music or anything else
   for that matter). <sic>

The cover page of the "List of lists" says:

   To get on the list (or to submit updates to existing
   mailing-lists or to add new ones),  send requests to 
   interest-groups-requests@nisc.sri.com.

I personnally know nothing of the operation of the above, since the "List.."
was given to me on a floppy after having been dumped from UNIX.  The list was 
HUMONGOUS, as I remember, probably .5 Meg.  

However, after several recent posts by us (KaTe people) and him (an infidel) on 
the subject of the description of the Love Hounds, I thought I'd post the
above info.  By the way, who is Gene Spafford?  Kevin Martin mentioned him.
