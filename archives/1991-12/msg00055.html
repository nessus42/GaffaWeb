Reminded of Kate

From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward J. Suranyi)
Date: Wed, 10 Apr 91 19:13:02 PDT
Subject: Reminded of Kate


I was listening to the radio the other day when a song came on that
seemed very familiar, yet I didn't recognize it immediately.  But for
some reason I was strongly reminded of Kate.  It certainly wasn't
a Kate song, but the opening bars made me think of her.  A few seconds
later I realized why:  The song was "Don't Give Up", which I hadn't heard
in a LONG time.
