This is strictly from memory but most of it was the usual "thank you
for your support" type thing.  The part that I found interesting was
that she said that the album was coming along well but that she really
couldn't say much about it until it had been "recorded properly".

Perhaps I'm grasping at straws here but that would seem to indicate
that most of the writing and perhaps the initial demo recording is
complete.  Of course, this means we probably only have another year or
two to wait.  Hopefully the folks at Little Light will include the
message in their next issue.
