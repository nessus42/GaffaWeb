Last night on UK tv a film loosely based on Wilhelm Reich was shown.It was
called    'WR-The mysteries of the organism'.It originated from Yugoslavia and
is from 1971..It showed bits of film of him,and short interview clips with
Peter and Eva Reich,this was all intermingled with other stuff about sexual
theories.It showed orgone chambers being used etc.It was quite interesting!!
About the Comic Strip film,it is based on Ken Livingstone,who was the leader
of the G.L.C.(Greater London Council).This was abolished by Maggie Thatcher in
1983,because she didn't agree with Ken's views.Ken Livingstone is now an M.P
for the Labour party.
Also the programme about the history of Irish Music will be shown in the UK
from July 26th 1991,this is supposed to feature Kate.A book and CD will be
released in late May 1991.
