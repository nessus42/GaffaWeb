Interesting CD releases

From: RpK%acorn@LIVE-OAK.LCS.MIT.EDU

Subject: Interesting CD releases

* More Zappa, especially early stuff.  ``Freak Out'' is remixed, but as far
  as the booklet goes, there are no re-recorded tracks.

* ``From Brussels With Love'' 1981 cassette compilation with John Foxx, Thomas
  Dolby (home studio version of ``Airwaves''), Durutti Column, New Order,
  Alan Rankine, Brian Eno interview, Michael Nyman, Gavin Bryars, and others.

* ``Koyaanisqatsi'' soundtrack

* David Byrne's music for the ``Catherine Wheel.''  Might have even more music
  than the cassette.

* ``Le Mystere des Voix Bulgares'' on Nonesuch -- haven't actually seen the CD
  yet.

* And don't forget Sam the Sham and the Pharaos !
