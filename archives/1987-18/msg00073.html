I have seen _The Flat Earth_ on CD.  Also, a strange version of his
first album, which may be indentical to the domestic release (the name
of which slips my mind at the moment).  I prefer the inport album to
the CD.

> (4) have _The Smiths_ or _The Dead Kennedys_ put any of their albums
> on CD?

All the Smiths' albums are on CD, there are also quite a few CD
singles of various of their singles, including some songs that didn't
make it onto their albums.  All of the Dead Kennedys' stuff is
available, as far as I know, including their latest (a compilation).

Also available is the new Robbie Robertson eponymously titled CD,
which features work by Peter Gabriel, U2, Manu Katche', Tony Levin,
and is co-produced by Daniel Lanois.  Not bad.
