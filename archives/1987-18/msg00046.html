As my first posting to this auspicious group:

a request for information that I've been looking for for a while.
I have the 12" singles since "Running Up That Hill" and all the great
B-sides....are there 12" singles or normal singles from any of the
albums previous to Hounds that have unreleased B-sides?  I am aware
of "December will be Magic Again" but that's also released on the
B of the extended "Experiment IV".  Are there reviews of various
live boots available?  I found one ($24) live in Bristol, which has
such lousy sound quality that I would only recommend it to the hardest
core die-hard Kate fan.

as for Non-Kate progressive music.....has anyone heard of a group
called Digital Sex?  I have an album "Essence" and a mini-record
"Recovery" (which came with it and is by the lead singer with some
help from the other band members) that is quite good, especially the
song "Innocence at Will", which is primarily a very sparse but effective
drum track, very melodic bass lines that carry the song, and the
vocal....it's similar to some stuff friends of friends recorded some
years ago in a band called Emerald City, but I know that none of
Emerald City had anything to do with it.
