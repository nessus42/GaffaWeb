Pulse DID, again!

Subject: Pulse DID, again!
To: love-hounds@eddie.mit.edu


The new _Pulse!_ magazine just appeared in Tower Records (Sept. 1991),
and once again someone mentions Kate in his DID list.  Steven A.
Gutterman of New York City lists:

"4. _The Kick Inside_ -- Kate Bush.  Been in love with her since the 
 fourth grade."

For the record, here are his other choices:

1. Cheap Trick -- Cheap Trick
2. Life -- Thin Lizzy
3. Fugazzi -- Marillion
5. Moonhead -- Thin White Rope
6. Thunder and Consolation -- New Modal Army
7. Portent Hue -- Caterwaul
8. Elizium -- Fields of the Nephilim
9. Temple of the Dog -- Temple of the Dog
10. Repeater --Fugazi

Interestingly, the very next list, from Bahman Ghahremani of Granada
Hills, California, lists albums from the Cocteau Twins, This Mortal
Coil, Enya, and Dead Can Dance for eight out of his ten choices, but
no Kate.
