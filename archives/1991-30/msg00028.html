My Push Posters order arrived today. I had ordered the size C Sensual
World, Love & Anger and requested The Dreaming as my free extra one. I
also decided to start off my KaTe memorabilia colection with a Bill
Duffield concert security pass, but that hasn't arrived yet. The
turnaround on my order was 11 days.

Unfortunately they're now fresh out of The Dreaming, so they sent me a
TWS one instead. The L&A poster is much the same as the 7"/EP/CD5 cover
(ie. mostly black) but with an extra bit listing the single formats.
The TSW poster is a cropped version of the album cover (haven't checked
it for the KT-symbol yet 8^). Both are portrait-orientation. The TWS
one looks like the TWS cover, as you might expect.

BTW, if anybody wants the latest Homeground subscription rates, I got
those yesterday...
