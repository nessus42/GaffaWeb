Vickie again, as I said, I sent him a short discography. Just the albums
and TWS, TWW, The Cathy Demos and mentioned that there were lots of
b-sides and other non-album songs. I don't think he needs a complete
discography right now. Obviously, very little is available to him now
and a full listing won't do him much good.

As far as lyrics, we can't ftp, and I don't know if he can. Perhaps 
someone could start sending him lyrics (in very small chunks please!)
but whoever does should SPEAK UP!! so he doesn't get lots of lyrics
from lots of different people. Volunteers anyone? 

I can't help him with Peter Gabriel lyrics. I keep spacing off joining
the PG mailing list. Anyone out there who's part of it? Do they have
ftp-able lyrics? 

I asked him about his feelings toward what's been going on in the USSR
the past couple of weeks. He's going to write back with his thoughts.

This is GREAT! I'm so excited that now we'll have a Russian Love-Hound!
He's just getting into Kate though, and if he becomes a fan he can
spread the word to his friends.
