I've seen this several times, so my HO is:  this song really jars my nerves.
When I first bought the CD, I couldn't BELIEVE that KaTe didn't realize that
"Rocket's Tail" followed by "This Woman's Work" was the way to end the album.
I was astounded that she would break that beautiful last piece by tacking on
WSDtM as some sort of weak coda.  Later, of course, I found out that it was
just a B-side that was included as an extra track on the CD (curiously enough,
the liner notes do not mention this, which is surprising), and was much
relieved.

Drop that nasal twang, KaTe!
