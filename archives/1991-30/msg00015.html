Now I haven't been following this thread all that closely, but I dont'
recall anyone having said that there was something wrong with KaTe
writing a song that dealt with gay males! If my memory doesn't betray
me, the thread began when someone caught a comment about "Kashka..."
being about a pair of men and was thereupon surprised. Not offended,
just surprised.  I've thought it was about two men for as long as I
remember and possibly from the first time I ever heard the song since
I was reading LHs before I got the album.  

As you point out, she writes about anything and everything, so it
would seem that a fan should only be surprised if she *hadn't* written
a song like Kashka & Wow (speculation or fact, you be the judge
*grin*)
