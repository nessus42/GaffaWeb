>Hi. I was wondering if you know of any music that has technobabble or

Since you asked in the Kate Bush newsgroup I presume you mean Kate songs
with such. Well there is two: "Breathing" has a man talking about A-bombs
mixed low during a break in the end. And "Deeper Understanding" is about
fogetting the real life while playing with computers. Breathing was 
released on single in 1980. I have two copies, it's hard but far from
impossible to find. I suggest you get the CD album "Never For Ever" (it is
probably cheaper). Deeper Understanding is on the "The Sensual world" CD
and is not released on single.
