I bought a copy of "A Book of Dreams" nearly ten years ago, after the release
of Hounds of Love. I think it is now out of print here in the US because a
friend of mine and fellow Kate fanatic tried to buy it last year. To those
who are trying to find a copy, I suggest you look in  Books in Print at your
local library to see if it is still available or hope and pray you can find
it at a used bookstore. Good luck.
