Hello,
Last year in August I was in Italy, and every night on TV they showed old
recordings of the "San Remo" music festival. It's a competition 
of italian songwriters, but every year they have special guests from
all over the world (who don't partecipate in the competition).
Guess how surprised I was when Kate came up, singing "Hammer Horror"!
It was the festival of '78, I think, and she was dancing and moving like
a cat. She had a dancer dressed in black behind her, showing only his
white hands, embrassing her. I think it was the official "RAI" TV station
who broadcasted it. 
Just thought that it interested you, perhaps.

							Christian
