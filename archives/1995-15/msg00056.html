I also have the Spinning Pups promo, and I believe it is listed in the 
"Collectors Guide to Kate Bush" which we haven't mentioned in a long time 
-- a good place for those of you who are new collectors to view rare and 
unusual Kate collectibles.  A droolfest will ensue.

This wonderful and useful book is marred by some small errors -- many of 
the 12" singles listed have incorrect catalog numbers, as I discovered 
when double checking my collection against the book.  So you have to be 
careful if you are cataloguing your stuff to take numbers directly from 
the item and not shortcut by copying from the book.
