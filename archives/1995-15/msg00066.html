Someone asked me to "state my source" when referring to Kate's vocal
training.  On the Baktabak interview CD, the interviewer asks about a
certain name in the "Hounds of Love" dedications list.  Kate said the
name inquired about was her singing teacher.  Now, I do not doubt that
it is possible that she obtained a singing coach AFTER the release of
"The Kick Inside;"  But, I think it more probable that she had the
training before that.
