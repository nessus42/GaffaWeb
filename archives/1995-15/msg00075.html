Whilst laying in bed a few nights ago I lazily reached over to my shortwave
radio and switched on the BBS World Service, which turned out to be in the
middle of John Peel's program (uh, programme).  I was greeted by a few boys
with guitars (morning, Vickie) singing, of all things, Wuthering Heights.

Nothing particularly special here, just straight-ahead three-chord guitar
rock, but it obviously scores lots of points for taste.  The band is called
China Drum.
