"Suspended In Gaffa" & "Them Heavy People" are included on the NTSC VHS
tape The Single File which is available from Canada and Japan.

The Red Shoes video project has been released in the US on VHS &
laserdisc: it's correct title is _Kate Bush - The Line, The Cross And The
Curve_.

The Whole Story video is probably out of print on tape, but still
available in some stores if you look hard enough. I believe the laserdisc
version is still in print or at least readily available.
