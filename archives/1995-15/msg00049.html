I've found a record that wasn't listed in faq. It's called 
Spinning Pups from EMI America. The catalog number is SF-17099. 
I've only ran across one other copy of this record. Has anybody 
else seen this record? Its copyrighted '83, but I don't remember 
seeing this record new. I guess this must have been a promo.

If you haven't seen this record here are a list of the songs:

Side 1.

1. Stray Cats - "Fishnet stockings"
2. Kim Wilde - "Kids in America"
3. Fisher-Z - "So long"
4. Matumbi - "Point of View (Squeeze A Little Lovin"
5. Kate - "The Man With The Child In His Eyes" 

side 2.

1. Dexy's Midnight Runners - "Geno"
2. The Vapors - "Turning Japanesse"
3. Talk Talk - "Talk Talk"
4. Peter Tosh - "Coming In Hot"
5. Classix Nouveaux - "Guilty"

If you have any info on this record let me know!
