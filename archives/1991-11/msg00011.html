Vickie informs me that the Dave (Eurythmics) Stewart-produced
movie soundtrack entitled "Lily Was Here", Arista, has a track
by fledgling artist Virginia Astley, whose 2 brothers (yuk) have snatched
up some fame lately.  It is supposed to be a great track.
Other work by VA includes "Hope in a Darkened Heart."
Also appearing on LWH is a remake/mix of Here Comes the Rain Again
for all you Annie Lennox fans out there.  And something by
a sax player named Candy Duffer.
