Subject: danielle dax albums

Pop Eyes (UK - Awesome Records)
Jesus Egg That Wept (UK - Awesome Records)
Inky Bloaters (UK - Awesome Records)
Dark Adapted Eye (US - Sire Records)
Blast The Human Flower (US - Sire Records)

Dark Adapted Eye is a compilation of stuff from singles, b-sides, almost
all of Inky Bloaters, and a few random picks off the first two UK only
things as well.  A great introduction, but rather frustratinfg
for completists.

Also look for the Lemon Kittens.
