Subject: Yours truly will try to influence radio


I got an interesting phone call today.  The woman at the other end
worked for a company that does surveys for radio stations.  After
getting some preliminary information from me, she asked me if I'd
like to participate in a focus group in which radio programmers
ask a random sample of people about our tastes in music.  I agreed,
so I'm going in a couple of weeks.

YOU CAN BET WHAT KIND OF MUSIC *I'LL* TELL THE RADIO PEOPLE TO PLAY!!!
