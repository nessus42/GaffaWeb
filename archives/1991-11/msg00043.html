Subject: Happy Rhodes' song "Warpaint"


Well it seems a bunch of people have Happy's new album, so I figured
I'd go ahead and post this...

At about 5:30 into the title track, "Warpaint," there are some backwards-
masked vocals.  I sampled and reversed 'em so I wouldn't lie awake in bed
wondering what I was missing.

Sorry, no brilliant KaTe-like message here.  It's Happy singing, in an
interesting melody, the words, "I fight to the death," repeated a few
times.
