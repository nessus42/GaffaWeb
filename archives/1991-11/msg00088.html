On another note, did everybody else out there already realise why PG's song
'We do what we're told (Milgram's 37)' is so called ?
In case you didn't it basically appears to be based on a story about some
psychological experiments carried out by Milgram a few decades ago.
He found that ordinary people would administer fatal electric shocks to
an unknown victim if told to do so by a 'scientist', i.e. we do what we're told
If anyone is actually interested in the details I'll try to find out more at
some point e.g. why 37 ?
