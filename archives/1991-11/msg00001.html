at "Laser City" in San Francisco as of last Saturday.  I believe it retailed
for $38!! (pretty good deal, IMHO).  The Sensual World 8" is pretty common.
Laser City takes visa, and I believe that they'll ship to you.  Their number
is (415) 474-9722.  The store is located at 1433 Bush Street, S.F., CA. 94109.
If anyone reading this picks it up, I'd be interested to know.
