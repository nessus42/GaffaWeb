Subject: Q Magazine

Q Magazine is giving away with the next two issues a set of "Q sleevenotes"
- what it calls comprehensive guides to selected classic albums, designed to
fit inside a CD jewel box. On this month's issue (for May) are notes for
Blonde on Blonde, Brothers in Arms, Tunnel of Love and Revolver. With next
month's issue (on sale May 4th) will be 4 more including Kate's Hounds of
Love album.
