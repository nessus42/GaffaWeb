A local fanzine reported that they asked WHFS about the Kate id and
apparently the EMI folks (this is HOL-era) called and said "Great work
promoting the album, is there anything we can do for you?" and the
'HFS folks said "Yeah, get us a station id" and EMI asked Kate to do
it and sent it to 'HFS a while later.  Presumably she records these in
her home studio in bunches and sends them off.
