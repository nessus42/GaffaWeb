  I don't think anybody has mentioned Happy Rhodes.  I wonder why?   She's
wonderful!

other bands/artists I like:

    Pink Floyd
       -  The greatest band on earth, period.

    Grateful Dead
       -  once you get in, you can't get out....but oh boy!  what a ride!     
 
    Bjork
       -  She's soooo cute!

    Gentle Giant
       -  for progheads only.
 
    King Crimson
       -  A powerful band that can be as effective with hard and heavy rock as
          with soft meandering ballads.

    Moody Blues
       -  Their first 7 albums are among the best to have ever come out of rock 
          music.  Too bad they lost touch with their "reality". 

    Amazing blondel
       -  Part celtic, part folk, part acoustic rock.   Gentle, old fashioned,
lovely.

    Men Without Hats
       -  Pop Goes the Wolrd is a masterpiece of techno pop.
