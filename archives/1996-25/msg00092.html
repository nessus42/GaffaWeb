Guy Gavriel Kay, Jack Whyte and Charles de Lint are recently acquired

Keeping with the topic at hand, my recent acquisition of This Woman's 
Work was an interesting exercise:  first I find a copy available for 
$170.  Then I go down the street a few doors and find it at $140.  Being 
the impulsive guy that I am, I opted for the $140 set.  Once I opened it, 
I realized *why* it was $30 less - it was missing the "Lionheart" CD.

Most unusual.  Anyone else have a similar problem?  (The store does have 
a replacement set on order for me, but who knows how long that will take?)

Of course, in buying the set for a friend in Buffalo, I have quickly 
become a Kate Bush fan.  I wonder if he planned that?
