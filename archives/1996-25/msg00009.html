What is the problem with you people? In Oz(Australian) English:- Bush 
means: wilderness, forest or brush/savannah land; basically non-urban 
Oz. A bushfire is a forestfire!. Kate would have certainly been aware

Though the problem with this song from an OZ point of view; is the use 
of the word Motorway. At the time, Highway would have been the correct 
term. The term "Motorway" has only come into use with the introduction 
of non-government (i.e private) inner-city freeways. Tollway is such a
financially-incorrect word.
