Alan Stonebridge (Alan.Stonebridge@durham.ac.uk) wrote:

> > 5-Bareneked Ladies

> I loved their song on the "Friends" soundtrack (called "Shoebox"). What 
> other stuff (particularly albums) have they done?


2 albums: "Gordon" (1992) - very fun album, clever, included many hit
songs here in Canada (If I had $1000000, Grade 9, Be my Yoko Ono, &c.). 
Inspirational lyrics like "I'm lyin' in bed just like Brian Wilson did..."
Second album is "Maybe You Should Drive" - smarter, more intense, better. 
They are certainly one of the most underrated bands around.
