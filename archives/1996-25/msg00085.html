I too have been wondering if Happy Rhodes would turn up in anyone's list.
I have Rhodesongs, and although I don't rate it as high as Kate or
Loreena, I really like it and play it lots.  I'm hoping to obtain
more Happy disks, but 2 things get in the way--$ (mailorder only,
around here, and not from the record clubs either) and those
FREAKY album covers.  Most of them look like the kind of thing
lonely teenage boys used to draw back in junior high.  What's
the story anyway? (they have muddy gray-green horned alien creatures
with pointy teeth and drool--not quite capturing the spirit of the
music IMHO).  Happy, like Kate, has a very wide and flexible vocal
range.  Sometimes she sounds A LOT like Kate.
cheers,
Kelley
