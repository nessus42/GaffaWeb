There was a short interview of Mankowitz on MTV; he told about some of
the artists he had worked with.  He said something like this about
Kate and the early Wuthering Heights pictures:

	...they put the pictures in the front of buses and
	the buses would have to keep stopping because people
	would rush in front of them and just stand there,
	looking at this beautiful Kate Bush.
