     Alright!  I did it!

     WBCN in Boston is having an US giveaway this weekend "before everyone
     else!" can hear it.  Well, I managed to be the right caller, and I WON
     A COPY OF GABRIEL'S US!!!

     It just happened to air right after I uploaded my previous message
     to this list, too.  WBCN actually played a different one of Gabriel's
     past songs, "Biko."  Then they told us to be the fourth caller, and
     we'd have to name that song as well.  I called in - and it turned
     out that there were three other callers before me who couldn't
     answer the question.  "What was the question?"  "I don't know the
     song!"  So they asked me, and I said "Biko - from Peter Gabriel's
     third album!"  They said I'd won, and of course I flew into ecstasy.
     I babbled "He's my all-time favorite!" and they played it over the
     air and everything.  :)

     Don't mind me, people - I'm just in seventh heaven right now.
     And eighth and ninth heaven, too.  (Similar to Kate's Ninth Wave!)

     Hello Earth - Hello Earth - with just one hand held up high I can blot
     you out, out of sight...peek-a-boo Little Earth...

     I sure feel like that now!  :)
