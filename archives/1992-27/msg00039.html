I saw a great movie at a cheapo theatre last night ($1.50)
called Prelude to a Kiss.  This was a play and the movie has
been around for a while, but I was struck by how similar
a theme this movie has compared to a popular Kate Bush song.
Was anyone else struck by this convergence?
The subject is not *exactly* identical, but the theme
is very, very close.  I almost expected to here the song
as the credits were rolling.

This was one of the most moving film I've seen in a while,
and superbly acted, I thought.
