of my favorites, there are some really weird songs on there - the
artwork is pretty neat too - i don't remember what the cover was, but
there was a poster included with the album - something by - hmm, it's
been 10 years or so since i've *seen* the album - i remmeber how it's
pronounced but not spelled. something like hyronimous bosch.

Anyway the point is, i *love* the tape i have of that album, but not
only is it missing songs, but it is so old and over played it sounds
*terrible*! And the problem is my parents can't find the album!
