Nothing ever gets censored by the moderator.  The newsgroup is only
moderated to allow it to be automatically gated to a mailing list.
This is a crude way of handling it, but it's the one we've suffered
with for five years so we've sort of learned to live with it.

Your post did appear in the mailing list but not in the newsgroup.
My guess is that's because your post contains the header:

> Distribution: local

When this gets fed to the news program that does the posting it will
most likely cause your post to be limited to only the local system
there, ie it won't be propogated out to the rest of the world.  It's
best to leave the "Distribution" header out completely but if you must
have it, it should be set to "world" rather than "local."
