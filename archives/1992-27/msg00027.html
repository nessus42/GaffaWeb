Hello, I'm kind of a novice at this posting business, although I've been
reading gaffa for years now, but Hi all, it's nice to be posting here *:).

WRT the gabriel video, I agree that it is like Big Time and Sledgehammer
a little, but the mood of it is vastly different I feel. To put it simply
Big Time and Sledge are kind of 'up' and DitD is a bit more 'down'. I have
to say that I _love_ the video and the song, and I believe the only way to
'experience' the song is to watch the video through a good stereo. When I
listen to the song off the CD, the video pops into my head all the time.
It's great stuff!

By the way, does anybody have any information on the 'Revisited' album. All
I've heard is that it's going to be a kind of compilation of pg1 and pg2,
which seems very silly to me.
