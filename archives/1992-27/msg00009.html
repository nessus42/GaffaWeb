The German words are:

 Tiefer, tiefer, irgendwo in der Tiefe giebt es ein Licht
 - Ihr koennt es nie finden.

This means:

 deeper, deeper, somewhere in the depth there is a light
 - you (pl.) will never find it.

(and 'devil' would be 'Teufel'; 'Tiefel' is not a german word)

BTW, I would be glad if someone had lyrics for
theese mystical background vocals in the same song.
