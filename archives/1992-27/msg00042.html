To answer the first question:
    The photos in the box set seem to be from The Kick Inside, Lionheart 
and some from Never for Ever periods. There was only one or two in the 
book that I hadn't seen before (but then again I do have a large 
collection) and most were in black and white. The poster is of the all 
too familiar leotard picture and so is the postcard. The t-shirt is white 
and has in the upper left corner a "message" in black pen that reads 
"lots of love, Kate"; it comes in a Large size (atleast mine did). The cd 
of Lionheart is a UK issue.
 
To answer the second question/topic:
        The actress in Patriot Games is Ann Archer (a great but usually 
overlooked woman). As for Back to the Future I thinks it's Mary 
Steenburgen (sp?), if it's not then just add her to the list of 
look-a-likes. Now for my observation, is kate beginning to look like Joan 
Collins???
