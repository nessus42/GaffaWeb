I think that when people see computer users (especially modem users) as 
introverted and NOT communicating with people they are latching on to the 
missing human element. Its much safer for two people to communicate 
through a computer medium rather than face to face; this seems to be the 
theory explained to me. But then again could it not be argued that when 
two people communicate over a computer they tend to open up more and tell 
of rather personal incidents and thoughts? Most interesting.
