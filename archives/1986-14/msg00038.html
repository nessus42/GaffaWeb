Subject: 'EXPRESS' by Love and Rockets

You *must* buy this album NOW!
It is G R E A T!  The first album, 'Seventh Dream of Teenage Heaven'
was excellent...but most people I know thought it to be a bit dull; I
guess they just couldn't handle long songs...  Anyway, unlike the first
album, 'Express' is much more upbeat.  Best tracks on it: 'Yin and
Yang (the Flowerpot Man)', 'Life in Laralay', 'Kundalini Express', and
'All in My Mind'...however, the other tracks are also quite good.
Daniel Ash's characteristic distorted guitar and a lot of acoustic guitar
is on this album...

'Seventh Dream of Teenage Heaven' is more of an album to get spaced out
to... while 'Express', in contrast, is much more energetic and rich in
sound.
