Birdsongs of the Mesozoic @ dc space, 9/25/86.

These  dudes must like to start early, they started a half an hour
before they were suppossed to, thereby making people who showed up
on time to miss some goodies.  this is a show for to review greg, 
you can use all sorts of musical metaphors to explain these guys.  
I don't try to. There cool as shit, and that's that.   They don't 
do Steely Dan covers.  They do cover Stravinsky.  They don't do
"whipping post" yet, but they're working on it. They still do
Rocky and Bullwinkle, complete with tapes of Rocky.  They claim to have
a new album out, but since no one has seen it, they can't validate that
claim.  They do have cheap ($$.$$ wise) shirts for sale, so labovitz
bought me one cause he owed me money.  they do take checks.  They 
don't use anti-perpisepent.  They do drink beer.  They do
make great noises with a guitar.  They do use hupcaps and washboards
as percussion instruments.  They do not sound like jesus and mary 
wankoff.  They are cool.  They are worthy of checking out.
They don't tour that much.
