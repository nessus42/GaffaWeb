Hi, folks.
Well, two unimportant bits of trivia today.
First, the "Ninth Wave" t-shirt (U.S.-made)
which Doug described earlier (IED supposes he
saw it in Boston-area stores) has now cropped
up in West Hollywood. IED picked up a couple
inRene's All Ears today.

The other info is perhaps of more moment.
The most recent issue of Billboard Magazine,
in publishing a list of the major U.S. labels'
fourth quarter '86 releases, included under
the heading EMI-America a December release-date
for The Best of Kate Bush (a meaningless title
if ever IED heard one). No mention of whether
it would include any new material, but IED
will wager (foolishly, perhaps) that the U.K.
edition, should there be such a thing in December,
will have one new track. Kate will see that
fobbing off the public with one single in December
will give her a good 24 to 36 months' leeway
in preparing KBVI.
