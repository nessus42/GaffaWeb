If by BJH you are referring to Barclay James Harvest, (and I assume you are
since you mentioned Taking Me Higher), they already have several albums out on
CD.  The only one I'm familiar with now is "Live Tapes" which contains 
"Taking Me Higher", "Crazy City", "Suicide" and several others (it's a two CD
package).  I've seen a few other albums of theirs at local CD outlets.

Actually, being 26, I'm not old enough to remember BJH when they were first out
but I heard "Suicide" on WAER-FM back in Syracuse a few years ago, and it 
struck me enough to remember it.  (WAER has since joined the fate of WHJU or
whatever it was, that went to an NPR Jazz/News type format (oh no, it's 
spreading...).  Actually I have nothing against jazz but not when it replaces
the few alternative music outlets available.  Anyway, I just happened on "Live
Tapes" last year, and have since seen other albums.  

I mentioned BJH on this net a few months ago, but nobody at that point had
