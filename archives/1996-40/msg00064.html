KaTe was featured in a lookalike contest on Swedish national TV on
September 27th. The show is called "Sikta mot Stjärnorna" and is based on
Endemol Entertainment's format "Soundmix Show". People imitate well known
artists both by looking like them and singing like them. The song covered
in the show was "Wuthering Heigths". The show had 1,540,000 viewers.

A picture can be seen on http://www.mtv.se/tvp/sms96/4katebush.html
