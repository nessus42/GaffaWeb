Hi,

If you have really got TWS on CDi you will have to get a MPEG Card for you
computer or a CDi Player, but if your copy is a "Video CD" you have the
posobility to get the XING MPEG Driver from Xing Technology Corporation.
This Driver is able to play any .MPG or .DAT file from a MCI device (like a
CD rom), but you have to have a 80486 or pentium and a quart-speed CD rom
and a fast VGA-card to see it as a movie and not like a slide show.

Unfortunately I dont know if there is a MAC version of this Driver.

punki


PS.: Ask the producer of you VGA card for this driver (sometimes you need a
special DCI-Driver for your card).
