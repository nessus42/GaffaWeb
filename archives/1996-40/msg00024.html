I wonder if this will get through. . .

There's a fairly new radio station in 
San Francisco that goes by the name of 
"Alice".  (Don't ask.)  They play 
"modern rock", as defined by, say,
Billboard, but for a more female 
audience than normal.  That means
less grunge, more music with melodies,
and more female artists.

I've been listening for a few weeks now,
and I thought Kate would be a natural for
them.  Well, last night they played
"Running Up That Hill"!

Geez, it had been about a year since I last
heard Kate on the radio.
