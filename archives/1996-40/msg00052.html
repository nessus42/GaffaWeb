My favorite Kate 7" single. Not the song, but one of the reasons to be
a Kate fan. Trying to find the Kate items which you want in your collection.
This is an Irish release by EMI, thus the "I" in the catalog number IEMI 9001.
The first printings have IEMI 9001-A in the run off. Living in Dublin for
part of 
my young upbringing, finding this single was tough. But, its one of my favorite 
Kate pictures. I was in Dublin this summer, and just because this is an Irish
release does not make it any easier finding it there. Richard, I may be able
to direct you to a place which has this release, but they were asking
75 UK pounds sterling for it earlier in the year.
