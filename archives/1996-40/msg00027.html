really back to normal yet.  The NIC records for gryphon.com are still
wrong (condolences to Bill--I've been hearing a lot of horror stories
about dealings with the US NIC) and the normal posting mechanisms
still broken.  To get gaffa on the air, I'm gating love-hounds-bounce

until our humble pseudo-moderator tells me that it is no longer
needed.  Messages may take a little longer to appear than usual due to
some less than optimal steps along the way, but otherwise gaffa should
be functional.  Send any problems with the gated articles on gaffa
(only--I have no control over the mailing lists) my way.
