The Rolf Harris song you are refering to is "Sun Arise,"  and Rolf also 
plays the digeridu on "The Dreaming."  I have a dusty 7'' of "Sun Arise" and 
the digeridu track seems to be almost exactly the same in these two songs. 
 But apart from the beat (and some background clanging and banging), there's 
not many other similarities that make the leap into "The Dreaming."  "Sun 
Arise" is a simple song about the sun slowly rising (if you *really* want 
the lyrics I can post them) and relies heavily on a chorus of male vocalists 
to answer the lead.
