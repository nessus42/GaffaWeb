The Hounds of Love - Esp. The Ninth Wave side

The Sensual World - I don't know why this album is dismissed like it is.

think highly of this work could give me some insight into the flaws here?

Never Forever - Just a good solid piece of work

The Kick Inside - what a great introduction to someone!  the thing I love
about this is the writing...I am hearing impaired, but you don't need to
necessarily hear the music to listen to it.  And some of these songs are
just as beautiful signed as they are sung.

The Dreaming - Good for a change

The Red Shoes - again, I like the concept.  When I found out she was
making a movie, I started trying to envision it.  Once again, my concept
was very different, much better, but my imagination doesn't have a budget,
either!

Lionheart - I have to say it though, bad Kate Bush is still better than
anyone else at their best.
