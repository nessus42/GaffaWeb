Here's news that may be of interest to some of
you:  Sarah McLachlan's album "Fumbling Towards
Ecstasy" went platinum a couple of weeks ago,
believe it or not.  It's now been on the Billboard
Top 200 Albums chart for more than a year.

It's certainly very rare that an artist I like as
much as Sarah has a platinum record in the US!

Now if only another artist whom we all love would
have some similar success :-)
