OK. While a 1711 line posting may be a little long for any topic, I think 
the newer folks on the list/group need some enlightening about something 
that may not be readily apparent. I don't mean to pick on the poster of 
the above, who really was not all that out of line, but we seem to have 
been getting a lot of the "[whine] This isn't about Kate. It shouldn't be 
here!" messages, so I feel it is time explain.

Rec.music.gaffa and it's sister mailing list. Love-Hounds, got their names,
obviously, from the premier female musician of our time, Kate Bush.

However, the charter for this group and list make it clear that this list
is *not* for exclusive discussion of Kate Bush. This group is for the
discussion of all female musical artists (I won't use the word
"alternative" 'cause who the heck knows what it means) who follow in the
tradition of Ms. Bush -- the tradition of producing breathtakingly
different and beautiful music. 

This group is for the discussion of Tori Amos, Sara MacLachlan, Bjork, 
Jane Siberry, Happy Rhodes, Nanci Griffith, Annie Lennox, you name it -- 
any other female artist who has ever made you go "Wow!"

The primary focus of this group is and always will be Ms. Bush and her 
work, but discussions, arguments, digressions and long, involved threads 
about other artists are part of what this group is about.

So cut out the whining about "Non-Kate content" until you have RTFFAQ!
