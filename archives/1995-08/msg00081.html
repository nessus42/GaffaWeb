Right out of her head, "Karen L. Newcombe" <kln@crl.com>, wondered:

* Does anyone out there in the Love Kennel know whether or not the 
* overadvertised upcoming TV special on UPN, the "History of Rock and Roll" 
* contains any Kate material?

I've read the previews in both the SF Chron and the SJ Mercury News, and no
mention of Kate was made.  Not that that means anything definitive, 
especially considering most "rock and roll editors" know jacksh*t about Kate.

only toured once, over 15 damn years ago?

* show about music hoping to see Kate and getting one more disappointment.

the series is supposed to be the most comprehensive, well-made visual/audio
chronicle of rock ever produced.  I *can* say the Bowie is featured, and 
probably the Floyd, both heavy influences on Kate.  But I know this is stretching
things a bit..
