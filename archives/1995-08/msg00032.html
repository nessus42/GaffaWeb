Amongst Swedish broadcasters that special type of tape is called gaffa 
tape, not gaffer's tape. I have more than one time jumped in amazement 
when people around me have said the word "gaffa" meaning the tape, people 
that have never heard of Kate Bush or rec.music.gaffa. Gaffa is a word 
that is in use,m not a word that everyone uses every day since it is a 
word mostly used in the "trade", but anyway a normal word.

In the television studio (actual example): 
Lena:  Have Lars fixed the broken camera yet?
Malin: He probably has fixed it his usual way; by tying it up with gaffa tape.
