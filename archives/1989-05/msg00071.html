     Here is the latest, and IED hopes definitive, list of people
requesting copies of--and people offering to _make_ copies of--the
_Cathy_Demos_ etc. tape.
     WARNING: The deadline has now PASSED. If you missed it, don't
send in your request now--it will be ignored. Rather, try wheedling
a single copy from someone else on the list through personal and
very polite personal e-mail request.
     TAKE NOTE: Only the numbers assigned to people on THE EDITION
BELOW count. DISREGARD the number you were assigned on earlier
lists. Some changes in numbering have had to be made in order
that the new people offering to _make_ copies actually _receive_
a copy with which to make them! So those people have been
booted up on the list, necessarily displacing a few people down a few
notches. If you are a victim, please try to understand the logistical
problems that have necessitated this slight rearrangement--the purpose is
simply to enable everyone to get a copy as soon as possible.
     Another warning: all those who rushed ahead and sent off their
blank cassettes to someone expecting to get their orders in first
may be disappointed to discover that their places on the list have now
changed, and consequently the person to whom the blanks were so swiftly
sent off are not the ones assigned to make your copies. This is an
unfortunate possibility for which IED accepts partial blame--although
with a project as chancy and makeshift as this one has been such people
probably should have been a little more patient and cautious before
committing their packages to the post. If something like this happens
to you, pray that your blank tape is returned; then if it is, re-mail
it to the right person.
     IED also apologizes for the untold expense this repeated
posting of names is causing the system and your accounts. With luck
everyone with any interest will SAVE this list for their files, so
that IED can merely refer to it for whatever reason in future.

-- Andrew Marvick
