In article <6254@sdcsvax.UCSD.Edu> Love-Hounds@GAFFA.MIT.EDU writes:
>Really-From: bloch%galahad@ucsd.edu (Steve Bloch)

>I saw them here in San Diego last spring, and although I've always
>enjoyed their music, this concert included, they gave a distinctly
>"tired" impression, as though they'd done the Alleluia Chorus a
>thousand too many times.  I wonder if it's time for them to disappear
>into a studio for two years or so, relax and take their time.

I saw them last spring in San Juan Capistrano, supposedly the last
concert of their tour. I suspect the concert I went to was right after
the concert in San Diego. Yes, they were tired. They were a little off
at the beginning, but recovered by the middle of the show. They were
still lovely, however. I would gladly forego seeing them in concert if
they would record more.
