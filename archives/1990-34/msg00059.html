Subject: lush

someone asked about lush. they have three eps out on 4ad: _scar_, _mad
love_ and the third is (i think) eponymous. they also have one american
release called _gala_ which sums these three eps and adds two extra tracks:
"hey hey helen" (the old abba tune) and a remix of scarlet. there is one
other track of theirs that has been released: a campy cover of "chirpy 
chirpy cheep cheep" (who did the original escapes me right now, but i 
know i have it written down somewhere) that appeared on the british-only
anti-poll tax compilation called _alvin lives in leeds_.

i recently got an interview cassette with miki berenyi and emma anderson
(the two guitarists in the band) which i hope to get to transcribing some-
time later this week. i'll post that to the 4ad list - if you are inter-
ested, let me know and i'll send you a copy as i don't want to clutter up
love-hounds with it.
