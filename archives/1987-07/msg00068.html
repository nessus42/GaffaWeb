new Smiths album out - March 31

  • Subject: new Smiths album out - March 31
  • From: mike@uxa.cso.uiuc.edu (_Mike Trogni_)
  • Date: Tue, 31 Mar 87 17:45:19 cst
   This two-record set covers The Smiths from April 1983 to November 1986,
with Side One being all new stuff.  The other sides of the album has a lot
of stuff from 1984 on, including B-sides like "Unloveable" and "Asleep".

   Most of Morrissey's lyrics still deal with painful love, death, and
unfufilling relationships.  Couple of tracks I really liked: "Half a Person"
"London" "Ask" plus "You Just Haven't Earned It Yet, Baby".

   For those of you who know what the most important thing about a Smiths
album is, the cover star is Shelagh Delaney, some British tart who can 
"really smoulder" into the camera.  :-)

   Mail from Smiths fans is  appreciated.

  Mike
