So like I was in LA Chinatown one afternoon, casually glancing over a
streetside stand selling cheapo (pirated) Chinese tapes, when I sensed
something staring back at me among those pictures of unfamilar Chinese
singers.  Sure enough, one tape had "KB the Archer" photo on it!  It
reads "David Bowie N Mick Jagger N Kate Bush".  Turns out that they
put both sides of the "Dancing in the Street" and RUtH 12"s on the
tape, plus "James and the cold gun", "L'amour looks something like
you" from the "On Stage" EP.  Frankly, the sound was better than I
expected for $2.

By the way, I like the $20/week record suggestion.  Perhaps we can
do it on a semi-regular basis.
