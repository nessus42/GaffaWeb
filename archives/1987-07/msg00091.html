bad kitchen @ raji's in hollywood

raji's -- quite unpretentious, not too big, thirty people showed
up on this thursday night, no wavos, no glam freaks, etc -- i don't know
how they stay in business -- i was free, the folks in front of us got in
for $1.50, I didn't see anyone paying $6...

bad kitchen was pretty good -- cool high school sort of band with some
serious ska punk tunes w/wo synth.  could get real good if they tightened 
up.  anyone else heard of them?
