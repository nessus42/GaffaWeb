Here's a batch of fine albums (some probably mentioned previously).

The Bevis Frond - "Miasma"
Lord John -  "Six Days of Sound"
  These two are killer psychedelic albums, very guitar-based, fairly trippy
  songs though there are a few lighter, psych/pop tunes.  Bevis Frond has
  some rawness and wanking around between songs, but also has some excellent
  guitar solos.  Lord John is better produced.  Both are recommended.

The Shoutless -  "Bowery at Midnight"
Hidden Peace - "We All Have..."
  Both are garage psych things.  The Shoutless is a killer guitar assault 
  on top of excellent melodies and is completely FUN.  Better than Nomads.
  Hidden Peace is from Greece; a quirky psych pop thing with punky vocals.

Instigators -  "Phoenix"
  Strong hardcore with solid guitar work.

--John
