The Sensual World, a Kate Bush _Bash_!

From: n8344141@unicorn.wwu.edu (paul carpentier)
Date: Thu, 18 Jan 90 22:28:48 -0800
Subject: The Sensual World, a Kate Bush _Bash_!


I'm posting this on behalf of a few people.  The Bush League of Seattle and
The Portland Three proudly announce

			THE SENSUAL WORLD
			a Kate Bush Bash!

mmh, yes   when KaTe gave us the gift of music back from her mouth
we said   "KaTe Fans, go down
Take four big wheels and roll your bodies
Out of your house and down to a Bash"   mmh, yes
On the twenty-seventh (Saturday) of this month
At 5 pm we begin our party - yes
Steppin' out to the Bash we'll call "The Sensual World"
Steppin' out...
To where the freeway meets with 50th NE
To the east Univ Way lies   mmh, yes
Then you look for a place to park
(Like you looked for KaTe on TV)
But don't get lost in the sunset   mmh, yes
The party's underway in The Sensual World
You'll see these words above our alley doorway
Steppin' out of your car nearby The Sensual World
Don a guise, take a role, Dress for The Sensual World
And then our arrows of direction point the way   mmh, yes
As we whisper   "Find us
Behind" - mmh, yes - "The Grand Illusion"
In the third of the alley buildings   mmh, yes
We'll play KaTe tunes so if you've slipped along the way
They'll rescue you   mmh, yes
And the Bash takes life with you here   mmh, yes
We say   mmh, yes
About time!

Starting time is around 5 pm.  Notice, there is a dress code.  The hosts
say donations are not required, but they would appreciate a $1-2 from each
person to defray costs.  Also, bring money for pizza and for Kate Bush
Bash T-Shirts with a "Sensual World"-era picture of Kate.

Please bring any Kate-related items to display.  They plan on having
"hexaphonic Kate for your aural pleasure, and _perhaps_ (emphasized) a
large-size Kate in performance for your visual delight.  Plus maybe a
Knowledge Bowl and other assorted sundries."

		SPREAD THE WORD TO ALL KATE FANS!

--
Paul M Carpentier
WWU Kate Bush Club

ps:  The alley behind "The Grand Illusion" on 50th Ave NE and University Way
     Seattle, Washington (there should be signs)
     5 pm, Jan 27, 1990 (Saturday)
     (the freeway in the above song is Interstate-5)
