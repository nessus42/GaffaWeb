"Experiment IV" was a track specifically written for _The Whole Story_.
It doesn't appear on any other album.  It's not unusual for an artist
to include a new track on a compilation album; as a single it helps
promote the album, and it encourages fans who have all the previous
albums to buy the compilation anyway.  (Sure worked in my case!)

There is a single (both 7" and 12") of "Experiment IV".
