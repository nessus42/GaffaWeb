The Jan. 13th issue of Sounds has the results of their readers' poll.
Kate did well in several categories:

1989
Album: No. 7 The Sensual World
Single: No. 12 The Sensual World
        No. 14 This Woman's Work
Female Vocalist: No. 1 Kate Bush
     (Kate was also No. 1 in 1980, 85 and 86)
Musician: No. 12 Kate Bush
Promo Video: No. 8 The Sensual World
Comeback: No. 10 Kate Bush
Person: No. 9 Kate Bush

The 80's
LP of the 80's: No. 9 Hounds of Love
Single of the 80's: No. 14 Running Up That Hill
Person of the 80's: No. 11 Kate Bush
