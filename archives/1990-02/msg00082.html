IED's postings of his Catalogue of Kate Bush's Recorded Music are
much appreciated as usual.

>A complete discography, including all foreign pressings

>divisions throughout the world. I have seen advertisements for
>EMI-Singapore pressings of two Kate Bush records. Who knows whether
