Jane Sieberry on VH-1

From: jjw7384@ultb.isc.rit.edu (Jeff Wasilko)
Date: Wed, 17 Jan 90 00:12:06 EST
Subject: Jane Sieberry on VH-1


Jane Sieberry was on VH-1's New Visions - Folk show tonight (Tuesday). 
Unfortunately, I turned on the TV while she was already playing an acoustic 
version of Something About Trains. By the time I stuck a tape in the VCR, 
Something About Trains had ended and Jane had started to talk about Bound By 
The Beauty.

Here's what I caught of her comments:

[Acoustic Version of Something About Trains]

"...and watching the rooftops and the treetops and birds. There's a church 
spire and that was my view while I worked and {I was so moved (barely audible)} all the time. Bound By The Beauty is alot about the light changing across all 
these things."

[The Bound By The Beauty video is played.]

That was all there was of the Jane Sieberry segment. New Visions is on 
weeknights from 10-11 (EST), with each night specializing on one particular 
genre of music.

Does anyone know of a Jane Sieberry mailing list? Someone from the U.K.
mentioned one and I tried to mail him about it, but I never heard
back...
