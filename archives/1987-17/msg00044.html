Yep, Todd had this "be kind to the customer" solution when he put
together LP:s like "UTOPIA", the B-side called "ICON" is approximately
35 minutes long, and it's a completely whole song, even if it twistes
here and there.  Also the LP "Initiation" with the B-side called
"Treatise on Cosmic Fire" is 35 minutes long. It's one of the first
very synthesized albums ever made - and today it sounds very stunning
compared to what equipment was availiable at that time (1976?).

Due to this package of much music on a plastic platter the sound is
very compressed and both the highs and the lows are cutted so you have
to "crank up" your Hi-fi. Unfortunately there are a lot of pops, hiss
and noice that also gets amplified. There are going to be a major
release of Runt's albums on CD, and boy I'm saving my overtime pay
to get these. Imagine to hear these wacko songs on CD mixed from
the original tapes!
