More on the White Album rumor-

The CD in question may have been produced in Japan NOT Tiawan. (My
"Please Please Me" CD has a sticker that says "Compact Disc Made in
Japan").

Also, the screw up on the "A Hard Day's Night" CD was apparently this:

Some Kate Bush CD packages (sorry, I don't know what title, maybe you
Bush fans can help us) were apparently stuffed with "A Hard Day's
Night" CD. This foul-up occured BEFORE the "AHDN" CD was officially
released. All of the labelling was apparently correct on both the Kate
Bush package and the "AHDN" CD. It's just that the CD was mistakenly
stuffed (pre-release) into the Bush package, therefore, this
production error can be easily duplicated by already existing product
and is not a collectors item.

Sorry for the false alarm :-)
