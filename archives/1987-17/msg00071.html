Glad I found this bboard.  Great things are discussed here.  

Gentle Giant: They are amazing.  My particular favorite of theirs is
"Free Hand".  Songs like "Just the Same", "On Reflection", and "Free
Hand" just can't be beat... and all in a row!  Last year I found a
copy of Gentle Giant's live album... good stuff.  They have a great
medley of Octopus tunes, and a three recorder trio, or maybe it was a
quartet... anyway, it was good.  I recommend Gentle Giant.

The Might Be Giants: Just listen to them.  Truly good music.  Funny
lyrics... Lyrics that boarder on deeply intellectual, but really
aren't.  Everyone, should listen to them.  Now.  Well, anyway... I
like them.

-Mike
