> Someone just gave me a tape labelled 'Tuxedomoon / Halfmute' that had

> What can anyone tell me about Television?

> Tom Friedel

I can't tell you too much about Television, except that I think that's
Tom Verlaine's old band.  They were good (and Tom Verlaine still is),
but they have nothing to do with Tuxedo Moon.

Tuxedo Moon was a San Francisco band, originally including performance
artist Winston Tong.  He left the band fairly early on, don't know if
he made any records after leaving (I know he performed around the Bay
Area long after leaving the band).  Anyway, he sings on the only
Tuxedo Moon record that I have, a 12" 45 rpm EP that includes "new
machine", "litebulb overkill", "nite & day", and "no tears".  The
record is copyright 1978, no record company name anywhere on it.

"Half Mute" is the only LP that I know of by them, not sure when it
came out but I'd guess 1979-80.  There was one song on it - "What's
The Use" - that got a lot of airplay in the Bay Area.  I'm pretty sure
they've broken up, not sure when.
