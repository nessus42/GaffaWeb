There isn't much I've found on 4AD that hasn't been worth the price.
Both of the Clan of Xymox LP's (the second one was called "Medusa")
were very good, in my opinion, falling somewhere between Wolfgang
Press and Dead Can Dance. (whose latest LP "Within The Realm Of A
Dying Sun" is simply magnificent. While you await the new Cocteau
Twins album, 4AD is putting out some great new bands, including their
first top ten British dancefloor hit "Pump Up The Volume" by MARRS.
But if you only buy one record in the next month (or year), make it
the compilation "Lonely Is An Eyesore".
