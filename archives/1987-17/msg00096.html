Friday, October 23.  I wonder if it is because they will be at Metro

think at this point Throwing Muses may have too bright a future with
Sire records to be enjoyed locally for long.

 have an interest in The Living Room, and if not, how they could have applied
 pressure to prevent more than a single mention in the Phoenix from several
 weeks ago of the upcoming Providence appearance?

2.  Will anyone who reads this group be at The Living Room 10/23?  Let's
 have a drink (or something) to celebrate NetSolidarity (not to mention
 KT-appreciation)!
