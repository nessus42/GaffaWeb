The "field" video was the second video she did for this song (the
first was for her initial Top of the Pops appearance, in which she
said she felt like she was slowly dying), but it was dropped for
promotional use in favor of the Keith McMillan-directed version.

Other KTrivia from _Kate_Bush_Complete_:
In 1972, Kate made a demo tape with 30 songs that was circulated by a
family friend to his various record company connections. Their
comments were: "morbid, boring, and uncommercial"

Kate then started contemplating possible careers in psychiatry or
social work.
