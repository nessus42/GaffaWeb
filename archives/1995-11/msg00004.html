This weekend there were no less than two Kately appearances on TV
here...one was on "TOTP2" on Saturday, they were showing stuff from
1979 TotP shows, including Kate's Wow performance, complete with
deliberately obscured "hitting the vaseline" gesture; the other was on
a documentary about the satellite TV industry, called "Satellite
Wars", on Channel 4. Avid historians will recall that Kate was at
the opening ceremony of Sky TV in 1983, and indeed there was a video
clip of the event, with Kate visible for at least, ooh, two seconds...
