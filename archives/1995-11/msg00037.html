Coca-Cola to expand Fruitopia line

    ATLANTA (Reuter) - Coca-Cola Co. said Thursday it plans to expand its
Fruitopia line of fruit beverages in early 1995, adding a tangerine flavor
and two lighter tasting flavors -- apple raspberry and tropical.
    The Fruitopia line was launched six months ago.
    The lighter tasting flavors will have one-third less calories than
regular Fruitopia, the company said.
    To meet consumer demand, the company said it has invested more than
$150 million in additional production capacity for Fruitopia and other
non-carbonated products such as PowerAde.
    ``The consumer response to Fruitopia has been outstanding,'' said Jack
Stahl, president of Coca-Cola USA.
    REUTER
