That was a Fruitopia Ad for their Iced Tea.

I was sitting there just watching the Oscars with my husband, mostly
making comments on what everyone was wearing.  And just when I was going
to go move our laundry from the washer to the dryer I saw the begining of
the ad and sat back down.  I yelled to my husband "There's a Kate ad on
during the Oscars!" (he corrected me saying it was a Fruitopia ad not a
Kate ad, but you all know what I mean)  then I listened to the music
carefully and wasn't sure it was her, but then again the rest of the ads
were more experimental Kate, so I went on faith that it was.  I remember
awhile back someone saying she was recording more ads for them.

So was it really her? Did millions of people actually hear Kate last
night, but most didn't know it?

- Jennifer
