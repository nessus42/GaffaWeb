Well, I've never seen the cassette version (anyone did? I'd like
a description for the discography...), but I've seen an LP box.
Two notable differences (besides that they contain vinyl instead
of CDs...): 1st: the picture booklet is 12"x12", not 5"x5".
2nd: there are three volumes of This Woman's Work. Yes, the 2 CDs
have been split onto 3 LPs. As far as I know there are also 9 cassettes
in the cassette edition, so I guess the splitting is identical
to the LP version. Don't know about the booklet size, but it's
probably by far not as big as the LP one.
