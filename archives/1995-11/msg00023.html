OPEN THE HEAVENS

PURPOSE OF CONFERENCE
"Equipping the saints to do the worship ministry"

The primary purpose of this conference is to train and release people to
worship God.  Additionally, it is designed to equip those persons called
into worship ministry/leading.  To accomplish this, the conference will
cover topics such as "Centrality of Worship", "Worship in The Church",
and "Worship: A Window To Heaven".

To learn these truths, our prayer is that God will "Open The Heavens"
and visit us with His presence.  To facilitate this, we will have a
number of extended times of worship.



THE SPEAKERS
We are delighted to have some of the Vineyard's finest worship
leaders/speakers at this conference.  All three have extensive
experience in the area of ministering in, or teaching on, worship.

ANDY PARK
Andy is the worship pastor at Anaheim Vineyard and a prolific songwriter.  
He has been leading worship in Vineyard churches for 17 years.  Before
coming to Anaheim in 1992, he was the senior pastor of the Surrey
Vineyard in British Columbia, Canada, and prior to that an assistant
pastor at Langley Vineyard.  Andy's role at Anaheim involves training
and nurturing worship leaders and worship team members.  He has traveled
to various countries to lead worship and teach on worship leading.  Andy
has been the featured worship leader on many of the Vineyard Touching
the Father's Heart worship projects including, "Take Our Lives",
"Devoted To You", and "The River Is Here".  Andy and his wife, Linda,
reside in Anaheim, California with their five children.

CINDY RETHMEIER
Cindy is one of the prominent worship leaders at Anaheim Vineyard.  She
has been in the Vineyard since 1978 and has written many songs for the
Vineyard worship recordings.  Cindy was the worship leader for the Yorba
Linda Vineyard from 1984 to 1988.  She has also written several
children's worship songs and recently recorded a new children's worship
album for Vineyard Music Group.  Cindy's has a heart to see women
trained, equipped and released in worship leading.  Cindy is the
featured worship leader on the new Touching the Father's Heart worship
project "Spirit Of The Sovereign Lord".  Cindy is married and resides
in Yorba Linda, California were she home schools her two children.

[------------------- PRETEND THERE IS A PAGE BREAK HERE --------------------]

BRIAN ANDERSON
Brian is the founding pastor of Vineyard Christian Fellowship of North
Phoenix which is located in Phoenix, AZ.  Brian and his wife, Thora,
planted VCFNP as a home Bible Study with only a few people in September
1985.  It has steadily grown over the years into a congregation of
800-900 people.  VCFNP has also planted two other churches in the
Phoenix area out of their congregation.  Brian has a passion for prayer
and the Word of God.  He desires to expand the Kingdom of God through
training others to do the works of Jesus Christ wherever they are.  God
has used Brian to impart to people all over the world a greater hunger
for prayer, the Word of God, holiness and renewal.  Brian and Thora
have ministered in the U.S., England, Ireland, Scotland, Japan, Mexico,
Australia, and South Africa.  Brian is a Regional Overseer for the
Association of Vineyard Churches (AVC) and he also serves on the Board
of Directors for AVC of which John Wimber is the President.
