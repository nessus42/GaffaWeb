I get a kick when I see that people who have praised Lily (which IS very
good) have complained about Rocket Man being "one of the two or three
worst things she has ever allowed to be released". I would challenge you
to do this...make a cassette tape. Record Rocket Man, and pause the tape
just before the the fade (when Kate is singing her ahh, ahh, ahh's). Now
start Lily, but don't take the tape off of pause until after the music
begins (about 25 seconds in, I think). When you are finished, run the tape
back and listen to it, the transition will be so smooth that you will
almost miss it. In fact, I am recommending (Kate are you listening?) a
Rocket Man/Lily medley when she appears on Letterman or SNL. Well, I can wish.

Also, I swear, if you'll turn the volume up on Lily during the "prayer",
in the background you can hear a whiny, tinny voice saying something like
"Will you let me finish? You've been talking for 20 seconds, do I get to say
anything".
