> 	Forth--is the album Passing through air worth buying?
> The blurb says that it is rare studio mixes.  If so, of what?

There are two albums called "Passing through air". Both bootlegs. One is
a CD and contains home demo recordings (a MUST for fans...), the other is
a double LP and contains studio recordings that are not THAT rare - especially
after the This Woman's Work box is on the market. Only a few tracks on there
that are not in the box: Cloudbusting (video mix), The Wedding List (live),
RUTH (instr.), Dreamtime, Let It Be (Bill Duffield benefiz concert). Go for
it if you a) are a collector, b) haven't been able to track the other
tracks down. But remember that with the exception of Let It Be every title
is availlable on an official release - might be difficult to find, but not
impossible.
