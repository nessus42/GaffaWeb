In the UK there is no 7" single of Moments of Pleasure.

The 12" Posterbag has MoP, MoP (Instrumental) and Home for Christmas
The CD single has MoP, Show a Little Devotion, December will be Magic Again
and Experiment IV
The CD box has MoP, December will be Magic Again and Experiment IV
 - the box is described as having 4 colour prints but mine actually had 3 photos
 (one of the cover, and two of the same shots as on the poster bag) plus
 a lyric card with just a finger nail photo of the album cover.
The cassette single has MoP and MoP (instrumental) [I think - not 100% sure on this].

MoP (song and video) got a few seconds airing on BBC TV's Top of the Pops last night.
It is number 25 in their charts and the DJ said good things about it.
