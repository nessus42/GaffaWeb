I don't know if this is old news, but the other day I was listening
to the radio and heard what I thought was the intro to "The
Dreaming".  It turned out to be a composition by Tommy Emanuel, an
Australian guitar genuis, although I couldn't catch the title of the
song!  It has been played a few times since on alternative radio
stations like Triple J in Melbourne.
