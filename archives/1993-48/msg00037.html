    The "Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram" is contained in a set
of instructions called "Liber O," which is usually found as an appendix to
several editions of "Magick in Theory and Practice."  Similar versions can
be found in several currently available books about the Golden Dawn order.
It is considered to be an essential ritual to learn by most groups who teach
"western" (actually medieval Hebrew qabalism) magick.  
    Well, Crowley was not known for indexing well, but that's no excuse for
current publishers not to produce one.  I take it you have the paperback
Dover edition of "Magick...".  Weiser's hardbound edition has a very 
thorough index compiled by Symonds & Grant.
