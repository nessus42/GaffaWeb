Hi all,

Someone mailed me to say that there was a need for an update on the Kate
Bush Covered project. So here I am

Not much has changed since the last time I mailed. I am still hoping for
the last few contributions. A few people are quite busy working on their
recordings, but as much (or more) people have disappeared. And so I can
still use a few recordings. I hope to be able to finish the project soon,
but I'm totally dependant on the contributors! Please mail me if you're
interested in sending me something _soon_. 

I've received a wonderful illustration from Brian Wright, which will be
used for the cover of the compilation CD and tape. And I am rather busy
with my own track as well.... 

All contributors to Kate Bush Covered so far are once again thanked, they
will receive the tape of 90 minutes of Kate Bush covers once I have all
songs in. Everyone who wants to send me a recording, please mail.
