Hi everybody,

I guess I'm a bit late for this, but I just
found out that the German pay per-view TV-
station "Premiere" is broadcasting "Two Rooms" - A tribute
to Elton John and Bernie Taupin today,
Friday, 14.30 o'clock.
Unfortunately we cannot watch this, I wonder
if they show "Rocket Man"? But I guess it's
just the usual "Two Rooms"-Video.

I received my copy of the "The Kick Inside"
re-release vinyl yesterday, it was a good feeling
to buy a new "LP"-release in these modern compact disc
days. The shop assistant almost had tears in her eyes:
"Oh my god, a REAL vinyl album!" she said when
I bought the album. Strange but true!

Wishing you a nice weekend

Beate
