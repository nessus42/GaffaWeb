Hi everybody,

On Saturday I watched the German VIVA II TV show "Geschmackssache" (A 
Matter Of Taste).
This time the English pop-duo Erasure showed their favourite videos. 
They also chose Kates "Rocket Man"! Singer Andy Bell said that Erasure 
wanted Kate to produce one of their albums, but she had been to busy 
with the "Red Shoes" at that time.

They also said that Kate looks very sweet in the video, a bit like 
Marylin Monroe in one of her legendary films... and that Kate has "the 
smallest bum in the whole pop business". Cute, isn't it?
