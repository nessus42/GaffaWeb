Hello again,

I have created a neat image (well I think so) of the intro to "Feel It".
It is an animated gif which versions of netscape can view (animated),
check out:
	http://home.cray.com/~btd/kate-bush/
Click on the HTML version reference, and scroll down to the header of
the MIDI sections, just after the word search section.

For those who won't be able to view it, it is an image of the first
two bars (the intro) of "Feel It", looking like sheet music, with a
treble and a bass clef. The neat bit is that the music "plays". OK
it is silent, it is just the notes on the staves "lighting up" in
proper time to the music. I was particularly pleased to squeeze it
into *just* 4k (using "no dispose" and "positional image offset"
and VERY small image updates, for those techie amoungst you).
It loops forever, which is not quite right, but I like that intro
and could play it all day!

Enjoy, even if for a few seconds, or play along!

			Bryan Dongray

PS I also put a 12 second MIDI version of this intro (all of 552 bytes).
