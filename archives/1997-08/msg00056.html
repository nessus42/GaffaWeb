Rolf Peukert wrote:
>What's a good Jane Siberry album to start with? 
>(I've already heard "Bound By The Beauty", and didn't like it that much)

My favorite is "The Speckless Sky", for some of the same reasons I love
Kate. Wonderful, creative and original backing vocals. 

BTW, Laurie Freelove's "Smells Like Truth" is one of the closest I've heard
to Kate's HOL. The only problem is that it's no longer available except if
you can find it used. She has a gift for great melodies.

One more thing...I'll be here waiting until the next Kate Bush album, how
ever long it takes. Even if there are no more I'm thankful for the ones we
all have.
