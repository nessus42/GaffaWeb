Yeah, I've been silent for several weeks, but since others are complaining-and
I never miss a chance to complain!-enough already.  Lets communicate about
God, er KaTe...thats why I joined the list.

No offense meant to you Happy fans...but anything that I am continually forced
fed drives me nuts!

The lyrics are great, mucho appreciation for them, but next time, I would
suggest we simply put in a notice that stuff like that is available, and 
distribute via PRIVATE email.

Just  so I may launch a few more missiles, I was just about at the breaking
point with the Fripp/Eno talk.  Someone also said that the Jane Sibery (sp?)
talk (and the similar Happy talk) had started a trend of "Musical Correctness
" in the group and I kind of agree.

Let me say that I love everything KaTe has done.  I also can't stand Peter
Gabriel.  So, based on my love of Kate, but balanced with my dislike of PG
how do you predict my feelings for Jane Siberry, or Happy, or...

This is a KaTe list.  Let's enjoy her stuff with the occaissional side artist.
And not judge anyone else's opinion.

Whew!  where's the weekend?  Gentlemen, arm your flamethrowers!
