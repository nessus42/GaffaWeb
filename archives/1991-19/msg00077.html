Lovers of the more esoteric parts of British culture will be pleased
to learn that London chef Marco Piere White chose Wuthering Heights
by Kate Bush as one of his 8 gramophone records to take with him on
a desert island during the BBC Radio 4 programme Desert Island Disks,
broadcast 2nd and 7th June.
