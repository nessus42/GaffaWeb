In Washington, the best place around is Vinyl Ink in Alexandria and Bethesda.  Though I moved from DC two years ago, I remember that they had a good collection of KaTe stuff (including the 6 foot by 6 foot (!) Hounds of Love poster) and many other things a well.  They also host the record conventions in the DC area...

Should be worth the trip.
