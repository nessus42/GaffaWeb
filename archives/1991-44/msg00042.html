After the first request I emailed the person who requested, but now, after
the second request, I dumped the contents on Notes (c.q. News).

Well, here is the list of songs.

Disc 1
------
1.  The Empty Bullring
2.  Ran Tan Waltz
3.  Passing Through Air
4.  December Will Be Magic Agian
5.  Warm And Soothing
6.  Lord Of The Reedy River
7.  Ne T'En Fui Pas
8.  Un Baiser D'Enfant
9.  Under The Ivy
10. Burning Bridge
11. My Lagan Love
12. The Handsome Cabin Boy
13. Not This Time
14. Walk Straight Down The Middle
15. Be Kind To My Mistakes

Disc 2
------
1.  I'm Still Waiting
2.  Ken
3.  One Last Look Around The House Before We Go...
4.  Wuthering Heights (New Vocal)
5.  Experiment IV
6.  Them Heavy People [1]
7.  Don't Push Your Foot On The Heartbrake [1]
8.  James And The Cold Gun [1]
9.  L'Amour Looks Something Like You [1]
10. Running Up That Hill [2]
11. Cloudbusting (The Organon Mix)
12. Hounds Of Love (Alternative)
13. The Big Sky (Meteorological Mix)
14. Experiment IV (12" Mix)

Remarks:
[1] Live (from the 1979 tour)
[2] 12" version


1>(And, if anyone knows, which of her previously released tracks are *not*
1>included there?)

I'm not quite sure if it is complete, but the following officially released
songs are not on it:
1.  Dreamtime [instrumental version of The Dreaming]
2.  Running Up That Hill (instrumental version)
3.  The Confrontation [from the Comic Strip Film *GLC*, at the B-side of one
    of the singles taken from The Sensual World album, it's instrumental]
4.  The Sensual World (instrumental version)

Most of live versions of songs from the 1979 tour, but they are not officially
released, except for the ones on the box.

Hope you've got the information you wanted.

Ben.
