Well, surprisingly enough, I found it today. The cover is a photo of
KaTe almost identical to the one on the This Woman's Work single,
except she's smiling a bit more. There's also a purple bit at the top and
bottom. The CD-single comes in a sort of gatefold Ecopak thingy with
the same shot of KaTe inside the gatefold. The catalog(ue) number is
Mercury TRICD2 in the UK or 866 311-2 elsewhere.

BTW, Candle in the Wind is (P) 1991 Novercia Ltd, which might imply it
was a last minute job just as a B-side for Rocket Man and not
something she did at the same time.
