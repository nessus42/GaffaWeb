Long time, no sea...
I haven't seen much letters in Gaffa lately. 
But then: I haven't read any mail for quite some time. 
My budget says that I can _not_ by Kate's latest album.
My stomach says I must _not_ deviate from my curret budget.
How fortunate for me it is, that I have a younger brother, young enough
to take my word for Law. 
I said: The red Shoes is a great album, I'd like to lend it for a while...
That's how I came to have it.
Personally I think some songs could have been left out:
I don't like Eat the music. I think it is too much something that in Swedish is
called "trallande klaemtjaeck". I don't know English enough to translate it.
If anyone could do so, please let me know. With that I mean that I think that 
the song is too simple, too easy to digest. Or perhaps I missed a point here...

The really good ones are:
Lily  
Big stripy Lie    (My no. one favourite)
Constellation of the heart
The red shoes
and finally Why should I love you

Although Moments of pleasure is very beautiful, I'm sick and tired of it.
My three favourite albums are still:
1. The kick inside
2. Lionheart
3. Hounds of love

best regards...
