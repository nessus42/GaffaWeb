Hello friends,

First things first - is there anybody out there? I haven't had any
new gaffa postings here for two weeks. If there's been a global

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
+  A DATE FOR THE 1994 KATE BUSH CONVENTION !!! +
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++


Subject to final confirmation the 1994 Convention will take place on
Sunday April 10th at The Hippodrome in London's West End.

Dave is working on the programme now.

We are hoping to have "The Line The Cross and the Curve" and the 
film about making the film.  We have much of the recent video footage.

We are hoping Kate will attend. This is subject to final confirmation.

See you all there!
