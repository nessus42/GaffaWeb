As I posted a while back, "Teddy" is most likely Jon <somebody>, an
assistant engineer at Abbey Road Studios during the recording of Never
Forever.  The name "Teddy" comes from the nicknames Andrew Powell (?),
Kate and Jon gave themselves at the time (Andy Pandy, Loopy Lou and
Teddy, from the old Andy Pandy childrens' TV show). I got this from an
old KBC magazine article reproduced in The Garden.
