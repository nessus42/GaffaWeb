I was looking to find Roy Harper's Unknown Soldier (1980)
for the song "YOU" a duet with Kate Bush .
Instead I found "An Introduction To Roy Harper"
(Science Friction 1994) which includes "YOU".
On the cover there is no mention of Kate .
Inside it says "YOU" a duet with Kate Bush who
claims Roy as one of her major inspirations .
This song is very good , but I don't know if I would
still like it if it wasn't for Kate .
Roy Harper also wrote "Another Day" , the song
that Kate sung live with Peter Gabriel in a TV show
in 1979 .
