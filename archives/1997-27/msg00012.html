This is sort of fascinating -- I always heard the opium reference sort
of the same way I saw it in the Wizard of Oz -- sort of a harmless
reference, and very visually loaded, "...like poppies heavy with seed"
is a beautiful lyric that creates a pretty picture in my head, IMO.  But
then remembering that what awakened Dorothy was "snow...." well, I see
how these suppositions are made.

One more twist on the lyrics of Under Ice -- where she sings about there
being someone moving under the ice, and then the wailing "it's
MEEEEE...." at the end:  picture someone bending over that mirror and
snorting up the little lines, and looking at their reflection in that
mirror, 'under the ice....'

I don't know if I buy any of this, but its interesting to contemplate.
