Melanie wrote her "candle song" about the Woodstock experience.  I believe
the title is" Lay Down (Candles in the Rain)".  It doesn't have anything to
do with Marilyn Monroe, but it is an excellent song.  Her other general
recognizable hits were "Brand New Key" (the roller skating song) and "Look
What They Done to My Song Ma" which was bastardized recently in a
commercial (salad dressing?).
