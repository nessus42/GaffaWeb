> In Morning Fog she has been rescued.

I always felt that the album ended without resolution -- morning
arrived, and she had survived the night, and that the rest was more of a
"if I ever get out of this, this is what I'm going to do...." kind of
thing.  By the end, we didn't really know if she'd been rescued.  And, I
like not knowing -- I feel it makes it more poignant.
