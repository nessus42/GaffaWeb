   After Diana died, I put on Lionheart, and was deeply touched by "In
Search Of Peter Pan". To me that represented Diane, and her England. My
husband played the "Universal Mother" cd by Sinead Oconner and I cried so
hard he had to turn it off. The entire CD seemed to represent Diana. 
    I really liked Elton John's new "Candle in the Wind". I was okay
watching the funeral until he sang. I thought that was a beautiful
thoughtful gift to give someone. I am sure it meant so much to Diana's
sons, and the money from the sales of the single will go to charity. Just
my opinion.
