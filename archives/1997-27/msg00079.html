This has been answered here before (but I dunno if it's in the FAQ), 
but the references to the archangels (not "guardian angels," though I 
suppose they could be seen as that) is taken from the ritual called 
"The Lower Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram" or LBRP for 
short (though she reverses the order of some of the angels and 
directions). It's a ceremonial magick ritual that's also used by some 
Neopagans and Wiccans, especially by those who are Alexandrian (a 
kind of Wiccan).

Since the topic of Paganism and magick is a rather touchy one on this 
newsgroup/mailing list (rather needlessly, IMHO, since if you don't 
believe in it, just ignore posts on it, and it's clearly an 
*influence* on Kate's work--one of *many* influences--though there's 
no evidence she *practices*), I won't go into too much more details, 
but look through the archives, since I and others have posted more 
details on it, including part of a Kabbalah FAQ that mentions "Lily." 
Also, do web searches for LBRP, Golden Dawn (a ceremonial magick 
group, most probably the origin of the LBRP), Wicca, etc., and you 
can learn more about it than you ever wanted to know. I recommend 
AvatarSearch <http://www.avatarsearch.com> for occult and magickal 
related searches, as it's a search engine only for the occult.
