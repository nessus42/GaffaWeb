late 1860's - was able to transport himself to Mars, where he met 
the beautiful Princess DaDa Thorazine and fought many battles on 
her behalf (before she went to rehab). 

He currently lives in Kent, a consultant to the British Museum's 
Martian Artifact Exhibit.
