Very quick KateSpot (and a StevieSpot as well, actually!):

UNIVERSITY CHALLENGE (better known to US folks as COLLEGE BOWL),
BBC2, 8pm, Wednesday 10th January 1996,
UNIVERSITY OF WARWICK vs PEMBROKE COLLEGE, OXFORD.

Music round on the theme of songs with months in the title;

"December will be magic again" was one of the songs (along with Morrissey's
"November spawned a monster" and "September" by Earth, Wind and Fire).

Imagine my delight when as a member of the Warwick team, the Kate question had
to be included in my first round match, not anyone else's.  The delight on my
face was, I'm told, obvious.  I'm also getting a lot of stick from my mates
that that was the only reason we won!!

Quick plug for the next round involving Warwick:
8pm, BBC2,
Wednesday 21st February 1996!!

Cheers then,

Stevie
