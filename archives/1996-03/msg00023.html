Neither the promo nor the commercial release of "Symphony In Blue" on
blue vinyl came with a picture sleeve.  The sleeve Uli mentioned for
the yellow vinyl single of "Wow!" IS the picture sleeve.  The promo
yellow vinyl single for "Wow" doesn't come with one.  All were released
by Harvest Records and have (if in mint condition) sold for around
$50US each.  Prices may have declined in the last few years due to her
waning popularity (can blame it on the length of time inbetween
albums).
