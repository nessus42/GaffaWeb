	I've been a fan of her's since her "Wuthering Heights/Lionheart" 
period, but I must admit I'm really getting tired of waiting 3-5yrs per 
album, I mean whats keeeping her??? (Don't tell me the perfection 
story-there are many artists out there who achieve amazing feats of music 
without nearly as much delay).  Is she on a lax schedule, now that she's 
rich, or has she been emotionally distracted.

single but why is that?  A beautiful, and talented artist like her should 
be in great demand for a wife or at least a girlfriend?  Please, please 
let her release her albums quicker and at a steadier pace, I've been 
forced to turn to Tori Amos, Lorna McKinnett, and Enya, but it's just not 
Kate still!
