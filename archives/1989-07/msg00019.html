The May 1989 issue of Q with Peter Gabriel (on the cover and
interviewed) is $4.25 at newstands in the U.S. , my *guess* is that if
you send them $5.00 that would be enough to cover postage (if I
remember right, there is no overseas back issue price quoted) - but
only if they take US dollars, otherwise they might need another dollar
just to convert it.  It might be easier/cheaper just to find a place
in the US that has it available by mail order (check a US music
magazine for ads in the rear).
