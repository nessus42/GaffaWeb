Talk Talk is a good band. Their first two albums are both of high quality
and are quite accessible (although that's probably not a good thing for the
people in this group 8+). I thought the music in their third album was less
rich than the previous two. Although the lyrics are if anything more
thoughtful, and the music does have a spare elegance. I like all three
albums, but I have a slight preference for the fuller sound of their first
two. I think a fourth album is out, but I haven't heard it yet.
