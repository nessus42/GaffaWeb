I recently purchased a copy of THE COMPACT XTC.  It is a greatest hits
compact disc that takes full advantage of the added length of a CD.
I recommend it to someone who wants to pick up the best of the early XTC
(and who also has a CD player).   My copy was an import (approx. $18)
and I am not sure if it is available as an American release.
