After much searching, I finally located the Joy Division video.  The film
is dark and dreary and not too high in sound or video quality, but
otherwise incredible; *essential* to any music video collection.
   I've been able to track down three of four JD books I'm aware of;     
"An Ideal for Living", "Pleasures and Wayward Distractions", and Mike
West's "Joy Division".  I'm still looking for "Joy Division/New Order -- 
A History In the Cuttings".  Please write if you can obtain this book,
or have/want any other JD info.
