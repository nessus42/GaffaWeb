KT-related news:

The debut LP from British duo Shelleyan Orphan, called "Helleborine,"
was recently released on Rough Trade in the UK and has just appeared
domestically, as well. The group uses acoustic instruments
in unusual combinations, and among the musicians on the album are
Kate affiliates Stuart Elliot (percussion), Danny Thompson
(bass), Andrew Powell (harpsichord), and -- Paddy Bush
(taboura, strumento da porco, mandolin)!

So stick with it, musically productive Love-Hounds. Perhaps
one day Fred in a Phonebooth will be able to attract a guest
contribution from an authentic Bush-family session musician...
