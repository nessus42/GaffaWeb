The Love-Hounds Archives are available once again via anonymous FTP.
Unfortunately, they were unavailable for a period of of time, but the
situation has been remedied.  They are located on my VAXstation, which
is GAFFA.MIT.EDU, in the directory /pub/love-hounds/archives, and they
are numbered chronologically.  To use anonymous FTP, yous host must be
on the Internet, and you should use the FTP program with the username
"anonymous" and with your name as the password.  Love-Hounds postings
which have not yet made it in to the archives on GAFFA.MIT.EDU are
stored in a file on EDDIE.MIT.EDU, which is also available via
anonymous FTP: /u/nessus/s/lh/lh-archive.  There may also be on
occasion archives temporarily in the directory /u/nessus/s/lh/archives
awaiting shipment to GAFFA.MIT.EDU.  There is also on EDDIE.MIT.EDU a
directory /u/nessus/s/kb that may contain material of interest.  At
the present time, don't expect it to be in a particularly orderly
state.
