As various people have been discussing the different versions of KT CDs
and the markings, I thought I would have a butchers at my own set - and
what a motley collection they turned out to be.

  Hounds of Love - ADD Made in Japan
  The Dreaming - AAD Made in UK
  Never For Ever - AAD Made in UK
  Lionheart - AAD Made in UK
  The Kick Inside - (not marked) Made in West Germany

All were bought in standard record shops in GB. As I was keen to have
the discs I did not pay any attention to where they were produced when
I bought them.
