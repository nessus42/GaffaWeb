Subject: Katemas


We had a pretty good turn out in Santa Cruz (much better & Tracy's apartment
would've been overwhelmed).  9-1/2 hours (for me at least) of videos,
interviews, music, & discussion (& a singing of Happy Birthday).  A round of
thanks to our gracious host, Tracy.  A splendid time was guaranteed for all.
Even if we did go a bit off the deep end in the interpretation of Kate's
vidoes.  Tongues were planted firmly in cheek.

A belated Happy Katemas to all who were unable to attend one of the 3
advertised parties across the nation.

Now, time to hurry up and wait for _The Sensual World_.  Hopefully we'll see
some imported stuff around the time of the initial release.
