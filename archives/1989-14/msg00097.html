     Sorry, Love-Hounds, for this breach of protocol. The following
is a personal message to Justin Bur, whom IED could not reach directly.

Hi, Justin.
     Thanks very much for the note, but you can relax the search.
I've already been told to steer clear of the European _HoL_ that
lists the extended _Running_Up_That_Hill_ on its track-listing.
At least two people have told me that they went and bought that
edition because of the track-listing, only to find that the
extra track was _not_ on the CD itself. Seems someone simply
copied the track-listings for the cassette edition of the album
when that CD was made, and never noticed that it wasn't actually
on the CD version. Bummer, huh?
     So I hope this gets to you in time. Save your money! And
thanks again for taking the trouble to find out about it for me.

-- Andy
