It is unfortunate that Tim must feel so compelled to be obnoxious on the net.
It seems that he enjoys attacking others, not to prove a point, for that can
be done with more style than the above.

I have seen Tim pick net-fights in not only this news group, but 3 others, and

habit of being the instigator of needless flame-wars on other news groups that

called alt.maroney, so that he can further annoy fellow netters by his presence.

It is sad to see that someone who is skilled enough to create such helpful
Mac software as 'Backdrop', has nothing better to do with his time now, then to
cause so much noise on the net.
