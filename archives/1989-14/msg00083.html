From: ed@das.llnl.gov (Edward Suranyi)

Subject: DISCoveries letter


I think a collaboration between IED and Deb Wentorf would be
a wonderful idea.  I cannot stress enough the importance of
contacting the editor of DISCoveries *now* with your suggestion.
Be prepared to demonstrate your qualifications.  Only he knows
the answer to your questions, IED, such as the article's length
and its desired emphasis.  I'm sure a discography will be wanted.
It's important to get in touch with him now, because for all
we know he's already found someone else to do the article.

You should be able to find the magazine (and thus its address)
in many record stores, but if you can't I'll post the address
next time.  (I don't happen to have it with me now.)
