Second of all, in response to the Pink Floyd Discussion (re: Pink gets
caught in an infinite loop), the first time I saw the movie I was
always getting confused between old pink and pink's father, and his
mother and his girlfriend.  For a while I thought it was always
following the lives of one person, and until I saw it the second time
and spent a couple of hours in contemplation afterwards did I finally
get everything straight.  You see, Pink was following in similar
footsteps to his father. They both were soldiers, anyway.  Perhaps we
are to interpret it as "like father, like son"....
