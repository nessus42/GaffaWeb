     Since Ray Radlein asked, he'll be happy to know that IED _did_
catch the spelling error of his name before making up the final version
of the card. In fact, the version he posted in Love-Hounds was chosen
simply because it was the only version IED had re-formatted for
screen-reading. There were two or three versions after that one,
and Vishal will be happy to learn that IED caught the upper-case
I in his name before sending off the final card. Also, there were
about six or seven other people who were added to the final list
in time to make the card.
     A happy, happy Katemas to everyone! This is a pretty important
one, too--practically on the eve of KBVI's release, and there is
so much other "new" music to appreciate this time around, as well...
