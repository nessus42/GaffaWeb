Below is some news of Passion, taken from the Spring edition of Gabbleratchet.

These two albums are now due for release on 22 and 23 May by Geffen in the US,
and Virgin in the UK.  The Passion Sources will be one of the first of a
series on the Real World Label.

The Passion album is Peter's own material, developed from his soundtrack music
for THE LAST TEMPTATION OF CHRIST, and the Passion Soucres is longer than 60
minutes, it will be released on vinyl as a double album. (CD and cassette as a
single album).

There will be various publicity events and interviews, which are currently
being organised by the record company.

Peter has contributed a track to Youssou's next album, which is due for release
on 15th May.  Peter is working on his own version of the song which he may
release as a single under the title SHAKE THE TREE.
