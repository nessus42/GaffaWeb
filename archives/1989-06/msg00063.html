IED writes:
> His fervent hope is that Kate will eventually see the senselessness
> of withholding these demos from the public herself (since it only
> means that bootleggers will reap all of her own potential profit), and
> decide to put at least some of the early recordings out on EMI, in a
> properly produced edition.

This would be nice, but wouldn't Kate feel somewhat embarrassed about
these old recordings and not want to release them because she feels
they are not up to her current standards?  This is somewhat in line
with her wanting to re-record her old album tracks for "The Whole
Story", and her comments in one of the interviews IED posted that if
she were to go on tour now she wouldn't play any of the music from her
older albums.
