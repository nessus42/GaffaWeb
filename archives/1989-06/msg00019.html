I not completely sure if this belongs in this group, but when I saw
King Crimson in 1981 they played a couple of older songs. I believe
they were restricted to Fripp compositions, and I remember Lark's
Tongue in Aspic Pt 2.

I don't remember them playing 21st Century Schizoid man.

Trivia note:
Tony Levin and Kate Bush both appear on Roy Harper's 1981 release,
called "Unknown Soldier" in Canada (probably "Headquarters"
elsewhere).  Roy Harper is quite amazing himself if you ever want to
track down some of his stuff.  He has done slightly more melodic work
than his Kate Bush contributions.
