	What I heard, just yesterday, was that Tolhurst was out as
	of the report time, and that he would NOT be joining Robert
	on the current tour which is supposed to start VERY soon.
		
	No reasons were reported, but they simply mentioned that
	Tolhurst would no longer have any relation to the band.  I
	would have to say that's quite a bummer, seeing as how he's
	been there from the beginning, which means god only knows
	what things will sound like without him.  I didn't hear
	explicitly, but I am assuming he was involved on 'Disintegration'
	since I haven't heard otherwise.

	I'm looking forward to the album (of course) and even more so
	the tour.  Please let me know if you hear anything as soon as
	you do, and I will certainly do the same.
