Actually there are two albums I know of that came out after *Whatever
Happened to Jugula?*: *Born in Captivity* and *Looney on the Bus*.
Neither of these is completely new material, however.  *Born In
Captivity* is a remake of *Work of Heart* done much more simply and
not over-produced the way *Work of Heart* is.  *Looney on the Bus*
is a compilation, but I only recognize two songs on the rocord, so I'm
not sure where the rest of the songs come from.
