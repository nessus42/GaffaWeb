Subject: Misinterpretation


The recent highly entertaining sniping between IED and |>oug reminds me of 
one of my most embarrassing moments.  I was talking with the guitarist from 
Bourgeois Tagg - a great Sacramento band that, unfortunately, has disbanded - 
about one of the songs, "Electric Train", from their first album.  I asked him
if it was about masturbation - since that was my interpretation, and I'd
found many lyric clues which supported that assumption.  He got me together
with the songwriter, who had a hearty guffaw when I asked the same question.
It turned out that the song was a metaphor for the Biblical creation story.
My interpretation was a little off-base.

In my defense, all I can say is that I was listening to too much Prince at
that point... :-)
