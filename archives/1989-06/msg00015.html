Subject: Genesis bootlegs.


As much as Phil Collins gets slammed for the degeneration of GENESIS,
there is some great post Gabriel stuff around. In specific, Trick,
Wind and And Then are three very good albums.

I have two GENESIS boots from this period.

REVELATORY GENESIS is awesome. It is a double boot from the Duke tour.
The quality is pretty good. Double drumming in Duke's Travels great..

LIVE AT EARL'S COURT is a single, containing live stuff from Wind and
Trick. Quality is o.k.
