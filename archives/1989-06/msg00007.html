Some scoop on the late-great King Crimson videos:

For context, I divide their tours into 4 tours, which may be
inaccurate: "Discipline" in early to mid-81, "KC Discipline"
corresponding to their adoption of the name King Crimson in late 81
and early 82, "KC Beat" during late 82, and "KC 3-Pair" in 84.
