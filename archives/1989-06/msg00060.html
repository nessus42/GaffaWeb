Subject: Sarah McLachlan's Lagan Love

While listening to the song at the concert I was thinking how
familiar the music sounded but that the words seemed new to me
-- even to the point of wondering if it was a different song
set to the same melody, until I saw the sound man's list of
songs which had "LAGAN" at the top.  Conclusion: not Kate's
lyrics, probably traditional ones.
