Subject: Liner Notes for KC

Liner notes is a poor term. We are not talking about 1-4 pages here.
These "notes" are actually a booklet of at least 12, maybe as much as
20 pages. Also, the type is small, maybe 4 or 5 pts. tall.  Unless
someone somewhere entered thi in, don't expect someone to type them
in. Go out and LOCATE the album and booklet, then read it. Check out
used record stores, because The Young Person's Guide to KC shows up a
lot.
