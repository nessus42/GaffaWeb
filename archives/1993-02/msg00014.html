Hi all.  Was just perusing the latest issue of Tower _Pulse!_, and in the 
article "The Best Records of 1992" under 'Dance Music' I found (along with 
the expected R&B and hip-hop artists):

"_Warpaint_ -- Happy Rhodes (Aural Gratification): sensitive singer/songwriter
with a brilliant debut album and a vocal delivery like Kate Bush."

Not bad, but am I the only one who finds this placement odd?  Not that I'm a 
great fan of labels and pigeon-holing, but this is about the last place I
would have expected Happy's name to pop up.  At least they spelled it
correctly. :)

Oh well.  Shake it but don't break it.
