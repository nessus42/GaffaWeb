In article <63295@apple.Apple.COM>, daemon@Apple.COM (Wizard's
Assistant) writes:
> I read a lot of  messages of KaTemas in the US, but does  anyone  know
> something about previouw  KaTemases in the UK?  Or does  Homeground or
> KBC organize  something?  I'm in the UK only since November 1991, so I
> myself don't have any experience with that.

Well, last year was a bit of an anticlimax (unlike in the US where a
good time seemed to be had by all). Andy Semple (are you still out
there?) organised a list of potential KaTemas participants well in
advance, but in the end nothing happened (at least I didn't hear of
anything, apart from Colin's not-specifically-KaTemas party in Ipswich).

I don't think Hg or KBC organise anything officially, but from the
personals in Homeground, it seems to be a tradition for some KaTefans
to gather at KaTe's old local pub, The Fanny on the Hill (good grief,
what an embarrasing name 8^) on Katemas Day, in deepest Welling.
