"Ran Tan Waltz" --- I was once told that in Kent, Essex etc. the term 'dick' 
was slang for a type of jug used for homemade alcohol....anyone know the 
truth?
Organon v. Orgonon --- (you all must be sick of this by now) I think if Kate 
intentionally ment to say organon (although she did claim it was a spelling 
error) it would fit in quite nicely since Organon underwent similar 
persecution in the 12th century as Reich's theories in the 20th. It seems 
that the tracks used to make up Aristotle's Organon were discovered by 
western scholars and Churchmen AFTER they had reconciled neo-platonic theory 
with Christian doctrine thus Organon became a threat. But in the end, thanks 
to St. Thomas Aquinas Aristotle's works were finally brought into the fold 
instead considered heretical studies. I'd like to think perhaps Kate knows 
this bit of history and might be saying something about Reich's work....that 
as people now find Reich "crazy" only time would prove him right or wrong., 
as it did for Aristotle? But like I said earlier she just claimed it was a 
spelling mistake.
