[About "Ran Tan Waltz"]

>Does anyone know anything about this song, when it was recorded, where it's 
>available, etc.?

It was originally the B-side on the "Babooshka" single, released in 1980,
I think.  Kate performed it on her television special "Kate", which
appeared around Christmas, 1979.  It was made available on CD for the
first time as part of the "This Woman's Work" boxed set -- it appears
as one of the tracks on the first of the two extra CDs in that boxed set.
