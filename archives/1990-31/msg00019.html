Well, I have to admit that both I and Carina were as green of
envy as Deb Wentorf this Saturday... but, we're happy anyway!
Of course, we allowed ourselves to play only Kate Bush all day
long --- especially the newly arrived b-sides disks from the 
TWW box, and, for some reason, the NFE album.

Though, it is wondeful to know that all of us that didn't manage
to go there can look forward to reading all the marvellous reports
by you lucky ones who went! What should we do without you? For
example we couldn't start saving our kronor and oren already, in
order to go to the KT shows next year...!

Now for some questions:

Have any of you out there scanned the New KT symbol? If so,
please post the details, since I suppose there is a general
interest for this information.

I've been wodering lately: how do I recognize 1st pressings
of ordinary LP albums? I mean, TKI pic disk is easy, since
there's scratchings in the run-off area, different formed
stickers, etc. But how do I tell an ordinary LP album? If any
of you Love-Hounds out there have the knowledge: please post
it here! I suppose this also is general interest information.

That's that! Live Well!

In LUX,
