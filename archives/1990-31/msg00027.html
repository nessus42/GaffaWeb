Sorry to break discussion away from the KonvenTion (which by the way
is great to read about -- here's hoping it is held again next year and
I can go!) ---

I just got the new Danielle Dax CD this week;  it's called _Blast the
Human Flower_.  It seems to me to be a concerted pop effort, at least
compared to the bulk of material on _Dark Adapted Eye_.  It's very
nicely packaged with a booklet about twenty pages long containing the
lyrics.  Repeated listenings have made the music "grow on me" quite a
bit!

Has anyone heard whether she'll tour or not?
