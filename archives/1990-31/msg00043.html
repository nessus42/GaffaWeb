Subject: Kate on TV

I caught a trailer for a new set of BBC2 spots coming up in the next couple
of week called "Prisoners of Conscience". In these spots a celebrity
introduces the case history of a political prisoner - I believe the details
are provided by Amnesty International. Earlier this year Peter Gabriel was
one of the speakers. Anyway, amongst the list of names said to be involved
in the new series was Kate Bush. The programmes only lasts about five minutes
but they should be interesting to watch. Looking through the Radio Times for
next week I cannot see Kate mentioned, so chances are that it will be the
week after.
