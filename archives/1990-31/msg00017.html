First, to clear up this Canadian CD box set rumor.  There is no Canadian
version.  We get the UK version as does the US.  I bought mine last Saturday
(paid $147Cdn about $130US) at the local HMV outlet.  There are only two
differences from the Canadian releases that I can see.  These are:

1) The Canadian CD of TSW has the silkscreened picture mentioned above.  The
   UK version is disappointingly bland.  I guess I'll have to keep both copies.

2) The cover of TKI CD is different from the Canadian LP (and I think the
   Canadian CD).  Hopefully the following summary of TKI covers is correct:
   Canadian LP : Kate holding her hair away from her face (on the page before
                 the centre page of the TWW booklet in the box set; opposite
                 the picture of Kate with the bag pipe (?))
   Canadian CD : Kate in a box (one of the first pictures in the TWW booklet)
   UK CD       : Japanese style lettering and motif with Kate swinging/hanging
                 in front of the backdrop.

   I don't know if the US releases are the same as this or not.
