I have received some messages from people complaining that
mod.music.gaffa is not making it to their site.  (mod.music.gaffa is
the newsgroup version of the Love-Hounds mailing list, which is for
fans of Kate Bush, and other progressive music, e.g. the Butthole
Surfers, etc.)  mod.music.gaffa does exists and does have traffic.  If
you are not receiving it at your site, and you would like to, you
should ask your system administrator about it.  Some sites have
netnews configured such that new newsgroups must be explicitly added
by a system administrator.  The system administrators at sites with
problems, may very well have merely forgotten to add mod.music.gaffa
to the list of active newsgroups.

Alternatively, one may ask me to put him or her directly on the
Love-Hounds mailing list.
