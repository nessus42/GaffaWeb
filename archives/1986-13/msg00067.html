As for straigtening out warped records, Doug's method will work but can
be improved on by use of a hot water bath as opposed to an oven.  The
water temperature should be about 130-150 deg. Far. (go too much higher and
you stand the chance of compressing the records).  Another technique, which
I have successfully used in the past involves putting the warped records
in your collection but cram them in (the records should be placed in
a vertical arangement, i.e. side by side).  This latter method works best
in warm environments, although it takes a considerable time to achieve
the desired effect.
