IS THIS SOME KIND OF SICK JOKE, OR WHAT?

To the person who submitted the message about
"Murder Music", and who doesn't know how to
spell his own alleged title:

Contrary to your presumptious assumption,
"we" are NOT your "supporters".

The people who post to Love-Hounds
express a wide range of opinions about
musical and other matters.

We do NOT agree on all of these matters,
LEAST OF ALL ON OPINIONS ABOUT WHAT IS
HARMFUL MUSIC, OR EVEN WHETHER MUSIC HAS
THE CAPACITY TO CAUSE HARM.

Music has never been shown to
bear any causal relationship
to the behavior or moral attitudes of its listeners.
Rather, the opposite is true: people determine the
nature of the music they hear.

It's hard to believe the "paster"
is for real. If he is, however,
this Love-Hound would like him
to stop posting to Love-Hounds
immediately. This should not become
a forum for the propagation of
extremist moral positions.
It's bad enough seeing this kind
of Jesus mumbo-jumbo creep into
Break-Through.

Please, Love-Hounds, if you
are at all offended by the "paster's"
unexpected attempt to recruit
"converts" to the cause of
suppressing freedom of expression,
second IED's motion that sleazy
religious tract-mongers leave our midst forthwith.

Thanks.
            -- Andrew Marvick
