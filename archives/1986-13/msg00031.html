Hi folks, I'm back to pseudomoderate Love-Hounds.  It  might take me
a while to sort through the 300 pieces of mail I've gotten since
I've been gone, however.

Since, no one else seems to want to volunteer to print up a Love-Hounds
shirt, I volunteer to do it.  Don't be in too much of a hurry, however;
it may take a bit of time.  (If someone else wants to volunteer the job
away from me, that's fine!)  There aren't any legal issues -- no one is
going to sue me for printing up 30 unauthorized Kate Bush T-shirts that
are being sold at cost!  Be real!

Since I'm doing it, I get to be dictatorial about the design of the shirt.
It will have *The Dreaming* picture on the front, if that is possible to
do in a high quality manner.  It will have the quote "With a kiss I'd
pass the key" on it.  It will be 50% cotton, 50% polyester.  It won't
be done by next week.

			-Doug
