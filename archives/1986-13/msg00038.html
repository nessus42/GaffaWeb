Subject: The warped record of history

I've heard that the following method  works for unwarping records,  but I've
never tried it:

Get two big pieces of plate glass.  Clean them *very* well.  Take a record
to be unwarped and put it between the two pieces of plate glass. Clamp
the the sandwitch together.  Throw it in the oven for a little while.
Take it out and let it cool.

It will probably take a little experimenting to get the temperature
and time right.  I'd try it on a less-favoured album first.
