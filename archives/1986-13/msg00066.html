Ah, the fun I had while in college radio.  I know for a fact that
there are two version.  We got the album when it first premiered in 
its' original format.  Offhand, I think that the original album 
had 'Leipzig' as the only song not included on the second album.  The
album was re-mixed when 'She Blinded Me With Science' came out.  The
original songs were discofied to make them sound less ethereal.  I
couldn't figure out why they left off 'Leipzig' as it was one of the
more uptempo songs on the original.

As far as getting it, I found the original album on tape in the
throwaway cabinet at "Cheap Thrills", a local record store.  You'll
just have to keep your eyes open when you search through the bins,
though.  I almost didn't catch this one...
