In the very near future, David Sylvian will be releasing "Gone to Earth":
a 2 LP set (one vocal, one instrumental) of new stuff. I am reliably 
informed that I have an icicle's chance in Hell of getting a CD copy of
the new album here in the blasted terrain of Dairyland. I'm wondering if
there are any of our British compatriots who'd be willing to inquire about
availability and cost of such an opus and perhaps pick one up for me (I'll
give you the money and maybe even throw in a copy of the USENET cassette as
a gesture of goodwill. I am a desparate man, and certainly Kate Bush fans
would be able to appreciate the lengths to which we'll go to find things.

Thanks,
Gregory Taylor
