The parallel import ban has hit home once more!  My friendly independent
dealer, Howie at Music Machine, has been fined lotsa bucks for carrying
(gasp) CDs he bought in England earlier this summer, when those CDs are
available right from good ol' American companies like Warner Bros. and
(gag) CBS!  I was under the impression that CDs were still mostly
manufactured in Japan, so aren't all CDs imports?  Could somebody fill
me in?  Sob...I can't imagine life without imports!  F'rinstance, that
cool HOUSEMARTINS album I got in June is not being released here until
next month, and the TORCH SONG LP I was given last month is not getting
its domestic release 'til next year!!!  We're such a backward nation!
