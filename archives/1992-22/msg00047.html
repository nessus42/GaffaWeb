Also, Hine has made some fairly interesting stuff on his own. I
know of two solo albums: _Pick Up a Bone_ and _The Wildest Wish
to Fly_. He also formed a band called Thinkman, whose album _The
Formula_ (with a great song on it called "Best Adventures") was 
supposed to be the basis for a science fiction movie, but I don't
think it was ever made. His lyrics were written by Jeannette
Obstoj, whom we Fixx fans remember for "Secret Separation" and
"Woman on a Train."
