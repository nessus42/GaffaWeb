It's beginning to look a lot like KaTemas,....!
Well, in lieu of a mass celebration, it looks like a voyage to the Blue Meanie
or Off The Record and forth to _listen_. Figured it'd be a kick to see a
record shop after all this time :-)

Then switch on the cloudbuster and look for results- the pretty blue light
looks like a faint Rocket's Tail in the summer twilight. Tommorrow it would be
a good idea to start by aiming at the second star from the right straight
until morning and the ninth wave. Coming in with the golden light is my dented
Ford. And here I am wondering why I did it again,.....
Too-ree-ay too-ree-o too-ree-ay too-ree-o England, my Lionheart- 
Oh, I'll wake up to any sound of engines (preferably powered by orgone
energy).



   Well, then, Happy KaTemas to anyone out there,
                                    Hello......Earth????????
..___... ...___... ...___... 

The air is getting low, but Don't put your foot on the heartbrake because...
Suddenly, there in the road, is your old self. Trying to get out of the rain,
in the golden light of the morning fog. It's then all the voices begin-
telling you things that aren't happenning.

Don't drive to slowly. Everybody thinks you'll never make it. But every time,
you escape. Now is the place where the crossroads meet- will you look into the
future?
          Posing as the night and I'm home before morning.
