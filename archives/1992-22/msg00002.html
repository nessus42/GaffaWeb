>"Ravers/travellers Spiral Tribe have had a letter of support from Kate
>Bush over charges against them at the Castlemorton Common free festival

>Does anybody know who Spiral Tribe are, what they were supposed to get
>up to at Castlemorton, and what this has got to do with KaTe?

Spiral Tribe is a loose organisation of mainly traveller/raver/newage
people who organise outdoor raves and gatherings all over the
country.  They've had quite a bit of press recently over the cat and
mouse game they've been playing with the police in organising raves
and festivals in secret locations.  The Castlemorton Free Festival was
a victory of sorts for them in that a large number of people attended
and the whole thing caught the local police unawares.  They've also
been involved (as far as I know) in attempting to organise a Summer 
Solstice festival near Stonehenge but this was stopped by a huge
police presence.  I've no idea what the charges are against them or 
why Kate would want to get involved.  Presumably its a freedom of
movement issue.
