> Anyone out there happen to know who Neil is? As in "Neil says hi by
>the way" and "hi Neil" from _Tear in your hand_. She also seems to
>sing "Neil" twice at the end of _Little Earthquakes_. (maybe I'm

Neil is Neil Gaiman, the creator of "Sandman" - a comic by DC. Apparently
he and Tori are quite good friends. If you read "Sandman" ( and you should )
then you should be able to pick up on the multiple references to the comic
within the song "Little Earthquakes". If not, go out and grab a Sandman comic.
I promise you'll be hooked for life.
