I found an interesting tape in a pile of tapes that were being sold off
at 1.99 each. It was called only Kate Bush and it had a photo of her in
her Babooshka outfit (sword and everything) and the list of songs went
as follows:
	Sat in Your Lap
	James and the Cold Gun (live)
	Babooshka
	Suspended in Gaffa
	Un Baiser d'enfant

The live version of James and the Cold Gun is not one that I have heard
before. 

The program is the same on both sides and the tape was released by EMI.

Has anyone ever come across this before?
