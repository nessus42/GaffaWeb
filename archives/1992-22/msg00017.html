Orgonon is near Rangely, Maine and about a 5-hour drive from Boston.  It       
is what Wilhelm Reich called his property and is now the location of the       
Wilhelm Reich Museum.                                                          
                                                                               
The reason it is relevant to this list is because Kate based the song          
"Cloudbusting" on a book by Dr. Reich's son, Peter describing his              
experiences with Dr. Reich and his experiments during his childhood.           
                                                                               
If we are to believe what Dr. Reich and his proponents (and Peter) say,        
then the song *is* based on fact.  Dr. Reich apparently did build a            
cloudbuster and apparently it *did* work.  Information on orgonomy can         
be found in several books including _A_Book_of_Dreams_ by Peter Reich,         
_Me_and_the_Orgone_ by Orson Bean, _Orgone,_Reich,_and_Eros_ by W.             
Edward Mann, and _Fury_On_Earth_ by Myron Sharaff.
