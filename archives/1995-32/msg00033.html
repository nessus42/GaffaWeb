has become _SiG_, so let me restate:

A perusal of _Cloudbusting_ shows Kate to describe "Breathing" solely
as being about an unborn child.  I wish one of the interviews had
cleared-up the intention of the chorus imagery.  <EOD>

At times, I have wished that Love-Hounds was more like Yes' _Notes From
the Edge_ (a "truly" moderated mailing-list), where band members have
direct input into answering the questions that arrive.  The moderators
