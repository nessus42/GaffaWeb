Let me start by saying that I absolutely adore "Eat the Music", as I have
since I bought the single the day it was released in September of '93.  I
find the sing exciting: musically, vocally, and lyrically.

That said, I was a little disturbed by comments made by woj:

>there is also an emotional aspect to this song. the key to unlocking
>this facet is the look on KaTe's face in the video.

and Alan Stonebridge:

>The music itself is really energetic... I think that's apparent from the 
>video 

This idea that we need the *video* to unlock the emotion or grasp the
energy of the song is simply ridiculous.  "Eat the Music," like all of
KaTe's work, does not need a video to aid comprehension; the song stands on
its own.
