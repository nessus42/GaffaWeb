Apropos of nothing:
Whenever I listen to "Rocket's Tail" on "Sensual World," I'm reminded of 
a James Thurber cartoon. No caption. A dog-like body with a human head on 
each end. The male head is gazing up at a star in the heavens, and the 
female head is looking at a flower growing in front of her.

I'm not suggesting any kind of influence, but the song always reminds me 
of the cartoon. Kate's character can't understand why the male character 
wants to fly like the rocket, and there's that great line about "home to 
the quickening ground."

Just wanted to throw that into the void. Thanks for listening.
