C,mon folks let's get real. Here are some pearls of
wisdom from the Collins English Dictionary to give
us some Deeper Understanding....:-)

'Nit-Picking" - a concern with insignificant details
especially with the intention of finding fault.

'Poetic Licence' - justifiable departure from conventional
rules of form, fact logic etc.

'Ennui' - a feeling of listlessness and general dissatisfaction
resulting from a lack of activity or excitement.

'Rhythm' - the arrangement of words into a more or less regular
sequence of stressed and unstressed or long or short syllables.

(Not to be confused with 'Rhythm Method').

'Execute' - To put (a condemned person) to death; inflict capital
punishment upon.

'Condemned Person' - IMHO anyone who suffers enough from ennui to consider
Nit-picking Kate's Rhythm and Poetic Licence--- ;-)
