Just got back from Boston.  Had fun reading the Phoenix, Boston's
"Alternative" paper.  It seems one of the top-10 albums for WFNX
(Boston's play-the-smiths-every-ten-minutes, er, I mean "Alternative"
station) is "Two Rooms".  This would all be fine and dandy, but under
the "Artist" column, it lists "Kate Bush".  That's it.  Just Kate,
no "Kate et al" or (as they usually do for this kind of thing)
"Various".  Just Kate's name.  Well, *I* thought it was amusing.

-Stev0
