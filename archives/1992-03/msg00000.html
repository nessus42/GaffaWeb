Wow.  Maybe it was you folks talking about in here, but I think
it must have been seeing _The Sensual World_ videos this week, my
first look at KaTe in motion, that got my subconscious rolling.
What I'm talking about is that early this morning I had my
first dream of KaTe.

She was in my house, well, not my house, some imaginary house
with a nice stereo system.  I guess I was listening to _Love and
Anger_ on the stereo, adjusting the controls, and then realizing
that there was an /a capella/ voice: KaTe's.  It was lovely to
listen to.  I could have listened to her forever.  However, my
dream wasn't going to let me.  For some reason I spent some time
talking with her press manager (!?), a young bouncy woman, watching
and listening to KaTe from a distance (maybe it's the voyeur in me).

Well, it was wonderful.  I hope you all have a dream like that.

-john
