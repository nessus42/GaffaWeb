To continue with the etchings found (sorry about an earlier interruption)
        Hounds of Love --- "woof!" and mysteriously "cooperman"
        RUTH --- "Precision SRI"  I'm not sure about SRI it's hard to see
        Under the Ivy --- "Precision SM"  which is probably what SRI really 
        This Woman's Work --- "UP yours Ugly" and "Orlake"
        Be Kind to my Mistakes --- "Orlake"
I've noticed that the etchings are only on my UK issued singles and that my 
Irish and Spanish singles have no etchings. Also I was told by someone in 
the record biz that sometimes "crew memebers" creating the singles will put 
their own names onto them which might explain some of the messages such as 
Cooperman and Orlake, but then again perhaps not.
