Dear Katefans, 

A few weeks ago, I was reading the book "The Temple of My Familiar" by Alice
Walker, when I came across a character who reminds me of Kate.   He name is
Lissie and she claims that she can remember a thousand previous reincarnations.  
No one believes her and she begins to think that she is crazy.   But then one
of her lovers, a photographer called Rafe, discovers something amazing.   He
finds that in every photograph that he takes of Lissie, she looks like a
different person.   Lissie concludes that Rafe is able through photography, to
show what she looked like in each of her previous lives.

Every photograph of Kate that I look at, I see someone different.   Perhaps
other Katefans out there would want to offer their interpretations of what
reincarnations she is manifestat
