In the Feb. issue of _Stereo Review_ there is quite a good review of
_Two Rooms_.  Here's an excerpt:

"The album not only holds up as terrific entertainment but in several cases
makes the material come alive in a new way.  Kate Bush's version of
_Rocket Man_, for instance, uses her ethereal, otherworldly whisper to
bring out the loneliness and fear an astronaut in space might readily
experience."
