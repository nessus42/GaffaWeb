Kate Bush on Radio 1

From: nbc@inf.rl.ac.uk
Date: Wed, 22 Jan 1992 07:28:36 -0800
Subject: Kate Bush on Radio 1
To: love-hounds@eddie.mit.edu

For all UK Hounds:-

On Saturday 25th January, Radio One (BBC) will be broadcasting the
first in a new series of Classic Albums. The programme will feature
Hounds of Love by Kate Bush. Time 2.00 - 3.00 pm. The Radio Times
advertises it thus (under a small colour picture): "Kate Bush: A classic
album from the woman of many talents".

Also of interest, on Wednesday 29th January, Tori Amos will be performing
her new single "China" on the Wogan show, BBC 1 7.00pm.

Neil
