>Cooperman and Orlake, but then again perhaps not.

This is usually the case.  Most of the Awesome Records pressings of old
Danielle Dax vinyl have "a porky prime cut" etched in the run-out groove.
Porky is the name of the guy who cuts the discs.  Apparently he had a feud
with another well-known record cutter, so that when they did a bad job,
they'd each put the other's name in the groove!  (ie: if Cutter #2 whose
name eludes me did a bad job, he'd put "a porky prime cut" in the groove.
Clear?)
