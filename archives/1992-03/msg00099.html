Aug. 7, as the Japanese set translation says, here birth certificate is 
reprinted in KATE BUSH The illustrated documentary.  
        Under Sept 7,

(though he showed her where middle C was) but was the "encouraging force." 
        Also, it talks about her Violin lessions, but doesn't say that she 
didn't really like it.
