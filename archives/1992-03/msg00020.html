Jackie Zucconi (lionhart@netlink.cts.com) posted a list of the messages
inscribed on the various KT singles and made a query as to what any of them
might mean. I have an idle speculation about the "Sat in Your Lap" single,
which message states:
                     "Well done J.B. 1st Dan"
As KT seems to have some predilection for the Martial Arts (doesn't she
wear a Bushido uniform or some such item in one of her videos?), I would
have to guess that her brother John Carder passed his Tae-Kwon-Doh test!
Dans usually signify levels of achievement in various martial arts.
