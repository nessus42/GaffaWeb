After hearing about the backwards masking on "Leave it Open" from The
Dreaming, I dragged out my album copy of the album and proceded to
mutilate my phonograph.  Sure enough, this is what I heard....

	"They said they were very ^%#*GBVYA"

"They said they were buried here".  Does anyone else have any ideas as
to what it might be?
