Anthony Philips was one of the original five members of Genesis.  (I
suppose you've heard of THEM).  The other four were Peter Gabriel,
Tony Banks, Mike Rutherford and a drummer whose name I can't remember.
Phil Collins and Steve Hackett joined (I think this is right) the
group for the third album, when Philips and the drummer left.

There are several Philips albums available on this side of the ocean
and I assume there are more on the other.

I could be more specific but I don't have my collection on
hand...maybe I'll provide more details later.
