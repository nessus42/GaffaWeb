Subject: Kate video profile on USA cable(again...)

For the recent Kate converts in this group, USA cable network will be
showing a video profile of Kate next Saturday night Feb 13 on Night
Flight.  My local newspaper listing shows that it will probably be
shown during the last hour of the show (2-3AM Pacific and again at
6-7AM Sun morn).

This profile will most likely be a repeat of the 1985 profile which
contained "Wuthering Heights", "Babooshka", "Suspended in Gaffa" and
"Cloudbusting" as well as clips from an interview done during her 1985
U.S promotional visit.
