kate bush's new album

From: Niels P. Mayer <mayer%hplabsz@hplabs.HP.COM>
Date: Wed, 13 Jan 88 00:00:40 PST
Subject: kate bush's new album

Inside sources say that the new Kate Bush album will be called
"The Lick Inside" it will feature the cuts
-- the lick inside
-- A little more to the left, Mr. Daedalus
-- the darting tongue of love
-- face in your lap
-- night of the swallow
-- the man with the bimbo in his face
-- Kunnilingus from Bangcok
-- There goes a tremor
-- L'amour tastes and stenches something like you

This will certainly be one of her finest albums, what with a melange
of something with a precocious ambiance, the sheer presence of that
certain je ne sais quoi, and a penetrating weltanschau. This opus is a
stimulasting libidinal journey into the inner core of Ms. Bush's very
essence.  A must buy for the real kate bush fan; collectors will
cherish the velcro tear-away velveeta squirting 3D foldout cover done
in the neopaleocubist-deconstructivatavist style by Paddy Bush's pet
poodle "Frenchy".
