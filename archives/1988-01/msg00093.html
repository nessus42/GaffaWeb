Musings on Tour of Life II

From: IED0DXM@OAC.UCLA.EDU
Date: Tue, 16 Feb 88 10:53 PST
Subject: Musings on Tour of Life II

Looks as though some Love-Hounds were very enthusiastic about the idea
of going to England for the next Kate Bush tour. IED will keep their
names in mind, with thanks for their expressions of interest. If Kate
should ever actually announce _plans_ for a tour, you will read a
great deal more on the subject of travel in future IED postings. _If_
there ever is a tour, and if it does _not_ include U.S. dates, then
you can be very sure that the possibility of a group visit to the U.K.
will be thoroughly investigated. The chances are, however, that when
the next tour finally does materialize, there will be at least a
couple of U.S. stops, which would probably reduce the demand for a
group visit to England. Anyway, just don't hold your breath.

-- Andrew Marvick
