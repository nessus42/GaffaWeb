Attention all Stranglers fans: There's a new 12" single out (new to
San Francisco at least) called _All Day and All of the Night_ and yes,
it is a cover of ye olde Kinks' tune.  The flip side has "!Viva Vlad!"
which I believe is part 5 of the Vladimir saga, and a live recording
of "Who Wants the World?"  Pretty funny cover, complete with the horn
section they've been using for the last few albums.
