> Dave Rubin

Oldfield is indeed a genius, however, I feel he has been slipping up
BIG TIME.  Take Five Miles Out - a brilliant album if ever there was
one.  Then Crises, well, it's OK, but the song with Jon Anderson was
pretty lame.  Next, you have Discovery.  This album ROTS!  I like two
of the songs on it, and the instrumental piece but the rest is fluff!
What's happening to the man?  His gritty instrumentals have been
replaced by syrupy goop!  He put out a single after Discovery called
Shine and again featuring Jon Anderson.  More lightweight garbage.  I
haven't heard "Islands" yet, although my friend Dan has.  He says it's
pretty lightweight and insipid.  I wish Oldfield would get in gear and
throw these fluffy pop songs away!!  If you don't know, he's got
*gasp* BONNIE TYLER singing on "Islands"!!!  EEEEYUK!

And let me second :>oug's nomination of Sinead O'Connor.  She is really
cool.  Jumps right out of the radio.
