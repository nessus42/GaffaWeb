First of all, about Mike Oldfield.  His new single "Islands" (sung by
Bonnie Tyler) isn't that great, but there is a strange re-working of
the song on the B-side with a different (far more Kate or Maggie-like)
vocalist and it is much better.

Secondly, you're right.  Kate _defines_ god.

Thirdly, whoever included lyrics to "December": thank you very much!

night.  Right as Kate is singing the initial "Hello, Earth!  Hello,
Earth", during each of the "th" sounds, a very quiet male voice comes
on (sounding like its being projected from space) and says something.
Anyone have any clues as to what it is.  (If you want to hear this,
you will have to crank it up!)

specifically is meant by "Gaffa"?
