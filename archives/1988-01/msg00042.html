Robyn Somerville is publishing again

From: brandenberg@star.dec.com (Injustice is relatively easy to bear; what stings is justice)
Date: 4 Dec 87 18:51:46 GMT
Subject: Robyn Somerville is publishing again
Organization: Digital Equipment Corporation

	For those who haven't yet received the letter, Robyn
	Somerville (nee Carstairs?) of "Breakthrough" fame is again
	publishing a Kate Bush magazine.  The new magazine, "Still
	Breathing", is published in volumes, three issues to the
	volume.  Subscription price is $9.00 (Canadian, American, and
	overseas).  She requests that payment be in the form of a
	money order (postal money order for Americans) payable to T.R.
	Somerville.  The address is:

			Still Breating
			11588-72nd Avenue
			Delta, BC  V4E-1Z1
				CANADA

	She would also like to receive any material any reader might
	wish to contribute.

					- Monty
