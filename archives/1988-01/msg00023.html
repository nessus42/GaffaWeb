I believe that the album, 'Paint Your Wagon' was released at the end of
1986.  Note that the CD contains the first two singles. There is
another album 'Talk About The Weather' and an EP or two on Red Rhino.
Lately, RLYL has released an EP entitled 'Crawling Mantra' for
Homestead under the name of 'The Lorries' and a new 12" single called
'Open Up' on Situation Two.
