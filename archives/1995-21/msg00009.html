Well, I'm sticking to my guns on this one. Admittedly my LD player 
(an old Pioneer 1450) does have a very bright sound, much brighter 
than my CD player, and my speakers have metal dome tweeters, both 
of which factors may be exacerbating the problem, but all the same
the hiss is there. It is actually the hiss on the master tapes of the
songs that I meant - it fades out between the songs.  

> I have had a problem with this disc though, I am on my third copy

> I have the service manual and am intending to make sure the tracking

It does sound as though your player may have a problem. My first 
LaserVision player, one of the old Philips top-loaders, had problems
playing warped discs, but neither of the digital machines I've had 
since have ever had any trouble, even when playing discs that the 
Philips player couldn't.
