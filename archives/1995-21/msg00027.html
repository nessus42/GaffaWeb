Daer All,

I was wathcing Alexi Sayle last Friday (30th June) and I could have sworn I saw Kate. I know that Richard Frost of Australia had spotted her on a previous show and I'm pretty damned sure I saw her on this one. 

The section where I thought I'd seen her was the 'Bobby Chariott' spot. Booby 
Chariott had just made a rude joke (I can't remember the exact joke but it
involved touching Kate's bottom.) Anyway two rows in front of he was standing
was an extemely convincing Kate lookalike. I ususally tape this show as I'm often out on Friday nights but this time I forgot so I couldn't check for sure.
