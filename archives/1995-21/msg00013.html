I was at a local hotel earlier today, playing a wedding gig.
Pulled into the back parking lot, and came face to face with
the Fruitopia bus! It was a regular bus, painted with, you
guessed it, all kinds of fruit. It also had hubcaps fastened
to the side and the word "Fruitopia" in black 3-D.

I have no idea what it was doing in a hotel parking lot in
Birmingham, Michigan, but let's just say I look upon it as
destiny!

BTW, it put the Partridge Family bus to shame!  ;-)
