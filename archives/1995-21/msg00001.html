The Fruitopia ad described as seen on TV in Europe with predominantly 
yellow fruit (lemons, etc) is probably the ad titled "Nice" which indeed 
has music by Kate.  

None of the ads have a great deal of vocalization in them, the only 
identifiable words Kate sings are "Hey hey, juice", which has turned up 
in more than one Fruitopia ad now.  Apparently they have a limited amount 
of music from Kate and are re-using clips with new ads.

Kate's voice is clearly identifiable in at least three of the ads, and 
her unique piano style in another.

The lemony "Nice" ad is somewhat different from the others musically, but 
is Kate.

IED had detailed descriptions of each ad that he posted earlier in the 
year.  I wonder if this might be a good time to post them again for the 
benefit of those who are just now seeing them.
