   IED is sending this blind, since he hasn't received a Digest
in a solid week now, but working on the assumption that other people
are receiving Love-Hounds, here goes:
   IED has seen two different import bootleg CDs, both of which
contain nothing more than stereo dubs (presumably from the laser-disk)
of Kate Bush Live at Hammersmith Odeon.  One is (IED believes) a
German disk, called Super Golden Radio Hits No. 013.  The other is
an Italian product, called Feel It Live.  This brings compact disk
versions of the Hammersmith soundtrack to a total of four, including
the original (and best, since it included bonus tracks) Kate Bush Live
and the somewhat cruder dub, Burning Desire.

-- Andrew Marvick
