For those who can't FTP it, there are now three options cooking for
distributing Ron's Cloudbusting:

1) I'll be breaking it into 100k chunks and mailing it to anyone who asks. 
My current distribution list is:

larry@csccat.cs.com
sgihbtn!lipp%snomas.@uunet.UU.NET
shaun@iris.mincom.oz.au
craig@sco.COM

I'll wait a few more days to get latecomers.  (Sorry, earlybirds.)

2) There's some IBM format diskettes making the rounds by USMail, that Ron
sent me and I sent on to Tippi Chai (alias "Martin R. Lamb"
<martinn@csri.toronto.edu>).  I asked Tippi to forward them to Michael
Scott of Borland who had asked for them, but Michael we haven't been able
to get email to you.  Anybody who'd like to be involved in that path, and
promises not to break the chain (!!!), please write Tippi. (Hope that's
okay, Tippi?)

3)  I want to start posting parts, at least, to r.m.g., a song or two at a
time, along with lyrics, and invite everyone to add their 2 cents of
interpretations etc.  I picture people grabbing the bits they like and
compiling their own customized Kate-reference. Anybody have any problems/
suggestions on this?
