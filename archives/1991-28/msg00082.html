Thanks to Al for posting this info. I notice that Push have a small advert
in the Sept. issue of Q. Spotting this reminded me of an advert in the
August Q. from a company called The Concert Photo Co. who advertised
colour photos of a variety of artists from All ABout Eve to Frank Zappa.
Kate Bush is included in the list.

They state "a 20-photo collection is #9-60, #17-80 for 40 photos, or
just #33-40 fro a tremendous 80-photo collection. All on the band/Artiste
of your choice (p&p inclusive)."

"Photo size 5" X 3.5" , delivery 10-21 days)"

"Cheques/POs to: The Concert Photo Co. (Dept. Q)
 PO Box 828,
 Buckingham
 MK18 5YX
"

I have no other details and no idea whether these are a rip-off. Do they
really have 80 different (good quality) photos of Kate Live?? I may
write and see if they have a catalogue or something.

Back in the Sept. Q, there is also a small ad which states:

"Kate Bush - Never Forever 15, #1-50. News, reviews, TV appearances 78-90,
artwork, letters and much more from N. Williams, 28 Millbrook Street,
Plasmarl, Swansea SA6 8JY"

This is what used to be called "Cariad Kate". I don't remember seeing
them advertise in Q before.
