Well, since I havn't seen any messages for two days, I must assume that you
have all disappeared suddenly.  I think I understand how this happened.  I
remember reading the story The Nine Billion Names Of God, where the entire
universe blinked out after it had achieved it's purpose - printing out all the
possible names of God.  Recently all (er, actually almost all) of Kate's
Quotes have been gathered in one place (cloudbusting) and then you all
disappear.  Coinicidence?? I think not!!! So anyway, I have disided that the
purpose of Kate fans must have been to gather everything she said, and that
having been accomplished, we must all now disappear.  I don't know why I am
the last to go, but so be it.  Poor Kate.  Nobody will probably notice that
we're all gone until the album/tour and then when they happen, nobody will buy
the album or zVgo to the show!! What a drag!!
