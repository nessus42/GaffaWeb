I just picked up our copy of the Canadian release of The Single File 
video.  It's definitely the real item, "Made in Canada. Distributed under 
license by Capitol Records-EMI of Canada."  The cover appears to be the 
same as the original release, with a picture of Kate in the _Babooshka_ 
costume.  For those who have wondered, a note on the cover says that the 
"costume is based on an illustration by Chris Achilleos and realized by 
Pamela Keats".  

If you can't find the video locally and don't want to wait for the rumored 
U.S. release, Aardvark Video here in Columbus is stocking it and can
handle mail order.  We paid $23.99 plus tax.  Aardvark's phone number is
(614)461-6302.  We have no affiliation with Aardvark Video, other than as 
satisfied customers.  They have successfully located a couple of rare 
videos for us.  They are still looking for The Single File on Laserdisc.
