10 miles north of Morristown on I-287.  It is easily accessible by public
transportation -- we'll send details as needed.  And, IT'S VERY CLOSE TO
NEW YORK!!!  :)

from afar!

registered two resounding "yes!" votes. :)
 
Don't let another KaTemas pass you by -- celebrate with the pros!  :)
