Hello Everyone,

I'd like to thank Bill and Jessica (our humble love-hounds and Ecto pseudo-
moderators, respectively) for hosting a great Katemas in San Francisco.

The turnout was good (about 15 people) and the weather was hot for SF (in the
80's).  Highlights included:

Viewing the Sensual World laser disk on a cinema-wide television.

A new tape of rarieties that included Fruitopia commercials.

A big ham and lots of the sickly sweet Fruitopia.

The laser disk of TLTCTC.

A cut-throat game of KT Jeopardy!, complete with Single, Double and Final
Jeopardy! sections, writted by IED.  As usual the ringer, Stev0, had to be
carefully assigned to avoid imbalancing the game.  :)

An appearance by Courtney Love (or her double!)

A bunch of nifty prizes.
