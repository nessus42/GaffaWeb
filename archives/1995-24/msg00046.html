Subject: GREY LADY DOWN & JUMP- MARQUEE 28/7/95
Organization: The Curtain Shop
X-News-Software: Ameol

This Friday (28/7/95) Grey Lady Down and Jump will be performing
at the Marquee Club, Tottenham Court Road, London.

These two groups have been for some time widely regarded as being 
at the forefront of the new generation of British Prog Rock bands 
and this should be an evening to relish.

As any member of this conference will be painfully aware, Prog does 
not get anywhere near the exposure it rightly deserves, and this gig 
must be viewed as playing a major part in raising awareness of the 
wealth of neglected talent which abounds worldwide.

Major record companies will be attending this gig, and there is no 
doudbt that the signing of another Prog band to a high profile 
company will only serve to open the way for more underrated groups 
to emerge from obscurity. However, as you are probably aware, 
corporate policy dictates that only bands with an already large 
following stand a cats hope of being succesful.

It is for this reason alone that I have broken a personal rule by 
advertising this gig in the hope that you may be tempted to make 
the trip down to London to attend.

I can promise you an exceptional showcase of top notch musicianship, 
writing and performance guarenteed to please anybody into Prog.

For more information on either band, tour dates, CD availability or 
entry onto mailing lists please mail me at the below address.

Thank you for understanding the motivation behing this unashamed 
piece of advertising,
