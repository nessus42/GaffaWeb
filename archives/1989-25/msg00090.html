Actually, _La_Gadra_Lazza_ ("The Theiving Magpie") has been out on CD
since about last April. If you do buy it , purchase the CD for it in-
cludes a full-length version of Misplaced Childhood (I didn't know
that they could do the whole thing live, but I guess they did..).
It really isn't anything new - no major changes in any of the songs,
but it is an impressive collection and the playing was excellent on
all tracks.

As for Fish (his name was revealed after leaving the band), last time
I heard, there were rumors of him singing for the current Yes (as op-
possed to ABWH), but I haven't heard that repeated since last May,
so it probably was just a big rumor to scare people.

Solo album? No news.
