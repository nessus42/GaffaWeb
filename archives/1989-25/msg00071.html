     Hi, everyone. IED has returned to the pacK after a ten-day
stint in earthquake land.
     He finds more than five thousand lines of Love-Hounds screed
awaiting him. It will therefore be a couple of days before he
is ready to comment on discussions held during his absence.
     Meanwhile, however, IED would like to post the following
information about Vickie Mapes's radio programme, "Suspended in Gaffa".
As an example of what Kansas City-area listeners can expect to find
on this programme, here is the playlist of the October 21 edition:

  1. KATE BUSH        "Suspended in Gaffa" (_The_Dreaming_)
  2. A. C. MARIAS     "Just Talk" (_One_of_Our_Girls_is_Gone_Missing_)
  3. COCTEAU TWINS    "How to Bring a Blush to the Snow" (_Victorialand_)
  4. ENYA             "Smaotim" (_Orinoco_Flow_--CD-single)
  5. ELEANOR          "Vahala Na" (_Jungle_Wave_)
  6. SHELLEAN ORPHAN  "Anatomy of Love" (_Helleborine_)
  7. REBECCA JENKINS  "Through the Leaves" (_Legacy_) *Siberry BV-singer*
  8. ANNE BOURNE      "Blue Ballet" (_Legacy_) *Siberry keyboardist*
  9. LILLIE PALMER    "Insanity Street" (_Legacy_)
 10. HAPPY RHODES     "Oh the Drears" (_Rhodes,_Volume_1_)
 11. HAPPY RHODES     "Given In" (_Rhodes,_Volume_1_)
 12. HAPPY RHODES     "Let Me Know Love (_Rhodes,_Volume_2_)
 13. HAPPY RHODES     "The Perfect Irony" (_Rearmament_)
 14. ECTO             "Project 499" (_Ecto_) *Admitted KT fans*
 15. ECTO             "I Won't Break Down" (_Ecto_)
 16. LAURIE ANDERSON  "New York Social Life" (_New_Music_for_Elect_...)
 17. LAURIE ANDERSON  "Coolsville" (_Strange_Angels_)
 18. MICHELLE SHOCKED "Don't You Mess Around With My Little Sister"
                       (_Texas_Campfire_Tapes_)

 20. JANE SIBERRY     "Above the Tree Line" (_Jane_Siberry_)
 21. JANE SIBERRY     "Half Angel, Half Eagle" (_Bound_by_the_Beauty_)
 22. TRIO BULGARKA    "Sountseto trepti Zauda" (_The_Forest_is_Crying_)
                       *This is the track Kate sang with them on UK TV*
 23. Excerpt from UK TV interview with Kate and the Trio Bulgarka
 24. KATE BUSH        "Rocket's Tail" (_The_Sensual_World_)
 25. KATE BUSH        "The Sensual World" (_The_Sensual_World_)

     As Love-Hounds will see, Vickie takes great care in preparing
her playlists, and her choices are always fascinating and educational.
It's on Saturdays from 10 to midnight on KKFI, 90.1 FM.--Recommended!
