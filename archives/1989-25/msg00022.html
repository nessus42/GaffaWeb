Hello there fellow Love-Hounds--
  Just thought I would throw in my two cents' worth again.  Yesterday while
cruising between the tables at a local record convention in beautiful
downtown Albany, NY, I was lucky enough to snag a promo CD single of
"Love and Anger" (it set me back $20, but I'm a die-hard Kate fan, not to
mention spontaneous and compulsive).  The second tidbit I found was one of
the humongous 3' x 5' black-and-white posters for _The Sensual World_.  This
only cost me $12; for the size and overall appeal of the poster, I thought
it was worth the price.  One item I DID see and unfortunately didn't have the
dough to buy was the CD of _Kate Bush Live_ (basically the Hammersmith Odeon
show).  This had a nice cover, with 4 more unusual pictures of Kate on the
front, and the picture from the Hammersmith video cover on the back.  There
were three additonal songs -- live versions of _Breathing_ and _RUTH_, and
the studio version of _This Woman's Work_.  The CD was priced at $30.  Ah,
if only I'd had the cash....

                        Enough for now.

                                                 All the best,
                                                      Deb Wentorf
