Subject: The Oct. 28 issue of _Billboard_.


The local public library got the Oct. 28 issue of _Billboard_ today.
I wanted to buy my own copy, but the stores still have the Oct. 21
issue.  I think the new issue will be in magazine stands tomorrow.

Anyway, the Oct. 28 issue has a full page ad for the new album, as
well as a very nice review.  It also shows "Love And Anger" entering
the Modern Rock Tracks chart at number 13.

I'll post the details when I get my own copy, probably tomorrow.
(Assuming nobody else beats me to it.)
