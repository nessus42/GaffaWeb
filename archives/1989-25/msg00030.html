Hello, again.  I would have posted this last week but the 'quake and
an operating system upgrade have kept me locked out until now.

Tuesday (pre-quake, of course), I hopped on over to the record stores
south of campus to pick up my official copy of TSW.  I was disappointed
to find NO promotion, NO prominently-displayed Kate stuff, and generally
no hype at all about the blessed event.  (Maybe that's why She sent
an earthquake our way :-)   I asked in Tower about the situation.
"Why don't you guys have Kate displayed prominently?"  He laughed.
He said they didn't need to.  They were going to sell out, anyway.
Sure enough, after he said that, I began noticing that everyone seemed
to be buying one.

The bad part was that I couldn't listen to it until I got home, and
then the power was out!

Are there ANY other Love-Hounds at Berkeley?  The East Bay (except
Livermore)?   p.s.  Our library did NOT burn down.  Bogus rumors.
