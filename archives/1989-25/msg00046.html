So, how does The Sensual World stack up with the rest of Kate's
albums?  I guess I'd have to rank her albums like this:

     1.  The Dreaming
     2.  The Hounds Of Love  (remove the first 3 songs & it would be #1!)

Magic Again"?  It's available with the Experiment IV 12" single.
