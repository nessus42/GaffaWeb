autobiography. Describing an evening in the cookhouse of an Army camp in
Northern Ireland:

	were on the same TOTP [Top of the Pops]. Debbie Harry was singing
	"Denis" and Kate Bush was doing "Wuthering Heights". When Kate Bush
	came on the whole rifle company used to shout, "Burn the witch!".
