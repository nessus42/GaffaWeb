Hi everyone.

Isn’t it wonderful every time you find a new Kate Bush record at a 
local swap meet! As when I recently found the CD "Wow", which claimed 
to be a 1979 Amsterdam live recording. Printed in Italy (GDR CD 9213) 
and a sleeve with some quite interesting spelling (Oh England, My 
Linearity, etc. )  :-)

But how disappointed aren’t you when you find out that this wasn't any 
Amsterdam recording - but just another version of the Hammersmith 
concert. After a verification at the Gaffa-web, where other prints of 
"Wow" have been discussed, I just say: fooled again! So please be 
warned, if you ever find this tempting CD "Wow" :-(

But I know what would make me really happy again. A copy of the 
Lovehounds and Katetopia videos! So please, if anyone is willing to 
make a PAL copy of these masterpieces, send me a mail a s a p!  :-)

Thanks
