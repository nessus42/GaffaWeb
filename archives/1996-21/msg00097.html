"Aspects of the Sensual World" is a US only CD Single release.  It is
not included in the "This Woman's Work" Box Set which is  released from
the UK and Japan.  Besides, the tracks on the AofSW CD Single are 
available in the Box Set, the only missing track is "The Sensual World"
(Instrumental).

BTW, Track Listing for AofSW are The Sensual World, Be Kind to My
Mistakes, I'm Still Waiting, Ken, The Sensual World (Instrumental).
