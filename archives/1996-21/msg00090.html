for completeness sake, here is the full details on loreena's plans for
live performances straight from the horse's mouth.

>From: Karen Shook <karen@quinlan.demon.co.uk> 
>Subject: Quinlan Road tour update
>
>Dear Old Ways listmembers,
>
>Thank you to those listmembers who have posted information about dates. If
>we do 
>not update you first it is because we are waiting until all details are 
>confirmed, to make it more convenient and informative for you.
>
>I'm absolutely certain I will have to repeat all this eighty times (then again, 
>it shows that people are interested!) but I hope you will try to read the 
>following information carefully to save wear and tear on my typing fingers.
>
>
>                   Loreena McKennitt tour dates 1996
>
>1. Loreena is not doing a "tour" per se. She is working on a new album and she 
>will do a full tour -  yes, probably including your town or somewhere close
>- in 
>1997 when the new album comes out.
>
>2. She is doing four (or possibly five) dates this year, accompanied by her 
>band, in the month of July. I have no reason to think that there will be any 
>other dates or that she will suddenly play in Dallas, or Dublin, or Dusseldorf.
>If she does, I will put it on the website. I do not think it is likely that she
>will.
>
>3. Here are the dates. Please check your local papers for details of tickets 
>and showtimes and venue address. I cannot, unfortunately, give you exhaustive 
>information about every aspect of the dates. I do not,  for example, have 
>detailed information regarding the street address of your nearest ticket sales 
>office or the like.
>
>
>JULY 1 - MONTREAL, QUEBEC, CANADA - Place Des Arts capacity 2,900
>         This appearance is part of the Montreal Jazz Festival. 
>
>JULY 2 - POSSIBLE SECOND DATE, Montreal, Quebec, Canada - Place Des Arts
>         This possible second date is as yet unannounced and unconfirmed.
>
>JULY 6 - ISTANBUL, TURKEY - Open Air Theatre, Harbiye, Istanbul cap. 3,950
>         This appearance is part of the Istanbul Jazz Festival
>
>JULY 9 - ATHENS, GREECE - Lykabetus Theatre, Athens capacity 4,000
>         This show is being promoted by local agent RealMusic
>
>JULY 27 - SPILIMBERGO, ITALY - Folkest '96
>         Loreena's appearance is part of this well-known folk/diverse music
>         festival. It is an outdoor appearance.
>
>JULY 28 - CATANIA, SICILY, ITALY - Chiostro Instituto A. Gioeni - capacity 6,000
>         Loreena is appearing as part of the Estate Catanese week-long arts 
>         festival. Tickets 20,000 and 25,000 lire. Promoter is Modena 
>         International Music. 
>
>4. Those *were* the dates. I do not expect that there will be any other dates 
>this year. Please remember that I try to answer your correspondence promptly
>but 
>I receive between 30 and 50 e-mail queries a day, and that's only a tiny bit of 
>what I have to do in a day. Once again: these are the only dates this year. 
>Please check local listings for further details if you are considering
>attending 
>any of these concerts.
>
>Regards,
>Karen Shook
