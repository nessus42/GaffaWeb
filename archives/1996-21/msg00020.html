I have bought my version (UK LP) for 100$ in Toronto, but I have seen it for
320$ in a record shop in London. I think the price only depends on how
willing are the possible customers to pay the price they want. 

: I'm also wondering if it is difficult to find the box set (so I should buy

booklet is the same as in the CD Box, but it is much bigger, and therefore,
are the pictures are much better!
