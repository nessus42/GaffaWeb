It's amazing to me how hard it seems it was for a lot of people to get 
into "The Dreaming." My first Kate album was "The Whole Story," which I 
loved so much that I began collecting all her albums -- first The Red 
Shoes, The Sensual World, and Hounds of Love, because they were her 
newest. Well, aside from the singles from those albums, I was initially 
disappointed. I decided not to give up and bought The Kick Inside and 
Lionheart, and was even more disappointed. Finally, I decided to give 
The Dreaming a try, bought it, and on first listen knew it was one of 
the best albums I had ever heard. I loved it so much that I went back 
and gave the other albums a try (and eventually picked up Never for Ever 
as well), and grew to love them all. But The Dreaming remains the only 
Kate album that I instantly loved.

Patrick
