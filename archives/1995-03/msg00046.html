Hi Barbara and everyone else,

I've got this box-set.  It contains an English import CD of Never Forever, a 
white t-shirt with the words Kate Bush/Never Forever in printed in a nice font 
over the front right of the shirt, a book of Gerard Mankiewitz photos from the 
early eighties, and a promotional poster for "Babooshka" which has Kate dresses 
up like a valkyrie.  

Overall, I think its an interesting package.  The poster is fun and I wear the 
t-shirt regularly.  Looking at the photos in the book its easy to understand why 
many of them were never used; they just don't come off too well. But still, its 
an off-beat collection of visual Kate history.  

I would recommend it for completists/fanatics.

Have fun,
