Well, Happy herself says that she developed the higher range of her voice 
by singing along with Kate, so there's certainly some voice imitation; 
however, Kate does not have Happy's incredible range and has nothing like 
Happy's amazing lower voice.

There are lots of echos of Kate in Happy's musical style, but there's 
just as many echoes of Happy's other musical loves, and Happy's style 
ranges a lot, and certainly goes her own way--beyond any echoes that might 
be in her work.
