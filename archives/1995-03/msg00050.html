> I'm wondering if anybody can help me in obtaining the following Kate
> duets:
> 
> with Roy Harper   You (the Game, Part II), Once

"You" is currently available on a low-price compilation CD "An
Introduction To Roy Harper" (Science Friction HUCD017).

"Once" is available on the album of the same name (Science Friction
HUCD011). It's not actually a duet, Kate sings only backing vocals,
but it's still a very nice song.

Both CDs are in print. If you can't find them from any shops, try
writing directly to the label (include IRC):
