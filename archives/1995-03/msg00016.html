I recently purchased a CD titled _Smile_ by Julia Migenes. She is an opera
singer but this is one of her "pop" recordings. It is a concept album in
that all of the songs are from movies. And guess what, she does "Brazil".
It's not too surprising because the album was produced and arranged by
Michael Kamen who did the music for the film. The liner notes even mention
that he worked with Kate on her album _Hounds Of Love_.

The CD is made in Germany: ERATO 4509-96385-2
