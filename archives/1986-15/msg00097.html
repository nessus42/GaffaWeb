new Cocteau Twins will feature Harold Budd

From: rosen@ucbarpa.Berkeley.EDU (Rob Rosen)
Date: Mon, 20 Oct 86 10:00:36 PDT
Subject: new Cocteau Twins will feature Harold Budd

	...this from the September issue of "the Music Technology
Magazine," a new joint British-Californian venture.  Budd joined up
with them after discovering that CT were going to do a cover of 'Not
Yet Remembered'; he met up with them in L.A. when they were touring
last November, and then flew to London to work with them at Robin's
studio.  The release date was not mentioned in the article, probably
because the vocals had not yet been recorded (apparently Elisabeth
Fraser takes the instrumental tracks home and then composes the words
and melodies).

	Should be interesting.
