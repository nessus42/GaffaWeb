Subject: How do I get Kates singles set
Organization: University of Nevada ** Reno

I was told by Doug Alan that there is a boxed set of all of Kate's singles 
available at most records stores.  I went to my local store (the best in
the town where I live) and the manager told me that "that" was something
that only people on the east coast could get...hogwash! Someone please tell
me how that I can get this boxed set.  OR does someone know of a mail-
order record store that one can get basically anything still in print?
I would really be interested in a place if it exists.

He also told me (I think to passify me...hmmm?) that Kate was coming out
with a Best of record sometime in October...is there any truth to that? 
And if so...what are Kate's best?  I enjoy all...but what are really 
classified as her "hits."  

By the way, whoever it is that first mentioned the Cocteau Twins...I would
just like to thank them from the bottom of my ears.  I really enjoy 
what I have heard so far and look forward to buying more of their records in
the future.  Could someone suggest some other group...english or other that
records stuff that is different and very enlightening as compared to the 
pop stuff on the radio...I would be enthusiastic for suggestions?

Stranded out here on the west coast in little ole Nevada (gambling just
doesn't cut it...but Kate makes it sound better)
