Just a couple of warnings from a frequent buyer of bootlegs.

1) The recently issued KT bootleg (with the black, white, and pink cover,
   fuzzy photo of KT) is apparently a reissue of part of the Hammersmith
   Odeon video soundtrack, all of these tracks and more are on the first
   disc of the WOW LIVE double, so don't buy the new single disc issue if
   you've alreaddy got WOW.

2) The new 10-record Pink Floyd box is a reissue of:

   - VIOLENCE IN BIRMINGHAM (2 discs)
   - THE GREAT GIG IN THE SKY (2 discs)
   - IVOR WYNNE (2 discs)
   - WALLIVE (2 discs)

   and two discs of early BBC recordings (1967-69). The two BBC discs may
   have already come out as a separate double boot, or if not, they may in
   the near future.
