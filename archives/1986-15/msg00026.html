Herro,

I apologize to Doug for calling Kate a 'tart' at the net.party.  I
do not dislike her music nearly as much as I make out, however, I *do*
feel it is rather mediocre.  I feel Cyndi Lauper is much more talented
and I can relate much better to her music.  I know people out there will
critisize me for enjoying Cyndi.  To them I say, what right have you to
say that just because something is popular, it has no merit?

I agree, David Bowie (nee Davey Jones) has REALLY slipped downhill.  I
still enjoy hearing his voice, and I am sure he is much happier these
days, however, his music now fails to stir me.  His album "The Rise and
Fall of Ziggy Cyber and the Spiders from Mars" is, in my opinion, one of
the best composed and most incredibly beautiful albums in history.
His shit with Mick Jagger (mick gacker) is so different, so white bread,
that it is hard to believe the same performer did the two songs.

Salamir
