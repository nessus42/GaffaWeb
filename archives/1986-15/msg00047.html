with some words on it.  Even worse because Warner Bros. stuck one of
those huge unremovable stickers with the song titles on it, since

faces on it.  
DANIELLE DAX, new 12".  Really great cover.  Her covers always are,
though.  I did listen to this once and it was not that exciting.
I'd love to know who does her makeup.
