Subject: Venturing Into The World Of Love Hounds



       Greetings, everyone. I, a traveler from the world of mainstram rock

music (i.e. Pink Floyd, Led Zepplin, Yes, Stevie Nicks, Rush), have been

observing this newsgroup for the past few months and have finally decided

to venture into the realm of the Love-Hounds.

      So I bought "The Dreaming" around the first of last month. Now I'll

admit, when I first popped the cassette into the stereo and listened, I

thought it was a mite peculiar. But after I'd listened to the album a few

times, I found myself thinking, "Wow! This stuff is great! Why didn't I get

this before?"

     So now I play the cassette almost every day. And as you would expect

from a first-time Kate-listener, I've a few questions. (These have probably

been answered before, but as I said I'm new here). 1) What does the aborigine

say? 2) Exactly where does "The Dreaming" end and "Night of the Swallow" begin?

3) What or where is Gaffa? 4) Why is knowledge "something sat in your lap"?



Kate-land is a wonderous place indeed.....
