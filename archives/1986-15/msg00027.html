  Someone suggested a poll to see which album people preferred, "HoL" or "TD".
I much prefer "HoL" though I like "Sat in Your Lap" more than anything on "HoL".
On the other hand, I think "The Kick Inside" is at least an order of magnitude
more interesting.  How refreshing to hear someone who clearly sings in a
register that even Michael Jackson can't reach.
 
  As her voice has gone down, so has her music.
 
  Bob Kelner
