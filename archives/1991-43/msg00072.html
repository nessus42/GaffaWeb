Was just listening this morning to a local (Portland) non-commercial station (KBOO), 
they have Friday morning program called "Instant Breakfast" which is a pretty decent
sampler of alternative-female-vocal music.  Anyway, the host/DJ played "Rocket Man"
and "Rocket's Tail" back to back -- seems obvious in hindsight.  As I listened, I 
found myself thinking, "This kinda works, an album or EP like this would be fun."  I 
just can't think of any other songs to go on it!  As for a title, hmmm, I don't know,
maybe _R is for Rocket_? 

(Apologies to Ray Bradbury.  8-)
