I don't disagree that psychological pressure is a different matter.  It most
certainly is.

However, you overlook one crucial distinction - I control my psyche through
the choices *I* make.  I have never bought the argument that words/thoughts/
ideas/psychological manipulation are in the same class as physical restraint.

Yes, sociological forces are strong and compelling, and yes there have been
changes in our social milieu that must be acknowledged and dealt with
from a broad-minded and open perspective if we hope to grow and adapt
in this brave-new-world of mass communication and develop the potentials
that are the flip-side of the "cow-mentaility" that leads to the psycho-
logical manipulations to which you refer.  This short-sighted identification
of these issues with the physical restraint of individual choice is, however,
IMHO, *much* more dangerous than the pitiful lack of awareness and maturity
that leads to acceptance of said manipulation and the mindless-mooing to
the pied-piper tune of our pseudo-intellectuals and compound(:-) idiots
that advocate these so-called progressive concepts.

With all due respect, how old are you anyway?
