> wanted to say that I thoroughly agree with Ben: putting labels
> on music is *not* censorship (although it may be many other things;
> prudish and bone-headed spring immediately to mind), and the word
> should indeed be reserved for genuine cases.

The problem with the "don't call it censorship, it's just stupid
behavior" attitude is that it obscures the fact that one aim of
putting labels on music is to make certain kinds of music harder
to get and, if possible, cease to be produced at all.  That's
why we have the phrase "chill factor".  Pridish and bone-headed
just doesn't cover it.
