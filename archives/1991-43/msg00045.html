   Just a word on "Rocket Man".  The point, musically speaking, as this fan
sees it, is not that it has a "reggae" beat, but that it is the _first_
recording _ever_made_ which merges reggae with Irish folk music and Elton
John (not to mention Kate Bush, which is the clincher, of course).  It's
another flawless masterpiece, whether you all can find it in your hearts
to acknowledge that or not. The truth is, it's the sum of its ingredients
and much, much more.  Feel it.
