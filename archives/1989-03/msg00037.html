"unprofessional" and sticking out like a sore thumb.  This, they said,

Usenet to examine the newgroup in more detail.  When they then noticed

and shutdown Usenet altogther.

because of the mailing list name "Love-Hounds", but I guess they are
less picky than the people who pay for Usenet.
