	Yeah, Discipline is one of my *all time* favorites.  I also like
USA a lot just for the raw energy in it.

	There was some talk about the reformation of *another* King Crimson
containing Fripp, Tony Levin, Jerry Marotta, and other surprise guests,
but it seems that schedules or other conflicts have at least temporarily
put that to rest.  Fripp's latest band per se was a kind of informal thing
called "Fripp-Fripp", with Robert Fripp, his wife <oh hell I can't remember
her name>, Trey Gunn on the stick, and I forget who else on drums.  Trey
is one of the "League of Crafty Guitarists",  a group of "orchestra-like"
guitarists that is currently Fripp's main project.  They will be putting
a new album out within a few months that should be much better than the
first one.
