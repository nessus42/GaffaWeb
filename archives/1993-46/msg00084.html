Sarah McLachlan was in Toronto today and was interviewed at the MuchMusic studi
os.  Here are some tidbits....sorry I didn't tape it.  The song Possession was
based on a collection of fan letters that were demanding and possessive. This
disturbed her so she wrote the song as a way of dealing with it.  It is sung
from the point of view of the fan(s).

The interviewer was Ziggy but she did bring up something of interest:

Ziggy: "Do You know who's a big fan of yours...?  Tori Amos!"
Sarah: "Really?  I'm a big fan of hers too."
Ziggy: "You two should get together...that would be neat."

Anyway.....I'm looking forward to seeing her at Massey Hall next week.  She's
never played there before.
Les
