So, the big debate is:  "What order should I listen to the songs on
The Red Shoes"?

The answer:  my way, of course!  At home, I plop the C.D. down, and hit
"Scramble".  It's a new listening experience each time!

At work, I plop the cassette in, and just listen to it in the order it's
on the album, remembering (or rather, being rudely reminded) to hit
"fast forward" when "Y should I love U" comes on.

Note:  This is also the best way to listen to Hounds of Love.  Plop the
CD in, hit "Scramble", and prepare of a serious mindfuck!
