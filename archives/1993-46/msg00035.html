This is going to sound strange, but has anyone else been listening to 
"The Red Shoes" with headphones on and had the volume up so lound that 
you couldn't hear anything else.  I have and everytime I listen to this 
track I can swear I hear a phone ringing.  The first couple times this 
happened, I just thought it was the phone in my room.  But then I played 
the song while my roomate was in the room and the same thing happned.  He

phone ringing in the background when the song was recorded.  Does anyone 
have any comments on this.  By the way, I got my copy Saturday and have 
been listening to it for the last 36 hours basically nonstop.  And I'm 
enjoying every second of it.  All the songs are perfect even "Why Should 
I Love You".  I don't see why very many other people don't like.  That's 
only my opinion.
