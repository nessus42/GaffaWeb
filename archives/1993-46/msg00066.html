New KaTe guest appearance

From: GTP10@phx.cam.ac.uk
Date: Tue, 16 Nov 93 10:15:31 GMT
Subject: New KaTe guest appearance
To: Love-Hounds@uunet.UU.NET

KaTe appears doing BVs (of the aaah, oooh variety) and playing keyboards
on track 10 (entitled "Kimiad") on the new Ala(i)n Stivell album "Again".
This track also features John Giblin on bass, Charlie Morgan on drums,
Alan Murphy on guitar, Davey Spillane on uillean pipes. Del Palmer is
credited for sound mixing and KaTe for production. The album is made up
of rerecordings of songs Stivell recorded early in his career. They are
all traditional.

Incidentally, KaTe's brother Jay (John Carder) contributed lyrics for
a couple of songs on another relatively recent Stivell release "The Mist
Of Avalon".

Geoff Parks
