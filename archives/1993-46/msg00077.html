> Trivia for Kate's fans, I am sure. Does anybody know what G.O.C.
> (J.O.C.?) means, in the song Ken? And, basically, what the song is
> about.

At the risk of being one of many respondents. Ken is Ken Livingstone,
ex-head of the ex-Greater London Council. This was the local Council
in London in the early sixties, disbanded by Margaret Thatcher because
of its left-leaning ideology.  Ken was written for a comic-strip movie
based on the council.
