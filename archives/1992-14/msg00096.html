>   "Queer Positive?" Somehow, I find that to be an extreme contradiction in

The term "queer", since it was a derogatory term for so long, has been
"reclaimed" by gay groups to be a positive terminology for their lifestyle of
late.  Just like the appropriation of the pink triangle for the symbol, which
was the Nazi symbol for prisoners in concentration camps whose "crime" was
homosexuality.  The point is to make it a non-degrading term.  I'm assuming
that's what was meant by "queer positive".  Though I don't see what that should
have to do with someone's music, unless they're outwardly gay-bashing.
