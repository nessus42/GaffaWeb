turn are copying the tape for the people below them (B). If people can and 
want to trade, then person A and person B just send each other a tape in the 
~~~~~~~~mail with the recordings on them. If person B is unable to trade (or doesn't have anything that the person A wants), then person B sends person A a blank tape and enough postage (cash or stamps) for person A to send the tape back 
when it has been recorded. I personally have no qualms about person B just sending money to person A to cover the cost of the tape, postage, and mailer (please use padded mailers if possible to protect the tape). Person A should 
try to contact their people B and each person B should try to contact their respective person A. If anybody has any email problem drop me a line and we'll see what we can do (I'm no expert at email, but everybody here got through to me).
