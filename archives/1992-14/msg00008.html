I'd like to take a stab at this subject. My experience over the years shows 
that whenever I wlk into a store belong to a major chain (ie Tower or 
Wherehouse) the young 16 yr. old kid behind the counter barely recognizes 
the name Kate Bush. If I wander into a small used/alternative store where 
the employees are serious music fans and not looking for easy cash and a 
"cool job" they consistently know about Kate Bush and her work. I think it 
just indicates what type of people really pay attention to Kate, atleast 
here in the US.
