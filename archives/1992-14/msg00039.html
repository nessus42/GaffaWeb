Yet another "the first time I heard such-n-such KaTe song on the radio"

Cerphe played "Hounds of Love" on his progressive show tonight around 9:15.
It was weird because I'd just been listening to a tape of misk KaTe stuff
and had popped out the cassette and was flipping between radio stations. 
When I heard the familiar "Boom boom boom-boom" beat, my first thought
was that my fingers had slipped and had re-inserted the cassette instead of
hitting a preset button.

Chalk up another for Cerphe, who's also played the live RUTH on his show.
