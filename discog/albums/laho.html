Live at Hammersmith Odeon

This album is only available as part of the CD&Video Double Pack 'Live at Hammersmith Odeon' which also includes the video tape. The video tape was available since 1981, while the package including the CD only came out in 1994, maybe as a reaction of the ever increasing number of bootlegs that featured the Hammersmith Odeon soundtrack:
