The LP version of this album is missing the last song, Walk Straight Down The Middle Of It. In 1989, LPs were somehow still the 'original' format an album would appear in, so Walk Straight Down The Middle was a bonus track on the CD, CC, and later MD as well. You might still note the slightly longer pause before the last track on the CD, and doesn't This Woman's Work make a perfect end of an album?
