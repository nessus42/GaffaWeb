This Woman's Work

This 'album' is part of a boxed set, also called 'This Woman's Work'. The CD version consists of 2 CDs, while the LP and Cassette versions consist of 3 LPs/CCs. This album is not available separately, only as part of the boxed sets, but actually some shops split up a couple of boxed sets and sold the CDs separately.

There are also two bootleg CDs, This Woman's Work Extended Edition I and This Woman's Work Extended Edition II, both contain extra tracks.
