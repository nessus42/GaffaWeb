Uillean Pipes

 Also known as union pipes or Northumbrian pipes, these are an Irish wind instrument not unlike Scottish bagpipes, but operated by means of a bellows worked by the player's arm, rather than by mouth. The Uillean pipes are played by Liam O'Flynn on the songs Jig Of Life and Hello Earth, and by Davy Spillane on the songs The Sensual World and Never Be Mine.

Under Ice

A song from The Ninth Wave song-cycle on the album Hounds Of Love. Although some of the songs on this album were recorded over a long period of weeks, this song falls at the opposite extreme: it was begun and completed in one day.

Unicorn

Bassist Pat Martin and drummer Peter Perrier of this group played on demos recorded at Dave Gilmour's home studio in 1973. Gilmour returned the favor by playing pedal steel guitar on their subsequent albums Blue Pine Trees in 1974 and Too Many Crooks in 1976.

Ure, Midge

The former guitarist of Ultravox and Visage, Ure also co-wrote the original Band Aid single Do They Know It's Christmas with Bob Geldof. Ure joined the all-star band supporting Kate in her performance of the song The Wedding List at the Prince's Trust Rock Gala. Kate later provided backing vocals for the song Sister And Brother on his 1988 solo album Answers To Nothing.

Utterly Utterly Live At The Shaftesbury Theatre

A recording of the Comic Relief benefit concert for famine relief in Africa held at the Shaftesbury Theatre, London, on 4 - 6 April 1986, released as both an album and a video cassette. Kate performs a version of the song Breathing, accompanying herself on piano, as well as the comic duet Do Bears... with Rowan Atkinson. Also appearing on the album and video are performances by Lenny Henry, Dawn French and Jennifer Saunders, and Cliff Richard, among many others.
